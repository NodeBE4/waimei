<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">8</span>日，本网获悉获刑<span lang="EN-US">4</span>年半即将刑满的山东维权人士于新永先生已年届<span lang="EN-US">60</span>岁，<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">9</span>日，他将刑满<span lang="EN-US">4</span>年半而走出牢房。于新永先生因追求社会正义与法治、依法监督公权力，依法伸张公民权利而身陷囹圄。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">于新永：</span></b><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">1963</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年出生，山东省济南市人，网名“济南老于”，前山东省冶金工业总公司工作人员，自由职业者，警察非法暴力监督组发起人之一，民间抗霾志愿者，民主维权公民，中国在押政治犯。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">因个性为人实在仗义，热情智慧，而广受友人赞许和信任；近年来，因追求民主理念，积极参与帮助蒙冤访民维权活动，要求政府信息公开，起诉山东省多个政要部门等，故而遭到山东省当局的严厉打压被迫；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2015</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年，曾因国内爆出多起警察非法开枪打人、打死人新闻，而与他人共同发起成立“警察非法暴力监督组”，以关注呼吁诸如张六毛案、舒向新案、山东临沂少年派出所死亡案、徐明死亡案等社会热点案件，故而触及当局敏感神经，曾被警方多次约谈和警告；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2016</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">-2017</span>年，曾因参与民间抗霾自愿者聚会，并率先起诉了济南市环保局，而遭到当局的干预与遏制；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2018</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">16</span>日，被济南市警方突然从家中带走传唤，并以涉嫌“寻衅滋事罪”刑拘；同年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">22</span>日，被济南市检察院又新添涉嫌“侮辱罪”正式批捕，控其曾经撰写的文章侮辱了前任和现任的国家领导人，涉嫌侮辱罪，从而被逮捕；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">其被提审过程中，主要被审问的是<span lang="EN-US">5</span>年前其与他人纪念六四学运之事，被指有人举报他们在<span lang="EN-US">5</span>年前曾非法聚会，纪念<span lang="EN-US">6.4</span>学运并拍摄视频上传网络等；<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">31</span>日，其案被济南市历下区检察院以“侮辱罪”、“寻衅滋事罪”起诉至历下区法院；<span lang="EN-US">2019</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">21</span>日，其案在山东省济南市历下区法院开庭审理；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2020</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">28</span>日<span lang="EN-US">,</span>终被历下区法院以「寻衅滋事罪」判处有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">4</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月；其不服，提起上诉；<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">20</span>日，被济南市中级法院二审裁决：驳回上诉，维持原判；刑期至<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">9</span>日。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"></p><div class="separator" style="clear: both;
