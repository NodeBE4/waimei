<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">26</span>日，本网获悉：山东青岛市法轮功学员王国辉和于春丽夫妇分别获刑<span lang="EN-US">4</span>年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>个月、<span lang="EN-US">4</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>个月。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">王国辉和于春丽夫妇系山东省青岛市法轮功学员，<span lang="EN-US">50</span>岁左右，原住青岛市城阳区棘洪滩街道段家庄。<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年因警方多次迫害，夫妇两人离家避祸。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">2022</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">27</span>日，城阳区国保大队和棘洪滩派出所警察包围了王国辉、于春丽夫妇的租住处，将夫妇两人抓捕，先关押在派出所内几十天，于<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">7</span>日，于春丽、王国辉才被关押到普东看守所。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">2023</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">18</span>日，王国辉、于春丽夫妇在青岛即墨区法院被庭审。当庭于春丽被判刑<span lang="EN-US">4</span>年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>个月，刑期至<span lang="EN-US">2027</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">26</span>日，王国辉被判<span lang="EN-US">4</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>个月，刑期至：<span lang="EN-US">2027</span>年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">26</span>日。王国辉和于春丽当庭提出上诉。<span lang="EN-US" style="color: #00b050;"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p>
