<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">2</span>日，本网获悉获刑<span lang="EN-US">7</span>年的河南省安阳市汤阴县法轮功学员王朝杰的案情及简历，现在通报如下：<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">王朝杰：</span></b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">河南省安阳市法轮功学员，<span lang="EN-US">1987</span>年生，现年<span lang="EN-US">37</span>岁，辽宁省建昌县人，河南省安阳市汤阴县第一中学的教师。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">王朝杰因宣讲法轮功，于<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">16</span>日，王朝杰被文峰区国保警察抓捕，关押到安阳市看守所。同年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月，王朝杰被构陷到安阳市法院，进入庭审阶段。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">2024</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月获悉，安阳市法院于<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年对王朝杰判刑<span lang="EN-US">7</span>年，罚金<span lang="EN-US">5</span>万元。安阳市法院直接从王朝杰的公积金中扣押罚金，余额逼迫家属补齐到<span lang="EN-US">5</span>万元整。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">王朝杰于<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">1</span>日被转监到郑州新密市监狱，现在他被关押在新密市监狱九监区。刑期至：<span lang="EN-US">2029</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">15</span>日。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p>
