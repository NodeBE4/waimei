[中国国家电网：最大可能避免拉闸限电](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210927-1197821)
------

<div>
<p>中国多个省份本月相继拉闸限电。中国国家电网今天（27日）称，要最大可能避免出现拉闸限电情况，坚决守住民生、发展和安全底线。</p><p>据微信公众号“国家网电“发布的信息，针对当前供电形势，国家电网表示，要充分发挥“大国重器”“顶梁柱”作用，践行“人民电业为人民”企业宗旨，综合施策、多措并举，全力以赴打好电力保供攻坚战，保障基本民生用电需求。</p><p>国家网电称，要强化全网统一调度，合理安排运行方式，服务好发电企业，做到应并尽并；要统筹调配资源，充分发挥大电网平台优势，挖掘跨区跨省通道送电潜力。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>国家网电还要求，加强用电情况监测，全力做好居民生活用电保障，严格执行政府制定的需求响应与有序用电方案，严格落实供电保障责任等。</p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/zbpe-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=zbpe399&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_desktop-zbpe399_2.png?dHEiGouxxZpWwWTJE04dyGyUN74JOU2z" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_desktop-zbpe399_2.png?dHEiGouxxZpWwWTJE04dyGyUN74JOU2z" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/zbpe-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=zbpe399&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_mobile-zbpe399_2.png?qZnBcIGCJvPR8A2IAZOZuOxdoGZDatiI" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_mobile-zbpe399_2.png?qZnBcIGCJvPR8A2IAZOZuOxdoGZDatiI" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
