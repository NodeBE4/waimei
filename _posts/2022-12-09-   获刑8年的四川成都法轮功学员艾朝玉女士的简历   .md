font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2001</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2007</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">-2008</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月，曾因根据北京高层指令不惜严守、确保<span lang="EN-US">2008</span>年奥运会在北京顺利、圆满召开，而几次被当地龙泉驿区警方暴力抓捕，关进成都市新津洗脑班接受洗脑式教育培训；后其与一同是法轮功信仰者再婚，婚后生活相对平稳幸福，但其夫妇及其家中其他成员仍遭到中共当局各级<span lang="EN-US">610</span>办公室的迫害，多次被关押在洗脑班，多次被迫流离失所；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2019</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">10</span>日，其夫妇二人再次被成都市成华区警方抓捕刑拘，其家遭查抄；同年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">17</span>日，二人又被成华区警方以涉嫌邪教罪转正式逮捕，并被关押在成都市看守所；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">后其二人案因证据不足曾被成华区检察院两次退侦，但成华区警方一再构陷；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体;
