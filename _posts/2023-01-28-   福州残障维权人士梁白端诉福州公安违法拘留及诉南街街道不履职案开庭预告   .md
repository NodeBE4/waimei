[福州残障维权人士梁白端诉福州公安违法拘留及诉南街街道不履职案开庭预告](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2023/01/blog-post_32.html)
------

<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">28</span>日，本网获悉：福州维权人士梁白端诉福州公安违法拘留及诉南街街道不履职案即将于明天开庭。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">福州残障维权人士梁白端诉：<span lang="EN-US">1</span>、福州市公安局鼓楼分局违法行政拘留案。<span lang="EN-US">2</span>、诉鼓楼区南街街道办事处不履行信息公开法定职责案。分别于明天（<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">29</span>日）上午<span lang="EN-US">9</span>点和<span lang="EN-US">10</span>点<span lang="EN-US">30</span>分在晋安区法院第三法庭开庭，地址：福州秀峰路<span lang="EN-US">16</span>号。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">案件简介</span></b><span style="font-family: 宋体;
