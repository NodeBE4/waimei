<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">8</span>日，本网获悉：曾两次获刑、坐牢<span lang="EN-US">7</span>年，狱中曾遭受野蛮酷刑的湖北民主维权人士袁奉初仍持续遭当局逼迫，工作遭无端解聘。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">袁奉初今天通报：<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></b></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">“我现在所在的位置在苏州华硕电脑厂区内，早上下夜班刚回宿舍即被公司无端解聘，工资没发，且没提任何赔偿之事宜，亦没给任何之说法。随后我被带至公司警务室，湖北咸宁石某聂某等一行国宝在此等候我谈话，其目的很明确，两会来临，要我配合他们的维稳工作，要随时报告我的行踪，否则只要我在中国境内类似的事情会接二连三的发生。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">在过去的两年多里，我一贯不与国宝及政府的任何维稳人员接触来往，即使长时间被他们监视控制穷困潦倒之际也不接受他们的工作，始终坚守正义，维护尊严，做一位有良知的中国人，这一点我做到了。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">袁奉初<span lang="EN-US"><span style="mso-spacerun: yes;">&nbsp; </span>2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">8</span>日于苏州华硕电脑厂区内”<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">对袁奉初的境况，本网将持续关注。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">袁奉初简介：</span></b><span style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">本名袁兵。<span lang="EN-US">1982</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">19</span>日出生，湖北省咸宁市人，原广州轻工技师学校职工，南方街头运动倡导者，“赤壁举牌五君子”之一，维权公民，中国曾押政治犯。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">因自幼受其家庭自由民主思想影响，追求民主理念，近年来，多以实际行动奔走于全国各地，广结民主维权人士，积极探讨实现民主共和的路径，随招致当局的恐慌不安，曾屡遭警方恐吓与拘押；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">2012</span><span style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">24</span>日，曾因联络各地维权公民并多次聚餐继续维权热评，被警方强行驱离广州；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">2013</span><span style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">10</span>日，曾因声援南周事件而遭警方殴打；同年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">24</span>日，曾因举牌声援及宣传民主理念而被广州市警方以涉嫌“非法集会罪”行政拘留<span lang="EN-US">7</span>日；<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">25</span>日，又因与黄文勋、袁小华等街头运动者在湖北省赤壁市参与《周游华夏践行光明中国梦》活动，而被赤壁市警方以涉嫌“非法集会罪”行政拘留<span lang="EN-US">15</span>日；<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">19</span>日，被以涉嫌“煽动颠覆国家政权罪”刑拘；<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">17</span>日，被赤壁市警方以同罪名正式逮捕，并与同年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月认定与袁小华同案；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">2016</span><span style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">9</span>日，被湖北省赤壁市法院以“聚众扰乱公共场所秩序罪”、“寻衅滋事罪”判处有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">4</span>年；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">2017</span><span style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">17</span>日，刑满出狱；</span><span style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12pt;">据悉，狱中曾遭严重酷刑虐待，致面部受伤缝合多针，宣判前被超期羁押近</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12pt;">3</span><span style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12pt;">年；</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">出狱后，因赤壁市公安局一直派人监视、干预骚扰其日常活动及行动自由，随与维权友人陈进新、梁一鸣于<span lang="EN-US">2017</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">2</span>日去当地国保大队投诉，但终因双方理念不同、争论颇激而不欢而散；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">回家后，因其将在公安局的遭遇简况发到朋友圈，遂于当天午后被赤壁市警方以涉嫌“寻衅滋事罪”从家中拘捕；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">2017</span><span style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">8</span>日，被赤壁市检察院正式批捕，羁押于赤壁看守所：<span lang="EN-US">2019</span>年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">12</span>日，终被湖北省赤壁市法院一审以“寻衅滋事罪”判处有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">3</span>年；其不服上诉，后经咸宁市中级法院二审裁决，依然维持原判；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">2020</span><span style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">2</span>日，刑满获释；狱中曾遭酷刑，因在里面说了<span lang="EN-US">"</span>反动<span lang="EN-US">"</span>话语，被吊七天，以致手差点残废。此前在湖北省咸宁监狱服刑 。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"></p><div class="separator" style="clear: both;
