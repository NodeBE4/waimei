<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">姜奎：</span></b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">贵州省黔东南苗族侗族自治州（简称黔东南州）凯里市人，基督徒，黔东南州凯里市基督复临安息日教会神职人员，教会聚会点骨干成员及带领人（之一），中国在押良心犯。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">自从参与贵州省凯里市基督复临安息日教会聚会点的敬拜活动以来，因一直致力于热忱服务教会的多种事工活动，随深得教会聚会点长老与众信徒的信任和赞许；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">但因该教会聚会点<span lang="EN-US">20</span>多年来一直属于家庭教会聚会模式，对中共当局严格把控的“三自教会”管理拒不认同也不接受，虽然早已向贵州省宗教局提交请求教会聚会点合法的申请，但却始终未获审批，故遭贵州当局在意识形态上的严苛限制；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2020</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月，又因该聚会点信徒不断增多，聚会点迅速由原先的<span lang="EN-US">1</span>个增加到<span lang="EN-US">3</span>个，遂引起贵州省黔东南州当局的注意与恐慌，并迅速成立项目组，对其聚会点进行全面侦查；之后，其及教会长老等<span lang="EN-US">4</span>位神职人员悉数被黔东南州凯里市警方带走，并被以涉嫌“诈骗罪”刑拘，自此失联；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">后其<span lang="EN-US">4</span>人又被黔东南州凯里市检察院以同罪名正式批捕，控其因聚会点申请未被国家审批，众信徒所交奉献款均属于诈骗款，故其一切教会行为属于违法；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2021</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">14</span>日，其<span lang="EN-US">4</span>人案被黔东南州凯里市法院一审分别以“诈骗罪”枉法判处<span lang="EN-US">12</span>—<span lang="EN-US">3</span>年不等刑期；其中，姜奎被重判有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">6</span>年，刑期至<span lang="EN-US">2026</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月；其不服判决，已向黔东南州中级法院提起上诉；二审正在等待中。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">目前姜奎被羁押于黔东南州凯里市看守所（贵州省<span lang="EN-US">/</span>黔东南苗族侗族自治州<span lang="EN-US">/</span>凯里市<span lang="EN-US">/</span>永乐路，邮政编码：<span lang="EN-US">556099</span>）。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p>
