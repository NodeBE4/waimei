<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">6</span>日，本网获悉：石家庄法轮功学员刘顺芝、梁业宁、蒲杏池<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">12</span>日被石家庄市桥西区法院分别判刑<span lang="EN-US">8</span>年、<span lang="EN-US">6</span>年和<span lang="EN-US">4</span>年。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2022</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">23</span>日，农历新年前夕，家住石家庄市恒大雅苑小区的刘顺芝一家四口被方兴派出所和裕华分局警察绑架；梁业宁和张林玉夫妇被正定公安局在正定租房住处绑架；同一时间，石家庄市桥西区教小孩画画的蒲杏池，在家被红旗派出所和桥西分局绑架。后她们家属被放回家。刘顺芝、梁业宁、蒲杏池三人被拘禁在石家庄元氏县水利宾馆近两个月。期间，被警察用戴黑头套，戴手铐、长期坐铁椅子等方式，对她们进行精神和身体上的折磨，并威胁恐吓，逼供诱供。<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">16</span>日，三人被元氏县检察院刑事拘留，被转到石家庄市第二看守所关押。后遭批捕、起诉。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2023</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年7月<span lang="EN-US">12</span>日她们被石家庄市桥西区法院判刑。刘顺芝被判刑<span lang="EN-US">8</span>年，并处罚金<span lang="EN-US">1</span>万元；梁业宁被判刑<span lang="EN-US">6</span>年，并处罚金五千元；蒲杏池被判刑<span lang="EN-US">4</span>年，并处罚金五千元。据悉，刘顺芝、梁业宁不服判决结果，已上诉到石家庄市中级法院。目前，三人仍被关押在石家庄市第二看守所。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p>
