[捷克政府正在讨论赠送疫苗给台湾](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210723-1172575)
------

<div>
<p>曾于去年率团来台访问的捷克参议院议长韦德齐（Milos Vystrcil）22日发推文透露，捷克政府正在讨论赠送疫苗给台湾。</p><p>据联合新闻网报道，韦德齐表示，捷克政府打算向台湾提供冠病疫苗。他说，自己已就此事和捷克卫生部长渥伊泰赫（Adam Vojtech）通电话讨论，会乐观看待26日政府会议的结果。</p><p>在此之前，立陶宛政府上月底宣布将捐赠台湾2万剂AZ疫苗，预估于9月底前运抵台湾，另外，斯洛伐克政府日前也宣布将捐赠台湾1万剂疫苗。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>韦德齐去年8月30日至9月4日率团来台进行六天访问，并在立院演讲时用中文说出“我是台湾人”，获得全体立法委员起立鼓掌。</p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
