<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">30</span>日，本网获悉获刑<span lang="EN-US">3</span>年的黑龙江省黑河市五大连池市<span lang="EN-US">77</span>岁的法轮功学员庞善喜的案情及简历，现在通报如下：<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">庞善喜：</span></b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">黑龙江省黑河市五大连池市法轮功学员，男，<span lang="EN-US">1947</span>年生，<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">77</span>岁，原是龙镇粮库职工，一九九九年开始修炼法轮功。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">庞善喜老人为了给五大连池市龙镇农场派出所警察宣讲法轮功，于<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月份给这个派出所写一封劝善信。随即遭抓捕抄家，后被刑事拘留、批捕、起诉。<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月，嫩江市法院对庞善喜判刑<span lang="EN-US">3</span>年，罚款一万元。庞善喜老人先被关押到北安监狱，一个月左右被转关至黑龙江省泰莱监狱服刑。刑期至：<span lang="EN-US">2026</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;"><o:p>&nbsp;</o:p></span></p>
