<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">30</span>日，本网获悉：狱中著名人权捍卫者张展女士昨日（<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">29</span>日）与母亲视频，身体情况危殆。张展母亲这样描述视频会见张展的情景：“今天视频了，情况更差，要人扶着走路，说话时，头都无力扬着。”本网强烈要求中共当局立即予以张展保外就医，不要继续制造曹顺利、刘晓波这样的惨剧。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">早期，曾就读于西南财经大学金融专业并获取硕士学位；自<span lang="EN-US">2010</span>年起，离开家乡到上海谋职；曾为追求自由理想而甘愿放弃在一家证券公司的高薪职位；曾任职律师，但后因在网上发表针砭时弊的文章而遭报复，被迫离开律师岗位；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2019</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">自<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年初以来，曾因武汉冠状病毒疫情蔓延，其为了获取一手数据，不惧风险，亲赴武汉做实地疫情考察与报导；此举很快遭到中共当局的极端不满和迅速打压；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2020</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">14</span>日，被武汉警方从一家暂住旅店抓走，次日被上海市浦东新区警方以涉嫌“寻衅滋事罪”刑拘；<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">19</span>日，被浦东新区检察院予以批捕；<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">18</span>日，其案被移送至上海市浦东新区法院；<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">28</span>日，被该法院一审以“寻衅滋事罪”判处有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">4</span>年，刑期至<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">14</span>日；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体;
