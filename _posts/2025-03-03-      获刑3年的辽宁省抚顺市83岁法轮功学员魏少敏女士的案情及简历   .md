<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2025</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">3</span>日，本网获悉获刑<span lang="EN-US">3</span>年的辽宁省抚顺市<span lang="EN-US">83</span>岁法轮功学员魏少敏女士的案情及简历，现在通报如下：<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">魏少敏：</span></b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">辽宁省抚顺市法轮功学员，女，<span lang="EN-US">1943</span>年生，<span lang="EN-US">2025</span>年已经<span lang="EN-US">83</span>岁。在中共对法轮功的迫害中，魏少敏老人曾于<span lang="EN-US">2007</span>被劳教<span lang="EN-US">3</span>年，<span lang="EN-US">2014</span>年被判刑<span lang="EN-US">7</span>年半。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">2022</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">8</span>日，魏少敏在顺城区新华街车站等车时，给一位老人宣讲法轮功时遭人跟踪举报，遭抓捕，被拘留十天，罚款<span lang="EN-US">1000</span>元之后回家。过后警察继续罗织构陷老人的材料，其实当时释放回家是所谓“取保候审”。后中共警察将老人构陷到检察院，并于<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月中旬再次到魏少敏的女儿家将老人抓捕，关押在抚顺市看守所。近日得知魏少敏被判刑<span lang="EN-US">3</span>年，已经关押在辽宁女子监狱服刑。刑期至：<span lang="EN-US">2027</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p>
