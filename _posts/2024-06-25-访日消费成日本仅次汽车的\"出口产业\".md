[访日消费成日本仅次汽车的"出口产业"](https://cn.nikkei.com/industry/tradingretail/55967-2024-06-25-15-04-02.html)
------

<div>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="text-indent: 24.0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0;"><span style="font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: 11pt;">访日游客已经成为日本经济的支柱。<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>~<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月的访日游客名义消费换算成年度数据为<span lang="EN-US">7.2</span>万亿日元，<span lang="EN-US">10</span>年里扩大到了<span lang="EN-US">5</span>倍。与主要品类的出口额相比，访日消费的规模仅次于汽车出口额。日本向海外推销的品类正在从商品转向服务。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 11pt; font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif;"><span style="mso-spacerun: yes;"></span><o:p></o:p></span></p><p class="MsoNormal" style="text-indent: 24.0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0;"><span style="font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: 11pt;">从日本国内生产总值（<span lang="EN-US">GDP</span>）统计中相当于访日游客消费的“非居民家庭的国内直接购买”来看，新冠疫情前的<span lang="EN-US">2019</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>~<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月换算成年度数据为<span lang="EN-US">4.6</span>万亿日元。<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>~<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月超过了疫情前的水平，之后继续扩大，进入<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年以后，站上<span lang="EN-US">7</span>万亿日元大关。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 11pt; font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif;"><o:p></o:p></span></p><p class="MsoNormal" style="text-indent: 24.0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0;"><span style="font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: 11pt;">访日游客消费在日本经济中的存在感提高。<span lang="EN-US">7.2</span>万亿日元的规模与日本各品类的出口额相比，虽然不到汽车（<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年为<span lang="EN-US">17.3</span>万亿日元）的一半，但超过了排在第<span lang="EN-US">2</span>位的半导体等电子零部件（<span lang="EN-US">5.5</span>万亿日元）和排在第<span lang="EN-US">3</span>位的钢铁（<span lang="EN-US">4.5</span>万亿日元）。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 11pt; font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif;"><o:p></o:p></span></p><table border="0" height="506" style="border-collapse: collapse; width: 25.1241%; height: 506px; border-width: 0px; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;"><colgroup><col style="width: 100%;"></colgroup><tbody><tr><td><img src="https://cn.nikkei.com/images/2024/06/0625/0625-03-2-M.jpg" loading="lazy" width="500" height="553" data-path="local-images:/2024/06/0625/0625-03-2-M.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></td></tr></tbody></table><p class="MsoNormal" style="text-indent: 24.0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0;"></p><p class="MsoNormal" style="text-indent: 24.0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0;"><span style="font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: 11pt;"><span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年与<span lang="EN-US">2019</span>年相比，汽车和钢铁的出口额增长<span lang="EN-US">45</span>％左右，半导体等电子零部件增长近<span lang="EN-US">4</span>成。<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>~<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月访日游客消费比<span lang="EN-US">2019</span>年同期增长<span lang="EN-US">6</span>成多。虽然不能单纯对比，但可以看出，访日游客消费的上涨率也呈现出了超过主要出口品类的势头。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 11pt; font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif;"><o:p></o:p></span></p><p class="MsoNormal" style="text-indent: 24.0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0;"><span style="font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: 11pt;">瑞穗<span lang="EN-US">Research &amp; Technologies</span>的酒井才介指出，“<span lang="EN-US">2010</span>年代日本企业纷纷将国内的生产基地迁移至海外，而且半导体等的国际竞争力下降，因此，尽管日元贬值，产品出口也很难增长”。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 11pt; font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif;"><o:p></o:p></span></p><p class="MsoNormal" style="text-indent: 24.0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0;"><span style="font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: 11pt;">来自海外的访日游客超过了疫情前。从日本国家旅游局公布的访日游客人数来看，<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月，单月数据首次突破<span lang="EN-US">300</span>万人，截至<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月，已连续<span lang="EN-US">3</span>个月达到<span lang="EN-US">300</span>万人水平。今年仅<span lang="EN-US">1</span>~<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月访日游客人数就达到了<span lang="EN-US">2019</span>年全年的近<span lang="EN-US">5</span>成。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 11pt; font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif;"><o:p></o:p></span></p><table border="0" height="510" style="border-collapse: collapse; width: 27.4081%; height: 290.985px; border-width: 0px; margin-left: auto; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0;"><span style="font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: 11pt;">与疫情前相比，日本的入境游客消费额的复苏也比其他国家更为明显。旅游白皮书显示，<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>~<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月主要国家的入境游客消费额与<span lang="EN-US">2019</span>年同期相比，日本增加<span lang="EN-US">38.8</span>％，西班牙增加<span lang="EN-US">30.7</span>％，意大利增加<span lang="EN-US">16.5</span>％，美国减少<span lang="EN-US">4.3</span>％，新加坡减少<span lang="EN-US">1.6</span>％。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 11pt; font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif;"><o:p></o:p></span></p><p class="MsoNormal" style="text-indent: 24.0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0;"><span style="font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: 11pt;">日本<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年的入境游客人均消费单价也比<span lang="EN-US">2019</span>年增加<span lang="EN-US">31</span>％。平均住宿天数从<span lang="EN-US">6.2</span>晚增加到了<span lang="EN-US">6.9</span>晚。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoPlainText" style="text-indent: 24.0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0;"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 11pt; font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif;"><o:p></o:p></span></p><p class="MsoPlainText" style="text-indent: 24.0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0;"><span style="font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: 11pt;">原因之一是汇率的影响。<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年日元兑美元汇率为<span lang="EN-US">1</span>美元兑<span lang="EN-US">140.58</span>日元，与<span lang="EN-US">2019</span>年的平均汇率（<span lang="EN-US">108.98</span>日元）相比，日元贬值、美元升值近三成。这种划算感促使消费扩大。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoPlainText"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 11pt; font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif;"><o:p></o:p></span></p><br><p class="MsoPlainText" style="text-indent: 24.0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0;"><span style="font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: 11pt;"></span></p><p class="MsoPlainText" style="text-indent: 24.0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0;"><span style="font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: 11pt;">消费构成发生变化。目前，<span lang="EN-US">2010</span>年代出现的廉价游轮旅行和免税店爆买行为已经消退。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoPlainText"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 11pt; font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif;"><o:p></o:p></span></p><p class="MsoPlainText" style="text-indent: 24.0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0;"><span style="font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: 11pt;">按品类来比较<span lang="EN-US">2019</span>年和<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年的消费单价，结果发现购买商品的费用减少，而住宿费、餐饮费、交通费以及跟团费等“娱乐等服务费”明显增加。这反映了游客的在日消费正在转向重视服务。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoPlainText"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 11pt; font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif;"><o:p></o:p></span></p><p class="MsoPlainText" style="text-indent: 24.0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0;"><span style="font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: 11pt;">备受期待的是开拓奢侈消费群体的市场。日本观光厅的数据表明，<span lang="EN-US">2019</span>年消费超过<span lang="EN-US">100</span>万日元的富裕阶层仅占访日游客人数的<span lang="EN-US">1%</span>左右，但其总消费额约达<span lang="EN-US">6700</span>亿日元，占到整体的大约<span lang="EN-US">14%</span>。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoPlainText"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 11pt; font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif;"><o:p></o:p></span></p><p class="MsoPlainText" style="text-indent: 24.0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0;"><span style="font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: 11pt;">疫情后的访日游客呈现出比疫情前更向东京、大阪等三大都市圈集中的趋势。日本政府正在推动吸引游客到地方旅游，而不是仅限于城市。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoPlainText"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 11pt; font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif;"><o:p></o:p></span></p><p class="MsoPlainText" style="text-indent: 24.0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0;"><span style="font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: 11pt;">日本面临的课题是接待访日游客的观光基础设施跟不上增长。住宿业及机场地勤等领域的人手短缺问题变得严重。近来，一些地方机场还出现了由于航空燃料短缺而无法接受外国航空公司增加航班的情况。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoPlainText"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 11pt; font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif;"><o:p></o:p></span></p><p class="MsoPlainText" style="text-indent: 24.0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0;"><span style="font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: 11pt;">在热门旅游地，过度旅游问题也变得严重。日本要想实现市场的进一步增长，还需要缓解地方的负担。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoPlainText"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 11pt; font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif;"><o:p></o:p></span></p><p class="MsoPlainText" style="text-indent: 24.0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0;"><span style="font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: 11pt;">入境消费也存在容易受国际形势影响的缺点。疫情期间，因访日游客急剧减少，住宿业和餐饮业受到了重创。过去，跨境旅游客流量在<span lang="EN-US">03</span>年非典等疫情下都曾大幅下降。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoPlainText"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 11pt; font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif;"><o:p></o:p></span></p><p class="MsoPlainText" style="text-indent: 24.0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0;"><span style="font-size: 11pt; font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif;">如果出现新的传染病或日本周边发生紧急情况等导致访日游客数量减少，可能会对日本经济造成比以往更大的冲击。在访日游客增加支撑经济期间，日本还需要培育人工智能（<span lang="EN-US">AI</span>）和半导体等增长产业。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoPlainText" style="text-indent: 24.0pt;
