<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">10</span>日，本网获悉被逮捕的辽宁沈阳维权人士雷凤春的案情及简历，现通报如下：<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">雷凤春：辽宁沈阳维权人士，男，原是政府体制内人士。因举报领导徇私舞弊、弄虚作假，被领导陷害开除公职，自此开始上访维权。雷凤春从<span lang="EN-US">2012</span>年开始，雷凤春积极参与营救被关黑监狱访民、现场声援受迫害访民的庭审、参加在京访民纪念<span lang="EN-US">“9•18”</span>声讨官僚腐败等活动，屡次遭到当局的打压和迫害，多次被拘留、非法软禁。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">2024</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">年初，雷凤春因到国家信访局多次登记，被沈阳市地方政府强行将他从北京截访回沈阳。回到当地后，沈阳市公安局铁西分局立即于<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">19</span>日以涉嫌<span lang="EN-US">“</span>寻衅滋事罪<span lang="EN-US">”</span>将雷凤春刑事拘留。<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">23</span>日，雷凤春被沈阳市铁西区检察院批准逮捕。目前，雷凤春被羁押在沈阳市铁西区看守所。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"></p><div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;"><a href="https://blogger.googleusercontent.com/img/b/R29vZ2xl/AVvXsEgxPaxJsPiBpJUlmKVBC2SCRwq1rh9gaQXuMRVoKZZG3VQG818XTj2Tc7Aw6cai5JkGIk1m2ajwF64s2g9BTfyBQBXBHF2MOjL5DZnu7n_esgrMsHP_7hkLtBEBINZAVJ1yHgUqZLq810F7Gp1UVzB4Fvna0D6Edtg_rp-S4D9F9fv3af6UjtGUevNhM2-v/s680/QQ%E6%88%AA%E5%9B%BE20240310145435.png" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;"><img border="0" data-original-height="680" data-original-width="469" src="https://blogger.googleusercontent.com/img/b/R29vZ2xl/AVvXsEgxPaxJsPiBpJUlmKVBC2SCRwq1rh9gaQXuMRVoKZZG3VQG818XTj2Tc7Aw6cai5JkGIk1m2ajwF64s2g9BTfyBQBXBHF2MOjL5DZnu7n_esgrMsHP_7hkLtBEBINZAVJ1yHgUqZLq810F7Gp1UVzB4Fvna0D6Edtg_rp-S4D9F9fv3af6UjtGUevNhM2-v/s16000/QQ%E6%88%AA%E5%9B%BE20240310145435.png" /></a></div><p></p>
