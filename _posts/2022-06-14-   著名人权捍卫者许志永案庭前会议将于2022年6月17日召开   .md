<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">14</span>日，本网获悉：著名人权捍卫者许志永案庭前会议将于<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">17</span>日召开。同案的丁家喜律师案庭前会议将于<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">20</span>日召开。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">许志永博士是国内知名的青年法学家，人权学者，曾参与北大三博士上书全国人大废除收容遣送制度，曾为揭露黑砖窖事件奔走，曾为三聚腈胺受害者救助努力，<span lang="EN-US">2014</span>年曾为推动外地人子女在亦参加高考和推动官员财产公开而入狱，此次在临沂受审罪名是颠覆国家政权。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">许志永：</span></b><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;"> 1973</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">2</span>日出生，河南省商丘市民权县人，前北京邮电学院专业讲师，前北京市海淀区人民法院人民陪审员，“公盟”重要创始人，青年法学家、宪政学者和公民维权代理人，就全国人大对《城市流浪乞讨人员收容遣送办法》进行违宪审查提议者，新公民运动倡导者，中国在押政治犯。<span lang="EN-US"> <o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2013</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">16</span>日，其被北京市警方以涉嫌“聚扰乱公共场所秩序罪”刑拘，关押在北京市第三看守所；同年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">22</span>日，被正式逮捕；<span lang="EN-US">2014</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">26</span>日，被北京市第一中级法院以“聚众扰乱公共场所秩序罪”判处有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">4</span>年；<span lang="EN-US">2017</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">15</span>日，刑满获释。 <span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2020</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">15</span>日，许志永被广州市警方从番禺海鸥岛杨斌律师处带走；据悉，其此次被抓，此与<span lang="EN-US">2019</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月曾因参加“厦门聚会案”遭通缉有关，许志永自<span lang="EN-US">2019</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">26</span>日丁家喜律师被捕后就一直在中国南方秘密流亡。其亲友均遭多次骚扰跟踪，其女友李翘楚亦被强迫失踪，后取保获释。<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">19</span>日，被以涉嫌煽动颠覆国家政权罪正式逮捕，羁押于山东省临沭县看守所。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体;
