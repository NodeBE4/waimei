[腾讯将以17亿新元收购英国游戏公司Sumo](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210719-1171030)
------

<div>
<p>腾讯控股同意收购英国视频游戏公司Sumo Group的剩余股权，这家中国社交媒体和游戏巨头一连串的游戏领域投资由此又添一笔。</p><p>新浪财经报道，市值排名亚洲第一的腾讯出价以每股513便士购买Sumo的股权，价格相比这家英国公司的前收盘价高出了43%。基于这一出价，按完全稀释后的股权计算，Sumo的估值约为9.19亿英镑（约17.15亿新元）。腾讯先前于2019年收购了该公司9.96%的股权。</p><p>腾讯是全球最大的游戏公司，手中持有诸如Riot Games和Epic Games等业内巨头的股权。腾讯之前已表示，公司计划今年把更大比例的增量利润投入云服务、游戏和视频内容业务。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>Sumo为其他游戏发行商开发游戏，根据该公司网站信息，其客户和合作伙伴阵营中包括Xbox Game Studios、电子艺界、Ubisoft Entertainment等。</p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
