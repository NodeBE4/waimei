mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">次诚嘉措自幼即进入甘肃省甘南州的拉卜楞寺为僧；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2008</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">15</span>日，因对中共当局高压严管制度不满，甘肃省甘南州发生了大规模僧俗藏人抗议游行，同年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">14</span>日—<span lang="EN-US">15</span>日，其亦在拉卜楞寺示威，呼喊“祈请达赖喇嘛尊者返回西藏”等口号，遂被中共甘肃当局视为带领抗议者而欲加抓捕；为躲避被抓，其被迫逃离寺庙，但仍于一周之后的<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">22</span>日，被甘南藏族自治州警方在舟曲县抓捕、刑拘，送甘肃省安全厅看守所关押；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2009</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">21</span>日，被甘南州中级法院一审以“分裂国家罪”秘密判处无期徒刑；其不服上诉，后经甘南州中级法院二审裁决，依然维持原判；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">后当服刑至<span lang="EN-US">2014</span>年时，其因狱中“能认罪悔改，表现较好”而获甘肃省天水市中级法院减刑，使刑期更改为<span lang="EN-US">19</span>年，剥夺政治权利<span lang="EN-US">7</span>年，刑期至<span lang="EN-US">2033</span>年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">21</span>日；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月，又经天水市中级法院批准，被第二次减刑<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月；<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月获悉，其又可能将于<span lang="EN-US">2026</span>年释放。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;
