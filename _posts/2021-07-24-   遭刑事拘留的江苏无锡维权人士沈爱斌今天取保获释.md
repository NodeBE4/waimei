[遭刑事拘留的江苏无锡维权人士沈爱斌今天取保获释](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2021/07/blog-post_30.html)

<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">24</span>日，本网获悉：于<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">24</span>日 被无锡市公安局梁溪分局以涉嫌寻衅滋事罪刑事拘留的江苏无锡维权人士沈爱斌，今取保获释，已经回到家中。此前被关押在无锡市看守所。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">沈爱斌：男，<span lang="EN-US">1973</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">15</span>日出生，江苏省无锡市人，退伍军人，原无锡市锡山区城管局城管大队长，维权公民，公民记者。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2013</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">23</span>日，因与丁红芬、沈果冬等<span lang="EN-US">20</span>余人一起营救被非法拘禁于当地东郊宾馆内“黑监狱”的访民，及时救出<span lang="EN-US">5</span>人（其中包括一位<span lang="EN-US">82</span>岁的老妇人），遂被无锡市滨湖区警方于<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">26</span>日从家中带走；<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">3</span>日，被当地警方以“涉嫌聚众扰乱社会秩序罪”刑事拘留，并遭到刑讯逼供；<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">6</span>日，被警方正式逮捕；后因无锡市滨湖区检察院认定证据不足，两次退侦，于<span lang="EN-US">2014</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">18</span>日被取保候审；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2014</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">19</span>日再次因“<span lang="EN-US">2013.6.23</span>营救黑监狱受害人事件”被刑拘，关押在无锡市第一看守所，后被以涉嫌“故意毁坏财物罪”正式批捕；<span lang="EN-US">2014</span>年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">27</span>日，被江苏省无锡市滨湖区法院以同罪名判处有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">1</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月；<span lang="EN-US">2015</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">12</span>日刑满出狱后，依然坚持维权，同时累遭当局非法监视、跟踪、拘留及传唤等迫害；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2016</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">13</span>日，因无锡市再现<span lang="EN-US">4</span>·<span lang="EN-US">13</span>大抓捕访民事件再次被以涉嫌“故意伤害罪”刑拘<span lang="EN-US">37</span>天，释放后旋即被监视居住；同年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">23</span>日因恐其在<span lang="EN-US">G20</span>峰会期间持续维权而被无锡市警方行政拘留<span lang="EN-US">10</span>天，同年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">2</span>日被警方以涉“寻衅滋事罪”继续刑拘，后被转正式逮捕；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2017</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">27</span>日，被江苏省无锡市滨湖区法院一审以“寻衅滋事罪”判处有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">2</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月；<span lang="EN-US">2019</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">23</span>日，已刑满释放。此前被羁押于江苏省无锡市第二看守所。获释后仍遭当局持续的迫害打压。于<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">24</span>日又遭刑事拘留。一个月后，于<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">24</span>日取保获释。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"></p><div class="separator" style="clear: both;
