<span></span><p class="MsoNormal"></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span></span></p><a name='more'></a><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;"><br /></span><p></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">21</span>日，本网获悉获刑<span lang="EN-US">3</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月的重庆市巴南区<span lang="EN-US">75</span>岁的法轮功学员段在英女士的案情及简历，现在通报如下：<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">段在英：</span></b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">重庆市巴南区法轮功学员，女，<span lang="EN-US">1949</span>年生，<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">75</span>岁。段在英老人家住重庆市巴南区花溪街道花溪村。段在英于<span lang="EN-US">1998</span>年开始修炼法轮功，自中共一九九九年七月疯狂迫害法轮功后，段在英因坚持法轮功信仰，多次遭到绑架、关押，她曾三次被劳教，三次遭判刑，累计坐牢十二年。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">段在英于<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">20 </span>日在巴南界石镇给路人宣讲法轮功时被警察抓捕。虽然当晚得以回家，但八天后的<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">28</span>日，她又被巴南区国保警察抓捕，被关押到界石镇看守所。之后她遭刑事拘留、批捕、起诉。<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">5</span>日，九龙坡区法院庭审段在英。<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">14</span>日被重庆九龙坡区法院判刑<span lang="EN-US">3</span>年半。段在英已上诉。刑期至<span lang="EN-US">2027</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">28</span>日。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><br /><p></p>
