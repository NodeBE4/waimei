[广西维权律师陈家鸿律师煽颠案今宣判  陈家鸿获刑三年](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2021/12/blog-post_81.html)

<div>
<p><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;"><span></span></span></p><a name='more'></a>&nbsp;<p></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">14</span>日，本网获悉：广西维权律师陈家鸿煽动颠覆国家政权案于<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">14</span>日上午开庭宣判。玉林市中院宣判陈家鸿煽动颠覆国家政权罪成，判处有期徒刑三年。陈家鸿的辩护律师没有到场，陈家鸿当庭控诉遭受看守所警察殴打，陈家鸿前妻旁听了宣判，但在陈家鸿控诉遭受殴打时被赶出法庭，陈家鸿没有当庭决定是否上诉。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">陈家鸿：</span></b><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">1966</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月出生，广西壮族自治区北流县隆盛镇平坡村人，又名陈文胆，字数真，早年为广西北流县鸭壤小学教师，知名业余书法家、字画家，原南宁市百举鸣律师事务所执业律师，敏感案件代理人，中国人权律师团成员，人权律师，中国在押政治犯。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">自从成为“百举鸣律师事务所” （现该律师事务所已被当局取缔）中一名执业律师以来，因长期参与维权敏感案件，追求民主人权并为之积极发声，随屡遭中共当局严打逼迫；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2017</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月，曾因积极介入了震惊网络的“广西村民罗继标在看守所离奇死亡事件”，而遭广西警方的持枪围困；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2017</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月，曾因同一事件，其介入维权行为被广西律师协会视为违规，从而被该律师协会给予“公开谴责行业处分的通报”；<span lang="EN-US">2018</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月，又被南宁市司法局行政处罚停业半年；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2019</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">29</span>日，其被北流县警方以涉嫌“煽动颠覆国家政权罪”抓捕、刑拘，其家被查抄；之后，其又被北流县检察院以同罪由转正式批捕，并被指控因其反对官员腐败、呼吁建立民主宪政等带有政治倾向的公开言论、书法作品及行为艺术等涉及挑战现政权，尤其是其中一副书法作品，内含“成立暗杀突击队！清算邪恶官僚，加速民主进程”等激进敏感字眼，触犯了政府的相关法律法规，其行为属于违法；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2019</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月，其案件被地方检察院移交至玉林巿中级法院起诉；一直未有宣判。截至目前，其一直坚称无罪，且因患有高血压及脑萎缩等病症，健康状况极差。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">目前被羁押于广西玉林市看守所。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2021</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">14</span>日，玉林市中院宣判陈家鸿煽动颠覆国家政权罪成，判处有期徒刑三年<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">对陈家鸿律师的境况，本网将持续关注。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"></p><div class="separator" style="clear: both;
