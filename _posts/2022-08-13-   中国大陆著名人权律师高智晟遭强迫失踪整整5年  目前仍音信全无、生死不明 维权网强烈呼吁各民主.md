<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">13</span>日，本网获悉：中国大陆著名人权律师高智晟遭中共当局强迫失踪整整<span lang="EN-US">5</span>年，时至今日仍杳无音信生死不知，本网强烈呼吁各民主国家政府、国际社会为高智晟先生的自由而行动。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">高智晟律师是中国大陆著名人权律师，以其刚正不阿、铮铮铁骨而著名，历经残暴酷刑而不屈服，此次高智晟律师遭强迫失踪五年，当局至今没有任何法律手续和说明，其处境与生命安危令海内外各界高度担忧。 <span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">高智晟：</span></b><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">1964</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">20</span>日出生，陕西省榆林市佳县人，著名维权律师，曾被中国司法部选为“中国十佳律师”，被誉为“中国良心”，异见人士，中国在押政治犯。自<span lang="EN-US">1996</span>年起开始替弱势群体维权打官司，曾处理过多宗民间维权案件； <span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">自<span lang="EN-US">2004</span>年底起，开始多次上书中共当局高层，要求改变对法轮功等群体的非法处理手段，并参与对法轮功学员器官遭当局活摘指控的调查；<span lang="EN-US"> <o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2005</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月，与妻子公开宣布退出中国共产党；因其勇于为当局认定的敏感性案件（注：含法轮功信仰者、地下基督教徒以及与官员发生纠纷的底层农民和私营企业家）辩护，故而曾多次受到中共当局以黑社会手段的严重骚扰、打击报复； <span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2006</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月，其执业执照被当局吊销，且被秘密绑架，遭近<span lang="EN-US">4</span>个月的酷刑； <span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2006</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">22</span>日，被北京市第一中级法院以“煽动颠覆国家政权罪”判处有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">3</span>年，缓刑<span lang="EN-US">5</span>年； <span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2009</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">4</span>日，其被突然失联，<span lang="EN-US">2010</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月才被释放返京，之后又因接受了美联社采访，遂被再度失联，直至<span lang="EN-US">2011</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">16</span>日，家属才从新华社报导中的获悉，其已被北京法院收监逮捕，接续服刑<span lang="EN-US">2</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>个月； <span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2014</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">7</span>日，刑满出狱，但出狱后仍被软禁在陕北榆林老家。因其曾在狱中长期遭酷刑折磨和被单独囚禁遭非人虐待，导致牙齿脱落，健康欠佳。其要求治牙看病，但一直遭拒。 <span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2016</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月，因其秘密撰写书籍《<span lang="EN-US">2017</span>年，起来中国》在台湾正式印刷出售，且因该书主要涉及中共当局对其长期绑架、囚禁和施以酷刑等，及其从信仰角度看中共<span lang="EN-US">2017</span>年将会灭亡和对<span lang="EN-US">2017</span>年后的中国未来宪政民主化展望等三大内容，遂令中共当局十分恐惧恼怒，对其监控愈加严密。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2017</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">13</span>日，高智晟在山西维权人士李发旺、邵重国等人帮助下逃离陕西老家到山西躲藏准备看牙医，但<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">19</span>日被警方搜捕到带走。此外，高智晟一直没有消息，北京、陕西和山西当局都否认羁押高智晟。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">自高智晟律师<span lang="EN-US">2017</span>年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月遭中共当局强迫失踪至今已经整整<span lang="EN-US">5</span>年，中共当局对高智晟不审不判，对其亲属的追问不做任何回答和解释，对国际社会的关注和抗议完全置之不理。中共当局对高智晟律师的人权的侵害已经到了随心所欲为所欲为的地步。目前高智晟被秘密关押的地点不详，生死不明。 <span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;
