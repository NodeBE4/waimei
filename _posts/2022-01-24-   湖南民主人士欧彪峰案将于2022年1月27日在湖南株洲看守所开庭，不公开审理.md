[湖南民主人士欧彪峰案将于2022年1月27日在湖南株洲看守所开庭，不公开审理](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2022/01/2022127.html)
------

<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">24</span>日，本网获悉：湖南民主人士欧彪峰案将于<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">27</span>日在湖南株洲看守所开庭，不公开审理，家属也不能参加。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">欧彪峰于<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">4</span>日，被以“寻衅滋事”罪由处行政拘留<span lang="EN-US">15</span>天，其家被查抄；后遭刑事拘留、指定居所监视居住，<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">22</span>日经株洲市检察院批准遭湖南株洲市公安局正式逮捕。后遭起诉。现羁押于株洲市第一看守所。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">欧彪峰简介：</span></b><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">1980</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">30</span>日出生，湖南省株洲市人，人称“小彪”，湖南省宁乡市黑金时代小区居民，维权公民，公民记者，“公民运动”践行者，中国在押政治犯。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">近年来，曾因积极参与公民维权活动，经常发布被中共当局打压的公民行动者的第一手信息，随遭湖南省当局的维稳监控；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2020</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月及<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月，曾因为公开声援许志永和常玮平等良心犯而被当地警方传唤；同年，曾因声援香港反送中运动，并在香港著名媒体大亨、社会活动家黎智英被抓捕后，公开表态支持黎智英及香港《苹果日报》，而遭湖南警方多次警告，并被威胁、暗示当局正在搜寻对其实施抓捕的证据；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2020</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">3</span>日，因关注并协助曾于<span lang="EN-US">2018 </span>年在上海街头向习近平肖像泼墨而被“维稳”（即被当局非法关进精神病医院）的“泼墨女孩”董瑶琼，在推特上发求救呼吁，随立遭当局的严厉打压，被湖南省株洲市芦淞区警方从在宁乡市的家中带走传唤；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2020</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">4</span>日，被以“寻衅滋事”罪由处行政拘留<span lang="EN-US">15</span>天，其家被查抄；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">同年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">18</span>日，又被株洲市芦淞区警方以涉嫌“颠覆国家政权罪”转为指定居所监视居住；期满后转株洲市第一看守所关押。于<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">22</span>日被正式逮捕。将于<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">27</span>日湖南株洲看守所开庭，不公开审理。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体;
