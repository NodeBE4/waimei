[湖北枣阳市失地农民、维权人士白建强遭当地警方刑事拘留](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2023/07/blog-post_19.html)

<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">19</span>日，本网获悉：湖北枣阳市失地农民、维权人士<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">15</span>日遭当地警方以涉嫌寻衅滋事罪刑事拘留。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">白建强系枣阳市北城办事处北关村二组农民，其所在的北关村二组的土地，在<span lang="EN-US">2012</span>年来被当地政府以城市改造为名，强征强拆搞房地产开发。白建强由于得不到赔偿，多次到政府各级部门上访。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">白建强因坚持举报遭枣阳维稳当局绑架、拘留、关精神病院迫害。<span lang="EN-US">2014</span>年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月，白建强在北京旅游之际被枣阳维稳人员抓回拘禁<span lang="EN-US">10</span>天，后又被绑架到襄州精神病医院关押迫害<span lang="EN-US">56</span>天。<span lang="EN-US">2016</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">1</span>日，白建强准备搭乘火车外出时再次被维稳人员绑架到襄州精神病院关押迫害<span lang="EN-US">70</span>天。<span lang="EN-US">2017</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">22</span>日，白建强在枣阳市武装部附近行走时又一次被维稳人员绑架到襄州精神病院迫害约一个月时间。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">白建强土地遭抢夺至今没有合理合法的解决，白建强仍然持续不断地维权，今获悉其于<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">15</span>日遭湖北省枣阳市警方刑事拘留。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">对白建强的境况，本网将持续关注。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"></p><div class="separator" style="clear: both;
