<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">6</span>日，本网获悉：湖南维权公民谭兵林于<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">29</span>日遭当局抓捕。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">谭兵林于<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">29</span>日下午被有司带走<span lang="EN-US">,</span>一并抄走了手机和笔记本电脑。谭兵林失去自由当晚<span lang="EN-US">,</span>他的父亲被告知谭被带走并在有司送来的一份文件上签了字<span lang="EN-US">,</span>文件的具体内容不详。<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">30</span>日晚<span lang="EN-US">,</span>谭兵林的父母被带到派岀所分别做笔录到很晚<span lang="EN-US">,</span>内容涉及谭的父母被接种新冠疫苗后<span lang="EN-US">,</span>谭兵林到村委会举牌的具体细节。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">7</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">月<span lang="EN-US">1</span>日晚<span lang="EN-US">,</span>谭兵林母亲电询县国保大队<span lang="EN-US">,</span>得知谭并不在国保大队<span lang="EN-US">,</span>是涉及另一个部门管辖的另一件案子<span lang="EN-US">,</span>尚在处理中。若有新的消息<span lang="EN-US">,</span>再行通报。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">谭兵林简介</span></b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">：湖南省祁东县步云桥镇人，曾参与推动教育平权运动；曾倡导俗称“三免”的免费医疗、免费教育、免费养老的民生诉求；多年来一直以义工身份参与为良心犯送饭的公益活动等。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">4</span>日早上，谭兵林在老家湖南省祁东县步云桥镇举牌要求民生“三免”，举牌位置分别位于市集中心地段、祁东县政府门口、县人大常委会门口等地，举牌内容包括免费医疗、免费教育、免费养老及其它诉求。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2021</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;
