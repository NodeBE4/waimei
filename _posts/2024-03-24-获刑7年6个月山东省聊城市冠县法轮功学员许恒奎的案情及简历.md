<p><span></span></p><a name='more'></a><p class="MsoNormal"><br /></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">24</span>日，本网获悉获刑<span lang="EN-US">7</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月山东省聊城市冠县法轮功学员许恒奎的案情及简历，现在通报如下：<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">许恒奎：</span></b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">山东省聊城市冠县法轮功学员，男，一九六三年三月五日出生，现年<span lang="EN-US">61</span>岁，农民，家住冠县甘官屯村。因修炼法轮功，许恒奎曾于<span lang="EN-US">2002</span>年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月被劳教两年。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">许恒奎<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">13</span>日被官屯派出所警察抓捕、抄家；六月初被保外就医，后被构陷到检察院。<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">4</span>日其被山东省聊城市冠县法院判刑<span lang="EN-US">7</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月，罚款一万八千元。其不服上诉，聊城市中级法院二审维持原判。刑期至：<span lang="EN-US">2029</span>年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">12</span>日。许恒奎目前关押在山东省监狱服刑。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><br /><p></p>
