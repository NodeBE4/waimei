<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">27</span>日，本网获悉获刑<span lang="EN-US">3</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月的四川成都法轮功学员骆常勇的案情及简历，现在通报如下：<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">骆常勇：</span></b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">四川成都法轮功学员，男，<span lang="EN-US">1965</span>年生，一九八八年毕业于四川农业大学，他原在成都市新都区农村发展局农技中心工作，从一九九六年起，他开始修炼法轮功。<span lang="EN-US">2009</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">20</span>日，骆常勇被抓捕后，被龙泉驿区法院判刑<span lang="EN-US">5</span>年。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">2022</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">15</span>日上午，骆常勇在新都区斑竹园街道达利园食品厂附近被便衣抓捕。第二天被取保候审。<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>、<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月份，新都检察院通知骆常勇去签字，据说是关于延长“取保候审”的时间，被骆常勇拒绝。由于不堪派出所和检察院的多次骚扰，骆常勇于<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">15</span>日离家出走。<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">21</span>日，骆常勇在成都市成华区天回镇被抓捕。随即遭逮捕，关押在新都区看守所。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">2024</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月初，骆常勇被新都区法院开庭，<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">20</span>日，他被判刑<span lang="EN-US">3</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月，并被罚金一万。刑期至：<span lang="EN-US">2027</span>年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">20</span>日。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p>
