[因印刷基督教读物而获刑7年的陕西基督徒常玉春的案情通报](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2021/11/7.html)
------

<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">19</span>日，本网获悉因印刷基督教读物而获刑<span lang="EN-US">7</span>年的陕西基督徒常玉春的案情，现通报如下：<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">常玉春：</span></b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">男、<span lang="EN-US">1968</span>年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">21</span>日出生，曾用名常玉光，陕西省西安市人，西安市高陵区泾渭上城小区<span lang="EN-US">41</span>号楼<span lang="EN-US">1</span>单元<span lang="EN-US">3</span>层<span lang="EN-US">10</span>号公民，基督徒，陕西光义爱圣印务公司法人代表，中国在押良心犯。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">自<span lang="EN-US">2015</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月起，因其所属印务公司大量印刷了与基督教信仰有关的数据及图书，随引起地方当局的高度关注与警惕；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2020</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">21</span>日，西安市文化执法部门联合西安市国家安全部门对其公司进行了查封与鉴定，现场扣押各种宗教类书籍<span lang="EN-US">21</span>万余册，后又经陕西省新闻出版和电影质量检测中心对其中《改革宗信仰》等<span lang="EN-US">24</span>种图书鉴定，认定该<span lang="EN-US">24</span>种图书为“未经批准擅自印刷的出版物”的非法出版物，随于次日（<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">22</span>日），立刻与其妻李琛慧被西安市高陵区警方一并抓走，后被以涉嫌“颠覆国家政权罪”指定居所监视居住；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">同年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">12</span>日，又被更换罪名以涉嫌“非法经营罪”刑拘；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">同年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">22</span>日，被西安市高陵区检察院以同罪名予以批捕，指控其在无印刷许可证的情况下经营印刷业务，印刷非法出版物，扰乱市场秩序，起行为均构成非法经营罪，故而应予以逮捕；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2021</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">19</span>日，被西安市高陵区法院一审以“非法经营罪”重判有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">7</span>年，并处罚金<span lang="EN-US">25</span>万元；其不服上诉；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2021</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">15</span>日，经陕西省西安市中级法院二审裁决，最终仍然驳回上诉，维持原判，刑期至<span lang="EN-US">2027</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">1</span>日。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">目前仍被羁押于西安市高陵区看守所，未来将在何监狱服刑，暂不明晰。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"></p><div class="separator" style="clear: both;
