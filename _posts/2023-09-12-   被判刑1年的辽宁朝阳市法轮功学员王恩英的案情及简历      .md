<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">11</span>日，本网获悉被判刑<span lang="EN-US">3</span>年的辽宁朝阳市法轮功学员张秀英的案情及简历，现在通报如下：<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">王恩英：女，辽宁朝阳市法轮功学员。<span lang="EN-US">2003</span>年王恩英曾因为发法轮功资料被抓捕。在被关押期间，她绝食反迫害，遭到警察野蛮灌食折磨。王恩英后被中共当地法院判刑三年，被关押到辽宁女子监狱迫害。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2023</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">7</span>日上午，朝阳市法轮功学员王恩英、张玉凤、张秀英在朝阳市凌北乡集市上发法轮功资料，被便衣警察抓捕到高铁派出所。之后，锦州铁路公安处、朝阳市北塔公安分局警察联合对三名法轮功学员抄家。她们随后被关押在锦州市女子看守所。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">近日，王恩英、张玉凤、张秀英、三名朝阳市法轮功学员被判刑。张玉凤和张秀英分别被锦州凌海法院判刑<span lang="EN-US">3</span>年，王恩英<span lang="EN-US">1</span>年。王恩英的刑期至：<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">6</span>日。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p>
