<div>
<p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">8</span>日，本网获悉：因反对当局填海被以“寻衅滋事”判刑<span lang="EN-US">5</span>年的广东湛江人权律师陈武权今天刑满出狱。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">陈武权：</span></b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">广东省湛江市东海岛调逻村人，早期曾为警察，后辞去警察工作考取执业律师执照，知名维权律师。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">1998</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年，曾毕业于警官院校后到湛江市政法部门当警察；</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">2004</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">年，曾因四二五事件受中共党内警告处分；</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">2005</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">25</span>日，辞去警察工作；<span lang="EN-US">2006</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月，通过司法考试成为执业律师；</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2012</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月，曾因代理盲人维权律师陈光诚的侄子陈克贵案而被迫解除合同、退出代理，其执业律师执照被吊销；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2017</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年，曾因协助家乡村民反对政府填海、保护海洋环境生态而遭到警方的严厉镇压，随后即参与村民要求游行示威表达要求；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2018</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">9</span>日，因其持续维权，而与另外<span lang="EN-US">6</span>名村民维权者被湛江市经济技术开发区警方诱骗至信部门，以其阻挠开工之名抓走，之后一行<span lang="EN-US">7</span>人被悉数以涉嫌“寻衅滋事罪”刑拘，并被羁押于广东省湛江市麻江区看守所；</span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">同年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">25</span>日，其“寻衅滋事”案在湛江市经济技术开发区法院开庭受审，最终其被该法院一审以“寻衅滋事罪”判处有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">5</span>年，同时被拘捕的维权村民陈伟梁、陈振明、陈还森、陈春霖、陈帅、陈隆群等<span lang="EN-US">6</span>人亦被法院以同罪名分别判处<span lang="EN-US">1</span>年<span lang="EN-US">-1</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月不等刑期；<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">8</span>日，刑满释放。此前在广东省四会监狱服刑。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"></p><div class="separator" style="clear: both;
