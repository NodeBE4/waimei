<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">2</span>日，本网获悉获刑<span lang="EN-US">2</span>年的辽宁省铁岭市昌图县法轮功学员孙万良的案情及简历，现在通报如下：<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">孙万良：</span></b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">辽宁省铁岭市昌图县法轮功学员。因宣传法轮功，<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">20</span>日，昌图县国保大队闯入孙万良家中，把孙万良抓捕。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">孙万良在被抓捕后，他八十三岁的老父亲因惊吓、担忧儿子，于<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">16</span>日去世。<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">16</span>日，律师会见孙万良时得知他在看守所被犯人踢断一根肋骨，在重伤期间还被单独关进一个间室坐板。家属非常气愤，控告主要责任人，要求依法追究昌图县看守所所长涉嫌虐待罪。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">孙万良于<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">25</span>日在昌图县法院被开庭，律师为他做了无罪辩护，有理有据。最终，孙万良被判刑<span lang="EN-US">2</span>年。刑期至：<span lang="EN-US">2025</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">19</span>日。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p>
