[与宇航员对话　林郑盼港青以国为荣](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210903-1189951)
------

<div>
<p>“时代精神耀香江”之仰望星空话天宫活动今天（3日）举行，中国神舟十二号载人飞船宇航员聂海胜、刘伯明、汤洪波，于天宫空间站与香港连线，与出席活动的香港青年交流。</p><p>据香港01报道，香港特首林郑月娥致辞时，感谢中国载人航天工程办公室配合促成今次“天地对话”，她称航天科学家热爱祖国、力求科学创新，是香港学生追求至善至美的榜样。</p><p>林郑说，虽然不可能每个学生都成为科学家，但可学习宇航员的爱国精神，以国为傲、以国为荣，为中国人服务。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>主办活动的中联办副主任谭铁牛称，中央继6月安排航天科学家访港，短时间内再举办与宇航员对话是一份“跨越天地的礼物”，希望港人可以培养家国情怀，以报国实现人生理想。</p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/zbpe-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=zbpe399&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_desktop-zbpe399_2.png?dHEiGouxxZpWwWTJE04dyGyUN74JOU2z" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_desktop-zbpe399_2.png?dHEiGouxxZpWwWTJE04dyGyUN74JOU2z" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/zbpe-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=zbpe399&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_mobile-zbpe399_2.png?qZnBcIGCJvPR8A2IAZOZuOxdoGZDatiI" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_mobile-zbpe399_2.png?qZnBcIGCJvPR8A2IAZOZuOxdoGZDatiI" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
