[法轮功APEC前要求中共立即释放10名在押学员](https://www.epochtimes.com/gb/23/11/15/n14117243.htm)
------

<div>

href="https://twitter.com/FalunInfoCtr" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">公告</a>，要求中共政府立即释放10名亲属在美国的<a href="https://www.epochtimes.com/gb/tag/%E6%B3%95%E8%BD%AE%E5%8A%9F.html">法轮功</a>学员，以及所有非法被关押的中国大陆法轮功学员。</p><figure id="attachment_14117250" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14117250" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14117250-F-7Om_gXsAAYd_D.jpeg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-14117250"
