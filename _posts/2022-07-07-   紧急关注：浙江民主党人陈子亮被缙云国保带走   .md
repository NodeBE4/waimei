<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）本网获悉：<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">6</span>日上午，在老家缙云养病的浙江民主党人陈子亮被缙云国保带走。正欲去医院看病陈子亮，在家中被缙云国保刑事传唤。身患脑梗病情加重的陈子亮，近日因民主党人徐光案，被多次传唤和抄家。脑梗病发的陈子亮，已行走不便，是被缙云国保强行抬上车带走。国保称再次传讯陈子亮，是为配合相关部门调查杭州徐光案。徐光<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">26</span>日被刑拘，而<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">24</span>日，陈子亮恰恰去杭州看望了徐光。故此，患病的陈子亮，被当局视为徐光案的重要同伙。此前，当局多次传唤陈子亮，均涉及徐光。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"></p><div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;"><a href="https://blogger.googleusercontent.com/img/b/R29vZ2xl/AVvXsEh-Qnn22kNVVDVT7V0AC1aojjOTo0pvMzxyHZyq7uCYep2xPH09b6M_REejX4uEmHRHs9k43sv2NNmSDeV_VYdkD2pa0iga56BJs_HhfnJMXPAzm2_KubuGs8YPUjuO1R2kLS1YcTgs2ljluvMffG7pOtJu9tci1yKHNNItPfuuD9s-H-KopnUMDdl2Mg/s1280/photo_2022-07-07_23-43-23.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;"><img border="0" data-original-height="1280" data-original-width="960" height="320" src="https://blogger.googleusercontent.com/img/b/R29vZ2xl/AVvXsEh-Qnn22kNVVDVT7V0AC1aojjOTo0pvMzxyHZyq7uCYep2xPH09b6M_REejX4uEmHRHs9k43sv2NNmSDeV_VYdkD2pa0iga56BJs_HhfnJMXPAzm2_KubuGs8YPUjuO1R2kLS1YcTgs2ljluvMffG7pOtJu9tci1yKHNNItPfuuD9s-H-KopnUMDdl2Mg/s320/photo_2022-07-07_23-43-23.jpg" width="240" /></a></div><p></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">这是<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">6</span>日上午，患病的民主党人陈子亮，被浙江丽水缙云县国保抬上车，带走的照片。陈子亮家人联系电话<span lang="EN-US">15824186158<o:p></o:p></span></span></p>
