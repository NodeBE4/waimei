[宋会春因举报长期违法闲置土地请求复查，许昌市自然资源和规划局要求其当面举证](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2021/10/blog-post_11.html)

<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2021</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">9</span>日，宋会春与其父亲通过邮寄向许昌市自然资源和规划局提交了《复查申请书》，请求依法确认被申请人许昌市自然资源和规划局东城区分局作出的《信访事项处理意见书》违法，责令其重新作出行政行为。其主要理由是：<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">宋会春与父亲宋西章向国家自然资源督察济南局举报许昌市东城区管理委员会自<span lang="EN-US">2010</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月以来打着拆迁旗号在许昌市魏都区半截河办事处宋庄<span lang="EN-US">82</span>号（现东城区天宝路街道办事处大坑李社区）所在地区打着拆迁旗号大规模圈地，闲置土地达<span lang="EN-US">11</span>年之久，明显属于违法。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">许昌市东城区管理委员会系许昌市人民政府派出机构，其法律责任应有许昌市人民政府承担。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">宋西章父女请求国家自然资源督察济南局依法查处河南省许昌市人民政府闲置土地达<span lang="EN-US">11</span>年之久的违法行为。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2021</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">18</span>日，许昌市自然资源和规划局东城区分局作出了《信访事项处理意见书》，其处理意见为：“该项目地块因拆迁清表不到位，造成企业一直无法进场施工，土地处于闲置状态。下一步督促东城区管委会加大拆迁力度，尽快交付净地，督促许昌市腾飞房地产开发有限公司及时进场开工建设”。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">宋西章父女认为，该《信访事项处理意见书》有以下违法行为：<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">1</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">、没有告知案件的来源。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">、我们要求查处闲置土地达<span lang="EN-US">11</span>年之久的违法行为。但对闲置土地达<span lang="EN-US">11</span>年之久的行为是否属于违法，该局避而不谈，却决定“下一步督促东城区管委会加大拆迁力度，尽快交付净地，督促许昌市腾飞房地产开发有限公司及时进场开工建设”，显然犯了文不对题的错误。本案的关键在于是否应当对闲置土地达<span lang="EN-US">11</span>年之久的行为进行查处，而不是督促建设。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">3</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">、宋西章父女要求查处许昌市人民政府闲置土地的违法行为，但许昌市自然资源和规划局东城区分局搞错对象，未针对许昌市人民政府闲置土地的违法行为，而是针对许昌市腾飞房地产开发有限公司的施工，将督促其开工建设，犯了张冠李戴的错误。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">4</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">、许昌市自然资源和规划局东城区分局系许昌市政府的下属，故对许昌市政府的违法行为难以作出处罚，应当移交有管辖权的行政机关处理。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">值得一提的是，宋西章举报土地违法行为并要求依法查处，系自然资源和规划部门的职责。故自然资源和规划部门应当依法查处，而不是作为信访处理。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">宋西章父女请求许昌市自然资源和规划局纠正。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">许昌市自然资源和规划局电话：<span lang="EN-US">0374 2988508<o:p></o:p></span></span></p>
