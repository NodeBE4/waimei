<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">13</span>日，本网获悉：著名人权律师丁家喜案将于<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">20</span>日召开庭前会议。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">丁家喜的代理律师今天通报说：“一审主办法官今天下午电话通知我：丁家喜案于下周一即<span lang="EN-US">20</span>日上午在临沂市中级法院召开庭前会议。书面通知将邮寄给我。”<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">1990</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">至<span lang="EN-US">1996</span>年在沈阳航空工业总公司、北京航空工业总公司任工程师。<span lang="EN-US">1996</span>年起转为专职律师，任职于北京市多家律师事务所。<span lang="EN-US">2003</span>年参与设立北京市德鸿律师事务所任高级合伙人。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">自<span lang="EN-US">2010</span>年起积极参与推动“随迁子女就地高考”教育平权活动。<span lang="EN-US">2012</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">9</span>日与许志永等人发表致习近平等中共高层领导人公开信，要求包括习近平在内的<span lang="EN-US">205</span>名中国部级以上官员率先财产公示。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2013</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">17</span>日丁家喜被当局以“非法集会”罪名刑事拘留，关押在北京市第三看守所。同年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">21</span>日，公安机关将丁家喜罪名更换为“寻衅滋事”提交检察院。同年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">4</span>日北京市检察院一分院将案件退回公安局补充侦查，控罪更换为“聚众扰乱社会秩序罪”。<span lang="EN-US">2014</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">18</span>日，北京市海淀区法院判处丁家喜有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">3</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月。<span lang="EN-US">2016</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">16</span>日丁家喜刑满出狱。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体;
