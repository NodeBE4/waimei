[获刑4年6个月的知名藏人学者和异见作家图丹罗珠的案情及简历](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2022/06/46.html)

<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">24</span>日，本网获悉获刑<span lang="EN-US">4</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月的知名藏人学者和异见作家图丹罗珠的案情及简历，现通报如下：<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">图丹罗珠</span></b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">：藏族，<span lang="EN-US">1988</span>年出生，四川省甘孜藏族自治州（简称甘孜州）石渠县蒙宜乡蒙宜村人，原石渠县一中学教师，藏语言及文化倡导者，知名藏人学者和异见作家，中国在押政治犯。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">早年曾就读于中国一所大学，毕业后返回老家石渠县，并在当地的一所中学任教多年；曾因多次针对东西方文学、西藏文学和经济等领域发表藏文及汉文文章，深受读者好评，遂逐渐成为一名受境内学术界认可的藏人作家；然而因其发表反对（或不赞同）中共政府对藏人的同化政策文章，尽管其内容并未违宪，但却触碰到中共当局的敏感神经，以致于甘孜州当局指派石渠县警方多次对其进行警告，并肆意搜查其个人计算机等物品；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2021</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月，其被甘孜州石渠县警方莫名强行带走，自此杳无音讯；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2022</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">22</span>日，其亲人才被当局告知，其已于同年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">14</span>日被四川省甘孜藏族自治州石渠县法院以“分裂国家罪”判处有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">4</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月，刑期至<span lang="EN-US">2026</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月。目前疑似被羁押于甘孜藏族自治州石渠县看守所。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"></p><div class="separator" style="clear: both;
