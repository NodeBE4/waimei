[港大法律学院院长吁给四名被捕学生机会](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210819-1184022)
------

<div>
<p>香港大学学生会四名成员昨天因涉嫌违反《香港国安法》、宣扬恐怖主义被捕。港大法律学院院长傅华伶向学院发讯，称会向涉事学生提供支持，希望社会给予机会。</p><p>据星岛日报报道，傅华伶昨向学院发讯，称其中一名被捕学生是修读政治与法学双学位的三年级生，他会为牵涉在内的学生提供必要和适当的关怀和支持。</p><p>傅华伶说，翻查资料，被捕四人过往未见从事违法或暴力激进行为，当中时任港大学生会会长郭永皓更强调，学生会从不举办违法活动。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>傅华伶说，要让他们从错误中反思及学习，希望香港社会可以给予他们一个机会。</p><p> </p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
