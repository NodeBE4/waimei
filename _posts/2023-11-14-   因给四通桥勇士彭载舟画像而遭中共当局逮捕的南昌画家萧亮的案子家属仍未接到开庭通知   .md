[因给四通桥勇士彭载舟画像而遭中共当局逮捕的南昌画家萧亮的案子家属仍未接到开庭通知](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2023/11/blog-post_14.html)
------

<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">14</span>日，本网获悉：因为四通桥勇士彭载舟画像的南昌画家萧亮于<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">16</span>日被中共当局抓捕。他的案子<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月就已经已到法院，检察院当时建议量刑一年三个月。起诉理由为推特画“危险人物”并获得大量点赞。萧亮先生现关押在南昌市第一看守所。萧亮先生目前已被羁押近<span lang="EN-US">1</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>个月了，但萧亮家属依然没有接到开庭通知。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">萧亮：江西省南昌市人，网络活跃人士，常用推特号：<span lang="EN-US">@xiaoliang999</span>，南昌画家，异见表达者，中国在押政治犯。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2022</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">13</span>日，因中共二十大前夕，有“四通桥勇士”彭立发（网名彭载舟）在北京四通桥上悬挂倒习横幅，指责习近平是独裁者，要求他下台，随后即被抓捕；此事件一经传播，很快促使国内外许多华人站出来表达他们的对彭载舟先生的支持。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">此期间，萧亮因画了一幅四通桥勇士彭立发的画像并发到推特上，遂于<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">16</span>日被江西省南昌市东湖区警方带走，家遭查抄，而后被以“寻衅滋事”为由行政拘留；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">但之后其很快又被警方以同罪由转为刑拘，同时，其妻子被问讯，主要涉及其有何嗜好，都交些什么朋友，有参加什么组织或收谁的钱等等问题，均被其妻子一一否定；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2022</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">7</span>日，其被南昌市东湖区检察院以涉嫌“寻衅滋事罪”予以批捕；目前案子已到法院了。目前被羁押于南昌市第一看守所（南昌市新建县南昌警察学校<span lang="EN-US">100</span>米附近，邮政编码：<span lang="EN-US">210036</span>）。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">对南昌画家萧亮的境况，本网将持续关注。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"></p><div class="separator" style="clear: both;
