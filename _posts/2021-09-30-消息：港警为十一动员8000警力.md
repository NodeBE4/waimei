[消息：港警为十一动员8000警力](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210930-1198733)
------

<div>
<p>中国十一降至，消息人士透露，香港警方为此动员超过8000名警员执勤。警方也从今天（30日）凌晨起封锁湾仔金紫荆广场及香港会议展览中心一带，为升旗仪式及十一酒会作准备。</p><p>综合香港01、大公网和《星岛日报》报道，消息人士称，警方今明两天在港九地区部署这些警员，确保升旗仪式、十一酒会及各区相关公众活动能安全及有秩序地进行。</p><p>这些警员来自港警总部及总区应变大队、又称“速龙小队”的特别战术小队、反恐特勤队、铁路应变部队、冲锋队等。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>警方也在通往金紫荆广场及香港会议展览中心的主要通道设置路障。</p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/zbpe-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=zbpe399&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_desktop-zbpe399_2.png?dHEiGouxxZpWwWTJE04dyGyUN74JOU2z" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_desktop-zbpe399_2.png?dHEiGouxxZpWwWTJE04dyGyUN74JOU2z" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/zbpe-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=zbpe399&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_mobile-zbpe399_2.png?qZnBcIGCJvPR8A2IAZOZuOxdoGZDatiI" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_mobile-zbpe399_2.png?qZnBcIGCJvPR8A2IAZOZuOxdoGZDatiI" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
