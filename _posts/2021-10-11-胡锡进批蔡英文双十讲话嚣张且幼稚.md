[胡锡进批蔡英文双十讲话嚣张且幼稚](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20211011-1202351)
------

<div>
<p>中国大陆官媒《环球时报》总编辑胡锡进昨晚（10日）痛批台湾总统蔡英文的双十讲话“很煽情，也故作强硬”。胡锡进批评，蔡英文扬言台湾要加强防卫能力，“不仅嚣张，而且幼稚”。</p><p>胡锡进在微博上写道，中国历史上拔除过很多顽固的割据政权，这个国家的大一统传统就是这样形成的。</p><p>胡锡进还录制了一段近四分钟的短视频。他在视频中形容，蔡英文的讲话是“经过精心编导的、欺骗国际社会和岛内民众的表演。”</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>他说，蔡英文的演讲充斥着“一边一国”的论调，民进党所做的是“根本性的背叛，这与他们早就不承认自己是中国人一脉相承。”</p><p>胡锡进认为，蔡英文的讲话，进一步展示了蔡英文政府的狭小格局，和一条路走到黑的偏执狂妄。“他们自我放弃了战略调整权，把自己投入到只能满足短期私利、从长远看毫无胜算的政治赌博中，并且用一些花枝乱颤的政治辞藻使劲煽情。”</p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/zbpe-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=zbpe399&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_desktop-zbpe399_2.png?dHEiGouxxZpWwWTJE04dyGyUN74JOU2z" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_desktop-zbpe399_2.png?dHEiGouxxZpWwWTJE04dyGyUN74JOU2z" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/zbpe-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=zbpe399&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_mobile-zbpe399_2.png?qZnBcIGCJvPR8A2IAZOZuOxdoGZDatiI" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_mobile-zbpe399_2.png?qZnBcIGCJvPR8A2IAZOZuOxdoGZDatiI" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
