<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">20</span>日，本网获悉被判刑<span lang="EN-US">4</span>年的吉林图们市法轮功学员王慧女士的案情及简历，现在通报如下：<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">王慧：</span></b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">女，吉林图们市法轮功学员。王慧是一九九五年五月开始修炼法轮功的。一九九九年七月二十日中共开始镇压法轮功，迫害法轮功学员。王慧曾遭多次中共关押、酷刑。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2021</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">15</span>日，图们市法轮功学员王慧等多名法轮功学员，被图们市国保警察抓捕。当时图们市<span lang="EN-US">610</span>在五工村生态园办洗脑班，当时关押迫害<span lang="EN-US">40</span>名左右的法轮功修炼者。王慧等三名法轮功学员后来被从图们洗脑班转到延吉进一步迫害。后王慧被图们市检察院构陷，被图们市法院判刑<span lang="EN-US">4</span>年，<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年被关押到吉林省女子监狱。刑期至：<span lang="EN-US">2025</span>年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">14</span>日。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p>
