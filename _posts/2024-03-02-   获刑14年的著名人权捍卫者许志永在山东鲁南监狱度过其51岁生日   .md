[获刑14年的著名人权捍卫者许志永在山东鲁南监狱度过其51岁生日](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2024/03/1451.html)

<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">2</span>日，本网获悉：<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">2</span>日是获刑<span lang="EN-US">14</span>年的中国著名人权捍卫者许志永的生日，今天许志永先生在鲁南监狱度过了其<span lang="EN-US">51</span>岁生日。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">许志永：<span lang="EN-US">1973</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">2</span>日出生，河南省商丘市民权县人，前北京邮电学院专业讲师，前北京市海淀区人民法院人民陪审员，<span lang="EN-US">“</span>公盟<span lang="EN-US">”</span>重要创始人，青年法学家、宪政学者和公民维权代理人，就全国人大对《城市流浪乞讨人员收容遣送办法》进行违宪审查提议者，新公民运动倡导者，中国在押政治犯。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">2013</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">16</span>日，其被北京市警方以涉嫌<span lang="EN-US">“</span>聚扰乱公共场所秩序罪<span lang="EN-US">”</span>刑拘，关押在北京市第三看守所；同年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">22</span>日，被正式逮捕；<span lang="EN-US">2014</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">26</span>日，被北京市第一中级法院以<span lang="EN-US">“</span>聚众扰乱公共场所秩序罪<span lang="EN-US">”</span>判处有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">4</span>年；<span lang="EN-US">2017</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">15</span>日，刑满获释<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">2020</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">15</span>日，许志永被广州市警方从番禺海鸥岛杨斌律师处带走；据悉，其此次被抓，此与<span lang="EN-US">2019</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月曾因参加<span lang="EN-US">“</span>厦门聚会案<span lang="EN-US">”</span>遭通缉有关，许志永自<span lang="EN-US">2019</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">26</span>日丁家喜律师被捕后就一直在中国南方秘密流亡。其亲友均遭多次骚扰跟踪，其女友李翘楚亦被强迫失踪，后取保获释。<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">19</span>日，被以涉嫌煽动颠覆国家政权罪正式逮捕，曾羁押于山东省临沭县看守所。<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">10</span>日，许志永遭中共当局以颠覆国家政权罪判处<span lang="EN-US">14</span>年重刑，刑期至<span lang="EN-US">2034</span>年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月。其不服上诉，二审遭驳回，维持原判。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">许志永二审后已转监到位于山东日照的鲁南监狱服刑。鲁南监狱地址：山东省日照市东港区南湖镇西黄山前村西<span lang="EN-US">500</span>米。其邮寄地址：山东省日照市<span lang="EN-US">979</span>信箱<span lang="EN-US">15</span>分箱。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体;
