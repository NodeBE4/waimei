<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">6</span>日，本网获悉：山东省烟台市公民刘杰<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">29</span>日遭北京丰台区警方以涉嫌寻衅滋事罪刑事拘留。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">后经其朋友们多地询问得知，刘杰已经于<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">29</span>日被北京市公安局丰台分局以涉嫌寻衅滋事罪刑事拘留，现在被羁押在北京市丰台区看守所。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"></p><div class="separator" style="clear: both;
