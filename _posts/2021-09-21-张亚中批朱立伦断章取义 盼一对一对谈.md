[张亚中批朱立伦断章取义 盼一对一对谈](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210921-1195775)
------

<div>
<p>针对被国民党主席候选人朱立伦指控为红统，张亚中今天批评朱立伦断章取义，他并称如果朱立伦愿意，希望能与朱立伦来场一对一对话，向朱立伦解说所有疑虑。</p><p>综合中时新闻网、中央社、网媒“ETtoday”报道，张亚中今天受访时说，不知道朱立伦一直说他是红统、会搞分裂、走极端；但他对朱立伦，没有所谓的私人攻击或扣帽子，他希望的是两岸政策辩论。</p><p>张亚中指出，朱立伦称看过他的著作，但应该是朱立伦助理提供所谓的断章取义片段，且朱立伦根本没有弄清楚上下文，就轻率的发言，非常不得体。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>张亚中也说，如果朱立伦愿意，希望电视台能够安排他与朱立伦来场一对一对话，他很乐意向朱立伦解说所有疑虑，也让社会了解，谁的主张能让国民党更好。</p><p>国民党主席将于本周六（25日）改选，由寻求连任的党主席江启臣、前主席朱立伦、孙文学校总校长张亚中、前彰化县长卓伯源等人角逐。</p><p> </p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/zbpe-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=zbpe399&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_desktop-zbpe399_2.png?dHEiGouxxZpWwWTJE04dyGyUN74JOU2z" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_desktop-zbpe399_2.png?dHEiGouxxZpWwWTJE04dyGyUN74JOU2z" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/zbpe-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=zbpe399&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_mobile-zbpe399_2.png?qZnBcIGCJvPR8A2IAZOZuOxdoGZDatiI" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_mobile-zbpe399_2.png?qZnBcIGCJvPR8A2IAZOZuOxdoGZDatiI" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
