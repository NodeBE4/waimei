[一审获刑8年的诗人、艺术家、法轮功学员许娜（又名许那）女士二审被维持原判](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2023/05/8_20.html)
------

<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">20</span>日，本网获悉：因疫情期间发布一些国内疫情照片，诗人、艺术家、法轮功学员许娜（许那）于<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">16</span>日一审获刑<span lang="EN-US">8</span>年。<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">18</span>日，许娜二审开庭，二审驳回上诉，维持原判。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">许娜简介：</span></b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">又名许那，<span lang="EN-US">1968</span>年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">8</span>日出生，吉林省长春市人，<span lang="EN-US">89</span>六四亲历者、广场静坐绝食学生，北京独立画家、艺术家和诗人，法轮功信仰者，中国在押良心犯。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">1987</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">-</span>­<span lang="EN-US">1991</span>年，曾就读于北京广播学院（现更名为中国传媒大学）文艺编导专业；毕业后因对当时的现实失望，随拒绝进入广电系统，转而进行诗词创作，后又学习美术，成为可独立举办画展的艺术家；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">曾以诗人自居，潜心创作出大量优秀诗词作品，并于<span lang="EN-US">1993</span>年精选、编撰成稿，交由漓江出版社出版成诗集，标题为《隐蜜》；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">1994</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年，又将兴趣与精力转入绘画创作；<span lang="EN-US">1997</span>年，曾积极参加中国油画学会主办的主题为“走向新世纪——中国青年油画展”和中国文化部主办的中国艺术展；在此期间，因追求自由信仰，成为一名忠实的法轮功修炼者，随遭北京当局的无情打击和迫害；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2001</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年，曾被北京市当局以信仰邪教罪判刑<span lang="EN-US">5</span>年，至<span lang="EN-US">2006</span>年刑满出狱；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2007</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年，回归绘画创作，曾再次参加中国油画学会的“首届中国青年百人油画展”，其作品《静物》荣获优秀作品奖；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2008</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月，其与其丈夫于宙（当时民间知名的音乐人）被北京警方突然一起抓捕，并送北京市通州看守所关押；几天后，其丈夫于宙突然死于看守所，原因不明。但据北京当局称，其丈夫于宙死于绝食或糖尿病，而其真实死因至今成谜；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">几个月后，其亦被北京市通州区法院以“邪教罪”判刑<span lang="EN-US">3</span>年；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2011</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年获释后，其继续从事绘画创作，并于<span lang="EN-US">2012</span>年曾先后在北京市西五画廊举办个人作品展及参加北京市与台北市的艺术展；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2020</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">19</span>日，再次与另<span lang="EN-US">12</span>名法轮功信仰者一起被北京市顺义区空港派出所警方抓走拘留，家遭查抄，一些电子设备被搜走；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">同年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">26 </span>日，被北京市东城区警方以涉嫌“破坏法律实施罪”转为刑拘；同年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">1</span>日，被北京市东城区检察院以同罪名正式批捕，指控其等在疫情期间发布相关国内疫情照片和其信仰法轮功等信息，故此言行属于违法；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">之后，其等数人案卷被北京市东城区警方移交给东城区检察院准备起诉，但因东城区检察院核实后认为其案“证据不足”，而被两次退回卷宗要求补侦；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2022</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">14</span>日<span lang="EN-US">-16</span>日，其案在北京市东城区法院庭审宣判，终被以“破坏法律实施罪”重判有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">8</span>年，并处罚金<span lang="EN-US">2</span>万元，剥夺政治权利<span lang="EN-US">1</span>年，刑期至<span lang="EN-US">2028</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">18</span>日；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">其等不服，表示上诉；北京市第二中级法院，于<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">18</span>日开庭，二审驳回上诉，维持原判。目前被羁押于北京市东城区看守所。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体;
