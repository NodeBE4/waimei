font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">家属在<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">23-26</span>日向四家相关核酸公司赔偿了<span lang="EN-US">3995</span>元，并分别得到了这几家核酸公司的谅解书。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体;
