[一审获刑4年的浙江杭州民主党人、八九学生领袖徐光狱中情况通报](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2024/04/4_25.html)
------

<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p><p class="MsoNormal" style="tab-stops: 66.0pt;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">25</span>日，本网获悉一审获刑<span lang="EN-US">4</span>年的浙江杭州民主党人、八九学生领袖徐光的狱中情况，现在通报如下：<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal" style="tab-stops: 66.0pt;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">刚被判刑四年的杭州异议人士徐光提起上诉，理由是指控他寻衅滋事犯罪的理由不成立，是莫须有的罪名，要求无罪释放。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal" style="tab-stops: 66.0pt;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">徐光<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">7</span>日传出话说：他现在体重<span lang="EN-US">85</span>斤，他经常绝食，公安监管医院很恼火，不许他洗澡，不许看书，不许别人和他说话。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal" style="tab-stops: 66.0pt;"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">2024</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">4</span>日，徐光的律师要求会见徐光，监管医院不许律师会见。当时徐光脚带脚镣和手铐，重达二十几斤，根本起不来，他很想见律师，可是看守所监管医院不让他见。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal" style="tab-stops: 66.0pt;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">监管医院的葛品洪多次给徐光带脚镣和手铐，每次<span lang="EN-US">15</span>天，还有一个姓郑的医生用拳头殴打徐光的脸，骂、威胁徐光，反而指责徐光骂人。徐光承认骂过他们是法西斯。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal" style="tab-stops: 66.0pt;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">徐光<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">3</span>日一审被以寻衅滋事罪判刑<span lang="EN-US">4</span>年。徐光是于<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">26</span>日被刑事拘留的，至今已被羁押近两年。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal" style="tab-stops: 66.0pt;"><span style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">徐光简历：<span lang="EN-US">1968</span>年出生于浙江省富阳市，<span lang="EN-US">1990</span>年毕业于杭州大学生物系，大学期间曾参与<span lang="EN-US">1986</span>年及<span lang="EN-US">1989</span>年学生爱国民主运动，<span lang="EN-US">“</span>八九学运<span lang="EN-US">”</span>杭州大学学运领袖，中国民主党浙江委员会核心成员。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal" style="tab-stops: 66.0pt;"><span style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">其于<span lang="EN-US">1998</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月在家乡富阳市环保局环境监测站工作的徐光组建了<span lang="EN-US">“</span>中国民主党浙江省富阳市筹委会<span lang="EN-US">”</span>，是中国民主党第一个县级组织。撰写了《抓不完的中国民主党》等文章，并起草《中国民主党富阳市筹备委员会成立公开宣言》，<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">23</span>日被杭州市抓捕，<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">15</span>被逮捕。同年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">9</span>日徐光被以<span lang="EN-US">“</span>颠覆国家政权罪<span lang="EN-US">”</span>判处有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">5</span>年，剥夺政治权利<span lang="EN-US">2</span>年；出狱后的徐光仍坚守信念，抨击时弊。<span lang="EN-US">2014</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">2</span>日因为在<span lang="EN-US">QQ</span>上参加中国民主党话题的闲聊，被浙江当局以涉嫌<span lang="EN-US">“</span>颠覆国家政权罪<span lang="EN-US">”</span>刑事拘留，<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">9</span>日被正式逮捕，同年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">31</span>日获释，期间从未获准律师会见。后获释。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal" style="tab-stops: 66.0pt;"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">2022</span><span style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">24</span>日，因<span lang="EN-US">“</span>六四<span lang="EN-US">”</span>将近，徐光收到居住区西湖区公局分局玉泉派出所的传唤单，徐光未予理睬。<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">25</span>日上午，徐光在所居东山弄小区采购，途经辖区的西湖区公安分局玉泉派出所附近，正遇上派出所民警，被带进派出所，告诫徐光<span lang="EN-US">“</span>六四<span lang="EN-US">”</span>将近，不要有什么涉<span lang="EN-US">“</span>六四<span lang="EN-US">”</span>的敏感言行，强行收走了他的手机。徐光<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">26</span>日去玉泉派出所，带着写着<span lang="EN-US">“</span>平反六四<span lang="EN-US">”</span>的纸张，抗议公安对自己迫害。当日徐光被国保刑事拘留，被抄家，后被羁押。徐光从当日绝食抗议至今仍在绝食中，鼻饲灌注维持。<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">24</span>日下午<span lang="EN-US">5</span>时杭州国保第二次对徐光家搜查，并于<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">2</span>日正式逮捕徐光。徐光被控的罪名为涉嫌寻衅滋事。当局指控徐光<span lang="EN-US">“</span>在<span lang="EN-US">2018</span>年至<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年期间，在微信，脸书，电报，油管发布视频，文章，散布损害国家形象，危害国家利益的虚假信息。指控徐光在网络上寻衅滋事。<span lang="EN-US">”</span>徐光认为系当局构陷，徐光称：自己作为当年杭州六四学运的参予者，每年都公开纪念<span lang="EN-US">“</span>八九六四<span lang="EN-US">”</span>和举牌<span lang="EN-US">“</span>勿忘六四<span lang="EN-US">”</span>。当局为六四维稳需要，打压中国民主党人，在六四前抓捕自己。徐光坚决要求律师做无罪辩护。现徐光关押在杭州市西湖区分局看守所。其在狱中持续绝食抗议。<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">3</span>日，徐光被杭州西湖区法院开庭宣判，以寻衅滋事罪判刑<span lang="EN-US">4</span>年。刑期至：<span lang="EN-US">2026</span>年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">19</span>日。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal" style="tab-stops: 66.0pt;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">对徐光先生的境况，本网将持续关注。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal" style="tab-stops: 66.0pt;"></p><div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;"><a href="https://blogger.googleusercontent.com/img/b/R29vZ2xl/AVvXsEiGsRdfsYOaZKez12QV193kE6x6gCfx1JRWsFlApcfE_1HncGNkNyJyEkfmzfIq5JAfuRZ7F7h5P4DCanZgsd0ZJcP4-mAmO60W7pCrKZMXoJIq2HEZgzr3JcpiHHWF91fdXg2StnAC3PsDejMgdBB_Vbri0GUhgxhK3inidX-GwV_4D8Ch-zmyE5fGDL3Q/s320/%E5%BE%90%E5%85%89.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em;
