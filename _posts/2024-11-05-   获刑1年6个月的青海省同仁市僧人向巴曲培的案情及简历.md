font-size: 12.0pt;">早年曾于<span lang="EN-US">1986</span>年前往印度，并在达兰萨拉辩经学院修学<span lang="EN-US">10</span>年<span lang="EN-US">; <o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; <o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">2024</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">10</span>日，因其在西藏自由抗暴纪念日之际在微信朋友圈里分享了一段达赖喇嘛的语音，遂很快被同仁市警方以政治敏感人物为由，因言获罪而秘捕，并被关押在同仁市古拉唐监狱<span lang="EN-US">; <o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">2024</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月，其被青海省中共当局以不确罪名秘判处有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">1</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月，刑期至<span lang="EN-US">2025</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">9</span>日<span lang="EN-US">;
