[遭刑事拘留的重庆维权人士唐云淑的案情通报](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2021/11/blog-post_68.html)
------

<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">4</span>日，本网获悉遭刑事拘留的重庆维权人士唐云淑的案情，现通报如下：<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">唐云淑：</span></b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">女、<span lang="EN-US">1973</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">29</span>日出生，重庆市丰都县龙河镇人，重庆市两江新区大竹林康庄美地二期<span lang="EN-US">5</span>号门暂居公民，维权访民，中国在押维权人士。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">1990</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年代，曾因地方当局在拆迁土地及基建过程中违法行使权力，强拆损毁其家<span lang="EN-US">3</span>处房产，且拆迁安置补偿又遇不公，发生纠纷，其随与父母家人共同信访投诉讨公道；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">后因在不断维权抗争中，重庆当局又逼死其母亲及祖母，其前夫与公公亦遭突然莫名死亡（其中，验尸显示，其前夫尸体上有多处伤痕，且<span lang="EN-US">3</span>根肋骨断裂等），故其立志再行控告投诉，但警方竟对其受害亲人拒不立案和侦查；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">多年来，因其不断上访维权，曾多次被重庆市丰都县政府成员殴打、辱骂和抢劫财物，多次被警方非法抓捕和被关精神病医院，数次被警方处以行政拘留、刑事拘留，甚至被以“寻衅滋事罪”枉法判刑（实关一千多天）；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2021</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">15</span>日，因其前往重庆市公安局信访办（当天是局长接待日），被当地维稳监控人员知晓，遂被立即赶来的重庆市丰都县警方从其暂住地拦截带走，其家被查抄，后被丰都县警方以涉嫌“寻衅滋事罪”刑拘；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2021</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">22</span>日，又被丰都县检察院以涉嫌同罪名予以正式批捕；据悉，其与代理律师会面时坚称自己无罪，因其全家所遇不公，长期得不到政府部门真实有效的解决，随维权上访，不属于犯罪。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">目前唐云淑被羁押于重庆市丰都县看守所。</span></p><div class="separator" style="clear: both;
