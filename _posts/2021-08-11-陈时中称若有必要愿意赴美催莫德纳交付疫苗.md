[陈时中称若有必要愿意赴美催莫德纳交付疫苗](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210811-1180910)
------

<div>
<p>韩国本周将派代表团赴美国抗议莫德纳延迟交货，对于台湾是否也赴美催货，防疫指挥中心指挥官陈时中今天说，若有必要，台湾也愿意去做。</p><p>据台湾中央社报道，韩国政府今年共采购4000万剂莫德纳COVID-19疫苗，但仅到货245.5万剂。据韩联社报道，韩国本周将派代表团赴美，抗议莫德纳两度延迟交货，要求厂商不可再发生类似情况。</p><p>对于催促莫德纳药厂准时交货一事，陈时中认为，各地做法不同，派人直接面谈确实可了解买卖双方的想法。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>陈时中说，台湾可以通过驻美代表处，或与莫德纳总公司视讯联系，有多重管道即时掌握疫苗供应情况。若亲自赴美催货有必要性，“我们任何作为都愿意做。”</p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
