------

<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">杨紫荆：</span></b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2015</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年大学毕业后，曾因受新冠病毒疫情响而返回老家暂住，并因开始将目光投放于关注社会问题，故在准备考研时将所学专业定为社会学；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2020</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">-7</span>月，其开始前往广东省广州市谋生，并由此交到了许多志趣相投的朋友，同期，又因其个性极富洞察力，且对性别议题十分敏感、关心弱势群体疾苦等，遂广受朋友们的赞许和信任；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2022</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">4</span>日，其被广州市越秀区北京街派出所警方以“涉及海珠广场事件”为由传唤带走，家中电子设备遭搜查，次日（<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">5</span>日）即被广州市越秀区警方以涉嫌“寻衅滋事罪”刑拘；后其家人为其聘请了代理律师，但代理律师及其家人一直不被允许与其会见，并且警方拒绝告知家人和律师任何有关案件信息；据悉，其被办案人员问讯的内容主要涉及“是否境外势力、<span lang="EN-US">NGO</span>（非政府组织）”，“是否收钱举白纸”等，并其家人与朋友均被威胁“不要将此事发到网上”，否则对其安全不利；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体;
