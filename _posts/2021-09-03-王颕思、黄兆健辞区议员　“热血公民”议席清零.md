[王颕思、黄兆健辞区议员　“热血公民”议席清零](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210903-1190053)
------

<div>
<p>香港“热血公民”成员王颕思今天（3日）在社交网页宣布，辞去元朗逸泽区区议员一职，所有地区服务（包括办事处、联络电话、Facebook专页）也会中止，未完结的个案将转介相关机构。</p><p>据香港01报道，“热血公民”另一党员黄兆健也宣布辞任大埔区议员，称政治形势急速变化，区议员在议会的空间减少，故作出辞职决定。</p><p>随着主席郑松泰失去立法会议席，加上王颕思、黄兆健辞职，“热血公民”两级议会议席“清零”。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>王颕思辞职后，元朗区议会目前仅剩11名民主派区议员，其余12名区议员为建制派或乡事派。</p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/zbpe-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=zbpe399&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_desktop-zbpe399_2.png?dHEiGouxxZpWwWTJE04dyGyUN74JOU2z" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_desktop-zbpe399_2.png?dHEiGouxxZpWwWTJE04dyGyUN74JOU2z" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/zbpe-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=zbpe399&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_mobile-zbpe399_2.png?qZnBcIGCJvPR8A2IAZOZuOxdoGZDatiI" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_mobile-zbpe399_2.png?qZnBcIGCJvPR8A2IAZOZuOxdoGZDatiI" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
