<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">20</span>日，本网获悉：海南公民吴孔大煽动颠覆案已于<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">29</span>日开庭，吴孔大本人已经收到判决书，显示被判刑<span lang="EN-US">3</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月。吴孔大于<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">10</span>日被以煽动颠覆罪刑拘，刑期至<span lang="EN-US">2027</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">9</span>日。目前其被羁押在海口第二看守所。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><b><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;">吴孔大简介：</span></b><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt;">1975</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;">年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">25</span>日出生，曾是海南省定安县龙湖镇中心学校教师，微信网名<span lang="EN-US">“</span>天会再蓝<span lang="EN-US">”</span>、<span lang="EN-US">“</span>尧山魂<span lang="EN-US">”</span>、<span lang="EN-US">“</span>海棠血泪<span lang="EN-US">”</span>等。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;">多年来吴孔大长期关注并转发民主宪政维权方面的信息，并多次对学校和政府提意见，遭迫害。<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月底，吴老师因在微信群转发人权律师王全璋、李和平被逼迁、何方美案等良心犯情况，被当地警方带到派出所，随后又被行政拘留<span lang="EN-US">15</span>天。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;">吴孔大于<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">10</span>日被当地警方以涉嫌<span lang="EN-US">“</span>煽动颠覆国家政权罪<span lang="EN-US">”</span>刑事拘留，后遭逮捕、起诉，<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">29</span>日，吴孔大案在海口中院开庭。吴孔大老师认为自己某些言论虽有不当，但是完全属于公民行使宪法赋予的言论自由权，属于无罪。法庭当庭未宣判。于<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月获悉其被判刑<span lang="EN-US">3</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月，刑期至<span lang="EN-US">2027</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">9</span>日。目前其被羁押在海口第二看守所。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;">对吴孔大的境况，本网将持续关注。<span
