[苏州周金丹老太的房屋遭遇强拆](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2021/08/blog-post_24.html)
------

<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">24</span>日，本网获悉：今天上午九点许，苏州市姑苏区苏锦街道政法委书记、司法所长时所长及其平江新城管委会征收办主任带领强拆百余人的队伍，开着警车、铲车、搬家车及消防车包围了周金丹家，并对周金丹老人宣读了《苏州市人民政府苏府【<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>】<span lang="EN-US">113</span>号关于花锦村<span lang="EN-US">430</span>号周金丹、丁晓艳、丁静艳、丁善华<span lang="EN-US">(</span>户<span lang="EN-US">)</span>强制搬迁的通知》（简称<span lang="EN-US">113</span>号通知），称“周金丹、丁晓艳、丁静艳、丁善华<span lang="EN-US">(</span>户<span lang="EN-US">):</span>现通知你户，请你户自本通知书送达之日起十日内按照苏建房裁<span lang="EN-US">(2019]</span>第<span lang="EN-US">1</span>号房屋拆迁裁决书的规定，腾让花锦村<span lang="EN-US">430</span>号<span lang="EN-US">(</span>虎丘镇花锦村<span lang="EN-US">2</span>组<span lang="EN-US">)</span>丁善如名下全部房屋交苏州市土地储备中心查收。逾期仍未履行搬迁义务的，苏州市人民政府将依据苏州市姑苏区人民法院作出的<span lang="EN-US">(2020)</span>苏<span lang="EN-US">0508</span>行审<span lang="EN-US">81</span>号行政裁定书，对你户实施强制执行。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">随后，该强拆人员将周金丹老太及其女儿强行押送至古月大酒店关押，强行拆除其位于苏州市姑苏区花锦村<span lang="EN-US">430</span>号的房屋，现场一片狼藉。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">参与强拆的队伍相当可观，苏州辅警<span lang="EN-US">30</span>余人，城管<span lang="EN-US">40</span>余人、保安也有<span lang="EN-US">40</span>余人，外加公安、拆房队、拆迁公司等人员一起出动。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">强拆期间，周金丹老人的亲友打电话报警，无济于事。<span lang="EN-US"> <o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">周金丹老太今年<span lang="EN-US">75</span>周岁，维权<span lang="EN-US">13</span>年，还被关押过<span lang="EN-US">103</span>天，房屋终于被强行拆除。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体;
