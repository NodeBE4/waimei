------

<div>
<p><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;"><span></span></span></p><a name='more'></a><br /><p></p><p><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;">（维权网信息中心报道）</span><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt;">2022</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;">年</span><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt;">10</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;">月</span><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt;">5</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;">日，本网获悉：获刑</span><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt;">6</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;">年的山东诗人、作家张桂祺（笔名鲁扬）目前被羁押于聊城市看守所（山东省聊城市东昌府区梁水镇驻地北</span><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt;">500</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;">米路西，邮政编码：</span><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt;">252037</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;">）。鲁扬是</span><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt;">2020</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;">年</span><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt;">5</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;">月遭中共当局拘捕，后获刑</span><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt;">6</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;">年。其不服一审判决，已提起上诉，二审正在等待中，家人为其聘请二审代理律师艰难。</span></p><p><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;">以下是鲁扬的简历及案情，现在通报如下：</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">张桂祺（笔名鲁扬）：</span></b><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">1971</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月出生，山东省莘县人，笔名鲁扬，网名“鲁西狂徒”，前“扬子宛诗社”、“中国当代诗歌论坛”、“中国自由文化论坛”及“中国诗人大讲堂——文殊书院”等媒体平台创办人，原山东省聊城外国学校教师，著名山东诗人及作家，《零八宪章》签署人，独立中文笔会会员，民主异见人士，中国在押政治犯。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">1994</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年起开始诗歌文学创作活动，曾创办“扬子宛诗社”，并主编出版了《扬子宛诗刊》；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2003</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年，曾创办“中国当代诗歌论坛”，并主编出版了《中国当代诗歌》刊物；后因该论坛被中共当局查封，故又于<span lang="EN-US">2005</span>年始在《星星》、《诗选刊》等国内著名诗歌刊物发表诗歌；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2004</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年，曾创办“中国自由文化论坛”，后因颇具影响而被当局多次叫停，后被移入海外“自由中国”网站，最终被并入博讯网站；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2006</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年，曾因自由写作而被迫从所在山东省聊城外国学校辞职；因其妻工作曾遭牵连，随夫妻二人一起开山东，被迫到南方谋生，此间由诗歌创作转入文化自由写作，因其大量作品在国内各大网站发表后引起社会共鸣，随被海外多家媒体转载；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2008</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年，曾因参与“零八宪章”签署，夫妻二人工作又受影响，双双失业；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2009</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月，曾因关注、支持被入狱判刑的政治犯刘晓波而成为独立中文笔会成员；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2010</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年，曾因受“中国茉莉花运动”影响，而再度失业并被遣返山东老家；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2017</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年，曾因举牌声援批评毛泽东而遭解职的山东省济南建筑大学教授邓相超，在邓教授所在大学遭到暴徒殴打，受到中外媒体关注，同时也成为当局的重点监控对象； <span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2019</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年，因其致力于继续从事自由文化传播事业，故而又一次创建新的媒体平台“中国诗人大讲堂——文殊书院”，引起当局极度反感与打压；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2020</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月，因其被提名为独立中文笔会理事候选人，且在网上频发批评中共领导人等政治敏感性文图信息，<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">1</span>日，遂被山东省聊城市警方带走，家遭查抄；同年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">13</span>日，被聊城市警方以涉嫌“煽动颠覆国家政权罪”刑拘；<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">18</span>日，又被聊城市检察院以同罪名正式批捕，指控其大有“危害国家安全”之嫌；同年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月，其案在山东省聊城市法院秘密开庭审理，之后消息全无；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2022</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast;
