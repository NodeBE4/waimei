[港大派员更换学生会办事处及会室门锁](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210721-1171754)
------

<div>
<p>香港大学要求学生会迁出学生会综合大楼。今早（21日）约10时，多名工程人员在校方职员陪同下进入学生会大楼，更换学生会办事处、学生会评议会议事厅及大楼内学生会属会会室的门锁，全程有保安人员在场看守。</p><p>据明报报道，工程人员用约1小时更换所有会室的门锁，及后将学生会会室内的影印机，移至毗邻的影印中心暂存。</p><p>校方派保安人员在学生会会室前看守，暂时未见学生返回领取物品。至于各属会、学生会合作社及影印中心，登记后可领取新门匙。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>港大学生会学苑即时新闻于facebook专页表示，港大学生会今天正式迁出学生会综合大楼，“创会百年内，学生会会址经历过一共三次搬迁。今起，学生会再无会址”。</p><p> </p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
