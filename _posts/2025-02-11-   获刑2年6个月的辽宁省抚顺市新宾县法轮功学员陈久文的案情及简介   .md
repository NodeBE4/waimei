<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2025</span>年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">11</span>日，本网获悉获刑<span lang="EN-US">2</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月的辽宁省抚顺市新宾县法轮功学员陈久文的案情及简介，现在通报如下：<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">陈久文：</span></b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">辽宁省抚顺市新宾县法轮功学员，男，<span lang="EN-US">1954</span>年生，<span lang="EN-US">2025</span>年已<span lang="EN-US">71</span>岁，满族，退休职员。<span lang="EN-US">1996</span>年开始修炼法轮功。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">1999</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月法轮功遭受迫害后，陈久文去北京上访，在北京被绑架，并非法押回新宾看守所，遭抄家，被劳教<span lang="EN-US">3</span>年，在抚顺市吳家堡教养院被逼迫做苦役等迫害。<span lang="EN-US">2008</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">28</span>日，抚顺市国保大队闯入陈久文家，将其抓捕，将其关押在抚顺南沟看守所。遭残暴酷刑折磨，后被判刑<span lang="EN-US">3</span>年。在辽宁省本溪市西湖监狱遭迫害。<a name="_Hlk190167153"><span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></a></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="mso-bookmark: _Hlk190167153;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">陈久文</span></span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">2023</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">29</span>日被吉林省松原县公安局警察跨省抓捕，据说与他用手机微信发法轮功经文有关。他被关押在前郭看守所构陷。后得知，陈久文被松原县法院判刑<span lang="EN-US">2</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月，关押在吉林监狱服刑。刑期至：<span lang="EN-US">2026</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">28</span>日。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p>
