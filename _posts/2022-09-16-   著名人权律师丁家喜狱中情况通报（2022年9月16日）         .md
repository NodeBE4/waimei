font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">丁家喜代理律师今天介绍：“今天即<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">16</span>日下午我在临沭县以视频连线方式会见了被羁押在临沭县看守所的丁家喜。例行读丁妻信，已是第<span lang="EN-US">45</span>封了……老丁叮嘱家事之外，关注许志永等友人近况、时事新闻；对乌克兰战争中的牺牲及胜利感怀不已。老丁坚持每天早起在狭小空间内锻炼身体、精气神不错，令我钦佩。”<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">丁家喜简介：</span></b><span style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">人权律师、人权捍卫者。北京航空航天大学获得工学学士和工学硕士（飞机制造专业）。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">1990</span><span style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">至<span lang="EN-US">1996</span>年在沈阳航空工业总公司、北京航空工业总公司任工程师。<span lang="EN-US">1996</span>年起转为专职律师，任职于北京市多家律师事务所。<span lang="EN-US">2003</span>年参与设立北京市德鸿律师事务所任高级合伙人。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">自<span lang="EN-US">2010</span>年起积极参与推动“随迁子女就地高考”教育平权活动。<span lang="EN-US">2012</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">9</span>日与许志永等人发表致习近平等中共高层领导人公开信，要求包括习近平在内的<span lang="EN-US">205</span>名中国部级以上官员率先财产公示。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">2013</span><span style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">17</span>日丁家喜被当局以“非法集会”罪名刑事拘留，关押在北京市第三看守所。同年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">21</span>日，公安机关将丁家喜罪名更换为“寻衅滋事”提交检察院。同年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">4</span>日北京市检察院一分院将案件退回公安局补充侦查，控罪更换为“聚众扰乱社会秩序罪”。<span lang="EN-US">2014</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">18</span>日，北京市海淀区法院判处丁家喜有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">3</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月。<span lang="EN-US">2016</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">16</span>日丁家喜刑满出狱。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">2019</span><span style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">26</span>日晚<span lang="EN-US">9</span>点左右，北京人权律师丁家喜在朋友家中被山东国保警察带走，手机等物品被查抄。被构陷为所谓“厦门聚会案”主犯。<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">19</span>日遭正式逮捕。现已被起诉、不公开审理。丁家喜现关押在山东省临沭县看守所。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体;
