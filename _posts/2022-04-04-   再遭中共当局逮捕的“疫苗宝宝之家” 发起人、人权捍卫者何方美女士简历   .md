<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">何方美：</span></b><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">女，<span lang="EN-US">1985</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">8</span>日出生，河南省新乡市辉县人，网名“十三妹”，“疫苗宝宝之家”维权团体发起人，人权捍卫者，<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年度“曹顺利人权捍卫者奖共同获得者，中国在押维权人士。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">自<span lang="EN-US">2018</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">23</span>日其年仅<span lang="EN-US">1</span>岁<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月龄的小女打了百白破疫苗之后导致全身瘫痪，其多方求助投诉均被拒，遂逐级维权讨说法，后因多地亦发生类似疫苗伤害宝宝事件，随组建 “疫苗宝宝之家”维权团体共同呼吁，以求当局关注并给予合理赔偿救治；然当局却对此一拖再拖，其被迫到北京上访，故屡遭抓捕打击；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2019</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">5</span>日，因中共两会期间与各地问题疫苗家长到北京王府井为残儿募捐，随被新乡市辉县警方行政拘留<span lang="EN-US">15</span>天；<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">21</span>日，又被以辉县警方以涉嫌“寻衅滋事罪”转刑拘，关押在河南省新乡市看守所；因期间一直拒绝认罪，<span lang="EN-US">2019</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">26</span>日，被新乡市辉县检察院以同罪名批捕，后被起诉并罗列<span lang="EN-US">6</span>项罪，建议量刑<span lang="EN-US">1</span>年；同年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">15</span>日，其案在河南省辉县法院正式开庭；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">10</span>日，又被辉县检察院以“事实不清、证据不足”为由撤诉，后被无罪获释；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2020</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">18</span>日，因其不断发声请求国家立法解决疫苗问题、呼吁联合国关注中国疫苗问题等，而在北京再度遭遇强迫失踪；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">释放后，<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">2</span>日，因当地政府阻挠其带疫苗致残的女儿看病，随对辉县政府招牌泼墨抗议，而被行政拘留；后于<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">9</span>日第二次对辉县市政府泼漆抗议；<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">14</span>日，知其及其丈夫、六岁的儿子、三岁的残疾女儿一起被警方软禁，自此失联；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2021</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">23</span>日，其被河南省辉县市警方以涉嫌“重婚罪”和“寻衅滋事罪”正式逮捕；据悉，羁押期间，其产下一女，有关部门已致电其家属，称若不去领回孩子，其所有孩子将被送到福利院。目前被羁押于河南省新乡市看守所。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"></p><div class="separator" style="clear: both;
