<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">18</span>日，本网获悉获刑<span lang="EN-US">2</span>年的山东潍坊法轮功学员孔祥银的案情及简历，现在通报如下<span lang="EN-US">:<o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">孔祥银：</span></b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">山东潍坊法轮功学员，女，<span lang="EN-US">1958</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月出生，现年65岁。她于<span lang="EN-US">1997</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月开始修炼法轮功，一九九九年七月二十日中共江泽民集团发动了对法轮功的大迫害，孔祥银多次遭中共人员骚扰、抄家、勒索、绑架、关押等迫害。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">2023</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">23</span>日，孔祥银女士在早市宣传法轮功时，被人举报，她被奎文东关派出所、奎文公安国保抓捕，孔祥银被关押到看守所。之后被构陷到奎文区检察院、法院，<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">26</span>日遭庭审，被判刑<span lang="EN-US">2</span>年，罚金五千元。刑期至：<span lang="EN-US">2025</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">22</span>日。<span lang="EN-US" style="color: #00b050;"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p>
