[四川成都基督徒遭当局迫害 有家无法回](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2023/06/blog-post_71.html)
------

<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">7</span>日，本网获悉：四川成都秋雨圣约教会的基督徒遭当局迫害，有家无法回。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2023</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">6</span>日傍晚，居住在成都双流蓝光长岛国际的基督徒当日被小区物业阻止回到家中。秋雨教会牧者同工、法务人员前去探望祷告安慰。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">物业公司人员纠集街道社区雇佣的不明身份人士、网格员，聚集围攻居住此地的基督徒兄弟姐妹。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">一些被雇佣的黑恶歹徒疯狂殴打基督徒、律师。其中一位姐妹受到惨忍的殴打与极端下流的侮辱。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">律师被恶毒的歹徒追打，在生命受到严重侵害的情况下奔跑，这些被雇佣的恶毒的歹徒喊叫称律师是小偷，以便引起混乱让无辜的人们加入殴打律师。此时基督徒们拨打<span lang="EN-US">110</span>求救已过去半个小时仍没有警察出警。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">律师、基督徒被打伤。从晚上<span lang="EN-US">7</span>点多，被拦阻不能进入小区的弟兄就打<span lang="EN-US">110</span>报警，期间也有其他人报警，但是警察一直没有处警。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">居住在此小区的还有几位基督徒也被物业阻止回到自己的家。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体;
