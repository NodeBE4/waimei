mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">呼吁书中董广平家人向越南政府和中国政府紧急请求提供有关他下落的信息，并允许他前往加拿大与家人团聚</span><span style="font-size: 12pt;">。</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年出生，河南省郑州市人，人权捍卫者。曾为警察，民主异议人士，“郑州十君子”之一，河南六四公祭参与者。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年，曾因参与民主政治活动被当局于以“煽动颠覆国家政权罪”判处有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">3</span>年；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2004</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年出狱后仍恪守民主理念，要求当局平反六四，走民主宪政之路；<span lang="EN-US">2014</span>年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">2</span>日，因其亲赴在赵紫阳故乡河南滑县举行的“六四公祭”活动，随于<span lang="EN-US"> 2014</span>年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">26</span>日—<span lang="EN-US">27</span>日被河南省洛阳市警方传唤、抄家，并被郑州市警方以“涉嫌聚众扰乱公共场所秩序罪”刑拘；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">2</span>日，又被郑州市警方更换罪名，以涉嫌“寻衅滋事罪”正式逮捕，并被关押于河南省郑州市第三看守所；<span lang="EN-US">2015</span>年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">11</span>日，被取保获释；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2015</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月，因其一家逃往泰国避难，<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">28</span>日被泰国警方在其持有联合国难民署签发的“庇护申请者证明书”（实为保护信）的前提下又以涉嫌“偷渡罪”抓捕，并关押于泰国曼谷市沙吞移民监狱；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">后经各方民主人士努力和紧急呼吁，被联合国难民署获悉，于<span lang="EN-US">2015</span>年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">6</span>日答应拟启动紧急通道，力促使其全家获准难民身份；但<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">13</span>日仍被中共当局以“非法越境罪”遣送押解回国。后被羁押于重庆市第二看守所，家属未收到任何通知书。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">13</span>日重庆市第一中级法院对董广平宣判，认定犯煽动颠覆国家政权罪，判处董广平有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">3</span>年，剥夺政治权利<span lang="EN-US">1</span>年，犯偷越国境罪，判处有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">10</span>个月，并处罚金一千元，决定执行有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">3</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月，并处罚金一千元。刑期至<span lang="EN-US">2019</span>年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">2</span>日。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;
