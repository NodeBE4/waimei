[辽宁省大连市法轮功学员、八旬老人刘希永在狱中被迫害致死](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2022/01/blog-post_88.html)

<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">21</span>日，本网获悉：辽宁省大连市法轮功学员、八旬老人刘希永于<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">29</span>日在中共狱中被迫害致死<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">刘希永：</span></b><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">1941</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年出生，又名刘喜勇、刘希勇，辽宁省大连市金州人，大连市金州区农民，法轮功信仰者，中国曾押良心犯。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">自<span lang="EN-US">1997</span>年始信仰、练习法轮功以来，频遭当局打击迫害，曾被非法劳教<span lang="EN-US">2</span>年，判刑<span lang="EN-US">3</span>年半；羁押、劳教期间，曾惨遭酷刑折磨，蹲小号、睡死人床长达四十多天，绝食抗议<span lang="EN-US">60</span>余天，被野蛮灌食（尤其灌浓盐水，使其痛苦不堪，浑身发抖、全身缩成一团）、吃不明药物，导致肚子鼓胀，生活长期不能自理，双腿不能正常行走；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">出狱后，他坚持信仰法轮功，<span lang="EN-US">2017</span>年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">26</span>日，当他在大连市金州区石河集市上发放法轮功真相册子时，又被石河派出所警方传唤，<span lang="EN-US">5</span>天后再次被警方从家中带走，直接送到大连市金州区三里看守所羁押，但因年事已高，体弱多病，而被金州区看守所多次拒收；后仍被大连市金州区检察院批捕；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2017</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">24</span>日，其案被大连市金州区法院开庭审理，当庭即被以“利用邪教组织破坏法律实施罪”速判处有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">3</span>年（但其判决书显示签署日期却是<span lang="EN-US">2017</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">19</span>日，故知其案在开庭前就已被判）；其不服上诉；后经大连市中级法院二审裁决，维持原判，但暂未执行，直至<span lang="EN-US">2018</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">9</span>日被强制收监、执行；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2021</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">9</span>日理应刑满出狱，但因拒绝认罪和拒不放弃对法轮功信仰追求，又被大连市金州区警方从监狱直接送到金州区三里看守所继续关押，后再次被金州区法院枉法冤判有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">4</span>年；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2021</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">3</span>日，其因胸积水而被看守所方送到金州区中医医院救治；同年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">28</span>日，被转送到监狱羁押；并因其拒不接受思想转化，而报复性声称：“有一口气就不能让他回家”；<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">29</span>日，终被迫害致死。</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;">死前在大连市第三监狱</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;">服刑。</span></p><p class="MsoNormal"></p><div class="separator" style="clear: both;
