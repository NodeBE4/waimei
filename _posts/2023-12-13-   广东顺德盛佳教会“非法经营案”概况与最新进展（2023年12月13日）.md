<p><span></span></p><a name='more'></a><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;"><br /></span><p></p><p><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">（维权网信息中心报道）</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">2023</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">年</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">12</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">月</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">1</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">、<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">24</span>日，广东顺德盛佳教会被顺德公安、民宗、教育、工商等多部门冲击，邓燕祥传道、朱龙江执事、王伟才执事、朱巧玲姊妹被顺德公安以涉嫌非法经营罪抓捕，两处共学点也被上百号工作人员冲击，当天拉走了大量书籍、桌椅等物品。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">、同年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">9</span>日，朱龙飞长老被顺德公安传唤，并以涉嫌非法经营罪拘捕。至此顺德盛佳教会有五位同工被抓，其中朱巧玲姊妹、朱龙江执事、朱龙飞长老是三姐弟，朱家在大陆的儿女都被抓，剩下有心脏疾病的朱龙飞长老的妻子梁丹丹师母照顾年迈病重的公公婆婆。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">3</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">、朱龙飞长老、邓燕祥传道、朱龙江执事、王伟才执事、朱巧玲姊妹被羁押期间，他们的妻儿、老小常常以泪洗面，其中朱家三姐弟的母亲在<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月份病危住院，律师们多次给朱家三姐弟申请取保候审都被拒绝。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">4</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">、<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">30</span>日，朱龙飞长老与前期被羁押的邓燕祥传道、朱龙江执事、王伟才执事、朱巧玲姊妹的非法经营案并为一案，由顺德区检察院向顺德区人民法院提起公诉。教会内部印刷使用的灵修材料（《每日读经释义》）被冠以“非法经营罪”，且被定性为“”印刷、发行严重危害社会秩序和扰乱市场秩序的非法出版物，情节还特别严重”。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"></p><div class="separator" style="clear: both;
