[台湾双北23万户遭遇无预警停电](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210928-1198003)
------

<div>
<p>台湾双北部分地区今天清晨无预警停电。台电表示，因变电所变压器跳脱，造成共23万余户停电，经台电人员抢修，已于上午8时全数复电。</p><p>根据中央社报道，台湾网民在各大面簿群组、论坛等表示，约凌晨4时50分台北市、新北市无预警停电，因此睡到一半被热醒，或是追剧到一半突然全黑，停电范围包含台北市士林、社子，以及新北三重、芦洲等。</p><p>台电表示，今天清晨4时51分芦洲一次变电所主变压器跳脱，影响新北市芦洲区、五股区、三重区等一带共23万多户停电，台电随即派员转供及抢修，5时17分陆续复电；截至6时30分，主要剩下台北市社子地区尚有1万9千户持续抢修中，但已于8时全部复电。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>台电强调，此事故造成民众不便，台电深感歉意，后续将强化供电设备巡视检修，努力减少停电事故发生。<br> </p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/zbpe-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=zbpe399&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_desktop-zbpe399_2.png?dHEiGouxxZpWwWTJE04dyGyUN74JOU2z" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_desktop-zbpe399_2.png?dHEiGouxxZpWwWTJE04dyGyUN74JOU2z" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/zbpe-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=zbpe399&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_mobile-zbpe399_2.png?qZnBcIGCJvPR8A2IAZOZuOxdoGZDatiI" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_mobile-zbpe399_2.png?qZnBcIGCJvPR8A2IAZOZuOxdoGZDatiI" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
