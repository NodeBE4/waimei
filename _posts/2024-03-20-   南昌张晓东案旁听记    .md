[南昌张晓东案旁听记](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2024/03/blog-post_20.html)
------

<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">2024</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">19</span>日上午，张晓东寻衅滋事案在南昌市青山湖区法院开庭公开审理。本人拿到了旁听证，见证了该案审理全过程。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">张晓东与辩护律师均申请法官、公诉人回避，法官强行推进庭审。举证阶段，被告人要求公诉人一一展示所指控的<span lang="EN-US">488</span>条网络虚假信息，公诉人以避免二次传播为由予以拒绝。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">被告人当庭指出，该案侦查程序违法，起诉违法，是侵犯公民言论自由违法审判，害怕真相是所有独裁者的特征。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">该案庭审持续到近晚上九点。法院以张晓东的妻子曾经做过证人为由拒绝其进入法庭旁听。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">庭审期间，法院内外如临大敌，警察国保纷纷出动，很多前来旁听的南昌公民被强制带离法院。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"></p><div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;"><a href="https://blogger.googleusercontent.com/img/b/R29vZ2xl/AVvXsEhIZGFIu5fo0GUrjpM2Y0V76bjhXl33cM-PM4PXlLlW6-wf1w3SeiKs725lIajfjm5ytL0F3cYbDyL4HjtNxMSdiHmG0c6odlIwBrRhsxxQ6gyqiF6b5LQVTv8KJwpU1RVRjdokOz0i-lsQaTbhLJQP83PJ3ghrtpKkBOdRTUMD0Z4e_CbYjERKFbXI9pKL/s320/GJAXV1FbsAEdezB.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;"><img border="0" data-original-height="320" data-original-width="318" height="320" src="https://blogger.googleusercontent.com/img/b/R29vZ2xl/AVvXsEhIZGFIu5fo0GUrjpM2Y0V76bjhXl33cM-PM4PXlLlW6-wf1w3SeiKs725lIajfjm5ytL0F3cYbDyL4HjtNxMSdiHmG0c6odlIwBrRhsxxQ6gyqiF6b5LQVTv8KJwpU1RVRjdokOz0i-lsQaTbhLJQP83PJ3ghrtpKkBOdRTUMD0Z4e_CbYjERKFbXI9pKL/s1600/GJAXV1FbsAEdezB.jpg" width="318" /></a></div><p></p>
