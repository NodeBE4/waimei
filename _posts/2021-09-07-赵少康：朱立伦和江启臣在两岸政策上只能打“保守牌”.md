[赵少康：朱立伦和江启臣在两岸政策上只能打“保守牌”](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210907-1191214)
------

<div>
<p>台湾资深媒体人赵少康认为，国民党党主席竞选人朱立伦和江启臣，在政见发表会发表的关于两岸政策的说法都四平八稳，基本上就是延续台湾前总统马英九的两岸政策。现在国民党谈论两岸政策很困难，毕竟台湾反中意识高涨。朱立伦和江启臣都不敢讲，只能打“保守牌”，这很正常。</p><p>据《自由时报》报道，赵少康指出，国民党员分成三大类，一类是黄复兴党员，一类是地方派系，另一类则是自主党员，党主席选举就是诉求这三种人，因此两岸政策不能太跳脱，要从党员的角度出发。</p><p>赵少康认为朱、江关于两岸政策的说法四平八稳，基本上就是延续马英九的政策。他说，现在国民党谈论两岸政策很困难，毕竟台湾反中意识高涨。国民党应该在亲中与反中之间，要找到第三条路，但朱立伦和江启臣两人现在都不敢讲，只能打“保守牌”，这很正常。因为要先赢得党主席选举，否则就会失败。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/zbpe-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=zbpe399&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_desktop-zbpe399_2.png?dHEiGouxxZpWwWTJE04dyGyUN74JOU2z" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_desktop-zbpe399_2.png?dHEiGouxxZpWwWTJE04dyGyUN74JOU2z" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/zbpe-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=zbpe399&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_mobile-zbpe399_2.png?qZnBcIGCJvPR8A2IAZOZuOxdoGZDatiI" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_mobile-zbpe399_2.png?qZnBcIGCJvPR8A2IAZOZuOxdoGZDatiI" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
