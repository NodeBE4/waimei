<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">10</span>日，本网获悉：获刑<span lang="EN-US">7</span>年的的河北唐山法轮功学员田世胜因狱中患重病被突然保外就医。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">唐山市开平区越河乡西塔村法轮功学员田世胜、田淑兰夫妇，<span lang="EN-US">2019</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">3</span>日早晨被警察闯入家中抓捕，两人被秘密开庭，田世胜被判刑<span lang="EN-US">7</span>年、田淑兰<span lang="EN-US">5</span>年。田世胜于<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月被关押到河北省监狱管理局冀东监狱。田淑兰于<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月被绑架至河北省女子监狱。田世胜刑期至：<span lang="EN-US">2026</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">2</span>日。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">田世胜今年<span lang="EN-US">65</span>岁，河北省唐山市开平区越河乡西塔村人。自<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月开始，田世胜就出现了便脓、便血现象，无人关注。直到<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年的<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月，情况越来越严重，田世胜开始向监狱管理人员（主要是监区长）反映，要求做一下身体检查。可狱方一直拖延推诿。直到<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月，狱方在田世胜再三要求，甚至要向上级反映的情况下，才带他到监狱外边的医院做了检查。后来，狱方又安排田世胜到唐山市协和医院检查身体，一共九天，却没给用任何药，没有任何治疗。狱方把他带回监狱后，没告诉他检查结果，只是把他转到了病号队。<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">6</span>日，田世胜却突然被冀东监狱保外就医。据知情人透露，“他肠子上长满了瘤子”。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p>
