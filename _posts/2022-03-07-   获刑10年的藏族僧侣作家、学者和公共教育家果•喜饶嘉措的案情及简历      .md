[获刑10年的藏族僧侣作家、学者和公共教育家果•喜饶嘉措的案情及简历](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2022/03/10.html)

<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">7</span>日，本网获悉获刑<span lang="EN-US">10</span>年的藏族僧侣作家、学者和公共教育家果•喜饶嘉措的案情及简历，现通报如下：<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">果·喜饶嘉措：</span></b><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">1976</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">9</span>日出生，四川省阿坝藏族羌族自治州阿坝县人，藏名<span lang="EN-US">Go Sherab Gyatso</span>（</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: &quot;Microsoft Himalaya&quot;; font-size: 12.0pt;">སྒོ་ཤེས་རབ་རྒྱ་མཚོ།</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">），知名藏族僧侣作家、学者和公共教育家，藏族持不同政见者，人权捍卫者，中国在押政治犯。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">自幼即在四川省阿坝藏族羌族自治州阿坝县的格尔登寺（即该地区规模最大的藏传佛教格鲁派寺院）出家为僧，后又到西藏自治区拉萨市的哲蚌寺和色拉寺学习佛法，进行深造；曾因长期维护西藏地区的环境、宗教、语言和文化，以及出版过多本有关佛教哲学和西藏的传统、文化方面的书籍，而深受年轻一代藏民的喜爱与尊敬，但却引起中共当局的不悦、警觉和伺机迫害；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">1998</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">20</span>日，曾因参加反抗中共当局对阿坝地区僧侣强制实施的“爱国爱教”教育活动，而被四川当局拘捕，后被判刑<span lang="EN-US">3</span>年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>个月，送四川省绵阳监狱服刑；曾在狱中受虐囚，且感染慢性肺病，至今未能治愈；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2008</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年奥运会在北京召开前，曾因藏区（主要是西藏拉萨）发生藏人大规模抗议游行事件，而又遭中共西藏当局拘捕，并被判刑<span lang="EN-US">1</span>年；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2011</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年，曾因发文批评中共政府镇压藏人同胞和实施高压强权残暴政策等，以及在青海省西宁市出版相关书籍，而再次被四川省国安局警方跨省拘捕，并被羁押于成都市某看守所；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">获释后，<span lang="EN-US">2013</span>年<span lang="EN-US">-2016</span>年，曾为继续致力于对藏传佛教文化进行研究和著书立说，而广游和拜访了藏区的多家寺院，并到广东中山大学学习西方哲学史等，此阶段，其行动尚且平安；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">但<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">26</span>日，当其在成都为以前狱中所患慢性肺病定期就医时，却被西藏自治区国安局警方以涉嫌“煽动分裂国家罪”刑拘；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2021</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月，其被西藏自治区拉萨市法院以“煽动颠覆国家政权罪”开庭审判，至同年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月，终被重判有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">10</span>年，刑期至<span lang="EN-US">2030</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">25</span>日；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2022</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月获悉，其因在狱中遭酷刑虐待导致身体健康严重恶化，<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">9</span>日，国际人权组织“人权观察”随发表声明，要求中共当局善待在押政治犯，立即无条件将其释放，让其获得尽快有效治疗。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">目前在拉萨市曲水监狱服刑。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"></p><div class="separator" style="clear: both;
