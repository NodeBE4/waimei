font-size: 12pt;">年</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">10</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">月</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">31</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">覃永沛煽动颠覆国家政权案从<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">4</span>日起诉算起，法院受理案件至今<span lang="EN-US">2</span>年多了，是严重的超期羁押。一次次所谓的拖延审判，长达几年，就是不宣判，者是对被羁押人的人权的严重践踏的违法行为。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体;
