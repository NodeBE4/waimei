[福州残障维权人士徐钟富诉镇政府不履行法定职责案开庭](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2023/06/blog-post_16.html)
------

<p><span></span></p><a name='more'></a><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;"><br /></span><p></p><p><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">（维权网信息中心报道）</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">2023</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">年</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">6</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">月</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">16</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">日，本网获悉：福建省福州市残障维权人士徐钟富诉闽侯县上街镇政府不履行政府信息公开法定职责案于、近日上午</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">9</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">点在福州市仓山区法院第六法庭开庭审理。</span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">事情经过：</span></b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">徐钟富所在的闽侯县上街镇岐头村位于此次旗山湖周边片区旧改项目房屋征收范围，然而徐却遭受不公对待。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">为了解本次征地相关信息，其于<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">14</span>日和<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">2</span>日向上街镇政府申请公开相关征地批文及安置方案等政府信息。镇政府多以其“申请属于咨询类问题，不属于政府信息公开申请”为由，不予答复；徐不服此违法行为，遂向仓山法院提起行政诉讼。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">徐钟富简介</span></b><span style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">：徐系二级肢体残障人士，宅基地权益及土地权益被剥夺维权。因与赤脚律师纪斯尊往来密切，徐于<span lang="EN-US">2014</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月被福建当局刑拘，列入“纪斯尊聚众扰乱社会秩序罪、寻衅滋事罪案”的同案人。<span lang="EN-US">2016</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">18</span>日纪斯尊遭数罪并罚，判处有期徒刑四年六个月；徐钟富被以寻衅滋事罪判处有期徒刑一年三个月，缓刑两年。徐至今申诉中。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体;
