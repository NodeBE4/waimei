[维他奶收集员工及家属资料 被指与刺警案有关](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210829-1187737)
------

<div>
<p>维他奶公司要求员工签署“个人资料处理同意书”，向员工收集资料，包括个人资历、专业协会会员资格及家庭成员等资料。香港职工盟认为这种做法罕见，怀疑与刺警案有关。</p><p>据星岛日报报道，维他奶公司的文件提到，若员工不提供资料，公司或无法支付薪酬。</p><p>维他奶公司回应传媒查询时称，公司人力资源系统近日进行电子化更新，因此需向员工确认将其个人资料转移至新系统。集团强调，一直谨慎处理员工的个人资料，所有文件经过外部律师事务所审阅和修改，符合法规。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>在7.1刺警案中，疑凶梁健辉刺伤警员后自杀身亡，梁健辉生前于维他奶公司任职采购主任。</p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
