mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">1972</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">早在<span lang="EN-US">2005</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月，即与妻子张敏共同在北京受洗成为耶和华见证人；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2011</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">-2013</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月，曾在新疆巴音州库尔勒市担任“耶和华见证人”会众长老，宣传信仰和发展信徒，并在当地的巴州医院家属院内组织上百次聚会；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2012</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月，曾因聚会点被库尔勒市民宗委查处取缔，为逃避打击而被迫每周更换地点，将长老林在务、信徒于秉汝等数人的住处作为聚会地点召集聚会<span lang="EN-US">100</span>多次，并设捐款箱鼓励信徒捐款，且历次收取信徒奉献款<span lang="EN-US">500</span>余元，其中将<span lang="EN-US">300</span>元寄给香港分部作为支撑全球传道工作，剩余奉献款用来制作书籍和宣传单张以向周边人士散发传教；同期，曾因积极宣传“政治中立”，且宣传拒绝参加所谓爱国主义教育，拒绝入党<span lang="EN-US">/</span>团、升国旗、向国旗敬礼、唱国歌和服兵役等，遂被库尔勒市民宗委再次查获，后被予以驱离；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2012</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月、<span lang="EN-US">2013</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月，曾与其妻带领数名信徒前往香港参加“耶和华见证人”香港分部举办的区域大会；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2013</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月，曾因其夫妇二人在库尔勒市楼兰世家组织聚会，而被民宗委再次查获并被迫离开该市，后于<span lang="EN-US">2014</span>年到韩国参加区域大会；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2018</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">5</span>日，因积极宣传和发展耶和华见证人，并组织会众聚会和传道数百次，而被巴音州库尔勒市警方以涉嫌“组织、利用邪教组织破坏法律实施罪”刑拘，并送库尔勒市看守所关押；同年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">10</span>日，又与同是“耶和华见证人”教会长老林在务、李义芳、林浩、姜锡俊和信徒林秉汝等<span lang="EN-US">18</span>人被库尔勒市检察院以同罪名正式批捕；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2019</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">24</span>日，其案被库尔勒市检察院提起公诉，指控其因与多名耶和华见证人长老和信徒违反国家宗教法规，“建立库尔勒会众体系，发展成员<span lang="EN-US">63</span>人，蛊惑、煽动信徒不入党，不入团，不服兵役，不升国旗，不向国旗敬礼，不唱国歌，不参加选举，与境外人员勾结，利用定期聚会控制信徒，借机敛财，影响群众的正常宗教信仰，扰乱社会秩序，破坏法律实施”，故建议量刑严判；同年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">-10</span>月，其及另<span lang="EN-US">17</span>人均被巴音州库尔勒市法院三次庭审，至<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">13</span>日终被一审以“组织、利用邪教组织破坏法律实施罪”统一判刑<span lang="EN-US">2</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月至<span lang="EN-US">6</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月不等，其中，其被重判处有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">6</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月，并处罚金<span lang="EN-US">3</span>万元；其等不服，均表上诉；后经巴音郭楞蒙古自治州中级法院二审裁决：驳回上诉，维持原判，刑期至<span lang="EN-US">2025</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">4</span>日。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;
