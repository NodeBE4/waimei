[著名反腐记者上官云开已被以涉嫌销售假药罪刑事拘留 律师会见遭拒](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2023/04/blog-post_38.html)
------

<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">25</span>日，本网获悉：身居湖北武汉的著名反腐记者上官云开<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">20</span>日上午突然与外界失去联系，现已证实上官云开已被湖北鄂州市公安局以涉嫌销售假药罪刑事拘留，目前羁押在鄂州市第一看守所。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">上官云开于<span lang="EN-US">1966</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">15</span>日生于湖北荆门，祖籍广东梅县，属于客家人。曾担任《法治日报》驻湖北记者站站长，著有《让弱者哭出声》、《珠海的爱与痛》等书，在其创作生涯当中，曾使近<span lang="EN-US">300</span>名官员、黑恶分子受到处理。他是于<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">18</span>日从澳大利亚返国，随后遭抓捕。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">据上官云开的儿子上官许可说，<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">25</span>日<span lang="EN-US">15:30</span>代理律师林辉律师在委托材料齐全的情况下要求会见，看守所接待人员经电脑查询明确表明因有指示备注不允许任何人会见上官云开。当日，林辉律师已经向市公安局、市检察院依法提出控告，要求按照刑诉法<span lang="EN-US">48</span>小时内安排会见。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">而李庆亮律师发视频解读了著名反腐斗士上官云开记者因涉嫌销售假药罪被刑事拘留一案，他认为本案是司法机关选择报复性执法。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体;
