<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">11</span>日，本网获悉香港女人权捍卫者、香港大律师邹幸彤的案情及简历，现通报如下：<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">邹幸彤：</span></b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">女，<span lang="EN-US">1985</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">24</span>日出生，英语名：<span lang="EN-US">Tonyee Chow Hang-tung</span>，原英属香港籍人，现中华人民共和国（香港）公民，香港大律师，原香港支联会副主席，第<span lang="EN-US">35</span>届“中国杰出民主人士奖”荣获者，民主人权斗士，社会活动家，中国在押政治犯。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">早年曾就读于英国剑桥大学哈默顿学院物理学系，毕业后又在香港大学专业进修学院研读法律专业，而后成为一名香港执业大律师；自<span lang="EN-US">2016</span>年起，因致力于中国的民主人权事业， 随开始担任香港支联会（即香港市民支持爱国民主运动联合会）副主席，期间，曾积极推动和举办了每年的维多利亚公园（简称“维园”）<span lang="EN-US">64</span>纪念烛光晚会；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2020</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年，当李卓人、何俊仁等香港民主前辈和支联会高层相继 遭遇中共香港政府的迫害身陷囹圄时，其勇敢的站出来，成为继续组织香港市民纪念<span lang="EN-US">64</span>活动的主要领导者；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2021</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">4</span>日，在中共主导下的港版“国安法”管控下，支联会被香港警方以防疫（<span lang="EN-US">2019</span>冠状病毒疾病，<span lang="EN-US">COVID-19</span>）为由禁止在维园举行当晚的悼念<span lang="EN-US">64 </span>烛光晚会；针此，支联会立即提起上诉，但因上诉很快被驳回，故其公开表态自己会以个人名义到场纪念；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">次日，即被警方以涉嫌“宣传未经批准集结”为由拘捕；同年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">5</span>日，暂获取保候审，但<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">8</span>日，又因拒绝在限期内向香港国安处提交支联会的文件而再被港警拘捕；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">次日，又被以涉嫌“煽动颠覆国家政权罪”和支联会主席李卓人、副主席何俊仁等共计<span lang="EN-US">8</span>人一起正式起诉，被指控因在社交网络和媒体发表意在鼓动民众参与未经批准的六四集会言论，其等言行已触犯港版《国安法》的相关法令，故应予量刑；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2021</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月，其一行<span lang="EN-US">8</span>人案被香港法院统一宣判，其中，其被以“煽动颠覆国家政权罪”和<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年“煽动他人参与纪念<span lang="EN-US">64</span>集会罪”判处有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">1</span>年（<span lang="EN-US">12</span>个月）；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2022</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">4</span>日，又被香港法院以<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年、<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年两次 “煽动他人参与纪念六四集会罪” 判处有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">1</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>个月（<span lang="EN-US">15</span>个月）；因先后两次判决的一部分刑期同时执行，故其最后实际执行刑期<span lang="EN-US"> 1</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>个月（<span lang="EN-US">22</span>个月 ）；据悉，庭审中其拒绝认罪，坚称其发帖与撰文旨在呼吁人们勿忘六四，并非鼓动众人聚集闹事。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体;
