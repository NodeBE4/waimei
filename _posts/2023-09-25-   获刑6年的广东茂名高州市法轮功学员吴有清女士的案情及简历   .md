<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">25</span>日，本网获悉获刑<span lang="EN-US">6</span>年的广东茂名高州市法轮功学员吴有清女士的案情及简历，现在通报如下：<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">吴有清：女，广东茂名高州市法轮功学员，<span lang="EN-US">1967</span>年生，她于<span lang="EN-US">1997</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月开始修炼法轮功，原</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 16px;">农村信用银行员工</span><span face="宋体">。</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;">在中共集团迫害法轮功的二十四年中，吴有清遭到残酷的迫害，多次被非法关押，还曾被非法劳教<span lang="EN-US">3</span>年，狱警强迫吴有清脱光衣服羞辱。劳教期间被农村信用银行无理开除。后来吴有清被判刑四年。<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">26</span>日晚，吴有清外出时，被高州市公安局国保便衣和警察抓捕，后被抄家。吴有清被关在在离高州<span lang="EN-US">50</span>公里的茂名信宜看守所。她在看守所绝食反迫害，身体虚弱，<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月中旬被送医抢救。<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">21</span>日，家属接到茂名第一看守所的电话，得知吴有清已被转送到茂名第一看守所。<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">22</span>日上午，吴有清被茂名市茂南区法院在茂名市第一看守所宣判：吴有清被判刑<span lang="EN-US">6</span>年、罚金<span lang="EN-US">20000</span>元。刑期至：<span lang="EN-US">2028</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">26</span>日。</span></p>
