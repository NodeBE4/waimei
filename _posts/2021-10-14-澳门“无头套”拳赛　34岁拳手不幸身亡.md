[澳门“无头套”拳赛　34岁拳手不幸身亡](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20211014-1203260)
------

<div>
<p>澳门发生拳赛打死人事件，两名泰拳爱好者相约打友谊赛，协议不戴头套，结果一人面部中拳、严重脑出血，留医后不治，对手被捕。</p><p>据香港01报道，澳门警方通报，事发于本月4日，34岁死者与28岁姓杨拳手为朋友关系，两人均有多年拳击经验。当日下午，两人相约在氹仔某拳馆进行友谊比赛，场内有安排合资格的教练作拳证。</p><p>比赛前，双方协议只配戴牙套、拳套及脚套，但没有配戴头套。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>比赛过程中，死者左脸中拳后跌坐地上，拳证马上叫停比赛。其后，死者在擂台下休息期间突然晕倒，在场人士随即报警并将事主送院抢救。</p><p>经仁伯爵综合医院医生诊断，发现死者右脑有血肿及严重脑出血，在接受脑部手术后转往深切治疗部留医，昨天（13日）下午4时50分抢救无效死亡。</p><p>澳门司警根据调查，以严重伤害身体完整性罪将杨姓拳手移交检察院处理。</p><div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>            <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/zbpe-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=zbpe399&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_desktop-zbpe399_2.png?dHEiGouxxZpWwWTJE04dyGyUN74JOU2z" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_desktop-zbpe399_2.png?dHEiGouxxZpWwWTJE04dyGyUN74JOU2z" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/zbpe-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=zbpe399&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_mobile-zbpe399_2.png?qZnBcIGCJvPR8A2IAZOZuOxdoGZDatiI" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_mobile-zbpe399_2.png?qZnBcIGCJvPR8A2IAZOZuOxdoGZDatiI" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
