[河北卢廷阁律师：举报河北省司法厅/违法设立省直所（2021年9月1日）](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2021/09/202191.html)
------

<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">河北省司法厅至今还保留着<span lang="EN-US">24</span>家由自己直接管理的律师事务所，即所谓的“省直所”，拥有律师约<span lang="EN-US">2400</span>多人。律所每年交纳会费约<span lang="EN-US">20000</span>元，律师每年交纳会费约<span lang="EN-US">2000</span>元。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">据《律师法》<span lang="EN-US">18</span>条、<span lang="EN-US">24</span>条，省司法厅没有直接受理律师事务所设立、年度考核的权力，跳过市级司法局，自己直接批准设立、考核的“省直所”，涉嫌违法。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">据司法部《律师事务所管理办法》<span lang="EN-US">64</span>条—<span lang="EN-US">66</span>条<span lang="EN-US"><span style="mso-spacerun: yes;">&nbsp; </span></span>省司法厅的法定职责中，没有对律师事务所的直接管理权，所谓“省司法厅直接管理”，没有法律依据和授权，涉嫌违规。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">全国的“省直所”大量、长期、违法地存在着，此顽疾，应该根除，现郑重举报。呵呵！<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体;
