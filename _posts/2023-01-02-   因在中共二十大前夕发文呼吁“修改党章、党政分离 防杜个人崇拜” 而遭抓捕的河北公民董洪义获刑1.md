<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">2</span>日，本网获悉：因在中共二十大前夕发文呼吁“修改党章、党政分离 防杜个人崇拜”而遭抓捕的河北公民董洪义的获刑<span lang="EN-US">1</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月：<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">1945</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">自<span lang="EN-US">1983</span>年成为中共党员起，一直关注中共党组织的建设与发展，并认同和主张中共要实行国家民主化管理；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">近年来，曾因不满习近平主政以来祸国倒行逆施，而在网上经常发一些批评“党和国家领导人，以及一些政府职能部门”等文字，并与同事、友人经常在网上评论国内外政治、经济、社会等形势讯息，遂频遭邯郸当局监控打压，并被数次带走传唤；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2022</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月，中共“<span lang="EN-US">20</span>大”前期，因现任中共总书记习近平欲在“<span lang="EN-US">20</span>大”召开期间搞个人崇拜，以求长期连任，其遂与另<span lang="EN-US">2</span>位中共老党员（田奇庄和马贵全）拟联名发表建议书，建议“<span lang="EN-US">20</span>大”上修改党章，指称：党章里“党政军民学，东西南北中，党是领导一切的”这种毛式文革用语纯属违宪，应予以删除；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2022</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">后又因其接受外媒采访，同年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">31</span>日，其等先后被邯郸市警方以涉嫌“勾结外国势力”罪由正式刑拘；后其与田奇庄被邯郸市检察院以同罪由正式批捕；同年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">6</span>日获悉，其<span lang="EN-US">2</span>人案宗已被邯郸市检察院移交到邯郸市法院，并应于本月庭审；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">据悉，其家人已帮其找到代理辩护律师，但律师被有关部门告知说“可以走法律程序、参与辩护，但不会起什么作用，具体怎么判，是上面已经定好的”。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体;
