[因合法销售圣经播放器而遭抓捕的傅炫娟、邓天永、冯群豪、韩丽等四基督徒获刑6年至1年3个月](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2021/07/613.html)

<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">30</span>日，本网获悉：因合法销售圣经播放器而遭抓捕的傅炫娟、邓天永、</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 16px;">冯群豪</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 16px;">、</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">韩丽等四基督徒获刑</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">6</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">年至</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">1</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">年</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">3</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">个月。</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2020</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">2</span>日，中共当局抓捕了合法销售圣经播放器的傅炫娟、邓天永、韩丽、冯群豪等四基督徒，<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">16</span>日，宝安区法院认定傅炫娟、邓天永、韩丽、冯群豪等四基督徒犯“非法经营罪”成立，宣判：傅炫娟有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">6</span>年，并处罚金<span lang="EN-US">20</span>万元；邓天永<span lang="EN-US">3</span>年有期徒刑，并处罚金<span lang="EN-US">5</span>万元；冯群豪<span lang="EN-US">2</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月有期徒刑，并处罚金<span lang="EN-US">3</span>万元；韩丽<span lang="EN-US">1</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>个月有期徒刑，并处罚金<span lang="EN-US">1</span>万元；另外没收六种型号播放机、电脑主机<span lang="EN-US">2</span>台；其它电脑<span lang="EN-US">4</span>台。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">傅炫娟、邓天永、韩丽、冯群豪所在的企业深圳市生命树文化传播有限公司是一家成立于<span lang="EN-US">2011</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月的合法企业。其公司简介是这样写的：本公司依托于<span lang="EN-US">1996</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">5</span>日年成立的译讯通电子有限公司，持续开发具有自主知识产权的先进多媒体数码终端和通讯技术产品，具有多年电子研发经验，依托深圳数码高科技领域的成熟产业链，面向全球需求，以丰富和充实群众文化生活并实现出口创汇和业价值。致力于圣经文化传播，可根据客户需求提供适合的内容，如赞美诗或经典的讲道等之类的，目前已与国内外多间有影响力教会及书店、机构和代理商等建立良好关系。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">这样一家合法企业因销售圣经播放器，竟然导致傅炫娟、邓天永、韩丽、冯群豪等四基督徒被逮捕。中共当局于<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">2</span>日，在深圳宝安区西乡街渔村横林大厦<span lang="EN-US">701A</span>单元以所谓非法经营罪将傅炫娟、邓天永、韩丽、冯群豪抓走。<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">3</span>日，四人因涉嫌“非法经营”被深圳市宝安分局公安局拘留；经宝安区检察院批准，被认定“非法经营”，<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">6</span>日被深圳市公安局宝安分局逮捕。目前傅炫娟、邓天永、韩丽、冯群豪等<span lang="EN-US">4</span>位基督徒都被关押在深圳九围看守所。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">深圳市“生命树科技发展有限公司”的法人代表傅炫娟等四人被控“非法经营”案的庭审已于<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">9</span>日在宝安区法院举行，一直未宣判。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">宝安区检察院的起诉书认定，<span lang="EN-US">2018</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月至<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月，深圳市生命树科技发展有限公司涉嫌非法经营金额为<span lang="EN-US">3661847.84</span>元。检察院认定，傅炫娟，邓天永，冯群豪，韩丽违反了《中华人民共和国刑法》第二百二十五条，应追究其非法经营的刑事责任，建议判处傅炫娟有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">5</span>年，邓天永<span lang="EN-US">3</span>年有期徒刑和罚款，冯群豪<span lang="EN-US">3</span>年有期徒刑和罚款，韩丽<span lang="EN-US">1</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月有期徒刑和罚款。他们家人和律师都被威胁，不得透露案情。目前未有判决消息。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">四人简历如下：<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">傅炫娟，<span lang="EN-US">1978</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">20</span>日出生，汉族，中专学历，原籍地是广西壮族自治区北海市合浦县常乐镇大教村；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">邓天永，<span lang="EN-US">1971</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">4</span>日出生，汉族，中学学历，籍贯为广东省中山市沙溪镇汇源社区居委会第<span lang="EN-US">40</span>组；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">冯群豪，<span lang="EN-US">1976</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">13</span>日出生，汉族，初中毕业，住所地为广东省肇庆市四会市东城街道陶冲村；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">韩丽，<span lang="EN-US">1981</span>年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">14</span>日出生，大学本科学历，籍贯海南省文昌市白秋二村。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">合法的生产经营活动仅仅因为是涉及圣经，<span lang="EN-US">4</span>基督徒竟然被以商业罪名抓捕判刑，这简直是赤裸裸的宗教迫害。对傅炫娟、邓天永、韩丽、冯群豪<span lang="EN-US">4</span>位基督徒的境况，本网将持续关注。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"></p><div class="separator" style="clear: both;
