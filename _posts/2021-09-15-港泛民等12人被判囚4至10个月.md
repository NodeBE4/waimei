[港泛民等12人被判囚4至10个月](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210915-1193809)
------

<div>
<p>包括支联会副主席何俊仁在内的香港12名泛民人士，因去年参与一项未经批准的集会，今天分别被判囚4至10个月，其中三人获准缓刑。</p><p>综合网媒“香港01”、《明报》、东网等报道，这12人上周已承认控罪，当中何俊仁，以及前民间人权阵线召集人陈皓桓等七人，承认煽惑他人明知而参与未经批准集结罪。</p><p>他们七人也连同前香港立法会议员梁国雄等五人，承认明知而参与未经批准集结罪。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>据报道，支联会去年6月4日在港岛维多利亚公园举办集会，尽管这项活动不获警方批出不反对通知书，但仍有不少人参与，当中包括26名泛民人士。</p><p>为此，他们早前分别被警方提控，其中黄之锋、岑敖晖、袁嘉蔚，以及梁凯晴，已分别承认一项明知而参与未经批准集结罪，被判监4至10个月不等。<br> </p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/zbpe-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=zbpe399&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_desktop-zbpe399_2.png?dHEiGouxxZpWwWTJE04dyGyUN74JOU2z" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_desktop-zbpe399_2.png?dHEiGouxxZpWwWTJE04dyGyUN74JOU2z" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/zbpe-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=zbpe399&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_mobile-zbpe399_2.png?qZnBcIGCJvPR8A2IAZOZuOxdoGZDatiI" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_mobile-zbpe399_2.png?qZnBcIGCJvPR8A2IAZOZuOxdoGZDatiI" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
