font-family: 宋体; mso-bidi-font-family: 宋体;"><o:p></o:p></span></p><p class="MsoPlainText" style="text-indent: 24.0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0;"><span style="font-size: 12.0pt; font-family: 宋体; mso-bidi-font-family: 宋体;">据罗桑介绍，通过供应商，新工厂将设在靠近新加坡的廖内群岛省<span lang="EN-US">(Kepulauan Riau)</span>的巴淡岛。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoPlainText"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12.0pt; font-family: 宋体; mso-bidi-font-family: 宋体;"><o:p> </o:p></span></p><p class="MsoPlainText" style="text-indent: 24.0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0;"><span style="font-size: 12.0pt; font-family: 宋体; mso-bidi-font-family: 宋体;">苹果高管<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">7</span>日访问印度尼西亚，并与该国相关部长就未来的投资进行了多次会谈。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoPlainText"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12.0pt; font-family: 宋体; mso-bidi-font-family: 宋体;"><o:p></o:p></span></p><p class="MsoPlainText" style="text-indent: 24.0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0;"><span style="font-size: 12.0pt; font-family: 宋体; mso-bidi-font-family: 宋体;">印度尼西亚政府因为苹果没有履行该国所规定的“当地采购义务”，未批准新款智能手机“<span lang="EN-US">iPhone16</span>”的销售。要想在该国销售智能手机，需要在当地采购<span lang="EN-US">35%</span>至<span lang="EN-US">40%</span>的零部件及软件等。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoPlainText"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12.0pt;
