<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">14</span>日，本网获悉获刑<span lang="EN-US">3</span>年的贵阳市法轮功学员陈金华的案情及简历，现在通报如下：<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">陈金华</span></b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">：贵阳市法轮功学员，男，<span lang="EN-US">1964</span>年生，现年<span lang="EN-US">60</span>岁，大学毕业，贵州平坝县黎阳厂退休职工，是一九九九年<span lang="EN-US">“</span>七<span lang="EN-US">·</span>二零<span lang="EN-US">”</span>中共迫害法轮功前修炼的老学员。迫害开始后，平坝公安警察竟然三次出面、逼迫陈金华跟妻子离婚。陈金华也多次遭绑架、劳教等迫害。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">2023</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">16</span>日，<a name="_Hlk161322844">陈金华</a>等一批贵阳法轮功学员，发放法轮功资料时，被清镇市公安抓捕。陈金华被关押在清镇市看守所。后遭刑事拘留、批捕、起诉。直到一年多以后，<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月才获悉，陈金华获刑<span lang="EN-US">3</span>年。刑期至：<span lang="EN-US">2026</span>年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">15</span>日。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p>
