[河南维权人士李玉凤被控寻衅滋事罪今获刑4年](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2021/09/4_30.html)
------

<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">30</span>日，本网获悉：河南省焦作市中站区法院今天以寻衅滋事罪将河南维权人士李玉凤判刑<span lang="EN-US">4</span>年。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">李玉凤：</span></b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">女，<span lang="EN-US">1958</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">10</span>日出生，河南省修武县人，基督徒，维权公民，中国在押维权人士。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">1990</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年代末，曾因家中惨遭两次政府不当拆迁，损失巨大，其被迫提起诉讼，打赢了官司，但因被告是当地政府，法院不予执行，故而其被迫上访维权；曾因不断维权，被当局进行恶意打击报复，并以“妨害公务罪”对其判刑<span lang="EN-US">2</span>年；出狱后其继续状告地方政府，再遭逼迫，又被劳动教养<span lang="EN-US">1</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月；两次入狱，备受酷刑折磨。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2014</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">30</span>日因与他人前往监狱迎接“新公民运动”维权代表袁冬出狱，并率先打出横幅“欢迎袁冬从小监狱回到大监狱”，又被北京大兴区警方以涉嫌“寻衅滋事罪”刑拘，同年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">6</span>日被北京市大兴区检察院批捕，至<span lang="EN-US">2015</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">5</span>日被取保候审释放；关押期间，曾被突击审讯（且大多是疲劳审讯）达<span lang="EN-US">50</span>余次，身心惨遭迫害；同年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">26</span>日，又因在北京南站照相被北京市丰台公安刑拘一个月后，交由焦作市当局带回原籍，补充侦查再次被刑拘，后转正式逮捕，被关押在河南省焦作看守所；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2017</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">16</span>日，被河南省焦作市中站区法院以“寻衅滋事罪”判刑<span lang="EN-US">4</span>年，并在郑州女子监狱服刑；<span lang="EN-US">2019</span>年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">21</span>日，刑满释放；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2019</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">5</span>日，因其与同伴封西霞到北京最高法院申诉，又到河南省法院驻京办事处反映司法腐败制造冤假错案问题，随遭焦作维稳办人员抓捕、刑拘；<span lang="EN-US">2019</span>年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">8</span>日，被焦作市警方以涉嫌“寻衅滋事罪”正式逮捕；<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月，其案已被地方检察院移送至焦作市中站区法院。今获刑<span lang="EN-US">4</span>年。目前被羁押于河南省焦作市看守所。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体;
