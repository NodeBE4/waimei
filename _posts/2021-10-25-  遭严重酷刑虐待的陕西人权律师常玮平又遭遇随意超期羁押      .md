font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">常玮平：</span></b><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">早年曾就读于重庆大学；毕业后，<span lang="EN-US">2009</span>年—<span lang="EN-US">2013</span>年，通过一系列的律师资格自修和考试，最终获取律师执业资格证，后在陕西立刚律师事务所成为正式律师；工作期间，不仅曾代理多起公益诉讼（含艾滋歧视、乙肝歧视、性别歧视等）案件，亦曾代理过多起诸如“无锡<span lang="EN-US">4</span>·<span lang="EN-US">13</span>寻衅滋事案”、“无锡访民王金娣寻衅滋事案”、“南京王健寻衅滋事案”等敏感人权及弱势群体维权案件；<span lang="EN-US">2018</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">14</span>日，曾因此而被陕西宝鸡司法局停业<span lang="EN-US"> 3 </span>个月；同年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">22 </span>日，其律师资格证被当地律师事务所注销；<span lang="EN-US">2019</span>年，在停业期满后，其基本处于无法在其他律师事务所获得工作机会的状态，因其曾在陕西省境内与至少<span lang="EN-US">10</span>家律师事务所接洽，但皆不顺利，或即将签约时临时变卦，或虽签约后又突然解除合约；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月，曾因参加了一个与多名维权律师和异见人士在厦门的非正式私人聚会，后因被中共官方知晓后定性为“厦门聚会案”，多人遭严打狠抓，<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">12</span>日，其亦被陕西省宝鸡市警方跨省抓走，并被以“危害国家安全”为由指定居所监视居住；后被取保候审；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2020</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月，又因对外发视频称其在拘押期间，遭受酷刑，即每天（<span lang="EN-US">24</span>小时<span lang="EN-US">/</span>次）都把他绑在一种叫做“老虎凳”的刑具上，连续审问<span lang="EN-US">16</span>次，致其右手拇指到食指至今都毫无知觉，且在取保获释后仍受到严密监视等，<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">22</span>日，随再次被陕西省宝鸡市警方抓走，后被以涉嫌“煽动颠覆国家政权罪”指定居所监视居住；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2021</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;
