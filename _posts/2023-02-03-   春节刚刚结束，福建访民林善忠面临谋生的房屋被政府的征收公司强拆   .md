[春节刚刚结束，福建访民林善忠面临谋生的房屋被政府的征收公司强拆](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2023/02/blog-post_91.html)
------

<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">3</span>日，本网获悉：<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">1</span>日上午，福建省福州市晋安区城管带着公证处的共计<span lang="EN-US">10</span>多人，到该区鼓山镇远东村访民林善忠的<span lang="EN-US">694</span>号房屋处，说根据<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">30</span>日由福州市宏榕房屋征收工程公司贴门口的通知，要对该房屋进行测量评估，然后实施强拆。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2022</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">12</span>日，福建省福州市晋安区鼓山镇政府拆违办，就曾到林善忠的<span lang="EN-US">694</span>号房屋门口贴出公告，称该房屋属于违法建筑，限令<span lang="EN-US">3</span>天内自己拆除，否则后果自负。在贴公告前两天，房屋就被断水断电了。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">据林善忠说，该房屋是在<span lang="EN-US">1989</span>年与其他村民一起统一报批统建的，土地使用权已经获得了，村委会在<span lang="EN-US">2003</span>年还出证明，请求有关部门给予办理房产证，该房屋共计<span lang="EN-US">281</span>平米，林善忠一直以该房屋经营装修、五金等谋生，所以根本不存在违法建筑之说。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2002</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年，林善忠户籍地的晋安区鼓山镇远东村<span lang="EN-US">154</span>号被强拆，该房屋登记建筑面积<span lang="EN-US">300</span>余平米，是福州市建设委员会拆迁办跟远东村村委会签订了房屋拆迁协议，至今没有得到解决，林善忠还因为上访、被关押黑监狱无数次，<span lang="EN-US">2016</span>年刑事拘留一次，后取保候审，<span lang="EN-US">2017</span>年全国“两会”关押看守所一年半，最后以“寻衅滋事罪”判刑一年半。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">林善忠在元旦及春节前躲过了镇政府的强拆，现在又跳出一个福州市宏榕房屋征收工程公司贴通知，准备对其房屋实施强制性测量、评估后强拆，林善忠不明白，房屋征收不是已经规定由县级以上人民政府作为主体吗？现在怎么又房屋征收的工程公司实施了？<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">对林善忠的质问，城管人员与评估公司、及公证处人员根本不予理睬，就在房屋外面自说自话做了测量、评估，然后扬长而去。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体;
