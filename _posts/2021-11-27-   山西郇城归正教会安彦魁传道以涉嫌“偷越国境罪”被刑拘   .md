<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">27</span>日，本网获悉：山西太原郇城归正教会安彦魁传道于<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">22</span>日被汾阳市公安局以涉嫌偷越国境罪刑事拘留，现羁押在方山县看守所。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">21</span>日，安彦魁传道和张乘豪被山西汾阳的吕梁县公安局带走，似与此前汾阳五位基督教徒被以“偷越边境罪”刑拘、逮捕一案有关。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">安彦魁、张乘豪和汾阳五位基督教徒都曾在<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月底，到马来西亚亚吉隆坡参加唐崇荣牧师组织的“<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>福音与文化大会”。当时他们都是持护照从海关正常出入，并无违法行为。后教会五位弟兄于<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">28</span>日上午被刑事拘留，被抓捕的理由是“偷越边境”。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">安彦魁传道被刑事拘留后，其妻子姚聪娅表示：“<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年年初，安彦魁拿着中华人民共和国给办理的护照，和合法签证手续去了马来西亚，连走带回顶多一周的时间。发这个信息为的是大家为我们家能有更多祷告，也是安慰为我们挂心的亲人。更想警戎周围的亲人朋友们出国游玩要警慎，免得哪天也被定个什么偷越罪……”<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体;
