font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2022</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月，曾因与在美国工作的兄长、维吾尔族工程师卡吾萨尔·瓦伊提通讯，聊天中告知其在<span lang="EN-US">TikTok</span>上发过一则有关乌鲁木齐火灾海外白纸抗议视频的讯息，故而其父亲很快接到新疆当地警方的来电警告，遂其很快将所发帖子及视频删除，但此一举动仍然引起当局的极度不满；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">12</span>日，其放寒假刚返乡，即被新疆克孜勒苏柯尔克孜自治州阿图什市警方以疑似“寻衅滋事”罪由带走刑拘，并送新疆国家安全拘留中心羁押，自此失联；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">25</span>日，被新疆克孜勒苏柯尔克孜自治州阿图什市法院以“宣扬极端主义罪”判处有期徒刑三年，刑期至<span lang="EN-US">2025</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月。目前在新疆喀什木什女子监狱服刑。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"></p><div class="separator" style="clear: both;
