<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">江增朝：</span></b><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">1973</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年出生，又名江曾潮，黑龙江省绥化市青冈县人，绥化市安达市常居公民，个体小商户，法轮功信仰者，自由信仰权利捍卫者，中国在押良心犯。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">1996</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年开始接受并信仰法轮功；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">1999</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">-9</span>月，曾因时任中共最高领导人江泽民开始打压迫害法轮功，为捍卫自由信仰权利，随进京上访被遣回，并被黑龙江省安达市警方非法关进看守所<span lang="EN-US">3</span>天，惨遭酷刑折磨和毒打，并被戴死刑犯使用的沉重脚镣，后又被青冈县警方继续关押<span lang="EN-US">21</span>天才被释放；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">同年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">8</span>日，又被安达市朝阳派出所警方以涉嫌邪教罪刑拘，并送安达市看守所关押<span lang="EN-US">5</span>个多月；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2000</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月，曾因坚持锻炼法轮功而被绥化市当局处以劳动教养<span lang="EN-US">1</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月，且被关在黑龙江省绥化劳教所服劳役；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">获释后，为生计，且为躲避当局打压和避免家人受牵连，而曾被迫在辽宁省盘锦市靠打工为生；然因其一边打工一边仍不忘进行法轮功宣传，于<span lang="EN-US">2001</span>年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月被辽宁省盘锦市警方野蛮拘捕；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">后其案件被移交到辽宁省盘锦市法院，同年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月，被该法院一审以“组织利用邪教组织破坏法律实施罪”枉法重判有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">10</span>年，并被先送到辽宁省沈阳第二监狱（大北监狱）创二监区服刑，后又转送到大连南关岭监狱（大连市第一监狱）接续服刑；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">刑满出狱后，其为生计被迫又到黑龙江省大庆市谋生；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2020</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月，再次因散发、宣传法轮功数据，故而与其妹江洪艳一同被大庆市让胡路区龙南派出所警方以涉嫌“宣传邪教罪”从家中抓走、刑拘，且被分别关押在大庆市林甸县看守所和太康县看守所；后其妹被取保释放，而其仍被继续羁押并被让胡路区检察院以同罪由予以正式批捕；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2021</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月，其案在黑龙江省大庆市让胡路区法院开庭审理，终被该法院一审以“组织利用邪教组织破坏法律实施罪”判处有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">5</span>年；其不服上诉；后经大庆市中级法院二审裁决，依然维持原判，刑期至<span lang="EN-US">2025</span>年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">目前在黑龙江省呼兰监狱（黑龙江省哈尔滨市呼兰区腰堡乡<span lang="EN-US">803</span>信箱，邮政编码：<span lang="EN-US">150521</span>）服刑。</span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体;
