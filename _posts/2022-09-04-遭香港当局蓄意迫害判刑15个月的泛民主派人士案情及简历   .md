<p><span></span></p><a name='more'></a><p class="MsoNormal"><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">1964</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2019</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年，曾因积极参选香港东区区议会议员，高票当选为<span lang="EN-US">C11</span>小西湾选区首位非建制派区议员，并连带推动其他民主派候选人共同组成“柴湾起动”（政党组织），以协助集体参选区议员，最终使“柴湾起动”的<span lang="EN-US"> 12</span>名候选人尽都胜出；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2020</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">1</span>日，其正式任职东区区议会议员，直至<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">15</span>日，因参与组织反对七一大游行等活动而被取消区议员任职资格；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2020</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">30</span>日，因《港区国安法》正式生效，当天下午，其与陈皓桓、曾健成、徐子见和胡志伟等<span lang="EN-US">8</span>名民主派人士在香港中环终审法院外接受传媒采访、召集市民七一游行，并手持“反对国安法，七一齐上街”标语，高喊“废除国安法”、“七一街头见”和“坚持五大要求”等口号，遂于同年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">8</span>日在其住所被警方带走，遭训诫后又被拘捕，其余<span lang="EN-US">7</span>名民主派人士亦被先后遭捕；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2022</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;
