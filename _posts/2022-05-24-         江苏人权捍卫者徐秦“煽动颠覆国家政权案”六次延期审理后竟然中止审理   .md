[江苏人权捍卫者徐秦“煽动颠覆国家政权案”六次延期审理后竟然中止审理](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2022/05/blog-post_24.html)
------

<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">24</span>日，本网获悉：今年2月，被以“煽动颠覆国家政权罪”逮捕的江苏人权捍卫者徐秦，再次被江苏省扬州市中级法院延期审理期限<span lang="EN-US">3</span>个月，至<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">24</span>日，这是徐秦案第六次被延期，而批准延期的是最高法院，这几乎是用完了所有的延期程序。但此次期限过后，江苏扬州当局仍非法关押徐秦不释放，江苏扬州当局在不开庭的情况下做出了裁定书中止案件审理。江苏扬州当局玩弄法律，中止不是终止，音同字不同，终止就意味着可以释放徐秦回家，中止审理意味着还要继续关押，不审理不宣判。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">徐秦：</span></b><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">女，民间维权组织“中国人权观察”前秘书长，“玫瑰团队”成员，人权捍卫者，中国在押政治犯。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">长期以来，一直关注中国人权状况，并与著名人权活动家、民主异议人士秦永敏一道筹建中国人权观察；自秦永敏被捕、秦之妻子赵素利“被失踪”后，她曾多次上街声援，跟进案件；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2018</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">31</span>日，被警方突然限制自由，之后一直遭跟踪与监控；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2018</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">9</span>日，其在从扬州高邮到昆山的途中被警方带走，后得知其被扬州市警方以涉嫌“寻衅滋事罪”刑拘；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">同年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">15</span>日，其家属委托的代理律师要求会见，遭到警方拒绝，但被告知其已于<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">11</span>日由刑拘转为“指定居所监视居住”；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">3</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">月<span lang="EN-US">21</span>日，又被追加罪名，以涉嫌“煽动颠覆国家政权罪”继续扣留，且不准律师会见；据悉，此次被捕，皆与其近年来坚持关注人权问题，后又积极组织联署声援维权律师余文生，以及为维权公民徐琳筹款等活动有关；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2018</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">31</span>日获悉，其已获取保释放；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2021</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">5</span>日，徐秦突然又被警方从家中带走；据悉，之前其案件一直处于延审中；此次被捕前，徐秦刚做完心脏搭桥手术，且患有脑梗、心梗、高血压等多种疾病。现在案件被中止审理，何时恢复审理未知。目前徐秦被羁押于江苏省扬州市看守所。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"></p><div class="separator" style="clear: both;
