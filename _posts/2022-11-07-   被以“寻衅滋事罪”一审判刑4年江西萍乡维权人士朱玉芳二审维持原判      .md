[被以“寻衅滋事罪”一审判刑4年江西萍乡维权人士朱玉芳二审维持原判](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2022/11/4.html)

<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">7</span>日，本网获悉：<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">6</span>日，江西萍乡维权人士朱玉芳被萍乡市安源区法院以“寻衅滋事罪”判刑<span lang="EN-US">4</span>年。法院没有公开开庭宣判，而是在看守所通知朱玉芳。后朱玉芳已提出上诉。<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">20</span>日萍乡市法院二审维持安源区法院对朱玉芳一审四年有期徒刑的判决。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">朱玉芳曾于<span lang="EN-US">2016</span>年因上访维权被江西萍乡安源区法院以“寻衅滋事罪”判刑三年。这是朱玉芳第二次被以同样罪名判刑。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">朱玉芳再次被迫害的原由是，其于<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">3</span>日到江西省高级法院对其之前的刑事判刑申诉，遭拒绝受理，在法院门外举牌呼吁。<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">11</span>日，朱玉芳即被江西省萍乡市安源区警方以涉嫌“寻衅滋事罪”刑事拘留的，后被批捕、起诉。近年来，众多维权人士被以“寻衅滋事”罪名拘留、判刑，“寻衅滋事”已经成了当局打压维权人士的“口袋罪”。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">朱玉芳简历：</span></b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">女，<span lang="EN-US">1975</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">2</span>日出生，江西省萍乡市人，私营企业主，维权访民，中国在押维权人士。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2006</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年，因其苦心经营的<span lang="EN-US">4</span>家洁具店被政府骗拆，且拒不兑现当时的拆迁协议，被迫走上维权之路，然上访<span lang="EN-US">10</span>年，不仅毫无果效，反被地方当局视为重点维稳控制和打击对象，且被多次绑架、关黑监狱、 遭受酷刑和截访遣返原籍；<span lang="EN-US">2012</span>年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月，因在北京上访维权而被非法拘禁于北京市大兴区某黑监狱<span lang="EN-US">4</span>天，而后被遣返原籍；<span lang="EN-US"> 2012</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月—<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月，曾被地方当局长期非法软禁家中，房门被反锁，不准出门；<span lang="EN-US">2014</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9 </span>月<span lang="EN-US">7</span>日，因到北京上访而被北京市公安局朝阳警方以涉嫌“寻衅滋事罪”刑拘，后因其怀孕又于同日被取保候审；<span lang="EN-US">2015</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US"> 28</span>日，因其身穿访民反腐文化衫到联合国驻京开发署呼吁要求人权，而被北京市朝阳区警方再次刑拘；<span lang="EN-US">2015</span>年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">26</span>日， 因持续在北京上访维权，并将自己遭受迫害经历发于网上，接受境外媒体采访，再度被北京警方以“查获访民身份”为由抓走和交由萍乡驻京办事处将其遣返原籍，自此失联；后得知其已于遣返当日（<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">26 </span>日）被萍乡市安源区警方以涉嫌“寻衅滋事罪”刑拘，并关押于萍乡市第一看守所；同年<span
