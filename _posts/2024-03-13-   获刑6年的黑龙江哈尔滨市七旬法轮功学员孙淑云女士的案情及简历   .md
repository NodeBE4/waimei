<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">13</span>日，本网获悉获刑<span lang="EN-US">6</span>年的黑龙江哈尔滨市七旬法轮功学员孙淑云女士的案情及简历，现在通报如下：<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">孙淑云：</span></b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">黑龙江哈尔滨市七旬法轮功学员，女，<span lang="EN-US">1953</span>年生，早年修炼法轮功。一九九九年七月中共疯狂迫害法轮功以来，孙淑云先后曾被非法关押十余次。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">2022</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月因宣讲法轮功被警方抓捕抄家，<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月，她被哈尔滨市呼兰区光明派出所监视居住。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">2023</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">20</span>日，孙淑云被呼兰区公安局国保大队警察抓捕。随即孙淑云被哈尔滨市道外区检察院批捕。孙淑云被非法关押在哈尔滨第二看守所（鸭子圈看守所）。在第二看守所，她拒绝坐板，拒穿囚服，坚持打坐炼功，以年逾七旬之躯绝食抗议。<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">4</span>日在哈尔滨市道外区法院遭开庭，她被判刑<span lang="EN-US">6</span>年，<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">9</span>日被转监到黑龙江省女子监狱服刑。刑期至：<span lang="EN-US">2029</span>年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">19</span>日。</span></p>
