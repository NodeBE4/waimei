<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">13</span>日，本网获悉遭中共当局判刑<span lang="EN-US">20</span>年的新疆伊犁哈萨克族清真寺伊玛目（即神职人员）赛里克•木海的案情及简历，现通报如下：<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">赛里克·木海（哈萨克）<span lang="EN-US">:</span></span></b><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;"> 1972</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">15</span>日出生，新疆维吾尔自治区伊犁哈萨克自治州塔城沙湾县商户地乡蘑菇湖村人，哈萨克族名<span lang="EN-US">Serik Muqai</span>，又被译为色力克·木海，穆斯林，清真寺伊玛目（即宗教领袖、神职人员），中国在押良心犯。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">因是当地中共政府批准的乡村清真寺伊玛目，遂长期在当地清真寺以敬拜、讲解和传播穆斯林教义为主，并深得穆斯林信众的尊敬和热爱；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">为要穆斯林信众更多、更好地参与聚会敬拜和研读或习得相关宗教知识，曾积极筹措资金新建清真寺，但后在中共政府对新疆各少数族裔（尤其是维吾尔族、哈萨克族和回族等）的高压严控下，<span lang="EN-US">2018</span>年，该清真寺竟被官方以“对村庄来说太大”为由摧毁；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2018</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">3</span>日，其被塔城地区沙湾县警方突然带走，送当地一所再教育培训营关押，后因其身体状况极差而暂被取保释放，并送进医院接受治疗；同年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月，再次被收监逮捕，指控其曾向儿童和民众传播、讲解了穆斯林教义，因而其言行与政府相关法律相抵触，自此失联；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2018</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月，其案在伊犁哈萨克自治州塔城地区沙湾县法院开庭审理，至<span lang="EN-US">2019</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月，最终被以传播宗教罪等重判处有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">20</span>年（其中向儿童传教判<span lang="EN-US">7</span>年，向民众传播宗教<span lang="EN-US">13</span>年），刑期至<span lang="EN-US">2038</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月。</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;">目前在新疆乌苏监狱（新疆维吾尔自治区塔城地区乌苏市，邮政编码：</span><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt;">833006</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;">）服刑。</span></p><p class="MsoNormal"></p><div class="separator" style="clear: both;
