<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">张盼成</span></b><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">：<span lang="EN-US">1995</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">28</span>日出生，甘肃省庆阳市合水县人，网名为“子慕予兮”，中国西北农村长大留守儿童，前北京大学保安，中国在押政治犯。<span lang="EN-US"><span style="mso-spacerun: yes;">&nbsp;&nbsp; </span><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">早年出生于甘肃农村，是典型的留守儿童；<span lang="EN-US">2018</span>年，曾以公民身份表达自己的意愿；在辞去北京大学保安工作之后，曾放胆自拍视频，发政治主张，称已为民主“做好了牺牲的准备”；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">被捕前，曾在视频中表示，不同意中国政府将<span lang="EN-US">600</span>亿美元未经法定程序送<span lang="EN-US">(</span>外国）人，要求言论自由，声援被失踪的董瑶琼、华涌、佳士工人和原北大学生岳昕等人，以及进学习班的百万新疆同胞；曾在视频中引用宋代名将文天祥的诗句“人生自古谁无死，留取丹心照汗青”，并说自己对国家有深沉的爱；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2018</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月，被抓捕刑拘；<span lang="EN-US">2018</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">18</span>日，被北京市警方以涉嫌“寻衅滋事罪”正式逮捕；审讯中，拒绝写保证书，并拒绝给律师签委托书；<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">21</span>日，其案在北京西城区法院开庭审理；<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">4</span>日，被北京市西城区法院一审以“寻衅滋事罪”判处有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">1</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月，至<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">20</span>日，刑满释放；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2021</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">14</span>日，因其在网上发布一段在甘肃山村老家里录制的视频，谴责中共暴行，表达心声称“凛凛寒冬终将过去，春天马上要来临”，遂被甘肃省合水县警方莫名刑拘，家遭查处，自此失联；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">后知又被当地合县检察院转正式批捕，被关押在甘肃省庆阳市合水县看守所；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">同年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">26</span>日，知其已在两周前被甘肃省合水县法院一审秘判，但其家人未获判决书；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2021</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">30</span>日本网获悉，其似被以“寻衅滋事罪”判处有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">3</span>年，刑期至<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">13</span>日。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;
