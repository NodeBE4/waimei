<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">22</span>日，本网获悉被中共当局判刑<span lang="EN-US">7</span>年的阮晓寰（网名“编程随想”）的案情及简历，现通报如下：<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2021</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">10</span>日被抓捕的阮晓寰（网名“编程随想”），终于在入狱<span lang="EN-US">1</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>个月后有了消息，据悉，阮晓寰一审被判刑<span lang="EN-US">7</span>年，刑期自<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">10</span>日至<span lang="EN-US">2028</span>年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">9</span>日止。目前其被关押上海杨浦区看守所（杨浦区中原路<span lang="EN-US">1101</span>号），电话<span lang="EN-US">021-22170505</span>，邮编<span lang="EN-US">200438</span>，<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">阮晓寰：网名“编程随想”，</span></b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">男，<span lang="EN-US">1977</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">10</span>日出生，原籍福建泉州人，计算机技术人员，曾担任<span lang="EN-US">2008</span>年北京奥运会信息安全系统总工程师。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">于<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">10</span>日，以“在海外个人博客中的部分言论涉嫌煽动颠覆国家政权罪”为由被带走关押，直到<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">10</span>日被上海二中院一审判处<span lang="EN-US">7</span>年，但判决书“（<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>）沪<span lang="EN-US">02</span>刑初<span lang="EN-US">67</span>号”里并没有提及“煽动颠覆”内容，一审也是由法院指定法援（法律援助）律师陆律师替阮“辩护”，但陆律师很谨慎，一直不和家属透漏案情，直到一审开庭，家属都不知道阮被抓被判的具体“案由”。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">一审判决后，阮妻接到一审董法官通知，告知阮已提出上诉，委托律师还是一审律师。阮妻明确告知将自己委托北京莫少平及尚宝军律师。但随后阮妻的父兄两家均遭警方约谈施压，“劝”其不要请莫、尚两位律师。其本人也于<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">19</span>日被警察堵在家里强行训话<span lang="EN-US">2</span>小时。阮妻于<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">20</span>日和莫、尚两位律师签订委托协议后，尚律师即联系董法官要求阅卷，董回复说案件在移交高院过程中，让尚律到高院阅卷。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2023</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">22</span>日，上海高级人民法院立案（案号：<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>沪刑终<span lang="EN-US">15</span>号），<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">28</span>日，分派承办法官须梅华。高院先在二审用“法援”律师侵占辩护席位，家属委托的莫、尚两位律师无法维护阮的合法辩护权。家属对相关司法人员持续信访、投诉、举报中，要求彻查纠正。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体;
