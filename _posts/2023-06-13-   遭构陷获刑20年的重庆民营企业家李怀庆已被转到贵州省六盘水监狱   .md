mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">重庆民营企业家、亿万富翁李怀庆，因在微信上说几句话，就被当局以煽动颠覆国家政权，及诈骗、敲诈勒索、非法拘禁等四项罪名羁押。在被关押近三年之后，于<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">20</span>日在重庆市第一中级法院一审宣判，获刑<span lang="EN-US">20</span>年，令世人震惊。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;"><b>李怀庆简历：</b><span lang="EN-US">1966</span>年出生，重庆市人，复转军人，“随手公益”的志愿者，重庆富华典当公司老板，民营企业家，中国在押政治犯。</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;">&nbsp;</span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2018</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">后其罪名被重庆市检察院更换为涉嫌“煽动颠覆国家政权罪”，指控其在<span lang="EN-US">2017</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月—<span lang="EN-US">2018</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月，曾七度在微信“以造谣、诽谤等方式煽动颠覆国家政权”，包括转发“红四方面军在四川杀人百万”文章，又转发过有“道义抗争”、“暴力革命”等字眼的录音，属于违法行为；<span lang="EN-US">2018</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">24</span>日，其罪名遂于“涉黑罪”变成“颠覆国家政权罪”；</span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;
