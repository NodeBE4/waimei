[获刑4年6个月的著名人权捍卫者季孝龙狱中度过其48岁生日](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2024/07/4648.html)
------

<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">11</span>日，本网获悉：<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">10</span>日是获刑<span lang="EN-US">4</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月的著名人权捍卫者季孝龙<span lang="EN-US"> 48</span>岁生日。季孝龙先生在狱中度过了他的四十八岁的生日。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">季孝龙于<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">31</span>日因替封城时的上海人民发声，向时任上海市委书记李强发出实名请愿书，要求其下台并承担重大事故责任，而遭抓捕，一审被以寻衅滋事罪判刑<span lang="EN-US">4</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月。季孝龙不服判决依法上诉。<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">17</span>日，上海第一中院就著名人权捍卫者季孝龙案作出二审判决<span lang="EN-US">——</span>维持一审<span lang="EN-US">4</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月的判刑结果。随后季孝龙案转监至上海市新收犯监狱。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">目前，季孝龙已被转至判刑人员“集训”地——上海市新收犯监狱（具体地址：上海青浦区外青松路<span lang="EN-US">7935</span>号，电话<span lang="EN-US">021</span>一<span lang="EN-US">69207230</span>，邮编<span lang="EN-US">201701</span>）进行所谓<span lang="EN-US">“</span>集训<span lang="EN-US">”</span>。<span lang="EN-US">“</span>集训<span lang="EN-US">”</span>后会再分往其它监狱服刑。季孝龙上次获刑也是先被关进这里然后转至白茅岭监狱服刑，并在服刑期间受到长时间单独关押、不准消费等酷刑。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><b><span style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">季孝龙</span></b><span style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">：<span lang="EN-US">1976</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">10</span>日出生，江苏省张家港市人，张家港市杨舍镇名都花苑居民，基督徒，维权公民，民主人士，人权捍卫者，中国在押政治犯。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">2015</span><span style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月，曾因宣传民主理念而被上海市浦东新区警方以<span lang="EN-US">“</span>寻衅滋事<span lang="EN-US">”</span>罪由传唤，并被处以行政拘留<span lang="EN-US">10</span>日；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">2018</span><span style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">20</span>日，曾因在网上发文，呼吁民主人士响应<span lang="EN-US">“</span>厕所革命<span lang="EN-US">”</span>，并在医院、大学等公共场所的厕所门上，用马克笔写宣传语<span lang="EN-US">“</span>大病治不起，习胖狂撒币，修宪妄称帝、苦难何时毕！打倒共产党。还政于民，结束民众苦难。推翻习核心，拒走文革路<span lang="EN-US">”</span>等字句，短时间内响应者众多，随于<span lang="EN-US">2018</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">27</span>日再次被浦东新区警方以涉嫌<span lang="EN-US">“</span>寻衅滋事罪<span lang="EN-US">”</span>刑事拘留；同年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">25</span>日，又被送到指定居所监视居住；同年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">30</span>日，被以同罪由转正式逮捕，关押在上海市浦东新区看守所；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">2018</span><span style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">30</span>日，其案被上海市浦东新区检察院起诉至上海市浦东新区法院；<span lang="EN-US">2019</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">14</span>日，其最终被该法院以<span lang="EN-US">“</span>寻衅滋事罪<span lang="EN-US">”</span>一审判处有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">3</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月，后送上海市白茅岭监狱七监区服刑；据悉，羁押期间，警方曾多次以让其<span lang="EN-US">“</span>死亡<span lang="EN-US">”</span>和用寒冷气温逼迫其认罪，致其身体健康虚弱且生病吐血；其家属曾送羽绒服和《圣经》书籍亦遭拒收；<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">9</span>日，刑满获释；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">同年上海封城，其为上海市民持续发声。<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">31</span>日，再次被上海市梅园新村派出所警察从家中带走，后被上海市浦东新区警方以<span lang="EN-US">“</span>寻衅滋事罪<span lang="EN-US">”</span>刑拘；同年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">23</span>日，又被浦东新检察院以同罪名正式批捕，后季孝龙于<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">17</span>日被起诉。后已经开庭，未宣判。最终于<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">27</span>日，被一些寻衅滋事罪判刑<span lang="EN-US">4</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月，此前被羁押于浦东新区看守所，现转监至上海市新收犯监狱。刑期至：<span lang="EN-US">2027</span>年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">28</span>日。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体;
