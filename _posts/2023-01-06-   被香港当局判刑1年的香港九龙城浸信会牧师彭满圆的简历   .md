<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p align="left" class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: left;"><span style="color: black; font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-themecolor: text1;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">6</span>日，本网获悉被香港当局判刑<span lang="EN-US">1</span>年的香港九龙城浸信会牧师彭满圆的简历，现在通报如下：<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p align="left" class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: left;"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="color: black; font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-themecolor: text1;">2003</span><span style="color: black; font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-themecolor: text1;">年，曾受邀在陕西师范大学从事唐代的景教（即基督教）研究工作，后又被该校基督教研究所聘任为新约圣经英文客座教授（主指用英文教学四福音书中的耶稣生平等内容）；聘任期满后，又返回香港，在香港九龙城浸信会担任分龄牧养（即把会众分成七个年龄组别进行牧养）工作；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p align="left" class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: left;"><span lang="EN-US" style="color: black; font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-themecolor: text1;">2007</span><span style="color: black; font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-themecolor: text1;">年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">23</span>日起，曾飞往英国，开始热心在牛津华人基督教会从事牧养工作；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p align="left" class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: left;"><span lang="EN-US" style="color: black; font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-themecolor: text1;">2019 </span><span style="color: black; font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-themecolor: text1;">年“理大围城”时，曾因在香港理工大学门外静坐，协助被困学生家长与警方交涉，表达希望解决人道问题等，而让中共香港当局倍感不适；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p align="left" class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: left;"><span lang="EN-US" style="color: black; font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-themecolor: text1;">2020</span><span style="color: black; font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-themecolor: text1;">年二人限聚令期间，曾因在其创办的“牧师和你颠”<span lang="EN-US">Youtube</span>频道上多次发起一人祈祷会（含为香港复兴和宗教自由祈祷、捍卫公义的法治、周梓乐（注：生前为香港科技大学学生，因抗议中与警方发生冲突从楼上坠下，<span lang="EN-US">4</span>天后不治死亡，死因存疑）逝世一周年等），并设网上直播，随引发中共香港当局极度不快而欲伺机打击报复；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p align="left" class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: left;"><span lang="EN-US" style="color: black; font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-themecolor: text1;">2021</span><span style="color: black; font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-themecolor: text1;">年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">6</span>日，其与多名民主公民共计<span lang="EN-US">6</span>人被香港国安处警方以涉嫌违反《刑事罪行条例》第<span lang="EN-US">9</span>和<span lang="EN-US">10</span>条“作出具煽动意图的作为”罪统一拘捕；后有<span lang="EN-US">4</span>人被交保释放，但其与民主人士赵美英仍被在押；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p align="left" class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: left;"><span lang="EN-US" style="color: black; font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-themecolor: text1;">2022</span><span style="color: black; font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-themecolor: text1;">年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">4</span>日，其等数人案在香港西九龙裁判法院提堂受审，期间，因其被指控发表“煽动文字”（即“极具意图引起对香港司法的憎恨、藐视或激起对其离叛；怂使他人不守法或不服从合法命令的文字”），且又被指控<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">17</span>日<span lang="EN-US">-2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">16</span>日之间，在香港制作、上载及广播<span lang="EN-US">YouTube</span>影片等，“作出具煽动意图的作为”，随曾数次申请保释均被拒；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p align="left" class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: left;"><span style="color: black; font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-themecolor: text1;">同年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">11</span>日、<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">19</span>日及<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">27</span>日，其被西九龙裁判法院<span lang="EN-US">3</span>次提堂庭审而无果，并因被控曾在法庭旁听前支联会副主席邹幸彤庭审时作出滋扰行为（主指在旁听时拍掌发言），遂又次申请保释遭拒而继续被押；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p align="left" class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: left;"><span style="color: black; font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-themecolor: text1;">后又历经几次香港高等法院庭审，<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月，终被以“发表煽动文字”及“作出具煽动意图的作为”二罪判处有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">1</span>年；<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">9</span>日，知其已刑满获释；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p align="left" class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: left;"><span style="color: black; font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-themecolor: text1;">据悉，羁押审讯期间，其无代理辩护律师，庭审之中均由自行辩护；自被捕在押以来，因其强调“历史将宣判我无罪”，故曾 先后<span lang="EN-US">8</span>次申请保释全部被拒。此前疑被羁押于荔枝角收押所。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p align="left" class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: left;"></p><div class="separator" style="clear: both;
