[获刑3年6个月、刚刚刑满出狱的北京维权人士杨秋雨先生的案情及简历](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2022/09/36.html)

<p><span></span></p><a name='more'></a><p class="MsoNormal"><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">1</span>日，本网获悉获刑<span lang="EN-US">3</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月、余<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">22</span>日刚刚刑满出狱的北京维权人士杨秋雨先生的案情及简历，现通报如下：<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">杨秋雨：</span></b><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">1963</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">8</span>日出生，北京市人，北京市东城区夕照寺东里<span lang="EN-US">6</span>栋居民，基督徒，抗暴维权者，维权公民，人权捍卫者，中国曾押维权人士。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2003</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年，曾因自家所在房屋被北京崇文区政府以危房改造名义强拆，随在抗强拆中与对方发生冲突，遭殴打，后被当局抓捕并被以“故意伤害罪”判刑<span lang="EN-US">2</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月；刑满出狱后，其多次向崇文区政府拆迁办公室讨说法，无果，随从此踏入维权之道，并自<span lang="EN-US">2007</span>年起开始帮助全国各地来京的访民维权，且与妻子一起宣传宪法与人权理念等；然而正因为频繁参与公民维权及记录维权个案，随遭当局严厉打压，多次遭劳动教养和刑事拘留，居无定所；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2009</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">12</span>日，曾被北京市西城区警方以涉嫌“聚众扰乱公共秩序罪”刑拘，一个月后因证据不足被取保获释；之后，又曾因被控违反监管规定而<span lang="EN-US">4</span>次被崇文区警方各拘留<span lang="EN-US">10</span>天；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2011</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">6</span>日，因其在西单购物中心附近拍照，被西城区警察再次抓走、刑拘，家被查抄；同年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">13</span>日知其已“因进行寻衅滋事、煽动闹事非法活动，妨害社会管理秩序”被处劳动教养<span lang="EN-US">2</span>年；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2019</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">9</span>日，在其欲前往教会参加聚会之时，突然被警方突击传唤，并迅速被以涉嫌“寻衅滋事罪”刑拘；<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">14</span>日，被丰台区检察院以同罪名正式批捕，并羁押在北京市丰台区看守所；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">其案原定于<span lang="EN-US">2019</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">10</span>日在北京市丰台区法院开庭审理，后又被以新任代表律师需时间阅卷为由取消，直至<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">29</span>日被再次庭审；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2020</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">5</span>日才宣判，终被该法院以“寻衅滋事罪”判处有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">3</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月；<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">22 </span>日，刑满释放；据悉，羁押期间曾遭酷刑虐待，被警方扣<span lang="EN-US">18</span>斤脚镣达半月之久。此前在北京某一监狱服刑。</span></p><p></p><div class="separator" style="clear: both;
