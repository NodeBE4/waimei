<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2021</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">31</span>日，曾因以重庆市合法公民身份积极参选区镇级人大代表换届选举，故引发社会热议，并对其等<span lang="EN-US">4</span>位公民的“重庆人大参选独立侯选人联合宣言”给予高度评价和首肯，但终因仅是普通百姓，毫无官场背景或官场背景的支持，而被当地小区以“不合格”为由拒绝报名参选；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2021</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">14</span>日，曾因摄录和发布了一段有关当地农贸市场涉及疫情的小视频，而被所住大竹林康美小区派出所警方传唤，并将其手机等当场收走；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">曾因其未婚女友唐云淑因自家三处房产遭政府强拆损毁而不断维权，遂多次被送进精神病院和被刑拘及判刑，<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">15</span>日其女友又被警方拘捕，在超押<span lang="EN-US">1</span>年多后，<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">16</span>日被重庆市丰都县法院以“寻衅滋事罪”重判<span lang="EN-US">4</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">此期间，其为营救女友四处奔波呼吁，后因不断为女友喊冤，并持续在网上发声寻求正义和声援，而亦被重庆市两江新区警方抓走失联；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">后经重庆友人钟声牛与朱春华多方打探，<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">21</span>日，终于获知其被重庆市渝北区警方以涉嫌“寻衅滋事罪”正式逮捕，至今其案已被法院秘密开庭两次，但尚未宣判；据悉，其二位重庆友人钟声牛与朱春华愿为其做代理辩护人，但遭法院拒绝。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体;
