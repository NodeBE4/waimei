<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">1956</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">23</span>日出生，天津市和平区人，天津市某中学教师，抗强拆维权公民，中国在押维权人士。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">早期吴刚曾因其住宅被天津地方政府强拆，后其工作岗位又遭辞退，随被迫走上维权上访之路；近年来，曾因参与多起维权活动，尤其是参与声援天津北辰抗暴维权者王国廷案被当局枉法重判、维权公民在天津市人大门前举牌进行和平请愿等，而遭当局多次拘捕打压：<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2009</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">8</span>日吴刚曾被天津警方以涉嫌“扰乱公共场所秩序罪”行政拘留<span lang="EN-US">10</span>天，<span lang="EN-US">2012</span>年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月又被警方行政拘留<span lang="EN-US">10</span>天，<span lang="EN-US">2013</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">20</span>日再次被警方以涉嫌“扰乱单位秩序罪”行政拘留<span lang="EN-US">10</span>天；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2015</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">3</span>日，吴刚再次因参与维权抗争而被天津市和平区警方以涉嫌“寻衅滋事罪”刑事拘留；<span lang="EN-US">2015</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">17</span>日，被以同罪名转正式逮捕，关押在天津市南开区看守所；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2016</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">11</span>日，吴刚案件在天津市和平区法院开庭受审；<span lang="EN-US">2016</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">28</span>日，被天津市中级法院第两次庭审，终以“寻衅滋事罪”判处有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">3</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月；<span lang="EN-US">2019</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">2</span>日，吴刚刑满释放；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2021</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月，因吴刚一直要求约见区人大代表，遂于<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月中旬突然失踪；<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">17</span>日，知其被天津市河西区大营门派出所警方以涉嫌“寻衅滋事罪”刑拘。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体;
