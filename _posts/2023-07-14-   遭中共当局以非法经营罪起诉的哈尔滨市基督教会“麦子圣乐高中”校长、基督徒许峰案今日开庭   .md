[遭中共当局以非法经营罪起诉的哈尔滨市基督教会“麦子圣乐高中”校长、基督徒许峰案今日开庭](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2023/07/blog-post_14.html)

<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">14</span>日，本网获悉：遭中共当局以非法经营罪起诉的哈尔滨市教会“麦子圣乐高中”校长、基督徒许峰案今日开庭。自<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月迄今许峰已经被羁押了一年零十个月。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">许峰：<span lang="EN-US">1979</span>年生，黑龙江省哈尔滨市人，基督徒，哈尔滨市基督教会音乐学校“麦子圣乐高中”校长，中国在押良心犯；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">因其所在“麦子圣乐高中”是哈尔滨市一所专门培养基督教音乐人才的艺术学校，尤以教授钢琴最为擅长，学生的培养方向主要是服务教会的唱诗班；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">然而，自中共新宗教事务条例实施以来，其及其学校频遭所在辖区哈尔滨当局的骚扰和威胁打击；自<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年以来，其家曾被警方两次莫名搜查，且其电话一直被监听；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2021</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">4</span>日，麦子圣乐高中突遭辖区内的特警、公安、宗教局、教育局等三四十人的莫名冲击，警方把学校所有老师及学生悉数抓进派出所；许峰亦在同日早上<span lang="EN-US">7:00</span>左右送孩子上学的路上遭警方拦截抓走；后获悉，许峰于<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">4 </span>日因涉嫌非法经营被哈尔滨市公安局松北分局刑事拘留，于<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US"> 11 </span>日，以涉嫌非法经营罪批准逮捕，被哈尔滨市公安局松北分局逮捕。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">之后其学校被查封，所有校财（如钢琴、电脑和资料等）被没收；<span lang="EN-US">2021 </span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">10</span>日，松北公安分局侦查终结，将许峰以涉嫌非法经营罪的名义移送哈尔滨松北区检察院。</span><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2023</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月，他们被以非法经营罪起诉到哈尔滨松北法院，<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">14</span>日，今日开庭。</span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">庭审情况尚不清楚，本网将持续关注许峰案的进展。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><div class="separator" style="clear: both;
