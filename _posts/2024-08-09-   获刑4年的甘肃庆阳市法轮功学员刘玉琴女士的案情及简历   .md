<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">10</span>日，本网获悉获刑<span lang="EN-US">4</span>年的甘肃庆阳市法轮功学员刘玉琴女士的案情及简历，现在通报如下：<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">刘玉琴：</span></b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">甘肃庆阳市法轮功学员，女，原是庆城县陇东中学一级教师，<span lang="EN-US">1961</span>年生，<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">61</span>岁。刘玉琴一九九八年四月开始修炼法轮功。因为其坚持修炼法轮功，在中共江泽民一伙发动的对法轮功的迫害中，她于<span lang="EN-US">2009</span>年被判刑三年，<span lang="EN-US">2010</span>年被学校开除。<span lang="EN-US">2009</span>年至<span lang="EN-US">2012</span>年，刘玉琴被关押在甘肃省女子监狱，身心遭受了极度摧残，身体骨瘦如柴，一度随时有生命危险。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">2023</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">19</span>日，刘玉琴在高楼乡发法轮功资料时被人举报，遭警方抓捕并抄家，后刘玉琴被关押进宁县看守所。随即遭刑事拘留、批捕、起诉。<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">11</span>日遭镇原县法院开庭，律师为她做无罪辩护。刘玉琴也当庭表示自己无罪。近悉，刘玉琴被判刑<span lang="EN-US">4</span>年，刑期至：<span lang="EN-US">2027</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">18</span>日。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;"><o:p>&nbsp;</o:p></span></p>
