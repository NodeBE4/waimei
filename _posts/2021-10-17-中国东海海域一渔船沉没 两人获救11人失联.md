[中国东海海域一渔船沉没 两人获救11人失联](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20211017-1204288)
------

<div>
<p>一艘渔船昨晚在中国东海海域沉没，目前已救起两人，仍有11人失联。</p><p>据中国央视新闻客户端消息，受风浪影响，昨晚在距离长江口东北约120海里处，一艘渔船沉没，13名船员失联。中国交通运输部东海救助局第一时间派遣救助船赶赴事发海域搜救。</p><p>今早，救助直升机加入搜救，发现遇险渔船已经完全沉没，并在附近海域发现有两名船员漂浮在海上，机组人员将两名落水船员位置告知正在附近搜救的“东海救101”轮；“东海救101”轮随后将两人救起。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>据报道，目前仍有11名船员失联，搜救工作还在进行中。</p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/zbpe-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=zbpe399&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_desktop-zbpe399_2.png?dHEiGouxxZpWwWTJE04dyGyUN74JOU2z" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_desktop-zbpe399_2.png?dHEiGouxxZpWwWTJE04dyGyUN74JOU2z" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/zbpe-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=zbpe399&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_mobile-zbpe399_2.png?qZnBcIGCJvPR8A2IAZOZuOxdoGZDatiI" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_mobile-zbpe399_2.png?qZnBcIGCJvPR8A2IAZOZuOxdoGZDatiI" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
