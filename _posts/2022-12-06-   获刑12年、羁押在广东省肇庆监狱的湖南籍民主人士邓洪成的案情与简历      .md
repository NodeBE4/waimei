[获刑12年、羁押在广东省肇庆监狱的湖南籍民主人士邓洪成的案情与简历](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2022/12/12.html)

<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">6</span>日，本网获悉获刑<span lang="EN-US">12</span>年、羁押在广东省肇庆监狱的湖南籍民主人士邓洪成的案情与简历，现通报如下：<b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></b></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">邓洪成：</span></b><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">1967</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">6</span>日，湖南省岳阳市人，网名“成主民”，微博名“湘人血性”，自由职业者、商人，广东省深圳市龙岗区阪田街杨美东四巷<span lang="EN-US">9</span>号租住户，深圳同城聚会公民，民主宪政追求者，深圳“<span lang="EN-US">11</span>•<span lang="EN-US">15</span>大搜捕”案受害人之一，中国在押政治犯。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">早期曾在海南省海口市经商，因亲眼目睹体制腐败黑洞而不满，随致力维权改变现状，以图促进中国之民主宪政；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">自<span lang="EN-US">2013</span>年起，开始到广东省深圳市从事公民运动；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2016</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">14</span>日，因与肖兵、王威、李南海等多位民主人士在深圳市五和餐厅聚餐，席间李南海与肖兵被陌生人拍照（事后得知是国保），<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">15</span>日中午，深圳市警方前往餐厅调查，手拿火焱跟肖兵的照片要求工作人员指认，当天午后随与深圳维权公民肖兵、沈力、李南海、王威被警方扣押，自此失联；其侄子邓剑峰与同城聚餐公民丁岩、董凌鹏、王军及等得知消息之后前往其住处（深圳市杨美村五巷）寻找，亦遭失联；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2016</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">6</span>日，其在江西的家属被当地派出所警察查问，且拒绝透露当邓洪成下落及具体情况；同年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">15</span>日，其叔侄二人被深圳市警方以涉嫌“颠覆国家政权罪”执行指定居所监视居住，其侄子邓剑锋后被取保候审释放；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2017</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">12</span>日，被深圳市检察院以同罪名正式批捕，并关押在深圳市第二看守所；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">因长期拒绝认罪，<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">13</span>日，终被深圳市法院以“颠覆国家政权罪”重判有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">12</span>年，刑期至<span lang="EN-US">2028</span>年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月；据悉，被押<span lang="EN-US">4</span>年多，其家属一直不能与其会见，入监后要求探监亦被拒绝。目前在广东省肇庆监狱十监区二分监区服刑。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"></p><div class="separator" style="clear: both;
