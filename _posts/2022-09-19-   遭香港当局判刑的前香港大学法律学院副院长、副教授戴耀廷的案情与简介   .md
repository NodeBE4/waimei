[遭香港当局判刑的前香港大学法律学院副院长、副教授戴耀廷的案情与简介](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2022/09/blog-post_69.html)
------

<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">19</span>日，本网获悉遭香港当局判刑的前香港大学法律学院副院长、副教授戴耀廷的案情与简介，现通报如下：<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">戴耀廷：</span></b><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">1964</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">12</span>日出生，英语名：<span lang="EN-US">Benny Tai Yiu-ting</span>，香港人，基督徒，香港法律学者，前香港大学法律学院副院长和副教授，“占中三子”，民主派意见领袖，中国在押政治犯。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">自幼生长于香港；<span lang="EN-US">1980</span>年代，中学毕业后曾就读于香港大学法律系，获取法律学士学位后，又取得法律深造证书，并在英国伦敦大学获得法律硕士学位；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">早年曾有浓烈的“中国情怀”，喜读中文和中国历史，后在大学期间，因吸收民主自由思想而认识到一国之强大不只是富国强兵，而是让它走向宪政，遂研究兴趣转为宪法、宪政、公法、法律和政治及宗教的关系、人权及参与式民主等，并成为民主派意见领袖；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">大学毕业后，曾在民主派领袖李柱铭办公担任法案助理，并是《香港基本法咨询委员会》两位学生代表之一，同时对“一国两制”的实施提出了批判与质疑；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">之后曾多年在香港大学法律系当副教授、并曾在<span lang="EN-US">2000</span>年<span lang="EN-US">-2008</span>年期间担任法律学院副院长；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2013</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年，曾因提出“让爱与和平占领中环”观念而闻名，并认为香港若未能有“真正普选”，即应该参与占领行动以争取<span lang="EN-US">2017</span>年香港行政长官选举得以有“真正选择”；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2016</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月、<span lang="EN-US">2017</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月、<span lang="EN-US">2019</span>年，曾因先后提出雷动计划、风云计划及民主派<span lang="EN-US">35+</span>初选等行动，而赢得香港市民的极大支持和响应，但却屡遭亲共建制派人士及媒体攻击。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2019</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年，随着港版国安法的正式实施，其很快被香港国安警方拘捕，被控曾因发起“占领中环”运动，并于<span lang="EN-US">2014</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">27</span>日<span lang="EN-US">-28</span>日在香港中环金钟不同地点“煽惑他人”或“煽惑他人煽惑”非法阻碍行车道，因而被捕；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">同年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">9</span>日，其被西九龙裁判法院庭审裁决“串谋犯公众妨扰罪”和“煽惑他人犯公众妨扰罪”罪名成立；至<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">24</span>日，被判监<span lang="EN-US">16</span>个月；其不服判决，提起上诉，并申请保释；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2019</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">15</span>日，在服刑<span lang="EN-US">4</span>个月后，终被获准以<span lang="EN-US">10</span>万港元保释等候上诉，并要交出旅游证件，不可离开香港；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2020</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">24</span>日<span lang="EN-US">-26</span>日其上诉在香港高级法院被聆讯；同年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">4</span>日，其保释被撤回而返监；<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">30</span>日，其上诉最终被驳回；同年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月，其副教授一职被香港大学校委会以在“占领中环”运动中有“不当行为”为由解聘，同期，其与另外二人又被廉政公署以在<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年协调民主派初选时涉嫌选举开支非法行为等为罪由起诉；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2022</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">24</span>日，被香港区域法院又以“串谋颠覆国家政权罪”等罪名判囚<span lang="EN-US">10</span>个月；并与之前罪刑同期执行；截至目前，其等多人涉嫌民主派初选案，因系漫长而系列的审讯，故其仍需在押，继续候审。</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">目前在香港大屿山石壁监狱服刑。</span></p><p class="MsoNormal"></p><div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;"><a href="https://blogger.googleusercontent.com/img/b/R29vZ2xl/AVvXsEid9yWsbpHmyCGNZOB6WUKJbIZTx2eQkjSRuq7emr02NF74PY2NNrS43shn_4Fgtf4OYZXp1zhsP1LTsqERp8GeplUlNa-td9R2iGJrCjjOkwa7QcftZCLPKXid1a3wpage0u0GvNpaMY9Mc6MbyQ_ZpfQaWMSmiQdw9PM-fdDbeU6iy3nQFBvDzbzqMw/s250/%E6%88%B4%E8%80%80%E5%BB%B7%E6%96%BC%E7%A4%BE%E5%8D%80%E7%B6%B2%E7%B5%A1%E8%81%AF%E7%9B%9F%E6%88%90%E7%AB%8B%E5%85%B8%E7%A6%AE.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em;
