mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">19</span>日，本网获悉：福州林兰英遭被告仓山区人社局克扣被征地农民养老保障金案，将于今日（<span lang="EN-US">2022.01.19</span>）上午<span lang="EN-US">9</span>点在鼓楼法院第十四法庭公开开庭审理。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体;
