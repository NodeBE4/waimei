[辽宁大连一居民楼发生燃气爆炸 造成两死七伤](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20211024-1206422)
------

<div>
<p>中国辽宁大连瓦房店市委宣传部透露，今天凌晨5时30分许，瓦房店市铁东街道办事处文圣社区一居民楼发生燃气闪爆事故，造成两人死亡，七人受伤。</p><p>据中新社报道，现明火已被扑灭，搜救工作已结束，伤员均已送医救治。同时，周边群众已紧急疏散并得到安置。目前事故原因正在调查中。</p><p>这是继沈阳之后，又一起燃气爆炸事故。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>沈阳一家餐馆周四（21日）发生燃气爆炸事故，爆炸威力强大，波及多栋建筑和多辆汽车，造成五人死亡，多人受伤。</p><p>沈阳官方在微博上公布，燃气爆炸事故于当天早上8时20分许发生，地点位于沈阳市和平区一家名为“王二牛烤骨头店”的餐馆。</p><p>沈阳燃气集团工作人员在答复中国媒体询问时说，事发区域当时正在进行燃气管网改造施工，通常都是在晚上进行，早上结束，但暂时无法确认爆炸事故是否与施工有关。</p><div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div><p>　　</p>            <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/zbpe-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=zbpe399&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_desktop-zbpe399_2.png?dHEiGouxxZpWwWTJE04dyGyUN74JOU2z" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_desktop-zbpe399_2.png?dHEiGouxxZpWwWTJE04dyGyUN74JOU2z" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/zbpe-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=zbpe399&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_mobile-zbpe399_2.png?qZnBcIGCJvPR8A2IAZOZuOxdoGZDatiI" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_mobile-zbpe399_2.png?qZnBcIGCJvPR8A2IAZOZuOxdoGZDatiI" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
