font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">堵路堵了<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个小时，长达<span lang="EN-US">3</span>公里，损失<span lang="EN-US">160</span>万<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></b></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">由于摆摊占道经营，造成道路拥堵混乱，导致多起交通事故，多人在此处丧生。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">王素霞不是普通人，区委书记都没办法解决<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></b></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体;
