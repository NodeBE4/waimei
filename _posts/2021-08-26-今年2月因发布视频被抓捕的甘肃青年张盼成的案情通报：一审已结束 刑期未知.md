[今年2月因发布视频被抓捕的甘肃青年张盼成的案情通报：一审已结束 刑期未知](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2021/08/2_26.html)
------

<div>
<p><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;"><span></span></span></p><a name='more'></a><br /><p></p><p>（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">2021</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">年</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">8</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">月</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">26</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">维权人士华涌今天通报了张盼成的案情：“两周前张盼成一审结束，没有得到律师和外界关注，现在不知道判决结果，家里没拿到判决书，开庭有官派律师。从张盼成母亲处得知张盼成拒不认罪并想上诉，父亲不同意贫困的家庭也无法承担律师费用。张盼成目前仍然没有律师，更没有得到外界会见，他在看守所的情况无人知晓，是否会像张展那样？让人担忧。”<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">1995</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2018</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">18</span>日，被北京市警方以涉嫌“寻衅滋事罪”正式逮捕；审讯中，拒绝写保证书，并拒绝给律师签委托书；<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">21</span>日，其案在北京西城区法院开庭审理；<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">4</span>日，被北京市西城区法院一审以“寻衅滋事罪”判处有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">1</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月，至<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">20</span>日，刑满释放；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2021</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">14</span>日，因其在网上发布一段在甘肃山村老家里录制的视频，谴责中共暴行，表达心声称“凛凛寒冬终将过去，春天马上要来临”，随后被当地警方带走，后遭刑事拘留、批捕和起诉。</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">目前被羁押于甘肃省庆阳市合水县看守所。</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast;
