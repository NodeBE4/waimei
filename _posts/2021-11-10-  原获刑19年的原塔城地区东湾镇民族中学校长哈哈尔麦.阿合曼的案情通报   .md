<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">10</span>日，本网获悉原获刑19年的原塔城地区东湾镇民族中学校长哈哈尔麦<span lang="EN-US">.</span>阿合曼的案情，现在通报如下：<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">哈哈尔麦<span lang="EN-US">.</span>阿合曼：<span lang="EN-US">1</span></span></b><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">970</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">3</span>日出生，新疆塔城沙湾县人，哈萨克族，塔城地区托里县托里镇复兴路小区居民，前塔城地区东湾镇民族中学校长，前塔城地区沙湾县畜牧局党委书记，原塔城地区沙湾县宗教事务办公室领导，原塔城地区沙湾县第二中学校长，中国在押政治犯。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">早年曾就读于新疆塔城师范学院中专，<span lang="EN-US">1989</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月毕业后又在新疆教育学院政治系进修大专，并于<span lang="EN-US">1992</span>年毕业；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">曾常年在民族学校历任校长，<span lang="EN-US">1995</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月曾任新疆塔城地区东湾镇民族中学校长，<span lang="EN-US">2000</span>年至被捕前，曾长期担任塔城地区沙湾县第二中学校长等；因工作敬业、成绩斐然，曾被当地政府多次评为“先进个人”<span lang="EN-US">/</span>“双文明先进个人”；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2018</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">5</span>日，被沙湾县警方突然带走，并被送某教育集中营接受“政治再教育”；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">后被转为正式逮捕，指控其在工作期间因严重违反法律和违反党纪，并在宗教事务办公室期间签署建造清真寺的合同，有贿赂之嫌，所以应予逮捕判刑；<span lang="EN-US">2019</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月，知其已在同年早些时候被新疆塔城地区法院以“违反党纪国法罪”和“受贿罪”秘密重判有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">19</span>年；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2021</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">9</span>日获悉，沙湾县中共纪律检查委员会（简称“纪检委”）曾向其家属明确表示，如要让其释放，必须交纳保释金<span lang="EN-US">40</span>万元人民币，后其家人实际交保<span lang="EN-US">80</span>万元，故其最终被执行有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">4</span>年，刑期至<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月—<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月；另据悉，其在被判刑前曾被迫认罪，身体极差，家属被软禁，探视权被剥夺。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;
