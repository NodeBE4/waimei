mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">张宝成的辩护人卢庭阁律师介绍说：“本案历经本院延审<span lang="EN-US">1</span>次，报请最高法院延审<span lang="EN-US">2</span>次，用尽了延审程序，理由均为案情重大复杂，理应开庭审理。我们辩护律师也多次提出书面和口头申请，要求开庭及理由。但，法院执意不开庭审理，最后做出意料之中的维持裁判，这种走过场的审判，严重违反了《刑诉法》设立二审终审的监督纠正程序，大大增加了冤假错案的发生率，责任谁来负？”<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">而今天福建公民陆祚钰、李穗财、张华到现场声援。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">1959</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月—<span lang="EN-US">2013</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月，再次因积极参与“官员财产公示”活动，并先后三次分别于<span lang="EN-US">2013</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">27</span>日在北京朝阳区朝阳公园和清华大学附近拉横幅、拍照，于<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">23</span>日在海淀区中关村广场等地继续拉横幅、发放传单，于<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">31</span>日在西城区西单文化广场再次拉横幅、发放传单，对围观群众演讲、录像和拍照等，这些活动讯息被迅速传到互联网上，引发网民高度关注，随引发了中共当局极度的恐慌和不满。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">10</span>日一审宣判，张宝成获刑<span lang="EN-US">3</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月。其不服上诉，今二审驳回上诉，维持原判。目前关押在北京市第三看守所。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;
