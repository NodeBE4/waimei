<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">29</span>日，本网获悉：于<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月被刑事拘留的北京维权人士全世欣女士一审获刑<span lang="EN-US">2</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月，其不服已上诉。刑期至：<span lang="EN-US">2025</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月，目前其被羁押在海淀看守所。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">全世欣女士<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">5</span>日就两会被维稳监控接受了美国之音采访，随即遭抓捕，其推特账号停留在<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">5</span>日。其身患心脏病，其狱中健康令人担忧。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">全世欣简历</span></b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">：女，<span lang="EN-US">1976</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">1</span>日出生，北京市人，北京市海淀区四季青东平庄<span lang="EN-US">56</span>号居民，维权人士，贪腐举报人，网络推友。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">早年曾因个人宅基地及住房被政府强拆而被迫维权多年，至今仍未获批宅基地或获赔住房，成为无家可归的北京土著农民；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">在维权过程中，因发现地方干部有严重贪腐现象，并公开向多个职能部门举报，随遭地方当权者严厉打压和非法拘押；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">2018</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">21</span>日，因使用互联网翻墙软件在推特上发表对中共当局及执政者批评言论，被网警监控发现，而被北京海淀区警方因言获罪，立刻以<span lang="EN-US">“</span>寻衅滋事<span lang="EN-US">”</span>罪由行政拘留<span lang="EN-US">10</span>天；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">2019</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">3</span>日，又因身穿写有<span lang="EN-US">“</span>蔡奇见家属<span lang="EN-US">”</span>字样的白色短袖<span lang="EN-US">T</span>恤，高喊要求与市政府相关负责人见面递交相关材料，寻求解决问题，而被海淀区警方以<span lang="EN-US">“</span>扰乱公共场所秩序<span lang="EN-US">”</span>罪由行政拘留<span lang="EN-US">5</span>天；<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">25</span>日，再次被海淀区警方莫名带走，后被以<span lang="EN-US">“</span>寻衅滋事<span lang="EN-US">”</span>罪由刑拘，好友疑称此与其曾在推特上多次发支持香港<span lang="EN-US">“</span>反送中<span lang="EN-US">”</span>抗议游行等言论有关；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">2019</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">31</span>日，其被北京市海淀区警方以涉嫌<span lang="EN-US">“</span>寻衅滋事罪<span lang="EN-US">”</span>正式逮捕；<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">26</span>日，其被起诉，起诉书称其<span lang="EN-US">“</span>自<span lang="EN-US">2019</span>年注册并使用推特，散布大量虚假信息，严重扰乱社会秩序<span lang="EN-US">”</span>，因而触犯刑法，需要提交法院予以审判；同年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">26</span>日，其案在北京市海淀区法院开庭受理，后又长期休庭；<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">21</span>日，终被该法院以<span lang="EN-US">“</span>寻衅滋事罪<span lang="EN-US">”</span>判处有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">2</span>年；<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">21</span>日，刑满释放。此前被羁押于北京市海淀区看守所。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">2023</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">5</span>日，又遭北京当局抓捕。后被刑事拘留、批捕、起诉。<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">29</span>日获悉，全世欣女士一审获刑<span lang="EN-US">2</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月，其不服已上诉。刑期至：<span lang="EN-US">2025</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月，目前其被羁押在海淀看守所。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体;
