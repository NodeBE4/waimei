[获刑7年的著名人权律师周世峰今天刑满出狱](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2022/09/7.html)
------

<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">24</span>日，本网获悉获刑<span lang="EN-US">7</span>年的著名人权律师周世峰今天刑满出狱。周世锋先生目前已经回到北京的家中，他非常想念朋友们。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">周世峰</span></b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">：<span lang="EN-US">1964</span>年出生，河南省安阳县人，北京锋锐律师事务所主任，<span lang="EN-US"> 2009</span>年度、<span lang="EN-US">2010</span>年度“社会公益法律服务先进个人”、<span lang="EN-US">2010</span>年—<span lang="EN-US">2011</span>年中国法律援助“优秀援助律师” 、<span lang="EN-US"> 2009-2011</span>年度“北京优秀律师”，知名人权律师，中国政治犯。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">自<span lang="EN-US">1995</span>年执业以来，曾代理过多起著名的受害人案件，并于<span lang="EN-US">2007</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月创立北京锋锐律师事务所，广纳包括王宇、刘晓原、王全章等在内的著名人权律师；<span lang="EN-US">2008</span>年，曾代理三鹿毒奶粉案，但因法庭故意不予受理，遭遇极大困难；<span lang="EN-US">2001</span>年，曾代理石家庄“<span lang="EN-US">3</span>·<span lang="EN-US">16</span>”特大爆炸案；<span lang="EN-US">2014</span>年，曾代理老作家铁流被控“非法经营罪”案、德国《时代周报》驻京记者助理张淼被控“寻衅滋事罪”案及北京维权公民叶洪霞被控“寻衅滋事罪”案等；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2015</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年，曾与杨金柱、杨学林律师发起成立“聂树斌案律师独立观察团”等，并公开表示欲出资<span lang="EN-US">800</span>万人民币建立“中国律师维权基金”，向全国各地受迫害律师家属提供资助；由于以上言行，随触怒了中共当局的敏感神经，遭致中共当局统一部署下的严厉打击；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2015</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">10</span>日，被北京市警方以“严重扰乱社会秩序”、“教唆、组织、策划、资助他人炒作所谓敏感案件” 等为罪由传唤并指定地点监视居住，其锋锐律师事务所及其办公室被查抄；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2016</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">8</span>日，被天津市警方以涉嫌“颠覆国家政权罪”正式逮捕，并被关押于天津市第一看守所；<span lang="EN-US">2016</span>年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">4</span>日，被天津市第二中级法院以同罪名判处有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">7</span>年，剥夺政治权利<span lang="EN-US">5</span>年，刑期至<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">24</span>日。此前在天津市第一监狱服刑。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"></p><div class="separator" style="clear: both;
