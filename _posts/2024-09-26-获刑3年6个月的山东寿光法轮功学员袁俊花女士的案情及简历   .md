<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">27</span>日，本网获悉获刑<span lang="EN-US">3</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月的山东寿光法轮功学员袁俊花女士的案情及简历，现在通报如下：<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">袁俊花：</span></b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">山东寿光法轮功学员，女，<span lang="EN-US">1964</span>年生，寿光市仙霞服装退休职工，她家住山东省寿光市教授新村。一九九八年开始修炼法轮功。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">2023</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">28</span>日上午，寿光市国保及寿光市古城街道派出所警察闯入袁俊花的家，抓捕了她。<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">29</span>日，袁俊花被放回家。袁俊花为了躲避迫害，被迫流离失所。<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">13</span>日上午，袁俊花因回家一趟，被警察跟踪，在租住处被寿光警察抓捕。当天下午，袁俊花被关押到潍坊市看守所。<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">19</span>日，山东省寿光法院第三次对袁俊花开庭，袁俊花被判刑期<span lang="EN-US">3</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月、罚款三万元。刑期至：<span lang="EN-US">2027</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">12</span>日。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p>
