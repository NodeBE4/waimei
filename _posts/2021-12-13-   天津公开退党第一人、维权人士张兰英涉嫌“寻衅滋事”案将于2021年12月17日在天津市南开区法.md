<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">13</span>日，本网获悉：遭羁押近<span lang="EN-US">1</span>年的天津公开退党第一人、维权人士张兰英涉嫌“寻衅滋事”案将于<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">17</span>日在天津市南开区法院开庭审理。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2009</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年，曾因其私人住宅被天津市南开区政府在无任何有效拆迁手续和拆迁补偿的情况下，强行拆除而致流离失所，并对其及年迈父母实施了一系列惨无人道的违法迫害，遂被迫走上维权抗争之路；又因屡次上访并支持、帮助他人维权，随遭天津当局强力打压；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2012</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2014</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">28</span>日，其及<span lang="EN-US">6</span>名捐款人随被当地派出所警方悉数抓走，其被以“扰乱公共场所秩序”为由行政拘留<span lang="EN-US">15</span>天；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">24</span>日，曾因持续奔走维权而被天津市南开区警方再以涉嫌“寻衅滋事罪”被刑拘，同年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">30</span>日又被南开区检察院转正式批捕，控其曾利用网络注册‘天津张兰英<span lang="EN-US">7</span>’等多个新浪微博账户发布“虚假言论”，散布南开区政府对其设置“黑监狱”等“不实言论”，随应批捕、判刑；后其被南开区法院冤判<span lang="EN-US">3</span>年，直至<span lang="EN-US">2018</span>年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">8</span>日才得释放；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2021</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">4</span>日，又被南开区检察院以同罪名批捕，指控其因在微博发布自己的冤情，引起社会热议，致其该微博转发量多达<span lang="EN-US">500+</span>，且网络媒体对其冤情进行追踪报导及其进京在国家信访局登记控诉等，其言行属于违法； <span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">17</span>日将在天津市南开区法院开庭审理。目前张兰英被羁押于天津市南开区看守所。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体;
