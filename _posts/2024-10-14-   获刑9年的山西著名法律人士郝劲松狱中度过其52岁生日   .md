[获刑9年的山西著名法律人士郝劲松狱中度过其52岁生日](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2024/10/952.html)
------

<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">15</span>日。本网获悉：获刑<span lang="EN-US">9</span>年的山西著名法律人士郝劲松<span lang="EN-US">1972</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">13</span>日出生，其在山西省太原市第二监狱服刑度过其<span lang="EN-US">52</span>岁生日。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">郝劲松：</span></b><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">1972</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">13</span>日出生，山西省忻州市定襄县人，中国政法大学法学硕士，法律公益研究中心研究员，<span lang="EN-US">2005</span>年度中国“十大法制人物”荣获者，“诉讼狂人”，维权代言人，知名公益法律人。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">郝劲松自<span lang="EN-US">2004</span>年开始先后<span lang="EN-US">7</span>次将国家税务总局、北京地铁运营公司、北京铁路局告上法庭，原他因对“打破行业‘霸王条款’起到了一定作用”而入选“<span lang="EN-US">2004</span>年构建经济和谐十大受尊崇人物”。郝劲松被先后提名为<span lang="EN-US">2005</span>中国法制新闻人物，<span lang="EN-US">2005</span>年度十大法制人物，并以“维权战士”的身份写入《<span lang="EN-US">2005</span>中国法治蓝皮书》。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">因介入推动铁道部春运不涨价案件入选《<span lang="EN-US">2006</span>中国法治蓝皮书》，并当选<span lang="EN-US">2006</span>中国十大消费维权人物，<span lang="EN-US">2008</span>年因介入陕西华南虎案件和杨佳案被《南方人物周刊》评选为“中国魅力人物——公义之魅”。<span lang="EN-US">2009</span>年，介入上海“钓鱼执法事件”。后成立山西定襄县劲松法务咨询中心。<span lang="EN-US">2017</span>年，郝劲松向定襄县环保局实名举报当地的诚泰纺织印染公司涉嫌污染空气和水源。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">郝劲松于<span lang="EN-US">2019</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">10</span>日、<span lang="EN-US">11</span>日就连续两天遭到户籍地山西省定襄县晋昌镇派出所以“寻衅滋事”为由传唤后，<span lang="EN-US">2019</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">17</span>日再遭该派出所传唤。随后被行政拘留<span lang="EN-US">15</span>天。<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">2</span>日，行政拘留期满被转刑事拘留，控罪为涉嫌寻衅滋事。当日，郝劲松被控涉嫌寻衅滋事遭定襄县公安局刑事拘留。羁押于五台县看守所。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">案件久不审理，一直拖延至<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">12</span>至<span lang="EN-US">13</span>日在山西定襄县法院开庭审理。开庭审理一年多后，于<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">20</span>日，被超期羁押超过<span lang="EN-US">1200</span>天的著名法律公益人、法律学者郝劲松先生被以诈骗罪判刑<span lang="EN-US">8</span>年、寻衅滋事罪判刑<span lang="EN-US">1</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>个月，合并执行有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">9</span>年。其不服上诉，<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">12</span>日二审驳回上诉，维持原判。刑期至：<span lang="EN-US">2029</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">1</span>日。郝劲松<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月被转监至<a name="_Hlk179853315">山西省太原市第二监狱服刑</a>。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">对郝劲松的狱中境况，本网将持续关注。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"></p><div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;"><a href="https://blogger.googleusercontent.com/img/b/R29vZ2xl/AVvXsEjsTElA9E9tkVCgprMYjPuVY7Gi2HJS6etbDCWoUZN8X531-pskA2XlEDkQ_Ptoimc2tcBjiQt2z7dEKASL8wxONcXiSsQSBklofZ5fkmaW25J1jYmIvMZgykvL1UCbmVKsF24vfrGIJvm9KxM_jAZ0bi5T53OmE1AgoL2JDWTvzcn_7Q_yYNFkLhrddbmW/s900/GZ0JkfYXYAEbknd.jfif" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em;
