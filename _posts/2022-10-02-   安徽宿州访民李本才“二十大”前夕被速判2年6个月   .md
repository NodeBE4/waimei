[安徽宿州访民李本才“二十大”前夕被速判2年6个月](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2022/10/26.html)
------

<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">2</span>日，本网获悉：安徽省宿州市访民李本才因为果园被侵占，房屋被强拆而上访维权已<span lang="EN-US">27</span>年。今年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月，李本才因为一句话地方不解决就找中央而被抓捕，<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">29</span>日宿州市埇桥区法院开庭审理了该案，最后以“寻衅滋事罪”进行了速判，判决<span lang="EN-US">2</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2022</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">4</span>日，李本才到宿州市信访局上访，要求解决其反映的事情，宿州市信访局给埇桥区开转办单。李本才到捅桥区信访局，给接待官员表明，如果区政府还不给解决问题，就只能到北京找党给解决了。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">到<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">20</span>日，宿州市埇桥区公安分局三八派出所到李本才家要抓李本才，因为当时拿着两个不同时间的刑事拘通知书被发现，李本才逃过一劫。而到<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">30</span>日，三八派出所警察又到李本才家，拿着<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">4</span>日的拘留通知书，把李本才从家里带走。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">李本才因为果园被埇桥区政府晚上没人看，安排人偷偷铲除而上访维权<span lang="EN-US">27</span>年。在李本才长年在北京边打工边上访维权至今，多次被拘留。访友吴继新认为，当局这么快就判决，明显跟“二十大”维稳有关。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;
