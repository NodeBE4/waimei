[获刑14年6个月的新疆克州阿图什市维吾尔族商人艾山江•卡日的案情及简历](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2023/01/146.html)

<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p align="left" class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: left;"><span style="color: black; font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-themecolor: text1;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">5</span>日，本网获悉获刑<span lang="EN-US">14</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月的新疆克州阿图什市维吾尔族商人艾山江•卡日的案情及简历，现在通报如下：<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p align="left" class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: left;"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="color: black; font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-themecolor: text1;">艾山江·卡日</span></b><span style="color: black; font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-themecolor: text1;">：<span lang="EN-US">1969</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">20</span>日出生，维吾尔族，维名<span lang="EN-US">Hesenjan Qari</span>，新疆克孜勒苏柯尔克孜自治州（简称“克州”）阿图什市松他克乡人，乌兹别克斯坦国移居者，纺织品对外贸易经销商，中国在押政治犯。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p align="left" class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: left;"><span style="color: black; font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-themecolor: text1;">早年曾生长于新疆西南部的克孜勒苏柯尔克孜自治州首府阿图什市，中学毕业后开始经商，并多年从事中国和中亚（主指乌兹别克斯坦国等）之间的纺织品贸易；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p align="left" class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: left;"><span lang="EN-US" style="color: black; font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-themecolor: text1;">1997</span><span style="color: black; font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-themecolor: text1;">年，因在乌兹别克斯坦遇到其妻子古尔森（维族名<span lang="EN-US">Gulshan Manapova</span>，乌兹别克斯坦国公民，从小讲维吾尔语和俄语，其父母家人曾因中国文化大革命而被迫于<span lang="EN-US">1969</span>年逃离中国前往哈萨克斯坦，后移居乌兹别克斯坦国而成为该国合法公民），遂结婚生子，并一起为自己的家庭及未来而努力经营、打造；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p align="left" class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: left;"><span style="color: black; font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-themecolor: text1;">自<span lang="EN-US">2017</span>年起，其在哈萨克开办了一家布料店，将新疆的纺织品拿到哈国买卖，生意十分兴隆，然当其在中国境内的家人电告要其回国探访，同时，新疆老家政府亦要求其尽快回国接受问讯之后，其人生轨迹骤然发生变化；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p align="left" class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: left;"><span style="color: black; font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-themecolor: text1;">起初回国，其及妻子儿女均未料到中共政府会对他感兴趣，但在<span lang="EN-US">2017</span>年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月返回新疆一周之后，其即被当地官员带走问讯，并将其护照和哈萨克居民卡没收；同年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月，又被送一所再教育营关押，从此失联；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p align="left" class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: left;"><span style="color: black; font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-themecolor: text1;">后知<span lang="EN-US">2018</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月，其曾被阿图什市法院以不具名罪判处有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">5</span>年，但同年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月，据在国内的亲戚说又被警方带回阿图什市第五区营地接受再教育，并警方要求其亲戚为他寄送衣服；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p align="left" class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: left;"><span lang="EN-US" style="color: black; font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-themecolor: text1;">2019</span><span style="color: black; font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-themecolor: text1;">年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月，其被阿图什市法院再次以“加入恐怖组织”和“利用极端主义破坏执法” 罪名重新重判有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">14</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月，刑期至<span lang="EN-US">2032</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p align="left" class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: left;"><span style="color: black; font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-themecolor: text1;">据悉，自其被刑拘羁押至一审判刑上诉以来，一直身体健康极差，警方通知其亲戚他已在医院接受治疗；因其被抓捕判刑，其妻子儿女一家人长期深陷生活窘境；其在再教育营培训期间，曾和众多人一起被强制灌输共产主义意识形态，教授中共强调的所谓中国文化，并拒绝沿袭和使用伊斯兰教习俗等等。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p align="left" class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: left;"><span style="color: black; font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-themecolor: text1;">目前在疆生产建设兵团第三师图木舒克监狱第五监区服刑。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p align="left" class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: left;"></p><div class="separator" style="clear: both;
