font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2000</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">25</span>日，曾因在天安门广场拉横幅为法轮功正名而和平请愿吁呼，故而连续遭广场便衣警察及通州某派出所警方暴力殴打致昏迷；后被关押于通州乔庄看守所期间，为胁迫其放弃法轮功信仰，又连续遭警方恶意暴打、电棍电击及强行灌食等非人道逼迫；同年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">27</span>日，曾因在家制作法轮功信仰者遭迫害讯息，而再遭北京市朝阳区警方抓捕，并被施以酷刑，最终导致其一只胳膊残废不能动，之后被北京市高级法院以“邪教罪”枉法重判<span lang="EN-US">8</span>年，后送北京市前进监狱服刑；刑满出狱后，<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2009</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">3</span>日，又因自由信仰事宜而被北京市朝阳区警方拘捕，后被朝阳区法院判刑<span lang="EN-US">4</span>年；第二次出狱后，<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月、<span lang="EN-US">2017</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月，因参加法轮功家庭式交流聚会和向他人宣传法轮功信息，而曾两次又被当地警方刑拘，后获释；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2022</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;
