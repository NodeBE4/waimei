[阿里巴巴跌向纪录低点 投资者押注京东](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20211123-1216231)
------

<div>
<p>阿里巴巴新一轮的跌势已吐回10月的涨幅，股价也迈向纪录低点；竞争对手京东的复苏正在扩大并赢得分析师的青睐。</p><p>据彭博社报道，德意志银行Leo Chiang周一以“近期挑战”为由，将阿里巴巴在香港的股票目标价下调了近4%，同时将京东的目标价上调了16%，原因是“在宏观不确定性中实现了弹性增长”。</p><p>晨星公司的Chelsey Tam在11月19日的一份报告中，也呼应了类似的观点，认为阿里巴巴的挑战不止是经济周期，京东在长期利润率的改善上更清晰。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>截至中午12时，阿里巴巴股价下跌3%至132.90港元，本月跌幅达到18%，抵消了10月份的所有涨幅。虽然京东盘中也下跌，但与大盘一致，且较8月份低点仍高出约46%。</p><p> </p>            <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/zbpe-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=zbpe399&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_desktop-zbpe399_2.png?VersionId=dHEiGouxxZpWwWTJE04dyGyUN74JOU2z" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_desktop-zbpe399_2.png?VersionId=dHEiGouxxZpWwWTJE04dyGyUN74JOU2z" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/zbpe-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=zbpe399&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_mobile-zbpe399_2.png?VersionId=qZnBcIGCJvPR8A2IAZOZuOxdoGZDatiI" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_mobile-zbpe399_2.png?VersionId=qZnBcIGCJvPR8A2IAZOZuOxdoGZDatiI" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>    <div class="cx_paywall_placeholder" id="sph_cdp_40"></div>
