<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">19</span>日，本网获悉：一审获刑<span lang="EN-US">4</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月的著名人权捍卫者季孝龙案的二审将于<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">21</span>日下午<span lang="EN-US">1</span>点<span lang="EN-US">45</span>分在上海第一中级人民法院第六庭开庭。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;">上海著名人权捍卫者季孝龙于<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">31</span>日因替封城时的上海人民发声而遭抓捕，一审被以寻衅滋事罪判刑<span lang="EN-US">4</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月。季孝龙不服判决依法上诉，二审将于<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">21</span>日下午<span lang="EN-US">1</span>点<span lang="EN-US">45</span>分在上海第一中级人民法院第六庭开庭。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><b><span face="楷体" style="font-size: 12pt;">季孝龙：<span lang="EN-US">1</span></span></b><span face="楷体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt;">976</span><span face="楷体" style="font-size: 12pt;">年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">10</span>日出生，江苏省张家港市人，张家港市杨舍镇名都花苑居民，基督徒，维权公民，民主人士，人权捍卫者，中国在押政治犯。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span face="楷体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt;">2015</span><span face="楷体" style="font-size: 12pt;">年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月，曾因宣传民主理念而被上海市浦东新区警方以<span lang="EN-US">“</span>寻衅滋事<span lang="EN-US">”</span>罪由传唤，并被处以行政拘留<span lang="EN-US">10</span>日；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span face="楷体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt;">2018</span><span face="楷体" style="font-size: 12pt;">年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">20</span>日，曾因在网上发文，呼吁民主人士响应<span lang="EN-US">“</span>厕所革命<span lang="EN-US">”</span>，并在医院、大学等公共场所的厕所门上，用马克笔写宣传语<span lang="EN-US">“</span>大病治不起，习胖狂撒币，修宪妄称帝、苦难何时毕！打倒共产党。还政于民，结束民众苦难。推翻习核心，拒走文革路<span lang="EN-US">”</span>等字句，短时间内响应者众多，随于<span lang="EN-US">2018</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">30</span>日，被以同罪由转正式逮捕，关押在上海市浦东新区看守所；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span face="楷体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt;">2018</span><span face="楷体" style="font-size: 12pt;">年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">30</span>日，其案被上海市浦东新区检察院起诉至上海市浦东新区法院；<span lang="EN-US">2019</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">14</span>日，其最终被该法院以<span lang="EN-US">“</span>寻衅滋事罪<span lang="EN-US">”</span>一审判处有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">3</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月，后送上海市白茅岭监狱七监区服刑；据悉，羁押期间，警方曾多次以让其<span lang="EN-US">“</span>死亡<span lang="EN-US">”</span>和用寒冷气温逼迫其认罪，致其身体健康虚弱且生病吐血；其家属曾送羽绒服和《圣经》书籍亦遭拒收；<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">9</span>日，刑满获释；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span face="楷体" style="font-size: 12pt;">同年上海封城，其为上海市民持续发声。<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">31</span>日，再次被上海市梅园新村派出所警察从家中带走，后被上海市浦东新区警方以<span lang="EN-US">“</span>寻衅滋事罪<span lang="EN-US">”</span>刑拘；同年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">23</span>日，又被浦东新检察院以同罪名正式批捕，后季孝龙于<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">17</span>日被起诉。后已经开庭，未宣判。最终于<span
