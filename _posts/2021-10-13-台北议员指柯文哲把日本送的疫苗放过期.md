[台北议员指柯文哲把日本送的疫苗放过期](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20211013-1202861)
------

<div>
<p>台北市无党籍议员林颖孟指，她将在今天公布证据，台北市有一批日本送的疫苗，系统显示已被放到过期。林颖孟就此事批评台北市长柯文哲：“你要怎么负责？你又要甩锅给谁？”</p><p>据联合报报道，林颖孟说，柯文哲平常爱讲“疫苗不够”，现在被抓到疫苗过期，白白糟蹋日本对台湾的支持。</p><p>林颖孟指出，7月台北市府拍胸脯保证，会将北市各医院剂量调配完成，在效期前接种完毕，也会到各医院盘点，确实做到没有任何一剂疫苗过期或被收回。结果现在台北市的疫苗放到过期，北市府对市民承诺又破功。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>林颖孟质疑：“请问柯市长，你到底怎么管理的？你要怎么负责？你又要甩锅给谁？”林颖孟也呼吁柯文哲，今天好好向大众说明情况。</p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/zbpe-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=zbpe399&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_desktop-zbpe399_2.png?dHEiGouxxZpWwWTJE04dyGyUN74JOU2z" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_desktop-zbpe399_2.png?dHEiGouxxZpWwWTJE04dyGyUN74JOU2z" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/zbpe-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=zbpe399&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_mobile-zbpe399_2.png?qZnBcIGCJvPR8A2IAZOZuOxdoGZDatiI" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_mobile-zbpe399_2.png?qZnBcIGCJvPR8A2IAZOZuOxdoGZDatiI" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
