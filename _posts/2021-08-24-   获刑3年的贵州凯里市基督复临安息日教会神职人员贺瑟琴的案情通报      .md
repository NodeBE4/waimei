font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">贺瑟琴：</span></b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">自<span lang="EN-US">2000</span>年左右贵州省凯里市基督复临安息日教会聚会点成立以来，因其积极热忱地服务于该教会聚会点中的各项事务，随很快成为教会福音事工活动的中间力量和相关侍奉工作的负责人，并为众信徒及其他侍奉同道所称许；但因其教会聚会点<span lang="EN-US">20</span>多年来一直采取非官方的家庭教会聚会模式，并对中共当局严格把控的“三自教会”管理模式拒不认同和接受，遂遭贵州当局在意识形态上的严苛限制，导致该教会聚会点虽早已向贵州省宗教局提交了请求聚会点合法申请书，但却始终未获审批；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2020</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月，又因其聚会点信徒不断剧增，聚会点由最先<span lang="EN-US">1</span>个扩建变成<span lang="EN-US">3</span>个，随很快引起贵州省黔东南州当局的极度恐慌，即刻组成立项目组对各聚会点进行全面排查，并将其及教会长老等<span lang="EN-US">4</span>名神职人员被黔东南州凯里市警方传唤带走，后被以涉嫌“诈骗罪”刑拘，自此失联；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2020</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">4</span>日，其等<span lang="EN-US">4</span>人被凯里市检察院以同罪名予以批捕，指控其等因所在教会聚会点“请求聚会点合法申请书”未被国家审批，众信徒所交奉献款均属于诈骗款，故其在教会里的一切敬拜、事工行为等均属违法；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2021</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">14</span>日，其<span lang="EN-US">4</span>人案在黔东南州凯里市法院统一开庭审理，最终被分别以“诈骗罪”秘判<span lang="EN-US">12</span>—<span lang="EN-US">3</span>年不等刑期；其中，贺瑟琴被判处有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">3</span>年，刑期至<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月；另其教会<span lang="EN-US">140</span>万余元奉献款亦被宣判扣押，称将“由凯里市公安局依法发还被害人”；其不服，提起上诉；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">据称，黔东南州中级法院似乎不愿对其上诉案公开审理，且对辩护律师提交的新证据也无兴趣；二审正在等待中。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体;
