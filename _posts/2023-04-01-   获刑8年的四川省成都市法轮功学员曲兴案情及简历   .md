font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">：<span lang="EN-US">1989</span>年出生，原籍辽宁省沈阳市，四川省成都市长居公民，自由职业者，原为一心想出家的寻法人（即想当僧侣），后成为法轮功修炼者和信仰者，中国在押良心犯。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2018</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2021</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">7</span>日，因其在送外卖时黏贴了法轮功讯息的不干胶，遂其及与其同住的母亲当天即被成都市高新区肖家河派出所警方非法抓走，租住屋遭查抄；后其母亲被监视居住，<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月后被行政处罚<span lang="EN-US">900</span>元，而其却被成都市高新区警方以涉嫌“邪教罪”刑拘、逮捕；<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月，其案被高新区检察院提起公诉，并移交到高新区法院审查；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2023</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;
