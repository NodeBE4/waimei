<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">1976</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2020</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">25</span>日因其为保护和弘扬藏族文化而在网上发文指出学习藏文藏语是所有藏人的义务，完全符合中国宪法，遂引起四川省阿坝州当局的强烈关注和维稳打压，同年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">4</span>日，被阿坝州红原县警方强行带走，自此失联；后知被以“在社媒上造谣”为由刑拘、逮捕；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2023</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast;
