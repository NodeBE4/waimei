[云南怒江州福贡县基督教归正教会教案王顺平传道等五位基督徒均取保获释](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2023/05/blog-post_8.html)
------

<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">8</span>日，本网获悉：被抓捕<span lang="EN-US">8</span>个多月的云南怒江州福贡县基督教归正教会教案王顺平传道等五位基督徒均已取保获释。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2022</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">17</span>日，云南怒江州福贡县基督教归正教会王顺平与怒族基督徒的怒桑邓三罗波以及另外两位外地的女基督徒等人被以涉嫌“组织、资助非法聚会罪”抓走刑拘；于<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">24</span>日被以同样罪名正式逮捕。被羁押于怒江州福贡县看守所。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">王顺平简介：</span></b><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">1990</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年代出生，云南省怒江傈僳族自治州（简称怒江州）人，怒族，基督徒，怒江州福贡县一基督教归正教会事工人员，中国在押良心犯。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">早期，曾因信仰耶稣基督而到云南神学院攻读神学；<span lang="EN-US">2014</span>年毕业后返回老家，并一直在怒江州福贡县一基督教归正教会做传道人；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">近年来，因其信仰纯正，性格和善，对教会的服事工作十分热心，尽职尽责，遂深得教会教友们的信赖；同时，又因其努力尽一个基督徒在其信仰中的爱神爱人的本分等，多次在福贡县城传讲福音时，免费教授当地民众有关弹奏吉他和钢琴等乐器的方式及普及乐理知识，多次在开办（或举办）的少儿音乐培训班上对贫困家庭的孩子免收培训费等，故而受到当地民众的喜爱和好评；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2022</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">17</span>日，其与数名傈僳族、怒族基督徒突然被云南省怒江州福贡县警方带走，后与同是怒族基督徒的怒桑邓等人被以涉嫌“组织、资助非法聚会罪”抓走刑拘；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">据悉，另有一基督徒三罗波亦被刑拘，但涉罪不清；还有两位外地籍女基督徒也情况不明，暂时无法确定她们是否也被一同刑拘；此次大抓捕，亲友疑指恐疑与他们经常传播福音遭打压有关。被羁押于怒江州福贡县看守所。被关押<span lang="EN-US">8</span>个多月后，于<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">8</span>日王顺平等<span lang="EN-US">5</span>人被取保获释。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"></p><div class="separator" style="clear: both;
