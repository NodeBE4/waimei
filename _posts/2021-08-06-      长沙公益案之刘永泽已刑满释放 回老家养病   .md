[长沙公益案之刘永泽已刑满释放 回老家养病](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2021/08/blog-post_53.html)
------

<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">6</span>日，本网获悉：长沙富能公益案之程渊被判刑<span lang="EN-US">5</span>年有期徒刑，同案刘永泽（刘大志）获刑<span lang="EN-US">2</span>年、吴葛健雄获有刑<span lang="EN-US">3</span>年。刘永泽于<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">22</span>日已刑满释放，目前回老家养病。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">程渊妻子施明磊今通报：“永泽<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">22</span>日凌晨出狱，出狱后便立即被送回怀化新晃老家，现在在家养伤病，跟老母亲住。整整两年！永泽，你经历了什么？！<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">小吴，你是否个性依旧？而程渊<span lang="EN-US">5</span>年的刑期，更让我无尽的痛！”<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">刘永泽（刘大志）：</span></b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">湖南省长沙市人，乙肝病毒携带者及艾滋病病毒感染者等群体维权诉讼代理人，民间公益组织“长沙富能”<span lang="EN-US">NGO</span>成员，中国政治犯。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">曾多年从事公益维权活动，代理过多起乙肝病毒携带者、艾滋病病毒感染者维权诉讼等案件，深受弱势群体欢迎；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">近年来，因从事公益维权活动且与海外人道服务机构多有来往，随遭中共当局警觉与严密监控；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2019</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月，因在推特上转发同情香港「反送中」抗议行动的言论以及自由亚洲电台对香港反送中游行的报导，遂于<span lang="EN-US">2019</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">21</span>日被湖南省长沙市国家安全局警方突然跨境从广州市其办公室带走，并被以涉嫌“颠覆国家政权罪”刑拘；审讯期间，多被问及其在长沙富能所做工作内容、资金何来、与哪些海外机构有染或合作及其近期发推特所涉内容等详情；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">据悉，其事先委托代理律师向长沙市国家安全局了解详情遭拒，仅告知被控涉嫌“颠覆国家政权罪”，并与“长沙富能”另两名<span lang="EN-US">NGO</span>重要成员程渊、吴葛健雄被视为同一罪案；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2019</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">26</span>日，其被长沙市国家安全局以涉嫌“颠覆国家政权罪”正式逮捕；<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月初，其三人案在长沙市中级法院秘密开庭受审，但却一直审而未判；直至<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">20</span>日，终被长沙市中级法院以“颠覆国家政权罪”统一宣判，其中，其被判处有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">2</span>年；<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">22</span>日刑满释放。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">据悉，因其在看守所内身患胃病，导致身体状况每况愈下，其原所在公益组织长沙富能曾于<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">16</span>日致信湖南省国家安全厅，就其及一同被押的程渊、吴葛健雄的健康状况与羁押处境深表担忧和关注。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体;
