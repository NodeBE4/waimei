mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">11</span>日，本网获悉： <span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">11</span>日上午，云南楚雄州中级法院开庭宣判王藏、王利芹夫妇构成煽动颠覆国家政权，分别判刑<span lang="EN-US">4</span>年、<span lang="EN-US">2</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月。王藏、王利芹均当庭表示不服判决，提起上诉。以下是其一审判决书<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">二人分别于<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">30</span>日、<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">15</span>日被抓捕，所涉事实为王藏的公开言论、行为艺术、诗歌、获奖感言、媒体采访内容等。案件于<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">15</span>日开庭。成都卢思位律师在为王藏辩护期间被吊销律师执照，未能出庭辩护。北京张磊律师、重庆张庭源律师出庭为二人做了无罪辩护。王藏本人当庭以诗言志申明无罪。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">王藏简介：</span></b><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">1985</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">26</span>日出生，原名王玉文，笔名王藏、“小王子”，云南省楚雄市人，先锋诗人，自由作家，影视编剧，独立中文笔会会员，中国自由文化运动成员，政治犯。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">王藏曾于<span lang="EN-US">2003</span>年底以“小王子”为笔名开始涉足网络诗歌习作，自此走上自由写作之路，并对极权文化予以批判。<span lang="EN-US">2005</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月，加入独立中文笔会，同年遭到官方威胁和长达<span lang="EN-US">2</span>个多月的监视居住。<span lang="EN-US">2006</span>年—<span lang="EN-US">2007</span>年间因积极参与“中国自由文化运动”签名活动，又在《自由圣火》网站发文并设专栏、在博讯网上开设专栏发表文集等，而被贵州省遵义市警方于<span lang="EN-US">2007</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月再次强制监视居住<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月。<span lang="EN-US">2008</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月，荣获“<span lang="EN-US">2008</span>《自由圣火》写作奖”。<span lang="EN-US">2009</span>年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月，正式启用笔名“王藏”，藉以表达“来生愿做藏人”的意愿。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">自<span lang="EN-US">2007</span>年起，因其积极参与多项维权民主活动、广交民主正义之士，并创作、推出了大量诗歌作品以及行为艺术，曾被北京当局多次警告威胁并驱离工作室和居住地，给其家庭造成诸多困扰。<span lang="EN-US">2014</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">1</span>日，因其在网上发布声援香港“雨伞革命”的打伞图片，随被北京市宋庄警方带走，其家被抄，之后被以涉嫌“寻衅滋事罪”刑拘于北京市第一看守所。<span lang="EN-US">2014</span>年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">6</span>日，被北京市当局以同罪名正式逮捕，其罪由包括行为艺术和诗歌、文章，网上发布声援香港“占中”图片，声援郭飞雄，声援建三江被虐打人权律师，在网上祭奠林昭，关注藏人自焚事件，声援新疆维吾尔族学者伊力哈木•土赫提、法轮功问题、纪念“六四”及揭批文革等多项内容。<span lang="EN-US">2015</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">9</span>日，被取保候审释放；据悉，其在被审讯期间遭受酷刑。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2020</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">30</span>日早上<span lang="EN-US">4.30</span>分左右，被云南省楚雄市公安机关突然以涉嫌煽动颠覆罪由带走，后遭刑事拘留逮捕，被正式批捕的日期是<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">3</span>日。现在羁押在云南楚雄市看守所。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2022</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">11</span>日上午，云南楚雄州中级法院开庭宣判王藏、王利芹夫妇构成煽动颠覆国家政权，分别判刑<span lang="EN-US">4</span>年、<span lang="EN-US">2</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月。王藏、王利芹均当庭表示不服判决，提起上诉。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">女，云南省昆明市人，又名王丽，职业不详，著名自由派先锋诗人及作家王藏（本名王玉文）之妻。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2017</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月，曾因云南当局强行要求王藏一家从北京迁回云南昆明原籍，其又被不明人士威胁、殴打，故而精神再度失常，导致其抑郁症发作，严重时曾出现精神恍惚、昏迷不醒，离家出走甚至跳湖自杀等现象；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体;
