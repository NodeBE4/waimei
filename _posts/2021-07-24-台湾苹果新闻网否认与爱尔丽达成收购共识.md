[台湾苹果新闻网否认与爱尔丽达成收购共识](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210724-1173024)
------

<div>
<p>台湾苹果新闻网今天在网站发声明指出，外传“苹果新闻网与爱尔丽集团协商已完成初步共识，交易金额（暂未透露）与员工保留规划已向香港母公司陈报”是假消息，公司特此声明绝无此事。</p><p>据台湾中央社报道，壹传媒6月于香港交易所公告，与潜在买方就出售台湾苹果新闻网订意向书。市场当时传出，前两大潜在买家为宝佳集团与台湾医美爱尔丽集团，随后爱尔丽集团董事长常如山证实有在接洽。有消息称，双方已达初步共识。</p><p>对此，苹果新闻网表示，苹果新闻网隶属壹传媒上市公司，如果有业务变动，自会发布公告，严正呼吁外界勿再以讹传讹，浪费社会资源。如果有假消息影响苹果新闻网声誉，必追究法律责任。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
