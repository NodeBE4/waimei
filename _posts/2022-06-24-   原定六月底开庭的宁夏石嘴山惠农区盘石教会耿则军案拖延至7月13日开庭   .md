[原定六月底开庭的宁夏石嘴山惠农区盘石教会耿则军案拖延至7月13日开庭](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2022/06/713.html)
------

<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">耿则军：</span></b><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">1972</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">31</span>日出生，宁夏回族自治区石嘴山市人，基督徒，石嘴山市惠农区盘石教会传道人，中国在押良心犯。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">耿则军笃信耶稣基督；因在宁夏回族自治区石嘴山市惠农区盘石教会做全职传道人，并其所在教会属归正福音家庭教会，拒不接受有官方背景的“三自教会”管理，遂长期遭石嘴山市当局的监控和伺机打压；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2021</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">5</span>日，因其家庭教会坚持一贯性的聚会（即每个礼拜天<span lang="EN-US">/</span>星期天 的主日敬拜），正当其及教会众信徒分别在惠农区育才路<span lang="EN-US">140</span>号、静安一区<span lang="EN-US">3-3-402</span>号举行聚会时，突遭石嘴山市惠农区警方、两地派出所民警及宗教局人员联合冲击，除在场老年人被登记之后准予送回住处外，其等多人均被抓捕；次日，其被警方以“扰乱社会秩序”为由处行政拘留<span lang="EN-US">15</span>天，并送是最上市行政拘留所关押；另有其妻子郝瑛和教友罗时平、叶宁芳、朱俊英及王素芳等<span lang="EN-US">5</span>人亦被警方以同罪由处行政拘留<span lang="EN-US">10</span>天；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2022</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">4</span>日，其再次被传唤，后被嘴山市惠农区警方以涉嫌“组织、资助非法聚集罪”刑拘；同年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">25</span>日，又被嘴山市惠农区检察院以涉嫌“组织非法聚集罪”正式批捕，指控其因多次在惠农区多地“利用基督教进行非法聚会”，严重扰乱了社会秩序，故其行为触犯了政府相关法规；后其案被地方检察院提起公诉，又移交至石嘴山市惠农区法院； <span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">对耿则军案的进展，本网将持续关注。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt;
