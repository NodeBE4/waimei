<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">王丽珍：</span></b><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">女，<span lang="EN-US">1978</span>年出生，山东省淄博市人，淄博市张店区刘家村公民，强拆受害者，中国在押维权人士。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">18</span>日，其父母家曾因遭当地政府强拆，家产被抢劫，房屋变废墟，其父母辛苦一辈子的成果一夜全无，遂其父亲王玉杨被迫上访，但结果是不仅各项维权要求未被解决，返遭当地政府捏造罪名枉法构陷，被淄博市张店区法院以“寻衅滋事罪”判刑<span lang="EN-US">4</span>年；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">其母亦被列为网上逃犯，逃亡在外不敢回家；为讨公道和追回家产，其开始替父伸冤，上访维权，期间，因屡次维权投诉并数次围观、发视频声援其他与自家相类似的强拆受害事件，遂屡遭政府打压；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2017</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">14</span>日，曾因在网上曝光“淄博开发区强拆事件”（即当地村民为抗强拆，手拿<span lang="EN-US">4</span>个煤气罐和多个酒瓶与警方对恃，情况十分紧急），而被潍坊市张店区警方带走传唤，后被以涉嫌“寻衅滋事罪”刑拘；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2021</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">22</span>日，曾因其父举报官员非法强拆，多年上访而无果反被构陷入狱，遂其不得不向社会发出求助视频，但此举立遭打击，其家多日半夜被警察敲门恐吓、骚扰，并被断电；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2023</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月，因其父家房产遭政府强拆已达<span lang="EN-US">8</span>年问题一直不予解决，故而录制视频进行控诉，以期引起社会各界关注，解决实际问题，遂其将视频发到各微信群，寻求声援和呼吁；<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">29</span>日，其即被淄博市张店区警方以涉嫌“寻衅滋事罪”带走、刑拘。目前被羁押于淄博市看守所（山东省淄博市张店区傅家镇傅家村，邮政编码：<span lang="EN-US">255022</span>）。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">对王丽珍女士的境况，本网将持续关注。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"></p><div class="separator" style="clear: both;
