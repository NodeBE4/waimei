mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">5</span>日，本网获悉：北京维权人士葛志慧就北京市公安局丰台分局及青塔派出所非法限制其人身自由，报警不处警，申请行政复议遭驳回。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">北京丰台区维权人士葛智慧曾被以“寻衅滋事罪”判刑<span lang="EN-US">4</span>年，<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">11</span>日才刑满出狱。出狱后，仍被北京市公安局丰台分局及青塔派出所非法限制其人身自由，报警不处警，最后才申请行政复议，但均于<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月被驳回。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">葛志慧是强拆的受害者。<span lang="EN-US">2010</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">16</span>日，北京市丰台区岳各庄政府及雇佣的社会人员，联合到葛志慧所在的卢沟桥乡蒋家坟<span lang="EN-US">324</span>号房屋，强行撬门闯入葛志慧的家中，将坚守在房屋内的葛志慧打昏拖走，然后强拆了她的房屋。政府的暴力强拆，不仅让葛志慧落下终身残疾，还因为上访维权，多次被非法拘禁，<span lang="EN-US">5</span>次行政拘留，<span lang="EN-US">6</span>次刑事拘留，丈夫离婚。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2019</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">20</span>日，葛志慧女士就其房屋被强拆，人被伤害致残的遭遇，向中央扫黑除恶驻北京的督导组、同时也是给习近平主席写的举报信，结果被公安报复性抓捕、刑拘，关押于北京市丰台区看守所。一周后的<span lang="EN-US">2019</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">26</span>日，公安又对其进行了抄家。<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">15</span>日，葛志慧被“寻衅滋事”案在丰台区法院开庭，后被枉法重判<span lang="EN-US">4</span>年有期徒刑。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体;
