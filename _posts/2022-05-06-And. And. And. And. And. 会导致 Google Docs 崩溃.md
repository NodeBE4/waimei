[And. And. And. And. And. 会导致 Google Docs 崩溃](https://www.solidot.org/story?sid=71441)
------

<div>
Google Docs 用户在支持论坛上<a href="https://support.google.com/docs/thread/162510194/including-and-and-and-and-and-in-a-google-doc-causes-it-to-crash?hl=en">报告</a>，如果 Google Docs  含有特定字符串如“And. And. And. And. And.”或 “But. But. But. But. But.”或 “However. However. However. However. However.”应用程序会崩溃。类似格式的 Also, Therefore, Anyway, Who, Why, Besides 等都会报错。问题似乎只影响英文字符。Google 开发者表示已经知道问题正在着手解决。关闭语法检查可暂时解决问题。
</div>
