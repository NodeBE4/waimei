[无牌按摩院案  辩方拟传召蔡展鹏作证](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210901-1189061)
------

<div>
<p>已调任的香港警务处前国家安全处处长蔡展鹏早前光顾无牌按摩院，按摩院四人否认控罪，辩方拟传召蔡展鹏作为证人。</p><p>据明报报道，蔡展鹏光顾的无牌按摩院VIET SPA的三名女按摩师及租用肇事单位的男子，被控三项管理卖淫场所罪、一项管理没有有效牌照的按摩院罪、一项经营卖淫场所罪及一项经营没有有效牌照的按摩院罪，四人今（9月1日）于香港东区裁判法院否认所有控罪。</p><p>辩方计划传召蔡展鹏作供，并要求控方提供闭路电视原片。控方则拟传召10名控方证人，不包括蔡展鹏。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>蔡展鹏今年5月因被曝光顾无牌按摩店，被下令休假接受调查，经调查后证实不涉刑事成分，蔡展鹏复工后，从国安处平调至警务处人事及训练处长。</p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
