<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">本名王战武，湖南省衡阳市常宁市人，衡阳市常宁市培元区占家村公民，抗强拆受害者，酷刑致残者，维权公民，民主宪政追求者，中国曾押政治犯。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年，曾因自家房屋被当地政府强拆强占，其父为此抗争被打死，其母因此被诱发老年精神疾患，其本人亦因强拆维权、抗争而被湖南省常宁市警方抓捕，并被酷刑而导致身体伤残；后其经常参与各种维权活动，并开始认真思考和探寻中国民主宪政的出路；为此，其曾多次游历于全国各地，广结同道朋友，积极交流思想，互访互助，以便更多、更好地宣传、推广公民意识与公民应具备的基本权利，然而也正因如此，而成为中共当局对其不断打压；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2017</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">24</span>日，曾因到广东省惠州市惠东县黄埠镇寻访互助友人，而突然被抓捕失联，后知被当地惠东县黄埠镇派出所警方以“在全国到处乱窜，关他几天治治他才会成为良民”为由抓走，而后又被惠东县警方以涉嫌“寻衅滋事”刑拘，并被羁押于惠州市惠东县看守；被取保候审后，<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2018</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2019</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">11</span>日，其案在广东省惠州市惠东县法院二次庭审，最终被一审以“寻衅滋事罪”重判有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">4</span>年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>个月；其不服，上诉，二审依然维持原判；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2022</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;
