<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">1964</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">自<span lang="EN-US">2004</span>年底起，开始多次上书中共当局高层，要求改变对法轮功等群体的非法处理手段，并参与对法轮功学员器官遭当局活摘指控的调查； <span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月，与妻子公开宣布退出中国共产党；因其勇于为当局认定的敏感性案件（注：含法轮功信仰者、地下基督教徒以及与官员发生纠纷的底层农民和私营企业家）辩护，故而曾多次受到中共当局以黑社会手段的严重骚扰、打击报复； <span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2006</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月，其执业执照被当局吊销，且被秘密绑架，遭近<span lang="EN-US">4</span>个月的酷刑； <span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2006</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2009</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月，因其秘密撰写书籍《<span lang="EN-US">2017</span>年，起来中国》在台湾正式印刷出售，且因该书主要涉及中共当局对其长期绑架、囚禁和施以酷刑等，及其从信仰角度看中共<span lang="EN-US">2017</span>年将会灭亡和对<span lang="EN-US">2017</span>年后的中国未来宪政民主化展望等三大内容，遂令中共当局十分恐惧恼怒，对其监控愈加严密。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt;
