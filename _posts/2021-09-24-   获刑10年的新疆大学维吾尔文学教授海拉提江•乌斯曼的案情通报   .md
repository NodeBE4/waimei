<span><a name='more'></a></span><div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;"><br /></div> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">24</span>日，本网获悉获刑<span lang="EN-US">10</span>年的新疆大学维吾尔文学教授海拉提江•乌斯曼的案情，现通报如下：<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">海拉提江·乌斯曼：</span></b><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">1958</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年出生，新疆维吾尔自治区人，维吾尔族，维吾尔古典文学研究者，新疆维吾尔自治区政府科学文化研究所访问研究员，新疆大学维吾尔文学教授，中国在押政治犯。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">1978</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年，曾就读于新疆大学维吾尔语文学系，至<span lang="EN-US">1982</span>年毕业；后在新疆乌鲁木齐市的新疆大学长期任职，并致力于对维吾尔族古典文学的研究和撰写工作；曾撰写过<span lang="EN-US">30</span>多部书籍和<span lang="EN-US">100</span>篇学术论文，并参加过数十次国际学术会议；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2008</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年，曾因参加在土耳其的布尔萨举行的研讨会，并在会上讨论了维吾尔文化，随被中共新疆当局关注，后于<span lang="EN-US">2018</span>年被定为搞“分裂主义宣传”；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2018</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月，因曾在日本一所大学讲课，课堂上使用的例子被中共当局视为“对中国文化的排斥”，并曾应邀参加了一次土耳其的突厥学会议，随被乌鲁木齐市警方刑事拘留；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">后又被转正式逮捕，并被指控在其研究中，因过度赞扬维吾尔文化，而“在几代维吾尔学生中，灌输分裂主义意识形态”，属于“拒绝人数占大多数的汉族文化”，随有搞“分裂主义”活动之嫌；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2020</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月，被新疆乌鲁木齐市法院以“分裂主义”罪名判处有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">10</span>年，刑期至<span lang="EN-US">2028</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月。目前在新疆乌鲁木齐市一家监狱服刑。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal">&nbsp;
