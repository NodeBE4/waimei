[香港公务员考试拟加入考核《国安法》](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20211013-1202878)
------

<div>
<p>香港公务员事务局今天向立法会公务员及资助机构员工事务委员会提交文件，透露将把《国安法》纳入公务员考核范围。</p><p>据香港01报道，预计新的考核内容将在明年中的《基本法及香港国安法》测试使用。</p><p>按现行机制，所有学历要求为完成中学阶段程度或以上的公务员职位申请人，须参与《基本法》测试，公务员事务局在今年8月将《基本法》测试及格成绩，正式定为入职条件。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>局方透露，为确保新聘的公务员对《基本法》及《国安法》有基本的认识，将检视及更新《基本法》测试的考核内容，并把《国安法》纳入考核范围，使该测试更切合有关公务员职位的要求。</p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/zbpe-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=zbpe399&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_desktop-zbpe399_2.png?dHEiGouxxZpWwWTJE04dyGyUN74JOU2z" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_desktop-zbpe399_2.png?dHEiGouxxZpWwWTJE04dyGyUN74JOU2z" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/zbpe-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=zbpe399&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_mobile-zbpe399_2.png?qZnBcIGCJvPR8A2IAZOZuOxdoGZDatiI" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_mobile-zbpe399_2.png?qZnBcIGCJvPR8A2IAZOZuOxdoGZDatiI" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
