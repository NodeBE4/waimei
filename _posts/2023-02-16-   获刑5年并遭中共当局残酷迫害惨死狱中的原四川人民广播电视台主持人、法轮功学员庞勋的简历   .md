[获刑5年并遭中共当局残酷迫害惨死狱中的原四川人民广播电视台主持人、法轮功学员庞勋的简历](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2023/02/5_16.html)

<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">16</span>日，本网获悉获刑<span lang="EN-US">5</span>年并遭中共当局残酷迫害惨死狱中的原四川人民广播电视台主持人、法轮功学员庞勋的简历，现通报如下：<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">庞勋：</span></b><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">1993</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年出生，湖南省籍人，中国传媒大学播音系毕业生，前湖南广播电视台新闻中心出镜记者、腾讯视频<span lang="EN-US">Q</span>主播和长沙中艺传媒播音主讲教师，原四川人民广播电视台主持人，法轮功信仰者，良心犯。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">自幼因个性善良开朗，学习成绩优异，处事心态及行动能力都显阳光而积极的一面，故而深受父母家人自豪喜爱和就读校方师生的赞许和信赖；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2010</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年高中毕业后，其以优异成绩考入并就读于中国传媒大学播音系；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2014</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年大学毕业后，出于对未来的职业生涯规划的展望和积极活跃的尝试心态，曾先后做过湖南广播电视台新闻中心出镜记者、腾讯视频<span lang="EN-US">Q</span>主播和长沙中艺传媒播音主讲教师；同年，最终又被四川人民广播电视台录用，在该台一直当主持人；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">原本生活和事业满有顺意并对未来有无限的憧憬，然而因其对个人宗教信仰（尤其是法轮功信仰）的追求认知与中共当局的意识形态有明显偏差，遂最终招致四川当局的严打狠批；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2020</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">27</span>日，因在外张贴法轮功宣传单张而被人检举，遂当天即被成都市警方以“宣传邪教信息”为罪由带走刑拘、逮捕；后其案被成都市检察院起诉和被移交到成都市法院进行审理；同年冬季，其被该法院迅速以“利用邪教组织破坏法律实施罪”重判处有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">5</span>年，刑期至<span lang="EN-US">2025</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">26</span>日；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">但在其入监服刑后，<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">2</span>日，其母亲突然接到狱方电话，称其生命垂危，务请家人速往监狱；当其母亲从湖南老家匆忙赶到四川嘉州监狱时，其已于当天<span lang="EN-US">10</span>点被宣告死亡；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">据外网公开流传的国内视频显示，其遗体被装在黑色袋子里，身体伤痕累累，多处有被殴打、电击过的痕迹，故其母亲哭诉称，其儿子是被施暴酷刑“活活打死”，但面对其家人及外界质疑，狱方给出的致死理由却是甲亢；其母及同学好友吁请国际社会及正义人士对此予以关注。此前曾在四川省嘉州监狱（四川省乐山市市中区全福镇，邮政编码：<span lang="EN-US">614000</span>）服刑。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"></p><div class="separator" style="clear: both;
