[奥运冠军陈雨菲因李宁运动鞋开裂受伤](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210910-1192202)
------

<div>
<p>东京奥运羽毛球女单冠军陈雨菲昨天在中国全运会上受伤，原因是所穿的李宁牌运动鞋开裂。</p><p>据观察者网报道，昨天（9日）进行的第十四届全国运动会羽毛球比赛女团小组赛中，浙江队陈雨菲对战福建队韩悦。第二局，陈雨菲因运动鞋造成右脚大脚趾受伤，申请医疗暂停，只能换鞋继续比赛。</p><p>赛后陈雨菲透露伤情称，比赛过程中右脚的鞋头开了，她的大脚趾被钢丝戳到。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>官宣信息显示，今年浙江羽毛球队参加全运会由中国国产品牌李宁赞助。不少球迷对运动鞋质量提出质疑。观察者网记者就此事多次致电李宁客服，但对方始终无人接听，处于转接状态。</p><p>微博账号@李宁 和@李宁羽毛球 尚未就此事回应，但后者今天上午对微博评论区进行了“精选”处理，不显示全部评论内容。<br> </p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/zbpe-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=zbpe399&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_desktop-zbpe399_2.png?dHEiGouxxZpWwWTJE04dyGyUN74JOU2z" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_desktop-zbpe399_2.png?dHEiGouxxZpWwWTJE04dyGyUN74JOU2z" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/zbpe-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=zbpe399&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_mobile-zbpe399_2.png?qZnBcIGCJvPR8A2IAZOZuOxdoGZDatiI" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_mobile-zbpe399_2.png?qZnBcIGCJvPR8A2IAZOZuOxdoGZDatiI" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
