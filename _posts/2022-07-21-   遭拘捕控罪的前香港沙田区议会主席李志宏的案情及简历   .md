[遭拘捕控罪的前香港沙田区议会主席李志宏的案情及简历](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2022/07/blog-post_43.html)
------

<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">21</span>日，本网获悉遭拘捕控罪的前香港沙田区议会主席李志宏的案情及简历，现通报如下：<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">李志宏：</span></b><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">1995</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">22</span>日出生，英语名：<span lang="EN-US">Li Chi-wang</span>，原英属香港籍人，现香港籍人，前香港沙田区议会主席、前沙田区议会禾輋邨选区议员，香港民主派人士，中国在押政治犯。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">近年来，一直在民主派“公民议政平台”上做筹委（之一），曾因多次公开发表反政府言论和多次参与反香港国安法等活动，而被中共香港当局视为违法常客，进而屡遭打压，数度被捕；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2019</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年，曾因在香港区议会选举中，以独立民主派身分参选禾輋邨选区，而被港警于同年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">13</span>日以涉嫌“非法集结”罪由被抓，后又很快保释释放，其继续参与竞选活动；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2019</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">26</span>日，终在“民主动力”的支持下成功胜选区议员，任期由<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">1</span>日起生效，直至<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">7</span>日被香港政府硬性终止；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2020</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">24</span>日，因有网民发起“反国歌法、国安法大游行”，其等多人参与其中，故而又被当局拘捕，并被指控其在湾仔鹅颈桥底做出喧哗和扰乱秩序行为，导致民众起哄，因而被捕；后又因其拒保候查，遂暂获释放，但同年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">17</span>日，又被拘捕，而后又的释放；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2021</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">21</span>日，因沙田区议会召开大会重选主席，遂其在没有竞争下自动当选为区议会主席，任期由<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">22</span>日生效，直至<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">7</span>日被香港政府硬性裁定其宣誓无效，实时离任沙田区议员暨主席一职；之后，其再次因“反国歌法、国安法大游行”之事被港警拘捕；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2022</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">15</span>日，其扰乱秩序案在香港东区裁判法院庭审，因法官称其“虽无实质性暴力行为，但用言语辱骂警察、煽动现场其他示威者”，故裁定其“在公众地方做出喧哗或扰乱秩序行为罪”成立，并将于<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">28</span>日宣布判决。目前被羁押地点不详。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"></p><div class="separator" style="clear: both;
