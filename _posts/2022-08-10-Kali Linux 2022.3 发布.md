[Kali Linux 2022.3 发布](https://www.solidot.org/story?sid=72423)
------

<div>
用于渗透测试、安全审计和网络安全研究的发行版 Kali Linux 释出了 <a href="https://www.kali.org/get-kali/">2022.3</a>。主要新变化<a href="https://www.bleepingcomputer.com/news/security/kali-linux-20223-adds-5-new-tools-updates-linux-kernel-and-more/">包括</a>：改进虚拟机支持；新的网络分析工具 <a href="https://www.kali.org/tools/bruteshark/" rel="nofollow noopener">BruteShark</a>，开源应用漏洞关联和安全协调工具 <a href="https://www.kali.org/tools/defectdojo/" rel="nofollow noopener">DefectDojo</a>，漏洞利用框架 <a href="https://www.kali.org/tools/phpsploit/" rel="nofollow noopener">phpsploit</a>，针对 Lync/S4B/OWA/O365 的密码喷射攻击工具<a href="https://www.kali.org/tools/sprayingtoolkit/" rel="nofollow noopener">SprayingToolkit</a>，<a href="https://www.kali.org/tools/shellfire/" rel="nofollow noopener">shellfire</a>；Linux Kernel 5.18.5；改进  ARM 支持；等等。<br>
</div>
