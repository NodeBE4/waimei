font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">4</span>日，曾因信仰问题而与其母亲（中国籍香港公民）等人被上海市卢湾区警方与徐汇区<span lang="EN-US">610</span>办公室人员联合抓捕、刑拘，一月后又被以信仰法轮功为罪由转正式逮捕，并关押在上海市徐汇区看守所，羁押审讯期间，曾遭刑讯逼供；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">21</span>日，被上海市徐汇区法院以邪教罪名判处有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">1</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月；出狱后其返回美国工作生活，<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">8</span>日，当其回国与家人团聚和居住时，再次与其同为法轮功信仰者的丈夫何冰钢被黄浦区警方和长宁区警方连手以危害国家安全为罪由抓捕刑拘，后被上海市长宁区检察院以同罪由予以批捕，并被关押在长宁区看守所；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2022</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月，其案被长宁区检察院提起公诉，至同年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">10</span>日，被检察院移交至上海市奉贤区法院审查；看守所羁押期间，因遭受严重迫害而致胸部出现恶性肿瘤，最终导致身体状况极差，代理律师与其家人申请取保遭拒；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2023</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;
