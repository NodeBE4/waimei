<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">6</span>日，本网获悉获刑<span lang="EN-US">2</span>年的北京法轮功学员杨顺梅女士的案情及简历，现在通报如下：<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">因宣讲法轮功，杨顺梅于<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">14</span>日被闯入家中的警察抓捕、抄家，关押在海淀区看守所。<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年，她被海淀中级法院判刑<span lang="EN-US">2</span>年。杨顺梅现被关押在北京大兴女子监狱服刑。狱方不让家属探视，只让存一点钱。刑期至：<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">13</span>日。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"></p><div class="separator" style="clear: both;
