[成都秋雨圣约教会会友、基督徒作家、翻译家邢宏伟（笔名阿信）取保获释](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2022/08/blog-post_55.html)
------

<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: major-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: major-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: major-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">26</span>日，本网获悉：<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">14</span>日遭中共当局刑事拘留的成都秋雨圣约教会会友、基督徒作家、翻译家邢宏伟（笔名阿信）今天取保获释。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: major-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: major-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: major-fareast;">邢宏伟于今日从看守所被释放，办案机关为他办理了取保候审。邢宏伟被羁押了十二天。他很感谢教会和弟兄姊妹及他的朋友们对他的关心，感谢律师们对他案件的辩护，使得他可以如此快地被释放。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: major-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: major-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: major-fareast;">邢宏伟：</span></b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: major-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: major-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: major-fareast;">笔名阿信。<span lang="EN-US">1968</span>年生，八九学生民主运动的参与者。八九六四后，<span lang="EN-US">1992</span>年因参与组建胡石根等人组建自由民主党活动，曾被判刑三年。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: major-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: major-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: major-fareast;">邢宏伟是成都秋雨圣约教会会友、是一位基督徒作家、翻译家。他是八九一代中信主比较早的基督徒，后致力于通过写作和教育传播基督信仰，也参加了立人大学的教育活动。曾撰写《用生命爱中国——伯格理传》等多部作品，并翻译了大量基督教经典书籍。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: major-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: major-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: major-fareast;">2019</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: major-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: major-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: major-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">25</span>日，阿信因在微信朋友圈发帖通报王怡牧师的妈妈被警察侮辱、殴打一事而被警察从家中抓捕，行政拘留<span lang="EN-US">10</span>天。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: major-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: major-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: major-fareast;">2021</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: major-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: major-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: major-fareast;">年阿信为撰写伯格理传纪到云南采访时突发脑膜炎，在昆明<span lang="EN-US">ICU</span>抢救十几天，后有所好转。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: major-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: major-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: major-fareast;">2022</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: major-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: major-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: major-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">9</span>日，阿信在自己建的微信群为乌克兰祷告被警察带走，群友也被国宝喝茶，微信群被强行解散。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: major-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: major-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: major-fareast;">2022</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: major-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: major-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: major-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">14</span>日，成都警方冲击成都秋雨圣约教会线下礼拜，过程中邢宏伟因不愿配合登记信息，与警察发生肢体冲突，警察把邢宏伟弟兄按倒在地强制带走，随后警方以涉嫌袭警罪将邢宏伟刑事拘留，关押在成都市新都看守所。<span lang="EN-US">12</span>天后，取保获释。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"></p><div class="separator" style="clear: both;
