[因纪念六四获刑1年10个月的广州基督徒高恒刑满出狱  身体消瘦朋友几乎辨认不出](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2023/04/110.html)
------

<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">5</span>日，本网获悉：广州基督徒高恒因在六四<span lang="EN-US">32</span>周年（<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年）纪念日在广州地铁站举牌呼吁为国家祷告而被控寻衅滋事一案于<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">21</span>日终被判处有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">1</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>个月，刑期至<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">4</span>日。<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">4</span>日，高恒刑满出狱，现已回到老家。据悉，被关押在广州看守所<span lang="EN-US">22</span>个月之后，高恒身形极其消瘦，几乎让人辨认不出。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: 宋体;
