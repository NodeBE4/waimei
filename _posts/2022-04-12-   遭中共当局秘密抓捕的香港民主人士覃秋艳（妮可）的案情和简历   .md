<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">12</span>日，本网获悉遭中共当局秘密抓捕的香港民主人士覃秋艳（妮可）的案情和简历，现在通报如下<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">覃秋艳（妮可）：</span></b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">女，<span lang="EN-US">1979</span>年出生，广西壮族自治区柳州市籍人，香港新移民，香港身份证英文名：妮可，常用推特账户名：<span lang="EN-US">@Hk60740379Hk</span>，南方公民行动和公民聚会参与者，香港“返送中”运动参与者，香港、广州、深圳三地人权活动者，中国在押政治犯。<b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></b></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">早年曾在广东省深圳市生活，曾多次参与深圳等地的公民行动与公民聚会，并对生活贫困的公民有过资助；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">自<span lang="EN-US">2014</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月起，开始移民香港，并获得香港公民身份；曾因关注中国的人权状况而穿梭活跃于香港与内地的广州、深圳等城市，故而曾在广东省惠州市惠阳区办事时遭到中国秘密警察的跟踪；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2014</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">28</span>日，曾因支持港人对香港选举要求“真普选”，而积极参加了香港的“占中”运动；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2019</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年，曾因全身心投入香港的“反修例”运动，而经常在香港的街头进行站台宣传与抗议；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">同年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月，曾因原广州维权人士廖剑豪（现已定居香港）在香港铜锣湾东角道被港警烟雾弹击中，正在附近街头作采访节目的她随即加入对其的营救，并对其眼、口等处予以及时清洁；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2021</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">20</span>日，因其母亲<span lang="EN-US">80</span>岁生日将至，其随返回内地深圳市，准备在疫情所致的“宾馆隔离”<span lang="EN-US">14</span>天后赶回老家广西柳州，不料却在解除隔离日（即<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">4</span>日），被中国警方秘密抓捕，自此失联；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">后经友人多方查询打探，同年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">20</span>日，知其已被广西壮族自治区柳州市警方拘捕，涉嫌罪名不清；据悉，其此次被抓疑与其在香港参加反送中运动，且频繁将运动实时信息发于推特之上有关；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">截至当前，其已被超期关押近<span lang="EN-US">1</span>年，并因其家人恐受威胁而拒透其具体信息，故而友人对其的发声呼吁一直未引起国内外媒体的关注。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体;
