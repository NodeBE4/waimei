------

<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">2</span>日，本网获悉：中国民主党徐光案将于<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">3</span>日<span lang="EN-US">9</span>时<span lang="EN-US">30</span>分将在杭州西湖法院<span lang="EN-US">2136</span>法庭开审。徐光因身体原因在杭州看守所通过远程视频受审，并不到法院现场。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">徐光家属说：徐光自<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">25</span>被抓后，一直绝食，是杭州看守所医院鼻饲维持生命，律师会见时，徐光已极度虚弱，徐在看守所医院曾多次昏迷抢救。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">当局称：徐光“在<span lang="EN-US">2018</span>年至<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年期间，在微信，脸书，电报，油管发布视频，文章，散布损害国家形象，危害国家利益的虚假信息。指控徐光在网络上寻衅滋事。”徐光认为系当局构陷。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">徐光称：自己作为当年杭州六四学运的参予者（徐光系六四学运杭州武林广场绝食学生）每年公开纪念“八九六四”和举牌“勿忘六四”。当局为六四维稳需要，打压中国民主党人，在六四前抓捕自己。徐光坚决要求律师做无罪辩护。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">法院对律师说，徐光案庭审时间，可能会安排一整天。杭州当地警方已对中国民主党人邹巍告知，<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">3</span>日警方要对邹巍陪住。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"></p><div class="separator" style="clear: both;
