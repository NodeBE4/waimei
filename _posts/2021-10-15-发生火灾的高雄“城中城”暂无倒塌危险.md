[发生火灾的高雄“城中城”暂无倒塌危险](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20211015-1203576)
------

<div>
<p>台湾高雄市盐埕区“城中城”大楼昨天（14日）凌晨发生火灾，目前已造成46人遇难。经专业技师现勘评估，该建筑无立即倾倒危险。</p><p>据台湾中央社报道，城中城已有40年历史，建筑老旧，外界担心建筑体受到损坏，会有倾倒危险。高雄市政府工务局昨天发布新闻稿表示，经会同高雄市建筑师公会、土木技师公会及结构技师公会勘查，该建筑没有立即倾倒的危险。</p><p>工务局说，已要求建筑物所有权人及使用人维护该建筑物构造及设备安全，并在修缮未完成前，作好安全防护措施。工务局也说，将持续协助拆除该建筑物玻璃墙并设置围篱，防止外墙面及帷幕墙脱落，以维公共安全。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>工务局也呼吁民众，尽量不要靠近该大楼。</p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/zbpe-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=zbpe399&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_desktop-zbpe399_2.png?dHEiGouxxZpWwWTJE04dyGyUN74JOU2z" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_desktop-zbpe399_2.png?dHEiGouxxZpWwWTJE04dyGyUN74JOU2z" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/zbpe-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=zbpe399&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_mobile-zbpe399_2.png?qZnBcIGCJvPR8A2IAZOZuOxdoGZDatiI" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_mobile-zbpe399_2.png?qZnBcIGCJvPR8A2IAZOZuOxdoGZDatiI" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
