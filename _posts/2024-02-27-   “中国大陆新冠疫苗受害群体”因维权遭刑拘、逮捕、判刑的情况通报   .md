<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">27</span>日，本网获悉：因注射中国大陆新冠疫苗而产生严重后遗症的“新冠疫苗受害群体”，因向当局要求从立法、补偿、救助等各个方面予以行动，遭中共当局残酷镇压，数十位疫苗维权人士遭刑拘、逮捕、和判刑。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">河北保定疫苗受害儿童家长贾小玉因维权被当局判刑<span lang="EN-US">4</span>年；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">江西省吉安市泰和县的梁小强女士两次刑拘后判缓刑两年；梁小强儿子罗哲翰生前就读于泰和县龙江实验学校四年级一班。<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">2</span>日，接种新冠疫苗第一针，<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">3</span>日接种了第二针，<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">22</span>日确诊急性淋巴白血病，<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">21</span>日不幸离世；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">深圳伍松<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">1</span>日<a name="_Hlk159925269">国家卫健委维权示威</a>后刑拘近两个月，两次取保候审，现在检察院取保获释，等待开庭判刑；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体;
