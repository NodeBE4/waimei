[被山西当局以涉嫌“诈骗罪”逮捕的山西临汾圣约家园教会同工王强的简历](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2022/12/blog-post_20.html)
------

<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">20</span>日，本网获悉被山西当局以涉嫌“诈骗罪”逮捕的山西临汾圣约家园教会同工王强的简历，现通报如下：<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">王强：<span lang="EN-US">1993</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">7</span>日出生，山西省人，住山西省临汾市荛都区锦绣华庭，基督徒，临汾市基督教圣约家园教会同工，中国在押良心犯。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">因其所在教会属于基督教归正家庭教会，且因其等教会神职人员、同工及众信徒拒不愿加入中共“三自”教会，遂自新宗教事务条例实施以来，其所在教会即成为当局屡加迫害的家庭教会之一；在逼迫中，不仅教会时常被民宗局官员带着多人前来骚扰、威胁和冲击，其作为同工亦成为山西当局维稳打压的对象；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2022</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">19</span>日，因其参与教会在户外举办的<span lang="EN-US">70</span>人的“亲子营”活动，突遭当地警方<span lang="EN-US">100</span>余人的围堵和冲击，并将包括其在内的所有现场参与者悉数带走，教会物品被查抄，教会神职人员李洁和韩晓栋等人被临汾市警方以涉嫌“诈骗罪” 指定居所监视居住，后又转为刑拘、逮捕，遂于同年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">1</span>日，也被临汾市警方拦截、抓捕，并于次日被以涉嫌“诈骗罪”指定居所监视居住，至<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">9</span>日，又被临汾市警方以同罪由转为刑拘；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">同年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">16</span>日，其所在临汾圣约家园教会和阿斯兰学堂被临汾市尧都区民政局在政府官网发布正式公告而取缔；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2022</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">14</span>日，其被临汾市尧都区检察院以涉嫌“诈骗罪”正式批捕。目前被羁押于山西省尧都区看守所。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"></p><div class="separator" style="clear: both;
