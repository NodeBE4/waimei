<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">15</span>日，本网获悉遭中共当局判刑<span lang="EN-US">15</span>年的登卓寺僧侣阿旺益西的案情及简介，现通报如下：<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">阿旺益西：</span></b><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">1984</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年出生，藏族名<span lang="EN-US">Ngawang Yeshe</span>，西藏自治区日喀则市人，日喀则市定日县协格尔镇查那村当卓寺（藏民称之为（<span lang="EN-US">Tengdro</span>））僧侣，公益慈善捐助人，藏族异见人士，中国在押良心犯。<b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></b></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2019</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">-9</span>月，因当卓寺僧人曲嘉旺波（<span lang="EN-US">Choegyal Wangpo</span>）到西藏首府拉萨市做佛事访问时不甚将手机遗失在某咖啡馆，后被老板交予拉萨市警方处置；但因警方在查阅手机时，发现内存其及当卓寺众僧侣与尼泊尔同乡藏僧的聊天记录，尤其是存有<span lang="EN-US">2015</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月尼泊尔大地震时，他们向那里的藏民和寺院捐款赈灾等的信息记录，以及还存有和第<span lang="EN-US">14</span>世达赖喇嘛有关的照片及相关文献等；遂立即触碰到中共西藏当局的政治敏感神经，认定这是一起与境外敌对势力勾结，利用网络通讯对国家安全构成威胁的典型案件；同年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月，其所在当卓寺突遭日喀则市当局派出的大批武警及公安警察袭击，所有僧人均被强迫接受政治洗脑教育数天，多人遭殴打；之后有<span lang="EN-US">20</span>名僧侣（含其在内）被刑拘，当卓寺及其附近的网络被切断；<span lang="EN-US">3</span>天后，除一名僧侣因向当局抗议而自杀，其余<span lang="EN-US">19</span>名僧人仍被羁押，数月不放；后其与曲嘉旺波等<span lang="EN-US">4</span>名藏僧均被日喀则市检察院以涉嫌“跟境外藏人联系、赈济尼泊尔地震，或拥有和达赖喇嘛有关照片或文献”为罪由予以批捕，自此失联；<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">6</span>日获悉，其已被西藏自治区日喀则市中级法院以“危害国家安全罪” 枉法判处有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">5</span>年，刑期至<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月；其余三名僧侣亦被以同罪名分别重判<span lang="EN-US">20</span>年<span lang="EN-US">-17</span>年不等。目前疑似在拉萨市札基监狱服刑。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"></p><div class="separator" style="clear: both;
