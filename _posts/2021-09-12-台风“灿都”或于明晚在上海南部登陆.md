[台风“灿都”或于明晚在上海南部登陆](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210912-1192754)
------

<div>
<p>上海中心气象台消息，今年第14号台风“灿都”（超强台风级）预计将于明晚在上海南部登陆。</p><p>据微信公众号“上海发布”报道，预计“灿都”将以每小时15～20公里左右的速度向偏北方向移动，13日中午到傍晚登陆舟山或穿过舟山群岛，可能于13日夜间登陆上海南部（35-40米/秒，13级，台风级），14～15日在杭州湾到上海附近回旋少动，16日开始转向东北方向移动，逐渐远离上海市。</p><p>上海市今天开始受台风“灿都”外围影响，有阵雨或雷雨天气。13日早晨起受台风本体影响，风雨均趋于增大，强降水主要集中在13～14日。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/zbpe-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=zbpe399&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_desktop-zbpe399_2.png?dHEiGouxxZpWwWTJE04dyGyUN74JOU2z" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_desktop-zbpe399_2.png?dHEiGouxxZpWwWTJE04dyGyUN74JOU2z" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/zbpe-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=zbpe399&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_mobile-zbpe399_2.png?qZnBcIGCJvPR8A2IAZOZuOxdoGZDatiI" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_mobile-zbpe399_2.png?qZnBcIGCJvPR8A2IAZOZuOxdoGZDatiI" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
