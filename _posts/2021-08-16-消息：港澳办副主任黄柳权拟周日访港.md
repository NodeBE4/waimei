[消息：港澳办副主任黄柳权拟周日访港](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210816-1183005)
------

<div>
<p>消息人士透露，港澳办副主任黄柳权计划周日（22日）访问香港，并有可能在隔天到立法会与立法会议员出席关于十四五规划的讲座。</p><p>据香港《明报》报道，消息人士称，黄柳权将连同发改委及商务部等中国官员赴港出席这场宣讲会。黄柳权打算下周三（25日）离港。</p><p>消息人士也透露，宣讲会将闭门进行，出席者包括港府官员、立法会议员、商界及青年，并预料黄柳权等人届时可能传达对港政策新方向。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>立法会日志显示，立法会宴会厅下周一下午4时将举行“国家‘十四五’规划讲座”。</p><p>有立法会议员透露，早前已有专人向议员收集问题，黄柳权等官员届时会在讲座上解答。</p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
