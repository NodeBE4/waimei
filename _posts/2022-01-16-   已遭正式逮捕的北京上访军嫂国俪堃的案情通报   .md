mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">16</span>日，本网获悉已遭正式逮捕的北京上访军嫂国俪堃的案情，现通报如下：<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年过七旬的北京上访军嫂国俪堃<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">16</span>日被北京丰台公安分局六里桥派出所传唤，第二天（<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">17</span>日）即被丰台警方以涉嫌“寻衅滋事罪”刑事拘留。后遭正式逮捕。目前，国俪堃被羁押在北京丰台区看守所内，具体情况不明。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">据悉，现年<span lang="EN-US">71</span>岁的国俪堃原本是一名蒙古族退休医生，原海军航空兵后勤技术部纪卓图部长的妻子。<span lang="EN-US">2015</span>年，她的住屋遭遇强拆，而后为此上访、维权多年至今无果。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">据悉，国俪堃多次找过海军部门交涉强拆问题，但却是去一次被打一次。海军还非法扣留了国俪堃的手机，并将里面所有的维权资料全部删除，甚至抢走了国俪堃的全部财产。国俪堃遭遇多年的颠沛流离，无家可归。在<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">15</span>日，国俪堃前往军委巡视组所在的北京京海大厦上访，却遭受暴力殴打，导致其颈部、腰部、大腿带伤，活动受限。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体;
