[被判刑4年6个月的山东维权人士赵作媛的案情通报](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2022/01/46.html)
------

<div>
<p><span></span></p><a name='more'></a><p class="MsoNormal"><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">11</span>日，本网获悉<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年底被判刑<span lang="EN-US">4</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月的山东维权人士赵作媛的案情，现通报如下：<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">赵作媛：</span></b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">女，<span lang="EN-US">1965</span>年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">20</span>日，山东省长岛县黑山乡大濠村 村民。<span lang="EN-US">1997</span>年赵作媛、骆卫东夫妻二人在本村经营球石厂，赵作媛的母亲葛宏婵为此投资数万元。<span lang="EN-US">2004</span>年，因和生意合伙人<span lang="EN-US">6</span>万元的债务纠纷，长岛县法院查封了赵作媛价值百万余元的球石和石料，任由债权人倒卖。赵作媛一家被迫起诉至法院要求债权人赔偿损失，岂料，法院判决赵作媛一家赔偿债权人转运这些球石过程中产生的费用。为了寻求公道，赵作媛、骆卫东和母亲一家三口被迫上访维权。上访期间，夫妇二人关系破裂导致离婚。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2014</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">12</span>日赵作媛被北京市丰台区公安局以寻衅滋事罪刑事拘留，同年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">12</span>日取保释放。<span lang="EN-US"> 2017</span>年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">17</span>日赵作媛被绑架到鸿锦大酒店非法拘禁<span lang="EN-US">1</span>天。<span lang="EN-US"> 2017</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">11</span>日赵作媛被暴力绑架回黑山岛非法拘禁<span lang="EN-US">17</span>天。 <span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2019</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">7</span>日赵作媛被网上通缉抓捕，以寻衅滋事罪刑事拘留，同年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">13</span>日逮捕，现羁押在烟台市看守所。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">据北京市第二中级人民法院赔偿委员会国家赔偿决定书中显示：<span lang="EN-US">2014</span>年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月间，有人在北京南站以打标牌、拍照，并将照片上传到境外互联网网站的手段制造影响。该案经丰台分局侦查发现，赵作媛有重大嫌疑。经丰台分局口头传唤、讯问后，以寻衅滋事罪将其刑事拘留。对赵作媛的拘留符合法律规定的条件和程序，对赵作媛所提赔偿请求不予支持。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">丰台看守所不许家人为其存钱、存物，导致其受凉后落下腰疼、腹痛等疾病。在暴力劫访过程中被勒脖子，造成脖子发生病变肿大，至今未愈。在非法拘禁期间手机信号被屏蔽。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2021</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">26</span>日，赵作媛被烟台市蓬莱区法院以寻衅滋事罪判刑<span lang="EN-US">4</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月，刑期至：<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">5</span>日。</span></p><p></p><div class="separator" style="clear: both;
