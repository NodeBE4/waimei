<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">22</span>日，本网获悉获刑<span lang="EN-US">7</span>年的山东聊城市法轮功学员徐金凤的案情及简历，现在通报如下：<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">徐金凤：</span></b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">山东聊城市法轮功学员，女。徐金风于<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">31</span>日早在出租屋内被茌平区公安局国保警察抓捕，国保人员进屋后在徐金凤面前亮了一下东阿县法院的逮捕证和一张搜查证，然后把徐金凤带到公安局，并于当天送到聊城市看守所（位于东昌府区梁水镇）关押。后遭刑事拘留、批捕、起诉。徐金凤于<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">11</span>日被庭审，法庭上律师做了有理有据的无罪辩护。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2024</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">10</span>日被聊城市东阿县法院判刑<span lang="EN-US">7</span>年，并罚金一万元。当天，家属辩护人接到东阿法院通知，让其到法院领取判决通知书。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p>
