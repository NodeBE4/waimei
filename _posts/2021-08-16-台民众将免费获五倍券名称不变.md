[台民众将免费获五倍券名称不变](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210816-1183010)
------

<div>
<p>台湾政府决定直接向所有民众发放五倍券，民众无需用1000元（新台币，下同，48新元）来兑换，但“五倍券”名称将保持不变。</p><p>台湾行政院初步规划让民众以1000元现金兑换价值5000元的消费券，因为有“一换五”的倍数效应，所以叫“五倍券”。</p><p>综合台湾《联合报》和《自由时报》报道，行政院长苏贞昌今早（16日）与民进党立法院党团成员举行行政立法协调会报，商讨振兴五倍券政策。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>民进党团总召柯建铭会后受访时说，行政院决定全民免收1000元即可兑券，振兴五倍券的名称也不会改变，预计10月初可上路。</p><p>苏贞昌则解释，各方在考虑到成本和台湾今年经济成长率预测后，决定由政府吸收1000元。此外，外界已习惯将消费券称为五倍券，所以名称不变。</p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
