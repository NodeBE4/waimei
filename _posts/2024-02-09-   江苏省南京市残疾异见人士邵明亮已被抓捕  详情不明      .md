[江苏省南京市残疾异见人士邵明亮已被抓捕  详情不明](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2024/02/blog-post_11.html)
------

<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">10</span>日，本网获悉：曾获刑<span lang="EN-US">4</span>年的江苏省南京市残疾异见人士邵明亮又一次遭抓捕羁押，其家人不敢透露情况，具体详情不明。邵明亮服刑期间其一只手被打残废。出狱后仍遭监控，这次或遭关押至新疆。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><b><span style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">邵明亮简介：</span></b><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">1967</span><span style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">2</span>日出生，江苏省南京市浦口区人，南京市浦口区江浦街道华光小区响堂村<span lang="EN-US">19-1</span>号居民，残疾异见人士，<span lang="EN-US">“</span>中国民复党<span lang="EN-US">”</span>创始人，中国政治犯。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">因反对马列主义而一直高调反共，横遭中共当局的极端打压逼迫，长期被囚禁在响塘村家中，禁止外出购物、就医等；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">2013</span><span style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">年，曾因在南京市审计学院门口、浦口区闹市区、浦口区政府前举牌宣传宪政、民主、自由、人权遭警方殴打，后又多次被警方强制送精神病院关押；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">2014</span><span style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">25</span>日，曾在南京浦口区政府门前遭政府默许的车辆辗压全身，导致住院两年，落下终身残疾（盆骨、骶骨、耻骨、肋骨、脊椎、颈椎、胸骨骨折、头、肺、脊骨损伤<span lang="EN-US">)</span>，只能靠轮椅生活；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">2016</span><span style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月、<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月，又因反党言论两次被刑拘，其后一直遭家中囚禁；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">2017</span><span style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">17</span>日<span lang="EN-US">, </span>曾因被搜查住处时，发现室内有<span lang="EN-US">“</span>推翻共产主义、社会主义、集体主义三座大山<span lang="EN-US">”</span>，<span lang="EN-US">“</span>审判毛邓江胡习及其罪犯统治集团首恶分子<span lang="EN-US">”</span>等文字，屋外正面墙上写有<span lang="EN-US">“</span>民复党纪念八九六四<span lang="EN-US">”</span>、<span lang="EN-US">“</span>民复党是中国自己的政党<span lang="EN-US">”</span>等标语，被南京警方处以行政拘留<span lang="EN-US">10</span>天；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">2018</span><span style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">14</span>日，中共两会期间，因发反党及嘲讽修宪言论被南京警方处以行政拘留<span lang="EN-US">14</span>天，期间又遭警方暴力殴打；<span lang="EN-US">2019</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">16</span>日，再次被南京市浦口区警方抓捕，自此失联；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">2019</span><span style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">22</span>日，被浦口区检察院以涉嫌<span lang="EN-US">“</span>寻衅滋事罪<span lang="EN-US">”</span>正式批捕，并被关押在南京市浦口区看守所，指控因其在网上发涉及民主宪政要求的微信和几十条推特内容，所以属于违法行为；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">2019</span><span style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">24</span>日，其案在南京市浦口区法院开庭受审，后被以<span lang="EN-US">“</span>寻衅滋事罪<span lang="EN-US">”</span>判处有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">4</span>年；其不服，表示上诉；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">2020</span><span style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月，经江苏省南京市中级法院二审裁决，依然维持原判，刑期至<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">15</span>日。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体;
