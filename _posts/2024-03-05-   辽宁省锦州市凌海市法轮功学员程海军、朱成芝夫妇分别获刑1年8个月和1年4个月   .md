[辽宁省锦州市凌海市法轮功学员程海军、朱成芝夫妇分别获刑1年8个月和1年4个月](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2024/03/1814.html)
------

<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">5</span>日，本网获悉：辽宁省锦州市凌海市法轮功学员程海军、朱成芝夫妇分别获刑<span lang="EN-US">1</span>年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>个月和<span lang="EN-US">1</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>个月。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">程海军：<span lang="EN-US">1973</span>年生，现年五十一岁，家住辽宁省锦州市北镇市沟帮子开发区铁南街，他于一九九七年开始修炼法轮功。一九九九年江泽民发动对法轮功的迫害后，程海军曾在<span lang="EN-US">2000</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月、<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月两次去北京为法轮功鸣冤上访，第一次被绑架、关押，勒索三千元后被释放；第二次程海军被劳教一年半。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">2023</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">10</span>日晚，锦州市北镇市大屯乡派出所警察以发放法轮功资料为由，抓捕了法轮功学员程海军、朱成芝夫妇，并抄家。后遭刑事拘留、批捕、起诉。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">2024</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">26</span>日上午，凌海市法院判处程海军有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">1</span>年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>个月，朱成芝有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">1</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>个月。程海军刑期至：<span lang="EN-US">2025</span>年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">9</span>日。朱成芝刑期至<span lang="EN-US">2025</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">9</span>日。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p>
</div>
