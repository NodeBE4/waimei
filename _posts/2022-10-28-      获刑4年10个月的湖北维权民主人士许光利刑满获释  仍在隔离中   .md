<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">28</span>日，本网获悉：获刑<span lang="EN-US">4</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>个月的湖北维权民主人士许光利已经于<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">25</span>日出狱，目前在隔离中，大概需要隔离七天。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">许光利简历：</span></b><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">1968</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">曾因长期致力于推动中国的民主化进程，多次参与公民维权事件，而遭中共当局多次严厉打压；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2012</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月，曾因其发起“支持温家宝政改签名活动”，而与湖南维权人士尹卫和于同年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">26</span>日在湖南湘乡被警方抓走，最初控其涉嫌“颠覆国家政权罪”；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2012</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月，又被侦办方指控为“与他人合谋准备炸毁毛泽东故居”，并变更罪名为“放火罪、爆炸罪”，最终判处有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">1</span>年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>个月；<span lang="EN-US">2013</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">25</span>日，其被刑满释放；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2016</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2017</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">13</span>日，因其到沈阳祭奠当天病逝于沈阳医院的诺贝尔和平奖得主刘晓波，再次被沈阳市警方抓走，并被非法囚禁近<span lang="EN-US">40</span>个小时后才被释放；同年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">14</span>日，因知名维权公民“屠夫”（吴淦）案开庭，恰逢其又到天津旅游，故而在其落地天津之时，旋即被天津当局抓捕，后被关押<span lang="EN-US">10</span>余个小时后才得释放；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">同年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">26</span>日，其正在广州经商，因购买了广东清远至湖北的车票，随后失联，后知其已于<span lang="EN-US">2017</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">29</span>日被湖北省荆门市警方以涉嫌“寻衅滋事罪”刑拘；<span lang="EN-US">2018</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">11</span>日，被荆门市警方转正式逮捕；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2018</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">9</span>日，其案在湖北省荆门市法院开庭受审，<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">30</span>日被该法院一审以同罪名判处有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">4</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>个月，刑期至<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">25</span>日。此前被羁押于湖北省荆门市看守所。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"></p><div class="separator" style="clear: both;
