[福州硕士访民叶钟在北京四通桥遭截访后再遭非法拘禁14天](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2022/11/14.html)

<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">2</span>日，本网获悉：自从<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">11</span>日逃出黑监狱就与外界失去联系的福州硕士访民叶钟，终于在今天向外界通报获释的信息，原来他在中共“二十大”期间勇闯北京，持健康绿码情况下，在四通桥遭截访后又被非法拘禁<span lang="EN-US">14</span>天。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2022</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">11</span>日，叶钟曾在微信上通报：“因进京维权，践行宪法赋于公民的合法权益，我在<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">16</span>日<span lang="EN-US">~2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">11</span>日，被福州市晋安区腐败乱作为分子、区委林涛和鼓山镇委林华等，非法关押黑监狱（先关在晋安区寿山乡农民房，后搬至晋安区福马路加油站旁的全国连锁酒店的全季酒店，同被关被侵权人员还有林鸿和邱香英），<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">11</span>日，本人叶钟在祷告上帝后，在神的帮助下艰难逃脱。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2022</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">18</span>日，福州市硕士访民叶钟在中共“二十大”举行期间冒死前往北京，结果于<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">18</span>日在北京北三环路四通桥遭北京警察盘查后，遭福州市晋安区鼓山镇党委书记林华带人绑架非法拘禁在宦溪镇桂湖收费站一农民房内，共计<span lang="EN-US">14</span>天，到昨天（<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">1</span>日）凌晨，被扔在福州三环路动物园旁，叶钟报警后甴晋安分局新店派出所送到家。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">据叶钟说，这次北京四通桥管辖地的万寿寺派出所所长亲自给他做了笔录，谈到了信访事项及赔偿的问题，所长说已帮他通报公安部，要求地方依法处理。但被交给截访人员后，遭到殴打与伤害，叶钟说：在这被非法拘禁的<span lang="EN-US">14</span>天内，从未让吃饱过，每天仅有两个馒头，不让洗澡、洗脸、刷牙、洗头，全身臭死痒死，毫无人道主义可言。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">叶钟是福州市晋安区鼓山镇连潘村人，是地方政府违法征地拆迁的受害者。<span lang="EN-US">2016</span>年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月，福州市晋安区鼓山镇连潘村叶钟的房屋，被镇政府以“晋安区连潘棚户区改造项目”为由强拆，妻子已经白血病四年。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体;
