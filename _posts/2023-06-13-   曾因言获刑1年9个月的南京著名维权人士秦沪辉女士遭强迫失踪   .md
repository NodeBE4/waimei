<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; font-size: 12.0pt;">秦沪辉女士简历：</span></b><span style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">南京公民。<span lang="EN-US">(</span>网名<span lang="EN-US">:</span>无票公民伤不起<span lang="EN-US">) </span>女，生于<span lang="EN-US">1950</span>年。现年<span lang="EN-US">73</span>岁。秦沪辉女士来自中共红色家庭，其外公是<span lang="EN-US">1923</span>左右入党，和博古是把兄弟，<span lang="EN-US">27</span>年牺牲的烈士，父亲<span lang="EN-US">57</span>年的烈士，参军死在部队，母亲是离休干部。秦沪辉女士因在自家私家车上粘贴宪政、新闻自由、司法独立、官员财产公示等标语，于<span lang="EN-US">2018</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">4</span>号竟被南京警方以寻衅滋事的罪名刑事拘留，<span lang="EN-US">2018</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">10</span>日遭正式逮捕。秦沪辉女士一直坚持自己无罪。<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">12</span>日，江苏南京公民秦沪辉女士因言获罪被控寻衅滋事罪案宣判，南京江宁区法院判处秦沪辉<span lang="EN-US">1</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>个月有期徒刑。刑期至<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">3</span>日。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体;
