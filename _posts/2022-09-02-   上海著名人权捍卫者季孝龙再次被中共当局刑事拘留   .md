font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">季孝龙是<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">31</span>日下午约三点半，被警察再次从家里带走，估计跟他致信上海市委书记李强有关。这是季孝龙自<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">9</span>日刑满释放以来第七次被警察问询或带走，这一次被正式刑事拘留。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: 宋体;
