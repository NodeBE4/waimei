</span></span>中国的监狱法<span lang="EN-US">71</span>条规定，监狱应比照中国劳动法规劳动<span lang="EN-US">8</span>小时，可延长<span lang="EN-US">1</span>小时，但他在赤山监狱时，实际工作长达<span lang="EN-US">11</span>到<span lang="EN-US">12</span>小时，每天早上<span lang="EN-US">6</span>点出门，一直工作到晚上<span lang="EN-US">6</span>、<span lang="EN-US">7</span>点。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体;
