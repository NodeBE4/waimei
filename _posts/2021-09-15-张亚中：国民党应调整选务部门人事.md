[张亚中：国民党应调整选务部门人事](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210915-1193924)
------

<div>
<p>国民党选监委员会今天决议撤回党主席候选人张亚中移送考纪会案，张亚中表示，乐见选监会在三天内做此决议，但他认为这次风波已重创国民党形象，选务中立性已被外界质疑，党中央应调整选务部门人事。</p><p>综合《联合报》、中时新闻网、中央社等报道，张亚中下午发出新闻稿称，这次风波已经让各界强烈质疑此次主席选举，选务部门的中立性，国民党应有足以对几组参选人以及党员、社会大众释疑的具体做法，包括调整已有明确不中立证据的负责选务人员的人事。</p><p>张亚中也表示，对于这场风波，朱立伦为此错愕，江启臣深感诧异，他并不意外，因为这是他参选以来对处境的感受，也是他参选的原因，就是要为国民党赢回尊敬。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>国民党选监委员会前天开会决议，张亚中多次攻击前国民党主席朱立伦的言论，涉违反竞选公约，移请国民党考纪会查明。这件事随即引发党内风暴，有关阴谋论声也四起。</p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/zbpe-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=zbpe399&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_desktop-zbpe399_2.png?dHEiGouxxZpWwWTJE04dyGyUN74JOU2z" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_desktop-zbpe399_2.png?dHEiGouxxZpWwWTJE04dyGyUN74JOU2z" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/zbpe-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=zbpe399&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_mobile-zbpe399_2.png?qZnBcIGCJvPR8A2IAZOZuOxdoGZDatiI" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_mobile-zbpe399_2.png?qZnBcIGCJvPR8A2IAZOZuOxdoGZDatiI" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
