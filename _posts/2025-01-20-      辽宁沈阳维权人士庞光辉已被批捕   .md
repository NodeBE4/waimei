[辽宁沈阳维权人士庞光辉已被批捕](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2025/01/blog-post_20.html)
------

<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2025</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">20</span>日，本网获悉：辽宁沈阳维权人士庞光辉于<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">30</span>被沈阳公安抓捕后刑事拘留，<a name="_Hlk188253722"><span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">13</span>日被以寻衅滋事罪批捕。</a>目前羁押于沈阳市大东区看守所。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">庞光辉：</span></b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">男，<span lang="EN-US">1960</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">2</span>日生，汉族，户籍地：辽宁省沈阳市大东区新东三街。</span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;"><span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">5</span>日，庞光辉前往北京公安部上访，在途径唐山火车站时，被铁路民警以网络通缉逃犯身份扣押，后交由沈阳市公安局经济开发区公安分局押回沈阳。<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">6</span>日，庞光辉再次被以寻衅滋事罪刑事拘留，直至<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">3</span>日被取保候审释放，同年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">29</span>日下午，在公园下象棋聊天时，被经济开发区公安分局带走并于次日<span lang="EN-US">(11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">30</span>日<span lang="EN-US">)</span>刑事拘留，先羁押于铁西区看守所。后于<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">13</span>日以寻衅滋事罪批捕，目前羁押于沈阳市大东区看守所。</span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体;
