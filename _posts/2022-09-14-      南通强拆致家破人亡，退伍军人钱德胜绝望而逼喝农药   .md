[南通强拆致家破人亡，退伍军人钱德胜绝望而逼喝农药](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2022/09/blog-post_17.html)

<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p><p class="MsoNormal"> </p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">14</span>日，本网获悉：江苏省南通市崇川区永兴街道芦泾港村老退伍军人钱德胜因遭遇强拆，长期维权未果，万念俱灰，于<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">12</span>日喝农药，经抢救才起死回生，但会留下后遗症。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">退伍军人钱德胜今年<span lang="EN-US">74</span>岁，曾被评为省、市劳动模范。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">在上世纪<span lang="EN-US">80</span>年代，钱德胜响应政府发展经济的号召，自筹资金经营养殖场，搞得有声有色，颇使权势者眼红。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2021</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月，南通市崇川区政府以台风来临为名，在没有任何合法手续、也没有事先通知的情况下，风雨中，百十余名保安拥着几台挖机，非法强拆了钱德胜经营了四十年的<span lang="EN-US">2000</span>平方米养殖场，现场一片狼藉。钱德胜养殖场的生产设施和生活用品都被埋废墟之下，一些贵重物品还来不及搬走，在强拆中不翼而飞。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">从此以后，钱德胜老人坚持维权，到各级政府喊冤！当地派出所为此多次将钱德胜老人拘禁，不仅恐吓他，并动用器悈对付他，导致钱德胜老人精神崩溃。在维权过程中，钱德胜的家人被骚扰，甚至于被殴打致伤。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">钱德胜的母亲，不堪强拆和维权的痛苦，悲愤欲绝，于今年三月含冤去世。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">知情人江晓燕，电话<span lang="EN-US">17558729684</span>。</span></p><p class="MsoNormal"></p><div class="separator" style="clear: both;
