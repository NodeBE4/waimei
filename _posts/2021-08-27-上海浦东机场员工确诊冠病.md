[上海浦东机场员工确诊冠病](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210827-1186944)
------

<div>
<p>上海浦东机场昨天（26日）新增一起本土确诊病例，病例是一名境外货机作业人员。</p><p>据微信公众号“上海发布”的消息，确诊者系之前确诊本地病例的密切接触者，常住浦东新区。8月20日已被集中隔离观察，区疾控中心例行核酸检测结果为阳性。</p><p>经排查，上海市疾控中心冠病病毒核酸检测结果为阳性。经市级专家会诊，结合临床、影像学表现和实验室核酸检测结果，诊断为冠病确诊病例。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>目前，已追踪到该病例在该市的密切接触者11人。对病例曾活动过的场所已进行终末消毒。</p><p>上海昨天也新增七例境外输入病例，截至8月26日24时，上海累计境外输入性确诊病例2081例，累计本地确诊病例381例。</p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
