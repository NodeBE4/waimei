<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">17</span>日，本网获悉：<span lang="EN-US">71</span>岁山东冠县法轮功学员郭书群被判刑第二天即含冤离世。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">郭书群<span lang="EN-US">:</span></span></b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">山东冠县法轮功学员<span lang="EN-US">,</span>男，<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">71</span>岁，家住冠县清水镇文村。郭书群与儿子郭金勇在<span lang="EN-US">2014</span>年同时遭冤狱迫害。其长期受迫害而至双目失明。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">2023</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">年郭书群因宣讲法轮功时被抓捕，后虽被取保候审，当仍立案迫害。<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月、<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月被庭审。<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">27</span>日还是被警察强行拉去所谓的“走程序”，体检后图谋关押未遂，遂被冠县法院判刑三年半、监外执行，并处罚金一万元，折腾了一天，晚上送回家。第二天<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">28</span>日上午，郭书群就含冤离世。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p>
