<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">16</span>日，本网获悉：天津市滨海新区塘沽地区法轮功女学员冉官权，现年<span lang="EN-US">75</span>岁，<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">1</span>日被当地派出所警察抓捕、关押到天津市看守所。后遭刑事拘留、批捕、起诉。<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">26</span>日、<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">2</span>日，滨海新区法院先后两次庭审冉官权老人。现获知，她已被滨海新区法院判刑<span lang="EN-US">2</span>年半、罚金八千元，刑期至：<span lang="EN-US">2026</span>年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">31</span>日。这是冉官权第二次遭司法迫害，十年前她曾被滨海新区法院法院判刑<span lang="EN-US">3</span>年半。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p>
