[获刑4年6个月、曾遭酷刑的黑龙江法轮功学员王新民的案情及简历](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2022/09/46.html)

<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">9</span>日，本网获悉获刑<span lang="EN-US">4</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月、曾遭酷刑的黑龙江法轮功学员王新民的案情及简历，现在通报如下：<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">王新民</span></b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">：<span lang="EN-US">1949</span>年出生，黑龙江省牡丹江市人，牡丹江市爱民区公民，原黑龙江省烟草研究所高级农艺师，自由信仰追求者，法轮功信仰者，中国在押良心犯。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">早在<span lang="EN-US">1990</span>年代，曾因练习法轮功而深感身心受益，随后其全家人均开始接受并信仰法轮功；但自<span lang="EN-US">1999</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月中共当权者开始大规模迫害法轮功之后，其及家人屡遭黑龙江省当局的严酷打压；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2003</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">22</span>日，曾因坚持锻炼法轮功而与其妻子、女儿被牡丹江市爱民区警方一并抓捕，后其被牡丹江市爱民区法院以“邪教罪”枉判<span lang="EN-US">14</span>年，并在牡丹江监狱十监区服刑；服刑期间，曾惨遭毒打、奴役和吊铐等酷刑折磨；<span lang="EN-US">2014</span>年初，因其被残害严重导致急性出血坏死性胰腺炎，狱方怕其死于监狱而承担责任，遂于同年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月准其保外就医；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">但<span lang="EN-US">2015</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">4</span>日，六四敏感日期间，又被牡丹江监狱以欺骗手段强行收监，直至<span lang="EN-US">2016</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">23</span>日刑满释放；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2021</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">10</span>日，因牡丹江市以所谓“七一安全”为由，调动四区以及市下属六个市县的派出所警方，对凡不愿“转化”、不予签字、不放弃修炼和信仰法轮功者采取统一大抓捕，遂其与其他<span lang="EN-US">26</span>人被野蛮抓走，其家遭查抄；后其再度被以涉嫌“邪教罪”刑拘、逮捕，并被关押在牡丹江市看守所；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2021</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">29</span>日，其被黑龙江省牡丹江市爱民区法院一审以“利用邪教组织破坏法律实施罪”判处有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">4</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月，并处罚金<span lang="EN-US">5000</span>；其不服宣判，提起上诉；后经牡丹江市中级法院二审裁决，依然维持原判，刑期至<span lang="EN-US">2025</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">9</span>日。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">目前在黑龙江省泰来监狱老年监区（黑龙江省齐齐哈尔市泰来县泰来监狱老年监区，邮政编码：<span lang="EN-US">162401</span>）服刑。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"></p><div class="separator" style="clear: both;
