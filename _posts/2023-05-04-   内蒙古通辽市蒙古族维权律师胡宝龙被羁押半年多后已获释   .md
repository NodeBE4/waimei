[内蒙古通辽市蒙古族维权律师胡宝龙被羁押半年多后已获释](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2023/05/blog-post_93.html)
------

<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">4</span>日，本网获悉：于<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月被抓捕的内蒙古通辽市蒙古族维权律师胡宝龙被羁押半年多后，于<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月前已获释。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">胡宝龙简历：</span></b><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">1960</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年代出生，内蒙古自治区通辽市人，蒙古族，通辽市蒙嘎立律师事务所负责人，八九学运参与者，蒙古族维权律师，人权捍卫者，。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">早年就于内蒙古师范大学蒙文系，在读期间曾参与过八九学运；后从事律师职业，因立志要维护蒙古族的合法权益，故曾代理过内蒙古异议人士巴图张嘎案、著名蒙古族维权作家高玉莲案等；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2010</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">28</span>日，曾因代理巴图张嘎案遭打压，而被二连浩特市边防警察以涉嫌“危害国家安全”为由禁止出境，并被告知通辽市警方已将其列入“不能出境人员名单”；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2020</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月底至<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月初，因中共当局在内蒙古自治区全面实行汉化教育，从而引发通辽市、鄂尔多斯市、呼和浩特市等多地的数万学生及家长发起罢课及抗议集会，此举立即引起通辽市当局的极度恐慌与不满；其作为孩子的家长，因亦参与其中，并拒绝送孩子上课，以表达对政府强制实行新双语政策的不满；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">同年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月上旬，因在内蒙古的微信群中与境外朋友谈及内蒙古现状等话题，遂立即被通辽市警方以向境外“泄露秘密”为由刑拘；<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月，被通辽市检察院以涉嫌“寻衅滋事罪”正式批捕和起诉； 后于<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月获悉，其被羁押半年后取保获释。此前被羁押于内蒙古自治区通辽市看守所。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"></p><div class="separator" style="clear: both;
