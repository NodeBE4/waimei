[桃园市长郑文灿建议机组人员打第三剂疫苗](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210905-1190531)
------

<div>
<p>台湾长荣航空机师接种两剂疫苗后发生突破性感染，桃园市长郑文灿今天建议，必要时让机组员完成第三剂疫苗接种。</p><p>据联合新闻网报道，郑文灿指出，桃园市承担比别的县市更大风险，他在疫苗接种之初就特别跟中央要求，机场从业人员、航空业第一线机组人员，疫苗覆盖率要到100%；至于桃园市民目前疫苗覆盖率，至9月4日总接种剂次人口比约48.95%，略高于全台湾。</p><p>郑文灿说，社会各界对机组员应该关怀支持，不要标签化，毕竟空运是台湾经济支柱，台湾许多出口依赖空运，希望防疫标准严格，同时防疫保护也要完整周全。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/zbpe-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=zbpe399&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_desktop-zbpe399_2.png?dHEiGouxxZpWwWTJE04dyGyUN74JOU2z" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_desktop-zbpe399_2.png?dHEiGouxxZpWwWTJE04dyGyUN74JOU2z" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/zbpe-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=zbpe399&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_mobile-zbpe399_2.png?qZnBcIGCJvPR8A2IAZOZuOxdoGZDatiI" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_mobile-zbpe399_2.png?qZnBcIGCJvPR8A2IAZOZuOxdoGZDatiI" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
