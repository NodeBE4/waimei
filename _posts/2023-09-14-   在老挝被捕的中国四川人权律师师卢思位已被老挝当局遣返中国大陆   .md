[在老挝被捕的中国四川人权律师师卢思位已被老挝当局遣返中国大陆](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2023/09/blog-post_14.html)

<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">14</span>日，本网获悉：中国四川成都人权律师师卢思位先生于<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">28</span>日上午在准备登上一列开往泰国的火车时，被老挝警方逮捕的。卢思位的原定行程是在泰国搭乘班机前往美国与家人团聚。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">根据「对华援助协会」会长傅希秋牧师今天发布的最新消息：遭老挝警方逮捕的卢思位已经在上星期被老挝政府连同其他<span lang="EN-US">50</span>名中国人，一起分乘两辆车，遣返中国。傅希秋表示：国际社会都辜负了卢思位以及他全家人，没有完成这次救援任务。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">卢思位曾多次代理中国异议人士和香港活动人士。后被吊销律师证，并遭当局严密监控，处境极其艰难。卢思位律师这次被老挝当局遣返，将立即面临中共当局的监禁。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">：<span lang="EN-US">1973</span>年生，四川省知名人权律师，办理过成都纪念「八九六四」酒案，是成都「四君子」之一张隽勇的辩护人，曾代理十二港人“偷渡案”、余文生 、王藏 、覃永沛 、陈家鸿 等“煽动颠覆国家政权案”。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2017</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年，卢思位律师就发起公民联署，呼吁全国人大成立特别调查委员会，就“<span lang="EN-US">709</span>”系列案件是否存在强迫服药等酷刑进行独立调查，调查委员会的组成人员应当包括律师和公民代表，并向公开调查结论。厦门聚会案发生后，卢思位律师受到传唤及威胁，并被禁止出境。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2020</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">24</span>日卢思位律师因办理过广西律师陈家鸿「煽动颠覆国家政权案」，曾遭受四川司法当局和律师协会打压和处分。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2020</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月，余文生律师因煽动颠覆国家政权罪成，被徐州市中级法院判处徒刑<span lang="EN-US">4</span>年，卢思位律师作为二审辩护人参与辩护工作。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2020</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月，有十二位香港社运人士被深圳公安扣押在盐田看守所，卢思位律师曾经探访十二位被扣押香港人，又向他们提供法律援助。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">3</span>日成都市羊青区司法局对卢思位律师代理的所有案件材料检查。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2021</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">4</span>日，四川省司法厅对卢思位律师开出行政处罚案件当事人听证权利告知书，准备吊销卢律师的律师执业证。这是<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年当局对维权律师开出的第一张罚单。在这份标号为“川司罚告字<span lang="EN-US">[2021]</span>第<span lang="EN-US">1</span>号”的文件中，四川省司法厅吊销卢思位律师执业证的理由是，“在互联网上多次发表不当言论，时间跨度长，发文数量多，严重损害律师行业形象，造成恶劣社会影响”。</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;
