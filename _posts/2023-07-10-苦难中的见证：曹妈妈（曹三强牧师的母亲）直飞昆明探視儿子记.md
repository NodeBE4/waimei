[苦难中的见证：曹妈妈（曹三强牧师的母亲）直飞昆明探視儿子记](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2023/07/blog-post_10.html)
------

<div>
<p><span></span></p><a name='more'></a><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;"><br /></span><p></p><p><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">曹妈妈的泰国之旅是受恩光教会利君姊妹与曹老师的学生卫老师邀请，订机票等等从头至尾我未操心。</span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">六月二十五日我从网上获得昆二监家属探視日期是<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">4</span>号。我就毫不犹豫<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">2</span>号<span lang="EN-US">(</span>往返机票是回长沙<span lang="EN-US">)</span>我不回长沙，我要去昆明看儿子，我告诉利君，她就请她的儿子帮我改簽机票。反复好几次才改簽好。我谢谢利君的儿子。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">7</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">月<span lang="EN-US">2</span>日卫老师飞清萊，陈利君飞长沙，我就直飞昆明。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">半月中我们三人同行形影不离。彼此相爱，依依不舍。我这位高龄老人行动迟钝，利君和卫老师总是等着我帮着我，饭菜水果送到我手中。谢谢她俩。神賜福，我享受了神的恩典。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">荣耀神。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">上午到达昆明，下午就有从大理来的陳泽牧师来看望曹三牧师的妈妈。还有肖敏弟兄在<span lang="EN-US">6</span>号也来与曹妈妈分享曹老师对他的培养和侍奉神。他们都请我吃喝，我在何地都享受神的恩典。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">我的女儿<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">3</span>号深夜乘飞机赶到了昆明看她的哥哥，<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">4</span>号早晨我们乘的士按时到达昆二监，排着长长的队，但心中踏实。我们马上能見到自己的亲人。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">我和女儿在探視窗口等着。就看见三强大步向我们走来。三强向我们招手说“妈妈，妈妈，妹妹！妈妈，你什么时候从泰国回来的？……”<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">“我前天<span lang="EN-US">2</span>号从泰国回来的…”我回答说，看来他以为在七月份只有他的妹妹来看他，妈妈我还在泰国，眼前看见了妈妈。所以他表示惊讶！<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">“我在泰国遇见了你好几位学生，他们都惦记他们的曹老师，询问你在监狱中的健康状况？他们都想到昆明来看你，我说不行。只有妈妈和他的妹妹才能够探视。在中国孟连他的妻子，儿子来过，因为他们是美国人，外国人。当时没有让他们母子探視，他们都掉着泪回美国去了…疫情期间他的两个儿子来昆二监狱看过他们的爸爸…”我将在泰国时三强的学生和我的谈话告诉他。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">“哥哥，你近来身体好不？我有许多事情要跟你说…”我还要说，但是他的妹妹抢着说了。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">我打量着儿子三强的头部脸部颈项他的手臂手背和双手。他不是光头，长有半寸长黑发，脸不消瘦，手臂双手不是皮包骨，都有肌肉。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">“三强你的两个儿子都跟奶奶写有长长的信息。向爸爸问好，他们特别特别的惦记爸爸，还说她爱奶奶，她要来中国看爸爸和奶奶。洁敏有短信，惦记你…”<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">三强的妹妹还在说话，我又插话说。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">三强说<span lang="EN-US">:</span>“妈妈，你说的话我都听清楚了，只有四分钟啦，您代我向我的美国孩子们亲人们问候和泰国的弟兄姊妹们加拿大，新加坡等等全世界的我的弟兄姊妹们问候，说曹三强惦记他们，想念他们，…”<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">三强还没有说完，我和她的妹妹也还有话要跟他说，就听见叮的一声响，都听不见声音呐，时间到了，是自动控制的。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">三强起立双手张开拍在窗子玻璃板上，妈妈的左手板与他的右手板重合，他的妹妹的右手板与他的左手板重合，表示握手拥抱，再见。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">我们原地徘徊相互目语，直到警官催我们离开，我们才挥手告别。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">神与曹三强牧师老师同在，他身体健康信仰坚定。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">感谢神！荣耀神！<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;"><b>2023</b></span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;"><b>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">8</span>日曹妈妈于昆明</b><span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;"></span></p><div class="separator" style="clear: both;
