[复旦等研发测眼知冠病风险技术](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20211001-1199081)
------

<div>
<p>中国复旦大学大数据学院人体感知实验室与中国和海外多家机构联合研发了通过测眼得知冠病风险的技术。研发团队在测试时发现，在这项技术查出的高风险人士中，几乎99%以上的核酸检测呈阳性。</p><p>据微信公众号“智东西”前天（9月29日）报道，这项“COVID-19 EYE TEST新冠感染风险检测技术”借助手机等智能硬件拍摄眼部照片，在3秒内判断是否有冠病感染的风险。</p><p>测试者可随时随地使用该技术，操作便捷、几乎没有成本。这项技术已在中国永久免费开放让民众使用。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>研发团队受到中医目诊相关理论学说、西方眼表微循环理论、眼表检测技术等启发，组建起包含机器学习、临床医学和商业领域专家的团队，开展全球跨地区跨人种冠病眼部检测临床实验。<br> </p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/zbpe-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=zbpe399&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_desktop-zbpe399_2.png?dHEiGouxxZpWwWTJE04dyGyUN74JOU2z" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_desktop-zbpe399_2.png?dHEiGouxxZpWwWTJE04dyGyUN74JOU2z" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/zbpe-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=zbpe399&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_mobile-zbpe399_2.png?qZnBcIGCJvPR8A2IAZOZuOxdoGZDatiI" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_mobile-zbpe399_2.png?qZnBcIGCJvPR8A2IAZOZuOxdoGZDatiI" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
