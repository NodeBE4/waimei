[台湾医师不建议莫德纳与高端疫苗混打](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210917-1194489)
------

<div>
<p>台湾启动莫德纳疫苗混打高端疫苗的实验，医师潘建志指出，理论上第二剂的蛋白质抗原越接近第一剂，混打效果越好；莫德纳可以混BNT、AZ也可以混莫德纳及BNT，唯独莫德纳混高端，他个人“不建议”。</p><p>据中时电子报报道，潘建志在面簿发文称，若只看胺基酸序列，上述四种疫苗在抗原表达上几乎没差异，看起来好像都能混打。但事情没这么简单，他以“输血不能乱混”为比喻，称易出现免疫排斥反应。</p><p>潘建志强调，全世界还没有其他品牌的次蛋白疫苗上市，自然没有混打先例，台湾必须从头开始做临床实验，加上高端产能有限，他建议让高端专心“自已混自已”，等到保护力、安全性这些数据更清楚，再来考虑混打。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/zbpe-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=zbpe399&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_desktop-zbpe399_2.png?dHEiGouxxZpWwWTJE04dyGyUN74JOU2z" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_desktop-zbpe399_2.png?dHEiGouxxZpWwWTJE04dyGyUN74JOU2z" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/zbpe-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=zbpe399&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_mobile-zbpe399_2.png?qZnBcIGCJvPR8A2IAZOZuOxdoGZDatiI" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_mobile-zbpe399_2.png?qZnBcIGCJvPR8A2IAZOZuOxdoGZDatiI" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
