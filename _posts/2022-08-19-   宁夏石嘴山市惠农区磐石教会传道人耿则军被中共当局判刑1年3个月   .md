[宁夏石嘴山市惠农区磐石教会传道人耿则军被中共当局判刑1年3个月](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2022/08/13.html)
------

<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">19</span>日，本网获悉：宁夏石嘴山市惠农区磐石教会传道人耿则军因为组织十多个人的基督教聚会被判处一年三个月的有期徒刑。其简历和案情如下：<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">耿则军</span></b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">：<span lang="EN-US">1972</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">31</span>日出生，宁夏回族自治区石嘴山市人，嘴山市惠农区广西路<span lang="EN-US">38-6-201</span>号公居民，基督徒，嘴山市惠农区盘石教会传道人，中国在押良心犯。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">耿则军因在宁夏回族自治区石嘴山市惠农区盘石教会做全职传道人，并其所在教会属归正福音家庭教会，拒不接受有官方背景的“三自教会”管理，遂长期遭石嘴山市当局的监控和伺机打压；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2021</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">5</span>日，因其家庭教会坚持一贯性的聚会（即每个礼拜天<span lang="EN-US">/</span>星期天 的主日敬拜），正当其及教会众信徒分别在惠农区育才路<span lang="EN-US">140</span>号、静安一区<span lang="EN-US">3-3-402</span>号举行聚会时，突遭石嘴山市惠农区警方、两地派出所民警及宗教局人员联合冲击，除在场老年人被登记之后准予送回住处外，其等多人均被抓捕；次日，其被警方以“扰乱社会秩序”为由处行政拘留<span lang="EN-US">15</span>天，并送是最上市行政拘留所关押；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">另有其妻子郝瑛和教友罗时平、叶宁芳、朱俊英及王素芳等<span lang="EN-US">5</span>人亦被警方以同罪由处行政拘留<span lang="EN-US">10</span>天；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2022</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">4</span>日，其再次被传唤，后被嘴山市惠农区警方以涉嫌“组织、资助非法聚集罪”刑拘；同年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">25</span>日，又被嘴山市惠农区检察院以涉嫌“组织非法聚集罪”正式批捕，指控其因多次在惠农区多地“利用基督教进行非法聚会”，严重扰乱了社会秩序，故其行为触犯了政府相关法规；后其案被地方检察院提起公诉，又移交至石嘴山市惠农区法院；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2022</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">13</span>日，其案在嘴山市惠农区法院开庭审理月；庭审中，因其坚持不认罪，故而检方建议法院对其量刑<span lang="EN-US">11</span>个月，半个月出判决书。今被判刑<span lang="EN-US">1</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>个月。刑期至2023年3月19日。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体;
