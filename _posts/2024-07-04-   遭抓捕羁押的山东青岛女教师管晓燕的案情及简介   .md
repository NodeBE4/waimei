<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">5</span>日，本网获悉遭抓捕羁押的山东青岛女教师管晓燕的案情及简介，现在通报如下：<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><b><span style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">2018</span><span style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月起，因抵抗强拆、上访被潮海派出所传唤、拘留多次。<span lang="EN-US">2019</span>年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月拘留期间两处房屋被拆。<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月被截访控制人身自由，因高血压停药心脏病复发险些丧命。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">2021</span><span style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">16</span>日，即墨区山师实验学校领导王光迎询问联名建议书，劝管晓燕放弃维权，被拒。<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">24</span>日抓捕后关押在医院一年于<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月取保候审。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">取保期间管晓燕依然不放弃利用网络在访民群体中进行普法活动，于<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月份再次在网络访民群体开展题目为<span lang="EN-US">“</span>金色的翅膀”的签名活动，准备将签名提交人大申请成立访民监督管理委员会。总签名人数达到一万一千八百多人，签名者提供身份证正反两面照片作为实名签名依据。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">据悉案件已于<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">10</span>号在青岛第二看守所内开庭，尚无结果。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 楷体;
