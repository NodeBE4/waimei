font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">果洛州组织部调研报告承认了所谓的全过程人民民主在涉藏地区推行的困难和问题。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">一言堂问题在果洛州党组织系统中普遍存在<span lang="EN-US">,</span>在干部民主推选<span lang="EN-US">,</span>测评<span lang="EN-US">,</span>评议和选举工作中<span lang="EN-US">,</span>许多党员的参与浮于形式。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体;
