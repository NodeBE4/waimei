<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: major-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: major-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: major-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">3</span>日，本网获悉遭中共当局判处死缓的格尔登寺僧人罗让贡求的案情及简历，现通报如下：<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: major-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: major-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: major-fareast;">罗让贡求：</span></b><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: major-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: major-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: major-fareast;">1972</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: major-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: major-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: major-fareast;">年出生，四川省阿坝藏族羌族自治州人，格尔登寺僧人、中国在押良心犯。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: major-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: major-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: major-fareast;">曾因与境外藏族流亡组织“格尔底寺新闻联络小组”成员三木旦保持联系，<span lang="EN-US">2009</span>年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月起又因藏区出现自焚抗争浪潮，格尔登寺年轻僧人扎白等人在此亦以自焚行动向中共当局对藏区长期实施高压严控政策表达强烈不满与抗争，其为让外界获此消息，随将扎白等自焚者的身份、背景等信息发给流亡印度的藏人组织，引起中共当局的强烈不满；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: major-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: major-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: major-fareast;">2012</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: major-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: major-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: major-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月，因四川省阿坝县再次发生多起藏人自焚抗争事件，随引起中共高层的极度恐慌和严酷打压；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: major-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: major-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: major-fareast;">2012</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: major-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: major-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: major-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">13</span>日，其被四川省阿坝县警方以“煽动自焚杀人”罪名抓捕刑拘，并被指控“利用其格西身份，在众僧侣中大肆鼓动自焚，单独或伙同被告人罗让才让在阿坝县物色自焚人员，先后煽动、引诱、教唆或者胁迫多人自焚死亡，并将自焚的相关信息提供给境外组织，以使他们用此进行分裂活动”；后被当地警方正式逮捕；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: major-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: major-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: major-fareast;">2013</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: major-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: major-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: major-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">31</span>日，被四川省阿坝藏族羌族自治州中级法院以“故意杀人罪”判处死刑，缓期<span lang="EN-US">2</span>年执行，剥夺政治权利终身。目前疑似在四川省阿坝监狱服刑。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"></p><div class="separator" style="clear: both;
