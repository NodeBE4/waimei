font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin;">万，扣除</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12.0pt;"> 10</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin;">万元，</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12.0pt;">167</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin;">万元的金额抵了三套房，这个是否属实</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12.0pt;">?</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin;">”这显然在给原告挖坑。关于是否以“理财金抵扣房”的法律事实，应当由玖承公司举证，然后由原告质证。在玖承公司没有举证的情况下，陈法官不顾证据规则，用话来套原告。原告回答：“不属实”。但陈法官很不满意，继续问：“你这三套房一共多少钱</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12.0pt;">?</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;
