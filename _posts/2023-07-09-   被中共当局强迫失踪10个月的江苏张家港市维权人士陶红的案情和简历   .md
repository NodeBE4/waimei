[被中共当局强迫失踪10个月的江苏张家港市维权人士陶红的案情和简历](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2023/07/10_9.html)

<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">9</span>日，本网获悉被中共当局强迫失踪<span lang="EN-US">10</span>个月的江苏张家港市维权人士陶红的案情和简历，现在通报如下：<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">陶红：</span></b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">女，<span lang="EN-US">1967</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">15</span>日出生，江苏省苏州市张家港市人，张家港市杨舍镇范庄村村民，暴力强拆受害者，维权访民。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2012</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月，曾因张家港市土地储备中心和张家港市合力房屋拆迁有限公司（简称“杨舍镇动迁办”）在无合法手续和无补偿的情况下，采取欺骗手段将其等所在村民的合法房产暴力强拆，并强占其土地，导致其与多家被拆迁户流离失所，后经多次向政府索赔，政府将其等安置在一排有严重火灾隐患的临建房内暂住，承诺日后会给他们合理补偿；然而时至<span lang="EN-US">2017</span>年，政府承诺一直不予兑现，同年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">6</span>日，其所居临时房突遭火灾，导致一整排<span lang="EN-US">22</span>间房都被烧毁，其等多人财物均损毁殆尽；为讨公道，其等多位村民向政府部门投诉，要求赔偿，却遭相互推诿，不予赔偿；为挽回损失和追查事故责任人，其被迫走上维权上访路；后因江苏省各级信访部门对其投诉均不作为，其只得进京上访继续维权，但自此屡遭地方当局报复性打压和截访虐待；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2017</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月，曾因在京控告投诉，被北京警方拦截后送久敬庄拘押，遣返原籍后，又被当地警方继续囚禁<span lang="EN-US">10</span>多个小时；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2018</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月，曾因在北京投诉，从邮局寄信出来时又被北京警方送往久敬庄关押，后再被张家港市警方行政拘留<span lang="EN-US">15</span>天；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2019</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">26</span>日，曾因在京上访时遭截访人员再次野蛮绑架，并被张家港市警方以涉嫌“寻衅滋事罪”刑拘<span lang="EN-US">30</span>天，并遭非人道虐待；获释后其一纸诉状起诉维稳警方涉嫌非法行政强制自己，<span lang="EN-US">2019</span>年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月，其案在江苏省昆山市法院开庭庭理，且最终判赢，但被控警方依然不予道歉和补偿；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2020</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">16</span>日，其<span lang="EN-US">84</span>岁婆婆的合法房屋再遭当地小区政府偷拆，因该处房屋继承人是其及其<span lang="EN-US">4</span>个子女，虽再次投诉，又遭进一步逼迫打压；同年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">29</span>日，曾因去北京投诉维权，在途经河北沧州青县安检站时遭警方拦截，并被以其身份证显示是“上访人员”为由而非法拘禁<span lang="EN-US">10</span>小时后强制带回；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">因其自维权上访以来，多次遭打击报复和截访虐待，其身上的伤势从未间断，且其家人遭牵连，其大学毕业的儿子多次打工均被骚扰恐吓，以致于其儿子数次失业，在家一蹶不振；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2022</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月，因江苏省江阴市维权人士毛黎惠女士被火烧死亡事件发生，其积极联络各方朋友参与调查真相，并接受媒体采访，质疑毛女士是被江阴市当局制造的杀人灭口案所致，遂于同年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">9</span>日突遭强迫失踪；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">截至<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">9</span>日，其已被强迫失踪<span lang="EN-US">10</span>个月，一直杳无音讯，维权友人吁社会正义人士多加关注和给予声援；据悉，其在被失踪前，曾在微信朋友圈中表示“绝不会自杀”。目前被羁押地址不详。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"></p><div class="separator" style="clear: both;
