[狱中10年遭残酷迫害致残的河北省唐山市法轮功学员郑祥星的案情通报](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2022/01/10.html)
------

<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">28</span>日，本网获悉狱中<span lang="EN-US">10</span>年遭残酷迫害致残的河北省唐山市法轮功学员郑祥星的案情，现在通报如下：<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">郑祥星：</span></b><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">1968</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年出生，河北省唐山市唐海县（现改为曹妃甸区）人，原唐山市第十农场“星云家电”公司老板，法轮功信仰者，中国在押良心犯。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">早在<span lang="EN-US">2001</span>年，曾为当地有名的小混混，后因和朋友骑摩托车外出时与警察发生冲突而被拘留<span lang="EN-US">15</span>天，此期间，因和一名法轮功信徒关在一起两天三夜，随被其言行举止所触动，之后开始努力改掉身上的恶习，并归劝从前常混在一起的哥们儿“别打架了，做个好人”；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">信仰法轮功之后，其开了一家名叫“星云家电”的家用电器商店，自此成为当地有名的诚信慈善商人；然而，却因笃信和修炼、宣传法轮功而遭中共当局的严酷打压；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2012</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">25</span>日，曾因传播法轮功信息而被唐海县警方以涉嫌邪教罪由绑架、刑拘；同年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">26</span>日，被唐山市唐海县法院以“利用邪教组织破坏法律实施罪”重判有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">10</span>年；后其很快被送保定监狱服刑，刑期至<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">25</span>日；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">然而，入狱不久其家人即被获悉，<span lang="EN-US">2012</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">27</span>日，因其在狱中遭酷刑折磨，导致颅脑严重损伤，被送到保定市第一中心医院救治时被秘密做了两侧开颅手术，左右各摘掉直径约<span lang="EN-US">10</span>厘米的骨片，同时语言、记忆、视觉部份脑组织也被切除，以致于其被开颅致残，险被灭口；此举令其亲属和友人们大惑不解，不由得惊惧道：“一个<span lang="EN-US">40</span>多岁的壮汉子，从被劫持到保定监狱仅<span lang="EN-US">2</span>个多月，就被迫害的面目全非！”；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2013</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">21</span>日，因其已双目近失明，大小便失禁，瘫痪在床，故被滞留在医院重症监护室，而当其嘴里能发出声音时，又被狱方强行收监；同年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">25</span>日，其被秘密做了第二次开颅手术，术后不到一个月，又被收监；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">至今，其亲友们一致质疑：“究竟遭受了何种酷刑和折磨才使其脑部严重伤残，后又被秘密、残忍地做了两次开颅手术，导致其丧失记忆和语言能力、双目近乎失明、瘫痪在床”。目前在河北省保定监狱服刑。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"></p><div class="separator" style="clear: both;
