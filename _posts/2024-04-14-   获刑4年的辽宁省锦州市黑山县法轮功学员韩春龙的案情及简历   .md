<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">14</span>日，本网获悉获刑<span lang="EN-US">4</span>年的辽宁省锦州市黑山县法轮功学员韩春龙的案情及简历，现在通报如下：<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">韩春龙：辽宁省锦州市黑山县法轮功学员，男，<span lang="EN-US">1972</span>年生，现年<span lang="EN-US">52</span>岁，黑山县镇安乡营盘村人。一九九八年三月，韩春龙开始修炼法轮功。<span lang="EN-US">2013</span>年曾遭中共当局迫害，判刑四年。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">2023</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">2</span>日，韩春龙在沈阳故宫出口给民众发送法轮功资讯蓝牙服务器时遭人举报，被警察抓捕到皇城派出所，<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">3</span>日被关押到沈河区看守所。<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">27</span>日，沈阳市大东区法院开庭庭审韩春龙，并于<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">25</span>日对他判刑<span lang="EN-US">4</span>年，罚金三万。刑期至<span lang="EN-US">2027</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">1</span>日。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p>
