<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">4</span>日，本网获悉获刑<span lang="EN-US">3</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月的黑龙江省哈尔滨市法轮功学员肖崐女士的案情及简历，现在通报如下：<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">肖崐：</span></b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">黑龙江省哈尔滨市阿城区法轮功学员，女。肖洋、肖崐（肖昆）姐妹曾于<span lang="EN-US">2011</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">31</span>日在自己开的照相馆里被哈尔滨市香坊区国保大队、和平派出所警察抓捕。<span lang="EN-US">2012</span>年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">18</span>日，肖洋、肖崐姐妹均被香坊区法院判刑<span lang="EN-US">7</span>年，<span lang="EN-US">2012</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">19</span>日被关押到黑龙江省女子监狱迫害。肖洋、肖崐姐妹于<span lang="EN-US">2018</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">30</span>日结束冤狱。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">2023</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">23</span>日下午五点，肖崐和姐姐肖洋在家中楼下被哈尔滨市阿城区国保大队警察抓捕，抄家。警察将肖崐、肖洋劫持到阿城区公安分局，被审讯到半夜。后半夜肖洋被放回。肖崐则被警察关押到哈尔滨市第二看守所。肖崐对警察的一切要求都不配合，她不<span lang="EN-US">“</span>转化<span lang="EN-US">”</span>、不签字、零口供，之后被批捕。<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月左右，肖崐被阿城区法院判刑<span lang="EN-US">3</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月。刑期至<span lang="EN-US">2027</span>年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">22</span>日。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p>
