[遭香港当局迫害获刑的前香港支联会主席、香港大律师、前立法会议员何俊仁简历](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2022/05/blog-post_89.html)
------

<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">22</span>日，本网获悉遭香港当局迫害获刑的前香港支联会主席、香港大律师、前立法会议员何俊仁简历，现通报如下：<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">何俊仁：</span></b><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">1951</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">1</span>日出生，英文名：<span lang="EN-US">Albert Ho Chun-yan</span>，原英属香港籍、现中华人民共和国（香港）籍人，香港岛天后区公民，执业律师和公证律师，香港护士协会义务法律顾问，前香港支联会主席，前屯门区议会乐翠区议员，前民主党主席和前立法会泛民主派议员，中国在押政治犯。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">自幼成长于香港；曾于<span lang="EN-US">1971</span>年入读香港大学法律系，并在此期间曾积极参与<span lang="EN-US">70</span>年代的香港学运（含反贪污、盲人工潮、保卫钓鱼台等运动），以及担任香港大学学生会普选评议员；<span lang="EN-US">1977</span>年毕业，后成为执业律师；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">1982</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">-1985</span>年，曾投身政治，支持香港回归祖国和实行港人民主治港，并曾参与成立「民主政制促进联委会」，争取「八八直选」和民主基本法；曾多次回国参与现代化培训项目，并组织论政团体太平山学会等；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">1990</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年，曾与他人组建香港第一个政党“港同盟”，至<span lang="EN-US">1994</span>年又将之扩充为现时的民主党，并一直担任党内核心职位；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">1995</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">-2016</span>年，曾一直担任立法局议员，并在<span lang="EN-US">1998</span>年香港回归前，曾被广誉为“三料议员”（即立法局议员、区议会议员和区域市政局议员）；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2006</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年，曾因成立中国维权律师关注组，关注和支持内地维权律师和维权运动，以图促进国家尊重人权，法治与宪政体制的建立，随其鲜明的民主政治主张遭到中共政府的极端仇恨，自此屡遭暴力袭击；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2006</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">20</span>日，曾在出席反销售税大游行后到一家麦当劳餐厅用膳时，遭<span lang="EN-US">3</span>、<span lang="EN-US">4</span>名歹徒（实为反泛民主派人士指使）突然袭击受重伤，后被紧急送医院做手术救治才幸免遇难，但在其出院返家时，其律师事务所又收到匿名恐吓信，内附有刀片及威胁性文字；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2010</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月，曾以民主党主席身份建议政府接受终极普选联盟的区议员改良方案，后经不懈努力，终于<span lang="EN-US">2012</span>年使改良方案得以高票通过；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2013</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">-2014</span>年，曾因泛民主派人士发起让爱与和平占领中环运动，以不合作运动形式争取民主，尤其是<span lang="EN-US">2014</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">28</span>日，“学民思潮”的学生抗议集会引发<span lang="EN-US">9</span>·<span lang="EN-US">28</span>催泪弹镇压和雨伞运动之后，其在中间起到了积极的协调与推动民主党支持的作用；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2015</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">8</span>日，曾因为了争取香港民主 “真普选”，而自请辞去民主党主席职位，以便更好地推动补选公投和启动党内民主程序争取通过支持；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2019</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">19</span>日，曾因参与反送中运动而在当晚搭乘铁路返家时再遭围殴；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2020</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">18</span>日，其与多名同属前任和现任立法会议员和多名泛民主派人士，如区诺轩、梁国雄、李卓人、以及商人黎智英等<span lang="EN-US">15</span>人，突然被国安局警方以参加了<span lang="EN-US">2019</span>年的六四纪念集会游行为由，以涉嫌“非法集结罪”悉数拘捕；同年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">18</span>日，其一行<span lang="EN-US">15</span>在香港西九龙裁判法院提堂受审，后因被告人数众多，法院延迟受审至<span lang="EN-US">2019</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">1</span>日再讯；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">同年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">13</span>日，其发表声明，宣布辞去所有华人民主书院丶维权律师关注组及支联会所有职务，并退出有关组织；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">16</span>日，其案在西九龙法院再一次开审受审，至同年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">16</span>日，其被法院以“组织及参与非法集会罪”判刑<span lang="EN-US">12</span>个月，缓刑两年；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">15</span>日，又以曾于<span lang="EN-US">2019</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">1</span>日参与“没有国庆，只有国殇”游行为由，被西九龙法院的港版国安法指定法官再以“煽动参与未经批准集结罪”判刑入监<span lang="EN-US">10</span>个月；至今，先后两罪被判刑期共计<span lang="EN-US">1</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>个月（<span lang="EN-US">22</span>个月）。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体;
