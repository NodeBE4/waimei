<p><span></span></p><a name='more'></a><p class="MsoNormal"><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">8</span>日，本网获悉获刑<span lang="EN-US">8</span>年的鄂州市鄂钢桥家庭教会牧师郝志娓的案情及简介，现在通报如下：<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">郝志娓</span></b><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">：女，<span lang="EN-US">1972</span>年出生，山西省籍人，湖北省鄂州市鄂城区居民，基督徒，鄂州市鄂钢桥家庭教会牧师，中国在押良心犯。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">早年曾就读于湖北武汉的中南神学院（由豫、鄂、湘、粤、桂、琼等省市基督教协会合办）；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2001</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年毕业后，曾被邀请到湖北省鄂州市“三自”爱国会教堂讲道，并因讲道效果好，深得信徒赞许，故而最终成为教堂的专职神职人员；后因其不认同宗教局管理，将其所在教会从“三自”变为 “非三自”，遂其牧师身份一直未经鄂州市宗教局审批认证；之后其教会被官方取缔，但其依旧在原址教堂聚会讲道，并于<span lang="EN-US">2013</span>年按照家庭教会的传统惯例，由<span lang="EN-US">3</span>名牧师按立成为牧师，此外，教会的奉献款由几个主要同工进行管理和支出（含教会的日常支出、奉养牧师和支持别的传道人等）；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2018</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月，曾因当地政府对鄂钢桥教会的教堂提出拆迁要求，被信徒拒绝，故而鄂州市民宗局曾发《告知书》要求其等配合政府拆迁，但因同年其丈夫患肝癌去世而需打理家事，故长时间未去教堂也未能及时与民宗局沟通；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2019</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">31</span>日，其与两位教会同工突然被鄂州市鄂城区警方抓捕，其中，其被以涉嫌“诈骗罪”刑拘，另两人分别被以“诈骗罪”和“阻碍公务罪”刑拘，并都被关押于鄂州市第一看守所；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">同年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">6</span>日，其等被当地鄂城区检察院以同罪名予以批捕，指控其等因在未经基督教两会批准的情况下，擅自传道和收取奉献金等，故此行为已涉嫌诈骗罪，应予逮捕、判刑；<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月，另两位同工在被超期关押<span lang="EN-US">2</span>年之后，终获取保释放，但其仍被续押不放；同年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月，其案在湖北省鄂州市鄂城区法院一审正式开庭审理，至<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">11</span>日，终被该法院以“诈骗罪”枉法重判有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">8</span>年，刑期至<span lang="EN-US">2027</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">30</span>日；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">据悉，自其被抓捕后，其上初中的小儿子摩西出现了严重的抑郁症倾向，并于当年辍学一年；而其因长期超押遭迫害，曾在看守所得了<span lang="EN-US">4</span>次急性胰腺炎，幸得 急救，险些丧命。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">目前疑似在湖北省鄂州监狱服刑。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><br /><p></p>
