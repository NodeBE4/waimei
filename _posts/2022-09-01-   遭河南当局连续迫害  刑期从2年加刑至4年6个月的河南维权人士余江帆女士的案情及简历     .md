[遭河南当局连续迫害  刑期从2年加刑至4年6个月的河南维权人士余江帆女士的案情及简历](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2022/09/246.html)

<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">1</span>日，本网获悉遭河南当局连续迫害刑期从<span lang="EN-US">2</span>年加刑至<span lang="EN-US">4</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月的河南维权人士余江帆女士的案情及简历<span lang="EN-US">,</span>现通报如下：<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">余江帆：</span></b><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">1989</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">24</span>日出生，女，河南省南阳市籍人，河南省邓州市夏集乡杨河移民村公民，维权访民，人称“访二代”，人权捍卫者，中国在押维权人士。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2011</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年，余江帆因支持南水北调西线工程，随其全家从原籍河南省淅川县香花镇搬迁至河南省邓州市夏集乡移民村；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">搬迁后，<span lang="EN-US">2013</span>年，因发现移民安置房存有重大质量安全隐患问题，余江帆随其父与多位村民前往省政府投诉，讨说法，不料却被当地警方带回，后被以“上访带头人”之名遭拘押、训诫及殴打，不久含恨去世；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">为完成父亲遗愿，其自此成了“访二代”，并多次依法逐级投诉表达要求，但毫无果效反遭严打；曾<span lang="EN-US">11</span>次被邓州市警方以 “扰乱公共秩序”罪由行政拘留十数日；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2014</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年，再次被邓州市警方行政拘留后又转刑事拘留，至<span lang="EN-US">2015</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">23</span>日，被河南省邓州市法院以“寻衅滋事罪”判刑<span lang="EN-US">3</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月，之后在郑州女子监狱服刑；<span lang="EN-US">2017</span>年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">7</span>日，刑满出狱；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">之后，其为谋生计而前往北京打工，但因其又积极参与北京地区的公民维权活动（如公民聚餐、声援关注良心犯等活动），故频遭北京警方的逼迫打压；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2017</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">8</span>日、<span lang="EN-US">2018</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">16</span>日，<span lang="EN-US">2019</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">8</span>日，曾<span lang="EN-US">3</span>次被邓州市警方以“扰乱单位<span lang="EN-US">/</span>公共场所秩序”为由各处行政拘留<span lang="EN-US">10</span>天；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2018</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年，曾因其与地方政府就村民房屋修缮问题未达成一致（即拒绝地方当局仅就其个人房屋修缮问题提出解决方案，坚持要求政府先对全村<span lang="EN-US">295</span>户房屋进行质量评估，按照评估结果凭现金发放给各户自行修缮，完毕后再解决自己的房屋问题），遂再遭强力打压；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2019</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月，余江帆在河北活动时，再被赶来的邓州市维稳人员拉走遣返，之后先被邓州市警方处行政拘留<span lang="EN-US">18</span>天；同年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">26</span>日，又被以涉嫌“寻衅滋事罪”转正式刑拘，并被送南阳市看守所羁押；同年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">1</span>日，被邓州市检察院以同罪名予以批捕，指控其之前的在京上访行为有违国家法律，又是累犯，故建议法院应予从重处罚；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2020</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">18</span>日，余江帆案在河南省邓州市法院开庭审理，但庭审中因检察院控其三起上访行为均发生在北京，且其一直常驻北京，故而河南省邓州市法院根本无管辖权，其案长时被迫休庭；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2021</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">22</span>日，被邓州市法院以“寻衅滋事罪”判处有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">2</span>年，刑期至<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">25</span>日；然而，当其刑期获释日并未被获释，理由是邓州市法院以其上诉南阳市中级法院裁定案件发回重审为由，拒绝对其（以取保候审）予以释放；<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">25</span>日，河南省邓州市法院以涉嫌“寻衅滋事罪”判处有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">4</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月；刑期至<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">25</span>日。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">目前羁押于河南省南阳市看守所。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体;
