<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; line-height: 115%; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-GB">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-GB">1</span>月<span lang="EN-GB">8</span>日，本网获悉：福州残障维权人士梁白端自<span lang="EN-GB">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-GB">12</span>月<span lang="EN-GB">20</span>日到昨日<span lang="EN-GB">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-GB">1</span>月<span lang="EN-GB">7</span>日不间断地到福州市公安局鼓楼分局举牌，抗议鼓楼公安行政不作为：<span lang="EN-GB"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-GB" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; line-height: 115%; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">1.2021</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; line-height: 115%; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-GB">3</span>月<span lang="EN-GB">30</span>日，梁白端在古厝集团咨询办理房屋重建事宜过程中，被鼓楼分局南街派出所公安任晓丹无端暴力殴打致肋骨骨折、手筋断了一根，治疗费用已达人民币三万多元。然而报警后至今不予受理，鼓楼分局仅口头答复梁：我们已经内部处理任晓丹了，给他记过了，但不需要给你书面材料。梁不服，因其是当事人，且至今任晓丹未对他的暴行向梁道歉，并赔偿梁的各项损失，梁要求必须受案立案。<span lang="EN-GB"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-GB" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; line-height: 115%; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2.2021</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; line-height: 115%; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-GB">8</span>月<span lang="EN-GB">18</span>日晚，梁白端被南街街道工作人骗离家中，之后街道人员伙同不明身份人员撬锁侵入梁家中，盗取其家中财物后才嚣张离开。报案至今不予理睬。<span lang="EN-GB"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; line-height: 115%; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">然而鼓楼公安竟是以撕毁梁白端的诉求横幅，又派南街派出所粗暴的将梁带离现场来回应。<span lang="EN-GB"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; line-height: 115%; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">底层民众维权太过艰难，梁白端向友人阐述：因其位于福州鼓楼区三坊七巷的住宅被福州当局看重，企图收入囊中发财，人生发生重大变故。<span lang="EN-GB"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-GB" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; line-height: 115%; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2009</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; line-height: 115%; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年四层共计<span lang="EN-GB">360</span>平方合法房屋被鼓楼当局违法强拆，夫妻二人被暴力殴打。梁不服，到处申诉控告，多次被当局雇佣的黑保安“修理”，后又遭遇离奇车祸，致双腿残疾，亲友一致认为是当局有意打击报复所致。维权至今，数次被当局雇佣黑保安殴打，被抢劫，被非法拘禁。<span lang="EN-GB"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; line-height: 115%; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">然而，最令她痛苦的是，陪伴了她大半辈子的丈夫，因为无法接受当局持续的打击报复梁，自觉无力帮助梁维护属于他们唯一的合法房产，被逼自杀身亡。<span lang="EN-GB"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; line-height: 115%; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">梁白端艰难维权的过程，并不仅仅是个案。当局宣传的“有福之州”，华丽的都市背后流淌着无数底层百姓的血泪。梁白端表示：生命不息，维权不止，不仅为自己，更是为子孙后代。<span lang="EN-GB"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"></p><div class="separator" style="clear: both;
