[福建省福州市七十多岁法轮功老太太张丽玉女士第四次被判刑](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2024/06/blog-post_79.html)
------

<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">21</span>日，本网获悉：福建省福州市七十多岁法轮功老太太张丽玉女士第四次被判刑。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">张丽玉：福建省福州市七十多岁法轮功学员，女，现年七十多岁，家住福建省福州市仓山区。张丽玉因坚持修炼法轮功，<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">12</span>日，张丽玉被仓山区法院判刑<span lang="EN-US">9</span>个月，她已被抓捕到福州市女子监狱迫害。刑期至<span lang="EN-US">2025</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">11</span>日。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">这是她第四次被非法判刑。前三次为：<span lang="EN-US">2000</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月～<span lang="EN-US">2004</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月被判三年半，<span lang="EN-US">2010</span>年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月～<span lang="EN-US">2014</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月被判三年缓刑四年，<span lang="EN-US">2016</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月再次被判八个月。中共不法人员还执行“经济上截断”的迫害政策，扣发她的养老金，剥夺她的基本生存权。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p>
