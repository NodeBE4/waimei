[北京发布“升温令”确保室温不降](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20211121-1215595)
------

<div>
<p>为应对悄然到来的冷空气，北京据报已要求当地各供热单位做好供电、供热保障工作。其中，在供热方面，发布供热“升温令”，要求各供热单位提前升温运行，做到降温期间“气温下降，室温不降”。</p><p>据《北京青年报》报道，气象部门预计，今明两天北京风力较大，阵风可达七到八级。明天北京最高气温仅4℃，最低气温-5℃，将创今冬以来新低。为此，北京市城市管理委表示，已要求各区、国网北京市电力公司、各供热单位做好供电、供热保障工作。</p><p>同时，加强巡检，遇突发情况迅速处置。目前，北京35支应急抢险队已经在岗备勤，24小时值守，应急设备充足，随时应对供热突发事件。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>本月6日，北京局部地区降下暴雪，而且这场雪来得比往年要早23天。</p>            <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/zbpe-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=zbpe399&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_desktop-zbpe399_2.png?VersionId=dHEiGouxxZpWwWTJE04dyGyUN74JOU2z" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_desktop-zbpe399_2.png?VersionId=dHEiGouxxZpWwWTJE04dyGyUN74JOU2z" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/zbpe-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=zbpe399&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_mobile-zbpe399_2.png?VersionId=qZnBcIGCJvPR8A2IAZOZuOxdoGZDatiI" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_mobile-zbpe399_2.png?VersionId=qZnBcIGCJvPR8A2IAZOZuOxdoGZDatiI" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
