[南京将适时展开第二轮全员核酸检测](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210724-1173207)
------

<div>
<p>江苏省南京市将于即日起启动重点地区、重点人员第二轮核酸检测。</p><p>据南京日报报道，中共南京市委常委会暨市委应对疫情工作领导小组、市防汛抗旱指挥部23日晚召开会议，研究部署疫情防控有关工作。南京市委书记韩立明主持。</p><p>会议指出，禄口机场疫情发生以来，全市迅速开展流调溯源、全员核酸检测、隔离管控、医疗救治等工作。截至23日晚，对21个中高风险地区实施重点管控，全市第一轮全员核酸采样基本完成。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>会议要求，在基本完成第一轮全员核酸检测的基础上，即日启动重点地区、重点人员第二轮核酸检测，优化组织、适时展开第二轮全员核酸检测。</p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
