<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">8</span>日，本网获悉：日前，广州<span lang="EN-US">11</span>•<span lang="EN-US">12</span>大抓捕案中资深民运人士范一平被中共当局以颠覆国家政权罪判刑<span lang="EN-US">4</span>年。范一平是资深民运人士，于<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月遭中共广州警方抓捕的。目前被羁押于广州巿花都看守所。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">范一平：</span></b><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">1950</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年代生，广东省广州市人，中共干部家庭出身，曾就读于军医大学，毕业后任外科医生，后转行成为某食品贸易公司经营者，成功珠宝商，自民主墙以来的老民运，中国在押政治犯。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">自民主墙时期就参与民主运动，多年来一直致力于推动中国社会的人权及法治进步事业，故长期受到当局监控和敏感时期被限制人身自由；<span lang="EN-US">1990</span>年末，曾因接待秘密回国推动民主运动的中国异议人士王炳章，并帮助另一位异见人士王希哲逃往美国，而被中共广东当局判刑入狱三年；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">出狱后，因继续持守民主理念和抗争，<span lang="EN-US"> 2014</span>年六四前夕，曾再次被广州警方以“寻衅滋事”为由行政拘留<span lang="EN-US">15</span>天，其居所遭搜查；因中共最高领导人习近平主政以来，对民主异见人士监控、打压愈加强烈，<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2020</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">12</span>日，其与另四名民运人士范文成、赖见君、乔连红、韦亚妮在“广州<span lang="EN-US">11</span>·<span lang="EN-US">12</span>大抓捕”中，被广州市警方先后以涉嫌“颠覆国家政权罪”抄家后带走；次日，其被送进医院，而后被指定居所监视住；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2021</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast;
