[福建二次“大抓捕”的冤假错案受害者林兰英要求恢复失地农民养老金](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2021/10/blog-post_30.html)

<div>
<p><span></span></p><a name='more'></a><p class="MsoNormal"><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">30</span>日，本网获悉：在“福州大抓捕”事件中被判刑一年十个月的林兰英，自从<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">12</span>日释放至今，当局仍然未恢复失地农民养老金，贫病交加的林兰英致信正在福州的中央第八督导组，但目前还没有回音。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">今年<span lang="EN-US">70</span>岁的林兰英，是福建省福州市仓山区城门镇黄山村村民，因两起交通事故造成女儿郑磊死亡，大儿子郑行梅残疾，均未获得妥善处理而上访超过二十年。虽然处理事故的张敏辉因受贿败露被赶回了家，但林兰英冤案不仅没有翻过来，反而屡遭打击报复。到<span lang="EN-US">2015</span>年，城门镇政府带来<span lang="EN-US">200</span>多人，将其房子强拆了，一家人被打。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2018</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">12</span>日，轰动世界的“福州大抓捕”当事人之一严兴声出狱。大家按传统风俗放了一串鞭炮驱除晦气，迎接友人出狱，不料众人被早已预谋“蹲守”现场的公安围堵抓捕。众维权人士连同家属及刚刚获释的严兴声在内三十人多人，全部被带走拘留，公安制造了第二场“福州大抓捕”事件。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">第二场“福州大抓捕”事件一个月后，林应强、何宗旺、唐兆星和林兰英等<span lang="EN-US">4</span>人被以“寻衅滋事罪”刑拘，并批准逮捕。<span lang="EN-US">2019</span>年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">22</span>日，林应强、唐兆星和林兰英被控“寻衅滋事案”一审在仓山法院开庭审理。当局限制旁听，不允许关注案件的公民围观。<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">17</span>日，林应强和唐兆星被法院判刑<span lang="EN-US">3</span>年，林兰英被判刑<span lang="EN-US">1</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>个月。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">林兰英说：无缘无故判刑，判了一年十个月啊，我什么都没做，现场监控视频也当庭播放了，就坐在那边被抓呢，进去时我<span lang="EN-US">130</span>多斤，出来只有<span lang="EN-US">95</span>斤，差一点死在里面。出来后才知道，我们几个人的家都被抄了，我那一点微薄的被征地农民养老金被停。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">据了解，林兰英和唐兆星等冤假错案的受害者，向中央政法队伍教育整顿第八督导组，举报控告福州市公检法，要求归还被侵吞的财产，同时恢复农保金、及低保待遇。但举报与诉求杳无音讯。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">林兰英电话：<span lang="EN-US">18906925250</span>。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体;
