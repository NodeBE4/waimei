<p><span></span></p><a name='more'></a><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;"><br /></span><p></p><p><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">（维权网信息中心报道）</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">2023</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">年</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">10</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">月</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">11</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">日，本网获悉获刑</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">4</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">年的湖南宁乡市周景成的案情及简历，现在通报如下：</span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">周景成：</span></b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">湖南宁乡市法轮功学员，是原宁乡县人民南路饲料厂厂长，早年修炼法轮功。一九九九年七月中共迫害法轮功到现在，周景成被非法拘留八次、非法劳教两次、两次被枉判，受尽酷刑，牙齿被撬掉、肋骨被打断、耳朵被打的萎缩变形……屡次被勒索敲诈，饲料厂也蒙受巨大损失。周景成很多年都在监狱里度过，受尽了各种非人的酷刑折磨。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">2021</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">29</span>日下午，周景成被从家中绑架直接关进宁乡市看守所。周景成绝食抗议迫害，在持续绝食到第<span lang="EN-US">59</span>天，<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">27</span>日他被释放回家，被作监视居住处理。<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">5</span>日，周景成被长沙市望城区法院开庭。<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月他被带到湖南湘雅附属二医院体检，<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">25</span>日长沙市望城区法院骗周景成去取体检报告把他截留关入宁乡市看守所；<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">28</span>日他被送入湖南株洲攸县网岭监狱，后周景成被判了<span lang="EN-US">4</span>年刑期。刑期至：<span lang="EN-US">2026</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">25</span>日。<span lang="EN-US" style="color: #00b050;"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p>
