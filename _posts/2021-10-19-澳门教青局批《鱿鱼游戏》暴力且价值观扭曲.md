[澳门教青局批《鱿鱼游戏》暴力且价值观扭曲](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20211019-1204818)
------

<div>
<p>Netflix原创韩剧《鱿鱼游戏》在全球爆红掀起热潮。澳门教育及青年发展局不点名指近日有网络平台播映的热门剧集《XX游戏》，内容含有大量暴力血腥、令人惊慄的元素、被扭曲的价值观，当中涉及骇人的暴力情节，甚至在全球掀起模仿潮，提醒有关作品属限制级别，不适合儿童及青少年观看，呼吁切勿模仿剧集中的暴力不当行为。</p><p>根据《星岛日报》报道，澳门当局指出，如果儿童及青少年看到了剧情中暴力血腥的部分，会对其产生心理上的影响，甚至模仿剧中的暴力行为，扭曲的价值观也深埋内心，必须予以正视，亦需要家长和学校正确的教育及辅导。</p><p>据了解，《鱿鱼游戏》上架以来，统计显示，观看过的人数估计达1.32亿人，打破纪录。根据Netflix文件显示，估计该剧价值达9亿美元(约12.1亿新元)。 </p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/zbpe-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=zbpe399&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_desktop-zbpe399_2.png?dHEiGouxxZpWwWTJE04dyGyUN74JOU2z" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_desktop-zbpe399_2.png?dHEiGouxxZpWwWTJE04dyGyUN74JOU2z" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/zbpe-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=zbpe399&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_mobile-zbpe399_2.png?qZnBcIGCJvPR8A2IAZOZuOxdoGZDatiI" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_mobile-zbpe399_2.png?qZnBcIGCJvPR8A2IAZOZuOxdoGZDatiI" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
