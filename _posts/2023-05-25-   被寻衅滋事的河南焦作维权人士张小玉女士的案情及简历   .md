mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2023</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月底一月底至二月初，因其在中共召开二十大期间前夕到北京旅游，为求维稳，其再度在北京被警方抓捕，并被河南省焦作市中站区警方以寻衅滋事罪刑拘；自此失联；同年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">12</span>日，又被焦作市中站区检察院以涉嫌“寻衅滋事罪”正式批捕；迄今，其因家庭经济困难而一直尚无代理律师介入。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;
