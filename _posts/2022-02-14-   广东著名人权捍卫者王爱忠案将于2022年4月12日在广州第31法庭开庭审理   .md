mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2021</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">28</span>日，又一次被广州市天河区警方诱骗抓走，同时，其家遭查抄，其妻亦被传唤，被问及其夫近期的交往、网络言论及接受外媒采以同罪名访等情况；<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">29</span>日，其被天河区警方以涉嫌“寻衅滋事罪”刑拘；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2021</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">6</span>日，被广州市天河区警方以同罪名转正式逮捕。后遭起诉，将于<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">12</span>日在广州第<span lang="EN-US">31</span>法庭开庭审理。目前被羁押于广州市天河区看守所（广东省广州市天河区上社横街<span lang="EN-US">5</span>号大院，邮政编码：<span lang="EN-US">510665</span>）。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;
