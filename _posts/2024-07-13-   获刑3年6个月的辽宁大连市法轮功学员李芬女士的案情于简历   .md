<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">13</span>日，本网获悉获刑<span lang="EN-US">3</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月的辽宁大连市法轮功学员李芬女士的案情于简历，现在通报如下：<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">李芬：</span></b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">辽宁大连市法轮功学员，女，一九六七年七月十六日生，家住大连市甘井子区乾峰海景花园。一九九七年三月，李芬开始修炼法轮功。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">2023</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">15</span>日李芬女士被抓捕，李芬被关押进大连市看守所。<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">25</span>日，李芬被甘井子区法院庭审，庭审如同儿戏般，仅十分钟，草草结束，当庭没有宣判。<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">1</span>日，李芬的家属被告知，李芬被大连市甘井子区法院判刑<span lang="EN-US">3</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月、罚金三万元。李芬不服一审判决，已上诉。刑期至：<span lang="EN-US">2027</span>年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">14</span>日。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p>
