[李克强考察河南卫河决口堤段修复情况](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210819-1184040)
------

<div>
<p>中国国务院李克强正在河南省考察，他到访正在修复中的卫河大堤，强调要把堤坝水库等水利设施的修复和加固作为重中之重。</p><p>据中国政府网报道，李克强登上正在修复中的卫河大堤说，堤坝是守护人民群众生命财产安全的防线，要吸取这次特大洪涝灾害的教训，把堤坝水库等水利设施的修复和加固作为重中之重，确保今后遇到特大洪涝灾害时守得牢、扛得住。</p><p>李克强要求，不仅要保障大江大河安澜，也要增强中小水利设施抗灾能力。要加快河流堤坝水库隐患排查整治，筑牢薄弱环节，科学统筹规划，合理灾后重建，守卫好群众家园。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>值得注意的是，李克强8月17日与中国国家主席习近平开完经济会议，今日便到一线视察。昨日习近平主持召开中央财经委员会第十次会议，李克强以财经委员会副主任的身份参加。</p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
