<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">18</span>日，本网获悉获刑<span lang="EN-US">2</span>年的吉林农安县法轮功学员于永琴的案情及简历，现在通报如下：<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">于永琴：</span></b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">吉林省农安县法轮功学员，女，<span lang="EN-US">1962</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">22</span>日生，现年<span lang="EN-US">61</span>岁，家住吉林省农安县农安镇闫家村。一九九九年三月开始修炼法轮大法。后中共迫害法轮功后，于永琴多次遭到迫害。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">于永琴和于凤珍<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">7</span>日在德惠市同太乡集市向民众宣传法轮功时，被同太派出所警察抓捕，后被关押到德惠市拘留所、看守所关押。期间于永琴因出现严重病症被保外就医。<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月，于凤珍被法院判刑两年。<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">15</span>日，被关押到位于长春的吉林省女子监狱迫害。刑期至：<span lang="EN-US">2025</span>年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月。<span lang="EN-US" style="color: #00b050;"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p>
