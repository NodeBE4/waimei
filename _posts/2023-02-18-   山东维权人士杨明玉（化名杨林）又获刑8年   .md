------

<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">18</span>日，本网获悉：山东维权人士杨明玉（化名杨林）<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">21</span>日被山东省临清市警方以涉嫌“寻衅滋事罪”刑事拘留，后音信全无，近日获悉，杨明玉又获刑<span lang="EN-US">8</span>年。此前杨明玉于<span lang="EN-US">2013</span>年曾被抓捕，被判刑<span lang="EN-US">3</span>年，<span lang="EN-US">2016</span>年才刑满释放。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">杨明玉：</span></b><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">1968</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">14</span>日出生，化名杨林，山东省聊城地区临清巿人，维权人士、零八宪章签署者，中国在押政治犯。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">多岁起即至深圳打工谋生，<span lang="EN-US">2006</span>年因发生工伤事故而遇不公，自此加入上访群体，也因此曾被判处有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">1</span>年，被处劳动教养<span lang="EN-US">1</span>年，被行政拘留<span lang="EN-US">11</span>次。由于在上访过程中屡遭不公与不幸，深感只有追求民主宪政才是唯一的出路，随毅然投入到推进中国民主宪政的事业中，尤其是<span lang="EN-US">2012</span>年—<span lang="EN-US">2013</span>年，曾多次积极参与举牌要求官员财产公示、呼吁公民基本权利等活动；这些行为最终招致当局的不满与忌恨，随于<span lang="EN-US">2013</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">11</span>日—<span lang="EN-US">12</span>日被深圳市警方秘密拘捕；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2013</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">19</span>日被深圳巿警方以涉嫌“煽动颠覆国家政权罪”正式逮捕，并被羁押在深圳市第二看守所<span lang="EN-US">213</span>监仓；<span lang="EN-US">2014</span>年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">27</span>日，其案在深圳市中级法院开庭审判；<span lang="EN-US">2015</span>年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">24</span>日，终被深圳市中级法院以同罪名判处有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">3</span>年，剥夺政治权利<span lang="EN-US">4</span>年；<span lang="EN-US">2016</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">11</span>日，刑满释放；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体;
