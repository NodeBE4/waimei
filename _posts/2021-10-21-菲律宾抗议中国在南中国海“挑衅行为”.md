[菲律宾抗议中国在南中国海“挑衅行为”](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20211021-1205429)
------

<div>
<p>菲律宾指中国政府船只在南中国海进行“挑衅”行为，并对此提出抗议。这显示两国在该片争端水域的关系继续紧张。</p><p>据彭博社报道，菲律宾外交部周三表示，“中国政府船只对于围绕菲律宾领土和海上经济区进行合法、例行巡逻的菲律宾机构实施了超过200次非法的无线电挑衅，拉警报及鸣笛，对此菲律宾正式提出抗议”。</p><p>菲律宾外交部强调：“这些挑衅行为威胁到南中国海的和平，秩序和安全，违背了中国根据国际法应承担的义务”。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>过去几个月，中菲在争议海域的争端不断升级，菲律宾多次抗议中国船只在该地区出现。菲律宾的诉求得到美国支持，而北京方面坚称中国的行动是正常且合法的。<br> </p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/zbpe-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=zbpe399&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_desktop-zbpe399_2.png?dHEiGouxxZpWwWTJE04dyGyUN74JOU2z" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_desktop-zbpe399_2.png?dHEiGouxxZpWwWTJE04dyGyUN74JOU2z" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/zbpe-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=zbpe399&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_mobile-zbpe399_2.png?qZnBcIGCJvPR8A2IAZOZuOxdoGZDatiI" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_mobile-zbpe399_2.png?qZnBcIGCJvPR8A2IAZOZuOxdoGZDatiI" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
