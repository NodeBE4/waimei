font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin;">免得总是触景伤情。</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12.0pt;"><o:p></o:p></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin;">我说，不！</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12.0pt;"><o:p></o:p></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin;">因为，</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12.0pt;"><o:p></o:p></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin;">这里，曾经是我、我的妻子、我的儿子，还有我家的帅帅共同的家。</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12.0pt;"><o:p></o:p></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin;">我怕，我离开这里，</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12.0pt;"><o:p></o:p></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin;">儿子回来，会找不到家；</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12.0pt;"><o:p></o:p></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin;">于是，我就在大年二十九的这一天，提前在自己家过除夕了。</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12.0pt;"><o:p></o:p></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin;">吃过中午饭后，我就开始做过除夕的准备了。</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12.0pt;"><o:p></o:p></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin;">鱼，是我妻子生前买的小黄鱼，这是她喜欢吃的，我提前用油煎好了一碟。牛肉，我妻子不吃，但我儿子喜欢吃的，我前几天买来卤好的，现在把它切成大拇指大小的丁，再配上洋葱、辣椒。把朋友送来的鸡，洗干净，加入黄酒、葱、姜、蒜一起放入炖锅炖。又到小区边的超市，买了小白菜，这也是我妻子喜欢的。</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12.0pt;"><o:p></o:p></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体;
