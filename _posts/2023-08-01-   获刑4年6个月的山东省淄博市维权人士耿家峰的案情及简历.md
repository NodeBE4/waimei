<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">1</span>日，本网获悉获刑<span lang="EN-US">4</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月的山东省淄博市维权人士耿家峰的案情及简历，现通报如下：<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">耿家峰：<span lang="EN-US">1973</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">26</span>日出生，山东省淄博市桓台县马桥镇辛庄村村民，维权访民，实名举报人，中国在押维权人士。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">自<span lang="EN-US">2010</span>年起，就开始因所在村落官员严重贪腐问题而实名举报维权，随被山东省桓台县当局视为需要打压的异己和维稳对象；<span lang="EN-US">2010</span>年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">26</span>日、<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">23</span>日、<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">16</span>日，曾因先后三次上访投诉，被恒台县警方以“非正常上访”为由给予严厉警告；<span lang="EN-US">2010</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">4</span>日、<span lang="EN-US">2016</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">24</span>日，曾两次被恒台县警方以“非正常上访，扰乱公共场所秩序”为由处行政拘留<span lang="EN-US">10</span>天；<span lang="EN-US">2016</span>年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">30</span>日、<span lang="EN-US">2017</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">16</span>日，又被以警方“故意损害财物”、“扬言实施放火，非法携带管制刀具”为由分别处行政拘留<span lang="EN-US">15</span>天和<span lang="EN-US">10</span>天；<span lang="EN-US">2016</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">18</span>日，曾因所在辛庄村村主任郭世勋从金城石化公司往辛庄村的西北角运输化工废渣，污染土地，而向山东省各级环保部门举报，无果后，又于次日欲往北京环保部举报，随被县政府派人拦截并遭人毒打住院；过后其虽多次继续举报违法排污的村主任郭世勋，并要求警方尽快查处打人事件和为其被殴打导致常年半头痛后遗症做鉴定，但都被拒绝；<span lang="EN-US">2017</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">4</span>日，其再次被恒台县警方抓捕刑拘；同年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">22</span>日，被淄博市桓台县法院以“寻衅滋事罪”判处有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">1</span>年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>个月，并被要求退赔相关经济损失<span lang="EN-US">2803</span>元；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">出狱后，<span lang="EN-US">2019</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">25</span>日，因桓台县田庄镇文庄村的维权人士张岗遭抓捕，张岗之妹向其求助，其为营救张岗，遂不惧险境，多次向各级主管部门及新闻媒体举报、控诉恒台县公安局非法拘禁人，从而引社会热议；<span lang="EN-US">2019</span>年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">8</span>日、<span lang="EN-US">9</span>日，因维权者张岗已被批捕，其受张岗妹妹之托，立刻在微信群频繁发“紧急关注”，以呼吁更多正义人士关注张岗被捕事件；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2020</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月，再次为声援张岗被抓事件而在新浪微博网站发文，揭露张岗所在田庄镇文庄村村委主任组织黑社会，贪污受贿、公账私存、挪用公款等渎职行为，因此文被人关注、转发近千次，遂立遭恒台县当局强力打压；<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">22</span>日，被恒台县警方以涉嫌“寻衅滋事罪”等再次刑拘，随后又被恒台县检察院很快以多项罪由正式批捕，并关押于桓台县看守所；同年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">29</span>日，被淄博市恒台县法院一审以“寻衅滋事罪”（<span lang="EN-US">2</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月）、“妨碍公务罪”（<span lang="EN-US">1</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>个月）、“敲诈勒索罪”（<span lang="EN-US">1</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>个月）三罪，数罪并罚决定执行<span lang="EN-US">4</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月，并处罚金人民币<span lang="EN-US">1</span>万元；其不服上诉；<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">21</span>日，被山东省淄博市中级法院二审裁决：驳回上诉，维持原判，刑期至<span lang="EN-US"> 2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">21</span>日。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体;
