<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">杨明玉是<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">21</span>日被山东省临清市警方以涉嫌“寻衅滋事罪”刑事拘留，后音信全无，<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月获悉，杨明玉又获刑<span lang="EN-US">8</span>年。此前杨明玉于<span lang="EN-US">2013</span>年曾被抓捕，被判刑<span lang="EN-US">3</span>年，<span lang="EN-US">2016</span>年才刑满释放。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: 宋体;
