[因言获罪被判刑1年的北京自由作家、网络写手李贵宝（网名“肥猪满圈”）刑满释放](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2021/12/1_21.html)
------

<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">21</span>日，本网获悉<span lang="EN-US">:</span></span><span lang="EN-US"> </span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">因言获罪被判刑<span lang="EN-US">1</span>年的北京自由作家、网络写手李贵宝（网名“肥猪满圈”）日前刑满释放。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">李贵宝</span></b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">：<span lang="EN-US">1971</span>年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">10</span>日出生，北京市海淀区人，网名“肥猪满圈”、“肥猪满圈老李”，北京自由派作家、编剧，网络写手，中国曾押政治犯。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2020</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">9</span>日，曾因在网上发文，并用调侃语境撰写了一篇涉及武汉疫情的<span lang="EN-US">7000</span>字文章，被网警监控知晓，遂立遭北京当局严厉打压；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">同年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">11</span>日，被北京市海淀区警方莫名传唤，做多次笔录；次日，在被警方带去北京<span lang="EN-US">309</span>医院做体检、办案中心做核酸检测之后，旋即被送至海淀区看守所关押；后又因其体温过高，看守所拒收，<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">13</span>日，遂被暂时取保候审；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">之后，其案虽被警方提交至北京市海淀区检察院，但仍被继续取保；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">取保期间，在做两次补充侦查后，被检海淀区察院以“寻衅滋事”为由罪正式批捕，控其因发文章诉疫情，其言论触犯了当局相关法令，属于“寻衅滋事”，因而被捕；后其案被检察院移交至海淀区法院（此间仍被继续取保）；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2020</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">3</span>日，其案被海淀区法院开庭受理，至同年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">21</span>日，终被北京市海淀区法院以“寻衅滋事罪”判处有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">1</span>年，并于同年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">24</span>日被正式收押；<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">21</span>日，刑满释放。</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">此前在河北省承德监狱服刑。</span></p><p class="MsoNormal"></p><div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;"><a href="https://blogger.googleusercontent.com/img/a/AVvXsEg-3ik5AmTXfKtlDQqpMTs5jMVfTzHZ25IZO9V_MuZ_X3d7-M_6ovL3iqqdMTHcFjiF-cN9YeznMMd1ICUF_srz0TD8lCh9l36rZwqLFQeOYru01vopczeJT-eKdoMjo2t517ClS2o6DITwhJ6CioCIW-SnBLXdKelyM8H1dS2eGU85t5-X0JwhKyoppw=s200" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em;
