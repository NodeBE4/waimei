<div>
<p><span></span></p><a name='more'></a><p class="MsoNormal"><br /></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2025</span>年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">19</span>日，本网获悉获刑<span lang="EN-US">3</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月的山东省东营市法轮功学员张爱泉的案情及简历，现在通报如下：<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">张爱泉：</span></b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">山东省东营市法轮功学员，男，原在胜利油田滨海公安局交通管理中心工作，<span lang="EN-US">1999</span>年时已经是三级警督，他是山东省武术协会知名人士。<span lang="EN-US">1995</span>年，张爱泉开始修炼法轮功。他因坚持修炼法轮功，曾被判刑八年、劳教三年，共遭<span lang="EN-US">11</span>年冤狱迫害。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">2023</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">29</span>日，东营市公安局及胜利油田滨海公安局警察抓捕了多名法轮功学员，张爱泉在住所被警察抓捕，一直被关押在滨海看守所。后遭刑事拘留、批捕、起诉。<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月张爱泉被东营市东营区法院判刑<span lang="EN-US">3</span>年半。于<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月被转监到山东省监狱第十一监区关押迫害。刑期至：<span lang="EN-US">2027</span>年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">28</span>日。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><br /><p></p>
