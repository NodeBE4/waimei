<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">27</span>日，本网获悉曾被判无期徒刑的甘南州拉卜楞寺僧人次诚嘉措的案情及简介，现通报如下：<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">次诚嘉措</span></b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">：<span lang="EN-US">1972</span>年出生，官方身份证名“次成加措”，甘肃省甘南藏族自治州夏河县九甲乡人，甘南州夏河县拉卜楞寺僧人，藏族人权捍卫者，中国良心犯。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">自幼即进入拉卜楞寺为僧；<span lang="EN-US">2008</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">15</span>日，因对中共当局高压严管制度不满，甘肃省甘南藏族自治州发生了大规模僧俗藏人抗议游行，同年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">14</span>日—<span lang="EN-US">15</span>日，其亦在拉卜楞寺示威，呼喊“祈请达赖喇嘛尊者返回西藏”等口号；遂被中共甘肃当局视为带头抗议者欲被抓捕；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">为躲避被抓，其被迫逃离寺庙，但仍于一周之后的<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">22</span>日，被甘南藏族自治州警方在舟曲县抓捕、刑拘，并送甘肃省安全厅看守所关押；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2009</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">21</span>日，被甘南州中级法院一审以“分裂国家罪”秘密判处无期徒刑；其不服上诉，后经甘南州中级法院二审裁决，依然维持原判；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">据悉，其羁押期间，曾遭刑讯逼供，且其代理辩护律师私聘权、亲属探视权及庭审旁听权等均遭剥夺；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">另据悉，服刑至<span lang="EN-US">2014</span>年时，其因狱中“能认罪悔改，表现较好”而获减刑，使刑期更改为<span lang="EN-US">19</span>年，剥夺政治权利<span lang="EN-US">7</span>年，刑期至<span lang="EN-US">2033</span>年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">21</span>日；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2020</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月，又经甘肃省天水市中级法院批准，被二次减刑<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月；<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月获悉，其可能将于<span lang="EN-US">2026</span>年刑满。目前疑似在甘肃省天水监狱（甘肃省天水市秦城区建设路<span lang="EN-US">196</span>号 ，邮政编码：<span lang="EN-US">741000</span>）服刑。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"></p><div class="separator" style="clear: both;
