[上海著名人权捍卫者季孝龙案将于2023年6月21日在浦东新区法院开庭审理](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2023/06/2023621.html)

<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">18</span>日，本网获悉：上海著名人权捍卫者季孝龙案的辩护人已经接到电话开庭通知，季孝龙寻衅滋事案件将于<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">21</span>日<span lang="EN-US">9</span>时<span lang="EN-US">30</span>分在浦东新区法院第<span lang="EN-US">9</span>法庭开庭审理。</span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">季孝龙于<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">31</span>日被刑事拘留，<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">23</span>日被上海浦东新区检察院以涉嫌寻衅滋事罪批准逮捕。这次被抓捕距离他上次出狱才<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个多月。后被起诉。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">本次遭逮捕起诉就因为季孝龙反对上海封城，践行宪法赋予的言论自由权，为全体上海市民发声，导致其第二次被中共当局逮捕，本网呼吁全球网友和国际组织关注季孝龙，强烈要求中共上海当局立即释放季孝龙。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">1976</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月，曾因宣传民主理念而被上海市浦东新区警方以“寻衅滋事”罪由传唤，并被处以行政拘留<span lang="EN-US">10</span>日；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2018</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast;
