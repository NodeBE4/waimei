[西安“不倒翁小姐姐”传递全运会火炬被指“不配”](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210914-1193413)
------

<div>
<p>中国第十四届全国运动会和残特奥会火炬前天在陕西西安传递。其中西安大唐不夜城爆红的“不倒翁小姐姐”冯佳晨担任第八棒火炬手的消息格外引人关注，一度冲上了热搜。</p><p>冯佳晨也在个人微博上记录下传递火炬的瞬间，并希望大家多多关注全运会。评论区却出现了对冯佳晨的质疑：“她不是网红吗”，“选火炬手的标准是什么？”</p><p>但这番言论也引来不少网友反驳，称不倒翁小姐姐为西安旅游业作出了巨大贡献，这是她应该的。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>据陕西都市快报对火炬手的介绍中称，冯佳晨“作为‘陕西文旅推荐使者’，积极参与公益表演，助力西安文旅产业发展。”</p><p>冯佳晨也在近日发布的2021“西安好人榜”中被评为“敬业奉献好人”。推荐词中提到，96年出生的她“总能胜任各项演出任务，被亲切地称为‘大唐不夜城的一块万能砖’”。</p><p>此前，“不倒翁小姐姐”作为西安大唐不夜城的金字招牌吸引了无数的游客，她的表演视频也在网上走红。</p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/zbpe-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=zbpe399&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_desktop-zbpe399_2.png?dHEiGouxxZpWwWTJE04dyGyUN74JOU2z" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_desktop-zbpe399_2.png?dHEiGouxxZpWwWTJE04dyGyUN74JOU2z" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/zbpe-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=zbpe399&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_mobile-zbpe399_2.png?qZnBcIGCJvPR8A2IAZOZuOxdoGZDatiI" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_mobile-zbpe399_2.png?qZnBcIGCJvPR8A2IAZOZuOxdoGZDatiI" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
