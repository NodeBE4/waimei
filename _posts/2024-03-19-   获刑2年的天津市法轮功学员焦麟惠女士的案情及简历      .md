<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">19</span>日，本网获悉获刑<span lang="EN-US">2</span>年的天津市法轮功学员焦麟惠女士的案情及简历，现在通报如下：<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;"><b>焦麟惠：</b>天津市法轮功学员，女，<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年已年过七旬，系天津市大港区人，一九九九年三月她开始修炼法轮功。焦麟惠因坚持修炼法轮大法，曾两次被中共当局劳教，在天津板桥女子劳教所遭到酷刑虐待。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">2023</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">9</span>日，焦麟惠在现住地山东省烟台市下辖的龙口市的家中，被龙口市被龙口市公安局东江派出所警察撬门入室抓捕，关押在烟台看守所。<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">26</span>日，焦麟惠被构陷到龙口市检察院，<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">21</span>日被起诉到龙口市法院。<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">12</span>日，龙口市法院下达判决书，焦麟惠被判刑<span lang="EN-US">2</span>年。刑期至：<span lang="EN-US">2025</span>年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">8</span>日。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p>
