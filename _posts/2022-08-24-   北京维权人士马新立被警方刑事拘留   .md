[北京维权人士马新立被警方刑事拘留](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2022/08/blog-post_97.html)
------

<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">24</span>日，本网获悉：北京维权人士马新立于<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">18</span>日被北京警方以涉嫌寻衅滋事罪刑事拘留，目前羁押在北京西城区看守所。目前具体原因不详。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">马新立简历：</span></b><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">1966</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">1</span>日出生，北京市人，原北京市公交公司职员，北京要求官员财产公示的十公民之一，新公民运动参与者，民主维权公民，中国在押政治犯。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">曾因自身发明创造及知识产权得不到保障，与其他发明者共同维权，向来京的香港人大代表递交请求修改中国专利法建议书，而被北京警方处以行政拘留；获释后，积极参与要求官员公布财产、上街打横幅反腐败等公民活动，<span lang="EN-US">2013</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">31</span>日，因其与维权公民袁冬、 张宝成和侯欣在北京市西单广场打横幅，要求官员财产公示及公开中共中央政治局七常委财产和亲属国籍等信息，随被北京警方以涉嫌“非法集会罪”（后改为涉嫌“聚众扰乱公共场所秩序罪”）刑拘，且被关押在北京市第三看守所长达<span lang="EN-US">11</span>个月之久，<span lang="EN-US">2014</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月，被取保候审；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2014</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">10</span>日，因其声援人权捍卫者曹顺利、要求官员公开财产、要求当局释放许志永、丁家喜和所有良心犯，并在北京玉渊潭公园冰冷湖水中举牌，以示抗议，于<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">14</span>日又被警方抓捕收押；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">出狱后，<span lang="EN-US">2014</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">25</span>日，因前往北京市朝阳区惠新西街与<span lang="EN-US">6</span>名维权公民聚餐，声援香港占中、真普选，被当地警方野蛮抓捕，且又被以涉嫌“寻衅滋事罪”刑拘，后被取保释放；<span lang="EN-US">2018</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">30</span>日，因其在网转上发<span lang="EN-US">5.1</span>游行示威帖子，再被北京市西城区警方以涉嫌“寻衅滋事罪”刑拘；<span lang="EN-US">2018</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">6</span>日，被准予取保释放。此前被羁押于北京市西城区看守所。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体;
