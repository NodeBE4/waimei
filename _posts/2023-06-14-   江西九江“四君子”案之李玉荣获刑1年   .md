<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">14</span>日，本网获悉：<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">7</span>日遭中共当局抓捕的江西九江公民李玉荣<span lang="EN-US">,</span>日前获刑<span lang="EN-US">1</span>年，刑期至：<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">7</span>日。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">李玉荣：江西省九江市人，九江市濂溪区公民，微信公众号“仁即博爱”持有者，民主异见人士，中国在押政治犯。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">因常在其微信公众号“仁即博爱”发时政性贴文及视频讯息，<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">7</span>日，遂被江西省九江市浔阳区国保警方跨区带走；带走时，先说是口头传唤，但之后即转为以涉嫌“寻衅滋事罪”刑事拘留；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">同年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">16</span>日，其女儿在微信朋友圈发出“告亲友书”，通报其父李玉荣已于<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">8</span>日被浔阳区公安局国保跨区抓捕刑拘，但尚未收到拘留通知书，并表示全家人以及其他亲属都十分担心，不知其是否安康；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">随后，有网友查询其公众号，发现其公众号“仁即博爱”的文章已经被删除，故其本次被抓原因应与中共二十大召开，其网络言论令当局不安有关；今获悉其因言获刑<span lang="EN-US">1</span>年，刑期至<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">7</span>日。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">目前被羁押于九江市看守所（江西省九江市庐山区八里村，邮政编码：<span lang="EN-US">332000</span>）。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p>
