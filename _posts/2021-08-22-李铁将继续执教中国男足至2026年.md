[李铁将继续执教中国男足至2026年](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210822-1185290)
------

<div>
<p>中国男足将与主教练李铁续约，后者将执教到2026年。</p><p>据中国央视新闻报道，今天（22日）上午，中国足协在上海举行有关中国男足备战、竞争卡塔尔世预赛亚洲区12强赛的媒体沟通会。<br>　　<br>中国足协称，足协已与李铁就其进一步执教中国队至2026年签订了工作合同，从而确保中国男足国家队的长期稳定发展。</p><p>李铁2020年1月正式出任中国队主帅，率领中国男足在世预赛亚洲区40强赛中突围，闯入12强赛。李铁率队分别7-0击败关岛，2-0击败菲律宾，5-0击败马尔代夫，末轮3-1击败叙利亚。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>接下来李铁将带领中国男足征战12强赛，冲击卡塔尔世界杯入场券。中国足协透露12强赛目标是“全力拼搏，力争出线”。</p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
