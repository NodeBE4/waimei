[港公务员疫苗接种率近六成半](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210724-1173012)
------

<div>
<p>香港公务员事务局局长聂德权今天在电台节目上表示，当局早前要求6万多名前线政府雇员接种疫苗或定期检测，目前有关接种率超过七成，至于整体公务员接种率接近六成半。</p><p>据香港电台报道，被问到当局会否提出全体公务员都要接种疫苗或定期检测，他指出，公务员队伍需要维持公共服务和保护市民，有需要接种疫苗或定期检测，现时正分开不同阶段处理，部门同事亦知悉有关安排。</p><p>聂德权指出，公务员队伍人员不论任何岗位，都要支持特区政府施政，每个部门也要从各自的工作范畴，防范危害国家安全的情况，认为不同单位都需要有相关意识，以真正达致保障国家安全。他并称，每名公务员都有相关责任，其中高级公务员需要负上的责任为更大。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
