<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">13</span>日，本网获悉获刑<span lang="EN-US">17</span>年，西藏日喀则登卓寺寺院法师诺布•顿珠的案情及简历，现通报如下：<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">诺布•顿珠：</span></b><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">1956</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年出生，藏族名<span lang="EN-US">Norbu Dondrup</span>，西藏日喀则市定日县人，日喀则市定日县协格尔镇查那村当卓寺（藏民称之为登卓寺（<span lang="EN-US">Tengdro</span>））寺院法师，公益慈善捐助人，藏族异见人士，中国在押良心犯。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2019</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">-9</span>月，曾因当卓寺僧侣曲嘉旺波（<span lang="EN-US">Choegyal Wangpo</span>）到西藏首府拉萨市做佛事访问时不甚将手机遗失在某咖啡馆，后被老板交予拉萨市警方处置；但因警方在查阅其手机时，发现手机里存有其及当卓寺僧侣与尼泊尔同乡藏僧有过密联系的信息记录，尤其是存有<span lang="EN-US">2015</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月尼泊尔大地震时，他们向那里的藏民和寺院捐款赈灾等记录，以及还存有和第<span lang="EN-US">14</span>世达赖喇嘛有关的图片及文献等，遂立即触碰到中共西藏当局的政治敏感神经，认定这是一起与境外敌对势力勾结，利用网络通讯对国家安全构成威胁的典型案件；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">同年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月，诺布•顿珠所在寺庙突然遭到日喀则市当局派出的多名武警及公安警察袭击，所有僧人均被强迫接受政治洗脑教育数天，多人遭殴打，且其被殴打成重伤；之后有<span lang="EN-US">20</span>名僧侣（含其在内）被刑拘，当卓寺及其附近的网络被切断；三天后，除一名僧侣因向当局抗议而自杀，其余<span lang="EN-US">19</span>名僧人仍被超押，数月不放；后其与曲嘉旺波等<span lang="EN-US">4</span>名藏僧均被日喀则市检察院以涉嫌“跟境外藏人联系、赈济尼泊尔地震，或拥有和达赖喇嘛有关照片或文献”为罪由正式批捕，自此失联；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2020</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">6</span>日获悉，其等<span lang="EN-US">4</span>人案统一在日喀则中级法院开庭审理，最终被以“危害国家安全罪” 重判<span lang="EN-US">20</span>年<span lang="EN-US">-5</span>年不等刑期，其中，诺布•顿珠被判处有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">17</span>年，刑期至<span lang="EN-US">2036</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月。目前疑似在拉萨市札基监狱服刑。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"></p><div class="separator" style="clear: both;
