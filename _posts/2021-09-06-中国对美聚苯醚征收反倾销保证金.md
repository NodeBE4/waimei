[中国对美聚苯醚征收反倾销保证金](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210906-1190979)
------

<div>
<p>中国商务部宣布，原产于美国的进口聚苯醚存在倾销，并将从明天（7日）起对美国聚苯醚征收反倾销保证金。</p><p>商务部在官网上指出，他们在去年8月3日发布公告，决定对原产于美国的进口聚苯醚进行反倾销立案调查，初步认定美国聚苯醚存在倾销，中国国内聚苯醚产业受到实质损害，而且倾销与实质损害之间存在因果关系。</p><p>在保证金比率方面，商务部将对沙特基础工业创新塑料美国有限公司征收18.3%的保证金，其他美国公司为48.6%。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>聚苯醚是一种高性能热塑性树脂及其组合物，广泛应用在光伏、汽车、通信、电子电器、水处理、医疗器械等领域。</p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/zbpe-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=zbpe399&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_desktop-zbpe399_2.png?dHEiGouxxZpWwWTJE04dyGyUN74JOU2z" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_desktop-zbpe399_2.png?dHEiGouxxZpWwWTJE04dyGyUN74JOU2z" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/zbpe-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=zbpe399&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_mobile-zbpe399_2.png?qZnBcIGCJvPR8A2IAZOZuOxdoGZDatiI" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_mobile-zbpe399_2.png?qZnBcIGCJvPR8A2IAZOZuOxdoGZDatiI" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
