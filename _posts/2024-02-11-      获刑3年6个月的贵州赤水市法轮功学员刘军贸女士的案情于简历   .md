<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">11</span>日，本网获悉获刑<span lang="EN-US">3</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月的贵州赤水市法轮功学员刘军贸女士的案情于简历，现在通报如下：<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">刘军贸：</span></b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">贵州赤水市法轮功学员，女，<span lang="EN-US">1966</span>年生，贵州省赤水市第四小学教师。<b><span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></b></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">她坚持修炼法轮大法“真、善、忍”，<span lang="EN-US">2001</span>年用粉笔在电杆上写“法轮大法好”，于<span lang="EN-US">2001</span>年被赤水市法院判刑<span lang="EN-US">3</span>年，在贵州省羊艾劳改农场遭受迫害。刘军贸<span lang="EN-US">2012</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月再次被绑架，被秘密判刑<span lang="EN-US">10</span>年，<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">12</span>日从贵州省第一女子监狱（羊艾监狱）回家，女儿害怕到不敢认母亲。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">2023</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">9</span>日，法轮功学员刘军贸、汪泽宣去吃席，和人宣讲法轮功，被人举报，随后被赤水市派出所抓捕。随即两人被刑事拘留、批捕、起诉。<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月底，<a name="_Hlk158542565">刘军贸</a>被遵义市法院判刑<span lang="EN-US">3</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月，汪泽宣被判刑<span lang="EN-US">3</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>个月。刘军贸刑期至：<span lang="EN-US">2027</span>年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">8</span>日。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p>
