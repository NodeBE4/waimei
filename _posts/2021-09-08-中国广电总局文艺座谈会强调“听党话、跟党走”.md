[中国广电总局文艺座谈会强调“听党话、跟党走”](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210908-1191532)
------

<div>
<p>中国国家广电总局昨天（7日）在北京召开广播电视和网络视听文艺工作者座谈会。会议以“爱党爱国、崇德尚艺”为主题，要求进一步加强广播电视和网络视听文艺节目及人员管理，推动广播电视和网络视听文艺持续健康发展。</p><p>据中国广电总局网站报道，会议强调，广播电视和网络视听文艺工作者要明大德、立大德，厚植爱党爱国情怀。要始终把爱党爱国作为本分、作为职责，作为心之所系、情之所归，作为从艺的第一位要求。</p><p>会议强调，文艺工作者要增强“四个意识”，坚定“四个自信”，做到“两个维护”，坚定不移听党话、跟党走。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>会议指出，文艺工作者要培育和践行社会主义核心价值观，讲品位、讲格调、讲责任，自觉摈弃低俗、庸俗、媚俗的低级趣味，自觉反对拜金主义、享乐主义、极端个人主义的腐朽思想。</p><p>会议强调，文艺工作者要清楚“我是谁、为了谁、依靠谁”，强化人民立场，走好群众路线，在为人民立德立言中成就自我、实现价值。</p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/zbpe-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=zbpe399&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_desktop-zbpe399_2.png?dHEiGouxxZpWwWTJE04dyGyUN74JOU2z" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_desktop-zbpe399_2.png?dHEiGouxxZpWwWTJE04dyGyUN74JOU2z" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/zbpe-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=zbpe399&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_mobile-zbpe399_2.png?qZnBcIGCJvPR8A2IAZOZuOxdoGZDatiI" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_mobile-zbpe399_2.png?qZnBcIGCJvPR8A2IAZOZuOxdoGZDatiI" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
