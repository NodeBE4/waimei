[美参议院通过助台重获世卫观察员身份法案](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210806-1178851)
------

<div>
<p>美国参议院在当地时间今天（6日）凌晨通过了协助台湾重获世界卫生组织观察员身份的法案。</p><p>参议院官网资料显示，这项由参议员梅嫩德斯（Bob Menendez）推出的法案，获得参议院一致通过，下来将交给众议院审议。</p><p>法案将要求美国国务卿制定恢复台湾世卫观察员身份的策略，也要求政府在台湾未能在世界卫生大会期间得到观察员身份后，说明美国在协助台湾获得观察员身份的计划上做出哪些改变或改进。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>美国众议院外交委员会今年3月通过了内容几乎完全相同的众院版法案。</p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
