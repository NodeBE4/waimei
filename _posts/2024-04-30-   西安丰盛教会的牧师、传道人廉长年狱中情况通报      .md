[西安丰盛教会的牧师、传道人廉长年狱中情况通报](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2024/04/blog-post_65.html)
------

<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">30</span>日，本网获悉遭羁押的西安丰盛教会牧师年已<span lang="EN-US">70</span>的廉长年先生狱中情况，现在通报如下：<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">2024</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">28</span>日，代理律师会见了西安丰盛教会廉长年牧师。<span lang="EN-US">70</span>岁的廉牧师头发全白，满脸皱纹，精神萎靡并高度紧张，神情异常，行为异常，说话声音很低，无法清晰表达，但说<span lang="EN-US">“</span>这（为信仰坐牢）是求也求不来的<span lang="EN-US">”</span>，会见期间多次突然站起来，看起来非常恐惧。其在指定居所监视居住期间遭严重虐待，一张姓警察曾当其面殴打他儿子廉旭亮牧师。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">西安丰盛教会教案牧师、传道人廉长年、廉旭亮、付娟，自<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">17</span>日被控<span lang="EN-US">“</span>诈骗罪<span lang="EN-US">”</span>被羁押已超过<span lang="EN-US">1</span>年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>个月，今年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月底他们的案子已进入法院，起诉金额为<span lang="EN-US">39</span>万元，三人被检方均求刑<span lang="EN-US">5</span>年。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">西安丰盛教会自成立三十年以来，严格遵行《圣经》教导，未曾有人以被牧者诈辦为由报案，此教案纯属冤枉陷害。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">廉长年简介<span lang="EN-US">:</span>河南省人，基督徒，原河南<span lang="EN-US">“</span>中华福音团契<span lang="EN-US">”</span>（中国五大家庭教会之一）老一辈牧者，西安丰盛基督教教会创建人，主任牧师。上世纪<span lang="EN-US">90</span>年代廉长年牧师在西安市建立了丰盛基督教教会；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">但自<span lang="EN-US">2018</span>年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月中共新《宗教事务条例》正式生效实施起，全国各地基督教家庭教会纷纷遭遇空前严打和迫害，其所建西安丰盛教会亦遭严打和冲击；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">2022</span><span style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">年，自<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">20</span>日始，其教会信徒突遭西安市灞桥区十里铺派出所警方大规模口头传唤和上门问讯，甚至有些人家被多次搜查，旨在胁迫信徒们作证，控告牧师诈骗和传播邪教，甚至要求信徒捏造有关教会奉献的虚假信息；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">2022</span><span style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">17</span>日，其及妻子、儿子、儿媳以及教会同工等<span lang="EN-US">6</span>人被当地派出所警方强行带走并被抄家，次日，其父子二人和教会财务同工付娟等人被西安市灞桥区警方以涉嫌<span lang="EN-US">“</span>诈骗罪<span lang="EN-US">”</span>指定居所监视居住；后遭刑事拘留、逮捕。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">2022</span><span style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">19</span>日，其教会被西安市民政局迅速发布通告取缔，理由是其与<span lang="EN-US">“</span>中华福音总会<span lang="EN-US">”</span>、<span lang="EN-US">“</span>中华福音总会<span lang="EN-US">”</span>西安区联会，<span lang="EN-US">“</span>未经登记<span lang="EN-US">”</span>就擅自以社会团体名义开展活动，故被认定为非法社会组织；其案虽已有多名维权律师介入，但其等<span lang="EN-US">3</span>人曾被长期秘押，警方一直以当事人案情涉及危害国家安全为由拒绝律师会见。现案件已经到法院，检察院以诈骗罪对其求刑<span lang="EN-US">5</span>年。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">本网将持续关注西安丰盛教会教案的后续情况。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"></p><div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;"><a href="https://blogger.googleusercontent.com/img/b/R29vZ2xl/AVvXsEjOm54ydVetZBDLPTBKNY3kOS-RFfRbnKgJlZcpJ8ARfxAf_zPfXVjsa1bQQbadRlI0o7CE3YQWWeKzNov8mZsgCA0EOZaLkQIACpJGZIEkKJd5PhtCohIT8AsYmTZwtHGLCcN6DBe3gguykBOxVHAogqmwGbjsqWswbv4sGROlzw9hMvCq_wImPKloDr7z/s1280/GMVoInqbAAAHwPJ.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em;
