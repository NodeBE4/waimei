<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">唐之开：</span></b><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">1951</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年出生，四川省达州市大竹县人，重庆市常住居民，法轮功信仰者，中国在押良心犯。<b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></b></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">因笃信法轮功而屡遭中共四川省及重庆市两地当局长期逼迫与监控打压；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2019</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">27</span>日，因<span lang="EN-US">25</span>名法轮功信仰者同聚四川省达州市大竹县竹阳镇一居民家里阅读法轮功书籍，被人跟踪汇报，随四川省当局立即出动达州市各级警力（含达州市、大竹县、大竹县竹阳西区派出所和北区派出所警方）及“<span lang="EN-US">610</span>”办公室成员等多人，以敲门换锁等野蛮非法方式将其悉数抓捕，其亦被视为同案嫌疑人于同年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">28</span>日，被户籍地四川省大竹县的警方以涉嫌“邪教罪”拘捕；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">同天，其租住处被查抄，当场搜走其个人现金和打印机、播放器等物品多件，后又将其另两处房产也一并非法查抄；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">之后，虽有其中<span lang="EN-US">20</span>人先后被警方取保释放，但其及<span lang="EN-US">5</span>名法轮功信仰者尚仍被达州市大竹县警方刑拘不放；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">后其等均被达州市大竹县检察院以涉嫌邪教罪予以批捕，且羁关押于达州市大竹县看守所；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2020</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">5</span>日，其一行<span lang="EN-US">6</span>人涉邪教案被达州市大竹县法院以远程视频方式统一开庭审理；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">同年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">29</span>日，其<span lang="EN-US">6</span>人终被该法院一审以“组织和利用邪教组织破坏法律实施罪”共同宣判，其中，其被重判有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">5</span>年，并处罚金人民币<span lang="EN-US">3</span>万元，刑期至<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">27</span>日；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">其余<span lang="EN-US">5</span>人亦被分别枉法判处<span lang="EN-US">7</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月—<span lang="EN-US">1</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月不等刑期。目前疑似在四川省成都龙泉驿监狱服刑。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p>
