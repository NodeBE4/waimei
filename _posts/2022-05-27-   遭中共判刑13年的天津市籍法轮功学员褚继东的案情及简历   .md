<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">褚继东：</span></b><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">1975</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年出生，又名楚继东，天津市籍人，天津市滨海新区海滨街西苑小区公民，原中国石油天然气管道局第六工程公司（原天津市大港油田油建公司）职工，自由信仰追求者，法轮功修炼者，中国在押良心犯。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2001</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月，本应劳教期满获释，但因其坚持信仰，拒不认罪，故而又被天津市大港区“<span lang="EN-US">610</span>”（法轮功专项管理办公室）接续劳教<span lang="EN-US">3</span>年，并送大港区板桥劳教所关押；关押期间，曾惨遭“法制教育学习班”（实为洗脑班）暴力“转化”（实为酷刑虐待，如“熬鹰”，即昼夜不让睡觉；肉体折磨，即用拳头打腮帮和软肋、用手掌砍咽喉、用脚踢关节、用铁锤敲腿筋、用打火机深度烧肋骨等；严刑逼供，即用电棍击打脖子、腋下和阴部等）；后又经几次被加期劳教（共计<span lang="EN-US">5</span>年），终得获释返家和准予回单位继续工作；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2013</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月，曾因被单位外派到甘肃省庆阳市某工程项目工作，期间为阻止窃贼到工地偷窃而被窃贼重伤头部，随申请返回天津家中修养；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2021</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年，其在宁夏回族自治区银川机场登机时，因机场警察在检查其手机时发现内存法轮功电子书，故而立即将其扣留；后又因在公安部系统内的网络上查到他曾经有过被劳教的经历，随被宁夏警方认定“有前科”，且强行对其以涉嫌“邪教罪”予以拘捕；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2022</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">3</span>日，在被超期关押一年多后，被宁夏回族自治区银川市法院一审以“利用邪教组织破坏法律实施罪”枉法重判有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">13</span>年，刑期至<span lang="EN-US">2034</span>年，其不服判决，应已上诉。目前被羁押于银川市看守所。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p>
