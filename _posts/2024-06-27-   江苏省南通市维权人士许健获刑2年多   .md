[江苏省南通市维权人士许健获刑2年多](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2024/06/2_27.html)
------

<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-bidi-font-family: &quot;Times New Roman&quot;;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">27</span>日，本网获悉：江苏省南通市维权人士许健日前被江苏南通市海门法院以寻衅滋事罪判刑<span lang="EN-US">2</span>年。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-bidi-font-family: &quot;Times New Roman&quot;;">2024</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-bidi-font-family: &quot;Times New Roman&quot;;">年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">5</span>日，海门许健被控寻衅滋事案在海门法院开庭。近日，有消息传出许健被海门区法院判处有期徒刑两年有余。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-bidi-font-family: &quot;Times New Roman&quot;;">2024</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-bidi-font-family: &quot;Times New Roman&quot;;">年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">5</span>日当天许健案开庭，辩护律师李永恒申请了<span lang="EN-US">20</span>个旁听席，但是由于当地公安部门的阻挠，只有些许人进入到了旁听席。当日，李永恒律师怒斥法院将法院的旁听权让渡给公安，并怒斥那些相互矛盾的陈述作为证据出示。李永恒律师希望法官和检察官能够用自己的初心和良知评判法律事实，做出一个公正的判决。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-bidi-font-family: &quot;Times New Roman&quot;;">许健：</span></b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-bidi-font-family: &quot;Times New Roman&quot;; mso-bidi-font-weight: bold;">江苏省南通市维权人士。其妻子江苏省南通市海门区海门街道海兴新村<span lang="EN-US">207</span>幢<span lang="EN-US">501</span>室的朱培娟，因<span lang="EN-US">2019</span>年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">6</span>日海门区复兴新村一起管道液化气爆炸事件中，导致一直由她赡养照顾的叔叔朱中云烧伤死亡，公安机关没有公正处理而开始维权。该起管道液化气爆炸事件，不仅造成了朱培娟叔叔死亡，及另外两人一人重伤一人轻伤的后果，朱云中的遗体至今还在殡仪馆。<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">25</span>日下午<span lang="EN-US">16</span>点<span lang="EN-US">30</span>分左右，朱培娟在河北省廊坊市的出租房睡觉，被南通海门公安局截访人员以“网上通缉犯”之名，骗取辖区廊坊文安县滩里派出所民警撬开房间锁，强行将正在休息的朱培娟绑架回南通海门的，然后以“涉嫌寻衅滋事罪”指定监视居住。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-bidi-font-family: &quot;Times New Roman&quot;; mso-bidi-font-weight: bold;">2024</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-bidi-font-family: &quot;Times New Roman&quot;; mso-bidi-font-weight: bold;">年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月初许健为此替妻子聘请律师到检察院申请立案监督，被海门区公安局治安拘留八天，结果<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">11</span>日到期当局没有放许健回家，后家中老人收到刑事拘留通知书，称许健涉嫌寻衅滋事犯罪。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-bidi-font-family: &quot;Times New Roman&quot;; mso-bidi-font-weight: bold;">许健在被羁押近半年后，其被控寻衅滋事案在海门区法院开庭审理。<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月被判刑<span lang="EN-US">2</span>年多。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"></p><div class="separator" style="clear: both;
