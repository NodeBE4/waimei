[陕西公安厅民警吴永强进京维权遭刑事拘留](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2025/02/blog-post_26.html)
------

<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2025</span>年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">26</span>日，本网获悉：陕西公安厅民警吴永强于<span lang="EN-US">2025</span>年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">16</span>日进京维权，于<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">26</span>日遭北京市公安局西城分局刑事拘留。先拘押在北京西城区看守所，后转交陕西渭南警方，目前羁押在陕西渭南市临渭区看守所。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">去年，陕西公安厅治安局民警吴永强，实名举报陕西纪委书记王兴宁，充当黑恶势力保护伞等问题。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">此前，吴永强因反腐遭陕西当局强权打击报复。<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">28</span>日被宣布给予开除党籍、撤职处分后，他向公安厅党委、陕西省委组织部申请申诉。期间，被公安厅人事处多次要求强行谈话，遭吴永强拒绝。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><b><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">2022</span></b><b><span style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">7</span>日吴永强发微博称：</span></b><span style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">“我是一直举报陕西贪腐的陕西省公安厅治安局民警，前共产党员吴永强。我遭陕西腐败强权打击报复，<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">28</span>日宣布给予我开除党籍、撤职处分，之后，我依规向公安厅党委、陕西省委组织部申请申诉。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">在我申诉期间，公安厅直属机关党委（直工处）违反程序找我谈话，被我拒绝，而公安厅人事处多次让治安局、治安二处做我工作，要和我谈话，企图逼迫让我认可对我打击报复的处分。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">2022</span><span style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">7</span>日<span lang="EN-US">15</span>时许，治安局、治安二处领导再次劝我，被我拒绝。可是，我刚回到办公室，公安厅人事处三人便到我办公室，企图强行和我谈话，被我强硬拒绝，我提出警告并将公安厅人事处三人赶出办公室。</span><span style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12pt;">我强烈要求依法查处厅人事处溢用职权的违法行为！</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">”</span></p><p class="MsoNormal"></p><div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;"><a href="https://blogger.googleusercontent.com/img/b/R29vZ2xl/AVvXsEh8JRRN-lCL9yr7bIG4rceJLh30h8aOHUYSu5iUGo0nQybHqubOUAf2WfjoK_VqBZCyObs3FYhP13fpipQuPK9AJOOaU9L3xRVuSnkvsuowCAlTQs5Pk0FAIGSeXm2bmyIjvtil7M70n98o088eLeZBhJnx3Y6NkBchUEO7NIrukI6tUwyA-uxUOU07VEYz/s1280/35-22010R255190-L.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em;
