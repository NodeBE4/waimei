<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">18</span>日，本网获悉：著名法律人郝劲松被涉嫌诽谤罪、寻衅滋事罪、诈骗罪一案，<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">18</span>日上午<span lang="EN-US">9:00</span>在山西省定襄县人民法院审判庭开庭。庭审仅在中午休庭两个半小时以后，于当晚<span lang="EN-US">20:00</span>庭审结束，法庭没有当庭宣判表示会择期宣判。 <span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">郝劲松是在看守所视频参与庭审，据仅仅被允许参加旁听的两个家属所见，郝劲松的精神状态很好，对法院指控的罪名不予认可，旁听人员有十多个人是由相关部门安排参与的“占坑旁听”，据说有参与旁听的公民被法警强行带出法庭不能旁听。 据悉，因为涉嫌诽谤罪一案是不公开审理的，已经于昨日开庭完毕。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"></p><div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;"><a href="https://blogger.googleusercontent.com/img/a/AVvXsEiNN5mjv_tfaE-FqVn_LxRXYKLB7TQRtE9nYJySKnVwePgeXiG9-f91Z2aJoqLD4yy1v-D9S1t0KT5j_JFEeP8X_IlqzaVobUlFMtD5qTIgRCPh69X9gCXVuitzgJUmLEKszCc1AxPv7g_9GwUIpla1dNR9Vywei_Pp8FUN3IaememoS2jra9VPAQjktg=s750" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;"><img border="0" data-original-height="362" data-original-width="750" height="193" src="https://blogger.googleusercontent.com/img/a/AVvXsEiNN5mjv_tfaE-FqVn_LxRXYKLB7TQRtE9nYJySKnVwePgeXiG9-f91Z2aJoqLD4yy1v-D9S1t0KT5j_JFEeP8X_IlqzaVobUlFMtD5qTIgRCPh69X9gCXVuitzgJUmLEKszCc1AxPv7g_9GwUIpla1dNR9Vywei_Pp8FUN3IaememoS2jra9VPAQjktg=w400-h193" width="400" /></a></div><p></p>
