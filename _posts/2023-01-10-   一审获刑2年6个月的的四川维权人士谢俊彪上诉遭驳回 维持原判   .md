<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p align="left" class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: left;"><span style="color: black; font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-themecolor: text1;">1981</span><span style="color: black; font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-themecolor: text1;">2013</span><span style="color: black; font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-themecolor: text1;">年—<span lang="EN-US">2015</span>年，曾因所在成都市双流区政府在未提供任何有效档，也未与当地村民达成拆迁补偿协议的前提下，便强征强拆、驱逐村民，令其及众村民个人权益严重受损，随在信访投诉无望情况下，走上了从成都到北京等地的维权抗争之路；<span lang="EN-US">2017</span>年，曾因多次在成都市和双流区的上访无果，而两次进京上访投诉，随数次遭双流区警方传唤及登门威胁警告；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p align="left" class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: left;"><span lang="EN-US" style="color: black; font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-themecolor: text1;">2018</span><span style="color: black; font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-themecolor: text1;">年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">10</span>日，曾因习近平等中共高层常委将到成都市双流区视察，而遭双流区公兴派出所警方莫名破门辱骂和殴打，事后，又因其将此不幸经历和猜测发于微信群，爆料“明天可能有大爷到双流视察”，随旋即被双流区警方以“寻衅滋事”罪由行政拘留<span lang="EN-US">10</span>天；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p align="left" class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: left;"><span lang="EN-US" style="color: black; font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-themecolor: text1;">年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">6</span>日，曾因在微信群中表达心声及民主要求，又被当局警告，称其“接受媒体采访，要重点稳控“；同年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">10</span>日，因其在四川省人大、政协两会召开之际，又到四川省人大外举牌，牌子上写：“戴表（代表）不为民作主 不如回家抓老鼠”，随当天即被当地警方刑事拘留，一个月后获取保候审；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p align="left" class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: left;"><span lang="EN-US" style="color: black; font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-themecolor: text1;">年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">7</span>日，在其面临取保候审一年期满之际，成都市双流区警方以“取保候审期间离开居住地“之名将其再度以涉嫌“寻衅滋事罪”刑拘，家被搜查；<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月，知其被成都市双流区检察院以同罪名予以批捕，后其案宗被移送到成都市双流区法院；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p align="left" class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: left;"><span lang="EN-US" style="color: black; font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-themecolor: text1;">2022</span><span style="color: black; font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-themecolor: text1;">年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">7</span>日，其案在双流区法院开庭审理，同年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月，终被该法院一审以“寻衅滋事罪”判处有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">2</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月，刑期至<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">6</span>日；其不服，表示上诉，并称只要二审维持原判或改判即使一天的罪，即仍坚决申诉；后上诉被驳回。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p align="left" class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: left;"><span style="color: black;
