[获刑6年6个月的四川民主人士姜野飞今刑满出狱](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2022/07/66.html)
------

<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">30</span>日，本网获悉：获刑<span lang="EN-US">6</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月，实际被囚禁<span lang="EN-US">6</span>年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>个月的的四川民主人士姜野飞今刑满出狱<span lang="EN-US">.<o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">姜野飞：</span></b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">四川成都人，生于<span lang="EN-US">1968</span>年，民主人士，人权捍卫者，参加过“八九”学运，<span lang="EN-US">2008</span>年“汶川地震”后又积极参与维权活动，<span lang="EN-US">2009</span>年“六四”前夕，姜野飞在曼谷中共国大使馆门前参与纪念“六四”二十周年静坐活动，被泰国当局抓捕，关押近三年之久。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2015</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">28</span>日与董广平逃亡泰国，二人同被泰国警方在其持有联合国难民署签发的“庇护申请者证明书”（实为保护信）的前提下又以涉嫌“偷渡罪”抓捕，并关押于泰国曼谷市沙吞移民监狱；后经各方民主人士努力和紧急呼吁，被联合国难民署获悉，于<span lang="EN-US">2015</span>年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">6</span>日答应拟启动紧急通道，力促使其全家获准难民身份；但<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">13</span>日仍被中共当局以“非法越境罪”遣送押解回国。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">姜野飞于<span lang="EN-US">2016</span>年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">14</span>日被重庆公安局以涉嫌“颠覆国家政权罪”、“组织他人偷越国境罪”逮捕。羁押于重庆市第二看守所。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2018</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">13</span>日，重庆市第一中级法院对姜野飞颠覆国家政权、偷越国境案宣判，认定姜野飞犯颠覆国家政权罪，判处有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">6</span>年，剥夺政治权利<span lang="EN-US">2</span>年，犯偷越国境罪，判处有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">9</span>个月，并处罚金一千元（人民币，下同），决定执行有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">6</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月，剥夺政治权利<span lang="EN-US">2</span>年，并处罚金一千元。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2022</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;
