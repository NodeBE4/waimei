font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">1952</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">很早即笃信基督教，并在当地教会积极参与侍奉活动；后离开家乡到江西省南昌市生活，并成为南昌基督教召会（又名“呼喊派”、“神的教会”、“地方召会”）骨干信徒；<span lang="EN-US">1995</span>年起，因中共当局将“地方召会”确定为邪教组织“呼喊派”，遂其等所在江西众召会同工及信徒均遭逼迫，迫使教会只能转为地下教会，同时设立上饶召会、南昌召会和九江召会等多个召会；在此期间，其被按立为教会长老；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">自<span lang="EN-US">2011</span>年始，其被选任为“呼喊派”南昌召会负责人，因其努力扩展教会规模，故而很快发展出昌北区、红谷滩区、湾里区、文教路区等<span lang="EN-US"> 9</span>个区召会；然而因其所在南昌召会规模不断扩大，其等教会领袖、同工及众信徒频遭当地政府监控、打压；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2021</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">7</span>日，其所在召会的几个聚会点突遭南昌市经济技术开发区警方查抄，当场搜走《生命读经》、《圣经恢复本》等<span lang="EN-US">204</span>本呼喊派教会书籍及宣传品；后其与聚会点信徒黄华二人被带走传唤；同年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">8</span>日，其二人均被南昌市经济技术开发区警方以涉嫌“利用邪教组织破坏法律实施罪”行政拘留<span lang="EN-US">15</span>天；后黄华获取保候审，但其仍被续押至<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">25</span>日；后其二人又被警方反复收押、保释；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2022</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年，其案被当地警方移送到检察院审查，但证据不足，同年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">30</span>日，其二人再被南昌市西湖区检察院继续取保候审；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2023</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2023</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">14</span>日，其案一审在南昌市西湖区法院开庭审理，至<span lang="EN-US"> 5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">12</span>日，其二人均被宣判，其中，其被以“组织、利用邪教组织破坏法律实施罪”判处有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">3</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月，并处罚金<span lang="EN-US">30000</span>元，刑期至<span lang="EN-US">2026</span>年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">11</span>日；黄华亦被判刑<span lang="EN-US">3</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>个月，并处罚金<span lang="EN-US">25000</span>元。目前疑似被羁押于南昌市第一看守所。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体;
