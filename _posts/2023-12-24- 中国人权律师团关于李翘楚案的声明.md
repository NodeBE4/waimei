<p><span></span></p><a name='more'></a><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;"><br /></span><p></p><p><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">2023</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">19</span>日李翘楚涉嫌煽动颠覆国家政权案在山东省临沂市经济技术开发区法院<span lang="EN-US">(</span>以下称临沂经开法院<span lang="EN-US">)</span>开庭。公开的信息显示辩护律师之一李国蓓被法院阻止在门外，无法进入法庭履行辩护职责，李国蓓律师发给法院院长李殿基要求协助进入法院履行职责的短信也未获回应。</span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">李翘楚是一名人权捍卫者，关注劳工、女性权益。她是中国著名人权活动家、中国公民运动发起人、法学博士许志永的女友，当局指控李翘楚在网上协助开通许志永的博客，并上传许志永《美好中国》的文章，罪名涉嫌煽动颠覆国家政权。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">许志永<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年初被捕后<span lang="EN-US">,</span>李翘楚随后被监视居住<span lang="EN-US">,</span>四个月后获短暂自由<span lang="EN-US">,</span>但她自由后不惧国保的威胁<span lang="EN-US">,</span>坚持为许志永发声<span lang="EN-US">,</span>导致她<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月再次被捕<span lang="EN-US">,</span>李翘楚被捕后抑郁症病发<span lang="EN-US">,</span>出现严重的幻听<span lang="EN-US">,</span>其父母和辩护律师多次申请取保候审未获批准<span lang="EN-US">,</span>李翘楚目前在看守所内因服药原因身体发胖变形。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">鉴于以上事实<span lang="EN-US">,</span>中国人权律师团律师于<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">22</span>日发表如下声明：<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">按照《世界人权宣言》第十九条，人人有权享有主张和发表意见的自由<span lang="EN-US">;</span>此项权利包括持有主张而不受干涉的自由<span lang="EN-US">,</span>李翘楚协助许志永发表其主张和思想属于言论自由的范畴<span lang="EN-US">,</span>中国《宪法》第三十五条规定公民享有言论自由的权利<span lang="EN-US">,</span>李翘楚的行为不构成犯罪；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">联合国《关于律师基本作用的原则》规定应当保护律师正常履职。临沂经开区法院拦阻止律师履职是严重违法的行为<span lang="EN-US">,</span>违背了《六部委关于保障律师权益》的基本宗旨<span lang="EN-US">,</span>违反了人人有权获得律师帮助的基本原则；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">按照《刑法》和《刑事诉讼法》任何人在判决前都是无罪的<span lang="EN-US">,</span>符合取保条件的情况下均应当按照无罪推定的原则取保候审，临沂公安局无视李翘楚的身体和疾病情况<span lang="EN-US">,</span>明知李翘楚无任何社会危害性的情况下<span lang="EN-US">,</span>拒绝其律师和家人的取保候审申请<span lang="EN-US">,</span>是明显违法的。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">在此<span lang="EN-US">, </span>中国人权律师团律师声明<span lang="EN-US">,</span>临沂经开区法院应当严格按照法律的规定保障李国蓓的执业权利<span lang="EN-US">,</span>保障李翘楚依法享有律师帮助的权利。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">我们认为李翘楚为许志永发文和呼吁是属于言论自由的范畴<span lang="EN-US">,</span>不违反任何法律<span lang="EN-US">,</span>在这个万马齐喑的时代<span lang="EN-US">,</span>体现的是其独有女性孤勇者的气概<span lang="EN-US">,</span>古代尚罪不及妻儿<span lang="EN-US">,</span>在追求法治中国今天<span lang="EN-US">,</span>更不可将为许志永呼吁视为一种罪行。<span lang="EN-US"><span style="mso-spacerun: yes;">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
