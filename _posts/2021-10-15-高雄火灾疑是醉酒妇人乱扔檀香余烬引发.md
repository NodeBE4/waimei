[高雄火灾疑是醉酒妇人乱扔檀香余烬引发](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20211015-1203612)
------

<div>
<p>酿成46死41伤的高雄“城中城”火灾调查有进展，警方怀疑一名黄姓妇人有重大嫌疑。</p><p>据中时电子报报道，高雄市消防局火调人员研判，起火点在一楼古董店后方的小套房，昨天凌晨在内和友人饮酒、烧檀香的黄姓妇人被警方怀疑有重嫌。</p><p>黄姓妇人承认，她曾烧檀香驱蚊，离开时随意包裹丢到垃圾桶。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>但黄妇否认故意纵火，也不认为起火和她乱扔檀香余烬行为有关，不过警方已将她列为嫌疑人，将交由检察官讯问。</p><p>至于目击者听到的爆炸巨响，火调人员推测是火焰燃烧到一旁的瓦斯炉所引发。</p><p>台湾高雄市盐埕区“城中城”大楼昨天（14日）凌晨发生火灾，酿成严重伤亡，46人身亡，41人受伤。</p><div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div><p>火警发生前，大楼曾出现一声巨响，火势迅速蔓延，大楼瞬间陷入火海。附近居民称，由于大火发生在凌晨，大楼格局、隔间很乱，有杂物堆积在出入口，加上不少居民是行动不便的老弱者，导致逃生不易，死伤惨重。</p>            <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/zbpe-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=zbpe399&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_desktop-zbpe399_2.png?dHEiGouxxZpWwWTJE04dyGyUN74JOU2z" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_desktop-zbpe399_2.png?dHEiGouxxZpWwWTJE04dyGyUN74JOU2z" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/zbpe-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=zbpe399&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_mobile-zbpe399_2.png?qZnBcIGCJvPR8A2IAZOZuOxdoGZDatiI" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_mobile-zbpe399_2.png?qZnBcIGCJvPR8A2IAZOZuOxdoGZDatiI" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
