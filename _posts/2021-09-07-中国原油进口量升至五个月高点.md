[中国原油进口量升至五个月高点](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210907-1191313)
------

<div>
<p>中国的原油进口量升至五个月高位，此前民营炼油商获分配新的进口配额，而且因台风延迟的油轮终于交货。</p><p>据彭博社报道，中国国家海关总署周二发布的数据显示，8月份进口原油4453万吨。据彭博计算，这相当于1053万桶/天，为3月以来的最高水平。7月的进口合975万桶/天。</p><p>伦敦Energy Aspects Ltd.的分析师刘云涛（音译自Yuntao Liu）分析说，在8月中旬获发更多进口额度之后，民营炼油厂得以增加采购，而由于7月底台风烟花而推迟的原油终于交付，提振了总体进口量。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>独立炼油商上个月获得了2021年第三批进口配额，总量442万吨，为中国允许非国有企业直接进口原油以来的最低水平。Energy Aspects预测，今年余下时间，它们可能还会获得1100万吨的额度。</p><p>中国8月净燃油出口降至80万吨左右，为2020年6月以来最低。炼油商等候新的出口配额之际出口减少，它们上个月获得了额度。</p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/zbpe-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=zbpe399&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_desktop-zbpe399_2.png?dHEiGouxxZpWwWTJE04dyGyUN74JOU2z" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_desktop-zbpe399_2.png?dHEiGouxxZpWwWTJE04dyGyUN74JOU2z" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/zbpe-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=zbpe399&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_mobile-zbpe399_2.png?qZnBcIGCJvPR8A2IAZOZuOxdoGZDatiI" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_mobile-zbpe399_2.png?qZnBcIGCJvPR8A2IAZOZuOxdoGZDatiI" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
