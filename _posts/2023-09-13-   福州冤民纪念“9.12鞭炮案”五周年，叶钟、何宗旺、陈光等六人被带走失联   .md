<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">13</span>日，本网获悉：昨天是“<span lang="EN-US">9.12</span>鞭炮案”五周年的纪念日，唐兆星、林兰英、叶钟、廖俊、林兰英、张秀苹、唐何宗旺、陈光、林后勤、吴宏福两等<span lang="EN-US">11</span>人，在望江楼酒店相聚纪念，结果在吃饭时警察国保包围，其中叶钟，何钟旺、陈光、吴宏福等<span lang="EN-US">6</span>人被带走，至今下落不明。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2018</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">12</span>日，福州的访民不约而同去福州市看守所，迎接被释放的访友严兴声时，有访友按习俗燃放了驱邪的鞭炮，被当局大规模抓捕，当时被抓捕的有<span lang="EN-US">43</span>人之多，其中林应强和唐兆星分别被“寻衅滋事口袋罪”判刑三年，站在马路边的林兰英被判刑一年十个月，世称“<span lang="EN-US">9.12</span>”鞭炮冤案。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">据唐兆星说，警察国保一个个搜查大家的手机，查看有没有现场视频和照片，确认手机里没有现场视频和照片了，然后才放了一部分人。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">唐兆星电话：<span lang="EN-US">18750172948.<o:p></o:p></span></span></p>
