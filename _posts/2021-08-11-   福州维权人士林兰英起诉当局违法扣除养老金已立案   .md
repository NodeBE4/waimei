[福州维权人士林兰英起诉当局违法扣除养老金已立案](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2021/08/blog-post_33.html)
------

<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">11</span>日，本网获悉：福州维权人士林兰英已于近日收到鼓楼区法院作出的（<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>）闽<span lang="EN-US">0102</span>行初<span lang="EN-US">399</span>号《受理案件通知书》，即：其起诉仓山区城乡居民社会养老保险中心违法扣除养老金已立案。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">林兰英常年希望为捍卫自己、捍卫公民的尊严和权利做力所能及的事，此次起诉亦是；但因受教育程度低，多年维权，经济拮据，请不起律师，希望各界热心人士能够为其提供法律帮助。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">事情经过：<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></b></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">林兰英，女，<span lang="EN-US">70</span>岁，福建福州人，因遭受司法不公和强拆维权。热心公义，长期关注福州当地维权。<span lang="EN-US">2018</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">12</span>日，林兰英前往福州市第一看守所迎接当地维权人士严兴声出狱时被抓捕，此后林被指控寻衅滋事罪，在没有任何违法事实的情况下，依然被判处一年十个月有期徒刑。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2020</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">12</span>日，林出狱，她身体健康状况极差，需要不断看病住院。<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月至<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月，林却陆续收到仓山区城乡居民社会养老保险中心和福州市仓山区被征地农民养老保障工作中心领导小组办公室通知，要求其退还已领取的共<span lang="EN-US">15932.32</span>元的养老金，并已扣押了她多月的养老金，使得原本就拮据的家庭经济更是雪上加霜。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体;
