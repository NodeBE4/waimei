<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">13</span>日，本网获悉因言获罪遭抓捕的深圳年轻基督徒颜志鸿的案情。现通报如下：<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">颜志鸿：</span></b><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">1990</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年代出生，广东省人，基督徒，原深圳市某私立学校老师，深圳市某教会信徒，网络活跃人士，社交平台微信公众号运营者，社会良知人士，中国在押良心犯。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">笃信耶稣基督，并在深圳市某一教会是教会热心信徒；早期曾在深圳市一私立学校当老师，后因活跃于网络平台，而将精力转而投入运营社交平台微信公众号；<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年，上海疫情“清零”封城期间，因一位旅居上海市的德国人因不满中共当局强制隔离措施引发次生危害，录制了一段英语视频以表抗议，随其看到后将此段英语视频翻译成中文字幕，并上传到自己的微信公众号上，很快引发网络热议，但却使中共当局十分不悦，欲加伺机打击；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2022</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">23</span>日，其被深圳市国家安全局警方以涉嫌“煽动颠覆国家政权罪”带走刑拘，至此失联；其他以微信公众号流量谋生的同事及友人亦遭陆续传唤和调查；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">针此<span lang="EN-US">,</span>其所在深圳某教会发起为期<span lang="EN-US">7</span>天的接力禁食祷告行动<span lang="EN-US">,</span>日期为<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">4</span>日—<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">11</span>日，以期其能早日释放；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体;
