<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体;">22</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin;">日，本网获悉获刑</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12.0pt; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体;">15</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin;">年的原新疆大学维吾尔语言学教授乃比江•艾比布拉的案情，现通报如下：</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12.0pt; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体;"><o:p></o:p></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin;">乃比江•艾比布拉：</span></b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin;">维族，</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12.0pt; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体;">1983</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin;">年出生，新疆维吾尔自治区和田地区洛浦县人，前新疆师范大学、新疆大学和中央民族大学老师，新疆大学优秀本科生导师和研究员，维吾尔语言学教授，中国在押政治犯。</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12.0pt;"><o:p></o:p></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin;">早年，曾就读于新疆维吾尔自治区和田师范技术高中中文专业，并于</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12.0pt; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体;">2002</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin;">年毕业；</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12.0pt; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体;">2012</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin;">年</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12.0pt; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体;">-2014</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin;">年，曾出国留学，在德国哥廷根大学攻读博士学位；毕业后又返回新疆；</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12.0pt; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体;"><o:p></o:p></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin;">据悉，回国前，曾有多个友人警告他说，“新疆局势正在恶化，建议还是留在德国而不是回国找工作”；但其对此则认为，“若只专注于学术研究，远离社会冲突和其他当局认为有争议的问题，回国仍是安全的”；基于其认为自己回国可以做得更好，且相信通过对维吾尔学生的教授，可以为他们提供更上一层楼的社会机会及工作舞台，</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12.0pt; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体;">2014</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin;">年，随毅然返回新疆；</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12.0pt; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体;"><o:p></o:p></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin;">回国后，曾先后在新疆师范大学、新疆大学和中央民族大学学习与工作；</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12.0pt; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体;">2016</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin;">年</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12.0pt; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体;">6</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin;">月，曾因完成一篇题为《汉译佛经中古维吾尔语动词的词典意义》的博士论文，从而获取了</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12.0pt; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体;">4</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin;">项研究资助，并在著名学术期刊上发表了</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12.0pt; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体;">10</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin;">多篇文章；</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12.0pt; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体;"><o:p></o:p></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12.0pt; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体;">2018</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin;">年</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12.0pt; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体;">2</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin;">月，因中共当局对新疆维吾尔族、哈萨克斯坦族、回族等各族群开始大规模实行“再教育营”（中共称之为“职业技能教育培训机构”）的集中高压管控政策，遂与多位大学其他主要教师及研究人员悉数被警方传唤到所在大学保卫处，并在短暂审讯后被乌鲁木齐市国家安全局警员带走，后送“再教育营”进行洗脑式培训，自此失联；</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12.0pt; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体;"><o:p></o:p></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12.0pt; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体;">2021</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin;">年</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12.0pt; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体;">3</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin;">月，知其被新疆乌鲁木齐市法院秘密重判有期徒刑</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12.0pt;
