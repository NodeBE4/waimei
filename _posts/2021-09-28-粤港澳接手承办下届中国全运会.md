[粤港澳接手承办下届中国全运会](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210928-1197976)
------

<div>
<p>在陕西省举行的第十四届中国全国运动会昨晚（27日）正式闭幕，香港特首林郑月娥、澳门特首贺一诚及广东省省长马兴瑞，在闭幕礼上先后接过全运会会旗，标志粤港澳正式接手承办下届赛事。</p><p>据明报报道，全运闭幕礼在西安奥体中心举行，因为防疫关系，37个代表队各只有五名代表进场，不设运动员持旗手。国务院总理李克强其后宣布比赛闭幕，三地首长与国家体育总局完成全运会旗移交。</p><p>林郑月娥感谢中央支持承办赛事，她说，香港曾主办2009年东亚运，每年也举行多项国际体育赛事，可充分利用启德体育园及香港单车馆等场馆设施。港府将与中央有关部委、广东省及澳门特区政府紧密合作。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/zbpe-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=zbpe399&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_desktop-zbpe399_2.png?dHEiGouxxZpWwWTJE04dyGyUN74JOU2z" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_desktop-zbpe399_2.png?dHEiGouxxZpWwWTJE04dyGyUN74JOU2z" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/zbpe-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=zbpe399&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_mobile-zbpe399_2.png?qZnBcIGCJvPR8A2IAZOZuOxdoGZDatiI" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_mobile-zbpe399_2.png?qZnBcIGCJvPR8A2IAZOZuOxdoGZDatiI" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
