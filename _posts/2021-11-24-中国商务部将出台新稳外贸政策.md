[中国商务部将出台新稳外贸政策](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20211124-1216727)
------

<div>
<p>中国商务部副部长任鸿斌周三表示，完全有信心实现全年外贸“量稳质升”目标任务，将会同有关部门做好跨周期调节工作，适时出台新一轮稳外贸政策举措，确保外贸运行在合理区间。</p><p>据路透社报道，他在商务部举行的“十四五”对外贸易高质量发展规划专题新闻发布会上作出上述表示。但他也提到，外贸发展还存在很多隐忧，不能盲目乐观，许多外贸企业特别是中小微企业经营压力增大、困难增多，“有单不敢接”“增收不增利”现象较为普遍。</p><p>中国商务部周二印发“十四五”对外贸易高质量发展规划称，将优化进出口商品结构，降低进口关税和制度性成本。扩大先进技术、重要设备、关键零部件进口，增加能源资源产品和国内紧缺农产品进口，开展绿色低碳贸易合作。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section>            <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/zbpe-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=zbpe399&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_desktop-zbpe399_2.png?VersionId=dHEiGouxxZpWwWTJE04dyGyUN74JOU2z" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_desktop-zbpe399_2.png?VersionId=dHEiGouxxZpWwWTJE04dyGyUN74JOU2z" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/zbpe-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=zbpe399&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_mobile-zbpe399_2.png?VersionId=qZnBcIGCJvPR8A2IAZOZuOxdoGZDatiI" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_mobile-zbpe399_2.png?VersionId=qZnBcIGCJvPR8A2IAZOZuOxdoGZDatiI" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>    <div class="cx_paywall_placeholder" id="sph_cdp_40"></div>
