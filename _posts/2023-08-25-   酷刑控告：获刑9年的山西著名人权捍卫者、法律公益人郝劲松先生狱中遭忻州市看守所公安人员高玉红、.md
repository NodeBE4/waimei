<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">25</span>日，本网获悉：获刑<span lang="EN-US">9</span>年的山西著名人权捍卫者、法律公益人郝劲松先生狱中遭残暴殴打虐待，日前郝劲松及其代理律师已向山西省忻州市人民检察院提起正式控告。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2022</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月，因郝劲松拒理光头，忻州市看守所公安人员高玉红、曲彪、李瑞元将其带到备勤室殴打。曲彪抱摔郝劲松致头朝下摔倒。高玉红用脚踩郝劲松的头，李瑞元脱掉郝劲松的裤子进行羞辱，几个公安人员先是拳打脚踢，后动用警棍殴打臀部<span lang="EN-US">20 </span>多下，致使郝劲松严重受伤，内裤沾满鲜血。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">著名法律公益人郝劲松先生于<span lang="EN-US">2019</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">17</span>日遭抓捕，已被超期羁押<span lang="EN-US">3</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>个月后，于<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">20</span>日被以诈骗罪判刑<span lang="EN-US">8</span>年、寻衅滋事罪判刑<span lang="EN-US">1</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>个月，合并执行有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">9</span>年。郝劲松目前羁押于忻州市看守所。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">郝劲松：</span></b><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">生于<span lang="EN-US">1972</span>年，山西忻州人。著名人权捍卫者、法律人。郝劲松自<span lang="EN-US">2004</span>年开始先后<span lang="EN-US">7</span>次将国家税务总局、北京地铁运营公司、北京铁路局告上法庭，原他因对“打破行业‘霸王条款’起到了一定作用”而入选“<span lang="EN-US">2004</span>年构建经济和谐十大受尊崇人物”。郝劲松被先后提名为<span lang="EN-US">2005</span>中国法制新闻人物，<span lang="EN-US">2005</span>年度十大法制人物，并以“维权战士”的身份写入《<span lang="EN-US">2005</span>中国法治蓝皮书》。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">因介入推动铁道部春运不涨价案件入选《<span lang="EN-US">2006</span>中国法治蓝皮书》，并当选<span lang="EN-US">2006</span>中国十大消费维权人物，<span lang="EN-US">2008</span>年因介入陕西华南虎案件和杨佳案被《南方人物周刊》评选为“中国魅力人物——公义之魅”。<span lang="EN-US">2009</span>年，介入上海“钓鱼执法事件”。后成立山西定襄县劲松法务咨询中心。<span lang="EN-US">2017</span>年，郝劲松向定襄县环保局实名举报当地的诚泰纺织印染公司涉嫌污染空气和水源。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">郝劲松于<span lang="EN-US">2019</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">10</span>日、<span lang="EN-US">11</span>日就连续两天遭到户籍地山西省定襄县晋昌镇派出所以“寻衅滋事”为由传唤后，<span lang="EN-US">2019</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">17</span>日再遭该派出所传唤。随后被行政拘留<span lang="EN-US">15</span>天。<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">2</span>日，行政拘留期满被转刑事拘留，控罪为涉嫌寻衅滋事。当日，郝劲松被控涉嫌寻衅滋事遭定襄县公安局刑事拘留。羁押于五台县看守所。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">案件久不审理，一直拖延至<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">12</span>至<span lang="EN-US">13</span>日在山西定襄县法院开庭审理。开庭审理一年多后，于<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">20</span>日，被超期羁押超过<span lang="EN-US">1200</span>天的著名法律公益人、法律学者郝劲松先生被以诈骗罪判刑<span lang="EN-US">8</span>年、寻衅滋事罪判刑<span lang="EN-US">1</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>个月，合并执行有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">9</span>年。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">对郝劲松的狱中境况，本网将持续关注。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"></p><div class="separator" style="clear: both;
