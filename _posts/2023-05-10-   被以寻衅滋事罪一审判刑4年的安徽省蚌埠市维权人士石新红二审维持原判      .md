[被以寻衅滋事罪一审判刑4年的安徽省蚌埠市维权人士石新红二审维持原判](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2023/05/4.html)
------

<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">10</span>日，本网获悉：安徽省蚌埠市维权人士石新红于<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">21</span>日被安徽省蚌埠市固镇县法院以寻衅滋事罪一审判刑<span lang="EN-US">4</span>年，刑期至：<span lang="EN-US">2026</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">14</span>日。其不服上诉，日前二审驳回上诉，维持原判。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">石新红：</span></b><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">1970</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">13</span>日出生，安徽省蚌埠市人，蚌埠市<span lang="EN-US">/</span>固镇县<span lang="EN-US">/</span>石湖乡<span lang="EN-US">/</span>石湖村<span lang="EN-US">/</span>东圩组村民，维权访民，中国在押维权人士。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2001</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年，曾因其父石兆达房屋改造一事与当地石湖乡政府发生纠纷，遂其父一纸诉状告上法庭，但却久拖不予受理；等待受理期间，<span lang="EN-US">2003</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">11</span>日，又因其父房屋被当地乡长曹以星非法滥用职权霸占<span lang="EN-US">10</span>年不予归还，导致其父后来一直无家可归，被迫流浪在外，最后病死他乡；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2006</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年，其父状告石湖乡政府案被蚌埠市固镇县法院作出终审裁定，但其家人并不认同，认为该判决纯属枉法，遂其开始替父走上漫长艰辛维权路；由于其经常进京信访、讨公道，遂屡遭地方当局维稳打压，多次被非法关押和刑拘；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2013</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">15</span>日，曾因到北京最高人民检查院上访，而被北京警察拦截，并被送久敬庄关押，后被送回原籍又被软禁于一家小宾馆（实为黑监狱）里若干天；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2017</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月，中共“两会”期间，曾因在京上访又被维稳办人员抓回，并被固镇县警方以涉嫌“寻衅滋事罪”刑拘，一个月后被取保释放；同年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">9</span>日，因再度进京上访而遭特警拦截，交固镇县维稳办人员遣回原籍后，再次被固镇县石湖乡派出所警方拘押，之后失联多日；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2022</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">14</span>日，当其为求维权、投诉、讨公道而准备长居北京，一边打工一边上访之时，又被固镇县警方抓回，并被以涉嫌“寻衅滋事罪”刑拘；后被固镇县检察院以同罪由正式批捕和起诉；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2022</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">25</span>日，其案在安徽省蚌埠市固镇县法院开庭审理，至今尚未宣判；据悉，刑拘之初，曾惨遭刑讯逼供，被固定在审讯椅子上<span lang="EN-US">10</span>多个小时，不给饭吃，不给水喝，不准睡觉，<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">15</span>日被送到看守所时心脏早愽，不得不送到医院住院救治<span lang="EN-US">15</span>天。石新红于<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">21</span>日被安徽省蚌埠市固镇县法院以寻衅滋事罪判刑<span lang="EN-US">4</span>年，刑期至：<span lang="EN-US">2026</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">14</span>日。其不服上诉，二审驳回上诉，维持原判。目前被羁押于蚌埠市女子看守所（又称蚌埠市监管中心）。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"></p><div class="separator" style="clear: both;
