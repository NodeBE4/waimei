[20大前维稳  湖北宜昌维权人士向祚华被刑事拘留](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2022/09/20.html)
------

<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">15</span>日，本网获悉：<span lang="EN-US">20</span>大前维稳，湖北宜昌维权人士向祚华呼吁<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">17</span>日被秭归县警方以涉嫌“寻衅滋事罪”刑拘，至今未放。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;"><b>向祚华</b>：<span lang="EN-US">1970</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">27</span>日出生，湖北省宜昌市秭归县人，宜昌市秭归县九畹溪镇砚窝台村二组（原芝兰乡芦池湾村三组）村民，冤狱受害人，维权举报人，中国在押维权人士。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">1999</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年向柞华来因家人与邻居纠纷被宜昌市秭归县警方抓走，并被以涉嫌“故意伤害罪”拘捕；<span lang="EN-US">1999</span>年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">13</span>日，其被湖北省宜昌市秭归县法院一审以同罪名判有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">10</span>年；其不服，当庭表示上诉；同年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">15</span>日，因其向宜昌市中级法院递交了附有新证人名单的刑事上诉状，之后虽经二审，但仍一直不被宣判，直至其在湖北宜昌监狱服刑<span lang="EN-US">5</span>年之后，才将将维持一审判决的二审裁定书交给本人；其不服屡屡申诉，且其辩护律师也将数份足以证明一审判决存在认定事实不清的现场证人证言提交给各级司法部门，但都被耽延未审；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">出狱后，因其为讨公道而不惜长期举报、投诉因司法不公被冤狱，然而截至<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年，最高法院、最高检察院以及宜昌市中级法院均对其申诉驳回请求，以致于其不得不通过网络寻求社会正义力量的关注与支持；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2022</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">17</span>日，因当局要确保中共“二十大”顺利召开，其遂被秭归县警方以涉嫌“寻衅滋事罪”刑拘，至今未放。目前被羁押于湖北省秭归县看守所。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"></p><div class="separator" style="clear: both;
