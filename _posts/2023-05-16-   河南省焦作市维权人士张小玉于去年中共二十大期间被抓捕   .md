[河南省焦作市维权人士张小玉于去年中共二十大期间被抓捕](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2023/05/blog-post_17.html)

<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">17</span>日，本网获悉：河南省焦作市维权人士张小玉大概在中共去年召开二十大时被抓。目前没有律师探视，因经济原因没有代理律师。其被抓捕原因及详情尚不清楚。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">张小玉简历：女，<span lang="EN-US">1960</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">13</span>日出生，河南省焦作市人，河南省焦作市中站区李封东会<span lang="EN-US">25</span>号居民，维权冤民、抗暴维权者。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">1993</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年，曾因其家中从河南省焦作市中站区某集体组织中收购一煤矿，后遭遇违约收回，致其全家蒙受损失近<span lang="EN-US">7000</span>吨煤，其家虽提起诉讼获胜，但却一直无人赔偿经济损失，遂被迫与其夫许有臣二人走上维权之路；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">上访期间，因到各级政府管理部门信访投诉并多次到北京上访，而屡遭当局非法暴力截访、暴打、训诫及拘留等；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2014</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">17</span>日，因长期坚持在北京信访维权而被河南焦作警方截访，在被强行押往家乡途中，双方发生冲突，后被警方指控因其用水果刀刺死一名王姓警察，遂夫妇二人当即被以涉嫌“故意杀人罪”刑拘；事发后，其代理律师要求对死者验尸，但遭警方拒绝并将王姓死者尸体匆匆火化；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2014</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">2</span>日，被三门峡市检察院以涉“故意杀人罪”正式批捕，关押在焦作市看守所，并屡遭殴打而致双目失明；其案虽被移交法院，但后经<span lang="EN-US">3</span>次延长审查起诉期限，<span lang="EN-US">2</span>次退回补充侦查，<span lang="EN-US">2015</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">5</span>日，当地检察院最终对其做出不起诉决定，其遂被释放回家； <span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2015</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">1</span>日，因其长驻北京为在押的丈夫许有臣呼吁关注，遂又被河南警方抓捕关押；其丈夫被判处死刑，<span lang="EN-US">2018</span>年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月被执行死刑。<span lang="EN-US">2016</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">12</span>日，张小玉被河南省武陟县法院一审以“寻衅滋事罪”判处有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">3</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月； <span lang="EN-US">2019</span>年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">28</span>日，刑满释放。此前被羁押于河南省焦作看守所。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体;
