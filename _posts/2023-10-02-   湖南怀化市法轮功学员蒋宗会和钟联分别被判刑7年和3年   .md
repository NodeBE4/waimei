<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">2</span>日，本网获悉：湖南怀化市法轮功学员蒋宗会和钟联分别被判刑<span lang="EN-US">7</span>年和<span lang="EN-US">3</span>年。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2022</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">29</span>日上午，怀化市公安局、鹤城区公安分局、怀铁公安处，联合行凶，对怀化市鹤城区法轮功学员大面积抓捕和抄家，至少十八名法轮功学员遭抓捕，其中，法轮功学员蒋宗会和钟联聪被刑事拘留，关押在怀化市看守所。后遭批捕、起诉。近日获悉，蒋宗会和钟联聪被分别判刑<span lang="EN-US">7</span>年和<span lang="EN-US">3</span>年，蒋宗会被罚金五千元，钟联聪被罚金二千元。蒋宗会刑期至：<span lang="EN-US">2029</span>年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">28</span>日，钟联刑期至：<span lang="EN-US">2025</span>年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">28</span>日。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">蒋宗会原是怀化市邮政局的一名科级干部，年已七十多岁。由于坚持修炼法轮大法，被单位无理开除。因其被判刑，怀化市鹤城区社保中心还向将蒋宗会的家人索取他被非法抓捕后领取的养老金，现家属已缴纳。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p>
