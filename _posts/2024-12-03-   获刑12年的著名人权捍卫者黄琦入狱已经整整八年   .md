[获刑12年的著名人权捍卫者黄琦入狱已经整整八年](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2024/12/12.html)
------

<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">3</span>日，本网获悉：获刑<span lang="EN-US">12</span>年的著名人权捍卫者、六四天网创办人、年已<span lang="EN-US">61</span>岁的黄琦先生入狱</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 16px;">已经</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">整整八年。</span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">黄琦因从事人权事业多次被捕入狱，三次被判刑，<span lang="EN-US">2003</span>年因为弱势群体维权，呼吁保护人权，被判刑<span lang="EN-US">5</span>年；<span lang="EN-US">2009</span>年因曝光豆腐渣工程致死学生，被判刑<span lang="EN-US">3</span>年。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">2016</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">28</span>日再遭抓捕，<span lang="EN-US">2019</span>年被判刑<span lang="EN-US">12</span>年。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">其坐牢<span lang="EN-US">8</span>年，母亲蒲文清也已<span lang="EN-US">90</span>多岁，身体状况持续恶化，母子俩的会见权利没有得到任何保障。其母亲仍然处于<span lang="EN-US"> 24 </span>小时监控之中。而黄琦有肾功能衰竭、冠心病、脑积水等多种严重疾病，维权网敦促中国政府从人道主义出发，允许黄琦保外就医。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><b><span style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">黄琦简历：</span></b><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">1963</span><span style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">7</span>日出生，四川省内江市人 “六四天网”网站创办人、中国天网人权事务中心负责人，人权捍卫者，中国在押良心犯。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">因长期关注社会热点话题，<span lang="EN-US">1998</span>年曾成立“天网寻人事务所”，<span lang="EN-US">1999</span>年曾设立“六四天网”网站，并惯于在网上发表时政敏感性文章，从而累遭当局严控、传唤、刑拘和判刑；曾因言获罪，先后三次遭到警方的拘捕，且前两次都被判刑；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">2000</span><span style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">5</span>日，曾因国外异议人士在其网页上发表有关<span lang="EN-US">1989</span>年天安门事件的文章，而被四川成都警方刑拘，<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">12</span>日被正式逮捕，<span lang="EN-US">2003</span>年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">22</span>日被四川省成都市中级法院以“煽动颠覆国家政权”判处有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">5</span>年，剥夺政治权利<span lang="EN-US">1</span>年；狱中曾遭法警、狱警和其他罪犯多次毒打，导致脑积水，脑萎缩，双侧脑室扩大，导水管变窄等严重疾病；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">出狱后，<span lang="EN-US">2006</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">31</span>日，曾改组六四天网为中国天网人权事务中心，创办了中国大陆第一家综合性人权组织，同年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">3</span>日荣获第六届中国人权青年奖；<span lang="EN-US">2008</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">10</span>日，曾因发表有关四川地震灾难问政府的报导（即网上发文揭露汶川地震出现的“豆腐渣”工程），而第二次被成都市警方刑拘，<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">18</span>日被正式逮捕，<span lang="EN-US">2009</span>年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">23</span>日被成都市武侯区法院以“非法持有国家秘密罪”判处有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">3</span>年，<span lang="EN-US"> 2011</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">9</span>日刑满释放；然而此时因在狱中长期遭受煎熬和非人待遇，已罹患肾脏绝症；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">2016</span><span style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">28</span>日，因对一份内部文件内容曝光，其第三次被四川省绵阳市警方从成都家中带走刑事拘留，自此失联，同年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">16</span>日被以“为境外非法泄露国家机密罪”正式逮捕，并被关押在绵阳看守所；<span lang="EN-US">2018</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月初，其案被移交四川绵阳市法院审理；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">2019</span><span style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">29</span>日，被四川省绵阳市法院一审以“故意泄露国家秘密罪”、“为境外非法提供国家秘密罪”共判处有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">12</span>年；其不服上诉，后经绵阳市中级人民法院二审裁决，依然维持原判，刑期至<span lang="EN-US">2028</span>年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">27</span>日。目前被在四川省巴中监狱服刑。据悉，黄琦狱中曾遭虐待迫害，常被犯人殴打致腿部受伤，家人所寄存款亦被狱警随意扣押；身体健康极差，有肾功能衰竭危险，患脑积水、心脏病、肺气肿和肺炎等多种严重疾患。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">对黄琦先生的狱中境况，本网将持续关注。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"></p><div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;"><a href="https://blogger.googleusercontent.com/img/b/R29vZ2xl/AVvXsEjkGtRJdAA9SykPpvXq0WapSoSiGbhNPBJ2lztlpn0xCUsNYSImdvzgK02MoIStTIHkr88IYEFPvXh4dqayvx0oBCkFw-HEnwlqY9wEbFCWDxQ1naUzktmBVwS3AkSO-rOdNfhV2a_oLPPiE7DqLDZuyRiD3jarCLAaeceL9HSjrQha9BqXBWg4nu6DmeLe/s900/GTr-UI8WIAIsZXZ.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em;
