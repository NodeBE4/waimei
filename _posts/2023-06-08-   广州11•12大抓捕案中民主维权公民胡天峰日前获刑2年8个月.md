<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">8</span>日，本网获悉：广州<span lang="EN-US">11</span>•<span lang="EN-US">12</span>大抓捕案中民主维权公民胡天峰日前获刑<span lang="EN-US">2</span>年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>个月，目前家属还没收到判决书。胡天峰是于<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">17</span>日被广州警方抓捕的，目前羁押在目前被羁押于广州市国家安全局看守所。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">胡天峰简历：<span lang="EN-US">1981</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">10</span>日出生，河北省人，深圳市居民，前中国武警部队成员，原深圳市某派出所警员，民主维权公民，中国在押政治犯。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">自幼父母离异，跟随母亲在颠沛流离中长大；<span lang="EN-US">2002</span>年，曾因母亲拔牙发生医疗事故，在家乡求告无门的情况下踏上了茫茫维权之路；维权期间，曾因到北京国家信访局投诉时，在信访局门口竟遭到五六个警卫暴打，随愤而参加了中国武警部队，以期能锻炼出强健的体魄为母亲的不公遭遇讨回公道；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">在部队期间，曾因听闻缅甸军方在中国边境制造恐怖袭击，炸死无数中国边境人员，而中共当局却为面子和所谓团结，不对此类事件加以重视或回击，其遂聚集了数名热血青年毅然前往边境，希望以此为国效力；但当看到实际情况后，感觉有心无力，随改变认识，毅然加入缅北雇佣军，在热带雨林里与缅军对峙八个月；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">回国后，<span lang="EN-US">2004</span>年，又在深圳市某派出所做警员，期间结识众多仁人志士，并曾对许多陷入生活窘困的民主维权人士慷慨解囊；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">辞职后，因积极践行民主自由理念，不断为底层百姓不平之事呐喊、为被拐卖儿童奔波、为国家免费教育呼吁，遂遭致中共广东当局的不满与维稳打压；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2020</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">17</span>日，被广州市警方突然从家中带走，自此失踪，后知其与另<span lang="EN-US">4</span>名民主人士被广州市国家安全局以涉嫌“颠覆国家政权罪”监视居住后又转为刑拘；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2021</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">3</span>日，其案被移送至广州市检察院审查起诉；之后又于同年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">8</span>日，其案宗被移送至广州市中级法院，<span lang="EN-US">5</span>人均被以“颠覆国家政权罪”起诉，案由涉及组党，具体事实无非聚餐闲聊时政、去云南旅游等；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2023</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">7</span>日，终被广州市中级法院以“颠覆国家政权罪”判处有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">2</span>年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>个月，刑期至<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">16</span>日。</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;
