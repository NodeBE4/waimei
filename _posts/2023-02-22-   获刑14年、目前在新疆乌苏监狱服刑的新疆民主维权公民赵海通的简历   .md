<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">22</span>日，本网获悉获刑<span lang="EN-US">14</span>年、目前在新疆乌苏监狱服刑的新疆民主维权公民赵海通的简历，现通报如下：<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">赵海通简介：</span></b><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">1961</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">22</span>日出生，青海省海西州人，新疆民主维权公民，中国在押政治犯。</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;"><o:p></o:p></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">因长期致力于为新疆地区弱势群体、维吾尔族群体及政治犯、良心犯等维权呼吁，近年来又南下广东，积极与南方各地维权公民、人权捍卫者等一起参与街头民主运动，举行各种声援举牌和集会活动，故而在所处之地备受民众欢迎；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2013</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">21</span>日，曾因前往湖北武汉会见人权活动家秦永敏，惨遭武汉警方的野蛮殴打，差点丧命；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2013</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月，曾因努力表达维权公民正当合法的要求，而不惧风险在广州火车站多次举牌，宣传民主理念，<span lang="EN-US">“</span>抗议流氓政府打压民主人士<span lang="EN-US">”</span>等；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2013</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月，曾因参与声援安徽民主人士张林之女张安妮失学事件，而遭警方强行拘押；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2013</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">9</span>日，曾因在新疆吐鲁番市帮助维吾尔族公民进行维权，而被新疆警方刑拘，并被关押于新疆乌鲁木齐市六道湾看守所；<span lang="EN-US">2013</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">12</span>日，被新疆警方宣布以涉嫌<span lang="EN-US">“</span>煽动颠覆国家政权罪<span lang="EN-US">”</span>正式逮捕；<span lang="EN-US">2014</span>年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">7</span>日，终被新疆维吾尔自治区乌鲁木齐市法院以同罪名秘密重判有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">14</span>年，刑期至<span lang="EN-US">2027</span>年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">8</span>日。</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;"><o:p></o:p></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">目前在新疆维吾尔自治区乌苏监狱（新疆塔城市乌苏市乌苏监狱，邮编<span lang="EN-US">833006</span>）服刑。</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体;
