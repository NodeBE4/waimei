[遭行政拘留15天的湖南岳阳市磐石圣经教会黄磊牧师期满获释](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2024/05/15.html)
------

<div>
<p><span></span></p><a name='more'></a><p class="MsoNormal"><br /></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">10</span>日，本网获悉：<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">24</span>日，湖南省岳阳市岳阳楼公安局对磐石圣经教会的黄磊牧师作出了行政拘留<span lang="EN-US">15</span>日的处罚决定。黄牧师于昨天<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">9</span>日走出拘留所获释。这是<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年以来他第二次被行政拘留。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">近期，岳阳当地多间基督教教会受到冲击，其中一间晨光教会被打砸，搜走物品也没有任何手续。牧者被拘留<span lang="EN-US">5</span>天。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">黄磊牧师：男，汉族，<span lang="EN-US">1966</span>年生。户籍地：湖南省岳阳市岳阳楼区南湖大道三医院家属区。<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">17</span>日，黄磊牧师创建成立了<span lang="EN-US">“</span>岳阳楼区磐石圣经家庭教会<span lang="EN-US">” </span>，因教会发展迅速，信众会员众多，受到当局持续打压。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"></p><div class="separator" style="clear: both;
