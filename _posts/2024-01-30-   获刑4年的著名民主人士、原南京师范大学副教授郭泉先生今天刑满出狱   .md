[获刑4年的著名民主人士、原南京师范大学副教授郭泉先生今天刑满出狱](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2024/01/4_30.html)
------

<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">30</span>日，本网获悉：获刑<span lang="EN-US">4</span>年的著名民主人士、原南京师范大学副教授郭泉先生今天刑满出狱，现已回到家中，和亲人相见了。郭泉在其微信朋友圈里通报了回家的消息。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">郭泉：</span></b><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">1968</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">8</span>日出生，江苏省南京市人，原南京师范大学副教授、研究生导师，中国大陆新民党创始人及代理主席，中国政治犯。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2007</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年曾因发表公开信要求中国进行宪政改革，宣布组建“中国新民党”，主张在中国实行多党制和军队国家化等，遭受到中共当局的打压，<span lang="EN-US">2007</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">6</span>日，被南京师范大学党委以违反宪法和教师法为由撤销其副教授职务，调任数据员；<span lang="EN-US">2008</span>年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月，又因撰文批评中国政府处理四川地震的方式不当而被拘押<span lang="EN-US">10</span>天；<span lang="EN-US">2008</span>年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">13</span>日，再次因其在网上发表<span lang="EN-US">300</span>多篇名为《民主先声》的文章，其中涉及组成以宣传民主思想为目标的新民党，以及提出以不合作方式进行公民行动的蓝色运动倡议等内容，被南京市警方刑事拘留；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2009</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">16</span>日，被江苏省宿迁市中级人民法院以“颠覆国家政权罪”判处有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">10</span>年，剥夺政治权利<span lang="EN-US">3</span>年。<span lang="EN-US">2018</span>年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">12</span>日，刑满释放。此前在江苏省南京市浦口监狱服刑。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2020</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">31</span>日，原南京师范大学副教授、南京著名人权捍卫者郭泉因关注湖北武汉疫情，为疫区人民发声而遭中共当局抓捕，随即被刑事拘留。<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">26</span>日，郭泉的母亲顾潇向媒体证实，郭泉本月中旬已经被南京检察院以“涉嫌煽动颠覆国家政权罪”批捕。南京警方指郭泉在网络上发表了国情及疫情等信息大<span lang="EN-US">500</span>篇文章，其中半数是在煽动民众。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">斯伟江律师<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">12</span>日在南京市第二看守所，会见郭泉。郭泉以煽动颠覆国家政权罪，在徐州市法院审理。对他指控，主要是微信上他的郭泉语录。郭泉满面红光，穿个短袖，身体健康。郭泉声言不惧坐牢。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">由徐州中院审理的郭泉煽动颠覆国家政权案，于<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">9</span>日<span lang="EN-US">9</span>时<span lang="EN-US">30</span>分在南京市江宁区看守所开庭审理。郭泉的代理律师是郑州的常伯阳律师和上海的斯伟江律师。<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">14</span>日，郭泉被中共当局以煽动颠覆国家政权罪判处有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">4</span>年。刑期至：<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">30</span>日。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"></p><div class="separator" style="clear: both;
