[美日舰艇在南中国海展开演习](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20211026-1207076)
------

<div>
<p>美国军方昨天（25日）公布，美国卡尔文森号航母打击群正在南中国海与日本海上自卫队加贺号驱逐舰展开演习。</p><p>美国太平洋舰队在官网上说，美日双方在南中国海展开海上保安行动，包含水面和空中部队之间的协调战术训练、海上加油和海上打击演习。</p><p>太平洋舰队称，海上合作交往与美国航母在南中国海展开的行动，是美国海军在印太地区日常存在的一部分。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>打击群指挥官马丁（Dan Martin）说：“通过持续与我们的盟友和合作伙伴在国际水域和空域开展的例行行动，我们展示了我们坚定不移，致力于在海上和空中维护国际法，并确保所有国家可以毫无畏惧或在没有争议的情况下，也能够这么做。”</p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/zbpe-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=zbpe399&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_desktop-zbpe399_2.png?dHEiGouxxZpWwWTJE04dyGyUN74JOU2z" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_desktop-zbpe399_2.png?dHEiGouxxZpWwWTJE04dyGyUN74JOU2z" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/zbpe-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=zbpe399&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_mobile-zbpe399_2.png?qZnBcIGCJvPR8A2IAZOZuOxdoGZDatiI" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_mobile-zbpe399_2.png?qZnBcIGCJvPR8A2IAZOZuOxdoGZDatiI" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
