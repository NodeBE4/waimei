[因言获罪被判刑2年6个月的河南民主异见人士曹培植目前被关押在河南省开封第一监狱](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2024/05/26.html)
------

<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">29</span>日，本网获悉：因言获罪被判刑<span lang="EN-US">2</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月的河南民主异见人士曹培植目前被关押在河南省开封第一监狱<span lang="EN-US">15</span>监区（开封市龙亭区金耀路西环路口向西<span lang="EN-US">200</span>米路北），邮编<span lang="EN-US">475004</span>，电话<span lang="EN-US"> 0378-3664022</span>。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">曹培植先生多年坚持民主自由的普世价值原则，<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">3</span>日被河南省义马市公安局以寻衅滋事罪刑事拘留，<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">4</span>日被批准逮捕，后被判刑<span lang="EN-US">2</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月，刑期至：<span lang="EN-US">2025</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">2</span>日。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">1989</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">年，曾因参与年天安门民主运动并亲历<span lang="EN-US">“</span>六四惨案<span lang="EN-US">”</span>，遂自此改变了人生轨迹，坚定的走上一条中国民主运动不归路；曾因以民主推手、民权精英、博客骑士自许，而创建<span lang="EN-US">“</span>温馨小屋<span lang="EN-US">”</span>博客群，并在其努力经营下，使该博客群的点击量高达<span lang="EN-US">70</span>万人次；也正是因其频发民主信息和对中共当局不利的时政批评性信息，故而引起河南省当局的极度不悦与伺机打击；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">2022</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">3</span>日，因其在微信群里传播<span lang="EN-US">“</span>未经官方证实的海外消息，并对党和国家领导人有侮辱性言论<span lang="EN-US">”</span>，遂被河南省义马市警方以涉嫌<span lang="EN-US">“</span>寻衅滋事罪<span lang="EN-US">”</span>刑事拘留；同年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">4</span>日，又被义马市检察院以同罪名正式批捕。曹培植于<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">21</span>日被法院判刑<span lang="EN-US">2</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月。<a name="_Hlk167886667">刑期至：<span lang="EN-US">2025</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">2</span>日</a>。此前被羁押于河南省渑池县看守所，后转监到河南省开封第一监狱<span lang="EN-US">15</span>监区（开封市龙亭区金耀路西环路口向西<span lang="EN-US">200</span>米路北）。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"></p><div class="separator" style="clear: both;
