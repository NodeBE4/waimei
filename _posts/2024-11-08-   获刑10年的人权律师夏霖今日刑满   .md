[获刑10年的人权律师夏霖今日刑满](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2024/11/10.html)
------

<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">（维权网信息中心报道） <span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">8</span>日，本网获悉：获刑<span lang="EN-US">10</span>年的人权律师夏霖今日刑满。<span lang="EN-US">2014</span>年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">8</span>日，夏霖被警方从家中带走，期间坚持零口供。<span lang="EN-US">2017</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">21</span>日，二审被以诈骗罪判刑<span lang="EN-US">10</span>年。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">夏霖简介：</span></b><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">1970</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">15</span>日出生，贵州省人，北京市华一律师事务所合伙人，知名人权律师，中国政治犯。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">曾因为人公义正派、代理案件认真有效而在业界有较高知名度；近年来，曾代理过包括艾未未、冉云飞等案在内的多起敏感维权案件；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">2006</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月—<span lang="EN-US">2007</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月，曾为北京市小贩崔英杰杀死城管李志强案做免费辩护律师；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">2009</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月，曾为刺死官员女服务员邓玉娇涉嫌故意杀人案做代理律师；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">2009</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月，曾与人权律师浦志强一起为地震义工谭作人辩护；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">2014</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">年人权律师浦志强被中共当局抓捕时，曾为其备选辩护人之一；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">2014</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">8</span>日，因其为北京传知行社会经济研究所负责人郭玉闪被捕案担任辩护人而被北京市警方突然从家中带走，随后即被以涉嫌“赌博”和“诈骗”罪刑拘，并关押在北京市第三看守所；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">2014</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">15</span>日，又被北京市检察院第二分院以涉嫌“诈骗罪”正式批捕，转关押至北京市第一看守所；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">2015</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">16</span>日，其案被移送检察院审查起诉，但证据不足被三次延期；<span lang="EN-US">2016</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">17</span>日，其案在北京第二中级法院开庭审理，至<span lang="EN-US">2016</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">22</span>日，终被该法院经一审以“诈骗罪”判有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">12</span>年，剥夺政治权利<span lang="EN-US">3</span>年，并处罚金<span lang="EN-US">12</span>万元；其不服上诉，<span lang="EN-US">2017</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">21</span>日，经北京第二中级法院二审裁决改为有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">10</span>年，刑期至<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">7</span>日。此前在北京良乡监狱服刑。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"></p><div class="separator" style="clear: both;
