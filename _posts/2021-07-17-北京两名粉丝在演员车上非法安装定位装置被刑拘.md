[北京两名粉丝在演员车上非法安装定位装置被刑拘](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210717-1170295)
------

<div>
<p>北京警方今天通报，两名粉丝在演员王某某车上非法安装定位装置，并在掌握相关行程信息后曾售卖牟利，被昌平公安分局依法刑事拘留。</p><p>根据“平安北京昌平”微博今天发布的情况通报，北京警方近日接到一男子拨打110报警，称其所驾小客车为演员王某某在京长期租用车辆，车上被人安装了定位装置。</p><p>警方开展调查，将两名分别为24岁和25岁的女嫌疑人李某、张某某查获。经审查，两人系该演员粉丝，为了达到“追星”目的，在该车上非法安装了定位装置，掌握相关行程信息后利用网络社交平台炫耀，并曾售卖牟利。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>目前，两人已被昌平公安分局依法刑事拘留，案件正在进一步工作中。</p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
