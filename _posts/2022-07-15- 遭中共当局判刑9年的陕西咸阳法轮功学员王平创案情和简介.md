font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">因笃信法轮功，自早年起即遭中共陕西当局（尤其是<span lang="EN-US">610</span>办公室）残酷打压，曾遭<span lang="EN-US">3</span>次非法绑架和<span lang="EN-US">2</span>次非法劳教，并被警方多次上门骚扰威胁；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2002</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2007</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月，曾因信仰再次被武功县<span lang="EN-US">610</span>办公室领导和普集镇派出所警方共同将其抓捕，其家遭查抄；被送武功县拘留所关押<span lang="EN-US">20</span>天后，又遭武功县当局谎称其与海外“有关系”，遂将其又被处劳动教养<span lang="EN-US">2</span>年；获释后，其与同信法轮功的妻子赵淑娟被迫离开原籍，前往西安市做生意谋生；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2020</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">25</span>日，因其与妻子在新冠病毒肆虐之际，骑电动车在西安市公交站牌上粘贴法轮功信仰中的救人良方，后被警方知晓，遂于同年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">5</span>日，其及其妻被西安市周至县警方和西安市长安区警方共<span lang="EN-US">50</span>余人连手抓走，同天，其租住屋被查抄，且警方当场搜走其大量私人物品和现金<span lang="EN-US">1</span>万多元；后其二人均被以涉嫌邪教罪刑拘，并被关押在西安市三爻看守所（后因其身体极差，被转送至西安市安康医院关押）；同年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">24</span>日，其二人被西安市长安区检察院以同罪名予以批捕；同年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">20</span>日，其案又被更换为西安市雁塔区检察院提起公诉，并将其案综提交到西安市雁塔区法院；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2021</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">16</span>日，其二人案在被超押长达<span lang="EN-US">1</span>年半后，终被西安市雁塔区法院一审以“利用邪教组织破坏法律实施罪”等枉法宣判，其中，其被重判有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">9</span>年，其妻赵西娟被重判有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">7</span>年，二人共处罚金<span lang="EN-US">5</span>万元；其不服上诉；后经西安市中级法院二审裁决，依然维持原判，刑期至<span lang="EN-US">2029</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">4</span>日。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体;
