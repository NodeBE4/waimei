[习近平：中国必须把能源饭碗端在自己手里](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20211022-1205941)
------

<div>
<p>中国国家主席习近平昨天（21日）在山东省东营市考察调研时强调，中国作为制造业大国，要发展实体经济，能源的饭碗必须端在自己手里。</p><p>综合新华社和中新社报道，习近平当天前往胜利油田，登上钻井平台，察看作业情况，看望慰问石油工人。习近平说：“石油能源建设对我们国家意义重大。”</p><p>胜利油田是中国石化旗下油田之一，今年也是胜利油田发现60周年。关注石油领域的数据平台“立方石油”的数据显示，胜利油田的产量在去年达到2385万吨，成为中国第六大油气田。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>习近平当天也前往黄河三角洲农业高新技术产业示范区，以及垦利区董集镇杨庙社区。</p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/zbpe-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=zbpe399&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_desktop-zbpe399_2.png?dHEiGouxxZpWwWTJE04dyGyUN74JOU2z" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_desktop-zbpe399_2.png?dHEiGouxxZpWwWTJE04dyGyUN74JOU2z" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/zbpe-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=zbpe399&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_mobile-zbpe399_2.png?qZnBcIGCJvPR8A2IAZOZuOxdoGZDatiI" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_mobile-zbpe399_2.png?qZnBcIGCJvPR8A2IAZOZuOxdoGZDatiI" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
