[获刑5年的山西临汾著名维权人士常珈瑄的案情通报](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2021/10/5.html)

<div>
<p></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span></span></p><a name='more'></a><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;"><br /></span><p></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">（维权网信息中心报道）</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">2021</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">年</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">10</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">月</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">9</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">日，本网获悉获刑</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">5</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">年的山西临汾著名维权人士常珈瑄的案情，现通报如下：</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">常珈瑄：<span lang="EN-US">1970</span>年出生，山西省临汾市人，退役军人，临汾市尧都区解放西路<span lang="EN-US">54</span>号“常家大院”（祖宅）继承人之一，著名维权公民，维权致残者，人权捍卫者，中国在押维权人士。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">自<span lang="EN-US">1953</span>年以来，因其从清代传承下来的祖宅曾被山西省晋南专区地方国营建筑工程队借走，历经数十载后，借方竟不想再谈归还之事，其父常耀文遂被迫携其全家人走上投诉维权之路；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">1992</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年，其父带着妻儿数十人举家欲迁回祖宅居住，租房方不仅不如约归还，反以昔日领导“现在都管不了”为由久拖不给，其父无奈，同年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月，随一纸诉状将临汾市建筑工程总公司告上地方法院；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">同年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月，临汾市人民法院派人向当年的证人、中保等数人取证，经过比对，认为证词皆与常耀文所诉情况一致，所以于<span lang="EN-US">1993</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">31</span>日，法院对其案双方进行了调解；不料被告方一边应诉，一边却仍与中国建行银行临汾市支行办理房屋交割手续，临汾市法院针此最终判定：“买卖无效，撤销其违法买卖涉及本案房产的有关手续”；被告方（又即借房方）不服，提起上诉，后经临汾市中级法院二审裁决：支持一审裁判；但借房方仍不服判决，反复几次提起上诉，而当地的政府土地部门亦对此事久拖<span lang="EN-US">12</span>年不予裁定 ；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2011</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年，因临汾市建筑工程总公司谎称“上世纪<span lang="EN-US">50</span>年代已购得<span lang="EN-US">54</span>号院，<span lang="EN-US">1992</span>年被常耀文占用并在此院内违法建筑，<span lang="EN-US">2008</span>年临汾市国土资源局确权该院国有土地使用权归属该公司”，并以此为由将其父枉法诬告上法庭，要求其父“停止侵权、恢复原状、返还财产”；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">此案件被受理后，尧都区法院一审以“原告起诉状中涉及的违法建筑应当由有关行政执法部门查处，不属于法院民事受案范围”，驳回了该借房公司的起诉；该公司仍然不服，再次上诉，又经临汾市中级法院终审裁定：一审的裁定驳回起诉正确；但遗憾的是，<span lang="EN-US">2016</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">30</span>日，其父常耀文未等来土地证即与世长辞。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2017</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">26</span>日，其母再度委托律师、其及其姊常春枝，提出土地权属申请；<span lang="EN-US">2018</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">16</span>日，临汾市国土资源局受理了该案件，但此后除<span lang="EN-US">2</span>次发出延期调查处理通知书之外，一直未予办结；其全家在维权诉讼过程中，曾屡遭殴打侮辱，导致其父受伤卧床不起，其被临汾市规划局打断四根肋骨，成了残疾人；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2020</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">7</span>日，其姐弟二人相继被临汾市警方抓捕，并被以涉嫌“寻衅滋事罪”、 “妨碍公务罪”指定居所监视居住；后被正式逮捕被羁押于临汾市尧都区看守所。 <span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2021</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月获悉，常珈瑄因抗强拆，被判刑<span lang="EN-US">5</span>年，现在山西晋城监狱二监区服刑。何时开庭何时宣判，一概不知。同时被捕的其姐常春枝怎样，目前情况不详。</span></p><p></p><div class="separator" style="clear: both;
