[获刑3年6个月湖南人权捍卫者欧彪峰已被转入长沙监狱收押调遣中心](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2023/02/36.html)

<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">4</span>日，被以“寻衅滋事”罪由处行政拘留<span lang="EN-US">15</span>天，其家被查抄；同年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">25</span>日，又被株洲市芦淞区警方以涉嫌“颠覆国家政权罪”转为指定居所监视居住；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">17</span>日，被芦淞区警方以涉嫌“煽动颠覆国家政权罪”转为刑拘；同年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">22</span>日，又被株洲市芦淞区检察院以同罪名予以批捕；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt;
