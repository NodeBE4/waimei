[因关注常玮平案而被抓捕的甘肃徽县民主维权人士龙克海案明日（2022年11月16日）开庭](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2022/11/20221116.html)
------

<div>
<p><span></span></p><a name='more'></a><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;"><br /></span><p></p><p><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">（维权网信息中心报道）</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">2022</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">年</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">11</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">月</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">15</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">日，本网获悉：因关注常玮平案而被抓捕的甘肃徽县民主维权人士龙克海明日（</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">2022</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">年</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">11</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">月</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">16</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">日）在徽县人民法院开庭，龙克海被控涉嫌寻衅滋事罪。</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">龙克海此前因网络言论被判刑<span lang="EN-US">1</span>年半。二次被抓前在四川某地打工，被陇南政保强行带回，给予两个行政处罚合计<span lang="EN-US">20</span>日。释放几日后，因常玮平案开庭<span lang="EN-US">(2022.7.26)</span>前一日，入住凤县某酒店，当晚被陇南国保强行带离，几日后被刑事拘留。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">龙克海：</span></b><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">1966</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">1</span>日出生，又名龙刻海，甘肃省徽县嘉陵镇嘉陵村人，基督徒，陕西省宝鸡市长驻居民，民主维权公民，中国曾押政治犯。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">因为人急公好义，忧国忧民，且信仰基督教，经常参加成都秋雨之福教会的聚会活动，随广受他人赞誉；积极参与各种公民圈活动而曾数次遭到警方“喝茶”、警告；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2018</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月，曾因民主聚会而被甘肃省徽县警方以“寻衅滋事罪”行政拘留<span lang="EN-US">15</span>天；获释后，因不断向当地法院提出抗诉并要求警方予以国家赔偿，遭到当局的进一步憎恨和报复；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2019</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">4</span>日，被陕西省宝鸡市警方以涉嫌“寻衅滋事罪”抓捕，后转正式逮捕；<span lang="EN-US">2019</span>年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">30</span>日，其案件被地方检察院起诉至陕西省宝鸡市渭滨区法院，指控其在<span lang="EN-US">Twitter</span>、<span lang="EN-US">Facebook</span>发布和转发一系列诽谤侮辱中共领导人及辱骂中国共产党的言论信息，严重破坏社会秩序，因而构成寻衅滋事罪；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">同年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">11</span>日，其案在陕西省宝鸡市渭滨区法院不公开审理；<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">26</span>日，在不允许家属旁听的情况下，被该法院以“寻衅滋事罪”判处有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">1</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月；其不服，提起上诉，<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">10</span>日，经陕西省宝鸡市中级法院二审裁决，依然维持原判；<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">20</span>日，刑满释放。此前羁押于宝鸡市渭滨区看守所。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体;
