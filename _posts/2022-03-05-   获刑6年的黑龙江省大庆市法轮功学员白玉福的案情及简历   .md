font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">1953</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">自<span lang="EN-US">1996</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月起，因锻炼和信仰法轮功而屡遭当局打压逼迫；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">1999</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月—<span lang="EN-US">2000</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月，曾因中共当局开始打压法轮功，其与妻子张立新等人去黑龙江省政府参加和平请愿，并在街头、小区等处进行法轮功宣传和解释活动，随遭警方多次抓捕、毒打、勒索钱财、关洗脑班和被拘押许多天；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2000</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">5</span>日，曾因与其妻在住宅楼区附近散发法轮功宣传品，而被大庆市让胡路区创新派出所警方抓走，次日，虽被工作单位（大庆市石油管理局测井公司）带回，但很快其武保科科长职务被撤销，年底被迫买断工龄而离职；<span lang="EN-US">2005</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">23</span>日，曾与妻子突遭大庆市东湖区警方野蛮抓捕，家中物品尽被查抄，其小女受累亦遭恐吓和不准去学校上学；<span lang="EN-US">7</span>天之后其妻张立新被取保释放，但其却仍被在押，曾遭野蛮插管灌食等残酷虐待；同年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">25</span>日，又被大庆市当局处劳动教养<span lang="EN-US">2</span>年，并先后被关押在大庆劳教所和绥化劳教所；期间，曾因受苦役，编制汽车坐垫、装牙签、制作服装等每天时间长达十多小时，而且频遭狱警打骂、惩罚及关小号等，导致出现高血压、牙齿松动脱落、吃饭困难等现象；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2011</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">28</span>日，曾又被大庆市国保支队警方与东湖区警方联合抓捕，家再遭搜查洗劫；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2018</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">9</span>日，因当局部署对大庆市<span lang="EN-US">17</span>名法轮功信仰者统一大搜捕，其夫妇二人亦在被抓之列，随当天即被双双带走，后在交付保释金<span lang="EN-US">1</span>万元之后，又被继续拘留<span lang="EN-US">15</span>天，才获释放回家；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2019</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">2</span>日，因其与妻子等人一同在辽宁省阜新市彰武县挂横幅做法轮功宣传，故而被警方知晓后立即抓捕，其家再次被搜查，不仅搜光屋内所有法轮功宣传品，还将其家仅有的<span lang="EN-US">1000</span>元生活费也被抢走；后其夫妻二人均被彰武县警方以涉嫌邪教罪刑拘，其被关押于彰武县看守所；同月，其二人案很快被警方移交到辽宁省彰武县检察院要求起诉，并被诱骗称，只要写思想转化书，即可“立刻放人，不走法律程序”；后其案宗几经彰武县检察院退回，要求彰武县警方补充证据，故至<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年初才被正式提起公诉；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2021</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">目前在辽宁省沈阳市某一监狱服刑。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p>
