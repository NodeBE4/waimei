[常州西湖街道办九年未支付宅基地补偿费，维权人士王小琍申请依法履职维护农民权利](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2023/02/blog-post_13.html)

<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">7</span>日，本网获悉：<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">6</span>日，维权人士王小琍向江苏省常州市武进区西湖街道办事处邮寄《依法履职申请书》，请求支付宅基地补偿费。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">王小琍家的宅基地位于常州市武进区西湖街道办事处南坟头<span lang="EN-US">41</span>号。该宅基地于<span lang="EN-US">2014</span>年被征收，但至今未支付“宅基地补偿费”，属于未履行法定职责。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">王小琍的《依法履职申请书》中载明：常州市国土资源局武进分局作出【<span lang="EN-US">2013</span>】—工矿废弃<span lang="EN-US">2-03</span>号征地补偿安置方案公告第三条规定，征地补偿标准：……集体建设用地<span lang="EN-US">32000</span>元<span lang="EN-US">/</span>亩（宅基地补偿费，待安置结束后，测算建地面积，差额部分按上述标准予以支付）……。第七条又规定了付款方式。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">据上，常州市武进区西湖街道办事处应当支付“宅基地补偿费”。由于该街道长达九年未支付宅基地补偿费，应当支付利息。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">王小琍希望该街道办依法履职，尽快解决支付宅基地补偿费的问题，维护失地农民的合法权益，切莫再拖延。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p>
