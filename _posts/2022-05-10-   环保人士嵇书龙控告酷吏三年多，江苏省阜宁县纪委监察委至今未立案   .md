[环保人士嵇书龙控告酷吏三年多，江苏省阜宁县纪委监察委至今未立案](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2022/05/blog-post_92.html)
------

<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">10</span>日，本网获悉：今天，江苏环保人士嵇书龙女士持阜宁县检察院<span lang="EN-US">2019</span>年出具的<span lang="EN-US">6</span>号转办函，到阜宁县纪委监察委信访接待办，就自己三年前在冤狱中控告对自己实施酷刑的乔绍佰与张开新两个酷吏的转办事项，追问进展。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">嵇书龙女士因为带领村民抵制阜宁县政府从苏南引进有毒化工企业，保护村民耕地和自然环境，而在<span lang="EN-US">2012</span>年被原阜宁县公安局长、副县长沈恩山以“寻衅滋事”构陷判刑两年，差一点死在监狱中。<span lang="EN-US">2017</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">2</span>日，嵇书龙女士因为继续在北京边打工边上访维权，再次遭到阜宁县公检法以寻衅滋事“口袋罪”重判<span lang="EN-US">4</span>年！<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">在嵇书龙女士第二次被构陷过程中，遭到了办案人员乔绍佰与张开新两人令人发指的刑讯逼供，两人不仅长期对嵇书龙实施残酷的迫害，而且还扣押嵇书龙家人送进来的棉衣棉裤，让嵇书龙女士整个冬天只穿看守所发的一双凉拖鞋和一条大短裤，肆无忌惮实施毫无人性的折磨。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2019</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">7</span>日，监狱中的嵇书龙女士，将控告阜宁县公安局乔绍佰与张开新对其报复陷害刑讯逼供材料，请公诉人转到阜宁检察院。同年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">11</span>日，检察院就给嵇书龙回复函，称材料已转阜宁监察委。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2021</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">18</span>日，嵇书龙女士终于活着走出了冤狱。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">嵇书龙女士说：前段时间我打<span lang="EN-US">12388</span>查询阜宁监察委的处理情况，<span lang="EN-US">12388</span>要我带身份证去阜宁县纪委查，但去纪委楼下，保安不给进大门，要我去阜宁县政府信访局，我去了信访局又因疫情关门，直到今天他们才开门接待。纪委信访的一位男的接待员让我填了表，并在电脑上都记录了，嵇书龙女士又当场专门手写了一份补充材料。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">对公安机关在刑事侦查中实施酷刑的违法犯罪行为，人民检察院本来就有监督的法定职责，而阜宁县检察院在收到嵇书龙女士的控告，后转纪委监察委处理有待商榷不说，阜宁县纪委监察委居然三年多不作为，确实匪夷所思。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体;
