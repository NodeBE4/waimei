[获刑9年的山西著名法律人士郝劲松被转监至山西省太原市第二监狱服刑](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2024/05/9.html)
------

<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">25</span>日，本网获悉：获刑<span lang="EN-US">9</span>年的山西著名法律人士郝劲松被转监至山西省太原市第二监狱服刑。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">2019</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">17</span>日遭抓捕、后被超期羁押<span lang="EN-US">3</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>个月的郝劲松于<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">20</span>日被以诈骗罪判刑<span lang="EN-US">8</span>年、寻衅滋事罪判刑<span lang="EN-US">1</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>个月，合并执行有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">9</span>年。其不服上诉，<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">12</span>日，山西忻州法院二审维持一审对山西著名法律人士郝劲松诈骗罪、寻衅滋事罪有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">9</span>年的判决。忻州法院二审没有开庭，采取书面审理形式，终审裁定中没有引述辩护人的意见。而此前郝劲松在看守所内被暴力殴打虐待，家属向有关部门持续控告。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">目前郝劲松已经被转监至山西省太原市第二监狱服刑。中共当局对郝劲松的迫害使其家人的正常生活和工作受到严重影响，其子从山西大学法学院毕业后，因受他被中共当局构陷冤案的影响，谋职遭遇很大困难。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">郝劲松：</span></b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">生于<span lang="EN-US">1972</span>年，山西忻州人。著名人权捍卫者、法律人。郝劲松自<span lang="EN-US">2004</span>年开始先后<span lang="EN-US">7</span>次将国家税务总局、北京地铁运营公司、北京铁路局告上法庭，原他因对<span lang="EN-US">“</span>打破行业<span lang="EN-US">‘</span>霸王条款<span lang="EN-US">’</span>起到了一定作用<span lang="EN-US">”</span>而入选<span lang="EN-US">“2004</span>年构建经济和谐十大受尊崇人物<span lang="EN-US">”</span>。郝劲松被先后提名为<span lang="EN-US">2005</span>中国法制新闻人物，<span lang="EN-US">2005</span>年度十大法制人物，并以<span lang="EN-US">“</span>维权战士<span lang="EN-US">”</span>的身份写入《<span lang="EN-US">2005</span>中国法治蓝皮书》。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">因介入推动铁道部春运不涨价案件入选《<span lang="EN-US">2006</span>中国法治蓝皮书》，并当选<span lang="EN-US">2006</span>中国十大消费维权人物，<span lang="EN-US">2008</span>年因介入陕西华南虎案件和杨佳案被《南方人物周刊》评选为<span lang="EN-US">“</span>中国魅力人物<span lang="EN-US">——</span>公义之魅<span lang="EN-US">”</span>。<span lang="EN-US">2009</span>年，介入上海<span lang="EN-US">“</span>钓鱼执法事件<span lang="EN-US">”</span>。后成立山西定襄县劲松法务咨询中心。<span lang="EN-US">2017</span>年，郝劲松向定襄县环保局实名举报当地的诚泰纺织印染公司涉嫌污染空气和水源。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">郝劲松于<span lang="EN-US">2019</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">10</span>日、<span lang="EN-US">11</span>日就连续两天遭到户籍地山西省定襄县晋昌镇派出所以<span lang="EN-US">“</span>寻衅滋事<span lang="EN-US">”</span>为由传唤后，<span lang="EN-US">2019</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">17</span>日再遭该派出所传唤。随后被行政拘留<span lang="EN-US">15</span>天。<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">2</span>日，行政拘留期满被转刑事拘留，控罪为涉嫌寻衅滋事。当日，郝劲松被控涉嫌寻衅滋事遭定襄县公安局刑事拘留。羁押于五台县看守所。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">案件久不审理，一直拖延至<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">12</span>至<span lang="EN-US">13</span>日在山西定襄县法院开庭审理。开庭审理一年多后，于<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">20</span>日，被超期羁押超过<span lang="EN-US">1200</span>天的著名法律公益人、法律学者郝劲松先生被以诈骗罪判刑<span lang="EN-US">8</span>年、寻衅滋事罪判刑<span lang="EN-US">1</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>个月，合并执行有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">9</span>年。其不服上诉，<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">12</span>日二审驳回上诉，维持原判。刑期至：<span lang="EN-US">2029</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">1</span>日。郝劲松现被转监至山西省太原市第二监狱服刑。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">对郝劲松的狱中境况，本网将持续关注。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"></p><div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;"><a href="https://blogger.googleusercontent.com/img/b/R29vZ2xl/AVvXsEiH8_rkWQTsDa1WhufX02OPiw6tmegaoQUwyxeji5VCLHbQTVQ-vgytKz-lDssUKzRo7hsqN5aOeckrRZFZmedWj3zcLsayPP1_QiaRdUXzasqpyR1d3YT-q1uJNXfen6RgUL_cv0oLdmE2KnYhGuo9qoUmBbda1kdF7HmX4ZGHCielOLKAYtWOK_NHbiPh/s320/EMHSzQ5UcAAQQb1.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em;
