<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">8</span>日，本网获悉：近日，在中共二十届三中全会召开之前，<a name="_Hlk171309701">湖南株洲公民</a>、民主人士程晓峰被当地警方以涉嫌寻衅滋事罪刑事拘留，羁押在湖南株洲市看守所。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">程晓峰：</span></b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">网名大卫，株洲公民圈活跃人士，被带走前其居所和茶室均遭抄家搜查取证，后其被以涉嫌寻衅滋事罪刑事拘留。据悉这次被捕的直接原因与他去娄底双峰祭拜欧阳经华、参与网络要求苏州纪念胡友平有关，也是湖南株洲当局对程晓峰多年民主异议言行算总账。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">湖南株洲民主人士欧彪峰即将刑满出狱，泼墨女董瑶琼被关精神病院仍然生死不明，徐琳、程晓峰又相继被刑拘，上述公民系湖南株洲人士。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">对程晓峰先生的境况，本网将持续关注。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"></p><div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;"><a href="https://blogger.googleusercontent.com/img/b/R29vZ2xl/AVvXsEhVGR5IXnxTxlLtG7mneScTOJIo8OP7iWAUDCqd4A_YOR1s9MH6rEr7E0DXZMkDe7edjx5_t-Wp14alaDHbm5jwvPzKWegpgguobmaoDF0mkl5DIjtgGeRKFNUCGF0knCj1n5GZ3e92HUxViz24dT0lpRb6b8Sd3MIgZSjdaubaQ6BoUSNp1xlK79XkRkek/s620/image.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;"><img border="0" data-original-height="348" data-original-width="620" height="180" src="https://blogger.googleusercontent.com/img/b/R29vZ2xl/AVvXsEhVGR5IXnxTxlLtG7mneScTOJIo8OP7iWAUDCqd4A_YOR1s9MH6rEr7E0DXZMkDe7edjx5_t-Wp14alaDHbm5jwvPzKWegpgguobmaoDF0mkl5DIjtgGeRKFNUCGF0knCj1n5GZ3e92HUxViz24dT0lpRb6b8Sd3MIgZSjdaubaQ6BoUSNp1xlK79XkRkek/s320/image.jpg" width="320" /></a></div><p></p>
