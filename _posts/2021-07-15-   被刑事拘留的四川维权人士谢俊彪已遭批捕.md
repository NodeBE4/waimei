[被刑事拘留的四川维权人士谢俊彪已遭批捕](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2021/07/blog-post_83.html)
------

<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">15</span>日，本网获悉：四川省成都市双流区怡心街道维权人士谢俊彪，于<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">7</span>日被以寻衅滋事罪刑事拘留，在<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">14</span>号已被告知批准逮捕，家属暂未收到逮捕通知书，警察说要寄给谢俊彪的父亲。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">谢俊彪因房屋遭强拆从<span lang="EN-US">2013</span>年开始上访维权，后关注“六四天网”创办人黄琦冤案，并积极参加营救活动。他因此多次被警方上门警告、传唤、拘留。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2020</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">6</span>日，他在微信群中表示自己又被政府警告，说他接受媒体采访，要重点稳控。<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">10</span>日上午，谢俊彪到四川省人大外举牌，牌子上写“戴表（代表）不为民作主 不如回家抓老鼠”。当天下午<span lang="EN-US">6</span>点多，他在成都双流的朋友家里遭社区警察上门抓捕。后谢俊彪遭当地警方刑事拘留<span lang="EN-US">30</span>天，后于<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">9</span>日，谢俊彪获取保候审。其取保期马上到期，却又遭刑事拘留。现遭批捕。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体;
