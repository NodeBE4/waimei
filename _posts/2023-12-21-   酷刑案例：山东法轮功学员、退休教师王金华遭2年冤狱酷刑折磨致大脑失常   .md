<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">22</span>日，本网获悉：山东法轮功学员、退休教师王金华遭<span lang="EN-US">2</span>年冤狱折磨致大脑失常。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">王金华：</span></b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">山东省德州市平原县法轮功学员、男，山东省德州市平原县退休教师，<span lang="EN-US">1951</span>年生。一九九七年三月，与妻子荆传风一起开始修炼法轮功。在一九九九年“七•二零”中共发动对法轮功的疯狂迫害后，王金华和家人失去了往日的安宁，王金华九次被抓捕、勒索罚款，累计金额超过十五万元。妻子荆传风三次被绑架，并多次被非法跟踪、监控。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">王金华<span lang="EN-US">2019</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月被判刑<span lang="EN-US">2</span>年，<span lang="EN-US">2019</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">10</span>日他先被关押到山东省监狱，随后转南山监狱。在那里他遭受种种酷刑折磨，被强制吃药打针、做大脑强化<span lang="EN-US">CT</span>后，大脑中经常发出一种难听的声音，直到现在，不能睡觉，时常迷糊、胡思乱想、胸闷腹胀、说话不清楚。<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">9</span>日，王金华被释放出狱后，继续遭受迫害。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p>
