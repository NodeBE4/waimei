[河北平山县官员用“滚”回应民众短信被免职](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20211025-1206688)
------

<div>
<p>中共河北石家庄平山县委常委、政法委书记尹惠强因用“滚”回应民众短信，而被免职。</p><p>据石家庄新闻网报道，石家庄市委联合调查组前天（23日）在通报中，批评尹惠强用简单粗暴方式回复信息，“造成不良影响，反映其思想作风不实，自身修养不够。”调查组发现，尹惠强的手机上个月12日收到民众手机发送的短信，显示状态为“我给你发了长文本+网页链接”，尹惠强未打开该短信链接，未查看链接内容，就进行了回复。</p><p>石家庄市委因而决定，对尹惠强进行诫勉，并免去尹惠强平山县委常委、政法委书记的职务。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>短信内容截图在尹惠强回复不久后于网上流传，平山官方后来通报，尹惠强收到陌生人手机短信后，只看到一个网址链接，误以为是电信诈骗信息，于是进行了不当回复。尹惠强之后被停职。</p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/zbpe-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=zbpe399&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_desktop-zbpe399_2.png?dHEiGouxxZpWwWTJE04dyGyUN74JOU2z" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_desktop-zbpe399_2.png?dHEiGouxxZpWwWTJE04dyGyUN74JOU2z" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/zbpe-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=zbpe399&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_mobile-zbpe399_2.png?qZnBcIGCJvPR8A2IAZOZuOxdoGZDatiI" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_mobile-zbpe399_2.png?qZnBcIGCJvPR8A2IAZOZuOxdoGZDatiI" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
