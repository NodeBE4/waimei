mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">14</span>日，本网获悉：甘肃徽县民主维权人士龙克海<span lang="EN-US"> 2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">30</span>日因关注常玮平案而被以涉嫌寻衅滋事罪被刑事拘留。<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">28</span>日甘徽县法院以犯寻衅滋事罪判龙克海有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">2</span>年， 刑期至<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">29</span>日。目前龙克海被羁押在甘肃徽县看守所。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">：<span lang="EN-US">1966</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">1</span>日出生，又名龙刻海，甘肃省徽县嘉陵镇嘉陵村人，基督徒，陕西省宝鸡市长驻居民，民主维权公民，政治犯。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体;
