[郑州中原区政府随意撤销协议丧失公信力，宋金梅积极维权举报中原区政府违法行为](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2021/09/blog-post_38.html)
------

<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">10</span>日，本网获悉：<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">9</span>日，中原区柿园村拆迁安置工作指挥部向村民宋金梅送达《撤销协议通知书》，称“自本通知作出之日起，撤销我指挥部与你签订的动<span lang="EN-US">7-266</span>号《拆迁补偿安置协议》。” 并要求宋金梅将原协议交回指挥部，重新签订协议。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">宋金梅认为，该指挥部系中原区政府设立的临时机构。其行为应当视为中原区政府的行为。中原区政府随意撤销协议，无异于单方面撕毁行政协议，自丧公信力。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">中原区政府单方面决定，自本通知作出之日起撤销该协议，于理不通，于法无据。既然，中原区政府已经丧失公信力，毫无诚信可言，宋金梅就没有义务与中原区政府重新签订协议。中原区政府应当赔偿宋金梅房屋损失或者恢复原状。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">当初，宋金梅之所以愿意用<span lang="EN-US">789</span>平方米的自建房屋置换中原区政府<span lang="EN-US">396</span>平方米的商品房屋，并签订拆迁补偿协议，是基于对中原区政府的信任。但中原区政府随意撕毁协议，自丧公信力，侵害了宋金梅对政府的信赖利益。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">中原区政府认为过渡费计算有误，就应当与宋金梅核对流水账，多退少补，而不是随意撤销协议。既然中原区政府单方面决定撤销协议，就无权强制宋金梅重新签订拆迁安置协议。事实上，中原区政府支付的过渡费不是多了，而是少了。自<span lang="EN-US">2018</span>年起，郑州市政府制定的拆迁过渡费基数为建筑面积每月每平方米为<span lang="EN-US">30</span>元，而中原区政府却仍然按每月每平方米为<span lang="EN-US">8</span>元的过渡费基数发放。两者相差甚远！<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">中原区政府在柿园村的拆迁经历八年之久，仍然有约<span lang="EN-US">700</span>户农民拒绝签订拆迁安置协议。中原区政府指责村民不配合拆迁；而村民则认为中原区政府的拆迁不得人心，否则不会有那么村民拒绝搬迁。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">中原区政府撤销拆迁协议的另一条理由，更为荒唐。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">中原区政府认为，宋金梅以胁迫的手段，逼指挥部签订拆迁协议。这纯属天方夜谭。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">首先，在拆迁过程中，拆迁指挥部常常采取卑鄙手段逼迫村民搬迁。村民恐惧不已，怎么可能逼迫指挥部签订拆迁补偿安置协议呢？<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">其次，退一步说，即使宋金梅逼迫指挥部签订协议。指挥部也可以单方面撤销协议。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">最令人疑惑不解的是，既然是宋金梅逼迫指挥部签订协议，那么，指挥部为什么还要令宋金梅重新签订《拆迁补偿安置协议》呢？<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">中原区政府在拆迁过程中，还有一系列的违法行为：<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">1</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">、未完成拆迁，就建设安置房，违反了未经拆迁不得施工的规定。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">、未完成土地征收，大规模地建设安置房，属于违法占地。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">3</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">、未取得施工许可证而施工，违反了《建筑法》第三条规定，依法应当给予处罚。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">4</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">、在合法拆迁中，也应当在<span lang="EN-US">2016</span>年底完成。中原区政府违法拆迁八年未建成安置房，更应当问责。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">宋金梅将举报中原区政府上述违法行为，积极维护自己的合法权益。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p>
