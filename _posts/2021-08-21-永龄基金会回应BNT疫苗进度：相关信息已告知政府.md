[永龄基金会回应BNT疫苗进度：相关信息已告知政府](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210821-1185105)
------

<div>
<p>鸿海创办人郭台铭与台积电购捐政府1000万剂BNT疫苗，郭台铭日前去欧洲了解出货情形。鸿海永龄基金会执行长刘宥彤今天表示，郭台铭将于今天结束此次欧洲行程，预计于台湾时间明晚回，并称此次欧洲行公私兼顾，“郭台铭先生尽力了”。</p><p>根据联合新闻网报道，刘宥彤说，此次欧洲之行见到的朋友们都很温暖也很帮忙，谢谢社会大众给予的支持与鼓励。 </p><p>对于各界关心的采购疫苗到货进展，或是疫苗取得需要的协助与困难排除，刘宥彤说，相关信息都已告知政府单位，不论是时间表或进一步有利于大众的决策，尊重指挥中心的说明。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>台湾驻德代表谢志伟今天在面簿发文引述德国官员来讯称，刚获得好消息，BioNTech供货给台湾的进度有很正面的发展。</p><p>前往欧洲催货BNT疫苗的郭台铭此前外传带回好消息，首批疫苗到货量预估在100万至120万剂。</p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
