[获刑4年6个月湖南人权捍卫者谢文飞 (本名谢丰夏）将于2024年10月30日刑满](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2024/10/46-20241030.html)
------

<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">26</span>日年，本网获悉：获刑<span lang="EN-US">4</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月的湖南人权捍卫者谢文飞将于<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">30</span>日刑满。谢文飞先生作为南方街头运动的践行者为民主自由勇敢呼号，两次获刑，均是<span lang="EN-US">4</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月，被中共当局关进在牢狱中长达<span lang="EN-US">9</span>年。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">谢文飞简介：</span></b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">本名谢丰夏。<span lang="EN-US"> 1977</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">7</span>日出生，湖南省郴州市桂阳县人，网名“谢文飞”、“南街谢文飞”、“南街谢文飞<span lang="EN-US">@njxwf </span>”，人权捍卫者，<a name="_Hlk178991000">南方街头运动践行者</a>，政治犯。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">近年来，因经常活跃于广州街头，勇于为推动中国的民主宪政事业积极发声和献身，故而遭到广东当局的多次打压和拘留；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">2014</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">8</span>日，曾因前往围观广州公民利瓦伊国申请“六四游行”被拘提起诉讼案开庭，而被广东省佛山市警方以涉嫌“寻衅滋事罪”刑拘，关押于佛山市禅诚区看守所，事由是“纪念林昭和曹顺利”；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">2014</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">26</span>日，曾因与民主维权公民王福磊、王默等在广州举牌，发起寻找民主异见人士张圣雨的活动，被石围派出所警方带走，且被行政拘留<span lang="EN-US">7</span>日；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">2014</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">3</span>日，曾因穿着黑衫在广州市街头拉横幅声援香港民主抗争、“真普选”，被广州警方从增城区新塘镇住所再次带走，并以涉嫌“寻衅滋事罪”刑事拘留；审讯期间，遭酷刑折磨，被广州市越秀看守所警方实施“定镣”处罚，时间长达<span lang="EN-US">104</span>个小时；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">2014</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月，被广州市检察院更换罪名以涉嫌“煽动颠覆国家政权罪”正式批捕，并被转关至广州市第一看守所；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">2015</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">19</span>日，其案正式开庭受审；<span lang="EN-US">2016</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">8</span>日，被广州市中级法院以“煽动颠覆国家政权罪”判处有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">4</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月，剥夺政治权利<span lang="EN-US">3</span>年；<span lang="EN-US">2019</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">1</span>日，刑满释放。此前在广东省河源监狱服刑。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">谢文飞于<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">29</span>日晚上在苏仙区租房处被郴州警方强制带走，次日苏仙区公安分局以涉嫌寻衅滋事为由将其刑事拘留，<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">13</span>日其被办案单位苏仙区公安分局移送苏仙区检察院审查起诉，后检察院以谢文飞“涉嫌寻衅滋事”罪向苏仙区法院起诉。谢文飞羁押在郴州市下辖的资兴市看守所。<span lang="EN-US"> 2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">15</span>日开庭。一直未宣判。<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月宣判，谢文飞获刑<span lang="EN-US">4</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月。目前谢文飞<span lang="EN-US"> (</span>本名谢丰夏）关押在长沙监狱服刑。刑期至<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">30</span>日。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体;
