[山东维权人士赵长慧（网名赵未）目前被羁押在聊城市看守所](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2024/11/blog-post_65.html)
------

<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">29</span>日，本网获悉：山东维权人士赵长慧（网民赵未）今年六月去北京被其街道主任拦截，发生冲突，于<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">14</span>日遭刑事拘留、后于<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">28</span>日被批捕，羁押于聊城看守所。<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">9</span>日被以涉嫌寻衅滋事罪起诉于<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">16</span>日进行了庭审，未当庭宣判。目前赵长慧被关押在山东省聊城市看守所，电话：<span lang="EN-US">0635-7170486</span>，邮政编码：<span lang="EN-US">252001。</span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><b><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;">赵长慧：</span></b><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;">网名赵未。男，汉族，<span lang="EN-US">1966</span>年生，家住山东省聊城市东昌府区。其曾因言获罪，于<span lang="EN-US">2018</span>年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">30</span>被控煽动颠覆国家政权罪抓捕，被手铐脚镣铁椅，监视居住六个月。后于<span lang="EN-US">2019</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">29</span>日送聊城看守所，<span lang="EN-US">2019</span>年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">7</span>日开庭，后又被以寻衅滋事罪判刑<span lang="EN-US">2</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月，指控其<span lang="EN-US">2011</span>年来网络言论危害国家安全，于<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">29</span>日刑满出狱。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;">对赵长慧案的进展，本网将持续关注。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"></p><div class="separator" style="clear: both;
