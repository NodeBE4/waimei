[吴有水《被偷走的辩护权》自序：“我的妻子，我的儿！”](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2021/07/blog-post_99.html)
------

<div>
<p></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span></span></p><a name='more'></a><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;"><br /></span><p></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">半夜醒来，屋子里一片寂静。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">偶尔地，从厨房里会传来咕嘟咕嘟的声音，那是我妻子生前所养的两只别人不要的乌龟，在塑料桶里，想追求自由，往外爬。<span lang="EN-US"><span style="mso-spacerun: yes;">&nbsp;&nbsp; </span><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">睡不着了，拿起手机，看一下时间，才凌晨两点多。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">失眠也很准时，这大概就是所谓的生物钟了吧。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">起来，打开灯。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">不见我家的狗狗帅帅跑过来。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">往常，它总会一听到我房间里有动静，就会无声地跑过来，凑到我的床前，用它那冰冰凉的鼻子，朝我的脸上嗅。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">哦，它已经失踪了，或许，此刻，它正饿着肚子，浑身脏兮兮地踡在街头的哪个角落里呢。我妻子病后，因为我没时间照顾它，就让我的外甥女带回家去帮我照看了。后来，又送到她姐姐的厂里，结果因为有领导来视察，说是食品厂里不得养狗。于是，我的大外甥女又把它送到了武警中队的一家人那里，结果，就连夜失踪了。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">点起一支烟，在嘴里含着，但并不想抽。只是觉得，既然醒来了，总得做点什么，打发无聊。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">这时，心里总有点孤独的感觉。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">想起几年前，我家也是很热闹的：我有妻子、有儿子、我儿子还有女朋友。还有两条狗：小丑和帅帅，两只乌龟、一只鹦鹦和一只猫。猫盯着鹦鹉，鹦鹉啾啾地求援，两只狗会飞快地跑来，把小猫追得床上床底乱窜，狗儿们也在床上床下乱跳。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">我妻子不让狗上床，狗狗偏要上床，于是就直呼我帮忙把狗狗从床上赶下来，我的儿子和他的女朋友则是各拿一根小棍，到处找那只早不知道躲到哪儿去的起祸端的猫。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">现在，家里只有我和两只乌龟了。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">去年的<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月，我写了一系列的文章纪念我儿子被抓一周年。这些文章只是发布在我个人的微博和微信公众号上。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">结果，被删除得七凌八乱，最后干脆把我的不知道是第几个的微博账号给封了，后来，干脆就发不出来了。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">我就也没有再写下去。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">今年，施明磊说：吴有水，我想把你之前写的与你儿子的文章，整理成一本书。我一想，这也是个办法，就答应了。正好把妻子的去世也写进来，也算是对我妻子的一个纪念吧。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">于是，我在之前文章的基础上，进行了一番整理，再补充写上一些我妻子去世的内容，就成了这本书。</span></p><p class="MsoNormal"></p><div class="separator" style="clear: both;
