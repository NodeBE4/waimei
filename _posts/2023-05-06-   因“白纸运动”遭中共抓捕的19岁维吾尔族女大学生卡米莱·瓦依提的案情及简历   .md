mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月，曾因与在美国工作的兄长、维吾尔族工程师卡吾萨尔·瓦伊提通讯，聊天中告知其在<span lang="EN-US">TikTok</span>上发过一则有关乌鲁木齐火灾海外白纸抗议视频的讯息，故而其父亲很快接到新疆当地警方的来电警告，遂其将所发帖子和视频删除，但此一举动仍然引起当局的极度不满；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2022</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast;
