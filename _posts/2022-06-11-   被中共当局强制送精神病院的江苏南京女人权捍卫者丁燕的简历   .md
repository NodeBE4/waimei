[被中共当局强制送精神病院的江苏南京女人权捍卫者丁燕的简历](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2022/06/blog-post_11.html)
------

<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">11</span>日，本网获悉被中共当局精神病的江苏南京女人权捍卫者丁燕的简历，现在通报如下：<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">丁燕：</span></b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">女，<span lang="EN-US">1983</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">15</span>日出生<span lang="EN-US">, </span>江苏省南京市六合区人，网名“丁燕小美”，餐饮业界经营者，南京市春爱燕沪江烧烤店负责人，网络活跃人士，公共事务关注者，人称“泼墨女孩翻版”，被精神病受害者，人权捍卫者，中国在押政治犯。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">早年曾在上海打拼，从小餐厅起步，经多年奋斗，<span lang="EN-US">2013</span>年先开办了南京市六合区沪江烧烤冠城店，<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年又重新开办了春爱燕沪江烧烤店，自此成为当地业界的成功人士；然而，其在人生的奋斗中，不仅为谋求生计而努力，也为社会不公而发声；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">近年来，因具有强烈的正义感和社会责任感，曾多次为弱势群体和各种社会舆论事件而积极发声，曾多次参与公民运动等活动，信奉“不自由，毋宁死”，追求“感恩的心”，期盼“世界和平和国泰民安”，遂遭当局极端不满和欲加强力打压；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">曾因热衷于关注公共事务而十数次被抓，并强制送精神病医院拘押；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2022</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">19</span>日，因敢于为上海封城而向中共最高领导人习近平写公开信，促其聆听民情，而被南京市地方政府第<span lang="EN-US">11</span>次强制送进精神病院关押，<span lang="EN-US">10</span>天后才被释放；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">同年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">11</span>日，因其将致习近平的公开信发在微信群里，称“疫情短短三年，把我们打回旧社会”，遂于当晚即被南京市六合区警方和街道办事处领导连手将其带走，再次（第<span lang="EN-US">12</span>次）送进南京六合怡宁精神病院关押；后虽然答应将其<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">1</span>日释放回家，但至今失联，杳无音讯；友人疑指很可能已被秘押或刑拘，罪名暂不明晰。目前被羁押地点不详。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">对丁燕女士的境况，本网将持续关注。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"></p><div class="separator" style="clear: both;
