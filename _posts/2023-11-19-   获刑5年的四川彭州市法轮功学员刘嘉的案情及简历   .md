<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">19</span>日，本网获悉获刑<span lang="EN-US">5</span>年的四川彭州市法轮功学员刘嘉的案情及简历。现在通报如下：<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">刘嘉：四川彭州市法轮功学员，一九六九年生，曾任中国平安保险有限公司成都寿险分公司第三营业部资深经理。一九九五年开始修炼法轮功。一九九九年七月中共疯狂迫害法轮功后，刘嘉曾经多次遭受惨无人道的迫害：<span lang="EN-US">2002</span>年因上访被上海劳教局劳教<span lang="EN-US">2</span>年；<span lang="EN-US">2007</span>年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">13</span>日被抓捕，后被四川成都市武侯区法院判刑<span lang="EN-US">5</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月，刑至<span lang="EN-US">2013</span>年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">12</span>日。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2022</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">6</span>日早上刘嘉开车到彭州办事，被彭州市公安局刑侦大队彭州市军乐派出所抓捕，后抄家。刘嘉当天被关押到彭州市“都市云端酒店”四楼，宣布对其指定居所监视居住；住的房间有监控，窗户是全封闭，几个人轮流看守。关押期间对其残暴殴打、酷刑。四川彭州法院在<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">30</span>日对法轮功学员刘嘉判刑<span lang="EN-US">5</span>年。刑期至：<span lang="EN-US">2027</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p>
