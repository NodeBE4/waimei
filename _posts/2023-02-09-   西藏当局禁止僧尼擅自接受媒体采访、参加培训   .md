<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">9</span>日，本网西藏消息：自境内藏区发生自焚抗议事件后<span lang="EN-US">,</span>中国当局对西藏自治区僧尼的行动管控更加严厉。</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">自</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">2016</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">年起</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">,</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">当局规定西藏自治区的僧尼未经批准不得通过信息网络、广播、电视、报刊、书籍、讲座、论坛、报告会、座谈会等形式</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">,</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">发表妄议中央和西藏自治区大政方针的言论</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">,</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">不得擅自以团体或内设机构的名义发表言论</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">,</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">接受采访</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">,</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">开展活动</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">,</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">参加论坛和研讨会。</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">此外<span lang="EN-US">,</span>西藏当局更加限制西藏自治区僧尼出国<span lang="EN-US">(</span>境<span lang="EN-US">)</span>访问活动，要求如下：“未经批准<span lang="EN-US">,</span>不得擅自会见国<span lang="EN-US">(</span>境<span lang="EN-US">)</span>外宗教团体的人员<span lang="EN-US">,</span>延长访问时间<span lang="EN-US">,</span>增加访问地点<span lang="EN-US">,</span>不得与国<span lang="EN-US">(</span>境<span lang="EN-US">)</span>外组织擅自签订协议<span lang="EN-US">,</span>声明等。参加境外培训和学习要按规定报批<span lang="EN-US">,</span>不得擅自参加。”<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p>
