[台军方开课反制大陆“认知战” 将纳基层训练](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20211014-1203213)
------

<div>
<p>台湾国防部昨日送至台湾立院的书面报告显示，台湾为反制大陆“认知战”，今年7月开办认知战与识读能力相关课程，并于明年纳入基层部队政治作战训练内涵。</p><p>据《自由时报》报道，台湾立法院外交及国防委员会邀请台湾国防部长邱国正，就整体战力精进作为进行专案报告并备询。</p><p>台湾国防部报告统计，大陆军机今年度进入防空识别区已逾600架次，面对大陆各种军事威胁，台湾通过联合情监侦系统，严密监控大陆军事活动，并通过情资交换及早获知敌情。另据统计，大陆本月迄今已派153架次军机。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>台湾国防部表示，将整合三军雷、截情与影像情报，优化共同作战图像，使各部队同步掌握即时情资与动态。另也建立国防自主能量，积极筹获机动备援系统，强化各项监侦手段。</p><p>因应大陆军舰军机施压，台湾国防部表示，除持续完备“经常战备时期突发状况处置规定”及相关应处程序，也秉持“愈接近本岛、反制力愈强”作为，采多重复式手段妥慎应处，增进用兵弹性与兵力节约，并降低肇生事端，引发冲突风险。</p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/zbpe-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=zbpe399&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_desktop-zbpe399_2.png?dHEiGouxxZpWwWTJE04dyGyUN74JOU2z" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_desktop-zbpe399_2.png?dHEiGouxxZpWwWTJE04dyGyUN74JOU2z" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/zbpe-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=zbpe399&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_mobile-zbpe399_2.png?qZnBcIGCJvPR8A2IAZOZuOxdoGZDatiI" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_mobile-zbpe399_2.png?qZnBcIGCJvPR8A2IAZOZuOxdoGZDatiI" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
