[香港疑似发生烧国旗事件](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20211002-1199358)
------

<div>
<p>香港警方昨天（10月1日）清晨6时许接报，在黄大仙横头磡村宏安楼对面的富美街，有中国国旗着火焚烧。</p><p>据《明报》报道，警员到场发现地上遗有红色布碎灰烬，并发现附近一幅庆祝国庆横额被拆毁，附近宏安楼走廊外墙挂有多面国旗，疑挂在低层的其中一面国旗不翼而飞。警方正调查是否有关。</p><p>警方回应媒体询问时称，早上接获市民报案，发现宏安楼对面行人道有20面国旗跌落地面，部分国旗有烧过的痕迹。人员接报到场，经初步调查后，警方检走相关证物作进一步调查。案件列作侮辱国旗处理，交由东九龙总区重案组跟进，暂时未有人被捕。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>昨天是中华人民共和国成立72周年华诞，香港多处举行庆祝仪式。</p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/zbpe-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=zbpe399&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_desktop-zbpe399_2.png?dHEiGouxxZpWwWTJE04dyGyUN74JOU2z" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_desktop-zbpe399_2.png?dHEiGouxxZpWwWTJE04dyGyUN74JOU2z" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/zbpe-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=zbpe399&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_mobile-zbpe399_2.png?qZnBcIGCJvPR8A2IAZOZuOxdoGZDatiI" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_mobile-zbpe399_2.png?qZnBcIGCJvPR8A2IAZOZuOxdoGZDatiI" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
