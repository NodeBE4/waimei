[苏州苏锦街道发生气枪射击窗户实施逼迁，七旬老太周金丹再次向公安机关申请保护](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2021/08/blog-post_22.html)
------

<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">22</span>日，本网获悉：周金丹老太今年<span lang="EN-US">75</span>周岁，系独居老人。其房屋位于苏州市姑苏区花锦村<span lang="EN-US">430</span>号，是集体土地上的房屋。时常一伙不明身份的人打着拆迁的旗号，对周金丹老太实施逼迁，不断骚扰。但周金丹老太不屈服，坚持维权十三年之久，曾因围观、维权等原因被关押<span lang="EN-US">103</span>天。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2021</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">16</span>日，周金丹老太向苏锦派出所、姑苏公安分局、苏州市公安局、江苏省公安厅、国家公安部邮寄《紧急申请保护公民人身财产保护书》，请求查处以枪击窗户实施逼迁的违法行为。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2021</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">12</span>日上午<span lang="EN-US">8</span>点，周金丹老太发现上述房间的玻璃窗被气枪击穿一个洞，明显属于恐吓。周金丹老太难以忍受，当即报警。这是一个月内的第三次报警。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">在此之前，即今年七月份以来，一直有不明身份的社会闲杂人员到周金丹的家寻衅滋事，还自称是苏锦街道派来的，有政府文件，对周金丹进行驱赶，扬言要拆周金丹老太的房屋。周金丹老太于<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">19</span>日、<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">7</span>日两次报警，请求公安机关依法立案予以查处。但当地公安机关对周老太的报警置若罔闻，听之任之。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">拆迁方威逼周金丹搬迁，但从苏州市住建局于<span lang="EN-US">2008</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">23</span>日颁发的苏建拆许字（<span lang="EN-US">2008</span>）第<span lang="EN-US">2</span>号拆迁许可证看，周老太的房屋并不在该拆迁许可证的拆迁范围内。周金丹老太认为，自己的房屋不在拆迁范围内，任何人无权要求她搬迁，也无权威逼其签订拆迁补偿安置协议。周金丹老太理直气壮地拒绝搬迁。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">但拆迁方并不善罢甘休，竟然明目张胆勾结黑恶势力，雇佣纹身、戴大金链的黑社会人员上门寻衅滋事，实施威胁、恐吓、驱赶等不法手段进行逼迁。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">周金丹老太希望公安机关依法查处拆迁中的暴力行为，维护法律尊严，维护公民的合法权益。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体;
