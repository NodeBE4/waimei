[中共外事侨务部门在比利时秘密成立欧洲藏族同乡会分化瓦解旅欧藏胞社会](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2023/02/blog-post_6.html)
------

<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">6</span>日，本网西藏消息<span lang="EN-US">:</span>自<span lang="EN-US">2009</span>年起<span lang="EN-US">,</span>全藏地发生接连不断的自焚事件后<span lang="EN-US">,</span>中共当局于<span lang="EN-US">2012</span>年开始在西藏自治区成立外事侨务办公室<span lang="EN-US">,</span>将流亡藏人定义为所谓的“国外藏族华人华侨”<span lang="EN-US">,</span>接手原属于统战部管理的“对国外藏胞工作”。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">除了已经被曝光的北美藏族同乡会<span lang="EN-US">,</span>当局近年也在比利时秘密成立欧洲藏族同乡会<span lang="EN-US">,</span>负责对旅居欧洲的藏胞开展分化<span lang="EN-US">,</span>腐蚀和渗透工作<span lang="EN-US">,</span>并接待中共国安部特工人员<span lang="EN-US">,</span>对旅居欧洲的藏胞开展秘密监视和威胁<span lang="EN-US">,</span>也负责控制持中国护照<span lang="EN-US">,</span>在欧洲留学的藏族留学生。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">中共当局早于<span lang="EN-US">2001</span>年召开的第四次西藏工作座谈会上<span lang="EN-US">,</span>提出加强对国外藏胞二、三代的统战工作。此后<span lang="EN-US">,</span>中共某研究涉藏事务的学者于<span lang="EN-US">2006</span>年提出报告称“不要在达赖集团反动宣传前吃哑巴亏<span lang="EN-US">,</span>要敢于到国外藏胞居住国向藏独势力开展面对面的交流和斗争”。对此旅居海外的藏族同胞要防备小心。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p>
