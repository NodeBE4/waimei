<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">7</span>日，本网获悉：<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月在北京被抓捕的河南维权人士聂丽娜被关押<span lang="EN-US">16</span>个月后，于<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">27</span>日上午在许昌市看守所被鄢陵县法院秘密宣判——聂丽娜被以寻衅滋事罪判刑<span lang="EN-US">4</span>年，刑期至<span lang="EN-US">2025</span>年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">21</span>日。<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">27</span>日当天法院直接将判决书送到许昌市看守所，通过看守所交给聂丽娜，但聂丽娜拒绝签字。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">聂丽娜：河南访民。聂丽娜在<span lang="EN-US">2005</span>年开始举报村委支书师海法、村主任郭安祥等倒卖土地，侵害其宅基地的不法行为，期间遭到无数次的打击报复，及判刑迫害，其多位家人也同样遭到地方政府株连九族方式的打击报复，及黑社会的暴力伤害。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2021</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">8</span>日上午<span lang="EN-US">8</span>点，聂丽娜到国家信访局和中纪委信访举报，遭到北京警察<span lang="EN-US">(</span>警号<span lang="EN-US">023359)</span>阻拦，聂丽娜就拨打<span lang="EN-US">12345</span>市长热线和北京警务督察电话，被强制带走后交给举报人带回许昌市襄城县关押黑监狱，十五天后转刑事拘留，后被批捕起诉。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2023</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;
