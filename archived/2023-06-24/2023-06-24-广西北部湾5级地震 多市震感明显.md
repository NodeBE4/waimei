[广西北部湾5级地震 多市震感明显](https://www.epochtimes.com/gb/23/6/24/n14021934.htm)
------

<div>
<p>【大纪元2023年06月24日讯】6月24日凌晨，<a href="https://www.epochtimes.com/gb/tag/%E5%B9%BF%E8%A5%BF%E5%8C%97%E9%83%A8%E6%B9%BE.html">广西北部湾</a>发生5.0级<a href="https://www.epochtimes.com/gb/tag/%E5%9C%B0%E9%9C%87.html">地震</a>，多市震感明显 ，震区内3小时内共发生26次<a href="https://www.epochtimes.com/gb/tag/%E4%BD%99%E9%9C%87.html">余震</a>。</p><p>中国<a href="https://www.epochtimes.com/gb/tag/%E5%9C%B0%E9%9C%87.html">地震</a>台网测定，24日凌晨3时7分，在北部湾发生5.0级地震，震源深度20公里。</p><p>这次地震距离涠洲岛38公里，距离北海市85公里，距离南宁市245公里。</p><p>北海市、防城港市、钦州市、玉林市、南宁市，广东湛江市、茂名市，海南海口市均震感明显。</p><p>截至24日6时，震区共发生<a href="https://www.epochtimes.com/gb/tag/%E4%BD%99%E9%9C%87.html">余震</a>26次，其中0.9级以下20次，1.0-1.9级4次，2.0-3.0级2次。</p><p>最大余震为4时19分，北部湾（北纬20.76度，东经109.12度）2.5级地震，震源深度10公里。</p><p>网民纷纷热议，震感明显，睡梦中被摇醒。</p><figure id="attachment_14021940" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14021940" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14021940-7dc656c0e8348d137408f99029ed7331.png"><img class="size-large wp-image-14021940" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14021940-7dc656c0e8348d137408f99029ed7331-600x733.png" alt="" width="600" height="733" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14021940" class="wp-caption-text">（网页截图）</figcaption></figure><figure id="attachment_14021937" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14021937" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14021937-97c3869562fdedf8ede4d7103333bdc3.png"><img class="size-large wp-image-14021937" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/06/id14021937-97c3869562fdedf8ede4d7103333bdc3-600x415.png" alt="" width="600" height="415" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14021937" class="wp-caption-text">（网页截图）</figcaption></figure><p>责任编辑：李韵</p><div id="gtx-trans" style="position: absolute; left: 78px; top: 312px;"></div>
