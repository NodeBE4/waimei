[遭刑事拘留的北京维权人士叶国柱的案情与简历](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2022/08/blog-post_28.html)

<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">22</span>日，本网获悉遭刑事拘留的北京维权人士叶国柱的案情与简历，现通报如下：<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">叶国柱</span></b><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">：<span lang="EN-US">1955</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">30</span>日出生；北京市人，北京市宣武区光明里<span lang="EN-US">9</span>号楼<span lang="EN-US">7</span>号公民，满族，自由职业者，遭强拆受害者，维权公民，中国在押维权人士。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2003</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月，曾因其叶家（含其父母、其弟及其本人三户）房屋被政府为举办<span lang="EN-US">2008</span>年奥运会而以危房改造名义违法强拆，遂其全家维权抗争遭严打逼迫；期间，其年老父母被打伤，三户家人财产均不知去向，政府对其全家（三户）住房安置、拆迁补偿等承诺不予兑现等，终致其全家（三户）人无家可归，被迫沦落到白天上访、乞讨，夜间露宿街头的境地；然而纵使严遭逼迫，其及全家人仍然坚持维权投诉讨公道；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2003</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">3</span>日和<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">7</span>日，曾因与其弟、其儿子身穿“状衣”在使馆区游行控诉，而被执勤武警送到当地派出所；同年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">5</span>日和<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">11</span>日，又因在北京中纪委机关门前及天桥办事处院内维权控诉，而被警方以“扰乱社会公共秩序”为由先后两次处治安拘留<span lang="EN-US">15</span>天；同年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">25</span>日，再次被所在辖区警方以涉嫌“扰乱社会公共秩序罪”抓走刑拘，半个月后被取保释放；<span lang="EN-US">2004</span>年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">27</span>日，曾因申请“<span lang="EN-US">9.18</span>”万人大游行，而被北京市东城区警方以涉嫌“寻衅滋事罪”刑拘；后又被东城区检察院批捕和起诉，控其在<span lang="EN-US">2004</span>年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">-4</span>月，曾与其弟等家人多次在北京最高检察院门前上访，并用“手拿状纸、喊口号、打横幅、穿状衣等方式，引起路人围观，造成公共场所秩序严重混乱”，故其行为妨害了社会管理秩序，已构成寻衅滋事罪；同年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">18</span>日，其被北京市东城区法院以同罪名判刑<span lang="EN-US">4</span>年，后送往天津市茶店站潮北监狱服刑；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2022</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast;
