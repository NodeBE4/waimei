[黑龙江72岁老冤民刘杰获免于起诉后提国家赔偿遭威胁](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2022/08/72.html)
------

<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">22</span>日，本网获悉：今年已经<span lang="EN-US">72</span>岁的老冤民刘杰，因为拦截国家信访局局长李文章的车辆，被黑龙江宾县公安局以“涉嫌寻衅滋事罪”拘留，然后由宾县检察院批捕。在被关押了<span lang="EN-US">278</span>天后获免于起诉而释放，当刘杰提起国家赔偿后，遭到宾县检察院控申科科长谷献文的威胁。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">刘杰是因为在<span lang="EN-US">1998</span>年做生意时，其经销的<span lang="EN-US">70</span>余万商品被宾县检察院扣押拒不返回、且销毁案件的案卷材料而上访。<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">27</span>日，刘杰在北京时，哈尔滨市宾县政府的领导多次给刘杰打电话，说别去国家信访局登记了，回来就给解决问题，还给刘杰买了机票回宾县。当刘杰回宾县后，政法委书记刘宇峰确实组织了听证会，但提出给予<span lang="EN-US">20</span>万困难补助，一次性解决刘杰的诉求，刘杰拒绝了这样明显不合理的解决方案。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2021</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">18</span>日上午<span lang="EN-US">8</span>点<span lang="EN-US">40</span>分，刘杰因对由政法委书记刘宇峰组织的听证结果表达不满，被宾县公安局以辱骂了政法委书记刘宇峰为由行政拘留<span lang="EN-US">15</span>天。在同年“七一”建党百年前夕，宾县公安局曾以刘杰在今年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">12</span>日到检察院辱骂工作人员为由，又以“寻衅滋事”为由对刘杰行政拘留<span lang="EN-US">7</span>天。同年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">2</span>日，刘杰再次去北京上访，在北京拦截了国家信访局大局长李文章的车，被宾县公安局以“寻衅滋事”为由行政拘留<span lang="EN-US">15</span>天。<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">2</span>日，刘杰被转为刑事拘留，并由被举报单位的宾县检察院批准逮捕。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2022</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">26</span>日，刘杰在被关押了<span lang="EN-US">278</span>天，宾县检察院以“退回宾县公安局两次补充侦查，证据不足、免于起诉”而获得释放。同年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">1</span>日，刘杰向宾县检察院提交了国家赔偿申请书，宾县检察院也接受了国家赔偿的材料，但两个多月过去了，宾县检察院一直采取不作为态度，而且还对其“犯罪行为”威胁要进行补充侦查。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">为了维护自己的合法权益，这几天<span lang="EN-US">72</span>岁的刘杰一直在跑上级的哈尔滨市检察院，希望上级检察院能敦促宾县检察院依法给予赔偿。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">刘杰电话：<span lang="EN-US">13766842223</span>。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"></p><div class="separator" style="clear: both;
