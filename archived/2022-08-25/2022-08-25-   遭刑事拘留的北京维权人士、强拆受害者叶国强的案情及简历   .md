<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: major-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: major-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: major-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">25</span>日，本网获悉遭刑事拘留的北京维权人士、强拆受害者叶国强的案情及简历，现在通报如下：<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: major-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: major-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: major-fareast;">叶国强</span></b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: major-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: major-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: major-fareast;">：<span lang="EN-US">1960</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">28</span>日出生，北京市人，北京市宣武区光明里<span lang="EN-US">9</span>号楼<span lang="EN-US">7</span>号公民，满族，基督徒，北京市宣武区餐饮经营者，遭强拆受害者，残障维权公民，人权捍卫者，中国在押维权人士。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: major-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: major-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: major-fareast;">2003</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: major-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: major-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: major-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月，曾因叶家（含其父母、其兄长及其本人三户）私房及全家原本在北京宣武区经营的两处餐饮店，被政府为举办<span lang="EN-US">2008</span>年奥运会而以危房改造名义违法强拆，遂其全家维权抗争遭严打；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: major-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: major-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: major-fareast;">2003</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: major-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: major-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: major-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">5</span>日、<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">8</span>日、<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">18</span>日，曾因与其兄叶国柱等家人多次在北京最高检察院门前，用手拿状纸、喊口号、打横幅、穿状衣等方式进行上访游行，而被北京市宣武区警方<span lang="EN-US">3</span>次以“扰乱社会公共场所秩序”为由处治安拘留；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: major-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: major-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: major-fareast;">2003</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: major-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: major-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: major-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">1</span>日，曾因多方上访而无果，其为给年老父母找住处，多次向政府信访办求助均被轰出，故在万般无奈情况下纵身跳入天安门的金水河自杀（以示抗议），幸得被人及时救起才幸免遇难，随后即被北京市东城区警方以“给政府制造事端”为由，以涉嫌“寻衅滋事罪”刑拘；同年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">17</span>日，又被以同罪由予以逮捕，并关押于北京市第二看守所；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: major-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: major-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: major-fareast;">2003</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: major-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: major-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: major-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">28</span>日，被北京市第二中级法院以同罪名判处有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">2</span>年；其不服上诉，但经北京市高级法院二审裁决，依然维持原判；刑满释放后，因其与其兄叶国柱、其侄子叶明君等家人坚持维权投诉不罢休，遂又被数次抓捕和刑拘；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: major-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: major-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: major-fareast;">2022</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: major-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: major-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: major-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">9</span>日，因叶国强、</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 16px;">叶国柱</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">兄弟二人不断上访讨说法，北京当局恐其等上访影响即将来临的中共“</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">20</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">大”召开，遂又被北京市西城区警方带走，次日被以涉嫌“寻衅滋事罪”刑事拘留。目前被羁押于北京市西城区看守所。</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: major-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: major-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: major-fareast;">对叶国强、</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 16px;">叶国柱</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;
