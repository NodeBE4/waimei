[“维吾尔在线”网站前义工、维吾尔人权捍卫者阿提开姆•茹孜的简历与情况通报](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2022/02/blog-post_5.html)

<div>
<p><span></span></p><a name='more'></a><p class="MsoNormal"><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">5</span>日，本网获悉“维吾尔在线”网站前义工、维吾尔人权捍卫者阿提开姆•茹孜的简历与情况，现通报如下：<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">阿提开姆•茹孜：</span></b><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">1991</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">2</span>日出生，新疆维吾尔自治区乌鲁木齐市人，维吾尔族名<span lang="EN-US">Atikem Rozi</span>，又译为阿提克木·如孜，维吾尔著名学者伊力哈木•土赫提的学生<span lang="EN-US">,</span>“维吾尔在线”网站前义工，维吾尔人权捍卫者，中国在押政治犯。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2010</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年—<span lang="EN-US">2012</span>年，曾因个人护照申请连续三次被当局以政治原因而拒发（尽管其无任何政治不良记录），故而将其遭遇发布于新浪微博之上，随后各大媒体，如官方刊物《都市北京》<span lang="EN-US">(Metro Beijing)</span>、“维吾尔在线”和中国《环球时报》等媒体便竞相刊发或转载了涉及其本人与其他维吾尔人申请护照时遭遇双重标准的文章，引发社会热议；此一事件因很快触动了中共当局的敏感神经，遂遭到中共新疆当局的严厉打击和报复；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2013</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">5</span>日，被乌鲁木齐警方以在“媒体上辩论”为由传唤，且被审问<span lang="EN-US">6</span>小时后才得以获释，两天之后又到其家继续威吓、骚扰；<span lang="EN-US">2014</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">15</span>日，因其多次使用<span lang="EN-US">twitter,facebook</span>以及新浪微博等网站发布文章和讯息，并利用网媒广泛寻找被失踪多日的男友、维吾尔人权捍卫者穆塔力普•伊明，随被以背景是维吾尔著名学者伊力哈木•土赫提在北京的学生、曾是“维吾尔在线”网站义工为罪由被北京市警方传唤，<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">17</span>日其等<span lang="EN-US">8</span>人被以涉嫌“分裂国家罪” 统一刑拘（但据官方报道，其于<span lang="EN-US">2014 </span>年<span lang="EN-US">1 </span>月<span lang="EN-US">21</span>日被拘留）；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">同年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">20</span>日，因其案被定为“分裂国家犯罪集团案”，故被新疆乌鲁木齐市检察院正式批捕；<span lang="EN-US">8</span>个月后，其一行<span lang="EN-US">8</span>人案在乌鲁木齐巿中级法院一起开庭受审；<span lang="EN-US">2014</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">22</span>日，<span lang="EN-US">8</span>人终被乌鲁木齐巿中级法院统一宣判<span lang="EN-US">3-8</span>年不等刑期；其中，其被以“分裂主义罪”和“危害国家安全罪”判处有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">4</span>年；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2018</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">16</span>日，理应刑满释放，但据中国环球电视网（又称中国国际电视台，英文简称<span lang="EN-US">CGTN</span>）在<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月发布的一部宣传片中显示，其身着囚服出现在镜头前，故其是否如期释放，或是否被释放后又重新拘留，不得而知。目前或仍在新疆女子监狱（又名新疆第二监狱，新疆乌鲁木齐市新市区东站路<span lang="EN-US">1327</span>号女子监狱，邮政编码：<span lang="EN-US">830013</span>）服刑。</span></p><p></p><div class="separator" style="clear: both;
