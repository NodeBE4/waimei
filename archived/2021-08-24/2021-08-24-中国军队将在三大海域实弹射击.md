[中国军队将在三大海域实弹射击](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210824-1185657)
------

<div>
<p>中国海事部门发布多条航行警告，显示中国解放军将在南中国海、黄海和渤海三大海域进行实弹射击。</p><p>广东海事局的航行警告显示，解放军将在8月25日9时至下午6时于南中国海南澳附近海域实弹射击演习。</p><p>丹东海事局23日的航行警告显示，黄海北部大鹿岛自8月24日0时至26日晚上12时，将在四点连线范围内进行实弹射击。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>烟台海事局23日发布航行警告称，渤海海峡长岛海域，将于8月24日至25日每天5时30分至下午1时30分进行实弹射击训练，禁止船只驶入训练涉及的区域。</p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
