[江苏疫情有反弹迹象新增39起本土确诊病例](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210726-1173770)
------

<div>
<p>江苏冠病疫情出现反弹迹象，昨天（26日）新增39起本土确诊病例，除了一起来自宿迁市之外，其余的江苏本土病例来自南京。</p><p>中国国家卫生健康委员会在官方网站发布的数据显示，中国大陆昨天总共新增76起确诊病例，比起前一天的32起多，其中40起为本土病例，其余为境外输入病例。</p><p>新增本土病例包括39起江苏病例和一起辽宁病例；江苏在前一天只通报两起本土病例。在新增境外输入病例方面，云南有18例，广东八例，福建五例，内蒙古和河南各两例，北京一例。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>另据央视新闻报道，南京昨天也新增一名本土无症状感染者。</p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
