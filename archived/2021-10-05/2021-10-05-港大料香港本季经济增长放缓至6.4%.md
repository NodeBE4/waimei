[港大料香港本季经济增长放缓至6.4%](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20211005-1200455)
------

<div>
<p>香港大学预计，香港今年第三季实质经济增长同比增长6.9%，较第二季的7.6%有所放缓。</p><p>据《信报》报道，港大预测，受惠于持续上升的疫苗接种率及第二轮消费券计划，香港经济将持续增长，预计第四季实质经济增长为6.4%，但增速将连续三个季度放缓。</p><p>港大指，政府消费券计划对零售提供强大支持，8月零售业销货额上升10.5%，对香港需求增长提供强大动力。在蓬勃的外贸带动下，香港经济增长预料持续扩大。港大预计，第三与第四季私人消费开支同比增长7.8%和6.7%；货物出口分别增长19.6%和18.5%。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>另外，港大预计，就业市场将进一步改善，失业率将由第三季的预计4.6%，跌至第四季的4.3%，全年失业率5.3%。香港第三及第四季综合消费物价指数预测，分别按年增长2.5%和2%，全年增长1.6%。</p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/zbpe-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=zbpe399&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_desktop-zbpe399_2.png?dHEiGouxxZpWwWTJE04dyGyUN74JOU2z" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_desktop-zbpe399_2.png?dHEiGouxxZpWwWTJE04dyGyUN74JOU2z" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/zbpe-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=zbpe399&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_mobile-zbpe399_2.png?qZnBcIGCJvPR8A2IAZOZuOxdoGZDatiI" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_mobile-zbpe399_2.png?qZnBcIGCJvPR8A2IAZOZuOxdoGZDatiI" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
