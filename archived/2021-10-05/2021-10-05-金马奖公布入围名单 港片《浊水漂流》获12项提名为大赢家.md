[金马奖公布入围名单 港片《浊水漂流》获12项提名为大赢家](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20211005-1200479)
------

<div>
<p>台湾金马奖入围名单今天揭晓，香港电影《浊水漂流》获12项提名，成为入围大赢家，台湾电影《瀑布》、《缉魂》与《月老》则各获11项提名，并列第二。</p><p>据《联合报》等台媒报道，《浊水漂流》改编自2012年香港特区政府未经告知驱赶露宿者的新闻，描写露宿的街友不幸遇上政府突袭扫荡而家当尽失，向政府提告却处处碰壁。</p><p>《浊水漂流》由香港知名男演员吴镇宇主演，他也凭这部电影成功入围最佳男主角。1999年，吴镇宇曾凭杜琪峰执导的电影《枪火》，夺得金马奖最佳男主角。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>据报道，今年金马奖报名影片包括66部剧情长片、两部动画片、52部纪录片、296部剧情短片及87部纪录短片和70部动画短片，共计573部作品。</p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/zbpe-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=zbpe399&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_desktop-zbpe399_2.png?dHEiGouxxZpWwWTJE04dyGyUN74JOU2z" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_desktop-zbpe399_2.png?dHEiGouxxZpWwWTJE04dyGyUN74JOU2z" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/zbpe-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=zbpe399&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_mobile-zbpe399_2.png?qZnBcIGCJvPR8A2IAZOZuOxdoGZDatiI" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_mobile-zbpe399_2.png?qZnBcIGCJvPR8A2IAZOZuOxdoGZDatiI" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
