[退伍军人被打断肋骨申请鉴定石沉大海，江苏南通江晓燕申请政府信息公开](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2022/02/blog-post_46.html)

<div>
<p><span></span></p><a name='more'></a><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;"><br /></span><p></p><p><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">（维权网信息中心报道）</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">2022</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">年</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">2</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">月</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">26</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">日，本网获悉：南通市崇川区永兴街道芦泾港村村民江晓燕通过邮寄向南通市公安局崇川分局永兴派出所（简称永兴派出所）提交《政府信息公开申请书》，请求公开有关其父钱德胜被打伤后的人体损伤程度鉴定报告及其有关文件。</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2018</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">12</span>日，南通退伍军人钱德胜被一伙身份不明者殴打致肋骨断裂。钱德胜及其女儿江晓燕向永兴派出所申请对人体损伤程度予以鉴定。永兴派出所民警带领钱德胜前去鉴定，但至今渺无音讯。为此，江晓燕要求该派出所在在法定期限内以书面形式向上述政府信息。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">七旬老人钱德胜系退伍军人。<span lang="EN-US">2007</span>年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">8</span>日，钱德胜承包原芦泾港村十七组鱼场，包括鱼塘、建鸡房、路面等约计<span lang="EN-US">13</span>亩，且签订了《承包合同》。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">自<span lang="EN-US">2017</span>年起，就有人对该鱼塘实施破坏，填埋或堵塞给水河道，影响养鱼。钱德胜家属向市长热线<span lang="EN-US">12345</span>电话求救，无济于事。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2018</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">12</span>日下午<span lang="EN-US">3</span>左右，七旬老人钱德胜回家时，正遇见闯入自家的原港闸经济开发区管委会副主任高巍<span lang="EN-US">(</span>分管城管<span lang="EN-US">)</span>和原办公室主任邹祥指使一帮不明身份的人砸门窗玻璃，还有人爬上屋顶掀房揭瓦。这帮人见到钱德胜后，就不由分说将其掀倒在地，打断七根肋骨。钱德胜当场晕了过去。同时，钱德胜的老母亲及其妻子也遭遇了毒打。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">家人报警后，永兴派出所民警姗姗来迟，并未将违法嫌疑人带走。钱德胜向江苏省南通市公安局港闸分局报案。该局作出港公（信）不受字【<span lang="EN-US">2019</span>】<span lang="EN-US">12</span>号《不受理信访事项告知书》，不按信访处理，并转永兴派出所办理。但该派出所至今未有处理结果。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">为此，钱德胜的女儿江晓燕多次上访。当地政府为了阻止江晓燕继续上访，派人日夜盯梢。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体;
