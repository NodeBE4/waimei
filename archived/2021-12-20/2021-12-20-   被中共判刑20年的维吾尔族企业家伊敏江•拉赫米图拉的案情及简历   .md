font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">1962</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">此外，还在将新疆南部的农产品及其他产品引入中亚市场方面发挥了主导作用，故而深受新疆政府的极大赞许和肯定；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月，其突然被新疆阿图什市警方以涉嫌“支持恐怖分子”罪名刑拘，其女儿及其姐妹努尔古丽•拉赫米图拉<span lang="EN-US"> (Nurgul Rahmitulla) </span>亦被拘留；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">之后其又被阿图什市检察院以同罪名转正式逮捕，指控其在“为个人财产缴纳天课”（即从事慈善事业）时向被囚的维吾尔族政治犯的家属捐款，此行为触犯了政府相关政策和法律法规；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体;
