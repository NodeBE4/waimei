<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">李霖<span lang="EN-US">:</span></span></b><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">1987</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">6</span>日出生，辽宁省沈阳市籍人，新疆乌鲁木齐市水磨沟区红光山路户籍，电力软件工程师，民主自由追求者，中国在押政治犯。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">近年来，因在海外网络平台曾发文图、视频信息，批评中共当局对新疆各少数民族居住区域的政策，尤其是在网上发“新疆的网络不好用”，“一些清真寺的标志被拆了”，“新疆的警务站比较多，觉得有点浪费，新疆的一对一扶贫装样子，不起作用”等文图信息，遂引起中共新疆当局的极度不满和严密监控；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2018</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">20</span>日，被乌鲁木齐市南湖北路派出所警方带走，并被以“扰乱公共秩序”罪由行政拘留<span lang="EN-US">10</span>日；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">同年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">3</span>日，又被乌鲁木齐市水磨沟区警方以涉嫌“颠覆国家政权罪、煽动颠覆国家政权罪”刑事拘留，自此失联；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">同年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">2</span>日，被乌鲁木齐市水磨沟区检察院以同罪名正式批捕，指控其利用<span lang="EN-US">YouTube</span>、<span lang="EN-US">Twitter</span>等国际媒体软件“散布虚假言论，诋毁国家领导人、抨击现行国家政策及中央对新疆维稳相关政策，并恶意评论新疆维稳措施”，因其信息及言论在互联网上被大量转发、评论，给社会造成不良影响，故而违反了政府相关法律法规，属于违法行为，并关押在乌鲁木齐市第一看守所；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2018</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月，被乌鲁木齐市法院一审以“煽动颠覆国家政权罪”判处有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">4</span>年；因其案在法院办理、审理中有明显程序违法和伪造证据等现象，故其不服上诉；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2020</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月，经乌鲁木齐市中级法院二审裁决，依然维持原判，刑期至<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">22</span>日；据悉，入狱迄今，备受酷刑，戴脚镣已<span lang="EN-US">3</span>年。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">目前在兵团乌鲁木齐监狱（新疆维吾尔自治区乌鲁木齐市甲支<span lang="EN-US">008</span>信箱，邮政编码：<span lang="EN-US">830009</span>）服刑。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"></p><div class="separator" style="clear: both;
