[台检方起诉划橡皮艇偷渡的大陆男子](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210831-1188621)
------

<div>
<p>台中市检方今天依违反《入出国及移民法》等罪，起诉今年4月划橡皮艇偷渡到台湾的大陆周姓男子。</p><p>据中时电子报报道，有民众今年4月30日晚间于台中港西码头附近海岸发现可疑橡皮艇，警方循线追查隔天凌晨在堤防查获34岁周姓男子。</p><p>周姓男子自称在大陆电子厂当作业员，因“向往台湾自由的生活”，网购橡皮艇自福建省横渡台湾海峡，航行11个小时抵台。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>警方查获时，周男并未携带身份证件，身上只有100元人民币，橡皮艇是花1万6000元人民币在网络购得，再外挂舷外机。</p><p>本案经台中地案经台中地署侦结，依违反《入出国及移民法》等罪起诉。</p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
