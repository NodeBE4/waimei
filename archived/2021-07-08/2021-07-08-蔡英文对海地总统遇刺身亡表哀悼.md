[蔡英文对海地总统遇刺身亡表哀悼](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210708-1166246)
------

<div>
<p>台湾友邦海地共和国总统莫伊兹（Jovenel Moïse）遇刺身故，台湾总统府发言人张惇涵今晚表示，总统蔡英文代表台湾政府及人民致上深切哀悼与悲痛。</p><p>根据联合新闻网报道，张惇涵表示，蔡英文感念莫伊兹总统任内推动双边的深化合作，并感念莫伊兹总统屡次为台湾的国际参与仗义执言，期盼莫伊兹总统家属节哀保重。</p><p>张惇涵说，台湾与海地建交65年，双边邦谊深厚，在海地遭逢此不幸事故之际，总统蔡英文亦指示外交部长吴钊燮及台湾驻海地大使馆，向海地政府表达台湾诚挚的悼念及慰问，并持续密切关注后续状况与发展；同时，务必确保台湾侨民安全无虞。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>张惇涵还说，蔡英文也认为，台湾将坚定与海地风雨同路，做友邦坚强后盾；并期盼海地国政推动顺畅，双方合作各项建设计划推动如常，海地社会及人民生活早日恢复平静。</p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
