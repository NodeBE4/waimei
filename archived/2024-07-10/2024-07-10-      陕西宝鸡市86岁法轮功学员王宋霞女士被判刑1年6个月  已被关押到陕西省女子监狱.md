[陕西宝鸡市86岁法轮功学员王宋霞女士被判刑1年6个月  已被关押到陕西省女子监狱](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2024/07/8616.html)
------

<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">10</span>日，本网获悉：陕西宝鸡市<span lang="EN-US">86</span>岁法轮功学员王宋霞女士被判刑<span lang="EN-US">1</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月，已经被关押到陕西省女子监狱。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">王宋霞：</span></b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">陕西宝鸡市法轮功学员，女，<span lang="EN-US">1938</span>年生，现年<span lang="EN-US">86</span>岁。家住宝鸡市渭滨区姜谭路，她于一九九八年开始修炼法轮功。王宋霞因坚持信仰，多次被中共抓捕、关押，<span lang="EN-US">2010</span>年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月曾被判刑七年缓刑七年。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">王宋霞<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">8</span>日被宝鸡市公安局渭滨分局姜谭路派出所警察抓捕，<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">9</span>日被“取保候审”放回家。<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月初获悉：王宋霞已被当地法院判刑一年半、罚金五千元，六月她已被关押到陕西省女子监狱。刑期至：<span lang="EN-US">2025</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p>
