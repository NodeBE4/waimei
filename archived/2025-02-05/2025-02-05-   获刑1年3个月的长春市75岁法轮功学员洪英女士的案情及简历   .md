<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2025</span>年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">5</span>日，本网获悉获刑<span lang="EN-US">1</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>个月的长春市<span lang="EN-US">75</span>岁法轮功学员洪英女士的案情及简历，现在通报如下：<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">洪英</span></b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">：吉林省长春市法轮功学员，<span lang="EN-US">1950</span>年生，<span lang="EN-US">2025</span>年已<span lang="EN-US">75</span>岁，洪英于<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">6</span>日被金钱堡派出所警察从家中强制带走并抄家， “理由”是说洪英买菜时与人宣传法轮功内容被举报。洪英被关押在长春市看守所（苇子沟看守所）。后遭刑事拘留、批捕起诉。洪英及其余十几名法轮功学员被构陷到朝阳区法院。后得知洪英被判<span lang="EN-US">1</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>个月，她被关押在吉林省长春市兰家监狱。刑期至：<span lang="EN-US">2025</span>年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">5</span>日。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p>
