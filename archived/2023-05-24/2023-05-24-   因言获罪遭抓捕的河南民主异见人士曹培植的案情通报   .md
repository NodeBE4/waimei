<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">24</span>日，本网获悉：因言获罪遭抓捕的河南民主异见人士曹培植的狱中情况，现在通报如下：曹培植案件已到法院五个月，法定审理期限三个月，目前严重超期。律师取保及请求主审法官陈秋敏回避的申请至今也无任何答复。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">曹培植简历：</span></b><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">1960</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">1989</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年，曾因参与年天安门民主运动并亲历“六四惨案”，遂自此改变了人生轨迹，坚定的走上一条中国民主运动不归路；曾因以民主推手、民权精英、博客骑士自许，而创建“温馨小屋”博客群，并在其努力经营下，使该博客群的点击量高达<span lang="EN-US">70</span>万人次；也正是因其频发民主信息和对中共当局不利的时政批评性信息，故而引起河南省当局的极度不悦与伺机打击；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2022</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">3</span>日，因其在微信群里传播“未经官方证实的海外消息，并对党和国家领导人有侮辱性言论”，遂被河南省义马市警方以涉嫌“寻衅滋事罪”刑事拘留；同年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">4</span>日，又被义马市检察院以同罪名正式批捕。</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">目前被羁押于河南省渑池县看守所（河南省三门峡市渑池县英豪镇英东村，邮政编码：</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">472434</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">）。</span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体;
