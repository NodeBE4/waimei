[港东区区议员蔡志强拒宣誓将离任](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210910-1192176)
------

<div>
<p>香港东区一名区议员今日拒绝宣誓，民政事务局长徐英伟说，该区议员必须离任，政府不会安排重新宣誓。</p><p>据《明报》报道，香港现任211名区议员今日（10日）起分成四批宣誓，首批宣誓的是港岛区区议员。区议员须宣誓拥护《基本法》、效忠特区，民政事务局长徐英伟获香港特首委任为监誓人。</p><p>徐英伟在宣誓仪式后指出，东区区议员蔡志强今早表明拒绝宣誓，亦没有出席宣誓仪式，根据《宣誓及声明条例》，蔡志强已被视为拒绝或忽略作出誓言，因此他必须离任，不会安排重新宣誓。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>徐英伟介绍，今日共有24名区议员出席宣誓仪式，政府将发稿，统一公布有多少名议员的宣誓有效。如果监誓人对个别议员的宣誓的有效性存有疑问，亦会作出跟进。</p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/zbpe-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=zbpe399&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_desktop-zbpe399_2.png?dHEiGouxxZpWwWTJE04dyGyUN74JOU2z" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_desktop-zbpe399_2.png?dHEiGouxxZpWwWTJE04dyGyUN74JOU2z" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/zbpe-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=zbpe399&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_mobile-zbpe399_2.png?qZnBcIGCJvPR8A2IAZOZuOxdoGZDatiI" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_mobile-zbpe399_2.png?qZnBcIGCJvPR8A2IAZOZuOxdoGZDatiI" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
