<p><span></span></p><a name='more'></a><p class="MsoNormal"><br /></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">17</span>日，本网获悉获刑<span lang="EN-US">4</span>年的黑龙江佳木斯市法轮功学员黄邵溥女士的案情即简历，现在通报如下：<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">黄邵溥</span></b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">：黑龙江佳木斯市法轮功学员，女，一九七四年十月生，<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">50</span>岁。她于一九九七年开始修炼法轮功。黄邵溥曾就职于佳市职业技能鉴定所。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">中共江泽民集团一九九九年七月开始公开迫害法轮功后，黄邵溥多次遭到绑架、关押，被判刑、酷刑折磨。她出狱后，甚至被剥夺办理身份证的权利，失去户籍，十几年来，她无法正常生活、工作。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">黄邵溥<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">15</span>日被警察抓捕、关押在佳木斯市看守所，<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">23</span>日被佳市向阳区检察院批捕。<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">11</span>日，黄邵溥被向阳区法院庭审。后被以“利用邪教组织破坏法律实施罪”判刑<span lang="EN-US">4</span>年。刑期至<span lang="EN-US">2027</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">14</span>日。</span></p><p></p>
