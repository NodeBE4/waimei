<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">6</span>日，本网获悉获刑<span lang="EN-US">20</span>年的日喀则市登卓寺僧人曲嘉旺波的简历，现通报如下：<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">曲嘉旺波：</span></b><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">1973</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年出生，藏族名<span lang="EN-US">Choegyal Wangpo</span>，西藏自治区日喀则市定日县扎那村人，日喀则市定日县协格尔镇查那村当卓寺（藏人称之为登卓寺（<span lang="EN-US">Tengdro</span>））僧人，慈善公益捐助人，中国在押良心犯。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2019</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">-9</span>月，曾因到西藏首府拉萨市做佛事访问时，误将手机丢在一家咖啡馆，老板随将手机交给当地警方希望帮助查找失主；孰料拉萨市警方在翻查手机时，查出其与尼泊尔的多名同乡藏僧有联系或拥有和达赖喇嘛有关照片或文献，并有捐款帮助<span lang="EN-US">2015</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月尼泊尔大地震当地的藏人及寺院、小区重建等记录，随引起中共西藏当局的极大不快和恐慌；同年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月，其家乡扎那村和毗邻的当卓寺遭到日喀则市当局派出的多名武警和公安警察的夜袭，所有僧人及村民被强迫接受政治洗脑教育，多人被殴打，包含其在内的<span lang="EN-US">20</span>僧侣被拘留，所在区域的网络连接被切断；<span lang="EN-US">3</span>天后，一名僧人因向当局抗议而被自杀，其余僧人均被超押数月不予释放；后其与另<span lang="EN-US">3</span>名藏僧被日喀则市警方以涉嫌“跟境外藏人联系、赈济尼泊尔地震，或拥有和达赖喇嘛有关照片或文献”为罪由转正式逮捕，自此失联；<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">6</span>日获悉，其一行<span lang="EN-US">4</span>人案在日喀则中级法院庭审，最终被以“危害国家安全罪”判处<span lang="EN-US">20</span>年<span lang="EN-US">-5</span>年不等刑期；其中，其被判处有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">20</span>年，刑期至<span lang="EN-US">2038</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体;
