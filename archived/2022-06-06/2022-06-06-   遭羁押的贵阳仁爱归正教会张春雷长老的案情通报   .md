<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">6</span>日，本网获悉遭羁押的贵阳仁爱归正教会张春雷长老的案情，现通报如下：<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">今天，贵阳仁爱归正教会张春雷长老的妻子杨爱清在自媒体发信息说：“<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">2</span>日上午，律师依法到贵阳市看守所会见了春雷，清瘦，身体健康，心境从容，他说他在里面等待神的时间，请大家为他祷告，只要祷告，不要担忧。他向各牧者和弟兄姊妹们问安，他每天都为大家祷告。从<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月底，春雷案件从市检察院移交到市中级人民法院，至今为止已有四月有余，春雷和律师都没有收到立案起诉意见书。”<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">张春雷简历：</span></b><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">1964</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月出生<span lang="EN-US">, </span>贵州省人，基督徒，贵阳市仁爱归正家庭教会主要组建人，教会带领人、长老，<span lang="EN-US">2018</span>年《牧者联署：为基督信仰的声明》首批签名人之一，中国在押良心犯。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">早年，曾因笃信耶稣基督，为广传基督教福音而与人共同组建了不从中共“三自教会”的“仁爱归正教会”；起初，该教会仅有几十会众，但在其不懈努力和事工（即神职人员在教会里所做的服务）带领下，因能热情广泛接待南来北往的信徒，故而在后来的国度事工中起到和发挥了重要的作用；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2018</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年，曾因积极参与由四川成都王怡牧师发起的《牧者联署：为基督信仰的声明》的首批联署签名，随遭贵阳当局的严管监控；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2018</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">30</span>日，其教会被贵阳市南明区民族宗教事务局突然取缔，并被指称，因其等一行人“未经批准，擅自以‘仁爱归正教会’名义在南明区中天商务港设立宗教场所，组织、举行宗教活动，接受宗教性捐献，违反《宗教事务条例》相关规定，责令停止宗教活动，拆除相关设施和标识”，最终致使其及教会信徒在失去会堂后只能在餐厅、酒店进行主日敬拜；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2019</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年，曾因在当地某酒店举行主日聚会，而被数十名宗教局人员及警方先后<span lang="EN-US">3</span>次连手冲击，<span lang="EN-US">2</span>次被封门，敬拜所用的十字架被强摘，其计算机等用品被查收；<span lang="EN-US">2019</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月，曾因其教会新租房屋的房东再次被当局威胁，故而其被逼无奈只得再次从新租的房屋搬走；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2021</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">16</span>日，因其教会有十数名信众在参加小组学习时被突然闯入的贵阳市南明区民宗局人员和警方带走，遂其赶到派出所与警方交涉要人，不料竟遭粗暴野蛮对待，并被以“非法经营社团”为由处行政拘留<span lang="EN-US">11</span>天；拘留期满时，又被贵阳市云岩区警方以涉嫌“诈骗罪”刑拘；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2021</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">1</span>日，被贵阳市云岩区检察院以同罪名正式批捕；据悉，其妻子、儿子受牵连曾遭警方长时讯问且其妻遭虐待，教会多位同工成员亦遭抄家；<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">29</span>日—<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">21</span>日，其代理律师数次要求会见均受阻，被告知其“因涉嫌危害国家安全，在侦查期间未得到办案单位允许的情况下，禁止律师会见”。</span><span style="font-size: 12pt;">目前被羁押于贵阳市看守所</span><span style="font-size: 12pt;">。</span></p> <div class="separator" style="clear: both;
