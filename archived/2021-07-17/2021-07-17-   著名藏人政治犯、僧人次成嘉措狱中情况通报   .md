<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">17</span>日，本网获悉著名藏人政治犯、僧人次成嘉措狱中情况，现在通报如下：<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">次成嘉措：甘肃甘南州夏河县人，被捕前是甘南拉卜楞寺僧人。<span lang="EN-US">2008</span>年藏区爆发大规模抗议期间，他因于当年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">14</span>和<span lang="EN-US">15</span>日在拉卜楞寺示威，呼喊“祈请达赖喇嘛尊者返回西藏”等口号被当局逮捕，时年<span lang="EN-US">36</span>岁。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">次成嘉措于<span lang="EN-US">2009</span>年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">21</span>日在甘南州中级人民法院受审，当时被重判无期徒刑。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2014</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年，次成嘉措获减刑，刑期减为<span lang="EN-US">19</span>年，剥夺政治权利<span lang="EN-US">7</span>年。次成嘉措<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月经甘肃省天水市中级人民法院批准，被予以减刑<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月。这是他获得第二次减刑。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">天水市中级人民法院认为，次成嘉措在狱中“能认罪悔罪<span lang="EN-US">,</span>认真遵守监规纪律<span lang="EN-US">,</span>服从管理<span lang="EN-US">,</span>表现较好<span lang="EN-US">,</span>确有悔改表现”。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体;
