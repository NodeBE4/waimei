<p><span></span></p><a name='more'></a><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;"><br /></span><p></p><p><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">（维权网信息中心报道）</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">2022</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">年</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">4</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">月</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">19</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">日，本网获悉获刑</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">4</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">年的湖北省丹江口市基督徒林春芬的案情及简历，现通报如下：</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">林春芬：</span></b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">女，<span lang="EN-US">1970</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">10</span>日出生，湖北省丹江口市人，原丹江口市丁家营镇丁营大道<span lang="EN-US">2</span>号村民、丹江口市南水北调中线工程库区农村移民，现北京市海淀区黄庄小区<span lang="EN-US">813</span>楼<span lang="EN-US">312</span>室居民，基督徒，维权访民，人权捍卫者，中国在押维权人士。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">早年，曾因是湖北省丹江口市南水北调中线工程库区的农村移民，理应得到的政府占地补偿款一直未能得到，随向村镇等各级管理部门“反映问题”，自此屡遭打压；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2010</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">7</span>日和<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">14</span>日，曾因自家房屋面临强拆，其及与其同住的父母在拒强拆中遭殴打，情急之下 ，其用汽油欲自焚以示抗议；事后其虽赶到当地警局报案，但竟了无结果，当局依然不予公正、恰当解决；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2012</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">28</span>日，曾因上访而被丹江口市丁家营镇警方刑拘，并被关进丹江口市看守所；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2013</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">1</span>日，又被丹江口市警方以“扰乱单位秩序”、“阻碍执行职务”罪由行政拘留<span lang="EN-US">20</span>天；同年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">7</span>日，再被当地警方关进黑监狱，且其房屋被夷为平地，屋内贵重物品均丢失，财产、财务及家畜等尽遭掩埋和损坏；后其因此继续上访，并到北京寻求信访帮助，不料遭到更多的侮辱欺凌、拘押和关黑监狱等迫害；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2014</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">8</span>日，曾因住京维权遭截访，先被关在丹江口市驻京办招待所，后又被秘押至丹江口市文华宾馆<span lang="EN-US">103</span>房间，并被强行脱光衣服搜身，致其倍感羞辱，曾为此绝食抗议<span lang="EN-US">7</span>昼夜；同年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">24</span>日，又被更换地址持续秘关<span lang="EN-US">2</span>个月，期间遭十几名看守其的黑社会性质的男性青年多次殴打和虐待，致其因痛不欲生，拔下窗户上的钉子割腕自杀；后幸免遇难，但仍被当局强逼写材料，不写就打；同年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">8</span>日，其被迫在丁家营镇政府提供的协议书上签字画押，并写下不再上访保证书，才终得获释返家；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2015</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年，曾因在京上访期间，受到北京基督教圣爱团契家庭教会对其生活及信仰上的帮助与支持，随与教会信徒们一起学习《圣经》，并于次年受洗而成为一名基督徒；之后，其信访要求有所解决，地方政府为其及父母修盖了房子，作为其在水库移民中的经济补偿（当然还有土地等其他问题并未解决），而其此时也在北京结婚，其丈夫因是北京公民并在北京工作当医生，随其成为名副其实的北京媳妇，并与丈夫长居京城。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">之后，因其原籍住宅的户主名字，在当年登记的是其父母之名而非其本人，随其在父母明确授意的前提下，多次向有关部门提出申请，要求将户主姓名更为自己的姓名，然而相关政府部门却对其申请久拖未予解决，其随被迫再次在京信访投诉；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2018</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">22</span>日，其被丹江口市警方以“寻衅滋事”为由行政拘留<span lang="EN-US">7</span>天；<span lang="EN-US">2019</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">27</span>日，又被原籍截访人员突然带走，遣回原籍后被丹江口市十堰市警方以涉嫌“寻衅滋事罪”刑拘，并被关押在十堰市看守所；同年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">8</span>日，被丹江口市检察院以同罪名正式批捕，追溯其曾于<span lang="EN-US">2012</span>年以来一直因房屋拆迁问题在国家非信访部门上访，此行为已与现行相关法律规条相抵触，又加之中共十•一<span lang="EN-US">70</span>周年党庆将至，为防止其再度维权上访，给党庆造成不良影响，故而被以涉嫌“寻衅滋事罪”批捕；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2020</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">24</span>日，被湖北省丹江口市法院以同罪名判处有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">4</span>年，刑期至<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">26</span>日。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">目前在湖北省武汉女子监狱<span lang="EN-US">(</span>湖北省武汉市乔口区宝丰一路<span lang="EN-US">97</span>号，邮政编码：<span lang="EN-US">430030)</span>服刑。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"></p><div class="separator" style="clear: both;
