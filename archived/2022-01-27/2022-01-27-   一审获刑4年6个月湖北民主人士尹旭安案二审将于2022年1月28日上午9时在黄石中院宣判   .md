[一审获刑4年6个月湖北民主人士尹旭安案二审将于2022年1月28日上午9时在黄石中院宣判](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2022/01/4620221289.html)

<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">27</span>日，本网获悉：尹旭安寻衅滋事案案二审将于<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">28</span>日上午<span lang="EN-US">9</span>时在黄石中院宣判。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2021</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">21</span>日，尹旭安被大冶市法院以寻衅滋事罪一审判处有期徒刑四年六个月。此次获刑是因为他在纪念六四<span lang="EN-US">30</span>周年之际将一张含<span lang="EN-US">8964</span>车牌照片发到推特。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">尹旭安属于重病患患高血压病<span lang="EN-US">3</span>级、原发性高血压、高血压性心脏病伴有心力衰竭。<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月初常伯阳律师会见时已得知他曾多次病危送急救；<span lang="EN-US">2019</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月律师会见时得知其血压低压达<span lang="EN-US">200</span>，高压达<span lang="EN-US">300</span>，头、胸口、后脑勺、太阳穴都疼痛、发胀、眩晕，几次去看守所医务室吸氧。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2019</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月被捕时，他才出狱半年，正在北京寻医求药。上次因声援屠夫被判三年半，曾遭受酷刑。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">对尹旭安的境况，本网将持续关注。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">附尹旭安简介：<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></b></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">尹旭安：</span></b><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">1974</span><span style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">27</span>日出生，湖北省大冶市人，原湖北省大冶市保安镇村民，枉判上访者，公益维权公民，中国在押政治犯。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">2003</span><span style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">年，曾因控告湖北省鄂州市梁子湖区民政局就其夫妻离婚争议非法登记一案，被地方法院枉法裁判，而地方高院对此又不作为，随被迫于<span lang="EN-US">2007</span>年到北京上访；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">自<span lang="EN-US">2007</span>年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月以来，因多次上访而备受当局非法处罚及伤害；曾因上访维权和参与围观、声援、抗争行动而被当局非法拘留<span lang="EN-US">6</span>次、被关黑监狱<span lang="EN-US">8</span>次；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">2009</span><span style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">年，在美国时任总统奥巴马访华期间，曾因到北京市公安局治安总队申请游行示威，而被湖北当局处以劳动教养<span lang="EN-US">2</span>年，并延期释放<span lang="EN-US">3</span>个月；劳教期间，曾因身患高血压三期（极高危程度）及多种其他疾病（如乙肝、胆囊炎及结石等）而多次晕倒和急救；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">2012</span><span style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">年起，曾因在京维权，开始更多关注民主公益维权事业；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">2012</span><span style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">年，曾积极参与十八大民主直选学术代表，呼吁推进政治体制改革、废除劳教制度及打击黑监狱等；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">2012</span><span style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月—<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月，曾因举报和起诉湖北省当地政府非法征用土地、起诉湖北省劳教委非法延期及非法选举等问题，而遭当局非法拘禁<span lang="EN-US">42</span>天；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">2013</span><span style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">年始，开始积极介入声援围观行动，如江西新余刘萍独立候选人案、山东曲阜薛福顺事件、山东淄博孙峰“煽颠案”、<span lang="EN-US">4.29</span>祭奠林昭活动、湖南衡阳赵枫生“煽颠案”开庭、郑州十君子事件、焦作张小玉案、苏州老兵范木根血拆案开庭、与王福磊等维权网友声援维权公民王健案等。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">2015</span><span style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">8</span>日，曾因用微信转发“湖北大冶市驻京维稳人员名单”而遭当局抓捕，并被行政拘留<span lang="EN-US">15</span>天，<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">24</span>日被以涉嫌“泄露国家机密罪”刑拘，后被释放；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">2015</span><span style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">25</span>日，又因与王芳、耿彩文等维权人士在武汉市黄鹤楼前身着声援屠夫（吴凎）的文化衫，并拍照上传网媒，随于<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">28</span>日被警方从家中带走，拘留于大冶市保安镇派出所，同年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">23</span>日被转为刑拘；<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">26</span>日，被以涉嫌“寻衅滋事罪”批捕；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">2016</span><span style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">13</span>日，其案在湖北省大冶市法院开庭受审，至<span lang="EN-US">2017</span>年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">27</span>日被该法院以“寻衅滋事罪”判处有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">3</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月；关押期间，曾遭狱警和牢头狱霸酷刑殴打，身心俱毁，且多次要求会见律师遭拒；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">2018</span><span style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">27</span>日，被减刑提前释放。但在出狱后不久的中共两会期间又被湖北省大冶市警方以涉嫌“故意泄露国家机密罪”刑拘，且遭虐待，直至<span lang="EN-US">2019</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月才获释放；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">2019</span><span style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">23</span>日，又被以涉嫌“寻衅滋事罪“刑拘；<span lang="EN-US">2019</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月，其案被大冶市检察院起诉至地方法院；<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">19</span>日，其案被湖北省大冶市法院虽开庭审理，久拖至<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">21</span>日终被该法院以“寻衅滋事罪”判处有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">4</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月，刑期至<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">22</span>日；据悉，其在狱中身体状况极差，已被抢救数次。</span><span style="font-family: 楷体;
