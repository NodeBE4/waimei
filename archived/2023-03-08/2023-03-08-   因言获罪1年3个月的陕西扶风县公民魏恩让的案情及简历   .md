<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">8</span>日，本网获悉因言获罪<span lang="EN-US">1</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>个月的陕西扶风县公民魏恩让的案情及简历，现在通报如下：<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">魏恩让：</span></b><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">1955</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">7</span>日出生，陕西省宝鸡市扶风县人，扶风县段家镇东魏村东魏窑<span lang="EN-US">023</span>号村民，网络活跃者，推特账户名“<span lang="EN-US">wei550707</span>”、“魏恩让”，民主政治关注者和推动者，因言获罪者，中国曾押政治犯。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">自<span lang="EN-US">2016</span>年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月以来，因其使用苹果手机下载了<span lang="EN-US">Pupa</span>和<span lang="EN-US">VPN</span>网络翻墙软件，并在<span lang="EN-US">Twitter(</span>推特<span lang="EN-US">)</span>和<span lang="EN-US">YouTube </span>网站上多次传播和发布对中共政府及其国家领导人等的负面消息，被中共网络水军监控发现后，遂遭地方当局多次打击；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2020</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">21</span>日，因在境外网站发布所谓“攻击国家的不当言论”，而被扶风县警方处行政拘留<span lang="EN-US">15</span>天；同年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">29</span>日，又因同样理由被扶风县警方以涉嫌“寻衅滋事罪”指定居所监视居住，<span lang="EN-US"> 3</span>天之后，<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">2</span>日，又被以同罪由转为刑拘；同年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">4</span>日，被扶风县检察院追加罪名，又以涉嫌“寻衅滋事罪”和“诽谤罪”予以批捕，并被羁押在扶风县看守所；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2020</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">12</span>日，其案被检察院提起公诉并被很快被移交至扶风县法院审查，指控其犯有两部分罪行：一是通过登录<span lang="EN-US">Teitter</span>账号大量传播和发布涉及国内重大事件、诋毁国家政治制度和政党制度的推文、图片、视频等<span lang="EN-US">64</span>条，尤其是发信息称“中共病毒引起武汉肺炎”，中共是“一个集体性的邪恶组织”等；二是在<span lang="EN-US">Twitter</span>上转贴、发布推文、图片和视频<span lang="EN-US">24</span>条，诽谤党和国家领导人的捏造信息（如“毛魔强调贫穷是美德”，“毛是迫害大陆人民的刽子手”等）；基于以上事实，因其明知以上信息属虚假、捏造信息，仍却在境外网站传播、转贴、散布及诋毁，以致于使中共国的良好形象受到损毁，并使公共秩序产生严重混乱，故而其行为触犯了国家法律，应以寻衅滋事罪和诽谤罪追求刑事责任；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2020</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">11</span>日，被陕西省扶风县法院以“寻衅滋事罪”（判刑<span lang="EN-US">1</span>年）、“诽谤罪”（判刑<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月），二罪合并，最终被决定执行有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">1</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>个月，剥夺政治权利<span lang="EN-US">3</span>年；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2021</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">28</span>日，刑满释放；据悉，其出狱后仍遭当地维稳部门及国保警方的持续打压，导致其无法正常工作（或打工），生活一直十分艰困。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体;
