<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">李玉：</span></b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">女，<span lang="EN-US">1983</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">8</span>日出生，山东省枣庄市人，枣庄市市中区永安乡黄庄村<span lang="EN-US">523</span>号村民，维权访民，中国在押维权人士。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2008</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年，曾因其住宅及田产遭遇政府强拆而未得到任何补偿，自此无家可归，走上维权之路；曾因被迫在京维权，屡遭监控、警告、训诫、殴打、拘留、关黑监狱，甚至被<span lang="EN-US">2</span>次枉判共计<span lang="EN-US">6</span>年；因维权而屡遭打压，随开始意识到中共政府的专制与邪恶，后逐级成长为一名坚定的维权公民；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2013</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年，曾因多次信访维权而无果，倍感孤立无援，一时冲动跳天安门旁金水河，幸得一武警及时抢救，方得生还，但又于同年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">5</span>日、<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">15</span>日先后被北京市朝阳区警方和丰台区警方以“扰乱公共场所秩序”罪由分别处行政拘留<span lang="EN-US">10</span>天，但获释后，仍怀着身孕积极前往苏州铁玫瑰园祭奠林昭，并参加“柳丝四祈告”与“张元勋追悼会”；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2013</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2014</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2015</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2019</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">13</span>日，再被山东省枣庄市警方以“寻衅滋事罪”刑拘；同年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">16</span>日，被转正式逮捕；<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">7</span>日，被枣庄市法院以同罪名判处有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">2</span>年；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体;
