<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">27</span>日，本网获悉获刑<span lang="EN-US">1</span>年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>个月的重庆市法轮功学员张礼江的案情及简历，现在通报如下：<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">张礼江：</span></b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">重庆市法轮功学员，男，<span lang="EN-US">1967</span>年生，重庆璧山县七塘镇依凤中学教师。因坚持法轮功信仰，长期遭中共迫害：<span lang="EN-US">2005</span>年，他被璧山县法院判刑<span lang="EN-US">3</span>年，并被开除公职；<span lang="EN-US">2012</span>年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">4</span>日，他被重庆市渝北区公安局国保警察抓捕，<span lang="EN-US">2012</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">5</span>日被关押到西山坪劳教所劳教<span lang="EN-US">2</span>年。<b><span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></b></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">2023</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月一天，张礼江因乘坐出租车时给了司机一份法轮功真相册子，被司机举报。<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">28</span>午，江北区寸滩派出所、江北区国保到渝北区天宫殿他的出租房，砸坏门锁，破门而入，抓捕了张礼江，并抄家。随即，张礼江被刑事拘留，关押在江北区复盛看守所，后被批捕、起诉，<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月下旬被重庆市江北区法院判刑<span lang="EN-US">1</span>年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>个月，并罚金五千元。刑期至：<span lang="EN-US">2025</span>年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">27</span>日。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p>
