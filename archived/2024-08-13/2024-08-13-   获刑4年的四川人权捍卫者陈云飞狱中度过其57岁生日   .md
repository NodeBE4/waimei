[获刑4年的四川人权捍卫者陈云飞狱中度过其57岁生日](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2024/08/457.html)
------

<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">14</span>日，本网获悉：<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">13</span>日是获刑<span lang="EN-US">4</span>年的四川人权捍卫者陈云飞的生日，陈云飞在狱中度过其<span lang="EN-US">57</span>岁生日。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;">陈云飞先后入狱判刑<span lang="EN-US">2</span>此，两次刑期共八年。此次陈云飞是于<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">25</span>日被捕的，<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">3</span>日被判刑<span lang="EN-US">4</span>年；此前一次是<span lang="EN-US">2015</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">25</span>日，他与四川维权公民为六四死难学生扫墓，回程途中被捕；<span lang="EN-US">2017</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">31</span>日被以寻衅滋事罪判刑<span lang="EN-US">4</span>年。</span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><b><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;">陈云飞简历</span></b><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;">：<span lang="EN-US">1967</span>年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">13</span>日出生，四川省达州市达县人，个体农场主和农艺师，民主“业余驯兽师”，四川“民主行为艺术家”，著名维权公民、异议人士，中国在押政治犯。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt;">1989</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;">年曾参与过“<span lang="EN-US">89</span>学运”，六四镇压后返乡谋生，仍坚守民主理念，在当地展开多种帮助访民的维权活动，后因多次试图前往北京祭奠前中共总书记赵紫阳而被成都警方长期监控、威胁；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt;">2007</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;">年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">4</span>日，曾因在《成都晚报》刊登“向坚强的六四遇难者母亲致敬”的广告，被成都警方抓捕，后被以涉嫌“煽动颠覆国家政权罪”监视居住半年；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt;">2008</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;">年，曾因以发起人名义与维权公民谭作人、冉云飞等捐血纪念六四而被警方警告、威胁；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt;">2011</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;">年，曾因被怀疑参与“茉莉花革命”而被警方软禁家中多日；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt;">2013</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;">年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">26</span>日，因不满和抗议当局禁止其出境旅行，而为其护照和港澳通行证举行别样葬礼，自此开中国此类葬礼之先河；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;">同年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">5</span>日，曾因向四川省人大常委会递交“致习近平等先生的公开信”、“要求中国最有权力的<span lang="EN-US">205</span>名部级以上官员公开财产的公民建议书”以及<span lang="EN-US">7033</span>名有效公民的签名名单，而被拒绝签收；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt;">2013</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;">年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">13</span>日，曾因租赁房屋被当局联合黑社会逼迁，搬迁中又遭遇暴徒的殴打和恶警的威胁攻击，终致其身体严重受损；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt;">2015</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;">年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月至<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月，又因其开通“成都市区到彭州中石化厂一日游旅游线路”而被当局忌恨，其活动随即被成都市警方搅局破坏。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt;">2015</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;">年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">25</span>日，因其与维权公民<span lang="EN-US">20</span>余人到成都市新津县为当年的六四死难学生肖杰、吴国峰扫墓，遂在返程途中遭到上百名持枪特警的围追堵截，<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">26</span>日即被四川省新津县警方以涉嫌“煽动颠覆国家政权罪”、“寻衅滋事罪”刑事拘留；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt;">2015</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;">年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">30</span>日，被以同罪名正式批捕。后检察院取消“煽动颠覆国家政权罪”，只以寻衅滋事罪起诉。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt;">2017</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;">年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">31</span>日陈云飞被成都武侯区法院以“寻衅滋事罪”判刑<span lang="EN-US">4</span>年。</span><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt;">2019</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;">年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">25</span>日刑满释放。</span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt;">2021</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;">年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">25</span>日，陈云飞再次被抓捕，后遭寻衅滋事罪起诉，<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">3</span>日在庭审中突然更改罪名判刑<span lang="EN-US">4</span>年。刑期至：<span lang="EN-US">2025</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">24</span>日。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"></p><div
