[港澳办赞林郑《施政报告》致力解决深层次矛盾](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20211008-1201337)
------

<div>
<p>针对香港特首林郑月娥前天发布任内最后一份《施政报告》，提出“北部都会区”发展计划等，中国国务院港澳办发言人今天积极评价称，《施政报告》致力于解决香港深层次矛盾和问题，希望香港社会各界人士多提建设性意见建议。</p><p>据港澳办微信公号消息，发言人认为，林郑月娥的这份《2021年施政报告》立足当前，谋划长远，致力于解决香港深层次矛盾和问题，体现了担当作为的精神，受到社会舆论广泛好评。</p><p>发言人说，在制定实施香港国安法和完善香港选举制度的多重保障下，香港迎来良好发展局面。希望香港社会各界人士积极参与施政报告的讨论，多提建设性意见建议，支持行政长官和特区政府依法施政，共同谋划和建设香港更加美好的未来。<br> </p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/zbpe-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=zbpe399&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_desktop-zbpe399_2.png?dHEiGouxxZpWwWTJE04dyGyUN74JOU2z" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_desktop-zbpe399_2.png?dHEiGouxxZpWwWTJE04dyGyUN74JOU2z" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/zbpe-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=zbpe399&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_mobile-zbpe399_2.png?qZnBcIGCJvPR8A2IAZOZuOxdoGZDatiI" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_mobile-zbpe399_2.png?qZnBcIGCJvPR8A2IAZOZuOxdoGZDatiI" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
