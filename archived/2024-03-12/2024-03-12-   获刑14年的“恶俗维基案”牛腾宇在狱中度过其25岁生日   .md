[获刑14年的“恶俗维基案”牛腾宇在狱中度过其25岁生日](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2024/03/1425.html)
------

<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">12</span>日，本网获悉：生于<span lang="EN-US">1999</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">12</span>日的牛腾宇今天是其<span lang="EN-US">25</span>岁的生日。他是于<span lang="EN-US">2019</span>年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">22</span>日被捕的，已在狱中度过了第五个生日。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">他被指控是<span lang="EN-US">“</span>恶俗维基网站恶势力犯罪集团案<span lang="EN-US">”</span>主犯于<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">6</span>日被以寻衅滋事罪、侵犯公民个人信息罪、非法经营罪判刑<span lang="EN-US">14</span>年。其母亲为其冤案奔走呼号，其案受到国际社会的广泛关注。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><b><span style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">牛腾宇简介</span></b><span style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">：<a name="_Hlk161131985"><span lang="EN-US">1999</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">12</span>日</a>出生，山西省晋城市高平市人，有<span lang="EN-US">“</span>计算机天才<span lang="EN-US">”</span>之称，恶俗维基服务器运维技术人员，<span lang="EN-US">“</span>恶俗维基<span lang="EN-US">”</span>站长，<span lang="EN-US">“</span>恶俗维基网站恶势力犯罪集团案<span lang="EN-US">”</span>主要受害人，中国在押政治犯。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">自<span lang="EN-US">2014</span>年起，中国大陆及海外有网友开始频繁尝试搭建和运作被称为<span lang="EN-US">“</span>恶俗圈<span lang="EN-US">”</span>的网络平台，其中<span lang="EN-US">“</span>恶俗维基<span lang="EN-US">”</span>、<span lang="EN-US">“</span>支那维基<span lang="EN-US">”</span>和<span lang="EN-US">“</span>红岸基金会<span lang="EN-US">”</span>三大网站最为知名；此期间，因其对计算机技术的痴迷与天赋，以及网站宗旨是<span lang="EN-US">“</span>揭露并记录事实<span lang="EN-US">”</span>，随加入<span lang="EN-US">“</span>恶俗维基<span lang="EN-US">”</span>网站，并成为该网站技术维护员及站长；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">2019</span><span style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">年，曾因多家<span lang="EN-US">“</span>恶俗圈<span lang="EN-US">”</span>网站频发习近平及家人、和中共高官及家属的户籍和照片等信息，其所在<span lang="EN-US">“</span>恶俗维基<span lang="EN-US">”</span>网站亦将此类信息挂于网上，遂引发中共当局的极端不满与监控；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">2019</span><span style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">22</span>日，又因其在香港爆发<span lang="EN-US">“</span>反送中<span lang="EN-US">”</span>大游行之后，与<span lang="EN-US">“</span>恶俗维基<span lang="EN-US">”</span>创办人肖彦锐相约前去香港，对香港大游行进行围观了解，返回后又于同年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月初至<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">16</span>日到日本一游。但回国一周后，<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">22</span>日，其网站全部会员尽被各地警方突然抓捕，其亦被广东省茂名市警方抓捕，并遭毒打；同年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">24</span>日，被以涉嫌<span lang="EN-US">“</span>帮助信息网络犯罪活动罪<span lang="EN-US">”</span>刑拘，关押于茂名市第一看守所；同年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">30</span>日，被茂名市检察院以同罪名予以批捕，指控其曾在恶俗维基网上<span lang="EN-US">“</span>共发表评论<span lang="EN-US">8</span>条，上传图片<span lang="EN-US">20</span>张，共有<span lang="EN-US">748</span>条的用户贡献，其本人有<span lang="EN-US">285</span>条贡献值<span lang="EN-US">”</span>等讯息，散布虚假信息，恶毒攻击、诋毁政府和国家领导人及其家属形象等，是“恶俗维基网站恶势力犯罪集团案”主犯，故而应予逮捕和判刑；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">同年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">21</span>日，经茂名市检察院批准被延长侦查羁押期限<span lang="EN-US">1</span>个月；<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">10</span>日，又被广东省佛山市警方以涉嫌<span lang="EN-US">“</span>颠覆国家政权罪<span lang="EN-US">”</span>被指定监视居住，并转关至佛山市南海区看守所。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">2020</span><span style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">22</span>日，其被解除指定监视居住，同日又被茂名市警方再次逮捕；同年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">2</span>日，其一行<span lang="EN-US">24</span>人<span lang="EN-US">“</span>涉恶势力犯罪集团案<span lang="EN-US">”</span>在广东省茂名市茂南区法院开庭受审，至<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">6</span>日，其案<span lang="EN-US">24</span>人均被宣判，其中，牛腾宇被以<span lang="EN-US">“</span>寻衅滋事罪<span lang="EN-US">”</span>（判<span lang="EN-US">8</span>年）、<span lang="EN-US">“</span>侵犯个人信息罪<span lang="EN-US">”</span>（判<span lang="EN-US">5</span>年）、<span lang="EN-US">“</span>非法经营罪<span lang="EN-US">”</span>（判<span lang="EN-US">2</span>年），最终决定执行有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">14</span>年，并处罚金人民币<span lang="EN-US">13</span>万；其等均表不服，立刻上诉；据悉，其被重判与曾发布中共高层个人信息、镇压香港及新疆等内容有关，且二审法院对其速审速决，维持原判；另据悉，<span lang="EN-US">2019</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">10</span>日到<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">20</span>日期间，为令其屈打成招，曾遭长期刑讯逼供，被吊打致残；刑期至<span lang="EN-US">2033</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">10</span>日。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">目前被羁押在广东肇庆市四会市济广塘济广北路四会监狱 十五监区 邮编：<span lang="EN-US">526237<o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">对牛腾宇的境况，本网将持续关注。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"></p><div class="separator" style="clear: both;
