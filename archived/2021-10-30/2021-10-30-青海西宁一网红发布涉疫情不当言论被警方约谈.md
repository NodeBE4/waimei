[青海西宁一网红发布涉疫情不当言论被警方约谈](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20211030-1208626)
------

<div>
<p>青海省西宁市公安局城东公安分局今天发布消息，该分局网安大队联合西宁市城东区区委网信办前天（28日）对一辖区内抖音平台网红疫情期间通过抖音视频发布不当言论进行约谈。</p><p>根据澎湃新闻报道，该局称，西宁市前天新增三名冠病病毒核酸检测阳性人员。通告发布后，西宁市部分地区出现群众集中哄抢物资的行为，城东公安分局网安大队在网上巡查时发现，城东辖区某知名“网红”利用抖音平台发布视频，内容称：“这几天大家多买点，万一过一阵西宁市封城了就买不到了。”网安大队及时将情况上报上级网安部门，并在上级网安部门的支持下迅速查清网红身份。</p><p>为尽快消除影响，该局网安大队当晚8点联合城东区委网信办，对该网红进行约谈。在约谈过程中，这名网红对自己的行为懊悔不已，并表示自己发布后认为不妥也立即删除了，承诺以后在网络平台中不会发布不当言论视频讯息，会发布更多正能量视频。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/zbpe-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=zbpe399&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_desktop-zbpe399_2.png?VersionId=dHEiGouxxZpWwWTJE04dyGyUN74JOU2z" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_desktop-zbpe399_2.png?VersionId=dHEiGouxxZpWwWTJE04dyGyUN74JOU2z" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/zbpe-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=zbpe399&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_mobile-zbpe399_2.png?VersionId=qZnBcIGCJvPR8A2IAZOZuOxdoGZDatiI" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_mobile-zbpe399_2.png?VersionId=qZnBcIGCJvPR8A2IAZOZuOxdoGZDatiI" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
