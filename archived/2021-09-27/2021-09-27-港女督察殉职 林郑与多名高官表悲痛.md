[港女督察殉职 林郑与多名高官表悲痛](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210927-1197695)
------

<div>
<p>香港一名女高级督察殉职，特首林郑月娥、政务司长李家超、保安局长邓炳强、警务处长萧泽颐和公务员事务局长聂德权纷纷表示悲痛。</p><p>据《明报》报道，前天（25日）早上8时，一艘水警截击艇在香港西部水域追截走私快艇时，疑被对方碰撞翻艇，船上四名水警堕海，其中三人获救，女高级督察林婉仪失踪。</p><p>今早（27日）7时55分，警方接获报案，称在大屿山二澳海面发现漂浮物。水警随后捞起一具浮尸，经查证为失踪女高级督察林婉仪，遗体已送往大榄水警基地。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>林郑对林婉仪家人致以最深切慰问，并称“公务员事务局及警务处会为其家人提供一切所需支援”。</p><p>萧泽颐称，警队上下对事件感到痛心，并多次怒斥走私罪犯“冷血”，称会加强打击走私活动。</p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/zbpe-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=zbpe399&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_desktop-zbpe399_2.png?dHEiGouxxZpWwWTJE04dyGyUN74JOU2z" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_desktop-zbpe399_2.png?dHEiGouxxZpWwWTJE04dyGyUN74JOU2z" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/zbpe-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=zbpe399&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_mobile-zbpe399_2.png?qZnBcIGCJvPR8A2IAZOZuOxdoGZDatiI" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_mobile-zbpe399_2.png?qZnBcIGCJvPR8A2IAZOZuOxdoGZDatiI" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
