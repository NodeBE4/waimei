<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">4</span>日，本网获悉获刑<span lang="EN-US">4</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>个月的香港反送中运动参与者李翠婷的案情及简历，现通报如下：<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">李翠婷：</span></b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">女，<span lang="EN-US">2000</span>年出生，香港油尖旺区人，文员，香港逃犯条例修订草案（俗称“送中条例”、“逃犯条例”或“引渡条例”）反对者，<span lang="EN-US">2019</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>·<span lang="EN-US">1</span>尖沙咀抗议者，香港理工大学抗议学生的声援者，中国在押政治犯。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2019</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年，因中共当局在香港推出“逃犯条例修订草案”，遭到香港民众普遍反对，遂掀起“反送中”运动，并由香港民间人权阵线发起「没有国庆，只有国殇」游行，以期在庆祝中华人民共和国成立<span lang="EN-US">70</span>周年大会期间同步进行示威，由港岛铜锣湾游行至中环，向港府表达反对“逃犯条例修订草案”的要求，但却遭到香港警方的明确反对，并被公众集会及游行上诉委员会上诉后被驳回；不料港府官方的这一反对立场立即激怒了要求高度自治，“真普选”、“真自由”的香港民众的强烈反对，故有大批民众决定自我承担法律后果，于<span lang="EN-US">2019</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">-11</span>月（国庆节期间及之后数十日），在港多区自行发起“反对逃犯条例修订草案”示威抗议行动；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2019</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月，李翠婷与约<span lang="EN-US">100</span>名示威者在声援被实弹警察围城的香港理工大学（以下简称“理大”）抗议学生时，在突破警方封锁线从校内逃走时，被女警打倒在地，并被以“参与暴力”为由野蛮拘捕；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">之后又被警方指控，因参与声援抗议行动时向警方“投掷汽油弹”，并与多人统一“身穿深色衣物、戴面罩及手持雨伞等”，故被以涉嫌“参与暴动罪”交由香港区域法院提堂聆讯；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">此间虽有暂时交保释放，但后又被收监，并与同期被捕的另<span lang="EN-US">8</span>名抗议<span lang="EN-US">/</span>声援者（共<span lang="EN-US">9</span>人案）一同转交到香港西九龙裁判法院受审；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2022</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">12</span>日，其终被该法院以“暴动罪” 判处有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">4</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>个月，刑期至<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">29</span>日；另<span lang="EN-US">8</span>人亦被以“暴动罪”和“管有攻击性武器罪”分别判处<span lang="EN-US">4</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>个月至<span lang="EN-US">5</span>年不等刑期。目前在香港罗湖惩教所（香港新界上水河上乡路<span lang="EN-US">163</span>号）服刑。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"></p><div class="separator" style="clear: both;
