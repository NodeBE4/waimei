<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">4</span>日，本网获悉： <span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">23</span>日早上刘家财跟朋友说他原来的工作单位葛洲坝集团通知他回去处理一个事情，结果被抓了。随后其妻子王玉兰也遭抓捕。后王玉兰已获释回家，刘家财仍遭羁押。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">刘家财上次获刑<span lang="EN-US">5</span>年，<span lang="EN-US">2018</span>年出狱后，有段时间在东莞一个公司做监理。后被宜昌国保要求回到宜昌打工至今。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">：<span lang="EN-US">1965</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">26</span>日出生，湖北省宜昌市人，原宜昌市葛洲坝集团职工，原云南省某工程监理公司工程师，民主维权公民，政治犯。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">1998</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年—<span lang="EN-US">2000</span>年，曾因湖北葛洲坝下岗职工合法权利遭受非法侵害等问题，为了维护数千下岗职工切身利益，积极发起和组建独立工会，领导葛洲坝集团教育经费维权和反腐运动，而被湖北省宜昌市警方逮捕，并于<span lang="EN-US">2001</span>年以“煽动颠覆国家政权罪”判处管制<span lang="EN-US">2</span>年，剥夺政治权利<span lang="EN-US">2</span>年；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">出狱后，曾前往云南省昭通市谋职，并在某工程监理公司任监理工程师，但工作之余仍坚守民主理念，活跃于网络民主运动其间，为此曾多次遭云南省警方威胁、警告和传唤；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2013</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">2</span>日，再次因参与民主维权活动和维权公民发起的“同城饭醉”活动，并在腾讯微博、新浪微博、<span lang="EN-US">QQ</span>网上转发《李向阳复仇血洗公告（并招募敢死队员）》等，随被当地警方以涉嫌“扰乱公共秩序罪”行政拘留<span lang="EN-US">10</span>天，后又被以涉嫌“煽动颠覆国家政权罪”转为刑拘，且关押于湖北省宜昌市第一看守所；</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">2013</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">18</span>日，被再次更换罪名以涉嫌“寻衅滋事罪”正式逮捕；</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2015</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;
