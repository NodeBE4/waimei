[获刑3年的广西人权律师陈家鸿今刑满出狱](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2022/04/3_28.html)
------

<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">28</span>日，本网获悉：获刑<span lang="EN-US">3</span>年的广西人权律师陈家鸿今刑满出狱，现已回到家中。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">陈家鸿：<span lang="EN-US">1966</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月出生，广西壮族自治区北流县隆盛镇平坡村人，又名陈文胆，字数真，早年为广西北流县鸭壤小学教师，知名业余书法家、字画家，原南宁市百举鸣律师事务所执业律师，敏感案件代理人，中国人权律师团成员，人权律师，政治犯。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">自从成为“百举鸣律师事务所”（现已被当局取缔）中一名执业律师以来，因长期参与维权敏感案件，追求民主人权并为之积极发声，随屡遭中共当局严打逼迫；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2017</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月，曾因积极介入了震惊网络的“广西村民罗继标在看守所离奇死亡事件”，而遭广西警方的持枪围困；<span lang="EN-US">2017</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月，曾因同一事件，其介入维权行为被广西律师协会视为违规，从而被该律师协会给予“公开谴责行业处分的通报”；<span lang="EN-US">2018</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月，又被南宁市司法局行政处罚停业半年；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2019</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">29</span>日，其被北流县警方以涉嫌“煽动颠覆国家政权罪”抓捕、刑拘，其家被查抄；之后，其又被北流县检察院以同罪由予以批捕，并被指控因其反对官员腐败、呼吁建立民主宪政等带有政治倾向的公开言论、书法作品及行为艺术等涉及挑战现政权，尤其是其中一副书法作品，内含“成立暗杀突击队！清算邪恶官僚，加速民主进程”等激进敏感字眼，触犯了政府的相关法律条例，其行为属于违法；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2019</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月，其案件被地方检察院移交至玉林巿中级法院起诉；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2021</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">14</span>日，终被玉林市中级法院在其辩护律师未到场的情况下，一审以“煽动颠覆国家政权罪”判处有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">3</span>年，刑期至<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">28</span>日；</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;">此前被羁押于广西玉林市看守所。</span></p><p class="MsoNormal"></p><div class="separator" style="clear: both;
