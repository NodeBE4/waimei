font-size: 12.0pt;">马淑华</span></b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">：黑龙江哈尔滨市法轮功学员，女，<span lang="EN-US">1952</span>年生，现年<span lang="EN-US">72</span>岁。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">2021</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">年，马淑华两次遭哈尔滨警方的抓捕、关押。出狱后被迫流离失所，来到广东。<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">19</span>日其又在深圳被抓捕，<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月才获悉，她已被判<span lang="EN-US">4</span>年，于<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月底被转监到黑龙江省女子监狱。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">在黑龙江省女子监狱，她因为脖子上突出的大包吃不了饭，几次晕厥，后来她被检查出患甲状腺瘤，于<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月在医院做的手术切除，随后她又被送回女子监狱关押。马淑华的身体有多种疾病，胆囊炎、偏头痛、胃病、子宫粘连等，吃药、打针都不管用。刑期至：<span lang="EN-US">2027</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">18</span>日。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体;
