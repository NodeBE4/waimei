mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">28</span>日，本网获悉：遭判刑<span lang="EN-US">12</span>年的原河北省唐山市开滦第十中学教师、法轮功学员卞丽潮已于<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">24</span>日下午获释回家。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">1966</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年出生，河北省人，原河北省唐山市开滦第十中学教师，法轮功信仰者，良心犯。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">曾因信仰和修炼法轮功，而遭到中共警方长期监控；<span lang="EN-US">2012</span>年初，因中共当局通过监控手段得知有法轮功学员从事光盘盒生意，遂于<span lang="EN-US">2012</span>年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">25</span>日，统一部署，以“大案”“要案”为目标，在河北、辽宁、山东等十数个省份集结各地国保、“<span lang="EN-US">610</span>”派出所警员，按照监听查获的名单，野蛮逮捕了一百多名法轮功学员，卞丽潮亦因法轮功学员身份而被河北省唐山市警方刑拘；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">逮捕刑拘期间，多次遭遇严刑逼供，其妻女受到严厉威胁，其家被抄，且家中做生意用的流动资金（注：其被抓走时，家中约有十几万元现金，尚未来得及存入银行）竟被警方非法侵占，并以开假单据方式诱骗其妻签字，谎称是法轮功资金，以示为“证据”；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2012</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">26</span>日，被河北省唐山市中级法院以“信仰、修炼法轮功邪教”为罪由重判有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">12</span>年。最初在河北省保定监狱服刑，因其不堪忍受狱中酷刑折磨（注：狱方每天会派<span lang="EN-US">4</span>个狱警带着<span lang="EN-US">3</span>个犯人对其进行轮番“包夹”监控、折磨），致其心脏病等病情严重恶化，<span lang="EN-US">2013</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">6</span>日起转到河北省石家庄第四监狱第八监区服刑。今获悉卞丽潮于<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">24</span>日下午已经刑满回家。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"></p><div class="separator" style="clear: both;
