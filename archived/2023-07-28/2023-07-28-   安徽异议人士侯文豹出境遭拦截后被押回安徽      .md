[安徽异议人士侯文豹出境遭拦截后被押回安徽](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2023/07/blog-post_28.html)
------

<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">28</span>日，本网获悉：安徽宿州异议人士侯文豹自<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月初失业后，受大环的影响，半年多未能找到工作。其妻子吴敏因<span lang="EN-US">2015</span>年手术不成功落下后遗症，以致腰部经常性疼痛，导致无法工作劳动。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">据悉，侯文豹没有社保、医保，原本依靠他每个月近<span lang="EN-US">3000</span>元钱的收入还可以维持正常生活，失业半年时间，让其顿感生活压力陡增，再加上国保及派出所警察经常性的骚扰，故萌生去国谋生之意。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">7</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">月<span lang="EN-US">22</span>日上午<span lang="EN-US">9</span>点半，侯文豹在宿州火车站买到去徐州的车票后，正在候车时，于<span lang="EN-US">10</span>点<span lang="EN-US">30</span>分左右，宿州市埇桥区国保王海洋大队长来电两次被其拒接。<span lang="EN-US">11</span>点<span lang="EN-US">40</span>分到徐州后又有宿州市局国保支队负责人来电，他依然拒接并随及关闭手机。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">在徐州火车站买了到昆明的车票后，侯文豹于<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">24</span>日早上<span lang="EN-US">5</span>点到达昆明，随后买了去西双版纳的车票，下午去老挝驻景洪领事馆办公室办理签证。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">7</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">月<span lang="EN-US">25</span>日上午<span lang="EN-US">11</span>点<span lang="EN-US">40</span>分，侯文豹到达西双版纳的磨憨口岸，持护照出境时，被边检拦截并宣读法定禁止出境的告之书，随后边检联系宿州警方，并告诉他下午宿州警方会带他回宿州。当晚<span lang="EN-US">11</span>点多钟，宿州警方数人赶到西双版纳。经过两天的辗转，于<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">27</span>日晚上<span lang="EN-US">6</span>点多回到宿州。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"></p><div class="separator" style="clear: both;
