<p><span></span></p><a name='more'></a><p class="MsoNormal"><br /></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">27</span>日，本网获悉获刑<span lang="EN-US">5</span>年的广东省兴宁市法轮功学员谢育军先生的案情及简历，现在通报如下：</span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">谢育军：</span></b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">广东省梅州市下辖兴宁市法轮功学员，男，<span lang="EN-US">1968</span>年生，<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">56</span>岁。因</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">谢育军坚持修炼法轮功，多次被中共人员抓捕，被关押到拘留所、看守所、洗脑班、劳教所、监狱，他曾两次被劳教共计</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">4</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">年半，一次遭判刑</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">5</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">年，在劳教所、监狱</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">9</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">年半间受尽残忍的酷刑折磨。</span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">谢育军于<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">25</span>日被兴宁市公安局国保大队、兴田派出所警察抓捕，关押在兴宁市看守所迫害。后遭刑事拘留、批捕、起诉，谢育军<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">18</span>日、<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">12</span>日两次被开庭。<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">20</span>日，梅州市梅县区法院宣判：谢育军被判刑<span lang="EN-US">5</span>年、罚金七万元。刑期至：<span lang="EN-US">2029</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">24</span>日。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><br /><p></p>
