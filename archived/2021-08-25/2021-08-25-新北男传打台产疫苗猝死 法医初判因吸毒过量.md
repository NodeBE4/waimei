[新北男传打台产疫苗猝死 法医初判因吸毒过量](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210825-1186337)
------

<div>
<p>台湾新北市一名39岁男子前天接种台产高端疫苗，昨天昏倒后送院急救不治，检察官与法医今天（25日）相验后初步认定是吸毒过量致死。</p><p>据台湾《联合报》报道，新北市芦洲区39岁的彭姓男子，昨天在家吸毒后死亡，由于他前天刚打过高端疫苗，时机敏感令外界产生联想。</p><p>检察官偕同法医今天下午相验，初步认定是吸毒过量导致心肺衰竭致死，不需解剖进一步检验，家属没有异议。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>据报道，该男子没有慢性病史，但有吸食毒品记录，仅今年便已四次疑因吸毒昏倒送医，其中两次验出禁药阳性反应。</p><p>家属昨天上午发现男子倒卧家中浴厕地板，已无呼吸，手上还握着用过的海洛因毒品注射针筒，将其送医急救无效死亡，事后警方又在他家浴厕的卫生纸盒里找到安非他命残渣袋。</p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
