[台湾四艘纪德舰罕见同框操演](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210825-1186322)
------

<div>
<p>台湾汉光演习将于下月13日登场，海军舰队指挥部近日进行攻防模拟对抗为汉光暖身，四艘排水量最大的主战舰艇纪德舰，罕见“同框”操演，引人关注。</p><p>根据联合新闻网引述台湾海军舰队指挥部面簿粉丝团“中华民国海军舰队”消息，海军168舰队日前执行战术对抗操演，依程序、步骤与要领执行，展现训练成果。</p><p>根据操演照片显示，引人关注之处在于，海军四艘纪德级军舰罕见“同框”操演，气势十足。此外，除了舰队攻防对抗，也模拟飞弹来袭，由方阵快砲接战；MK45舰砲也实施实弹射击，场面震撼。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>台湾海军目前共拥有四艘纪德舰，满载排水量约9000吨，为海军排水量最大的主战舰艇，具有战场管理能力及防空火力，且纪德舰船艏下方有具庞大声纳音鼓，水下来袭的敌目标都能掌握，战力相当坚强。</p><p>对于四艘基隆级舰在济阳级舰率领下出海进行实弹射击，五艘舰艇同框的场面，有网民称赞为“海上长城”，也有人关切是否台海情势紧张，让四艘主力舰同时出动。</p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
