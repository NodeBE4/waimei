[四川泸州市古蔺县78岁法轮功学员胡彪遭中共当局判刑9年](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2024/01/789.html)

<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">2</span>日，本网获悉：四川泸州市古蔺县<span lang="EN-US">78</span>岁法轮功学员胡彪遭中共当局判刑<span lang="EN-US">9</span>年。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">胡彪</span></b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">：四川泸州市古蔺县岁法轮功学员，男，<span lang="EN-US">1945</span>年生。自一九九九年以来，胡彪多次被中共人员绑架，关押在看守所、洗脑班，他三次被劳教，曾被劳改四年半，共陷中共冤狱<span lang="EN-US">10</span>年有余。如今，他又遭九年冤刑。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">胡彪为避开古蔺县警察的骚扰，于<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3/4</span>月份被迫离家出走到异乡。<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">28</span>日，他在异乡被古蔺县警察抓捕回古蔺县，关押在看守所，警察扬言：不“转化”要重判。胡彪老人拒绝放弃法轮功信仰，遂于<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月被古蔺县法院判刑<span lang="EN-US">9</span>年重刑，他于<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">3</span>日被关押到四川嘉州监狱服刑。胡彪今年七十八岁，至服刑终止日2031年9月27日，老人年已八十七岁。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p>
