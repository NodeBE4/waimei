font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2019</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">31</span>日，被江苏省镇江市润州区法院以“寻衅滋事罪”（判<span lang="EN-US">3</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月）和“偷越国境罪”（判<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月），数罪并罚，最终执行有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">3</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>个月，并处罚金<span lang="EN-US">2000</span>元，刑期至<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">30</span>日；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体;
