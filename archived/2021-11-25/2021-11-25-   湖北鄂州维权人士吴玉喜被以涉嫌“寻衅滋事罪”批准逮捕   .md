[湖北鄂州维权人士吴玉喜被以涉嫌“寻衅滋事罪”批准逮捕](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2021/11/blog-post_49.html)

<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">25</span>日，本网获悉：近日，<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">29</span>日从北京带回后即被隔离关押的湖北鄂州维权人士吴玉喜的家属收到逮捕通知书。遂获悉，<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">19</span>日，吴玉喜被梁子湖区检察院以涉嫌“寻衅滋事罪”批准逮捕，现羁押在鄂州市公安局第一看守所。</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">吴玉喜是湖北省鄂州市梁湖区太和镇花黄村上吴湾村民，因政府侵占其耕地和宅基地、剥夺其被救济权而上访，因上访吴玉喜长期被地方当局非法监控维稳。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2021</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">30</span>日，吴玉喜被地方官员从北京带回来后强制隔离了一次；<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">10</span>日辖区警察又上门要强行带她去隔离，在她坚持拒绝下没被带走。此次<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">29</span>日从北京带回后即被隔离关押。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">近日，吴玉喜家属收到逮捕通知书，得知其已于<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">19</span>日被检察院以“寻衅滋事罪”批准逮捕。逮捕通知书显示，编号<span lang="EN-US">----</span>梁子公（太和所）捕通字<span lang="EN-US">[2021]3070</span>号，由鄂州市梁子湖区人民检察院批准，梁子湖区公安分局执行逮捕，现羁押在鄂州市公安局第一看守所，地址：鄂州市鄂城区葛山大道<span lang="EN-US">46</span>号。</span></p><div class="separator" style="clear: both;
