[获刑3年6个月的安徽合肥人权捍卫者、工伤致残人士、维权网义工周维林已刑满获释](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2023/10/36.html)
------

<div>
<p><span></span></p><a name='more'></a><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;"><br /></span><p></p><p><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;">（维权网信息中心报道）</span><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt;">2023</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;">年</span><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt;">10</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;">月</span><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt;">21</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;">日，本网获悉：获刑</span><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt;">3</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;">年</span><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt;">6</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;">个月的安徽合肥人权捍卫者、工伤致残人士、维权网义工周维林今天已刑满获释。</span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">周维林：</span></b><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;"><span lang="EN-US">1965</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">27</span>日出生，原安徽省拖拉机厂退休职工，安徽合肥人权捍卫者、工伤致残人士、维权网义工；<span lang="EN-US">2015</span>年曹顺利人权捍卫者奖获得者。曾于<span lang="EN-US">1986</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">18</span>日因工受伤，成为丧失劳动能力的伤残人员，但原所在单位一直不给于办理伤残认定和退休等手续，自此走上维权之路。自<span lang="EN-US">2009</span>年起，尤其是<span lang="EN-US">2011</span>年—<span lang="EN-US">2013</span>年期间，因长期坚持实名报导安徽及全国各地多种公权力违法侵权事件，并成为“维权网”信息员，而招致当局的强烈不满和忌恨，曾先后<span lang="EN-US">5</span>次被合肥警方处以行政拘留，并且多次被警方抄家、传唤、威胁甚至殴打。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2013</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">8</span>日，各地维权人士发起声援“安妮失学事件”的活动，周维林因前往进行实时报导，而被合肥警方于<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">16</span>日强行带走，处以行政拘留<span lang="EN-US">10</span>日；同年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">4</span>日，又因参与合肥网友聚餐纪念<span lang="EN-US">64</span>活动，遭到警方的报复性拘留，之后获释；但时至<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">4</span>日，合肥警方再次因<span lang="EN-US">64</span>纪念活动而以“涉嫌虚构事实扰乱公共秩序”事由对其进行传唤，并处以行政拘留<span lang="EN-US">10</span>日。<span lang="EN-US">2013</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">6</span>日，合肥警方第三次以参与声援安妮失学事件，维护安妮受教育权活动为由，将其与张林、李化平、姚诚等维权人士一同拘捕，并以涉嫌“聚众扰乱公共场所秩序罪”而刑事拘留；<span lang="EN-US">2014</span>年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月被当地检察院以同罪名正式批捕，之后被安徽省合肥市地方法院以“聚众扰乱公共场所秩序罪”判处有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">1</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月；<span lang="EN-US">2015</span>年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">23</span>日，刑满获释。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">出狱后仍然在一直坚持为劳工和残疾人维权。周维林于<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">12</span>日被合肥市公安局瑶海分局城东派出所（电话：<span lang="EN-US">0551-64483496</span>）秘密抓走了。后获悉遭刑事拘留、逮捕。周维林案于<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">20</span>日在合肥开庭审理，未当庭宣判。<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">1</span>日年获悉周维林获刑<span lang="EN-US">3</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月。现已刑满获释。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"></p><div class="separator" style="clear: both;
