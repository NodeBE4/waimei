[因拒交罚金山西太原郇城教会安彦魁传道和张乘豪弟兄再遭司法拘留十五天](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2023/08/blog-post_65.html)

<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">12</span>日，本网获悉：山西太原郇城教会安彦魁传道和张乘豪弟兄因“偷越国境罪”判有期徒刑一年（<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">22</span>日<span lang="EN-US">-2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">21</span>日已经执行完毕），并处罚金五千元。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">他们因为拒绝汾阳法院催缴罚金的通知，在带领夏令营而被行政拘留十五天后，在上周四（<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">10</span>日）旋即被汾阳法院执行局带走，执行司法拘留十五天，从太原市拘留所转至吕梁市离石区拘留所，继续为信仰的缘故坐监。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">2.</span><span style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">本人不回应<span lang="EN-US">5000</span>元罚款缴纳的通知，不是出于私欲害怕失去自己的财产；而是不愿意玷污个人的良心，屈从配合邪恶和谎言对善良的残害。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 楷体;
