<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">5</span>日，本网获悉获刑<span lang="EN-US">2</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月的广州<span lang="EN-US">11.12</span>案成员、民主异议人士韦亚妮的案情及简历，现在通报如下：<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">韦亚妮女士于<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">28</span>日刑满，韦亚妮在刑满后到广州打工，今天<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">5</span>日她收到广西国保打来电话，说要把她从广州抓回去，韦亚妮表示要以死抗争。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">韦亚妮简历</span></b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">：女，<span lang="EN-US">1972</span>年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">2</span>日出生，广西壮族自治区河池市天峨县人，河池市天峨县坡结乡坡结村达良屯<span lang="EN-US">29</span>号村民，维权代表，维权民主人士，中国曾押政治犯。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2006</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年，曾因广西当局在河池市天峨县龙滩水电站搞基建，该县村民被要求移民，但在发放移民补偿款过程中，却有原天峨县县长蒋家柏等人擅自截留丶贪污移民补偿款，及韦本人耕地被淹未获补偿及等事发生，当地村民为了主持公道，捍卫权益，就推举其等多人作为维权代表驻京信访，自此遂成为地方政府维稳打压的对象；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">因其持续不断维权，<span lang="EN-US">2008</span>年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">10</span>日，曾被河池市警方处行政拘留<span lang="EN-US">10</span>天；<span lang="EN-US">2008</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">25</span>日，又曾被河池市劳动教养管理委员会以“扰乱秩序”为由处劳动教养<span lang="EN-US">2</span>年，至<span lang="EN-US">2010</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">13</span>日予以释放；<span lang="EN-US">2010</span>年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">2</span>日，再次曾被河池市劳动教养管理委员会处劳动教养<span lang="EN-US">2</span>年，至<span lang="EN-US">2012</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">2</span>日予以获释；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2014</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">18</span>日，又被天峨县警方以涉嫌“寻衅滋事罪”等刑拘；后转逮捕，控其：<span lang="EN-US">1</span>、因与他人录制劳动教养期间被管教干部体罚虐待视频，并上传于博讯网，属于捏造事实诋毁他人形象，故而构成诽谤罪；<span lang="EN-US">2</span>、同年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">4</span>日因与他人“到最高法院大门口禁区进行集会、举牌、拉横幅、照相摄像、喊口号、散发传单等示威活动”，造成公共场所严重混乱，后把此次活动的照片视频又发于网上，故而构成寻衅滋事罪；<span lang="EN-US">2015</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">23</span>日，其被天峨县法院以“诽谤罪”和“寻衅滋事罪”判处有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">4</span>年，直至<span lang="EN-US">2018</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">17</span>日刑满释放；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2020</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月，其在云南省西双版纳景洪市打工时，突然失踪，后知在“广州<span lang="EN-US">11</span>·<span lang="EN-US">12</span>大抓捕”中被广州市国安局带走，并于<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">3</span>日被指定居所监视居住；后与范一平、范文成、赖天龙、胡天峰等四名民主人士被以涉嫌“颠覆国家政权罪”拘捕；<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">3</span>日，知其案被移送至广州市检察院审查起诉；之后，又于同年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">8</span>日，案宗被移送至广州市中级法院，<span lang="EN-US">5</span>人均被以“颠覆国家政权罪”起诉，案由涉及组党，具体事实无非聚餐闲聊时政、去云南旅游等；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2023</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">7</span>日，终被广州市中级法院以“颠覆国家政权罪”判处有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">2</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月；刑期至<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">28</span>日，现刑满释放。此前被羁押于广州市国家安全局看守所。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"></p><div class="separator" style="clear: both;
