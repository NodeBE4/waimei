[获刑4年的湖北省赤壁市民主人士陈剑雄转监至湖北省咸宁监狱服刑](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2024/08/4.html)
------

<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">6</span>日，本网获悉：获刑<span lang="EN-US">4</span>年的湖北省赤壁市民主人士陈剑雄（本名陈进新）与<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">24</span>日被转监至湖北省咸宁监狱服刑，地址：湖北省咸宁市桂乡大道特<span lang="EN-US">6</span>号，邮编：<span lang="EN-US">437100</span>。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">湖北省赤壁市民主人士陈剑雄（本名陈进新）案于<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">8</span>日上午在湖北省通城县法院一审宣判，其被以寻衅滋事罪判刑<span lang="EN-US">4</span>年。陈剑雄不服判决，依法上诉。<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">23</span>日，湖北省咸宁市中院对湖北异议人士 陈剑雄案作出终审裁定，维持原判<span lang="EN-US">4</span>年有期徒刑，刑期至<span lang="EN-US">2026</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">11</span>日。此前羁押在湖北赤壁市看守所。这是他第三次被抓捕判刑。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><b><span style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">陈剑雄（本名陈进新）简历：</span></b><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">1974</span><span style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">年出生，湖北省赤壁市人，网名陈剑雄，“赤壁五君子”之一，南方街头运动践行者，民主维权公民，中国在押政治犯。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">自<span lang="EN-US">2013</span>年以来，曾多次参与国内的围观声援活动，勇于多次举牌、拉横幅表达对民主宪政的政治要求，是南方街头运动杰出代表之一，亦因此曾多次遭到中共警方的拘留、刑拘、酷刑和强行驱赶等；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">2013</span><span style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">25</span>日，曾因与袁小华、袁奉初、黄文勋等民主维权者在湖北省赤壁市宣传民主政治，而被当地警方以“煽动颠覆国家政权”为由刑拘，<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">13</span>日被取保获释；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">2013</span><span style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">30</span>日，曾与民主维权者谢文飞在广东省佛山市街头举横幅，要求“废除一党专制，建立民主中国”，而被佛山警方再次以“煽动颠覆国家政权罪”刑拘，并关押在天河区看守所，备受酷刑折磨，<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">30</span>日被取保获释；<span lang="EN-US">2014</span>年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月，曾因参加山东曲阜薛福顺非正常死亡事件观察团奔赴曲阜追查真相，多次在曲阜遭警方殴打和驱赶；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">2014</span><span style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月，曾因到黑龙江省声援“建三江律师被拘事件”，而被当警方行政拘留<span lang="EN-US">15</span>日；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">2014</span><span style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月，曾因到武汉市与多名友人会见佛教法师徐志强，被武汉警方抓走，又被以“煽动颠覆国家政权罪”刑拘，<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">17</span>日被取保候审；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">2014</span><span style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月，因再次前往河南郑州参与公民声援“郑州十君子事件”，并到河南焦作市声援“张小玉夫妇杀警事件”，遂于<span lang="EN-US">2014</span>年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">1</span>日被警方在郑州第三看守所附近抓走，且被湖北省赤壁市警方直接报检察院以涉嫌“扰乱社会公共秩序罪”执行逮捕，羁押于赤壁市看守所；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">2016</span><span style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">16</span>日，被湖北省咸宁市赤壁市法院以“寻衅滋事罪”判处有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">2</span>年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>个月；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">2016</span><span style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">31</span>日，刑满释放；出狱后，其追求民主意志弥坚；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">2017</span><span style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">29</span>日，因与维权友人袁兵、梁一鸣去赤壁市公安局国保大队投诉出狱后遭长期监控、骚扰等事宜，终因双方理念不同、争论激烈而不欢而散；<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">3</span>日，再次被赤壁市警方以涉嫌“寻衅滋事罪”抓捕刑拘；<span lang="EN-US">2017</span>年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">8</span>日，被赤壁市检察院正式批捕，并被羁押于赤壁看守所：<span lang="EN-US">2019</span>年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">12</span>日，终被湖北省赤壁市法院一审以“寻衅滋事罪”判处有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">3</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月；其不服上诉；<span lang="EN-US">2019</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">28</span>日，经咸宁市中级法院二审裁决，依然维持原判；<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">2</span>日，刑满释放。此前在湖北省咸宁监狱服刑。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">2022</span><span style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">12</span>日陈剑雄又被当地警方刑事拘留，后被批捕<span lang="EN-US">,</span>罪名是寻衅滋事。后遭起诉，<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">9</span>日开庭审理，未宣判。<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">8</span>日宣判，被判刑<span lang="EN-US">4</span>年，刑期至：<span lang="EN-US">2026</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">11</span>日。此前被羁押在湖北赤壁市看守所，<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">24</span>日被转监到湖北省咸宁监狱服刑（地址：湖北省咸宁市桂乡大道特<span lang="EN-US">6</span>号，邮编：<span lang="EN-US">437100</span>）<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体;
