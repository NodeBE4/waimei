<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">4</span>日，本网获悉获刑<span lang="EN-US">3</span>年的山东省平度市法轮功学员李丽女士的案情及简历，现在通报如下：<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">李丽：</span></b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">山东省青岛市下辖平度市法轮功学员，女，<span lang="EN-US">1972</span>年生，现年<span lang="EN-US">52</span>岁。家住平度市凤台街道店前村，她于一九九九年正月开始修炼法轮功。在中共自<span lang="EN-US">1999</span>年开始对法轮功的疯狂迫害中，李丽多次被绑架、关押，曾一次被劳教、三次被判刑，累计陷冤狱十三年半。狱中曾遭残忍酷刑折磨。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">李丽女士于<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">30</span>日被国保警察从家中抓捕、关押构陷。<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">2</span>日，她在青岛普东看守所被青岛市黄岛区法院庭审。<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年三月下旬获悉：李丽被判刑<span lang="EN-US">3</span>年，罚金一万元。刑期至：<span lang="EN-US">2026</span>年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">29</span>日。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><br /><br />
