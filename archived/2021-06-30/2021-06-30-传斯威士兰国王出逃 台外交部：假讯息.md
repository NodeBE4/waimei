[传斯威士兰国王出逃 台外交部：假讯息](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210630-1162804)
------

<div>
<p>与台湾建交的斯威士兰近日政局动荡，据传国王已出国避难。台湾外交部今天表示，斯国政府已发声明澄清，国王仍在国内领导国家。</p><p>据《自由时报》报道，非洲国家斯威士兰为专制君主政体，示威者日前走上街头，要求进行民主改革。</p><p>南非广播公司（SABC）昨天引述数则未经证实的消息报道，斯国示威活动越演越烈，国王恩斯瓦帝三世（Mswati III）已离开斯国避难。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>台外交部发言人欧江安今天说，斯威士兰政府已发表正式声明澄清，恩斯瓦帝三世目前仍在国内持续领导国家，斯国政府同时呼吁人民保持冷静、自制及和平。</p><p>欧江安说，台外交部支持斯国政府及人民以和平理性的做法维持社会安定，并对有心人士散布假讯息，造成斯国社会不安表达谴责。</p><p> </p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
