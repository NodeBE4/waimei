[台湾男童学习柔道被重摔27次死亡](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210630-1162763)
------

<div>
<p>台湾台中市一名7岁黄姓男童4月21日在校外道馆上柔道课时，遭教练和学长重摔27次，导致其颅内出血住院治疗70天，昨晚在父母的同意下，拔管宣告不治。</p><p>据Newstalk新闻报道，重摔男童的何姓教练，在本月4日已被台中地方检察署依伤害致重伤罪起诉，如今男童死亡，法官估计会加重量刑。此前，这名何姓教练以新台币10万元（4817新元）交保。</p><p>男童家属4月23日举行记者会控诉，男童在校外道馆上柔道课时被教练及小四学长重摔27次，过程中男童求饶，仍被学长强拉侧摔，趴在地上无法起身。陪同上课的男童舅舅指出，外甥才学柔道两个礼拜，一共上八次课，事发当晚竟被学长摔了20多次，教练也出手至少七次，导致脑部受创濒临脑死，送医急救后仍陷昏迷。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>据了解，黄姓男童遭连续重摔导致颅内出血、中枢神经衰竭、昏迷指数三等重大难治伤害，紧急送医后一直住院治疗，但这几天心跳、血压一直往下降，状况每况愈下，医师与家属说明跟沟通后，家属同意拔管，昨晚9点多不治。</p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
