mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">30</span>日，本网获悉：昨天（<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">29</span>日）<span lang="EN-US">,</span>福州维权人士梁白端因<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月底被鼓楼区南街派出所公安任晓丹暴力殴打后，其右侧第<span lang="EN-US">4</span>前肋骨骨折，右肩筋断了一条（无钱手术），已花费<span lang="EN-US">2</span>万多元医药费，身体却始终未有恢复，要到医院复查身体；刚跨出家门就被已蹲守在她家门外的多名不明身份人员阻拦，不让梁白端就医，还拼命扯拽梁。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">据了解从本月<span lang="EN-US">11</span>日开始，梁白端就被一天<span lang="EN-US">3</span>班、一班<span lang="EN-US">2</span>名不明身份人员贴身跟踪，梁报警，公安处警后，只是要求那二人“文明”跟踪。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体;
