[消息：滴滴IPO价格可能定在指导价区间高端或以上](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210630-1162725)
------

<div>
<p>据一位直接消息人士称，中国网约车巨头滴滴将把其美国首次公开发行(IPO)价格定在指导价区间高端或更高水平。</p><p>根据路透社报道，滴滴将发行2.88亿股美国存托股份(ADS)，指导价区间为13美元（17.49新元）至14美元。</p><p>按指导价区间高端计算，IPO将筹资40.3亿美元，这将使滴滴的估值达到672亿美元。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>由于信息尚未公开，消息人士不能具名。</p><p>滴滴暂未回应置评请求。</p><p>根据路透看到的销售文件，IPO最终定价将在周二美国市场收盘时确定。</p><p>上周滴滴簿记建档第一天即获全额认购，周一提前一天结束认购。两位消息人士昨天称，此次IPO获得几倍的超额认购。</p><div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div><p>如果行使超额配售选择权，滴滴可能会额外发行4320万股。根据销售文件，滴滴股票将于今天在纽约市场开始交易。</p>            <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
