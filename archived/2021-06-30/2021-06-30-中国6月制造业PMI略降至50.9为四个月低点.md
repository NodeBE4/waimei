[中国6月制造业PMI略降至50.9为四个月低点](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210630-1162749)
------

<div>
<p>中国国家统计局和物流与采购联合会(CFLP)周三联合公布，宏观经济先行指标6月官方制造业采购经理人指数(PMI)降至50.9，为四个月低点。略高于路透调查预估中值50.8，略低于5月份的51。</p><p>据路透社报道，从分项指数来看，在构成制造业PMI的五个分类指数中，生产指数和新订单指数均高于临界点，原材料库存指数、从业人员指数和供应商配送时间指数均低于临界点。</p><p>从企业规模看，大、中型企业PMI分别为51.7和50.8，比上月回落0.1和0.3个点，均高于临界点；小型企业PMI为49.1，比上月回升0.3个点，低于临界点。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>中国国家统计局并公布，中国6月综合PMI产出指数为52.9，比上月回落1.3个点，表明中国企业生产经营活动总体扩张步伐有所放缓。</p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
