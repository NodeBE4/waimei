<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">13</span>日，本网获悉遭中共当局抓捕的山西省太原市郇城归正教会同工赵维凯的案情及简历，现在通报如下：<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">山西省吕梁市文水县人，基督徒，山西省太原市郇城归正教会同工，中国在押良心犯。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">赵维凯因信仰耶稣基督而成为基督徒，并在山西省太原市郇城归正教会是一名积极的同工（即为教会服务的义工）；又因其所在教会是家庭教会，且支持教会拒绝服从中共官方认可的“三自教会”管理等，随多次被太原市相关部门约谈，被禁止在家进行圣经研读和进行教会团契活动，并以不听警告就被抓捕相威胁；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2021</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">17</span>日，其被山西省吕梁市文水县警方突然在不出具任何有效文件的前提下，以“宗教诱骗”为名传唤带走，随后其家遭查抄，当场搜走家中书籍、计算机及硬盘等多个私人对象；其妻李鑫亦被传唤，直至当晚<span lang="EN-US">7</span>时才得释放，但其却仍被拘押，并被处以行政拘留<span lang="EN-US">15</span>天；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2021</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">20</span>日，曾有福建信恩行律师事务所邓庆高律师前往山西省文水县离石区拘留所申请与其会见，但遭拒绝；同年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">1</span>日，行政拘留期满日，又被文水县警方以“涉及宗教”为罪由转为刑拘；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2021</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">20</span>日，被山西省吕梁市文水县检察院以涉嫌“非法宣扬恐怖主义、极端主义物品罪”予以正式批捕；后其案又被吕梁市中级检察院以同罪名提起公诉，并移交到吕梁市中级法院；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2022</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">29</span>日，赵维凯案被山西省吕梁市中级法院正式庭审，至今尚无宣判。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体;
