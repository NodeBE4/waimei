[港警将检控“光城者”15岁男生  另外四人将获保释](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210713-1168356)
------

<div>
<p>“光城者”九名成员涉策划恐袭被捕后，香港警方国家安全处人员昨再拘捕五人，涉嫌“串谋策划恐怖活动罪”。消息指，警方今日将落案起诉15岁姓罗疑犯，其余四人今天稍后时间将获准保释。</p><p>据星岛日报报道，昨日被捕的四男一女中，三人仍就读中学，分别15岁姓罗男生、17岁姓杨女生及19岁姓郑男生，当中杨女在国际学校读书，疑是“金主”之一，另一被捕的37岁男子林X明，在英皇集团任职物管部门经理，怀疑也是“金主”，余下一名被捕者为报称任职地盘工人的28岁男子黄X涛，五人仍被通宵扣查。</p><p>英皇集团证实物管部一名员工被捕，目前已暂停该员工职务，并强烈谴责公然挑战法律的违法暴力行为。<br> </p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
