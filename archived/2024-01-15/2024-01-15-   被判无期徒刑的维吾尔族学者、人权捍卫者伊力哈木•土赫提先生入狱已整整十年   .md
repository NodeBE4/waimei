[被判无期徒刑的维吾尔族学者、人权捍卫者伊力哈木•土赫提先生入狱已整整十年](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2024/01/blog-post_78.html)
------

<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">16</span>日，本网获悉：<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">15</span>日是被判无期徒刑的维吾尔族学者、人权捍卫者伊力哈木•土赫提先生入狱已整整十周年的纪念日。他在狱中的健康和生命安全令人担忧，国际社会</span><span style="font-size: 16px;">多次</span><span style="font-size: 12pt;">强烈要求中共当局无罪释放伊力哈木先生，而中共当局对这些呼吁置若罔闻，毫不理会。</span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">伊力哈木•土赫提<span lang="EN-US">:1969</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">25</span>日出生，新疆维吾尔自治区阿图什人，原中央民族大学维吾尔族教师，维族学者，维汉双语网站“维吾尔在线”创建人，中国在押政治犯。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">曾于<span lang="EN-US">2006</span>年创办维吾尔在线网站，<span lang="EN-US">2009</span>年新疆乌鲁木齐发生“<span lang="EN-US">7</span>•<span lang="EN-US">5</span>”事件期间，因该网站被当局视为进行了“大肆煽动宣传，传播谣言”的活动，<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">8</span>日，伊力哈木及其家人即被中共当局带走，隔离软禁于北京周边某处，后来在作家王力雄等数百人联署的《关于维吾尔学者伊力哈木•土赫提遭拘押的呼吁》之下，<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">23</span>日，才被释放回家；<span lang="EN-US">2013</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月，又因北京发生天安门撞桥事件，遂于<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">2</span>日在开车带孩子及妻子外出时遭遇国保便衣警察的驾车冲撞及恐吓。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2014</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">15</span>日，再次被北京及新疆警察从家中带走刑拘，被羁押于新疆自治区公安厅看守所；<span lang="EN-US">2014</span>年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">20</span>日，被乌鲁木齐市检察院以涉嫌“分裂国家罪”正式批捕，理由是“伊力哈木•土赫提与境外‘东突’势力勾结”，利用互联网鼓吹‘新疆独立’，利用讲堂煽动<span lang="EN-US">'</span>推翻政府<span lang="EN-US">'</span>，利用教师身份从事分裂活动”；<span lang="EN-US">2014</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">23</span>日，最终被新疆自治区中级法院以“分裂国家罪”重判无期徒刑，没收个人全部财产；其不服上诉，<span lang="EN-US">2014</span>年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">21</span>日二审再次宣判：驳回上诉，维持原判；据悉，其亲属前往送钱及衣服等物品时遭狱方拒绝，理由是伊力哈木的钱尚未用完，被羁押者无需送衣物。目前在新疆自治区第一监狱服刑（乌鲁木齐市新市区四平路<span lang="EN-US">5</span>号，邮编<span lang="EN-US">830013</span>）。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">对伊力哈木•土赫提先生的境况，本网将持续关注。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"></p><div class="separator" style="clear: both;
