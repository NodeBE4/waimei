[因“白纸抗议运动”在广州被抓捕的影像工作者陈大栗（本名陈思冶）已取保获释](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2023/01/blog-post_8.html)

<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">5</span>日，本网获悉：因“白纸抗议运动”在广州被抓捕的影像工作者陈大栗（本名陈思冶）已取保获释。陈大栗已在被关押共计<span lang="EN-US">20</span>多天之后被取保释放，但仍需接受居住地（或返回原籍）同级公安机关监管，并保证随传随到。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span class="Char"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">1994</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;
