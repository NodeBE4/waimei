<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">2</span>日，本网获悉获刑<span lang="EN-US">4</span>年的河北邢台市法轮功学员胡辉女士的案情及简历，现在通报如下：<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">胡辉：</span></b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">河北邢台市法轮功学员，女，<span lang="EN-US">1967</span>年生，<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">57</span>岁。家住邢台市信都区长征二分厂家属院。<span lang="EN-US">1998</span>年初，她开始修炼法轮功。<span lang="EN-US">1999</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月中共疯狂迫害法轮功后，胡辉因坚持信仰法轮功，多次遭中共人员骚扰、绑架、抄家，曾被关押在看守所、劳教所。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">2020</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">28</span>日，胡辉到一位法轮功学员家一起阅读法轮大法著作《转法轮》，被邢台市信都区公安分局国保警察跟踪、抓捕，随即遭刑事拘留，后遭批捕、起诉。<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">17</span>日，胡辉被邢台市襄都区法院判刑<span lang="EN-US">2</span>年。<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">2</span>日，胡辉才结束冤狱，从河北省女子监狱回家。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">可是，胡辉回家还不到一年，<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">17</span>日，她在南宫市的婆婆家照顾婆婆时，再被南宫市政保大队警察抓捕、抄家。同时，胡辉在邢台市的家也被当地公安搜查。胡辉被关押在邢台市第一看守所。<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月，被关押一年零三个月后，胡辉女士被宁晋县法院判刑<span lang="EN-US">4</span>年。刑期至<span lang="EN-US">2027</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">16</span>日。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p>
