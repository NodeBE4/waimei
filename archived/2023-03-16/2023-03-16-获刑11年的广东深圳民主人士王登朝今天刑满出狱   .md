[获刑11年的广东深圳民主人士王登朝今天刑满出狱](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2023/03/11.html)
------

<div>
<p><span></span></p><a name='more'></a><p class="MsoNormal"><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">16</span>日，本网获悉：获刑<span lang="EN-US">11</span>年的广东深圳民主人士王登朝今天已经刑满出狱，其身体尚可。王登朝非常感谢朋友们这么多年的关注关爱<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">王登朝：</span></b><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">1974</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">27</span>日出生，陕西省武功县人，前深圳市公安局罗湖分局三级督警，曾任深圳市保安公司第七公司经理，民主活动人士，中国政治犯。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2012</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年，因在深圳市莲花山公园准备组织一个为纪念孙中山逝世<span lang="EN-US">87</span>周年而做的民主宣传活动，而于<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">12</span>日被刑拘；随后又指控他在<span lang="EN-US">2011</span>年负责深圳大运会保安工作时，涉嫌虚报保安人数贪污<span lang="EN-US">283</span>万元，被深圳警方正式逮捕；<span lang="EN-US">2012</span>年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">26</span>日，被深圳市罗湖区法院以“贪污罪”和“妨害公务罪”判处有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">14</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月。其不服上诉，<span lang="EN-US">2013</span>年经二审改判，“妨害公务罪”被取消，贪污数额被减为<span lang="EN-US">80</span>万元，刑期减为有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">11</span>年；刑期至<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">16</span>日。</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;
