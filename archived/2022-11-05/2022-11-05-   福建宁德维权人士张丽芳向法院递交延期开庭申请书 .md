<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">5</span>日，本网获悉：<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">3</span>日福建宁德维权人士张丽芳向霞浦法院递交了延期开庭申请书。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">事情经过：</span></b><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">11</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">月<span lang="EN-US">2</span>日下午<span lang="EN-US">4</span>时许，张丽芳突然接到霞浦法院电子送达的开庭传票和合议庭组成人员，告知其诉被告屏南县棠口镇政府在法定期限内拒不履行宅基地审批法定职责一案将于<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">8</span>号（下周二）开庭。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">说突然，<span lang="EN-US">1.</span>是第一次遇到行政案件开庭，传票、合议庭成员组成提前不到<span lang="EN-US">6</span>天才通知，<span lang="EN-US">2.</span>法院<span lang="EN-US">2</span>号当天收到被告的答辩状和证据，现材料还未发送给张，<span lang="EN-US">3.</span>目前张工作的福州，疫情严重，处于全民核酸一天一测，福州目前的防疫政策是：低风险区入、返榕人员须持<span lang="EN-US">48</span>小时内核酸阴性证明，三天内完成二次检测并做健康监测，结果为阴性者才解除。张不想给他人添麻烦，也不愿给自己添麻烦。因此无法到庭参与庭审。法院试图以视频方式开庭，被张拒绝。张遂提出延期申请。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: 宋体;
