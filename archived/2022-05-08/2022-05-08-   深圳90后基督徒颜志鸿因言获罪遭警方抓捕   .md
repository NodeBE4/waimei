mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">8</span>日，本网获悉：深圳<span lang="EN-US">90</span>后基督徒颜志鸿因言获罪于<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">25</span>日遭当地警方以涉嫌寻衅滋事罪抓捕。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">颜志鸿仅仅因为翻译关于上海疫情“清零”封城中，一位德国人对封控政策的批评言论，<span lang="EN-US">4 </span>月<span lang="EN-US"> 25 </span>日就被深圳国安人员抓捕，至今情况仍不明朗。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体;
