[因支持武汉退休老人抗议医改的“白发运动”的武汉出租车司机童梦兰现以不予起诉昨被释放](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2023/07/blog-post_82.html)
------

mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">12</span>日，本网获悉：因支持武汉退休老人抗议医改的“白发运动”的武汉出租车司机童梦兰（阿梦）已于昨日<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">11</span>日夜间被释放，共被关押近五个月之久，现以不予起诉被释放。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">月<span lang="EN-US">19</span>日，童梦兰被武汉市江汉区警方以涉嫌“寻衅滋事罪”刑拘，自此失联；同年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">29</span>日，又被武汉市江汉区检察院以同罪名正式批捕；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体;
