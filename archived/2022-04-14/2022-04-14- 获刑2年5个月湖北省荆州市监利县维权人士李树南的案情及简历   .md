[获刑2年5个月湖北省荆州市监利县维权人士李树南的案情及简历](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2022/04/25.html)

<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">14</span>日，本网获悉获刑<span lang="EN-US">2</span>年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>个月湖北省荆州市监利县维权人士李树南的案情及简历，现在通报如下：<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">李树南</span></b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">：<span lang="EN-US">1964</span>年出生，湖北省荆州市监利县白螺镇人，监利县白螺镇五里庙村村民，司法不公举报人，维权访民。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2003</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年，曾因承包邻村彭阮村<span lang="EN-US">110</span>亩土地搞多种养殖期间，当地监利县柘木乡政府官员非法平整土地致其岳母家农田出现严重损毁现象，其协助家人被迫向上反映，以求妥善解决问题，孰料多次投诉无果，反起冲突、被造假；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">后其逐渐认识到投诉无门，上访反被造假诬告皆因地方官员相互勾结贪污公款（即：以县委书记为首勾结乡村干部做假帐截留救灾资金，倒卖救灾物资，贪污工程青苗补偿款），坑害百姓所致，至此开始逐级上访；曾因其举报维权触动了地方官员的私利（即采用重复申报水利工程项目等诈骗国家资金），而遭当局强力逼迫打压；<span lang="EN-US">2011</span>年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月，其曾遭报复性殴打，致身体终生残疾；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2012</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月，曾因其夫妻二人承包的鱼塘在地方干部的纵容下被人破坏，承包合同被单方废止，而使其直接经济损失多达<span lang="EN-US">60</span>余万元；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">曾因其妻陈呈香出于义愤，采取极端抗议方式点火自焚，以求正义公道，其为此提起上诉，但在同年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">18</span>日信访投诉听证会上却因地方当局提供伪证，其夫妇二人被警方分别行政拘留<span lang="EN-US">15</span>天；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2012</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月、<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月，曾因其妻两次进京投诉而被监利县警方以涉嫌“扰乱公共秩序罪”刑拘，后又被宣布劳动教养，至<span lang="EN-US">2013</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">11</span>日才被释放，故而其维权抗争之心日益弥坚；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2015</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">20</span>日，曾因在京信访反映冤情时遭到北京警方地毯式抓铺，后被移交给监利县截访人员遣返原籍，又被关进某宾馆式黑监狱，遭到群殴，致其身体多处受伤；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2019</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">6</span>日，被荆州市监利县警方从家中突然带走，并被以涉嫌“寻衅滋事罪”刑事拘留，自此失联；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2020</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月，知其已被地方检察院正式批捕，后又被检察院以同罪名移交至湖北省荆州市监利县法院；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2020</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">3</span>日，其夫妻二人案在监利县法院视频开庭，期间，检察官以其二人多次扰乱公共秩序、敲诈勒索予以定罪，建议给予<span lang="EN-US">2-3</span>年刑期；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ansi-language: EN-US; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-bidi-font-family: &quot;Times New Roman&quot;; mso-bidi-language: AR-SA; mso-bidi-theme-font: minor-bidi; mso-fareast-language: ZH-CN; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2020</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ansi-language: EN-US; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-bidi-font-family: &quot;Times New Roman&quot;; mso-bidi-language: AR-SA; mso-bidi-theme-font: minor-bidi; mso-fareast-language: ZH-CN; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月，终被该法院一审以“敲诈勒索罪”等判处有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">2</span>年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>个月；<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">5</span>日，刑满释放。此前被羁押于荆州市监利县看守所。</span><div><div class="separator" style="clear: both;
