<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">19</span>日，本网获悉获刑<span lang="EN-US">5</span>年广东法轮功学员吴朝棋的案情及简历，现通报如下：<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">吴朝棋：</span></b><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">1992</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年出生，广东省茂名市电白区人，广州打工人，自由信仰追求者，法轮功信仰者，中国在押良心犯。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">曾因<span lang="EN-US">16</span>岁时患有“肺中积水”，虽做手术，但几度复发，致其疾病未予治愈反使花费颇多；后因“肺中积水”再次复发，呼吸困难，随开始信仰并修炼法轮功，但自此开始屡受广东当局属下的“<span lang="EN-US">610</span>”办公室严打迫害；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2017</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">16</span>日，曾因利用工余时间在广州市天河公园附近清理宣传栏中有关诬蔑、诽谤法轮功的内容，而被天河区天园派出所警方抓走，遭暴打，数次被脚踢、头撞墙等，之后被以涉嫌邪教罪刑拘，送广州市天河区看守所关押；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">同年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">16</span>日，又被转送到广东省法制教育学校接受洗脑式教育；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">因长期不被释放，其家人在为其聘请的两位维权律师帮助下逐级将控告信上告到广东省检察院、广东省公安厅等司法部门，终使其在被非法关押近<span lang="EN-US">2</span>个月后得以取保获释；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2018</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">29</span>日至<span lang="EN-US">31</span>日，吴朝棋及其母亲林丽珍、其姨妈林燕梅三人先后突然被茂名市滨海新区、电白区及电城镇派出所警方等<span lang="EN-US">20</span>多人联合撬门抓走，后被以涉嫌邪教罪刑拘，羁押于茂名市第一看守所；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2019</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">5</span>日，吴朝棋三人案被广东省茂名市茂南区法院统一在茂名市第一看守所内庭审；同年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">13</span>日，三人均被该法院一审以“利用邪教组织破坏法律实施罪”宣判；其中，吴朝棋被判处有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">5</span>年，罚金<span lang="EN-US">1</span>万元，其母亲林丽珍亦被判处有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">5</span>年，罚金<span lang="EN-US">1</span>万元；其姨妈林燕梅被判处有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">4</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月，罚金<span lang="EN-US">8</span>千元；其不服上诉；同年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">2</span>日，经广东省茂名市中级法院二审裁决，依然维持原判，刑期至<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">28</span>日；据悉，其在看守所时曾绝食抗议<span lang="EN-US">7</span>天，遭酷刑折磨，刚入狱时曾被匿名服刑。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体;
