<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">9</span>日，本网获悉因言获罪<span lang="EN-US">4</span>年的新疆巴音郭楞州公民刘建波的简历，现在通报如下：<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">刘建波：</span></b><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">1968</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">26</span>日出生，曾用名刘兵，新疆区巴音郭楞蒙古自治州（简称巴音郭楞州）焉耆回族自治县人，网络活跃人士，民主公民，因言获罪者，中国曾押政治犯。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">自<span lang="EN-US">2015</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月以来，因其使用电子设备在其国内微信群和境外推特（<span lang="EN-US">Twitter</span>）、脸书（<span lang="EN-US">Facebook</span>）多次发布对中共当局不利的敏感信息，尤其是曾在<span lang="EN-US">2015</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">-9</span>月、<span lang="EN-US">2015</span>年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">-2017</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月，因分别使用<span lang="EN-US">VPN</span>“翻墙”软件将脸书网站（<span lang="EN-US">facebook.com</span>）、推特网站（<span lang="EN-US">twitter.com</span>）与其在国内的账户<span lang="EN-US">zhifouyoudu@163,com</span>相连接，发布了<span lang="EN-US">30</span>篇有批评、揭露中共政府及其国家领导人有关政策及不当言论（或言行）内容的文章，曾在<span lang="EN-US">2017</span>年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">-2018</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月期间，因使用微信号在微信群（“党支部规范化指导建设群”）传播了<span lang="EN-US">18</span>篇对中共及其国家领导人不利的文章信息，遂被网警监控知晓；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">后被送库尔勒市委宣传部审读后认为均属有敏感信息的“问题文章”，故而<span lang="EN-US">2018</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">16</span>日，被巴音郭楞州库尔勒市警方以涉嫌“寻衅滋事罪”刑拘；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">同年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">29</span>日，又被库尔勒市检察院以同罪名正式批捕，并羁押在库尔勒市看守所，指控其因在境内外网站发布、传播<span lang="EN-US">48</span>篇含有“诋毁中国共产党及国家领导人内容”的文章，其言行触犯了国家相关刑法，故而应予逮捕和量刑；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2018</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">16</span>日，其案在巴音郭楞蒙古自治州库尔勒法院庭审，最终被一审以“寻衅滋事罪”判处有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">4</span>年，二审维持原判，刑期至<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">15</span>日；后因服刑中狱方认为其表现良好，遂被库尔勒法院减刑<span lang="EN-US">7</span>个月；<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">15</span>日，减刑提前释放。此前曾在新疆库尔勒监狱服刑。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"></p><div class="separator" style="clear: both;
