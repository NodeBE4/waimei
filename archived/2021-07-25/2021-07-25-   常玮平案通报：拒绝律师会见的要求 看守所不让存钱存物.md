<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">25</span>日，本网获悉：近日维权人士包龙军、李大伟陪同常玮平律师的妻子陈紫娟再次来到陕西省公安厅、陕西省检察院、宝鸡市检察院、宝鸡市公安局以及凤县看守所维权。检察院说常玮平案起先他们都不知道，后来人家才要求他们配合。说是公安部督办他们监督不了。在公安，案件承办人项贤宏什么也不说，只是声称他们是“依法依规办事”，并拒绝律师会见的要求。看守所不让存钱存物。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">常玮平：</span></b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">陕西人权律师，原陕西立刚律师事务所律师。常玮平律师因为代理所谓敏感案件，<span lang="EN-US">2018</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月被停业<span lang="EN-US">3</span>个月，及后原本的执业律师事务所被注销，需要在半年内转到其他律师楼继续执业，否则执照会被吊销。后他异地转所至浙江，也受到阻碍。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2019</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">22</span>日常玮平律师到杭州市西湖区法院递交诉状，起诉浙江省司法厅不履行调转律师档案法定职责。常律师自<span lang="EN-US">2019</span>年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">16</span>日向浙江省司法厅提交了转入浙江左契律师事务所的申请后，该厅始终不给办理。常玮平律师被迫处于被吊销律师执业证书的状态。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">常玮平律师因厦门公民聚会案于<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">12</span>日晚在西安住处被西安公安局高新区分局带走，办案单位是宝鸡市公安局高新区分，后常玮平的妻子接到宝鸡高新分局国保大队电话，被告知常玮平因危害国家安全，已经被指定居所监视居住。随后其律师执业证遭注销。后于<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">21</span>日取保获释回家。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">9</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">个月后，<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">22</span>日常玮平又遭指定居所监视居住。常玮平被再次抓捕的原因是他在海外网站上发布视频控诉其遭受的令人发指的酷刑，遭宝鸡警方挟私报复。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">后律师申请对其取保，遭宝鸡警方拒绝。<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">16</span>日家属收到了常玮平的逮捕通知书，他已被关到了秦岭山中的凤县看守所。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体;
