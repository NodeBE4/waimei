font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">6</span>日，其与多名民主公民共计<span lang="EN-US">6</span>人被香港国安处警方以涉嫌违反《刑事罪行条例》第<span lang="EN-US">9</span>和<span lang="EN-US">10</span>条“作出具煽动意图的作为”罪统一拘捕；后有<span lang="EN-US">4</span>人被交保释放，但其与民主人士赵美英仍被在押；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体;
