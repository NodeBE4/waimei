[江西省吉安市劳工维权法律援助者肖青山（本名肖高升）获刑4年6个月](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2023/03/46.html)
------

<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">1</span>日，本网获悉：江西省吉安市劳工维权法律援助者肖青山（本名肖高升）被以“虚假诉讼罪”、“寻衅滋事罪”判刑<span lang="EN-US">4</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月。目前其已经从江西省吉安市看守所转监到江西省吉安市吉安监狱（通信地址：江西省吉安市吉州区<span lang="EN-US">1126</span>信箱）。肖青山的刑期至：<span lang="EN-US">2026</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月底。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">肖青山：本名肖高升，<span lang="EN-US">1967</span>年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">17</span>日出生，江西省吉安市青原区富田镇下厅村人，吉安市吉州区白鹭苑<span lang="EN-US">16</span>栋<span lang="EN-US">1</span>单元<span lang="EN-US">1402</span>室居民，劳工维权法律援助者，“肖氏诉讼法”发明者，实名举报人，人权捍卫者，中国在押维权人士。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">早年在广东省广州市打工谋生期间，曾因工伤事故致残后自学法律，而后即在广州市成立了一家旨在帮助农民工群体维权的法律服务所；后又因受到广东当局对其帮助劳工维权行为的打压，随被迫进北京上访；期间，曾因成立肖青山维权团队，辗转全国各地转为劳工弱势群体维权，故而渐成为国内具有一定知名度的人权捍卫者；然正是基于其不断进行公义维权，因而屡遭中共当局打压逼迫；多年来，曾因多次在广东省东莞市、深圳市、江西老家及进京为劳工维权者和自家亲属子女受牵连，遭遇不公等，维权抗争、拉横幅呼吁、向地方司法机构提起诉讼等，而屡被抓捕、传唤和胁迫恐吓；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2009</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">9</span>日—<span lang="EN-US">13</span>日，曾遭广东省东莞市劳动局<span lang="EN-US">/</span>长安分局<span lang="EN-US">/</span>厦边劳动服务站以土匪暴行方式抄抢住房钱财，其报警后警方竟拒不出警，又向东莞市第二检察院投诉控告，亦遭无视；同年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">6</span>日，曾因在深圳市宝安区松岗镇政府大门前公开支持和纪念<span lang="EN-US">64</span>学运<span lang="EN-US">20</span>周年，而被警方抓走拘留数天；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">曾因替劳工维权抗议，其手机短信功能多次被东莞市警方封掉；曾因欲去香港旅游，而遭广东省东莞市警方和江西省警方联合拒办签证手续；曾因替他人维权，遭打击报复而致其在江西老家的田产被乡村干部霸占，未给任何补偿；曾因其公义维权，其亲属家庭纷纷受牵连，或遭查抄，或钱财被抢，喊冤多年，至今未果；曾因进京申冤，不但未果，反被几次拘押；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">近年来，因其不断举横幅和上网呼吁，要求江西省各级政法机关及纪检部门依法惩治司法败类，且举横幅喊口号都有出处，随屡遭警方警告威胁；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2021</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月底，被江西省吉安市警方秘捕，后知被刑拘；<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">5</span>日，被吉安市青山区警方以涉嫌“寻衅滋事罪”正式逮捕；<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">8</span>日，其案将在江西省吉安市青原区法院开庭受审；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">据悉，当局为其指派律师被其拒绝，表示若无人权律师援助，就将自己辩护。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2023</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">1</span>日获悉，肖青山被以“虚假诉讼罪”、“寻衅滋事罪”判刑<span lang="EN-US">4</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月。目前其已经从江西省吉安市看守所转监到江西省吉安市吉安监狱（通信地址：江西省吉安市吉州区<span lang="EN-US">1126</span>信箱）。刑期至：<span lang="EN-US">2026</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月底。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"></p><div class="separator" style="clear: both;
