font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2022</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">19</span>日，因其教会在户外举办<span lang="EN-US">70</span>人的“亲子营”活动，遂突遭当地警方<span lang="EN-US">100</span>余人的围堵和冲击，并将包括其在内的所有现场参与者悉数带走，同时扣押所有人员的手机、计算机、硬盘、投影仪、凳子、汽车等物品；之后虽有部分小孩被送回，但所有成人均被扣留，且其本人住处遭查抄，将其部分书籍数据等搜走；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">3</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">天之后的<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">23</span>日，其及同是教会神职人员的李洁等人被派出所警方带走，并均被临汾市公安局直属分局警方以涉嫌“诈骗罪”指定居所监视居住；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">同年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">2</span>日，其与李洁二人被山西省临汾市警方以涉嫌“诈骗罪”转为刑事拘留；<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">30</span>日，其与李洁又被临汾市检察院以同罪名正式批捕。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体;
