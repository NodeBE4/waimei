<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">2</span>日，本网获悉获刑<span lang="EN-US">3</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月的辽宁省锦州市法轮功学员张占安的案情及简历，现在通报如下：<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">张占安：</span></b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">辽宁省锦州市法轮功学员，男，原为葫芦岛人，水泥厂职工。<span lang="EN-US">1957</span>便生，现年<span lang="EN-US">67</span>岁。一九九五年十二月开始修炼法轮功。在这场对法轮功的迫害中，张占安夫妻俩和大儿子多次遭绑架、非法拘留、关洗脑班，多次被罚款，妻子被迫害致死。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">2023</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">31</span>日，在锦州市古塔公安分局策划下，警察抓捕了张占安等五位锦州法轮功学员。关押到锦州市看守所。<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">13</span>日，张占安遭凌海市检察院批捕。<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月初，张占安被判刑<span lang="EN-US">3</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月，罚金七千元。<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">1</span>日，梁树发被转监到盘锦监狱。刑期至<span lang="EN-US">2027</span>年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">28</span>日。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p>
