<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">21</span>日，本网获悉曾获刑<span lang="EN-US">16</span>年的北京法轮功学员王治文的简历，现通报如下：<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">王治文：</span></b><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">1949</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年出生，北京市人，别名王志文，原铁道部工程师，自由信仰追求者，北京“法轮大法研究会”义务联络人，法轮功修炼者，中国曾押良心犯。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">自<span lang="EN-US">1992</span>年修炼法轮功以来，<span lang="EN-US">1992</span>年<span lang="EN-US">-1997</span>年，曾担任北京“法轮大法研究会”义务联络人；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">1999</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">20</span>日，曾因组织法轮功<span lang="EN-US">4</span>•<span lang="EN-US">25</span>中南海上访请愿活动，而与李昌、纪烈武和姚洁等中共党政干部修炼者被北京市警方抓捕、刑拘，并被关押在河北省廊坊市的中国人民武装警察部队学院内；后其等<span lang="EN-US">4</span>人被北京市检察院以涉嫌“非法获取国家机密罪”和“组织邪教活动罪”正式批捕，指控其等因是法轮功骨干成员，随其为法轮功请愿等言行被视为违法；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">1999</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">26</span>日，其案在北京市第一中级法院以全国播放形式公开审理，次日其即被以“组织、利用邪教组织破坏法律实施罪”、“组织、利用邪教组织致人死亡罪”和“非法获取国家秘密罪”重判处有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">16</span>年，剥夺政治权利<span lang="EN-US">4</span>年，同案其他<span lang="EN-US">3</span>人亦被以同罪名予以重判；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2014</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">18</span>日，其虽刑满出狱，但当天又被警方送到北京西城区法制学习班接受洗脑教育，一周之后才准回家；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">据悉，其在狱中曾惨遭酷刑，狱警用脚踩其手指，再用牙签插进其每个指甲，直至十指鲜血不断；获释后，</span><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2018</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月，其在美国生活多年的独生女儿想要其到美一同生活，但在北京国际机场出关时却遭警方拦阻，并将其护照当场剪掉。</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;
