[郑州昨夜启动大规模消杀](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210807-1179017)
------

<div>
<p>郑州市城市管理局组织各区环卫部门昨晚出动680余台各类喷雾车设施、3200余人对市区重点区域和主要道路进行防疫专项消杀，以抑制冠病疫情蔓延。</p><p>根据《郑州日报》报道，此次消杀作业从昨晚22时开始，至今早5时结束，消杀作业采用浓度1∶300的84消毒液。各区环卫部门出动车辆680余台、人员3200余人，对全市1700多条道路进行消杀，同时把高铁站、火车站、医院、大型商场等周边道路作为重点进行消杀覆盖。</p><p>昨晚10点，大规模消杀工作正式开始，中原路西三环、中原路京广路、解放路立交等地多台雾炮车并排缓慢行驶，雾炮斜向上仰角45度高空喷射作业方式，对主次干道的道路路面和空气环境进行喷雾、喷射消杀作业。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>报道称，此次针对灾后市区重点区域和主要道路的专项消杀，将对预防和控制病媒生物传播性疾病的发生、阻断病毒传播途径有积极作用。</p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
