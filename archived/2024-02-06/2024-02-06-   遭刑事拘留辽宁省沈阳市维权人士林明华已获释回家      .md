[遭刑事拘留辽宁省沈阳市维权人士林明华已获释回家](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2024/02/blog-post_99.html)

<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">7</span>日，本网获悉：辽宁省沈阳市维权人士林明华于<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">28</span>日在北京被辽宁省沈阳市驻京人员带走。后被刑事拘留，罪名、关押地点不详，今获悉林明华已获释回家。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">林明华是辽宁知名人权捍卫者林明洁的哥哥。<span lang="EN-US">2016</span>年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">29</span>日，林明洁和沈阳访民到公安部，状告沈阳市公安局长许文有，随后林明洁就被沈阳的截访人员抓走，先是波以“涉嫌扰乱公共场所秩序罪”刑拘，后变更罪名以“寻衅滋事罪”判刑，后林明洁、林明华兄弟都因维权获罪，林明华曾获刑<span lang="EN-US">3</span>年，林明洁被判<span lang="EN-US">5</span>年。林明洁目前仍在狱中，在押期间多次遭遇狱方电击、体罚等酷刑迫害。林明华一直为监狱中的弟弟伸冤。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2023</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">27</span>日，辽宁省沈阳市驻京组找到林明华在北京的住处，次日（<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">28</span>号）拿出所谓“网逃”文书将林明华带走。沈阳警方在关押林明华<span lang="EN-US">37</span>天后，才将其释放。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"></p><div class="separator" style="clear: both;
