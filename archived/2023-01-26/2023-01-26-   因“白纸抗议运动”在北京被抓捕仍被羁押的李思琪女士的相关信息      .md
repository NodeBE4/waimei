font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">女，<span lang="EN-US">1990</span>年代出生，独立记者，自由媒体人，网络活跃人士，北京白纸运动参与者，中国在押政治犯。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2022</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">24</span>日，因新疆乌鲁木齐一栋大楼突发火灾，但政府当时的过度防疫措施导致火灾未能及时扑灭与救助，最终使得数十人死亡；消息一经传出，世界各地华人群体，尤其是海外留学生群体及中国大陆各地民众纷纷为死难者举行悼念和抗议活动；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">11</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">月<span lang="EN-US">27</span>日，其及多名群友与在网上看到当晚在北京市朝阳区亮马河将有为新疆死难同胞悼念的活动信息，遂与电报群中的好友曹芷馨、李元婧等数名好友一起带着蜡烛、鲜花以及写了诗句的便签与白纸，到亮马河桥上参与了悼念活动；熟料两天之后，其等十数名青年人尽都被当地派出所警方带走传唤；后因其等在悼念活动中并未有出格举动，故一天之后又都被无罪释放；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">然而<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月18日，李思琪又被北京市朝阳区警方以涉嫌“聚众扰乱社会秩序罪”秘密带走、刑拘，另有<span lang="EN-US">8</span>人亦在同期几天之内纷纷被朝阳区警方以同罪由刑拘，自此全都失联；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2023</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">20</span>日获悉，其与同是白纸抗议者的群友曹芷馨、李元靖等共计<span lang="EN-US">9</span>人被北京市朝阳区检察院以涉嫌“寻衅滋事罪”正式批捕；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体;
