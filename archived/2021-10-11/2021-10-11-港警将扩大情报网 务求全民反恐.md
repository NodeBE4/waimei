[港警将扩大情报网 务求全民反恐](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20211011-1202194)
------

<div>
<p>香港警队将扩大情报网，加强留意网上煽动暴力的人士。</p><p>据《星岛日报》报道，香港警务处处长萧泽颐昨天出席电台节目时说，警队也呼吁市民发现可能进行激进行为的人时，马上报警，务求“全民反恐”守护香港。</p><p>萧泽颐透露，今年上半年，香港共有3万871宗罪行，比去年同期下跌4.6%，而有关反修例事件的刑事毁坏、纵火及非法集结等案件也下降10%至45%，可见社会已回复平静，前线警员得集中精力进行防罪和灭罪工作。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>他指，别有用心之人仍正蠢蠢欲动，通过杂志、网上短片及文化艺术等“软对抗”方式“播毒”，煽动他人作出暴力行为。他呼吁市民反思，即使不尊重警察或特区政府，仍要尊重法治，他强调做任何事都要守法。</p><p>明年是香港回归25周年，加上即将有两场选举要举行，萧泽颐透露，警方已经在今年初成立了八个工作小组，开始检视装备和人力资源等，同时会加强搜集情报、展开行动及进行相关宣传教育工作。</p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/zbpe-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=zbpe399&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_desktop-zbpe399_2.png?dHEiGouxxZpWwWTJE04dyGyUN74JOU2z" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_desktop-zbpe399_2.png?dHEiGouxxZpWwWTJE04dyGyUN74JOU2z" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/zbpe-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=zbpe399&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_mobile-zbpe399_2.png?qZnBcIGCJvPR8A2IAZOZuOxdoGZDatiI" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_mobile-zbpe399_2.png?qZnBcIGCJvPR8A2IAZOZuOxdoGZDatiI" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
