[韩驻华大使称若无疫情 习近平访韩或早已成行](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20211014-1203215)
------

<div>
<p>韩国驻华大使张夏成上周表示，韩中两国已就冠病疫情趋稳后推进中国国家主席习近平访韩事宜达成共识。他并说，如果没有疫情，习近平访韩也可能早已成行。</p><p>据韩联社报道，张夏成上周三（6日）以视频连线方式出席韩国国会外交统一委员会国政监查会时，作出上述表示。他说，将继续为促成习近平访韩与中国保持紧密沟通。</p><p>张夏成也透露，自疫情去年2月暴发以来，习近平没有出访海外，也从未在北京会见外交使节。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>韩国总统文在寅曾于2017年12月以总统身份首次访华，后于2019年12月借出席韩中日首脑会谈之机第二次访华。但受疫情影响，习近平的回访日程至今尚未敲定。</p><p> </p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/zbpe-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=zbpe399&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_desktop-zbpe399_2.png?dHEiGouxxZpWwWTJE04dyGyUN74JOU2z" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_desktop-zbpe399_2.png?dHEiGouxxZpWwWTJE04dyGyUN74JOU2z" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/zbpe-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=zbpe399&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_mobile-zbpe399_2.png?qZnBcIGCJvPR8A2IAZOZuOxdoGZDatiI" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_mobile-zbpe399_2.png?qZnBcIGCJvPR8A2IAZOZuOxdoGZDatiI" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
