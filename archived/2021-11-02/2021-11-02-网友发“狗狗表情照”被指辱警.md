[网友发“狗狗表情照”被指辱警](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20211102-1209424)
------

<div>
<p>一名网友在微信群发了一张“狗狗表情照”，被指辱警，已构成寻衅滋事，依法被拘留九天。</p><p>据“平安青铜峡”官方澎湃号消息，2021年10月30日晚上6时，城关派出所民警接到群众举报，有人在微信群里面发送侮辱人民警察的信息。获此信息后，城关派出所组织警力进行调查。</p><p>据消息指，一名网友李某因对小区疫情管控不满，在330余人的微信群里发了把“狗狗表情照”放在警察脸上的照片。该行为被指控是公然侮辱警察，寻衅滋事。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>消息称，对此类违法行为，青铜峡公安始终坚持“零容忍”态度，并称将捍卫民警执法权威和法律尊严。经讯问后，李某供认在微信群组里侮辱警察的事实。</p><p>消息指，李某的行为已构成寻衅滋事，根据《中华人民共和国治安管理处罚法》规定，青铜峡市公安局依法对李某予以行政拘留九天的处罚。</p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/zbpe-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=zbpe399&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_desktop-zbpe399_2.png?VersionId=dHEiGouxxZpWwWTJE04dyGyUN74JOU2z" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_desktop-zbpe399_2.png?VersionId=dHEiGouxxZpWwWTJE04dyGyUN74JOU2z" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/zbpe-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=zbpe399&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_mobile-zbpe399_2.png?VersionId=qZnBcIGCJvPR8A2IAZOZuOxdoGZDatiI" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_mobile-zbpe399_2.png?VersionId=qZnBcIGCJvPR8A2IAZOZuOxdoGZDatiI" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
