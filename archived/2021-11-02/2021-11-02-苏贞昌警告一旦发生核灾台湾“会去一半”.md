[苏贞昌警告一旦发生核灾台湾“会去一半”](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20211102-1209464)
------

<div>
<p>台湾行政院长苏贞昌今天（2日）强调，不可能重启第四座核能发电站（核四），并说一旦发生核灾，台湾“一定会去一半”。</p><p>台湾将在今年12月就重启核四等四项问题举行公投。其他三项公投案为反莱猪进口、公投绑大选，以及天然气接收站迁离桃园大潭藻礁海岸及海域。</p><p>据台湾《自由时报》报道，苏贞昌在进入立法院前告诉在场记者，这次公投题目涉及稳定供电、国际贸易和选务是否顺利。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>苏贞昌认为，重启核四是不可能的，并说台湾已没有放置核废料的地方。</p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/zbpe-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=zbpe399&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_desktop-zbpe399_2.png?VersionId=dHEiGouxxZpWwWTJE04dyGyUN74JOU2z" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_desktop-zbpe399_2.png?VersionId=dHEiGouxxZpWwWTJE04dyGyUN74JOU2z" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/zbpe-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=zbpe399&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_mobile-zbpe399_2.png?VersionId=qZnBcIGCJvPR8A2IAZOZuOxdoGZDatiI" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_mobile-zbpe399_2.png?VersionId=qZnBcIGCJvPR8A2IAZOZuOxdoGZDatiI" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
