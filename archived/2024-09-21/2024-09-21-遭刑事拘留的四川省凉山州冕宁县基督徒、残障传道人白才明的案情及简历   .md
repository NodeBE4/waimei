------

<div>
<p><span></span></p><a name='more'></a><p class="MsoNormal"><br /></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">22</span>日，本网获悉遭刑事拘留的四川省凉山州冕宁县基督徒、残障传道人白才明的案情及简历，现在通报如下：<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">白才明：</span></b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">四川省凉山州<a name="_Hlk177867829">冕宁县</a>基督徒、残障传道人，白才明右手自小残疾<span lang="EN-US">(</span>但可以骑摩托车出去传福音<span lang="EN-US">),</span>平时以捡拾废品为生<span lang="EN-US">,</span>并支撑女儿蒙恩和儿子上学。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">白才明于<span lang="EN-US">1989</span>年就跟随其母亲皈依基督教了<span lang="EN-US">,</span>与妻子董世霞在教会相识<span lang="EN-US">,</span>一直在雅安农村传福音。<span lang="EN-US">1998</span>年<span lang="EN-US">,</span>因信徒在白才明家聚会<span lang="EN-US">, </span>白才明被劳动教养两年半。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">白才明持守基督教信仰<span lang="EN-US">,</span>唯独读《圣经》<span lang="EN-US">,</span>没有宗派观<span lang="EN-US">,</span>与其他教会没有联络。大概有十位左右老信徒愿意和，才明、董世霞夫妇一起过信仰生活<span lang="EN-US">,</span>他们并没有给这种聚会形式取个教会名字。由于都是农村老年信徒<span lang="EN-US">,</span>对白才明没有供奉。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">2024</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">11</span>日<span lang="EN-US">,</span>白才明因所谓的“搞违法基督教活动”被四川省凉山州冕宁县公安局刑事拘留<span lang="EN-US">,</span>罪名是“非法经营罪”，面临被起诉。其妻子董世霞因所谓的不配合被治安拘留<span lang="EN-US">10</span>天<span lang="EN-US">,</span>已于<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">19</span>日回到家中。目前白才明被羁押在冕宁县看守所。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"></p><div class="separator" style="clear: both;
