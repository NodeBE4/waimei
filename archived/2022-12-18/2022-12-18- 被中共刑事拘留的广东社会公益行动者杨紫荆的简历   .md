<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">18</span>日，本网获悉被中共刑事拘留的广东社会公益行动者杨紫荆的简历，现在通报如下：<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><b><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">杨紫荆：</span></b><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">女，<span lang="EN-US">1997</span>年出生，广东省广州市人，网名“点心”，社会公益行动者，异见表达者，中国在押政治犯。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">自<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年以来，因中共政府就新冠疫情的变种澳美克戎病毒采取全民“动态清零”政策，使全国各地民众均因处于被长期极端封控状态（主指全员封城、封小区、封楼层甚至封居住房舍、被强行送进方舱等）而无法获取正常食物及基础性生活物品，许多人在此期间身心造成极大伤害，故而终于在秋冬交界之际爆发了群众性抗议（又称“白纸抗议”）活动；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2022</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">27</span>日，其因陪同朋友到广州市越秀区海珠广场围观、声援了抗议“动态清零”的群体活动，遂很快遭遇广州市当局的秋后算账；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">同年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">4</span>日，其被广州市越秀区北京街派出所警方以“涉及海珠广场事件”为由传唤带走，家中电子设备遭搜查，次日（<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">5</span>日）即被广州市越秀区警方以涉嫌“寻衅滋事罪”刑拘；后其家人为其聘请了代理律师，但至今律师及其家人均不被允许与其会见，并拒绝告知家人和律师任何有关案件信息；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">据悉，其被办案人员问讯的内容主要涉及“是否境外势力、<span lang="EN-US">NGO</span>（非政府组织）”，“是否收钱举白纸”等等，并其家人和友人被威胁“不要将此事发到网上”，否则对其安全不利。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">目前被羁押于广州市越秀区看守所（广州市黄埔区广汕西路<span lang="EN-US">107</span>号，邮政编码：<span lang="EN-US">510520</span>）。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"></p><div class="separator" style="clear: both;
