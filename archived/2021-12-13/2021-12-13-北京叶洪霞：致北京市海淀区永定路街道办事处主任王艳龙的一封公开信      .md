<p><span></span></p><a name='more'></a><p class="MsoNormal"><b><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">自<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">3</span>日至今，贵办事处副主任王建峰受您的指派承办对我及我家人进行妥善安置的问题，通过短信沟通，我认为王建峰主任在工作中存在不实事求是、客观上制造和激化矛盾纠纷、漠视民众疾苦、对于自己无权限处理的事项不及时上报主管领导，不正确履行职责的情形，今特向您以短信形式进行汇报：<span lang="EN-US"> <o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">1</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">、王建峰主任短信曾称“已经于<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">27</span>日和永定路派出所负责同志当面对我进行了告知，告知我费用结算到<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">30</span>日，<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">1</span>日后再发生费用由我个人支付，本想与我沟通我面临的实际困难的问题，我没听他说完我我就走了”，与事实不符：<span lang="EN-US"> <o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">事实是：<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">27</span>日当天，由于我和永定路派出所王宝山副所长有案件要谈，我已经告知负责联系我的贵办事处信访办工作人员熊鹰（音），请<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">1</span>日后再与我联系，是王建峰主任带领信访办工作人员王永前来酒店打断了我与永定路派出所王宝山副所长的谈话。不错，我与王建峰主任是见面了，但是见面后谈的都是贵办事处工作人员陈志国在工作中的不当表现，我强烈要求陈志国当面向我道歉，由于王建峰主任护短，所以我才离席而去，这一切，都有酒店视频监控为证，王建峰主任当日并未告知我任何事项。<span lang="EN-US"> <o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">4</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">、王建峰主任短信曾称“目前街道垫付酒店各项服务费用截止到<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">30</span>日”，对此，我已于<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">8</span>日通过邮寄的方式向贵办事处申请政府信息公开，贵办事处已签收，请王艳龙主任及时做出答复，请依法对我进行告知：<span lang="EN-US"> <o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（<span lang="EN-US">3</span>）叶洪霞因配合海淀区政府维稳工作入住长峰假日酒店后，对<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月—<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">30</span>日期间酒店所发生各种服务费用作出许可先行垫付决定的工作人员姓名，职务的信息；<span lang="EN-US"> <o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;
