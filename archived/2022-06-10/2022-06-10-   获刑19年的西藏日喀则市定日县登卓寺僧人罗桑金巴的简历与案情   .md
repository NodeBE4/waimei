<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">10</span>日，本网获悉获刑<span lang="EN-US">19</span>年的西藏日喀则市定日县登卓寺僧人罗桑金巴的简历与案情，现通报如下：<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">罗桑金巴：</span></b><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">1976</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年出生，藏族名<span lang="EN-US">Lobsang Jinpa</span>，西藏自治区日喀则市定日县人，藏族僧侣，日喀则市定日县协格尔镇查那村当卓寺（藏民称之为登卓寺（<span lang="EN-US">Tengdro</span>））副院长，公益慈善捐助人，藏族异见人士，中国在押良心犯。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2019</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">-9</span>月，曾因当卓寺僧侣曲嘉旺波（<span lang="EN-US">Choegyal Wangpo</span>）到西藏首府拉萨市做佛事访问时不慎将手机丢失，后被当地警方获取，发现手机里存有其及当卓寺的多名僧侣与尼泊尔的同乡藏僧有过密联系的信息记录，尤其是针对<span lang="EN-US">2015</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月尼泊尔大地震，他们向那里的藏人及寺院捐款，以帮助地震灾区小区重建等记录，以及存有和第<span lang="EN-US">14</span>世达赖喇嘛有关的图片及文献等，遂引起中共西藏当局的极大不快和恐慌，认定这是一起与境外敌对势力勾结，利用在线通讯对国家安全构成威胁的典型案件；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">同年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月，罗桑金巴所在当卓寺遭到日喀则市当局派出的多名武警及公安警察突袭，所有僧人均被强迫接受政治洗脑教育，多人遭殴打，包含其在内的<span lang="EN-US">20</span>名僧侣均被拘留，所在区域的网络连接被切断；<span lang="EN-US">3</span>天后，一名僧人因向当局抗议而自杀，其及多名僧人仍被超押，数月不放；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">后罗桑金巴与另<span lang="EN-US">3</span>名藏僧被日喀则市警方以涉嫌“跟境外藏人联系、赈济尼泊尔地震，或拥有和达赖喇嘛有关照片或文献”为罪由转正式逮捕，自此失联；<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">6</span>日获悉，其一行<span lang="EN-US">4</span>人案在日喀则中级法院庭审，最终被以“危害国家安全罪”判处<span lang="EN-US">20</span>年<span lang="EN-US">-5</span>年不等刑期；其中，罗桑金巴被判处有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">19</span>年，刑期至<span lang="EN-US">2038</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月。目前疑似在拉萨市札基监狱服刑。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"></p><div class="separator" style="clear: both;
