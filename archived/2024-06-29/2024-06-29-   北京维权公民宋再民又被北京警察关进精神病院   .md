[北京维权公民宋再民又被北京警察关进精神病院](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2024/06/blog-post_29.html)
------

<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">29</span>日，本网获悉：北京维权公民宋再民于<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">31</span>日再被北京警察关进“北京市平谷精神病院”，至今已经马上一个月，仍未获释。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;">宋再民早年参加过八九学运，<span lang="EN-US">35</span>年来从没有放弃过民主事业，通过各种的渠道跟方式，为上访者和冤民声援，也曾是许志勇先生<span lang="EN-US">&lt;</span>公盟<span lang="EN-US">&gt;</span>的义工，经常参与维权行动。这些年来，宋再民多次被强制性关进精神病医院。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;">去年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">22</span>日，宋再民被警方强制关进德胜门外三甲疯人院<span lang="EN-US">-</span>——安定医院的戒酒断药<span lang="EN-US">13</span>病区<span lang="EN-US">48</span>天。直到今年春节前才在女儿的艰难努力下从精神病院中救出，救出后出现痴呆状况，才慢慢恢复。几个月后又遭警方强行关进精神病院。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;">对宋再民先生的境况，本网将持续关注。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"></p><div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;"><a href="https://blogger.googleusercontent.com/img/b/R29vZ2xl/AVvXsEiOQr7fsFyuFKvYzsfKtfAXhLntseLQmGHzfzbR8KbmUF3CPuXiIUoShbIHzPtp3_t_oKah-xLMvlFninBxVN6kynDNNMCClIayHqX_PX2Zaxt0rEUXFHhmUfRnS7Btz8WXGiLXiSqwuS21yCz1d4CXOkPVXwWOpCytfMt7TRHWOzMErmdONEBT1rIHJN2z/s526/00.jpg" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;"><img border="0" data-original-height="523" data-original-width="526" height="318" src="https://blogger.googleusercontent.com/img/b/R29vZ2xl/AVvXsEiOQr7fsFyuFKvYzsfKtfAXhLntseLQmGHzfzbR8KbmUF3CPuXiIUoShbIHzPtp3_t_oKah-xLMvlFninBxVN6kynDNNMCClIayHqX_PX2Zaxt0rEUXFHhmUfRnS7Btz8WXGiLXiSqwuS21yCz1d4CXOkPVXwWOpCytfMt7TRHWOzMErmdONEBT1rIHJN2z/s320/00.jpg" width="320" /></a></div><p></p>
