<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2025</span>年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">17</span>日，本网获悉获刑<span lang="EN-US">8</span>年的山东省莱州市<span lang="EN-US">74</span>岁法轮功学员林洪杰的案情及简历，现在通报如下：<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><b><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;">林洪杰：</span></b><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;">山东省莱州市法轮功学员，男，<span lang="EN-US">1951</span>年生，<span lang="EN-US">2025</span>年已<span lang="EN-US">74</span>岁。</span><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt;">2023</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;">年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">8</span>日下午，莱州市警察、“<span lang="EN-US">610</span>”约三、四十人开着十多辆警车包围了林洪杰、谢庆财、方瑞芹的学法小组资料点，把他们抓捕到派出所做笔录，并抢劫走林洪杰的电脑、打印机、耗材、法轮功资料、书籍等私人物品。林洪杰被关押，<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">10</span>日被关押到洗脑；<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">19</span>日才被放回家。</span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt;">2023</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;">年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月中旬其被起诉，<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">15</span>日，莱州市法院开庭。随后<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">20</span>日，莱州市法院通过律师以书面形式给林洪杰下达判决书：林洪杰被判刑<span lang="EN-US">8</span>年、罚款<span lang="EN-US">4</span>万元； 随后，林洪杰被收监，关押在莱州市看守所，刑期至<span lang="EN-US">2032</span>年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">2024</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月被转监到济南监狱。</span></p>
