------

<div>
<p><span></span></p><a name='more'></a><p class="MsoNormal"><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">11</span>日，本网获悉：获刑<span lang="EN-US">2</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>个月的广东维权人士张国兵今日刑满获释，据网友称他已与家人团聚。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">张国兵：</span></b><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">1975</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2017</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月，曾因维护权益而向当地河源市中级法院提起行政诉讼，状告东源县人民政府；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2019</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年，因广东省多地及外省多所中小学校出现食品卫生问题，其作为学生家长在各地众多家长们的认同和支持下，<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">30</span>日，与另一学生家长欧阳牡丹一起向广东省教育厅、食品药品安全监督局，以及广东<span lang="EN-US">21</span>个市的教育局和食品药品安全监督局发出申请函，要求公开校园食品安全监督信息，以期“了解一下各地学校食品安全监督的情况”，促使政府能给所有孩子创建一个“健康学习环境”；因其认为此举完全符合宪法公开条例规定，没有违法，大胆公开发言，不想却引起地方当局的极度恐慌和不满；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2019</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">12</span>日，其突然被河源市东源县警方传唤，家被查抄；<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">13</span>日，被警方以涉嫌“寻衅滋事罪”刑拘；同年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">19</span>日，被东源县警方以同罪名执行逮捕，被关押于河源市看守所；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2020</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">27</span>日，被广东省河源市东源县法院以“寻衅滋事罪”判处有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">2</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>个月；其不服，提起上诉；<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">24</span>日，经广东省河源市中级法院二审裁决，依然维持原判；其对一审认定事实无异议，但坚称拒不认罪；刑期至<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">11</span>日。此前在广东省揭阳监狱服刑。</span></p><p></p><div class="separator" style="clear: both;
