[因言获罪2年的贵州民主人士田维权的简历](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2022/06/2.html)
------

<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">19</span>日，本网获悉因言获罪<span lang="EN-US">2</span>年的贵州民主人士田维权的简历，现通报如下：<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">田维权：</span></b><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">1974</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">16</span>日出生，土家族，贵州省铜仁市沿河土家族自治县人，沿河土家族自治县思渠镇院山村猫岩沟组村民，原广东翔思新材料有限公司员工，网络活跃人士，民主异见人士，因言获罪者，中国曾押政治犯。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">早年，为谋生计而从贵州省沿河土家族自治县老家前往广东深圳打工；<span lang="EN-US">2011</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">16</span>日，曾被广东省深圳市中级法院以“故意伤害罪”判处有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">10</span>个月，缓刑<span lang="EN-US">1</span>年；后其到东莞市谋职，并被广东翔思新材料有限公司聘用为长期雇员；自<span lang="EN-US">2016</span>年起，曾因利用手机翻墙软件注册<span lang="EN-US">Twitter</span>、<span lang="EN-US">Telegram</span>、<span lang="EN-US">YouTube</span>用户名，并加入“天皇击杀五毛公海”等<span lang="EN-US">10</span>个网络组群，又于<span lang="EN-US">2018</span>年<span lang="EN-US">-2019</span>年多次在国际网络平台上发表对中共政府和最高领导人习近平等不利的批判性言辞和信息，遂遭广东省当局的嫉恨与打压；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2019</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">23</span>日，其被东莞市警方以涉嫌“寻衅滋事罪”刑拘；同年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">26</span>日，被东莞市第二市区检察院以同罪名正式批捕，被指控因从<span lang="EN-US">2018</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月到<span lang="EN-US">2019</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月期间，通过手机在推特上发表“辱骂政府和国家领导，散布有关政府、国家领导人、国内重事件等的虚假信息”共计<span lang="EN-US">557</span>条，在“天皇击杀五毛公海”等<span lang="EN-US">10</span>个网络组群发表相同信息共计<span lang="EN-US">183</span>条，故其言行触犯了国家的相关法律，遂应予以批捕和量刑；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2020</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月，其案在广东省东莞市第二法院开庭受审，至<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">30</span>日，终被该法院以“寻衅滋事罪”判处有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">2</span>年；其不服上诉；后经东莞市中级法院二审裁决，依然维持原判；<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">22</span>日，刑满释放。此前曾被羁押于东莞市第二看守所。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"></p><div class="separator" style="clear: both;
