[日本赠台50万剂疫苗运抵](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210925-1197114)
------

<div>
<p>日本捐赠台湾的50万剂阿斯利康（AZ）疫苗今天下午（25日）抵达桃园机场。</p><p>据《联合报》报道，这是日本政府第五度捐助台湾疫苗。</p><p>疫苗由日本航空JL-809航班运送，从日本成田机场起飞。疫苗抵台卸下后，由台湾海关关员执行机边验放的通关程序，随即在航警与海关车辆护送下前往仓储码头，最后运送至指定的冷储中心存放，等待完成后续检验封缄作业再提供接种。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>日本政府迄今已无偿捐赠台湾约390万剂AZ疫苗。</p><p>台湾总统府发言人张惇涵今天说，日本捐赠的次数及数量皆是国际民主伙伴之最，“再次表达诚挚且由衷的感谢，这份坚定的情谊，台湾人民深受感动，难以忘怀。”</p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/zbpe-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=zbpe399&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_desktop-zbpe399_2.png?dHEiGouxxZpWwWTJE04dyGyUN74JOU2z" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_desktop-zbpe399_2.png?dHEiGouxxZpWwWTJE04dyGyUN74JOU2z" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/zbpe-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=zbpe399&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_mobile-zbpe399_2.png?qZnBcIGCJvPR8A2IAZOZuOxdoGZDatiI" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_mobile-zbpe399_2.png?qZnBcIGCJvPR8A2IAZOZuOxdoGZDatiI" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
