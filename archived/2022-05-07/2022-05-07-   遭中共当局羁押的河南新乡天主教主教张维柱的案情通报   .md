<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">7</span>日，本网获悉遭中共当局羁押的河南新乡天主教主教张维柱的案情，现在通报如下：<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">张维柱：</span></b><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">1958</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年出生，河南省籍人，天主教徒，教名<span lang="EN-US">Joseph Zhang Weizhu</span>，河南省新乡市天主教教区（下简称“新乡教区”）主教，中国在押良心犯。<b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></b></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">自<span lang="EN-US">1991</span>年获梵蒂冈任命成为主教以来，因在河南省新乡教区拥有约<span lang="EN-US">10</span>万名信众，且拒不加入中共设立的中国天主教爱国会（简称 “爱国会” ），故而其主教称位一直不被当局承认；曾因此而被当局多次抓捕、刑拘和软禁；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2001</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年，曾因坚决维护信仰而被中共当局秘密软禁达<span lang="EN-US">1</span>年多之久；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2021</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US"> 5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">1</span>日起，因中共开始实施新制“宗教教职人员管理办法”，规定宗教教职人员必须“拥护中国共产党领导”，故而其及诸多天主教神职人员，凡拒绝登记加入官方“爱国会”者均被成为率先监控、打压的对象；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">同年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">20</span>日，因在河北省沙河桥一处工厂区设立修道院，以教牧培养神职人员，突遭河北当局百余名警力包围该修道院，后被以不明罪由抓捕，同天</span><span style="font-size: 16px;">张维柱与</span><span style="font-size: 12pt;">另</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt;">7</span><span style="font-size: 12pt;">名神父，以及正在上课的</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt;">10</span><span style="font-size: 12pt;">多位修士亦被抓捕，至自此失联；</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">截至<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">6</span>日，其已被超期关押近一年之久，依然尚未释放，并且音信全无。目前被羁押地点不详。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"></p><div class="separator" style="clear: both;
