<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">29</span>日，本网获悉获刑<span lang="EN-US">2</span>年的河北香河县法轮功学员王志山的案情及简历，现在通报如下<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">王志山：河北香河县法轮功学员，男，<span lang="EN-US">1971</span>年生，现年<span lang="EN-US">52</span>岁，老家香河县渠口镇东梨园村，是一位乡村医生。王志山因坚持真善忍信仰，曾经被中共人员多次绑架到派出所、洗脑班迫害，遭酷刑折磨，诊所被迫停业，他流离失所十几年。张宪、王志山两位法轮功学员在<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">28</span>日见面时被香河县政保大队警察及派出所警察抓捕，被关押在香河县看守所，十月被批捕，于<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月两人被取保候审。后于<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">5</span>日被香河县法院判刑，张宪被判刑<span lang="EN-US">1</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月，王志山遭判刑<span lang="EN-US">2</span>年。<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">6</span>日，张宪、王志山被派出所警察又关押到香河县看守所。刑期至：<span lang="EN-US">2025</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p>
