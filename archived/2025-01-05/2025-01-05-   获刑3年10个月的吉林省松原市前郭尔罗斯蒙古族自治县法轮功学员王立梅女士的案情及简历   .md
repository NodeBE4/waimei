<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2025</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">5</span>日，本网获悉获刑<span lang="EN-US">3</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>个月的吉林省松原市前郭尔罗斯蒙古族自治县法轮功学员王立梅女士的案情及简历，现在通报如下：<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">王立梅：</span></b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">吉林省松原市前郭尔罗斯蒙古族自治县法轮功学员，女，<span lang="EN-US">1961</span>年生，<span lang="EN-US">2025</span>年<span lang="EN-US">64</span>岁，家住吉林省松原市前郭尔罗斯蒙古族自治县套浩太乡碱巴拉村。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">2024</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">10</span>日，王立梅由于在网上和其他法轮功学员交流，被网监发现，被白城市镇赉县公安局警察抓捕，先被关押在镇赉县看守所，后被关押到白城市看守所。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">2025</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月获悉，大安市法院进行异地视频开庭，庭审王立梅，并对她判刑<span lang="EN-US">3</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月、罚金两万元。王立梅提起上诉，结果竟然被加判四个月，刑期成<span lang="EN-US">3</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>个月。刑期至：<span lang="EN-US">2028</span>年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">9</span>日。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p>
