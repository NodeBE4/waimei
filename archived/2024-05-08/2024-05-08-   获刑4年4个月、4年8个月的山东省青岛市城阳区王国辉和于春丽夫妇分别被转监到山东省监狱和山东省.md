[获刑4年4个月、4年8个月的山东省青岛市城阳区王国辉和于春丽夫妇分别被转监到山东省监狱和山东省女子监狱](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2024/05/4448.html)

<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">8</span>日，本网获悉：获刑<span lang="EN-US">4</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>个月、<span lang="EN-US">4</span>年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>个月的山东省青岛市城阳区棘洪滩街道王国辉和于春丽夫妇分别被转监到山东省监狱和山东省女子监狱。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">王国辉在<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">20</span>日从城阳区看守所被送往山东省监狱，妻子于春丽在<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">8</span>日从普东看守所被送往山东省女子监狱。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">王国辉和于春丽夫妇系山东省青岛市法轮功学员，<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">50</span>岁左右，原住青岛市城阳区棘洪滩街道段家庄。<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年因警方多次迫害，夫妇两人离家避祸。<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">27</span>日，城阳区国保大队和棘洪滩派出所警察包围了王国辉、于春丽夫妇的租住处，将夫妇两人抓捕，先关押在派出所内几十天，于<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">7</span>日，于春丽、王国辉才被关押到普东看守所和城阳看守所。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">2023</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">18</span>日，王国辉、于春丽夫妇在青岛即墨区法院被庭审。当庭于春丽被判刑<span lang="EN-US">4</span>年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>个月，刑期至<span lang="EN-US">2027</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">26</span>日，王国辉被判<span lang="EN-US">4</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>个月，刑期至：<span lang="EN-US">2027</span>年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">26</span>日。王国辉和于春丽当庭提出上诉<span lang="EN-US">,</span>二审遭驳回维持原判。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p>
