<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">3</span>日，本网获悉：<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">2</span>日晚上<span lang="EN-US">20</span>点，北京市房山区阎村派出所十几名警察闯入肖书君、尹登珍夫妇俩租住的出租屋，强制带到派出所后交给了肖书君的户籍地黑龙江省同江市截访人员，及尹登珍户籍地的湖北十堰的截访人员。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">据尹登珍发出的信息，称肖书君被黑龙省同江市公安局副局长袁海林、警察石行军，打伤后强行抬到警车上押回同江，现在还在医院躺着。而尹登珍则被十堰市信访局局长王万志和郧阳区政法要副书记时天乔下令，让郧阳区驻主办赵勇花<span lang="EN-US">2</span>万<span lang="EN-US">4</span>千元雇佣多名黑社会人员在大董村路上被打伤，绑架到黑车京<span lang="EN-US">Q.V5S60</span>上，并抢走了尹登珍身上的数百元现金，及手机，银行卡，身份证等。被押回十堰郧县为问黑社会们要被抢劫的财物。又被郧阳中岭社区年轻人和<span lang="EN-US">5</span>个黑社会殴打成伤。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">据了解，现在尹登珍被关押在民宿房黑监狱中稳控，没有给予起码的治伤看病。尹登珍希望社会各界关注他们夫妻俩的遭遇。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">尹登珍电话：<span lang="EN-US">19107299218</span>。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p>
