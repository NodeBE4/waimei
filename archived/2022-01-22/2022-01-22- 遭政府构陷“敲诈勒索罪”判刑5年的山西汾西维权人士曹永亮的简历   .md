[遭政府构陷“敲诈勒索罪”判刑5年的山西汾西维权人士曹永亮的简历](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2022/01/5_22.html)

<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">22</span>日，本网获悉遭政府构陷“敲诈勒索罪”判刑<span lang="EN-US">5</span>年的山西汾西维权人士曹永亮的简历，现通报如下：<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">曹永亮：</span></b><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">1981</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">20</span>日出生，县人，山西省汾西县永安镇郭村村民，职业不详，维权人士，中国在押维权人士。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">曾因汾西县政府在炸无证黑煤窑时，将住宅房屋震塌，且无人赔偿，自此走上维权上访之路，期间曾多次遭遇拘留，其户口本亦因累次上访而被警方恶意没收；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2012</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">12</span>日，曾因在京上访时遭截访、欺骗和毒打等迫害，地方当局先是答应给其赔偿<span lang="EN-US">6</span>万元，并当场支付现金<span lang="EN-US">1</span>万元，打欠条<span lang="EN-US">5</span>万元，但将其带到北京市长安西街派出所后，即遭警方毒和搜身，致其后来长期身体虚弱、吐血不止、大小便流血，其刚拿到的<span lang="EN-US">1</span>万元赔偿金亦被抢走、<span lang="EN-US">5</span>万元欠条被撕毁，随后被汾西县警方拘留<span lang="EN-US">20</span>天；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2014</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">28</span>日，曾因继续上访又被右安门派出所警方毒打，并被汾西县警方拘留<span lang="EN-US">10</span>天；此后，再次因上访而被拘留<span lang="EN-US">3</span>次，且每次都是<span lang="EN-US">10</span>天；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2015</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">1</span>日，当其在北京打工之时，被前来的汾西县警方突然抓回老家，遭毒打后被关至临汾市尧都区吴村看守所；同年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">4</span>日，即被汾西县警方以“伪造居民身份证、寻衅滋事罪”刑拘；同年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">2</span>日，被汾西县检察院正式批捕；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2016</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">17</span>日，被山西省汾西县法院以“伪造身份证罪、寻衅滋事罪”判处有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">1</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月；据悉，看守所关押期间，屡受非人折磨，曾被毒打导致其头破血流，眼睛看不见，眉骨骨折；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2016</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">28</span>日，刑满获释；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2021</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">22</span>日，山西省汾西县当局因惧怕其在“百年党庆”时期再到北京上访，影响党国形象，随指派当地警方为其提供生活困难补助<span lang="EN-US">5</span>万元，以令其立刻中止去京上访；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">同年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">16</span>日，因其认为中共“百年党庆”敏感期已过，遂就到国家信访局继续提交申诉材料，岂料<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">5</span>日在北京暂住地遭遇截访；遣回原籍之后，先是送山西省蒲县看守所被拒收，又于<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">11</span>日被送乡宁县隔离，自此失联；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">个多月后，在其家人委托的律师努力打听下终于获知，<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">11</span>日<span lang="EN-US">-21</span>日，其案被山西省汾西县法院庭审宣判，最终被以“敲诈勒索罪”判处有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">5</span>年；刑期至<span lang="EN-US">2026</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体;
