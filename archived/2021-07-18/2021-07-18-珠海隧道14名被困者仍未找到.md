[珠海隧道14名被困者仍未找到](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210718-1170473)
------

<div>
<p>广东省珠海市石景山隧道“7·15”透水事故的14名被困者仍然没有被找到。</p><p>据中国央视新闻报道，第四次珠海石景山隧道“7·15”透水事故新闻发布会今天（18日）召开。据珠海市副市长张宜生介绍，截至18日上午9点，救援作业面已向隧道内推进了598.5米，距离受困点561.5米，隧道内整体水位下降11.3米。</p><p>张宜生介绍，搜救团队已派出专业潜水队伍，但目前仍未搜救到被困人员。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>珠海兴业快线（南段）项目石景山隧道施工段15日凌晨3时30分左右发生透水事故，导致14名工人被困。</p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
