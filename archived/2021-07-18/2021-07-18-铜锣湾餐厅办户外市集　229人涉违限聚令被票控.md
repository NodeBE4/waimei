[铜锣湾餐厅办户外市集　229人涉违限聚令被票控](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210718-1170470)
------

<div>
<p>香港警方昨晚（17日）在铜锣湾围封一间餐厅举办的户外“市集”，向229人发出限聚令告票，并以涉嫌“组织受禁群组聚集”拘捕餐厅32岁男负责人。</p><p>据明报报道，警方表示，昨日根据情报及调查，发现铜锣湾陈东里及霎西街交界一间店铺外有大量人聚集，举行篮球比赛及音乐派对，于是在昨晚约6时40分动员湾仔分区牌照组、特遣队及机动部队到场，采取代号“松塔”行动，票控现场170男、59女违反限聚令。</p><p>涉事餐厅的32岁负责人遭警方以涉嫌“组织受禁群组聚集”带走，警方稍后将检控。餐厅其后在Instagram发动态消息称，今日（18日）会继续举行相关活动。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>根据网上资料，涉事餐厅除售卖食物及咖啡等饮品外，也提供酒精饮品及水烟。<br> </p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
