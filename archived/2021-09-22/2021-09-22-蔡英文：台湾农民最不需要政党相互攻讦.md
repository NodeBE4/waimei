[蔡英文：台湾农民最不需要政党相互攻讦](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210922-1196238)
------

<div>
<p>台湾总统蔡英文今天谈到水果事件时说，台湾农民受到对岸打压，“我们对外立场更应该一致”，而不是政党之间彼此攻讦。</p><p>据台湾中央社报道，蔡英文今天在民进党中常会指出，大陆出台禁令后，受影响产区的党公职，尤其是屏东县长潘孟安、立委刘櫂豪等，都在第一时间站出来力挺农民，安大家的心，“清廉、勤政、爱乡土”，民进党的招牌擦得很亮，是因为大家都很努力在拼。</p><p>蔡英文说，台湾农民受到对岸打压，一定要像先前处理凤梨事件一样，顺利化解这波打压。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>中国大陆从20日起暂停输入台湾的释迦和莲雾，连同半年前被禁的凤梨，台湾销陆三大主力水果全遭封杀。国民党方面指出，水果禁运大陆的真正症结不是病虫害，而是“有对抗无对话”的两岸关系。</p><p>对此，蔡英文再次呼吁朝野政党，“我们对外立场更应该一致”，此时此刻，农民需要全民力挺，最不需要的，就是政党之间彼此相互攻讦。</p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/zbpe-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=zbpe399&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_desktop-zbpe399_2.png?dHEiGouxxZpWwWTJE04dyGyUN74JOU2z" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_desktop-zbpe399_2.png?dHEiGouxxZpWwWTJE04dyGyUN74JOU2z" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/zbpe-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=zbpe399&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_mobile-zbpe399_2.png?qZnBcIGCJvPR8A2IAZOZuOxdoGZDatiI" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_mobile-zbpe399_2.png?qZnBcIGCJvPR8A2IAZOZuOxdoGZDatiI" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
