[哈尔滨书记称疫情防控形势严峻复杂](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210922-1196116)
------

<div>
<p>中共哈尔滨市委书记王兆力称，当前哈尔滨疫情防控形势严峻复杂，将全面落实“外防输出、内防扩散”的防控策略，快速开展核酸检测，全面进行流调溯源，努力做到应隔尽隔，坚决阻断疫情扩散蔓延。</p><p>据《哈尔滨日报》报道，中国国家卫健委副主任于学军昨晚（21日）率国务院联防联控机制工作组到哈尔滨指导疫情防控工作，连夜听取疫情防控工作情况汇报，王兆力在会上作了上述表述。</p><p>另据黑龙江卫健委网站报道，黑龙江省今天上午新增本土确诊病例五例（哈尔滨市巴彦县四例、哈尔滨市南岗区一例）。截至今天中午12时，全省现有本土确诊病例八例。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>哈尔滨市今天发布公告，要求市行政区域内全部人员进行核酸检测筛查。南岗区、道里区、松北区、巴彦县“三区一县”今天午夜前，其他区县（市）明天午夜前，必须完成第一轮全员核酸检测。</p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/zbpe-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=zbpe399&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_desktop-zbpe399_2.png?dHEiGouxxZpWwWTJE04dyGyUN74JOU2z" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_desktop-zbpe399_2.png?dHEiGouxxZpWwWTJE04dyGyUN74JOU2z" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/zbpe-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=zbpe399&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_mobile-zbpe399_2.png?qZnBcIGCJvPR8A2IAZOZuOxdoGZDatiI" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_mobile-zbpe399_2.png?qZnBcIGCJvPR8A2IAZOZuOxdoGZDatiI" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
