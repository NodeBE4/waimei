[王藏狱中近况通报](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2021/07/blog-post_55.html)

<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">23</span>日，本网获悉王藏狱中近况，现通报如下：<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2021</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">23</span>日，张磊律师到云南楚雄市看守所会见王藏，和前两次一样，看守所依然以防疫为借口不安排见面，经过到检察院投诉控告看守所也没有纠正违法侵害律师会见权利的行为，看守所安排王藏和律师通了电话。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">王藏之前曾在看守所短暂绝食抗议约见不了驻所检察官、看守所伙食差、在他家门口设岗看守他母亲和四个孩子、株连他的妻子等，后来驻所检察官见了他、稍改善了伙食，王藏就停止了绝食。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">王藏其他方面基本正常，可以看《古文观止》、《鲁滨逊漂流记》等书。心态平和，无所畏惧。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">案件可能在近期开庭审理。王藏特别感谢所有朋友对他家人的关注和支持。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">诗人王藏及夫人王利芹“煽动颠覆国家政权”案，王藏于<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">30</span>日被拘捕，王利芹于<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">17</span>日被拘捕，二人于<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月中旬被移送云南省楚雄州检察院审查起诉。当局指控王藏煽动颠覆国家政权罪名案件事实基本上就是他<span lang="EN-US">2015</span>年那次被抓捕出来后所公开的言论、接受的采访、书写的诗歌文章、以及行为艺术。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">王藏，</span></b><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">1985</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">26</span>日出生，原名王玉文，笔名王藏、“小王子”，云南省楚雄市人，先锋诗人，自由作家，影视编剧，独立中文笔会会员，中国自由文化运动成员，政治犯。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">王藏曾于<span lang="EN-US">2003</span>年底以“小王子”为笔名开始涉足网络诗歌习作，自此走上自由写作之路，并对极权文化予以批判。<span lang="EN-US">2005</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月，加入独立中文笔会，同年遭到官方威胁和长达<span lang="EN-US">2</span>个多月的监视居住。<span lang="EN-US">2006</span>年—<span lang="EN-US">2007</span>年间因积极参与“中国自由文化运动”签名活动，又在《自由圣火》网站发文并设专栏、在博讯网上开设专栏发表文集等，而被贵州省遵义市警方于<span lang="EN-US">2007</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月再次强制监视居住<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月。<span lang="EN-US">2008</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月，荣获“<span lang="EN-US">2008</span>《自由圣火》写作奖”。<span lang="EN-US">2009</span>年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月，正式启用笔名“王藏”，藉以表达“来生愿做藏人”的意愿。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">自<span lang="EN-US">2007</span>年起，因其积极参与多项维权民主活动、广交民主正义之士，并创作、推出了大量诗歌作品以及行为艺术，曾被北京当局多次警告威胁并驱离工作室和居住地，给其家庭造成诸多困扰。<span lang="EN-US">2014</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">1</span>日，因其在网上发布声援香港“雨伞革命”的打伞图片，随被北京市宋庄警方带走，其家被抄，之后被以涉嫌“寻衅滋事罪”刑拘于北京市第一看守所。<span lang="EN-US">2014</span>年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">6</span>日，被北京市当局以同罪名正式逮捕，其罪由包括行为艺术和诗歌、文章，网上发布声援香港“占中”图片，声援郭飞雄，声援建三江被虐打人权律师，在网上祭奠林昭，关注藏人自焚事件，声援新疆维吾尔族学者伊力哈木•土赫提、法轮功问题、纪念“六四”及揭批文革等多项内容。<span lang="EN-US">2015</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">9</span>日，被取保候审释放；据悉，其在被审讯期间遭受酷刑。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2020</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">30</span>日早上<span lang="EN-US">4.30</span>分左右，被云南省楚雄市公安机关突然以涉嫌煽动颠覆罪由带走，后遭刑事拘留逮捕，被正式批捕的日期是<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">3</span>日。现在羁押在云南楚雄市看守所。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">王利芹</span></b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">：女，云南省昆明市人，又名王丽，职业不详，著名自由派先锋诗人及作家王藏（本名王玉文）之妻。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">自从与自由派先锋诗人、作家王藏结婚以来，虽低调生活，但仍因其夫是异见诗人屡遭北京、昆明等地当局牵连与恐吓；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2014</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年，曾因其夫王藏被云南省楚雄市警方以涉嫌“寻衅滋事罪”拘捕，而遭当局指使的不明人士抢劫，且被以若拒绝与警方合作，家即被逼迁和断水断电，甚至“会弄死孩子”等做要挟，致其因恐惧伤及<span lang="EN-US">3</span>个幼子而得精神分裂症；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2017</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月，曾因云南当局强行要求王藏一家从北京迁回云南昆明原籍，其又被不明人士威胁、殴打，故而精神再度失常，导致其抑郁症发作，严重时曾出现精神恍惚、昏迷不醒，离家出走甚至跳湖自杀等现象；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2020</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">30</span>日，其夫王藏被云南省楚雄市警方突然以涉嫌“煽动颠覆国家政权罪”带走刑拘，自此失联，因其夫多日毫无下落，其被迫无奈，遂在各路网站（尤其是<span lang="EN-US">twitter</span>）携子为丈夫被抓捕呼吁，以求正义人士广泛关注与声援，后得知其夫已于<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">3</span>日被云南省楚雄州检察院以同罪名正式批捕；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">于<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">24</span>日，因其在<span lang="EN-US">twitter</span>上为丈夫呼吁受株连，亦被楚雄州检察院以涉嫌“煽动颠覆国家政权罪”批捕；据悉，其<span lang="EN-US">4</span>个幼子被长期囚禁家中，除准予姥姥临时照看外，<span lang="EN-US">4</span>幼子已多日未见父母；友人恐其因此而使抑郁症倾向加重。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体;
