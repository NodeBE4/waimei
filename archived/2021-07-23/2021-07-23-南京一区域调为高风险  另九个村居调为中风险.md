[南京一区域调为高风险  另九个村居调为中风险](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210723-1172604)
------

<div>
<p>南京江宁区禄口街道围合区域自今（23日）起调整为高风险地区。</p><p>据南京市卫健委微信公公众号“健康南京”报道，江宁区禄口街道该围合区域四至范围为：东至禄铜路，南至达练岗河，西至原东湖迟家村，北至启航大道。</p><p>南京市部分区域疫情风险等级调整的还有：江宁区禄口街道陈巷村前陈巷自然村，桑园村排驾口自然村、驻驾山自然村，埂方村卷蓬自然村，钟村社区永欣新寓春兰苑，彭福村彭福自然村，秦村村永欣新寓紫荆苑，湖熟街道周岗社区张巷自然村，秣陵街道殷巷社区龙湖文馨苑小区调整为中风险地区。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>南京目前有一个高风险区域，20个中风险区域。</p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
