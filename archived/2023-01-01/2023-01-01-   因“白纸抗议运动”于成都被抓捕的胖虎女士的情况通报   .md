[因“白纸抗议运动”于成都被抓捕的胖虎女士的情况通报](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2023/01/blog-post_1.html)

<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">1</span>日，本网获悉因“白纸抗议运动”于成都被抓捕的胖虎女士的情况，现在通报如下：<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">胖虎：</span></b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">女<span lang="EN-US">1980</span>年代出生，江苏省籍人，四川省成都市公民，本名不详，网名“胖虎”，职业纹身师，“白纸抗议运动”参与者，中国在押政治犯。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">胖虎虽出生于江南，但因有一颗川渝的灵魂，故而自述愿意生活在成都，做“一个没有纹身的纹身师”，同时也因技艺精巧而备受友人（包括网友）称许；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2022</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">24</span>日，因疫情强制封控期间新疆乌鲁木齐发生火灾，最终由于救援不及时、不到位而导致至少有<span lang="EN-US">10</span>人遇难，为了纪念大火中被无辜烧死的逝者，全国各地（尤其是广州、上海、成都、北京、西安等一线大城市）纷纷发起了“白纸抗议运动”（即举白纸抗议，要求政府“不要核酸要自由”、“不自由、毋宁死”、“要生存”等要求）；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">11</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">月<span lang="EN-US">27</span>日，成都市民亦从四面八方纷纷聚集到望平街，以为在乌鲁木齐火灾中的死难者进行祭奠，胖虎和其新婚不久的丈夫黄颢也前往参与之中；然此一行动立即遭到各地中共当局的强力打击；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">次日，其夫妇二人即被成都市警方从家中带走，后被以涉嫌“寻衅滋事罪”刑拘，自此失联；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">后其母亲从江苏省老家赶来查询其被抓真实原因及关押何处，经多方打探，最终获悉其被关押在成都市双流看守所，但其丈夫黄颢关押处所不详；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">现虽已有律师介入，但当局拒绝二人家属及代理律师与其会见；至今，其后续被押情况尚不清楚。目前胖虎被羁押于成都市双流看守所。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">对胖虎女士的境况，本网将持续关注。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"></p><div class="separator" style="clear: both;
