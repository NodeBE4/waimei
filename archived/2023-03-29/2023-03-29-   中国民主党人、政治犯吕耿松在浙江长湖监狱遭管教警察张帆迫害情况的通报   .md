[中国民主党人、政治犯吕耿松在浙江长湖监狱遭管教警察张帆迫害情况的通报](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2023/03/blog-post_29.html)
------

<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">29</span>日，本网获悉中国民主党人、政治犯吕耿松在浙江长湖监狱遭管教警察张帆迫害情况，现通报如下：<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">在浙江长湖监狱关押的政治犯、中国民主党人吕耿松，因遭长湖监狱管教警察张帆整肃迫害，于<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">18</span>日下午，向探监的妻子汪雪娥和妹妹发出求救信息。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">现在吕耿松被取消每月一次的家属探监。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2023</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">28</span>日晚<span lang="EN-US">7</span>时，长湖监狱让吕耿松在电话告知汪雪娥：“吕耿松因家属探监呼救被扣分，取消家属会见”。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">长湖监狱的民警也在电话称：“汪雪娥若再就吕耿松事去信访和向外界披露情况，就不太方便了”。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">吕耿松年老体弱，患有严重风湿关节炎等多种疾病，身体畏寒怕冷。此前，长湖监狱新换监狱管教民警张帆，收走了吕耿松御寒的毛毯和热水袋，当面摔掉，并且威胁吕耿松。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">此前，<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">18</span>日下午，吕耿松曾对探监妻子和妹妹说，现在他人格权、健康权、生命权都受到威胁了，要求請维权律师来探监。吕耿松在会见时，隔着玻璃会见门，突然翻起了戴着的口罩，上面露出“救救我，帆”四个字。家属才得知吕耿松在监内受到新任管教民警张帆虐待迫害。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">目前，长湖监狱对吕耿松管束升级，抢走的御寒物品也未发还，迫害亦未有停止迹象。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">杭州地区公安在追查，谁向外媒透露了吕耿松在狱中受虐待情况。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">本网呼吁海内外朋友关注吕耿松先生的安全及健康。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">浙江监狱管理局<span lang="EN-US">0571-56022114</span>，长湖监狱<span lang="EN-US">0572-2355110</span>。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">附：吕耿松简历：</span></b><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">1956</span><span style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">7</span>日出生，浙江省杭州市人，前浙江省高等公安专科学校教师，独立撰稿人，中国民主党浙江委员会重要成员，人权活动家，中国在押政治犯。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">1993</span><span style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">年，曾因支持民主运动而被浙江省高等公安专科学校开除公职，从此成为自由职业者和独立撰稿人；<span lang="EN-US">2000</span>年，撰写了《中共贪官污吏》一书，并在香港文化艺苑工作室出版发行，主要文章有：《建立公民弹劾制度》、《建立刑事连带责任制度》、《买官、骗官与官杀官》、《论当代中国黑社会的社会基础》以及《当代中国黑社会的主要类型》等；<span lang="EN-US">2007</span>年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">24</span>日因言获罪被抄家拘捕，并被羁押于浙江省杭州市西湖看守所；<span lang="EN-US">2008</span>年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">5</span>日，被杭州市中级人民法院以“煽动颠覆国家政权罪”判处有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">4</span>年，剥夺政治权利<span lang="EN-US">1</span>年；同年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">14</span>日，二审维持原判，<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">18</span>日被送往杭州市余杭区西郊监狱服刑；<span lang="EN-US">2008</span>年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">15</span>日，被独立中文笔会授予<span lang="EN-US">2008</span>年“狱中作家奖”；<span lang="EN-US">2011</span>年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">23</span>日，刑满获释。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">出狱后，因继续持守民主理念，关注民主宪政建设，积极帮助访民维权，遂多次遭到中共当局的严密监控和打击报复；<span lang="EN-US">2014</span>年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">12</span>日，被杭州市警方突然传唤并抄家；同年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">7</span>日，被杭州市警方以涉嫌“颠覆国家政权罪”刑拘，并被羁押于杭州市看守所；<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">13</span>日，被杭州市检察院以同罪名正式批捕；<span lang="EN-US">2016</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">17</span>日，被杭州市中级法院以“颠覆国家政权罪”重判有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">11</span>年，剥夺政治权利<span lang="EN-US">5</span>年；其不服上诉，<span lang="EN-US">2016</span>年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月，经杭州市高级法经二审裁决，依然维持原判，刑期至<span lang="EN-US">2025</span>年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">11</span>日；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 楷体;
