<p></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span></span></p><a name='more'></a><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;"><br /></span><p></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">现在<span lang="EN-US">(2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">3</span>日<span lang="EN-US">)</span>广州市荔湾区规划局，被业主投诉部分文件违规，不合理，最无耻的是，准备偷偷摸摸的补办手续，提交修改市规划局的规划。</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">3</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;
