[武汉老太潘学英追索退休金18年，换来了长期被监视稳控的苦难生活](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2021/07/18.html)

<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">3</span>日，本网获悉：今年是中共建党一百周年之际，街道上到处挂满了“不忘初心”“牢记使命”及“为人民谋幸福是共产党奋斗的目标”等横幅，而这些横幅背后，是武汉市江汉区北湖街道老太潘学英追索退休金<span lang="EN-US">18</span>年，而被长期监视稳控家中的苦难。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">潘学英今年已经是<span lang="EN-US">72</span>岁的老人，其在<span lang="EN-US">1969</span>能<span lang="EN-US">2</span>到国营企业武汉市铝厂工作，在<span lang="EN-US">1995</span>年因国营企业不景气内退，拿每月<span lang="EN-US">200</span>元左右的退休金。到<span lang="EN-US">2003</span>年，潘学英发现同单位工友领到的退休金比自多出很多，就到当时的社保局调取档案，发现自己<span lang="EN-US">1997</span>年，单位就已经办理了正式退休，但没有按正式退休计算退休金，导致自己的权益严重受损。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">为了维护自己的足额领取退休金的权利，潘学英开始到武汉市社保局落实政策，要求按国务院办公厅的<span lang="EN-US">1998</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>号文件，及武政<span lang="EN-US">1998</span>年<span lang="EN-US">66</span>号文件的第四章第<span lang="EN-US">28</span>条<span lang="EN-US">29</span>条的政策规定落实，但武汉市与湖北省的人社机关，均以超过期限为由不予受理。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">由于退休金权利终生受损、受侵害一事上访，潘学英老人遭遇武汉市江汉区北湖街道办事处用尽一切手段的监视稳控，如家门口被按装监控摄像头，长年来被限制人身自由，长期处于被软禁状态，甚至还多次遭到维稳人员的殴打，多次把老人从区政府部门押送到北湖派出所，坐刑具椅子折磨。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">潘学英就自己的遭遇，陈述如下经历与事实：<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2021</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">17</span>日上午，在武汉市解放公园路惠济路车站拦截，不让我刷积案卡和身份证，表明我的问题没有解决。<span lang="EN-US">21</span>日上午在武汉市洪山路地铁<span lang="EN-US">8</span>号<span lang="EN-US">A</span>出口拦截，不让出站到省信访局反映！把我押送到武汉市常青三路治安分流中心！违法剝夺我逐级上访权利和权益！<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2019</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">2</span>日，是春节的前夕，我拿省信访局回复，向江汉区人民政府反映问题！被江汉区北湖街工委书记毛芸派人押送到北湖派出所，她要所长周雅斌以治安管理关押，因我没有违法事实，所长不同意，未得呈而不甘心，指使她人写黑材料而且要写硬些（我亲耳听见）。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2020</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">8</span>日武汉市信访局长约谈，毛芸派<span lang="EN-US">6</span>名大汉，坐派出所警车把我押回不让我接谈，只要有能解决问题的领导接谈，毛芸就把我搞定不让去！<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2021</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">5</span>日下午<span lang="EN-US">3</span>时<span lang="EN-US">1</span>分，我在监控看守人员的监视下出门去买菜，不到<span lang="EN-US">20</span>分钟回家，大门被撬锁，上涂有不明液体，要求看监控，北湖街综治中心调度室不让，无奈投诉到市长热线，怀疑他们撬门扭锁涉嫌进家投毒！几次向省市区公安部门报案，但都不受案。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">现在我的请求是：依据国务院办公厅<span lang="EN-US">1998</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>号文件，武政<span lang="EN-US">1998</span>年<span lang="EN-US">66</span>号第四章第<span lang="EN-US">28</span>条<span lang="EN-US">29</span>条的政策规定，解决落实我<span lang="EN-US">2000</span>年政策退休待遇，补工龄，纠正计算错误，让我这个<span lang="EN-US">72</span>岁的老太，感受一次共产党真的是在为人民谋幸福。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体;
