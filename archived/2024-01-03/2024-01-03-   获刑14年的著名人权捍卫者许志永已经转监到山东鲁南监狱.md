mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">4</span>日本网获悉：获刑<span lang="EN-US">14</span>年中国著名人权捍卫者许志永二审后已经转监到位于山东日照的鲁南监狱服刑。目前监狱还未允许亲属会见。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">2013</span><span style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">16</span>日，其被北京市警方以涉嫌“聚扰乱公共场所秩序罪”刑拘，关押在北京市第三看守所；同年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">22</span>日，被正式逮捕；<span lang="EN-US">2014</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">26</span>日，被北京市第一中级法院以“聚众扰乱公共场所秩序罪”判处有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">4</span>年；<span lang="EN-US">2017</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">15</span>日，刑满获释<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">2020</span><span style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">15</span>日，许志永被广州市警方从番禺海鸥岛杨斌律师处带走；据悉，其此次被抓，此与<span lang="EN-US">2019</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月曾因参加“厦门聚会案”遭通缉有关，许志永自<span lang="EN-US">2019</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">26</span>日丁家喜律师被捕后就一直在中国南方秘密流亡。其亲友均遭多次骚扰跟踪，其女友李翘楚亦被强迫失踪，后取保获释。<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">19</span>日，被以涉嫌煽动颠覆国家政权罪正式逮捕，羁押于山东省临沭县看守所。<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">10</span>日，许志永遭中共当局以颠覆国家政权罪判处<span lang="EN-US">14</span>年重刑，刑期至<span lang="EN-US">2034</span>年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月。其不服上诉，二审遭驳回，维持原判。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体;
