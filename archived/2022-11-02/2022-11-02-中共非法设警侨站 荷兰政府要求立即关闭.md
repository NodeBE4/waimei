[中共非法设警侨站 荷兰政府要求立即关闭](https://www.epochtimes.com/gb/22/11/1/n13857411.htm)
------

<div>

href="https://www.epochtimes.com/gb/tag/%E8%AD%A6%E4%BE%A8%E7%AB%99.html">警侨站</a>。<a href="https://www.epochtimes.com/gb/tag/%E8%AD%A6%E4%BE%A8%E7%AB%99.html">警侨站</a>必须立即关闭。”</p></p><blockquote class="twitter-tweet"><p dir="ltr" lang="nl">Buitenlandse Zaken heeft de Chinese
