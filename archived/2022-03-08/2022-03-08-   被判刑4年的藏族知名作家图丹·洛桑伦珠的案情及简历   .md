<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">8</span>日，本网获悉被判刑<span lang="EN-US">4</span>年的藏族知名作家图丹•洛桑伦珠的案情及简历，现在通报如下：<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">图丹•洛桑伦珠：</span></b><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">1971</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年出生，青海省果洛藏族自治州班玛县迪达村人，笔名：迪拉丹或迪拉登（<span lang="EN-US">Dhi Lhaden</span>），曾在寺庙为僧，后还俗成为青海藏族知识分子和知名藏族作家，成都市某民办（私立）教育中心教师，藏族人权捍卫者，中国在押政治犯。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">早年<span lang="EN-US">13</span>岁时，曾在当地寺院出家为僧；<span lang="EN-US">2</span>年后曾前往四川省甘孜藏族自治州色达县，进入色达喇荣五明佛学院进修学习；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;"><span style="mso-spacerun: yes;">&nbsp;</span>27</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">岁时，曾又前往西藏自治区拉萨市的哲蚌寺和色拉寺分别进修；数年后还俗结婚，与其妻共育一女；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2008</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年，曾因西藏各地爆发大规模的藏人和平抗议活动，其随遍访西藏各地，记录下各地藏族民众对抗议活动的了解及认识，之后经过观察与慎思，曾撰写、出版及发表了多部相关书籍和多篇文章，尤其是《消极抵抗的艺术》一书的出版发行，引起藏人群体热议，亦触及和引发了中共西藏当局的极端反应；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2019</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月，其在四川省成都市一家民办（私立）教育中心当老师时，被成都警方从工作单位带走，后转交给青海省果洛藏族自治州班玛县警方刑拘；后又被班玛县警方以涉嫌“聚众扰乱社会秩序罪”等予以逮捕，指控其：<span lang="EN-US">1</span>、因撰写发表书籍，尤或指《消极抵抗的艺术》一书，其内容涉及批评中共政府对藏区的统治和刻意“在公众中制造混乱”，<span lang="EN-US">2</span>、使用违禁教材给学生上课，故而触犯了法律，应予逮捕法办；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2021</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月，其被青海省果洛藏族自治州班玛县法院庭审宣判，最终以“聚众扰乱社会秩序罪”等判处有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">4</span>年，刑期至<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">据悉，<span lang="EN-US">2019</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月被拘捕后至<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">25</span>日，一直被单独监禁；被判入狱后，其家人要求探监及为其寄送衣物，遭拒；同时，其亲友均因其被抓被判而遭恐吓和被禁言；至今，其是否狱中遭遇酷刑虐待以及身体状况释放欠佳等，均鲜为人知。目前服刑地点不确。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"></p><div class="separator" style="clear: both;
