<p><span></span></p><a name='more'></a><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;"><br /></span><p></p><p><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">（维权网信息中心报道）</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">日在越南河内遭抓捕，关押两个月后被遣返中国大陆。随即被指定居所监视居住</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">2</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">个月，后遭判刑</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">93</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">岁老母亲家中。</span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">董广平简历：</span></b><span style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">男，曾为员警，<span lang="EN-US">2</span></span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-bidi-font-family: 宋体;">•</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">2</span><span style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">年曾因参与民主政治活动被当局于以“煽动颠覆国家政权罪”判刑<span lang="EN-US">3</span>年；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">2014</span><span style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">2015</span><span style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月因其一家逃往泰国避难，<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">28</span>日被泰国警方在其持有联合国难民署签发的“庇护申请者证明书”（实为保护信）的前提下又以涉嫌“偷渡罪”抓捕，并关押于泰国曼谷市沙吞移民监狱；同年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月被中共警方押解回国，并被重庆市警方以涉嫌“偷越国境罪”刑拘；<span lang="EN-US">2016</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月，又被重庆警方更换罪名，以涉缣“颠覆国家政权罪”正式逮捕，关押在重庆市第二看守所；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">2018</span><span style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">13</span>日被重庆市第一中级法院以同罪名判处有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">3</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月，后送重庆市渝都监狱<span lang="EN-US">9</span>监区服刑；<span lang="EN-US">2019</span>年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">2</span>日，刑满释放；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 楷体;
