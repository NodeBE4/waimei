<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">1</span>日，本网获悉获刑<span lang="EN-US">3</span>年的甘肃张掖市山丹县法轮功学员周军奇的案情及简历，现在通报如下：<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">周军奇：甘肃张掖市山丹县法轮功学员。生于一九六四年十月，原是甘肃省山丹县焦化厂职工，从事电工技术指导。<span lang="EN-US">1999</span>年中共江泽民集团迫害法轮功后，周军奇因坚持信仰，多次遭迫害，<span lang="EN-US">2002</span>年他被迫下岗（失业），以后打零工也常遭无理辞退。<span lang="EN-US">2004</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月被山丹县国保警察抓捕、关押，期间遭酷刑折磨；后他被山丹法院判刑<span lang="EN-US">3</span>年半，在兰州监狱关押期间被迫做奴工，直到<span lang="EN-US">2007</span>年。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">周军奇<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">18</span>日被抓捕、关押构陷，<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">31</span>日，他被甘州区法院判刑三年。上诉后，<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">31</span>他日被中院裁定维持原判。周军奇被关押到兰州监狱。刑期至：<span lang="EN-US">2025</span>年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">17</span>日。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p>
