[被控涉嫌非法经营罪的哈尔滨市基督教会“麦子圣乐高中”校长、基督徒许峰今日获释](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2023/09/blog-post_80.html)
------

<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">3</span>日，本网获悉：被控涉嫌非法经营罪的哈尔滨市基督教会“麦子圣乐高中”校长、基督徒许峰今日获释。据方县桂律师：“今天主日上午，当事人哈尔滨传道人许峰走出看守所，期待案件能获得无罪判决，或者检察院撤诉。”<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">遭中共当局以非法经营罪起诉的哈尔滨市教会“麦子圣乐高中”校长、基督徒许峰案<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">14</span>日开庭。自<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月迄今许峰已经被羁押了<span lang="EN-US">2</span>年。今日获释。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">许峰简介：</span></b><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">1979</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年生，黑龙江省哈尔滨市人，基督徒，哈尔滨市基督教会音乐学校“麦子圣乐高中”校长，中国在押良心犯；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">因其所在“麦子圣乐高中”是哈尔滨市一所专门培养基督教音乐人才的艺术学校，尤以教授钢琴最为擅长，学生的培养方向主要是服务教会的唱诗班；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">然而，自中共新宗教事务条例实施以来，其及其学校频遭所在辖区哈尔滨当局的骚扰和威胁打击；自<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年以来，其家曾被警方两次莫名搜查，且其电话一直被监听；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2021</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">4</span>日，麦子圣乐高中突遭辖区内的特警、公安、宗教局、教育局等三四十人的莫名冲击，警方把学校所有老师及学生悉数抓进派出所；许峰亦在同日早上<span lang="EN-US">7:00</span>左右送孩子上学的路上遭警方拦截抓走；后获悉，许峰于<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">4 </span>日因涉嫌非法经营被哈尔滨市公安局松北分局刑事拘留，于<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US"> 11 </span>日，以涉嫌非法经营罪批准逮捕，被哈尔滨市公安局松北分局逮捕。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">之后其学校被查封，所有校财（如钢琴、电脑和资料等）被没收；<span lang="EN-US">2021 </span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">10</span>日，松北公安分局侦查终结，将许峰以涉嫌非法经营罪的名义移送哈尔滨松北区检察院。<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月，他们被以非法经营罪起诉到哈尔滨松北法院，<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">14</span>日，开庭审理。<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">3</span>日获释。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"></p><div class="separator" style="clear: both;
