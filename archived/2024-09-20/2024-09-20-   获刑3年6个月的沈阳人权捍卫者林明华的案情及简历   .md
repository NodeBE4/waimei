<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">20</span>日，本网获悉获刑<span lang="EN-US">3</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月的沈阳人权捍卫者林明华的案情及简历，现在通报如下：<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><b><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;">林明华：</span></b><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;">辽宁省沈阳市人权捍卫者，男，<span lang="EN-US">1963</span>年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">9</span>日生，是辽宁知名人权捍卫者林明洁的哥哥。因维权屡遭沈阳当局打压，<span lang="EN-US">2018</span>年被以寻衅滋事罪判刑<span lang="EN-US">1</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月，<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年又被以寻衅滋事罪、妨害公务罪判刑<span lang="EN-US">3</span>年。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;">因持续赴北京维权，于<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">28</span>日被沈阳市公安局铁西分局以涉嫌寻衅滋事罪刑事拘留，<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">2</span>日被监视居住，<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">4</span>日再次被刑事拘留，同年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">12</span>日被批准逮捕。林明华寻衅滋事案于<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">31</span>日上午<span lang="EN-US">9</span>时在沈阳市铁西区人民法院开庭。<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">6</span>日林明华第三次被以寻衅滋事罪判刑<span lang="EN-US">3</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月。刑期至：<span lang="EN-US">2027</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">26</span>日。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"></p><div class="separator" style="clear: both;
