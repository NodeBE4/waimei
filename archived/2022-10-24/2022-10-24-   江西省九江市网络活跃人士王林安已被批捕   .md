[江西省九江市网络活跃人士王林安已被批捕](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2022/10/blog-post_24.html)
------

<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">24</span>日，本网获悉：<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">7</span>日被抓的江西省九江市网络活跃人士王林安已被批捕。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">王林安：<span lang="EN-US">1957</span>年生，江西省九江市人，公职退休人员，网络活跃人士，网名“陶洼的铁鱼”，维权民主公民，中国在押维权人士。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2022</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">7</span>日，其在家里写文章时突然被江西省九江市国保警方带走，并搜走其个人电脑等物品，自此失联；后其家人接到办案警察电话告知，其被行政拘留<span lang="EN-US">15</span>天；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">同年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">20</span>日，行政拘留期满日，又被九江市警方以疑似“寻衅滋事罪”转为刑事拘留；<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">24</span>日获悉，其已被九江市检察院以同罪名正式批捕；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">据悉，因其与在押政治犯李玉荣同天被捕，故其二人或属同一案件，且与中共二十大即将召开，其网络言论令当局不安有关；至今，是否已有律师介入，尚不明晰。目前被羁押于江西省九江市看守所。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">对王林安的境况，本网将持续关注。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p>
