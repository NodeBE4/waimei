<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">15</span>日，本网获悉：获刑<span lang="EN-US">3</span>年湖南省株洲市法轮功学员喻颖祝在湖南女子监狱遭严重迫害。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">喻颖祝：湖南省株洲市法轮功学员，女，<span lang="EN-US">1974</span>年生，原株洲冶炼厂职工，家住株洲市石峰区清水塘沈家湾社区。因坚持修炼法轮功，在中共对法轮功持续<span lang="EN-US">24</span>年的迫害中，曾三次被劳教，共计入狱七年。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">喻颖祝于<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">29</span>日，她在家中被当地警察抓捕，后被关押至长沙市第四看守所。喻颖祝绝食抵制迫害两个月，身体非常虚弱，人瘦得皮包骨。<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">1</span>日，喻颖祝被释放回家，被“监视居住”。<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">24</span>日下午，喻颖祝再次在家中被雨花区法院指使的警察抓捕、后又被关押在长沙市第四看守所。<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">8</span>日，雨花区法院在第五审判庭对喻颖祝进行庭审，对喻颖祝判刑<span lang="EN-US">3</span>年。刑期至：<span lang="EN-US">2026</span>年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">8</span>日。<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">26</span>日她被转监到湖南女子监狱。家属最近才得知喻颖祝现在在湖南女子监狱被迫害得相当严重，身体非常不好，随时都有生命危险。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p>
