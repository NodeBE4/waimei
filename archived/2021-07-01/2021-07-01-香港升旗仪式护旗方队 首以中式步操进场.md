[香港升旗仪式护旗方队 首以中式步操进场](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210701-1163227)
------

<div>
<p>今天是香港特区成立24周年，升旗仪式于今早8时在湾仔金紫荆广场举行，护旗方队首次采用中式步操进场。</p><p>据《星岛日报》报道，香港新任政务司司长李家超首次以署理行政长官身份主礼，行政会议召集人陈智思、前行政长官曾荫权伉俪，以及部分中国中央驻港机构代表出席。</p><p>此次是护旗方队首次采用中式步操，另外飞行服务队两架直升机亦分别悬挂中国国旗及香港区旗，飞过维港上空，消防轮及水警轮则在会展对开海面进行水礼。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>自昨日起，会展一带明显加强保安，警方于港湾道入口架设水马围封，大批警员及保安在现场驻守，部份岀入口关闭。</p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
