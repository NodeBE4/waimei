<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月9日，本网获悉：湖南长沙姑娘段桃园举牌声援张展女士遭传唤未归，现获悉段桃园被行政拘留十天，人已从同升派出所送往长沙市拘留所关押。目前家属并未收到拘留通知书。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;">这位举牌声援张展的勇敢姑娘叫段桃园，湖南长沙人，因为<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">3</span>日举牌声援张展，于<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">7</span>日下午两点左右被长沙雨花区洞井派出所带走传唤，后遭行政拘留<span lang="EN-US">10</span>天。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;">获释不久的的著名人权捍卫者张展女士上个月底因营救被抓捕的甘肃青年活动人士张盼成，从上海只身赴甘肃，说服张母签署了委托书。随后其回到老家陕西咸阳，后在当地被上海警方带回，音信全无。后确认张展女士已遭当局拘留，关押在浦东看守所。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"></p><div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;"><a href="https://blogger.googleusercontent.com/img/b/R29vZ2xl/AVvXsEiyW_obs8JWCsgah0TvMlkYqO5F1MHahkyS96SqDSa7djQjmpOYczVy29WGtkAoaQ7Oa0fo5GZNFCzxpWNSWeVxRTzCqvpV1HnMh6W19zJVze3sqGHwLOt5_NCd_JC3wngRCv_C-SZPCVxfnGH9_bbjr2EwNQXjZcIGlX3q_mm2x1sSeQvU0oUDVvqbDgOa/s900/111.jfif" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;"><img border="0" data-original-height="900" data-original-width="900" height="320" src="https://blogger.googleusercontent.com/img/b/R29vZ2xl/AVvXsEiyW_obs8JWCsgah0TvMlkYqO5F1MHahkyS96SqDSa7djQjmpOYczVy29WGtkAoaQ7Oa0fo5GZNFCzxpWNSWeVxRTzCqvpV1HnMh6W19zJVze3sqGHwLOt5_NCd_JC3wngRCv_C-SZPCVxfnGH9_bbjr2EwNQXjZcIGlX3q_mm2x1sSeQvU0oUDVvqbDgOa/s320/111.jfif" width="320" /></a></div><p></p>
