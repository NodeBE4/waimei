[被羁押的广东民主人士王爱忠案已到法院  等待开庭](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2022/01/blog-post_86.html)
------

<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">因长期致力于推动中国的民主宪政，用实际行动践行着无组织、无领袖、无纲领的“南方街头运动”抗争，随备受当局的监控和骚扰；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月，又因参与街头反“朝核”抗议活动，而被广州市警方行政拘留<span lang="EN-US">7</span>天；<span lang="EN-US"> 2013</span>年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">26</span>日，因六四纪念日将至，故遭到广州市警方的约谈和警告；之后不久，因要求官员公布财产再遭广州警方拘留，后提起起诉行政拘留案；由于多次介入关注各地的维权事件，其身份亦在广州当地愈发“敏感”，于<span lang="EN-US">2014</span>年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">29</span>日，即六四<span lang="EN-US">25</span>周年前夕，被广州市警方从家中以“喝茶”名义带走，并被以涉嫌“寻衅滋事罪”刑拘；同年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">26</span>日，获取保候审释放；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2021</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">28</span>日，又一次被广州市天河区警方诱骗抓走，同时，其家遭查抄，其妻亦被传唤至当地派出所，主要问及其夫近期的交往及言论情况，并被警告不得发声；<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">29</span>日，其被以涉嫌“寻衅滋事罪”正式刑事拘留。<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">7</span>日获悉，王爱忠已被正式逮捕。<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">24</span>日获悉案件已到法院，等待开庭。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体;
