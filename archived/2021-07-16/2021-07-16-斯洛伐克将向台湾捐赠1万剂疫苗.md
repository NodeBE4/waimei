[斯洛伐克将向台湾捐赠1万剂疫苗](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210716-1169674)
------

<div>
<p>中欧国家斯洛伐克官方宣布，将向台湾捐赠1万剂冠病疫苗，作为台湾曾在疫情初期向斯洛伐克捐赠70万片口罩的回报。</p><p>欧盟昨天（15日）在官网公布，斯洛伐克将通过欧盟民防机制（European Civil Protection Mechanism），向台湾捐赠疫苗。台湾中央社引述消息人士称，斯洛伐克近两个月前便表达捐赠意愿。</p><p>斯洛伐克将是继立陶宛之后，第二个向台湾捐赠疫苗的欧盟成员国。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>斯洛伐克经济文化办事处也在面簿上证实，斯洛伐克将捐赠台湾1万剂疫苗，以表达支持，并说：“斯洛伐克并没有忘记自己的朋友，一年前台湾向斯洛伐克雪中送炭，捐赠了口罩。”</p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
