<div>
<p><span></span></p><a name='more'></a><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;"><br /></span><p></p><p><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">3</span>日，本网获悉获刑<span lang="EN-US">4</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月的宁夏动物保护志愿者李根山的案情及简历，现在通报如下：</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">&nbsp;</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;"><b>李根山：</b>男，<span lang="EN-US">1963</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">1</span>日出生，汉族，宁夏动物与环境保护志愿者。户籍所在地为宁夏回族自治区中卫市沙坡头区农牧厂。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月至<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月，李根山因参与举报腾格里沙漠排污案引发关注。涉事企业是他曾经供职的宁夏美利纸业集团有限公司。污染案曝光后，生态环境部挂牌督办。同年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月，他与其他志愿者一同举报了持枪盗猎案，获得<span lang="EN-US">1500</span>元人民币奖励。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2019</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月，他又举报了宁夏三元中泰冶金有限公司罐车偷排废水问题。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2020</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年，李根山一直在举报当地风力发电项目未经审批开工建设的问题。其认为中卫市的风力发电项目可能会破坏国家二级保护动物黄羊的栖息地。栖息地所在的麦垛山有两个风力发电项目，分别属于宁夏中卫宁清风力发电有限公司和宁夏中卫麦垛山新能源有限公司。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2020</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">9</span>日，宁夏回族自治区中卫市公安局沙坡头区分局抓捕了李根山。9月10被</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 16px;">以</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">涉嫌寻衅滋事罪刑事拘留。</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">9</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">月</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">29</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">日，李根山人被以涉嫌非法猎捕、杀害珍贵野生动物罪，敲诈勒索罪，寻衅滋事罪被中卫市沙坡头区人民检察院批准逮捕。</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">李根山被抓捕在网络上引起争议，有网友指他是因为举报环保问题而遭到了报复。但中卫警方回应称，此案与举报环保问题无关，警方是在依法办案。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体;
