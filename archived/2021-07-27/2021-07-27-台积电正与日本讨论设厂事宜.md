[台积电正与日本讨论设厂事宜](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210727-1174270)
------

<div>
<p>全球半导体巨头台湾积体电路制造公司（TSMC）董事长刘德音透露，目标是在日本设厂，每周都与对方讨论。</p><p>据共同社报道，刘德音在26日的线上股东大会上说，已进行多次实地考察，等有结果后说明。但刘德音未提及具体合作方和建设地点。</p><p>在全球半导体短缺导致各国需求高涨的情况下，TSMC提出未来三年投入1000亿美元（1358.21亿新元）增强产能的方针。日本经济产业省为强化半导体的国内生产，在背后就入驻日本与TSMC展开商讨。TSMC也在15日公布财报时暗示正在推进探讨。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>刘德音指出，日本生产成本高于台湾，有必要与日方进行磋商。他还称与德国也在沟通设厂事宜。</p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
