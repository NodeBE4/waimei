[鸿星尔克因订单量巨大昨暂停平台直播](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210727-1174276)
------

<div>
<p>中国运动品牌鸿星尔克昨天在抖音、快手、天猫旗舰店相继发布公告，表示由于有大量订单需要加急处理，为了给消费者更好的体验，暂时停止直播。</p><p>根据《南方都市报》报道，自鸿星尔克上周四（22日）宣布向河南捐赠5000万元（人民币，下同，约1000万新元）物资并在次日登上热搜后，中国网民对鸿星尔克的购物热情高涨。鸿星尔克在抖音、快手、淘宝天猫旗舰店三大直播平台均已布局店铺直播，这些直播间也成为网友们购物的主战场。</p><p>据鸿星尔克在快手的服务商石狮青创男装直播基地透露，24日凌晨直播间爆发后，他们决定立刻启动72小时主播无缝衔接不下播，最终单场直播销售额突破400万元。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>但在销售爆发后，鸿星尔克的发货能力未能跟上消费者的发货需求。鸿星尔克前天在各大平台发布公告称，目前订单延迟数据不准，发货能力不能跟上发货需求，将加急处理并尽快发货，同时希望消费者理性消费。</p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
