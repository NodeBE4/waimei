[因抗议过度防疫而遭刑事拘留的中央美院毕业生张东辉因检察院撤诉而无罪获释](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2022/12/blog-post_19.html)
------

<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">10</span>日，本网获悉：因抗议过度防疫于<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">22</span>日而遭刑事拘留的中央美院毕业生张东辉案被披露后受到广泛关注，<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">8</span>日检察院撤诉，当日张东辉无罪获释。</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">&nbsp;</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年出生，河北省石家庄市人，北京市雕塑艺术从业者，中国曾押良心犯。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体;
