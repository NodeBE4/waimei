<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2025</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">13</span>日，本网获悉获刑<span lang="EN-US">4</span>年的福建省福州市法轮功学员何鹏飞的案情及简历，现在通报如下：<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">何鹏飞：</span></b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">福建省福州市法轮功学员，<span lang="EN-US">1968</span>年生，<span lang="EN-US">2002</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月从部队转业到福州市广播电视局稽查队工作。<span lang="EN-US">2011</span>年单位合并后，他转到福州市文化市场综合执法支队当科级干部。<span lang="EN-US">2011</span>年开始修炼法轮功。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">2015</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">年，因何鹏飞写法轮功标语传播，被福州市仓山区公安、检察院、法院构陷判刑<span lang="EN-US">5</span>年半，遭到残酷迫害，<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月出狱。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ansi-language: EN-US; mso-bidi-font-family: &quot;Times New Roman&quot;; mso-bidi-language: AR-SA; mso-bidi-theme-font: minor-bidi; mso-fareast-language: ZH-CN;">2024</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ansi-language: EN-US; mso-bidi-font-family: &quot;Times New Roman&quot;; mso-bidi-language: AR-SA; mso-bidi-theme-font: minor-bidi; mso-fareast-language: ZH-CN;">年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">22</span>日，何鹏飞被闯入家中的福州市马尾区国保大队警察抓捕，被关押在福州市第二看守所。后遭刑事拘留、批捕、起诉。<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">5</span>日他被福州市闽清县法院庭审。后悉，何鹏飞被判刑<span lang="EN-US">4</span>年，刑期至：<span lang="EN-US">2028</span>年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">21</span>日。</span>
