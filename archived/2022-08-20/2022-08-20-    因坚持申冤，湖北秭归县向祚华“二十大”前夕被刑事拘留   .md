[因坚持申冤，湖北秭归县向祚华“二十大”前夕被刑事拘留](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2022/08/blog-post_20.html)

<p><span></span></p><a name='more'></a><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;"><br /></span><p></p><p><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">（维权网信息中心报道）</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">2022</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">年</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">8</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">月</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">8</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">月</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">17</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">日，住湖北省宜昌市秭归县九畹溪镇砚窝台村二组村民向祚华，因不服正当防卫案被判十年徒刑而一直在申冤，被秭归县公安局以“涉嫌寻衅滋事罪”抓捕现羁押看守所，向祚华之妻颜金芳请求社会各界，就公安机关为“二十大”维稳而滥用职权行为给予关注。</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">1999</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;
