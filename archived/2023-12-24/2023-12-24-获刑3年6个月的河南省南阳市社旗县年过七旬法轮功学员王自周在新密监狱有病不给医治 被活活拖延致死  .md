mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">24</span>日，本网获悉：获刑<span lang="EN-US">3</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月的河南省南阳市社旗县年过七旬的法轮功学员王自周在新密监狱有病不给医治，于<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">14</span>日被狱方活活拖延迫害致死，终年<span lang="EN-US">74</span>岁。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">王自周：河南省南阳市社旗县法轮功学员。<span lang="EN-US">1950</span>年生，家住河南省南阳市社旗县，家中妻子双目失明，靠他照顾生活起居。自一九九九年中共集团疯狂迫害法轮功后，经常遭当地派出所、县国保警察骚扰、抓捕、关押，他曾两次被判刑。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">王自周<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">8</span>日被社旗县国保大队警察从家中抓捕、构陷，后被唐河县法院判刑<span lang="EN-US">3</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月。王自周被关押在郑州市新密监狱。刑期应至：<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">7</span>日。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">据悉，王自周被关押在新密监狱九监区，遭狱方高压洗脑迫害，致使他出现严重病症，起居不能自理，而狱方故意不给他及时治疗，最后于<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">14</span>日将他活活拖延致死。狱方还称是心脏病所致。王自周被绑架前非常健康，也从来没有过心脏病史。。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><br /><p></p>
