<p>【大纪元2023年11月07日讯】11月7日17时40分左右，四川<a href="https://www.epochtimes.com/gb/tag/%E9%9B%85%E5%AE%89.html">雅安</a>境内一<a href="https://www.epochtimes.com/gb/tag/%E6%B2%B9%E7%BD%90%E8%BD%A6.html">油罐车</a>发生<a href="https://www.epochtimes.com/gb/tag/%E7%88%86%E7%82%B8.html">爆炸</a>燃烧，引燃旁边两辆货车，已致2死2伤。</p><p>综合陆媒报导，四川<a href="https://www.epochtimes.com/gb/tag/%E9%9B%85%E5%AE%89.html">雅安</a>S8邛名<a href="https://www.epochtimes.com/gb/tag/%E9%AB%98%E9%80%9F.html">高速</a>名山服务区（成都方向）内的危化品运输车辆发生<a href="https://www.epochtimes.com/gb/tag/%E7%88%86%E7%82%B8.html">爆炸</a>燃烧事故，已致2人死亡，另有2名伤者已送医救治。</p><figure id="attachment_14111604" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-14111604" style="width: 580px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14111604-2c3a464c052da41a1cf2636217d63fee.png"><img class="size-full wp-image-14111604" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id14111604-2c3a464c052da41a1cf2636217d63fee.png" alt="" width="580" height="343" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-14111604" class="wp-caption-text">（网络截图）</figcaption></figure><p>18时45分，成名<a href="https://www.epochtimes.com/gb/tag/%E9%AB%98%E9%80%9F.html">高速</a>应急救援已对成名高速成都往雅安方向所有车辆在百丈湖分流，关闭成雅高速上成名高速双向匝道。</p><p>目前，应急救援还在进行中。</p><p>责任编辑：夏松#</p>
