[河南省安阳市五十多位农民维权人士起诉省政府](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2022/04/blog-post_23.html)
------

<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">23</span>日，本网获悉：现安阳市五十多位农民维权人士已起诉至郑州市铁路中级人民法院。希望法院可以维护法律的公平。希望省政府可以正确的看待农民的维权。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">事情经过：<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">23</span>日，河南省政府收到安阳市五十位维权人士提交的信息公开申请。原告申请公开<span lang="EN-US">:</span>“河南省安阳市文峰区中华路七个城中村拆迁是否是河南省重点项目。”<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2021</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">29</span>日，河南省政府向原告邮递告知书《豫政办依申告<span lang="EN-US">[2021]391</span>号》告知原告描述不准确，不便查找。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2022</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">12</span>日河南省政府收到了原告的补正材料，<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">14</span>日向原告邮递了政府信息公开申请延期答复告知书《豫政办依申告<span lang="EN-US">[2022142</span>号》告知原告需要延长答复期限，延长期限不超过<span lang="EN-US">20</span>个工作日。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">从<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">12</span>日至今，已经超过了<span lang="EN-US">40</span>个工作日，超出了信息答复的时间，原告至今没有收到信息公开答复。根据《信息公开条例》<span lang="EN-US">,</span>原告请求被告依法对原告的信息公开进行答复。目前，河南省安阳市五十多位农民维权人士起诉了河南省政府。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p>
