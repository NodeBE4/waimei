[一名中国驻韩领事醉驾被送检](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210712-1167925)
------

<div>
<p>韩国光州西部警察署12日发布消息，中国驻光州总领馆的一名领事酒驾，被移交检方审查起诉。</p><p>据韩联社报道，该领事6月20日凌晨1点30分至2点25分在韩国光州市东区全南大学医院至西区枫岩洞路段酒后驾驶，被路人举报后在其所住小区地下停车场被警方查获。</p><p>仪器显示，A某血液酒精浓度达到0.119%，构成吊销驾驶证的条件。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>该中国领事声称，要到医院探望中国公民后开车回家，属于执行公务，应享受外交豁免权。但韩国警方认为，很难将醉驾视为公务。</p><p>这名人员涉嫌违反《道路交通法》被移交检方审查起诉，可能面临刑事处罚。</p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
