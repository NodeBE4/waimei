[蔡英文：感谢各方努力顺利推动鸿海台积电购买疫苗案](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210712-1168152)
------

<div>
<p>台积电及鸿海创办人郭台铭委托的永龄基金会成功签约购买1000万剂德制BNT疫苗，台湾总统府今天表示，总统蔡英文感谢各方努力，让相关专案能够顺利推动。</p><p>根据联合新闻网报道，总统府发言人张惇涵表示，蔡英文还肯定了行政院、中央流行疫情指挥中心及政府法律团队，在谈判过程中日以继夜的努力，依照台湾防疫计划，全力协助捐赠单位，迅速完备复杂繁琐的法律程序。</p><p>发言人还说，政府与民间共同协力，让此次疫苗采购捐赠专案，符合“原厂制造”、“原厂标签”、“直送台湾”三原则的共识与期待，并捍卫台湾人健康安全之各项权益。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>张惇涵表示，虽然这两项专案已经完成采购契约及捐赠契约的签署，但后续仍有确保疫苗供应的品质与时程等繁重的工作要进行，期盼大家共同努力，让后续工作顺利推进。</p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
