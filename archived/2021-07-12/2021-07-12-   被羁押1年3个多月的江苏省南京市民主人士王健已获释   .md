[被羁押1年3个多月的江苏省南京市民主人士王健已获释](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2021/07/13.html)
------

<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">王健：江苏省南京市人，民主维权公民。<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">17</span>日因言获罪遭强迫失踪。后获知遭刑事拘留。现已获释。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2015</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">1</span>日—<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">5</span>日，因到江苏省苏州市声援维权人士、人权捍卫者范木根血拆案庭审，连续到苏州市中级法院围观，随被警方抓至当地派出所关押，次日即被警方刑拘，被关押在南京市江宁看守所；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">后在取保候审期间再次前往苏州声援范木根案庭审，又被警方行政拘留<span lang="EN-US">10</span>天；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2015</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">3</span>日，六四敏感日期间，其被南京市警方突然从家中强行带走，并被以涉嫌“寻衅滋事罪”正式逮捕，且被指涉嫌危害国家安全；<span lang="EN-US">2016</span>年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月，其案件开庭审理；<span lang="EN-US">2016</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">13</span>日，被南京市江宁区法院一审以“寻衅滋事罪”判处有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">2</span>年；<span lang="EN-US">2017</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">27</span>日经南京市中级法院二审宣判，依然维持原判；<span lang="EN-US">2017</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">27</span>日刑满释放。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2018</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">17</span>日，因曾向当地公安部门申请“反对美国发动贸易战”、“支持国家采取一切反制措施”游行示威而被南京市警方带走，次日被刑拘，后又被以“寻衅滋事罪”监视居住<span lang="EN-US">4</span>个半月；<span lang="EN-US">2018</span>年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">30</span>日，取保获释。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体;
