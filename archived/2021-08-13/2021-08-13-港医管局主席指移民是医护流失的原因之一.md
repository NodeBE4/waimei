[港医管局主席指移民是医护流失的原因之一](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210813-1181578)
------

<div>
<p>香港医院管理局主席范鸿龄指，香港医护流失率上升，部分是因移民所致，情况令人忧虑。</p><p>据星岛日报报道，范鸿龄说，去年7月至今年6月底，全职医生包括实习医生及牙医的流失率为4.6%，全职护士的流失率则为6.5%，较去年同期上升0.9%。</p><p>他说，从离职员工申请详尽服务纪录及提早报税等行动推断，医护流失率上升部分是因移民所致，形容情况令人忧虑。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>范鸿龄表示，私家医院面临医生移民致人手不足的问题，从公立医院挖角，令情况雪上加霜。他指出，香港人口老化及患病年轻化，令公立医院的服务需求增加，局方将采取“截上游、放下游、中间分流”的策略，期望供求平衡，包括年底推行专科公私营协作计划。</p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
