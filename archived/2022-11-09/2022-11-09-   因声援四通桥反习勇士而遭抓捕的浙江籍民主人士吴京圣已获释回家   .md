[因声援四通桥反习勇士而遭抓捕的浙江籍民主人士吴京圣已获释回家](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2022/11/blog-post_93.html)
------

<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">9</span>日，本网获悉：因声援四通桥反习勇士而遭抓捕的浙江籍民主人士吴京圣已获释回家。他是<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">16</span>日被警方抓捕拘留的，关押<span lang="EN-US">15</span>天后，于<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">31</span>日获释。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">吴京圣简历：</span></b><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">1965</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月出生，曾用名吴海古，浙江省衢州市人，早期职业原为教师，后成为自由职业者，六四亲历者，维权公民，民主人权斗士，中国在押政治犯。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">早年曾就读于浙江师范大学；毕业后，<span lang="EN-US">1989</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">15</span>日<span lang="EN-US">-6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">4</span>日，曾因给学生收听美国之音、台湾的亚洲之声播放六四真相，而被浙江当局知晓和很快拘捕，并于同年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">12</span>日被衢州市法院以“反革命宣传煽动罪”判刑<span lang="EN-US">1</span>年，直至<span lang="EN-US">1990</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">12</span>日刑满出狱；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">后其为谋生计而移居北方，常年在河北省廊坊市三河市燕郊镇以开“摩的”载客为生，一直独身；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2013</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">25</span>日<span lang="EN-US">-8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">30</span>日，曾因在<span lang="EN-US">QQ</span>群里转发声援民主人士许志永的联署签名信及访民图片，而被北京市房山区警方以“编造传播虚假恐怖信息罪”刑拘，并关押于北京市房山区看守所，后被取保释放；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2018</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">17</span>日，曾因其坐在摩的驾驶座上怒斥城管的视频被人发于网上广传，而被河北省三河市警方以不确罪名抓捕，并被关押于河北省三河市看守所；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2022</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">15</span>日，因在微信群、脸书（<span lang="EN-US">Facebook</span>）转发声援北京四通桥勇士彭立发贴文，而被当地派出所警方立刻喝茶；后虽被释放回家，但因其在脸书上又发被喝茶经过，称皆因把彭义士的标语图片编辑发到微信群里及个人界面而被监控，并表明将继续声援彭勇士及其贴文，以唤起更多人的心智，遂于次日（<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">16</span>日）被老家浙江省衢州市衢江区警方抓捕，，拘留<span lang="EN-US">15</span>天后，于<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">31</span>日获释。</span></p><div class="separator" style="clear: both;
