<p><span></span></p><a name='more'></a><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;"><br /></span><p></p><p><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;">（维权网信息中心报道）</span><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt;">2023</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;">年</span><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt;">6</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;">月</span><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt;">29</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;">日，本网获悉被中共当局判刑</span><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt;">6</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;">年的上海法轮功学员何冰刚的案情及简历，现在通报如下：</span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">何冰刚</span></b><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">：<span lang="EN-US">1977</span>年出生，上海市人，上海市徐汇区东安二村<span lang="EN-US">74</span>号公民，原复旦大学<span lang="EN-US">98</span>级计算器系硕士研究生，软件工程师，上海长宁区“郁文科技”计算机经营部经办人和总经理，宗教信仰追求者，法轮功修炼者和信仰者，中国在押良心犯。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">何冰刚系从小个性温良友善，乐于助人，天资聪慧；<span lang="EN-US">1991</span>年，曾因发明了“盲人用计算器有声辅统”，而获得“第六届上海市亿利达青少年发明奖”和第六届全国发明奖银奖；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">1994</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年，曾因品学兼优而被保送复旦大学，后被直升为复旦大学研究生；然而，读研期间，因开始修炼法轮功，遂于<span lang="EN-US">2000</span>年被复旦大学强制休学，并很快被上海市杨浦区警方带走拘押、劳教；次年又被上海市徐汇区法院以邪教罪重判<span lang="EN-US">6</span>年，后在上海提篮桥监狱服刑；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2007</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年刑满出狱后，其开始自创“郁文科技”公司（又即计算机经营部）<span lang="EN-US">,</span>但自公司开业起，其公司即被当局视为维稳对象而长期监控；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2010</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">15</span>日，曾因上海世博会即将召开，当局恐其成为不稳定因素，故而被上海市长宁区警方设陷，将其公司员工一起抓捕，后其被以“拥有法轮功书籍，并将一张光盘赠送给公司某经理”为由转正式拘捕；拘押期间，曾遭酷刑虐待，最终导致身体瘫痪致残；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2011</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月，其被上海市长宁区法院再次以邪教罪判刑<span lang="EN-US">5</span>年；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2021</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">9</span>日，其与回国探亲、同为法轮功修炼者的妻子张轶博又被上海市长宁区警方强行带走，家被查抄，当场搜走个人计算机、电子书等多个私人物品；后其二人均被刑拘，并被羁押于长宁区看守所；同年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">15</span>日，其被长宁区检察院以涉嫌危害国家安全罪由予以批捕；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2022</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">-3</span>月，其夫妻二人案被长宁区检察院起诉，并移交至上海市奉贤区法院审查；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2023</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">12</span>日，在超期羁押<span lang="EN-US">2</span>年之后，终被奉贤区法院重判有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">6</span>年；其不服，上诉；后经上海市第一中级法院二审裁决，依然维持原判；刑期至<span lang="EN-US">2027</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">8</span>日；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">据悉，看守所关押期间，其身体原本已半四肢瘫痪，但因又被长期拘押而致身体病情又被恶化，迄今其四肢已完全瘫痪；其代理律师多次要求会见，均被拒绝。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">目前在上海市提篮桥监狱（上海市虹口区长阳路<span lang="EN-US">147</span>号，邮政编码：<span lang="EN-US">200082</span>）服刑。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"></p><div class="separator" style="clear: both;
