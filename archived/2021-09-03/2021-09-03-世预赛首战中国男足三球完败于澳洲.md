[世预赛首战中国男足三球完败于澳洲](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210903-1189861)
------

<div>
<p>在今天凌晨进行的世界杯预选赛亚洲区12强赛首轮比赛中，中国男足0:3完败于澳大利亚。</p><p>据中新社报道，本场比赛中国队主教练李铁基本沿用40强赛阶段阵容，前锋依旧是艾克森搭档武磊，中场方面则是吴曦、张稀哲、吴兴涵和金敬道，后防线上做出一定调整，张琳芃回到右后卫，取代受伤未能出征的唐淼，于大宝出任中卫搭档蒋光太，左后卫依旧是王燊超，门将是颜骏凌。</p><p>第23分钟，澳大利亚队中后场长传找到禁区内的马比尔，后者推射被颜骏凌封堵出，而后接塔加特传中抢点破门。第26分钟，澳大利亚打出反击，形成前场2打2，博伊尔远射扩大比分。第69分钟，马比尔禁区左侧射门被颜骏凌扑出，博伊尔补射帮助澳大利亚扩大比分。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>整场比赛，中国队处于明显下风。最终，中国男足0:3不敌澳大利亚。</p><p>中国队9月7日将对阵日本队，对手在首轮比赛中主场0:1不敌阿曼。</p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/zbpe-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=zbpe399&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_desktop-zbpe399_2.png?dHEiGouxxZpWwWTJE04dyGyUN74JOU2z" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_desktop-zbpe399_2.png?dHEiGouxxZpWwWTJE04dyGyUN74JOU2z" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/zbpe-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=zbpe399&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_mobile-zbpe399_2.png?qZnBcIGCJvPR8A2IAZOZuOxdoGZDatiI" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_mobile-zbpe399_2.png?qZnBcIGCJvPR8A2IAZOZuOxdoGZDatiI" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
