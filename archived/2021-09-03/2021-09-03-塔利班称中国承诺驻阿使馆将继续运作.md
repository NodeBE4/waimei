[塔利班称中国承诺驻阿使馆将继续运作](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210903-1189876)
------

<div>
<p>中国外交部部长助理吴江浩昨天（2日）同阿富汗塔利班驻多哈政治办事处副主任哈纳菲通电话。吴江浩在通话中强调，中国一贯尊重阿富汗主权独立和领土完整，奉行面向全体阿富汗人民的友好政策。</p><p>据中国外交部官网发布的信息，吴江浩说，阿富汗形势已经发生根本性变化，阿前途命运重新掌握在阿富汗人民手中，希望阿富汗尽快实现和平稳定，重建美好家园。</p><p>塔利班发言人沙欣（Suhail Shaheen）则在推特上引述吴江浩称，中国承诺驻阿使馆将继续运作，并会继续为阿富汗提供与增加人道援助，特别是在冠病治疗方面。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p><strong>延伸阅读</strong></p><p><a href="https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/world/story20210903-1189865">塔利班称中国承诺喀布尔使馆继续运作</a></p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/zbpe-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=zbpe399&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_desktop-zbpe399_2.png?dHEiGouxxZpWwWTJE04dyGyUN74JOU2z" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_desktop-zbpe399_2.png?dHEiGouxxZpWwWTJE04dyGyUN74JOU2z" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/zbpe-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=zbpe399&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_mobile-zbpe399_2.png?qZnBcIGCJvPR8A2IAZOZuOxdoGZDatiI" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_mobile-zbpe399_2.png?qZnBcIGCJvPR8A2IAZOZuOxdoGZDatiI" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
