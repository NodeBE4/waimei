[沙特8月对华原油出口按年增长53%](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210920-1195458)
------

<div>
<p>由于主要产油国放松减产，全球最大的石油出口国沙特在8月份连续第九个月保持中国第一大原油供应国的地位。</p><p>据路透社报道，中国海关总署今天的数据显示，8月中国自沙特的原油进口较上年同期猛增53%，达到806万吨，或196万桶/日。</p><p>相比之下，去年7月为158万桶/日，8月为124万桶/日。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>中国8月从俄罗斯进口原油653万吨，或159万桶/日，基本持平于7月的156万桶/日。</p><p>据报道，俄罗斯与沙特进口量存在很大差距，是由于中国当局决定削减独立炼油商的原油进口配额，而这些炼油商青睐俄罗斯的ESPO混合油。</p><p>来自马来西亚的原油进口比去年同期增加一倍多，达到175万吨。</p><p>与此同时，来自阿联酋的原油进口同比下降了近40%。</p><div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div><p>自今年年初以来，官方数据显示，从伊朗或委内瑞拉的进口一直为零。</p>            <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/zbpe-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=zbpe399&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_desktop-zbpe399_2.png?dHEiGouxxZpWwWTJE04dyGyUN74JOU2z" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_desktop-zbpe399_2.png?dHEiGouxxZpWwWTJE04dyGyUN74JOU2z" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/zbpe-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=zbpe399&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_mobile-zbpe399_2.png?qZnBcIGCJvPR8A2IAZOZuOxdoGZDatiI" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_mobile-zbpe399_2.png?qZnBcIGCJvPR8A2IAZOZuOxdoGZDatiI" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
