<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">25</span>日，本网获悉获刑<span lang="EN-US">2</span>年的哈尔滨法轮功学员王宏的案情及简介，现在通报如下：<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">王宏</span></b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">：黑龙江哈尔滨法轮功学员，男，<span lang="EN-US">1967</span>年生，<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">57</span>岁。<span lang="EN-US">2007</span>年开始修炼法轮功。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">王宏在<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">13</span>日下夜班回家，在自家小区内被五名警察拦截，他们扣留王宏车内未装订的《天赐洪福》等月刊半成品，并在当天将王宏关押到哈尔滨市第二看守所。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">2024</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">11</span>日，律师到国保支队依法提出撤销王宏涉嫌利用“邪教组织破坏法律实施罪”一案并立即释放的要求；家属提出对王宏取保申请均被铁路公安处国保支队拒绝。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">2024</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">17</span>日，王宏被哈尔滨铁路运输法院判刑<span lang="EN-US">2</span>年。王宏不服上诉。<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">9</span>日，哈尔滨铁路运输中级法院开庭，驳回上诉，维持原判。刑期至：<span lang="EN-US">2025</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">13</span>日。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p>
