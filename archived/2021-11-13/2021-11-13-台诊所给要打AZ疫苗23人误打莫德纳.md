[台诊所给要打AZ疫苗23人误打莫德纳](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20211113-1213175)
------

<div>
<p>台北市卫生局今天（13日）指，某诊所12日原订23人要打阿斯利康（AZ）疫苗第二剂，却误接种成莫德纳。该诊所已被暂停接种服务，卫生局并启动健康关怀。</p><p>综合联合新闻网与中央社报道，台北市卫生局称，共20名民众透过COVID-19公费疫苗预约平台，在该诊所预约第二剂AZ疫苗接种，另三名登记残剂接种。不过该诊所人员取出疫苗，未落实“三读五对”检查，误拿莫德纳疫苗，导致23人打接种莫德纳。</p><p>卫生局说，诊所至当日看诊结束后，盘点药物时发现AZ疫苗未减少，即通报卫生局。卫生局指，该诊所11月10至12日施打AZ疫苗，预计13至17日接种莫德纳疫苗。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>卫生局已启动民众健康关怀，并通报卫生福利部疾病管制署，已暂停该诊所疫苗接种服务，至确认完成改善为止。</p>            <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/zbpe-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=zbpe399&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_desktop-zbpe399_2.png?VersionId=dHEiGouxxZpWwWTJE04dyGyUN74JOU2z" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_desktop-zbpe399_2.png?VersionId=dHEiGouxxZpWwWTJE04dyGyUN74JOU2z" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/zbpe-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=zbpe399&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_mobile-zbpe399_2.png?VersionId=qZnBcIGCJvPR8A2IAZOZuOxdoGZDatiI" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_mobile-zbpe399_2.png?VersionId=qZnBcIGCJvPR8A2IAZOZuOxdoGZDatiI" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
