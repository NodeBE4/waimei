[遭中共当局一审判刑7年的网络安全工程师阮晓寰（网名“编程随想”）的案情及简历](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2023/03/7_24.html)
------

<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">24</span>日，本网获悉遭中共当局一审判刑<span lang="EN-US">7</span>年的网络安全工程师阮晓寰（网名“编程随想”）的案情及简历，现通报如下：<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">阮晓寰：</span></b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">网名“编程随想”。<span lang="EN-US">1977</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">10</span>日出生，福建省泉州市人，上海市杨浦区民京路<span lang="EN-US">558</span>号长住居民，网络安全工程师，民主异见人士，中国在押政治犯。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2009</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">-2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月间，曾因撰写含有针对中共当局及其政体制度等不利信息的文章百余篇，并将其发布于境外网络平台，在引发网民广泛热议的同时；也引起了中共官方的高度警惕、极端不满和伺机打击；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2021</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">10</span>日，因其曾经常使用网名“编程随想”，被中共国安人员查出其在豆瓣网注册过用的个人信息（内含大量针对中共政权的批评性言论），遂被上海市杨浦区警方从家中带走，次日即被以涉嫌“煽动颠覆国家政权罪”刑拘，家遭查抄，当场搜走正在使用中的计算机、手机及多部<span lang="EN-US">/</span>件私人电子设备等物品；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">同年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">17</span>日，被上海市杨浦区检察院以同罪名正式批捕，指控其因长期存有对中共政权的不满情绪，多次“使用计算机编写<span lang="EN-US">100</span>多篇带有造谣、诽谤煽颠性文章，内容涉及攻击抹黑现行政治体制、煽动颠覆国家政权、意图推翻社会主义制度”，并通过境外网站进行发布，造成大量网民浏览、评论、转发和效仿等“恶劣后果”，因而触犯了国家相关法律，应当逮捕法办；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">在被严重超押近<span lang="EN-US">2</span>年，<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">10</span>日，终被上海市第二中级法院一审以“煽动颠覆国家政权罪”秘密重判有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">7</span>年，剥夺政治权利<span lang="EN-US">2</span>年，没收财产人民币<span lang="EN-US">2</span>万元，刑期至<span lang="EN-US">2028</span>年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">9</span>日；其不服，上诉；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">据悉，其被宣判后，其父兄两家均遭警方约谈施压，“劝”其听任政府为其聘用律师，其妻亦遭警察围堵训诫<span lang="EN-US">2</span>小时；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">截至<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">28</span>日，同月<span lang="EN-US">22</span>日其案已被上海市高级法院立案，<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">28</span>日，鉴于上海高级法院率先在二审期间使用“法援”律师侵占辩护席位，导致其妻委托律师无法行使合法辩护权，故其家人对相关司法人员的渎职行为和侵犯被告人辩护权等要求彻查纠正，不断信访、举报。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体;
