<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">10</span>日，本网获悉获刑<span lang="EN-US">15</span>年狱中遭酷刑致死的藏族社会工作者丹增曲卡的情况，现通报如下：<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">1986</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年出生，西藏自治区拉萨市人，又名丹曲，藏名<span lang="EN-US"> Tenzin Choedak</span>或<span lang="EN-US">Tenchoe</span>，社会工作者，中国曾押政治犯。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">曾于<span lang="EN-US">1990</span>年代在印度达兰萨拉的西藏儿童村完成<span lang="EN-US">11</span>年级的教育课程；<span lang="EN-US">2005</span>年返回西藏，在当地的欧洲非政府组织红十字会工作，并在拉萨和日喀则等多个地区推动环保工作；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2008</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">10</span>日，因参与拉萨的和平抗议活动，被当地警方拘捕；据悉，在拘捕期间曾遭遇严刑逼供，理由是其父曾在西藏政治舞台上活跃多年，但<span lang="EN-US">1993</span>年被迫离开西藏流亡印度，严刑拷打他目的是确定其抗议行动是否受到父亲的影响；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2008</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">27</span>日，被拉萨市中级法院以“间谍罪”判处有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">15</span>年，并处罚款人民币<span lang="EN-US">1</span>万元，剥夺政治权利<span lang="EN-US">5</span>年；刑期应至<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">9</span>日；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">据悉，其在狱中惨遭酷刑折磨，致其吐血、脑部损伤，身体备受摧残；<span lang="EN-US">2014</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">3</span>日，其家人得知其被狱方紧急送往医院，称其手、腿在送达医院时已经严重粉碎性骨折；同年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">5</span>日，其在保外就医第二天，不治身亡。此前在西藏自治区拉萨市曲水监狱（西藏自治区拉萨市曲水县聂当乡 ，邮编<span lang="EN-US"> 850600</span>）服刑。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"></p><div class="separator" style="clear: both;
