<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">23</span>日，本网获悉：山东平度市七旬法轮功学员<a name="_Hlk170050623">綦瑞英</a>女士被判刑<span lang="EN-US">1</span>年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>个月。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">綦瑞英：</span></b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">山东平度市法轮功学员，女，<span lang="EN-US">1954</span>年生，<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">70</span>岁。家住山东省平度市蓼兰镇西王家庄村。她从一九九八年开始修炼法轮功。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">2023</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">17</span>日上午，綦瑞英正在集市上发法轮功材料，恰巧将资料发到平度<span lang="EN-US">610</span>人员手里。那人打电话叫来万家镇派出所警察，抓捕了綦瑞英。第二天，<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">18</span>日，綦瑞英被关押到即墨市普东看守所。后綦瑞英遭刑事拘留、批捕、起诉。<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月下旬，在普东看守所，被平度法院非法开庭，她被判刑<span lang="EN-US">1</span>年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>个月，罚金一万元。刑期至：<span lang="EN-US">2025</span>年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">16</span>日。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p>
