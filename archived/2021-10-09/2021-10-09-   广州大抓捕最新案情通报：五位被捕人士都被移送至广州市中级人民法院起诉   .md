<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">9</span>日，本网获悉：历时十个月，广州大抓捕中五位被捕人士范一平、范文成、胡天峰、赖天明、韦亚妮，终于在<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">8</span>日案宗被移送至广州市中级人民法院。五人被以”颠覆国家政权罪“起诉，案由涉及组党，具体事实无非聚餐闲聊时政、去云南旅游等。广州市检察院的量刑建议是：范文成七年、范一平五年，其余胡天峰、赖天明、韦亚妮均为三年。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">据悉，胡天峰获许会见律师时称是他自己签字解除对李国蓓律师的委托，但说话呆滞、显得不自然。胡妻早先指出该信的字迹并非胡天峰本人的。胡天峰在指定居所监视居住期间及羁押于广州国安局看守所期间不知遭遇了什么，精神状态是否正常，令人担忧。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2020</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">12</span>日，广州国安局以涉嫌颠覆国家政权罪为由抓捕广州民运人士。范一平、胡天峰、范文成、赖见君、韦亚妮随后均被以涉嫌“颠覆国家政权罪”指定居所监视居住，后被刑事拘留、批捕，现案件起诉到法院。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">对广州<span lang="EN-US">11.12</span>大抓捕案进展，本网将持续关注。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p>
