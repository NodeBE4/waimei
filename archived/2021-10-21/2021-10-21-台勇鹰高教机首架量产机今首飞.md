[台勇鹰高教机首架量产机今首飞](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20211021-1205610)
------

<div>
<p>由台湾国防承包商汉翔公司于2017年研制的“勇鹰”高级教练机，两架原型机在去年已完成首飞，而首架量产型高教机今早首度曝光，并且升空顺利完成首度试飞。</p><p>据《联合报》报道，这架还没完成完成涂装的勇鹰高级教练机，卸除原型机多处测试天线，今早11时40分升空，在台中周边空域进行试飞，顺利于中午12时40分落地。</p><p>根据台湾空军司令部规划，勇鹰新式高级教训机办理期程为2017至2028年度，计划总经费686亿3901万元（新台币，下同，约33亿新元），截至2021年度已编列预算244亿5131万元。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>空军司令部规划，勇鹰新式高级教训机预计于2021至2026年间分年依序交机两架、八架、17架、18架、18架及三架，明年预计交机八架。</p><p>高级教练机是训练飞行员从最初级的飞行技术，到能够单独飞行与完成指定动作的特殊机种。</p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/zbpe-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=zbpe399&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_desktop-zbpe399_2.png?dHEiGouxxZpWwWTJE04dyGyUN74JOU2z" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_desktop-zbpe399_2.png?dHEiGouxxZpWwWTJE04dyGyUN74JOU2z" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/zbpe-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=zbpe399&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_mobile-zbpe399_2.png?qZnBcIGCJvPR8A2IAZOZuOxdoGZDatiI" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_mobile-zbpe399_2.png?qZnBcIGCJvPR8A2IAZOZuOxdoGZDatiI" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
