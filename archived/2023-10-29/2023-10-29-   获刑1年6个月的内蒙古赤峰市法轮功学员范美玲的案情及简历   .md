<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">29</span>日，本网获悉获刑<span lang="EN-US">1</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月的内蒙古赤峰市法轮功学员范美玲的案情及简历，现通报如下：<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">范美玲：</span></b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">内蒙古赤峰市法轮功学员，女，<span lang="EN-US">1961</span>年生，现年<span lang="EN-US">62</span>岁，是内蒙古赤峰市宁城县忙农镇三家村一位普通农妇，她因时法轮功学员而频遭当地中共人员骚扰迫害，于<span lang="EN-US">2018</span>年被迫离家漂泊，近年在河北廊坊居住。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">2023</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">2</span>日，范美玲在廊坊市安次区租住屋被南门外派出所警察抓捕，关押到廊坊市看守所。随后遭批捕、起诉。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">2023</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">27</span>日其被霸州市法院开庭，<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">17</span>日被判刑<span lang="EN-US">1</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月，并罚金<span lang="EN-US">5000</span>元。范美令上诉至廊坊市中级法院，于<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">24</span>日被廊坊市中级法院驳回上诉，维持原判。刑期至：<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">1</span>日。<span lang="EN-US" style="color: #00b050;"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p>
