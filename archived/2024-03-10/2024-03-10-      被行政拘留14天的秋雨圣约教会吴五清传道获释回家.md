------

<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">10</span>日，本网获悉：<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">25</span>日被成都公安局双流分局行政拘留<span lang="EN-US">14</span>天的吴五清传道被释放，于今天<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">10</span>日中午已经平安回到家中。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">2024</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">25</span>日上午，秋雨圣约教会在成都举行了<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年第一次的全教会线下主日崇拜，并举行了洗礼和圣餐。崇拜结束后，教会吴五清传道被成都东升派出所带走。随后，吴五清传道被成都市双流区公安分局以“涉嫌组织非法集会”为由行政拘留<span lang="EN-US">14</span>天，随即送往双流区拘留所执行。今天吴五清传道获释回家。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"></p><div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;"><a href="https://blogger.googleusercontent.com/img/b/R29vZ2xl/AVvXsEjZpddFIPEULB4_uqUKFBwdOj2CfooNwyiNHkhtdslf2uvrB3fvuQFlMCpgp0zcSzOd_EfMARwjG2KPBb0G-SSOs8262D32CKV3miaxd5dfvHZEluzzWYWzyEhMyCHsHkrmuHXAGkztclYY_7pGbuooCQRUgbZsYfUXc8zBXw9WZPu9Q6k6NL_dFk3Tl7NA/s1280/photo_2024-03-10_14-26-17.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;"><img border="0" data-original-height="589" data-original-width="1280" height="184" src="https://blogger.googleusercontent.com/img/b/R29vZ2xl/AVvXsEjZpddFIPEULB4_uqUKFBwdOj2CfooNwyiNHkhtdslf2uvrB3fvuQFlMCpgp0zcSzOd_EfMARwjG2KPBb0G-SSOs8262D32CKV3miaxd5dfvHZEluzzWYWzyEhMyCHsHkrmuHXAGkztclYY_7pGbuooCQRUgbZsYfUXc8zBXw9WZPu9Q6k6NL_dFk3Tl7NA/w400-h184/photo_2024-03-10_14-26-17.jpg" width="400" /></a></div><p></p>
