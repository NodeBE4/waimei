<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">7</span>日，本网获悉：获刑<span lang="EN-US">1</span>年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>个月的现年<span lang="EN-US">69</span>岁的甘肃白银市法轮功学员李凤兰女士被兰州女子监狱迫害致死。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><a name="_Hlk160719636"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">李凤兰</span></a><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">：甘肃白银市法轮功学员，女，<span lang="EN-US">1955</span>年生，现年<span lang="EN-US">69</span>岁，原是甘肃省白银市平川区财政局职工。一九九七年开始修炼法轮功。一九九九年七月中共恶党疯狂迫害法轮功，其屡遭迫害。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">2022</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">6</span>日，李凤岚被警察叫去白银法院，当晚被关押到白银看守所，随即遭逮捕。后李凤兰女士被判刑<span lang="EN-US">1</span>年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>个月后，于<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">17</span>日被关押到兰州女子监狱。刑期至：<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">5</span>日。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">2023</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月，李凤兰乳腺癌细胞都已经扩散，平川区公安局仍不许她保外就医。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">直到<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月，李凤兰家属请了律师，才从监狱接回她。当时的李风兰已经被监狱迫害得奄奄一息，不吃不喝，被直接住进医院，只能在医院打点营养液维持生命，最后医院也不要了，她丈夫无奈把她接回家。没几天，李风兰于<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">10</span>日早上含冤离世。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p>
