[浙江台州基督徒、网上书店“小麦书房”负责人陈煜案二审维持7年一审原判](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2022/01/7.html)
------

<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; line-height: 115%; mso-ansi-language: EN-US; mso-bidi-font-family: &quot;Times New Roman&quot;;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">8</span>日，本网获悉：浙江台州“小麦书房”陈煜因销售基督教书籍，被控“非法经营罪”，日前其案二审维持一审原判：有期徒刑七年，并处罚金人民币二十万元。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; line-height: 115%; mso-ansi-language: EN-US; mso-bidi-font-family: &quot;Times New Roman&quot;;">陈煜</span></b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; line-height: 115%; mso-ansi-language: EN-US; mso-bidi-font-family: &quot;Times New Roman&quot;;">：网名张小麦，浙江省临海市人，基督徒，网上书店“小麦书房”负责人，自由思想追求者，中国在押良心犯。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; line-height: 115%; mso-ansi-language: EN-US; mso-bidi-font-family: &quot;Times New Roman&quot;;">近年来，由于中共当局加大了对宗教信仰及其信徒的打压，强化了对非官方宗教书籍的严控和取缔，为了解决书源问题，其<span lang="EN-US">"</span>小麦书房<span lang="EN-US">"</span>遂代中国信徒到台湾及海外代购基督教出版物，然后通过网上书店举行销售<span lang="EN-US">;<o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; line-height: 115%; mso-ansi-language: EN-US; mso-bidi-font-family: &quot;Times New Roman&quot;;">因其<span lang="EN-US">"</span>小麦书店<span lang="EN-US">"</span>坚持长期销售台湾、美国宗教书籍，颇受浙江、山东、河南等省基督徒顾客欢迎，曾被上万人次点击购买，随引起浙江当局的强烈不满， 并将其及其书店视为<span lang="EN-US">"</span>反华势力<span lang="EN-US">";<o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; line-height: 115%; mso-ansi-language: EN-US; mso-bidi-font-family: &quot;Times New Roman&quot;;">2019</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; line-height: 115%; mso-ansi-language: EN-US; mso-bidi-font-family: &quot;Times New Roman&quot;;">年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月，其被浙江省临海市警方以涉嫌<span lang="EN-US">"</span>非法经营罪<span lang="EN-US">"</span>抓捕刑拘<span lang="EN-US">;</span>后被以同罪由转正式逮捕，指控其在未经境内合法书号和引进许可证的情况下，通过电商网站向一万多人售出<span lang="EN-US">2</span>万多本书，涉及金额<span lang="EN-US">282</span>万，其中，<span lang="EN-US">771</span>种属境外<span lang="EN-US">"</span>非法出版物<span lang="EN-US">"</span>，所以违反了刑法<span lang="EN-US">; <o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; line-height: 115%; mso-ansi-language: EN-US; mso-bidi-font-family: &quot;Times New Roman&quot;;">2020</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; line-height: 115%; mso-ansi-language: EN-US; mso-bidi-font-family: &quot;Times New Roman&quot;;">年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">27</span>日，被浙江省临海市法院一审以<span lang="EN-US">"</span>非法经营罪<span lang="EN-US">"</span>判处有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">7</span>年，罚金<span lang="EN-US">20 </span>万，刑期至<span lang="EN-US">2026</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">12</span>日<span lang="EN-US">;</span>现二审维持原判。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体;
