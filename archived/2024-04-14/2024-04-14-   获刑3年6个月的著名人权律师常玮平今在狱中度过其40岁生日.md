[获刑3年6个月的著名人权律师常玮平今在狱中度过其40岁生日](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2024/04/3640.html)
------

<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">14</span>日，本网获悉：今天是著名人权律师常玮平先生<span lang="EN-US">40</span>岁生日，这是他在狱中度过的第四个生日。<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">8</span>日常玮平律师被以颠覆国家政权罪判处有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">3</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月，现在陕西省渭南监狱（陕西省第二监狱）服刑。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><b><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;">常玮平简历：</span></b><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt;">1984</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;">年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">14</span>日出生，陕西省宝鸡市凤翔县城关镇周家门前村人，原陕西立刚律师事务所执业律师，人权律师，人权捍卫者，中国在押良心犯。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;">早年曾就读于重庆大学；毕业后，<span lang="EN-US">2009</span>年<span lang="EN-US">—2013</span>年，通过一系列的律师资格自修和考试，最终获取律师执业资格证，后在陕西立刚律师事务所成为正式律师；工作期间，不仅曾代理多起公益诉讼（含艾滋歧视、乙肝歧视、性别歧视等）案件，亦曾代理过多起诸如<span lang="EN-US">“</span>无锡<span lang="EN-US">4·13</span>寻衅滋事案<span lang="EN-US">”</span>、<span lang="EN-US">“</span>无锡访民王金娣寻衅滋事案<span lang="EN-US">”</span>、<span lang="EN-US">“</span>南京王健寻衅滋事案<span lang="EN-US">”</span>等敏感人权及弱势群体维权案件；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt;">2018</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;">年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">14</span>日，曾因此而被陕西宝鸡司法局停业<span lang="EN-US"> 3 </span>个月；同年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">22 </span>日，其律师资格证被当地律师事务所注销；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt;">2019</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;">年，在停业期满后，其基本处于无法在其他律师事务所获得工作机会的状态，因其曾在陕西省境内与至少<span lang="EN-US">10</span>家律师事务所接洽，但皆不顺利，或即将签约时临时变卦，或虽签约后又突然解除合约；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt;">2019</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;">年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月，曾因参加了一个与多名维权律师和异见人士在厦门的非正式私人聚会，后因被中共官方知晓后定性为<span lang="EN-US">“</span>厦门聚会案<span lang="EN-US">”</span>，多人遭严打狠抓，<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">12</span>日，其亦被陕西省宝鸡市警方跨省抓走，并被以<span lang="EN-US">“</span>危害国家安全<span lang="EN-US">”</span>为由指定居所监视居住；后被取保候审，<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt;">2020</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;">年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月，又因对外发视频称其在拘押期间，遭受酷刑，即每天（<span lang="EN-US">24</span>小时<span lang="EN-US">/</span>次）都把他绑在一种叫做<span lang="EN-US">“</span>老虎凳<span lang="EN-US">”</span>的刑具上，连续审问<span lang="EN-US">16</span>次，致其右手拇指到食指至今都毫无知觉，且在取保获释后仍受到严密监视等，<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">22</span>日，随再次被陕西省宝鸡市警方抓走，后被以涉嫌<span lang="EN-US">“</span>煽动颠覆国家政权罪<span lang="EN-US">”</span>指定居所监视居住；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt;">2021</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;">年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">7</span>日，被宝鸡市检察院以涉嫌<span lang="EN-US">“</span>颠覆国家政权罪<span lang="EN-US">”</span>予以批捕；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt;">2022</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;">年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">26</span>日，其案在陕西省宝鸡市凤县法院秘密开庭，其妻子前往要求参加旁听，遭警方恶意围堵；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt;">2023</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;">年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">8</span>日，终被宝鸡市凤县法院一审以<span lang="EN-US">“</span>颠覆国家政权罪<span lang="EN-US">”</span>判处有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">3</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月，刑期至<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月。此前被羁押于宝鸡市凤县看守所。<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span
