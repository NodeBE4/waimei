<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">8</span>日，本网获悉获刑<span lang="EN-US">1</span>年的山东省临沂市莒南县法轮功学员、<span lang="EN-US">70</span>岁的退休女教师毛德君女士的案情及简历，现在通报如下：<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">毛德君：</span></b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">山东省临沂市莒南县法轮功学员，女，<span lang="EN-US">1954</span>年生，现年<span lang="EN-US">70</span>岁。东北某中学的退休物理教师。她于一九九八年十月开始修炼法轮功。<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月曾遭抓捕判刑，被判刑<span lang="EN-US">1</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">2023</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">27</span>日毛德君遭当地警方抓捕，非法审讯后被 “取保候审”手续后，放她回家。后遭起诉、开庭。<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">23</span>日，临沭县法院通知毛德君去拿判决书。当天，毛德君在家属的陪伴下到临沭县法院，毛德君被判刑一年。随即毛德君被收监，现被关押在临沂看守所。刑期至<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">22</span>日。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p>
