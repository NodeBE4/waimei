font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">后因强行教育培训过程中不与官方妥协、合作，遂于<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月之后，被察布查尔县警方刑拘和逮捕，指控其“念经（古兰经）即违法”；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体;
