mso-char-indent-count: 2.0; mso-line-height-rule: exactly; text-indent: 32pt;"><span face="仿宋">辩护人李爱军，山东言洽诚律师事务所律师，住所地青岛市黄岛区阿里山路<span lang="EN-US">101</span>号东方花园一号别墅，电话<span lang="EN-US">13969836367</span>。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal" style="line-height: 27pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0; mso-line-height-rule: exactly; text-indent: 32pt;"><span face="仿宋">被控告人沈阳市公安局、沈阳市公安局于洪分局<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal" style="line-height: 27pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0; mso-line-height-rule: exactly; text-indent: 32pt;"><span face="仿宋">被控告人沈阳市公安局原局长杨建军、沈阳市公安局原局长许文有、现任沈阳市公安局二级巡视员邓万宏、沈阳市公安局刑侦局副局长姜贵国、沈阳市公安局经济开发区分局局长单强、沈阳市公安局李博等人。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p align="center" class="MsoNormal" style="line-height: 27pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0; mso-line-height-rule: exactly; text-align: center; text-indent: 32.15pt;"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span face="仿宋">反映控告事项<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></b></p><p class="MsoNormal" style="line-height: 27pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0; mso-line-height-rule: exactly; text-indent: 32pt;"><span face="仿宋">请纠正沈阳市公安局的严重违法行为，停止对郭泓打击迫害，立即释放郭泓，或者指定沈阳市以外的公安机关管辖郭泓案。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p align="center" class="MsoNormal" style="line-height: 27pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0; mso-line-height-rule: exactly; text-align: center; text-indent: 32.15pt;"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span face="仿宋">事实与理由<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></b></p><p class="MsoNormal" style="line-height: 27pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0; mso-line-height-rule: exactly; text-indent: 32.15pt;"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span face="仿宋">一、案情简介——局长涉嫌贪腐亿元逍遥法外举报人却被沈阳公安三易罪名逮捕。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></b></p><p class="MsoNormal" style="line-height: 27pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0; mso-line-height-rule: exactly; text-indent: 32pt;"><span face="仿宋" lang="EN-US">2014</span><span face="仿宋">年沈阳市委贯彻中央“八项规定”和“反四风”精神到沈阳市信访局考核，郭泓实名反映时任局长陈国强涉嫌贪腐亿元信访资金并向公安利益输送，该反映问题后被沈阳市委巡察组查证属实（附该巡察报告）。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal" style="line-height: 27pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0; mso-line-height-rule: exactly; text-indent: 32pt;"><span face="仿宋">郭泓反映的问题揭开了信访资金贪腐的黑盖子，触怒了利益集团，结果是“局长涉嫌贪腐亿元逍遥法外举报人却被沈阳公安三易罪名逮捕”。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal" style="line-height: 27pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0; mso-line-height-rule: exactly; text-indent: 32pt;"><span face="仿宋" lang="EN-US">2015</span><span face="仿宋">年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">17</span>日，沈阳公安以非法获取公民个人信息罪刑拘郭泓，后撤案，郭泓称刑拘期间遭受残酷的刑讯逼供；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal" style="line-height: 27pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0; mso-line-height-rule: exactly; text-indent: 32pt;"><span face="仿宋" lang="EN-US">2022</span><span face="仿宋">年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">24</span>日沈阳公安以故意毁坏财物罪刑拘郭泓，刑拘<span lang="EN-US">5</span>个月后——刑事诉讼法规定的刑拘不能超过<span lang="EN-US">37</span>天在辽宁沈阳不管用——发现仍然不够批捕条件，遂于<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">24</span>日换一个万能口袋罪——寻衅滋事罪提请逮捕郭泓，在沈阳市公安局居高临下的压迫下（区检察院办案人明确称郭泓专案组是市局的他们管不了），沈阳市于洪区检察院于<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">30</span>日批捕郭泓。沈阳公安涉嫌打击报复举报人欲加之罪何患无辞？<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal" style="line-height: 27pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0; mso-line-height-rule: exactly; text-indent: 32pt;"><span face="仿宋">沈阳公安把信访局干部办成信访寻衅滋事罪，既是对我国信访制度的否定，更是法治的耻辱。反映人及辩护人六次向辽宁省公安厅反映无果，现向公安部反映控告，请纠正沈阳市公安局的违法，立即释放郭泓，或者指定沈阳市以外的公安机关管辖。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal" style="line-height: 27pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0; mso-line-height-rule: exactly; text-indent: 32.15pt;"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span face="仿宋">二、沈阳公安用刑事手段打击报复举报人郭泓，先后用三个罪名，违法立案，违法管辖。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></b></p><p class="MsoNormal" style="line-height: 27pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0; mso-line-height-rule: exactly; text-indent: 32.15pt;"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span face="仿宋">（一）<span lang="EN-US">2015</span>年非法获取公民个人信息罪<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></b></p><p class="MsoNormal" style="line-height: 27pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0; mso-line-height-rule: exactly; text-indent: 32pt;"><span face="仿宋" lang="EN-US">2015</span><span face="仿宋">年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">17</span>日，沈阳市公安局以下级沈河分局的名义抓捕郭泓，涉嫌罪名是侵犯个人信息罪，一个月后案子办不下去就把郭泓放了，后来撤销案件。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal" style="line-height: 27pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0; mso-line-height-rule: exactly; text-indent: 32pt;"><span face="仿宋">对于撤案的理由，沈阳市公安局的说法是“过追诉失效”，意思是“郭泓干了违法犯罪的事，但是过了追诉期限”，所以我们没有抓错你。但法律监督机关检察院意见是“事实不清证据不足”，意思是“公安局没有证据证明郭泓干了违法犯罪的事，抓错了郭泓”。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal" style="line-height: 27pt; mso-char-indent-count: 1.5; mso-line-height-rule: exactly; text-indent: 24pt;"><span face="仿宋">该案名义上是沈河分局办案，<b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;">但辽宁省信访局给公安部的报告中写明了“市局刑警支队组织侦办”（附该报告）。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></b></span></p><p class="MsoNormal" style="line-height: 27pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0; mso-line-height-rule: exactly; text-indent: 32pt;"><span face="仿宋">郭泓称刑拘期间两套手铐、脚镣关押在黑监狱里一个月，<span style="mso-bidi-font-weight: bold;">酷刑导致左耳骨膜打穿孔双耳骨膜塌陷，听力损失，持续耳鸣耳痛、头鸣头痛；头部<span lang="EN-US">8</span>厘米伤口；手神经断裂；颈椎、腰椎纤维环撕裂和断裂等伤残，全身关节受伤，牙齿活动出血，颈椎血管损伤等造成严重伤残。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal" style="line-height: 27pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0; mso-line-height-rule: exactly; text-indent: 32.15pt;"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span face="仿宋">（二）<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年故意毁坏财物罪<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></b></p><p class="MsoNormal" style="line-height: 27pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0; mso-line-height-rule: exactly; text-indent: 32pt;"><span face="仿宋" lang="EN-US">2022</span><span face="仿宋">年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">14</span>日，郭泓驾车前往北京，行至辽宁省盘锦市高速公路服务区，被沈阳市公安局、沈阳市信访局安排的<span lang="EN-US">30</span>多人<span lang="EN-US">10</span>多辆车围堵，非法拘禁剥夺人身自由长达九天九夜。期间郭泓报警<span lang="EN-US">40</span>多次，盘锦公安受理报警后，以“无权管辖”为由，未对现场违法犯罪行为进行处置。<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">23</span>日，郭泓几近崩溃，迫不得已顶开围堵的车辆逃离现场。<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">24</span>日被沈阳市公安局于洪分局以故意毁坏财物罪刑拘。<b><span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></b></span></p><p class="MsoNormal" style="line-height: 27pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0; mso-line-height-rule: exactly; text-indent: 32pt;"><span face="仿宋" style="mso-bidi-font-weight: bold;">该案案发地为辽宁盘锦市，依法由盘锦公安优先管辖，沈阳市公安局和辖区分局均不适宜管辖。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal" style="line-height: 27pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0; mso-line-height-rule: exactly; text-indent: 32pt;"><span face="仿宋">管辖是整个刑事诉讼的开端和入口，是保障司法公正的第一道程序关口，关系到嫌疑人的自由和生命。如果管辖错误，不但侵犯人权，更会因为人情案而严重破坏法治，污染依法治国的水源。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal" style="line-height: 27pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0; mso-line-height-rule: exactly; text-indent: 32pt;"><span face="仿宋" lang="EN-US">1.</span><span face="仿宋">该案依法应当由盘锦公安优先管辖。我国刑事诉讼管辖的基本原则是<span lang="EN-US">“</span>以犯罪地管辖为主<span lang="EN-US">,</span>被告人居住地管辖为辅<span lang="EN-US">”</span>。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal" style="line-height: 27pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0; mso-line-height-rule: exactly; text-indent: 32pt;"><span face="仿宋">盘锦为“犯罪地”，优先管辖。本案案发地为盘锦高速公路服务区，依据《公安机关办理刑事案件程序规定》第十五条规定<b>“刑事案件由犯罪地的公安机关管辖。”</b>所以，盘锦公安对本案有优先管辖权，且郭泓在被非法拘禁的九天九夜期间，曾多次向盘锦公安报警，盘锦公安亦有出警，所以本案应当有盘锦管辖优先管辖。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal" style="line-height: 27pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0; mso-line-height-rule: exactly; text-indent: 32pt;"><span face="仿宋">嫌疑人郭泓的居住地为沈阳市于洪区，根据《公安机关办理刑事案件程序规定》第十五条规定“如果由犯罪嫌疑人居住地的公安机关管辖更为适宜的，可以由犯罪嫌疑人居住地的公安机关管辖。”但本案恰恰存在居住地沈阳市于洪区公安不适宜管辖的情形，所以本案应当由盘锦公安管辖。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal" style="line-height: 27pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0; mso-line-height-rule: exactly; text-indent: 32pt;"><span face="仿宋" lang="EN-US">2.</span><span face="仿宋">沈阳市公安局于洪分局及沈阳市辖区内其他公安分局均不适宜管辖。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal" style="line-height: 27pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0; mso-line-height-rule: exactly; text-indent: 32pt;"><span face="仿宋">沈阳市公安局主要领导是郭泓长达八年控告违法犯罪的对象。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal" style="line-height: 27pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0; mso-line-height-rule: exactly; text-indent: 32pt;"><span face="仿宋">沈阳市公安局原局长杨建军、沈阳市公安局原局长许文有、现任沈阳市公安局二级巡视员邓万宏、沈阳市公安局刑侦局副局长姜贵国、沈阳市公安局经济开发区分局局长单强、沈阳市公安局李博等人一直是郭泓控告的对象。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal" style="line-height: 27pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0; mso-line-height-rule: exactly; text-indent: 32pt;"><span face="仿宋" lang="EN-US">3.</span><span face="仿宋">“被故意毁坏财”单位沈阳市信访局局长宁勇曾任沈阳市公安局指挥中心主任，与沈阳市公安局有利害关系。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal" style="line-height: 27pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0; mso-line-height-rule: exactly; text-indent: 32pt;"><span face="仿宋">综上，根据《公安机关办理刑事案件程序规定》第三十二条规定，沈阳市辖区内公安机关负责人和侦查人员“或者是被控告的当事人或者是与本案当事人有其他关系，可能影响公正处理案件的”，应当整体回避，即不适宜管辖。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal" style="line-height: 20pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0; mso-line-height-rule: exactly; text-indent: 24pt;"><span face="宋体" style="mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">《公安机关办理刑事案件程序规定》<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal" style="line-height: 20pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0; mso-line-height-rule: exactly; text-indent: 24pt;"><span face="宋体" style="mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">第十五条刑事案件由犯罪地的公安机关管辖。如果由犯罪嫌疑人居住地的公安机关管辖更为适宜的，可以由犯罪嫌疑人居住地的公安机关管辖。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal" style="line-height: 20pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0; mso-line-height-rule: exactly; text-indent: 24pt;"><span face="宋体" style="mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">第三十二条公安机关负责人、侦查人员有下列情形之一的，应当自行提出回避申请，没有自行提出回避申请的，应当责令其回避，当事人及其法定代理人也有权要求他们回避：<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal" style="line-height: 20pt; mso-line-height-rule: exactly;"><span face="宋体" style="mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（一）是本案的当事人或者是当事人的近亲属的；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal" style="line-height: 20pt; mso-line-height-rule: exactly;"><span face="宋体" style="mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（二）本人或者他的近亲属和本案有利害关系的；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal" style="line-height: 20pt; mso-line-height-rule: exactly;"><span face="宋体" style="mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（三）担任过本案的证人、鉴定人、辩护人、诉讼代理人的；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal" style="line-height: 20pt;
