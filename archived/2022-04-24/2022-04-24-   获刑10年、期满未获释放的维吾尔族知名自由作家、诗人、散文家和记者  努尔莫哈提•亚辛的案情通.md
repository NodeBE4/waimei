<p><span></span></p><a name='more'></a><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;"><br /></span><p></p><p><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">（维权网信息中心报道）</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">24</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">日，本网获悉获刑</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">10</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">年期满未获释放的维吾尔族知名自由作家、诗人、散文家和记者努尔莫哈提•亚辛的案情，现通报如下：</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">努尔莫哈提•亚辛：</span></b><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">1974</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">6</span>日出生，新疆维吾尔自治区喀什地区巴楚县人，又名努尔莫哈迈提•雅辛、努力默赫默德•亚森，维吾尔族名<span lang="EN-US">Nurmuhemmet Yasin</span>，笔名欧尔开西，维吾尔族知名自由作家、诗人、散文家和记者，中国在押政治犯。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2004</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年，曾因在《喀什噶尔文学》杂志上发表一篇寓言体小说《野鸽子》（<span lang="EN-US">Yawa Kepter</span>），随被中共新疆当局认为其内容批评了新疆地方政府的管理现状，<span lang="EN-US">2004</span>年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">29</span>日，被喀什地区巴楚县警方以涉嫌“煽动分裂主义罪”抓捕、刑拘；后被喀什地区检察院以同罪名转正式批捕，指控其因小说内容有主张维吾尔族独立的思想意味，故而其言行属于违法；</span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;"><span lang="EN-US">2005</span>年<span lang="EN-US">2 </span>月<span lang="EN-US">2</span>日，被新疆喀什地区中级法院以“煽动分裂国家罪”、“煽动民族仇恨、民族歧视罪” 秘密判处有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">10</span>年；其不服上诉；喀什地区中级法院原打算对其减刑<span lang="EN-US">3</span>年，但因遭时任中共喀什地区党委书记的强烈反对，随二审依然维持原判；</span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">据悉，其聘请代理律师权被剥夺，狱中惨遭殴打；</span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;"><span lang="EN-US">2013</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月，曾有消息误传其死于狱中，但其一亲属则表示，<span lang="EN-US">2012</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月家人曾去探监，见其身体状况似乎良好，且同年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月，又收到一封来自狱中的家信，认为此信应是他本人所写；</span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">原本应于<span lang="EN-US">2014</span>年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">28</span>日刑满释放，但截至<span lang="EN-US">2017</span>年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">28</span>日，其仍在狱中服刑，仅因狱中很难被允许探视且新疆对外信息难以联络，故其因何一直不被释放以及后续情况缺详。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体;
