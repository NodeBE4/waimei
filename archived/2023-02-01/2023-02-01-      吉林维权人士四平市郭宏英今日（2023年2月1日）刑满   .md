[吉林维权人士四平市郭宏英今日（2023年2月1日）刑满](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2023/02/202321.html)
------

<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">1</span>日，本网获悉：吉林维权人士四平市郭宏英今日刑满，其兄郭洪伟因维权于<span lang="EN-US">2016</span>年被判刑<span lang="EN-US">13</span>年，于<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">9</span>日在狱中被迫害致死。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">郭宏英：女，<span lang="EN-US">1966</span>年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">30</span>日出生，吉林省四平市人，四平市铁东区海银花园小区居民，下岗工人，维权人士。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2006</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月，曾因其兄郭洪伟举报吉林省龙潭区检察院控申科长和其亲友虚开公款票据，遭打击报复被捕入狱冤判<span lang="EN-US">5</span>年；其兄不服，出狱后随多次携母一同进京上访投诉，再遭地方当局打压，<span lang="EN-US">2015</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月母子二人均被刑拘、逮捕，<span lang="EN-US">2016</span>年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">1</span>日，其兄（郭洪伟）和其母亲（肖蕰苓）分别被吉林省四平市铁东区法院枉法重判<span lang="EN-US">13</span>年和<span lang="EN-US">6</span>年，且服刑其间，母子二人因多次出现病危，请求保外就医遭拒；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">为了给亲人讨回公道，其勇敢维权，并因长期驻京投诉和积极参与各种维权活动，遂又遭地方当局严厉打压；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2018</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">24</span>日，曾因到长春市向吉林省“两会”代表递送伸冤材料，而被维稳人员拦截绑架；同年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">6</span>日，又因去探监看望其兄，而被四平市警方以“越级上访，殴打他人”为由处行政拘留<span lang="EN-US">15</span>天；同年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">24</span>日，又被警方以涉嫌“妨害公务罪”刑拘；一个月后，被四平市检察院起诉，且新增“寻衅滋事罪”；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2019</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">18</span>日，其案在吉林省四平市铁东区法院开庭受理，两天后被以“妨碍公务罪”（判<span lang="EN-US">1</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月）、“寻衅滋事罪”（判<span lang="EN-US">4</span>年），二罪并罚，最终执行有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">5</span>年；其不服上诉；<span lang="EN-US">2019</span>年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">21</span>日，经吉林省四平市中院二审裁决，依然维持原判，刑期至<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">1</span>日。曾在吉林省长春女子监狱服刑。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"></p><div class="separator" style="clear: both;
