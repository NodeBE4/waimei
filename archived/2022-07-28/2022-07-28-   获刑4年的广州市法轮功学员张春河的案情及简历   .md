[获刑4年的广州市法轮功学员张春河的案情及简历](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2022/07/4.html)
------

<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">28</span>日，本网获悉获刑<span lang="EN-US">4</span>年的广州市法轮功学员张春河的案情及简历，现通报如下：<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">张春河：</span></b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">女，<span lang="EN-US">1959</span>年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月出生，广东省广州市人，广州市增城区荔城街公民，中级会计师，原广州市增城物协公司财务科长兼会计，原增城胶袋厂任聘员工，自由信仰追求者，法轮功信仰者，中国在押良心犯。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">早在<span lang="EN-US">1994</span>年，即开始锻炼和信仰法轮功，并因个性大为改观，待人接物为人谦和，处处为他人着想，而在亲朋好友及单位里的同事中间称为公认的好人；但自<span lang="EN-US">1999</span>年中共开始迫害法轮功之后，其因一直坚持自由信念而成为广州市增城区“<span lang="EN-US">610</span>”办公室的重点监控、打压的对象；曾被当局指派人员多次骚扰，并被<span lang="EN-US">5</span>次非法绑架、拘押；曾被强行关进戒毒所接受洗脑教育长达半年；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2002</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">20</span>日，曾被广州市增城区警方抓走并被迅速以邪教罪由处劳动教养<span lang="EN-US">1</span>年，后被送到广州市槎头劳教所关押，同期，其被单位降两级工资；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2008</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">23</span>日，曾因北京奥运会即将召开，其在上班途中被增城区“<span lang="EN-US">610</span>”办公室指使的增城警方和西园派出所便衣警察共同拦截，先后被关押在广州法制学校和增城区看守所，直至次年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">15</span>日，被增城区法院冤判<span lang="EN-US">3</span>年，后在广东省女子监狱服刑；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">狱中，曾屡遭酷刑虐待，不仅被上手铐脚镣、被剥夺正常睡眠<span lang="EN-US">150</span>天，还被关小黑屋遭惩罚迫害若干天，导致其不能吃喝、大小便，造成肾水肿，出现生命垂危，最终狱方不得不将其送进广州武警医院救治；后其丈夫实在不堪压力重负而与其离婚，从此其被迫流离失所；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2021</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">10</span>日，因其坚决抵制中共在“清零”行动中的强制洗脑转化，故而再遭广州市增城区警方和西园派出所警方联合抓捕，当日即被以涉嫌“邪教罪”刑拘，其家遭查抄；同年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">17</span>日，被增城区检察院以涉嫌同罪名正式批捕，并被关押在增城区看守所；同年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">1</span>日，其案又被转到海珠区检察院，并被海珠区检察院以同罪由提起公诉；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2022</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">13</span>日，其案在广州市海珠区法院庭审，终被一审以“利用邪教组织破坏法律实施罪”判处有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">4</span>年，并处罚金<span lang="EN-US">1</span>万元；至今，尚不知其是否已提起上诉；刑期至<span lang="EN-US">2025</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">9</span>日。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">目前被羁押于广州市增城区看守所。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"></p><div class="separator" style="clear: both;
