[被以涉嫌寻衅滋事罪刑事拘留的河北维权人士张爱民有严重疾病   但取保候审仍遭拒](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2023/08/blog-post_97.html)

<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">19</span>日，本网获悉：河北维权人士张爱民于<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">15</span>日被唐山市公安局古冶区分局以涉嫌寻衅滋事罪刑事拘留，被羁押在唐山第一看守所。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2023</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">10</span>日上午，律师到唐山市第一看守所，第二次会见了唐山维权人士张爱民。会见时，张爱民向律师表示其已于<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">8</span>日收到了检察院的起诉书。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">张爱民患有严重疾病，如高血压<span lang="EN-US">3</span>级、急性冠脉综合症，随时有生命之虞，唐山市第一看守所也曾一度拒收，后在京华派出所的强力干预下才勉强收押。<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">21</span>日，张爱民被批准逮捕。仅仅三天之后，京华派出所就于<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">24</span>日将案件快速移送至唐山市古冶区检察院审查起诉。目前，张爱民的案件处于一审待审阶段。<span lang="EN-US"> <o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">代理律师与家属都提出递交了取保候审手续，随后法院不批准变更强制措施告知书，拒绝张爱民取保候审手续。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2023</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">1</span>日河北唐山出租车司机张爱民，在正常进京时，被以曾有上访经历为由拦截。拦截他的京华办事处工作人员在得知张爱民出租车出现故障后，深夜将张爱民弃之不顾，张爱民不得不在临时处置故障后，在高速口接到深夜睡在路边身患疾病妻子。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">张爱民于<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">15</span>日上午到国家信访局反应京华办事处工作人员有失人道救援的行为，明知张爱民车出问题不能前去接患病的妻子看病，深夜把张爱民丢在高速公路上扬长而去。在国家信访局反应这一事件时张爱民却被拦截在国家信访局入口处，随后被移交给唐山市古冶区京华派出所。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">随后将张爱民遣返回当地，随即被以涉嫌寻衅滋事罪刑事拘留，被羁押在唐山第一看守所。近些年，越来越多的合法上访公民竟然被所在地当局以所谓寻衅滋事罪大口袋罪刑事拘留构陷迫害，这已是大陆的普遍现象。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体;
