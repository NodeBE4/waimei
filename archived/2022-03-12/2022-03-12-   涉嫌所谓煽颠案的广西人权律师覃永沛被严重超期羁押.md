[涉嫌所谓煽颠案的广西人权律师覃永沛被严重超期羁押](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2022/03/blog-post_83.html)
------

<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">12</span>日，本网获悉：涉嫌所谓煽颠案的广西人权律师覃永沛被严重超期羁押。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">覃永沛律师妻子邓晓云通报：“覃永沛煽动颠覆国家政权案<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">4</span>日起诉，法院受理案件至至今<span lang="EN-US">21</span>个月多了，别给我说没有超期羁押！看看刑诉法吧，<span lang="EN-US">208</span>条规定了一审不得超过三个月。有本法<span lang="EN-US">158</span>条的情形之一的，经上一级人民法院批准，可以延长三个月。合计<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月。再要延长就得报请最高人民法院批准了。”</span><span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">广西人权律师覃永沛是<span lang="EN-US">2019</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">31</span>日被抓捕的。当时，南宁警方十数人闯入原百举鸣律师事务所（现百永鸣法律咨询服务有限公司）办公室，将覃永沛控制并带走，同时覃的办公室被警方查封。随即被刑事拘留。后覃永沛被指涉煽动颠覆国家政权罪于<span lang="EN-US">2019</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月遭正式逮捕。<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">2</span>日，覃永沛涉嫌煽动颠覆国家政权案已由南宁市公安局侦查结束，已经移交南宁市检察院审查起诉。后不审不判。多年来，覃永沛一直以敢言著称，曾公开悬赏征集广西司法厅及公安厅厅长的犯罪证据，加上曾公开控告司法部长傅政华，因此遭到政治报复。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">目前覃永沛律师被羁押在广西自治区第二看守所。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体;
