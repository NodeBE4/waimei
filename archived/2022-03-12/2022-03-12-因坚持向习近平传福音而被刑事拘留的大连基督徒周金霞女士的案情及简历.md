[因坚持向习近平传福音而被刑事拘留的大连基督徒周金霞女士的案情及简历](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2022/03/blog-post_80.html)

<div>
<p><span></span></p><a name='more'></a><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;"><br /></span><p></p><p><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;">（维权网信息中心报道）</span><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt;">2022</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;">年</span><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt;">3</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;">月</span><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt;">12</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;">日，本网获悉因坚持向习近平传福音而被刑事拘留的大连基督徒周金霞女士的案情及简历，现在通报如下：</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">周金霞：</span></b><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">女，<span lang="EN-US">1970</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">3</span>日出生，辽宁省大连市人，大连市西岗区民权街<span lang="EN-US">41</span>号<span lang="EN-US">1-10</span>公民，强拆受害者，维权访民，中国首例公开持续向中共当局最高领导人传福音的基督徒，中国最执着的宣教勇士，中国在押良心犯。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">早在<span lang="EN-US">2009</span>年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">3</span>日，曾因自家房屋遭大连市西岗区政府暴力强拆（注：其拥有完整的合法产权），使其所有家具、财物尽被埋于废墟之中，随其被迫投诉但无果；后因其房屋在强拆之后被很快改造成广场，随开始逐级上访、讨公道；期间，因不断上访，其及胞弟被拘留且失去工作，其本人因多次进京信访而屡招打击和报复；后因其接受基督教福音而成为基督徒，认为接受并广传福音远胜过仅为一己之利、疲于奔命而维权，随更多心思传福音；然又因其立定心志，执着向中共当权者传基督教福音，故而招致更多打压；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2014</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年，曾因与北京爱契教会多名信众在大连传福音，并到自家房屋改造为广场的原址上举行敬拜聚会，而遭西岗区警方强烈干预和传唤；同年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月，又因连续多次到大连市政府和市委正门口举牌，向大连市市长李万才和市委书记唐军等传福音，而遭大连当局多次夺牌和驱离；<span lang="EN-US">2015</span>年，曾因持续到北京上访并举牌在中南海门前给当权者传福音而被警方野蛮绑架，后被大连市警方处行政拘留<span lang="EN-US">10</span>天；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2016</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">5</span>日，曾因在中南海门前手撑写着：“神爱世人，呼召习近平、彭丽媛。‘自由、平等、公正、诚信’是来自神的……天国近了，你们应当悔改！”字样的横幅，而被北京警方当即抓捕，后被大连市警方处行政拘留<span lang="EN-US">10</span>天；同年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">5</span>日，因第<span lang="EN-US">45</span>次到中南海上访并给当权者传福音，而再被大连市西岗区警方处行政拘留<span lang="EN-US">10</span>天；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2017</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">5</span>日，曾因到北京中南海周边“非正常”上访地区上访和举横幅传福音，而被北京市丰台区警方旋即绑架，关进丰台区久敬庄黑监狱，后被大连市西岗区警方以“扰乱公共场所秩序”为由行政拘留<span lang="EN-US">10</span>天；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">同年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">19</span>日，中共召开<span lang="EN-US">19</span>大，故其又被大连市黄河路派出所警方带走，并被以涉嫌“寻衅滋事罪”刑拘；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2018</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">15</span>日，中共“两会”前夕，因其又到中南海新华门前举横幅向习近平等人传福音，次日随再次被大连警方以涉嫌“寻衅滋事罪”刑拘；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2020</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月，其开始定居于北京市丰台区小白楼村，并致力于传播福音和参加教会聚会等活动，但至<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">27</span>日，因丰台区宗教管理部门开展基督教私设聚会点排查行动，而又遭小白楼村干部及派出所警方强行驱赶及抄家；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2022</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">20</span>日，因在北京市长安街新华门拉横幅，累计第<span lang="EN-US">50</span>多次向习近平传福音，而被值班警察再次抓捕和被遣返原籍，次日，被大连市西岗区警方以涉嫌“寻衅滋事罪” 刑事拘留；至今未予释放。目前被羁押于大连市看守所。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"></p><div class="separator" style="clear: both;
