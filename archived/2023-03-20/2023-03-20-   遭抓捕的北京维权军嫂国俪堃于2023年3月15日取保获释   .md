<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">20</span>日，本网获悉：遭抓捕的北京维权军嫂国俪堃于<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">15</span>日获释。北京基督徒徐永海长老通报说：“<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">19</span>日，我去看望了刚刚出狱几天的国俪堃大夫。<span lang="EN-US">73</span>岁的国俪堃大姐<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">16</span>日被抓，<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">15</span>日出狱，一直被关在看守所。目前是以取保的方式被释放，法院一直没有下判决。在看守所里，国俪堃老姊妹经历了很多苦难，求主安慰怜悯她，使她尽快恢复健康。望肢体们为国俪堃姊妹代祷。”<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">：女，<span lang="EN-US">1950</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">27</span>日出生，北京市人，北京市（海军）丰台区东大街<span lang="EN-US">5</span>号院居民，蒙古族退休医生，维权海军军嫂。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2014</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">15</span>日，曾因其到军委巡视组所在的北京京海大厦上访，而再遭暴打，导致其颈部、腰部及大腿受伤；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体;
