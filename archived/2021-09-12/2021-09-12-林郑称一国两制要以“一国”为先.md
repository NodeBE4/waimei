[林郑称一国两制要以“一国”为先](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210912-1192767)
------

<div>
<p>香港特首林郑月娥说，香港的一国两制，要以“一国”为本、“一国”为先。</p><p>据香港01报道，林郑将于10月公布这届任期最后一份《施政报告》，今早（12日）在西九戏曲中心举行公众咨询，听取市民意见。</p><p>有市民称，香港以往集中“两制”，忧虑日后会否过于偏向“一国”。对此，林郑回答说，香港有很多优势，最大的正是一国两制，需要重视。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>林郑说，香港回归多年没有做好教育，今后要回到“一国”为本、“一国”为先。</p><p>她也说，以“一国”为本的同时，也不应忘记香港的制度，例如有独立司法权、金融中心、货币等。</p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/zbpe-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=zbpe399&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_desktop-zbpe399_2.png?dHEiGouxxZpWwWTJE04dyGyUN74JOU2z" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_desktop-zbpe399_2.png?dHEiGouxxZpWwWTJE04dyGyUN74JOU2z" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/zbpe-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=zbpe399&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_mobile-zbpe399_2.png?qZnBcIGCJvPR8A2IAZOZuOxdoGZDatiI" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_mobile-zbpe399_2.png?qZnBcIGCJvPR8A2IAZOZuOxdoGZDatiI" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
