[福建仙游县枫亭镇全镇列为高风险地区](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210912-1192751)
------

<div>
<p>中国福建莆田的仙游县已升级疫情防控措施，该县枫亭镇全部列为疫情高风险地区。</p><p>新华社今早在官方微博透露上述消息。据报道，截至昨天下午4时，仙游县累计报告冠病核酸阳性24例，其中确诊病例六例、无症状感染者18例。</p><p>另一方面，莆田市人民政府新闻办公室昨天（11日）召开新闻发布会，通报疫情防控最新情况。通报称，自从前天（10日）发现本土病例后，截至昨天下午4时，当地组织对仙游县枫亭镇进行扩大核酸检测，累计采集8万1938人份样本，已出结果1万102份，正检及结果待报7万1836份。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>中国央视报道，福州、厦门、泉州已调派456名采样和核酸检测人员紧急驰援莆田。同时，福州、厦门抽调两个移动方舱实验室和一台移动检测车，抵达莆田，莆田市全市核酸检测能力可达一天检测12万管。</p><p>另据中国国家卫健委官网消息，中国大陆昨天新增确诊病例46例，其中本土病例20例均在福建。</p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/zbpe-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=zbpe399&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_desktop-zbpe399_2.png?dHEiGouxxZpWwWTJE04dyGyUN74JOU2z" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_desktop-zbpe399_2.png?dHEiGouxxZpWwWTJE04dyGyUN74JOU2z" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/zbpe-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=zbpe399&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_mobile-zbpe399_2.png?qZnBcIGCJvPR8A2IAZOZuOxdoGZDatiI" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_mobile-zbpe399_2.png?qZnBcIGCJvPR8A2IAZOZuOxdoGZDatiI" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
