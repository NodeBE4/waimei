------

<div>
<p>【大纪元2023年10月20日讯】在<a href="https://www.epochtimes.com/gb/tag/%E4%B8%AD%E5%9B%BD%E7%BB%8F%E6%B5%8E.html">中国经济</a>不断下滑、民众不敢消费之际，中共党媒<a href="https://www.epochtimes.com/gb/tag/%E6%96%B0%E5%8D%8E%E7%A4%BE.html">新华社</a>在10月20日发文“中国经济持续恢复、总体回升向好态势”，引发网络骂声一片。</p><p>目前，<a href="https://www.epochtimes.com/gb/tag/%E4%B8%AD%E5%9B%BD%E7%BB%8F%E6%B5%8E.html">中国经济</a>持续下滑，房地产接连爆出债务危机，青年失业率居高不下。虽然中共官方祭出一些刺激措施，但民间已经信心丧失殆尽，陷入困境的中国房地产市场在短期内几乎没有复苏迹象。</p><p>但是中共喉舌<a href="https://www.epochtimes.com/gb/tag/%E6%96%B0%E5%8D%8E%E7%A4%BE.html">新华社</a>的评论称，“从工业、服务业、消费、投资等指标来看，经济呈现持续恢复向好势头。” 这引发了国内网民一边倒的讥讽。同时当天早盘，上证指数在经历了将近230个交易日之后，再度跌破<a href="https://www.epochtimes.com/gb/tag/3000%E7%82%B9.html">3000点</a>，引发股民哀叹，“破了3000点，哪来的自信？”</p><p><a href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14099728-1.png"><img class="size-large wp-image-14099728 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14099728-1-600x180.png" alt="" width="600" height="180" /></a></p><p><a href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14099729-4.png"><img class="size-large wp-image-14099729 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14099729-4-600x66.png" alt="" width="600" height="66" /></a></p><p><a href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14099730-5.png"><img class="size-large wp-image-14099730 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14099730-5-600x246.png" alt="" width="600" height="246" /></a></p><p><a href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14099731-3.png"><img class="size-large wp-image-14099731 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14099731-3-600x124.png" alt="" width="600" height="124" /></a></p><p><a href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14099732-2.png"><img class="size-large wp-image-14099732 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id14099732-2-600x218.png" alt="" width="600" height="218" /></a></p><p>责任编辑：徐简#</p>
