<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">20</span>日，本网获悉获刑<span lang="EN-US">1</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>个月的江苏南京法轮功学员胡元勤的案情及简历，现在通报如下：<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">胡元勤：</span></b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">江苏南京法轮功学员，男，<span lang="EN-US">1954</span>年生。曾任南京水泥厂领导。一九九六年十二月，胡元勤开始修炼法轮功。自<span lang="EN-US">1999</span>年中共开始迫害法轮功后，其多次被绑架、关押，甚至于<span lang="EN-US">2015</span>年被判刑<span lang="EN-US">3</span>年。<b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></b></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">胡元勤因发放法轮功资料，于<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">4</span>日被南京市公安局江宁分局、东山派出所警察抓捕，随即被关押在南京市江宁区看守所。<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月中旬获悉：胡元勤被南京市雨花台区法院判刑<span lang="EN-US">1</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>个月。刑期至<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">3</span>日。<span lang="EN-US" style="color: #00b050;"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p>
