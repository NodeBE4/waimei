font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">由于多次介入关注各地的维权事件，其身份亦在广州当地愈发“敏感”，于<span lang="EN-US">2014</span>年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">29</span>日，即六四<span lang="EN-US">25</span>周年前夕，被广州市警方从家中以“喝茶”名义带走，并被以涉嫌“寻衅滋事罪”刑拘；同年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">26</span>日，获取保候审释放；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体;
