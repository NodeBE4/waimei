[已服刑5年的曹三强牧师简历](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2022/05/5.html)
------

<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">4</span>日，本网获悉：<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">3</span>日是曹三强牧师<span lang="EN-US">63</span>岁生日，他因传福音获刑<span lang="EN-US">7</span>年，现已被囚<span lang="EN-US">5</span>年。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">曹三强简历：<span lang="EN-US">1959</span>年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">3</span>日出生，湖南省长沙市人，早期曾任教师，长沙市邵阳市某家庭教会创办人，“中国福音会”牧师，中国在押良心犯。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">早年，曾在湖南长沙市邵阳市其家乡创立家庭教会；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2008</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年汶川地震后，曾前往灾区从事四川基督教爱心工作，帮助学生及地震灾民度过难关；曾到云南省普洱市地区从事宣教、慈善救济及兴教办学事业，并在当地创办多所学校，帮助贫困儿童入学；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2017</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">5</span>日，在其与另一基督徒同工景如霞从缅甸佤邦（缅甸第二特区）返国时，出于便利，使用了非正式方式入境中国，被边防武警拦截，后被普洱市孟连县警方以涉嫌“偷越国境罪”刑拘；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">同年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">25</span>日，被孟连县检察院以涉嫌“偷越国境罪”正式批捕，并被关押在普洱市孟连县看守所；<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">13</span>日，其二人案被移送检察院审查起诉；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2018</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">9</span>日，其案在云南省普洱市孟连县法院正式开庭，其亲友参加了旁听；尽管辩护律师认为其当事人行为不应构成犯罪，但<span lang="EN-US">2018</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">23</span>日其仍被普洱市孟连县法院一审以“偷越国境罪”重判有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">7</span>年，并处罚金<span lang="EN-US">2</span>万元人民币；其不服上诉，<span lang="EN-US">2019</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">12</span>日经普洱市中级法院二审裁决，依然维持原判，刑期至<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">4</span>日。目前在云南省第一监狱（又即昆明市东郊王大桥监狱）服刑。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"></p><div class="separator" style="clear: both;
