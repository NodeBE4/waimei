<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">今天上午在成都市看守所视频会见了谢俊彪。了解到如下情况：<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">1.</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">今年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">12</span>号从双流区看守所转到成都市看守所下<span lang="EN-US">10</span>监区<span lang="EN-US">1012</span>监室。监室里原本应该关押<span lang="EN-US">20</span>个人关押了<span lang="EN-US">26</span>人，有些拥挤。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2.</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">成都市看守所能够保障每天的放风时间，但是伙食还不如双流区看守所，每天早饭一人一个馒头、一碗稀饭、两块萝卜干咸菜不够吃。看守所不提供牙膏牙刷、手纸等生活用品，需要自己买。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">3.</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">谢俊彪这几天正患感冒，咳嗽，呕吐，拉肚子，浑身酸痛无力。看守所有许多人都感冒了。我怀疑他患的是新冠感冒，建议他多喝热水。他说看守所提供的热水不够喝。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">4.</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">成都市看守所存在警员殴打被关押人员情况，他曾经亲眼看到一警号<span lang="EN-US">00238</span>的警员殴打一被关押人员。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">谢俊彪曾经就看守所伙食问题、生活用品问题向有关部门向过举报材料，但是看守所不给转交又退回了。谢俊彪要求见驻所检察室检察官，看守所不允许。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">了解上述情况后，我们就二审后的申诉问题交换了意见。他表示只要二审维持一审判决（<span lang="EN-US">2</span>年半）或者改判即使一天的罪，他就坚决申诉。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">我向他转达了父母的关切，他委托我转达对他父母、妻子儿女的牵挂，感谢所有关注他的案件的朋友。他希望父母能在双流区公检法和省政府、市政府等部门门前举牌抗议对他们的儿子的迫害，牌子上写“谢俊彪无罪，双流公检法有罪”，让人拍照發到网上。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">随后，我向他介绍了谭兵林、黄晓敏案件情况，告诉他鲍彤先生去世的消息和北京四通桥孤勇者的事迹，以及轰轰烈烈的白纸运动。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体;
