[获刑10年8个月的黑龙江哈尔滨法轮功学员李力壮的案情与简历](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2023/02/108.html)

<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">7</span>日，本网获悉获刑<span lang="EN-US">10</span>年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>个月的黑龙江哈尔滨法轮功学员李力壮的案情与简历，现在通报如下：<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">李力壮</span></b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">：<span lang="EN-US">1973</span>年出生，又名李立壮，黑龙江省哈尔滨市南岗区公民，原哈尔滨医科大学附属第一医院骨外科主治医生，个体小商品经营者，法轮功信仰者。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">自<span lang="EN-US">1995</span>年从哈尔滨医科大学毕业后，即成为哈尔滨医科大学附属第一医院骨外科医生，并因工作勤恳、热心帮助患者而深受患者及单位同事的青睐和赞许；然而，自<span lang="EN-US">1990</span>年末中共政府将法轮功视为“邪教”进行疯狂迫害起，因坚持自由信仰而进京和平上访和经常宣传法轮功，而屡遭政府逼迫打压；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">李力壮曾遭多次关押、抄家以及被<span lang="EN-US">2</span>次劳教、<span lang="EN-US">1</span>次判刑，并在羁押和劳教期间惨遭多种酷刑折磨；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2000</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月，曾因进京为法轮功讨公道维权上访，而被哈尔滨市警方遣回原籍，并旋即被以邪教罪由拘捕；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2001</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月，被哈尔滨市当局处劳动教养<span lang="EN-US">2</span>年，送哈尔滨长林子劳教所关押；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2002</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月，因劳教期间多次抗议绝食，而曾被哈尔滨市南岗区法院以是绝食组织者为由又追加判刑<span lang="EN-US">4</span>年，并先后送哈尔滨新建监狱和大庆监狱服刑；服刑期间，均遭酷刑，至其身体几乎残废；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2008</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">1</span>日，曾因在公共汽车上向他人讲述央视所谓“天安门自焚”伪案和法轮功实际宣传的内容，而被哈尔滨学府路一派出所民警抓走，当日即被刑拘于哈尔滨市南岗区看守所；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">同年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">24</span>日，再被地方当局处劳动教养<span lang="EN-US">1</span>年，并送哈尔滨市长林子劳教所关押；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2020</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">8</span>日，因用电话进行“反宣”（即反对中共对法轮功的迫害、反对中共的强权统治等），又被黑龙江省大庆市龙南区警方蹲坑、跨区抓捕，并被以涉嫌邪教罪刑拘；后又被龙南区检察院转正式批捕，指控其因在电话簿中存有<span lang="EN-US">8</span>万多个电话号码和有<span lang="EN-US">4</span>名警察接听过法轮功信息的电话，故其行为违反了相关《刑法》第三百条；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2021</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月，其案在大庆市让胡路区法院历经先后四次庭审之后，被一审以“利用邪教组织破坏法律实施罪”重判处有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">10</span>年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>个月，并处罚金<span lang="EN-US">8</span>万元；其不服，上诉；后经大庆市中级法院二审裁决，依然维持原判，刑期至<span lang="EN-US">2030</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">7</span>日。目前在黑龙江省呼兰监狱（黑龙江省哈尔滨市呼兰区腰堡乡<span lang="EN-US">803</span>信箱，邮政编码：<span lang="EN-US">150521</span>）服刑。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"></p><div class="separator" style="clear: both;
