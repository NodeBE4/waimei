<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">7</span>日，本网获悉：获刑<span lang="EN-US">3</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月、在黑龙江女子监狱服刑的黑龙江佳木斯市法轮功学员刘丽杰会见权遭剥夺。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">包龙军今天在推特通报：“因信仰而在黑龙江女子监狱服刑的刘丽杰一直被拒绝会见家属、律师。一年来一直在集训监区，集训监区严管队号称最严苛的监区，大队长陶淑萍。今天，家属、律师八人再次来到女子监狱要求会见，接待室人员就给了两个电话<span lang="EN-US">045186639021</span>、<span lang="EN-US">22</span>，打不通让其再行联系，往出推我们说：想找哪个部门自己到外面上网查”<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">刘丽杰<span lang="EN-US">:</span></span></b><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;"> </span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">黑龙江佳木斯市法轮功学员。<span lang="EN-US">1970</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">20</span>日出生于黑龙江省佳木斯市，毕业于东北师范大学生物系，在佳木斯市职教集团职业学院学报编辑部任职（该职业学院的前身是佳木斯市教育学院）。<span lang="EN-US">1998</span>年她开始修炼法轮功，连年被学院评为优秀教师。刘丽杰于<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">14</span>日被当地国保警察抓捕，后以“取保候审”回家。之后，刘丽杰仍被当局构陷到向阳区检察院，法院对她实施期限半年的“监视居住”，在她家的楼旁安了监控摄像头，有便衣警察带车蹲守。<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">13</span>日早上，刘丽杰和家人到派出所索要所谓的“监视居住”通知书时，被带到了法院。法院刑庭庭长宋涛问刘丽杰是否认罪认罚，刘丽杰拒绝。<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">16</span>日上午，建设派出所所长和向阳公安分局国保大队长门茂盛来到刘丽杰家，说她写“<span lang="EN-US">3</span>书”（放弃修炼的所谓“保证书”等）的话，检察院和法院的人说了，她的事就会缓下来。刘丽杰仍然拒绝。后遭关押。<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">1</span>日<span lang="EN-US"> ,</span>在没有辩护人出庭、被告人的辩护权没有获得保障的情况下，刘丽杰被判<span lang="EN-US">3</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月。刑期至<span lang="EN-US">2025</span>年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月。刘丽杰现在黑龙江女子监狱服刑，其合法会见权遭非法剥夺。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"></p><div class="separator" style="clear: both;
