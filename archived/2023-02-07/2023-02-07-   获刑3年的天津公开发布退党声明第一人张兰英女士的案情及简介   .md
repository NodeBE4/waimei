mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年，曾因其私人住宅被天津市南开区政府在无任何有效拆迁手续和拆迁补偿的情况下，强行拆除而致流离失所，并对其及年迈父母实施了一系列惨无人道的违法迫害，遂被迫走上维权抗争之路；又因屡次上访并支持、帮助他人维权，遭天津当局强力打压；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2012</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年，曾因其父母为其遭强拆多次到北京上访，被天津市南开区警方数年关押在时代宾馆<span lang="EN-US">103</span>号（实为黑监狱），又因其父母长期被关生病无法医治，尤其是其老父曾多次在黑监狱里摔伤导致脑梗死，极高危高血压，生活己不能自理，政府不管救治，</span><b><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">2014</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月张兰英透过媒体公开声明退党。</span></b></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;"><span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">后其无奈而向社会求助，因众人纷纷募捐，<span lang="EN-US">2014</span>年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">28</span>日，其及<span lang="EN-US">6</span>名捐款人随被当地派出所警方悉数抓走，其被以“扰乱公共场所秩序”为由行政拘留<span lang="EN-US">15</span>天；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2015</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">张兰英虽刑满释放，但仍被当局警告务必做到三不准，即“不准翻案，不准发声，不准上微博，否则就抓你”；其家人（老母、女儿、姐姐等）均受牵连，遭当局长期性的骚扰及恐吓；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2021</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">4</span>日，又被南开区检察院以同罪名批捕，指控其因在微博发布自己的冤情，引起社会热议，致其该微博转发量多达<span lang="EN-US">500+</span>，且网络媒体对其冤情进行追踪报导及其进京在国家信访局登记控诉等，其言行属于违法；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2022</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast;
