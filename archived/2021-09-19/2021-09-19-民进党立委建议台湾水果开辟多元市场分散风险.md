[民进党立委建议台湾水果开辟多元市场分散风险](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210919-1195125)
------

<div>
<p>中国大陆海关总署今天发通知，自明天（20日）起将禁止进口台湾莲雾、番荔枝（台湾称释迦）。民进党立委周春米建议，台湾水果开辟更加多元化的市场，以分散风险。</p><p>据中时电子报报道，周春米说，台湾近年来和大陆多次“交手”，也清楚大陆的做法，目前台湾生鲜水果的外销市场也从过去最倚赖大陆，逐渐分散到其他市场。</p><p>周春米说，农委会数据显示，今年上半年水果外销大陆金额占所有市场的比重，已从去年上半年的75％大幅降低到51.6％。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>周春米说，早在今年2月底的凤梨事件后，农委会就加速开发多元市场，未来也希望农委会能协助农民努力拓展其他市场，并搭配相关行销措施，加速开辟多元市场以分散风险。</p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/zbpe-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=zbpe399&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_desktop-zbpe399_2.png?dHEiGouxxZpWwWTJE04dyGyUN74JOU2z" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_desktop-zbpe399_2.png?dHEiGouxxZpWwWTJE04dyGyUN74JOU2z" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/zbpe-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=zbpe399&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_mobile-zbpe399_2.png?qZnBcIGCJvPR8A2IAZOZuOxdoGZDatiI" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_mobile-zbpe399_2.png?qZnBcIGCJvPR8A2IAZOZuOxdoGZDatiI" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
