<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">4</span>日，本网获悉：今天上午<span lang="EN-US">11:11</span>分季孝龙的未婚妻收到了季孝龙先生的刑事拘留通知书。季孝龙被控所谓寻衅滋事罪而被刑事拘留，目前羁押在上海浦东新区看守所。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">季孝龙先生几个月来一直为上海<span lang="EN-US">2500</span>万市民请命呼吁——反对封城、反对核酸、反对过度防疫，因此言论而得罪中共上海当局，终对季孝龙先生实施了抓捕。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">：<span lang="EN-US">1976</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">10</span>日出生，江苏省张家港市人，张家港市杨舍镇名都花苑居民，基督徒，人权捍卫者，民主人士。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2015</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月，曾因宣传民主理念而被上海市浦东新区警方以“寻衅滋事”罪由传唤，并被处以行政拘留<span lang="EN-US">10</span>日；<span lang="EN-US">2018</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">20</span>日，曾因在网上发文，呼吁民主人士响应“厕所革命”，并在医院、大学等公共场所的厕所门上，用马克笔写宣传语“大病治不起，习胖狂撒币，修宪妄称帝、苦难何时毕！打倒共产党。还政于民，结束民众苦难。推翻习核心，拒走文革路”等字句，短时间内响应者众多，随于<span lang="EN-US">2018</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">27</span>日再次被浦东新区警方以涉嫌“寻衅滋事罪”刑事拘留；同年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">25</span>日，又被送到指定居所监视居住；同年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">30</span>日，被以同罪由转正式逮捕，关押在上海市浦东新区看守所；<span lang="EN-US">2018</span>年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">30</span>日，其案被上海市浦东新区检察院起诉至上海市浦东新区法院；<span lang="EN-US">2019</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">14</span>日，其最终被该法院以“寻衅滋事罪” 一审判处有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">3</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月；据悉，羁押期间，警方曾多次以让其“死亡”和用寒冷气温逼迫其认罪，致其身体健康虚弱且生病吐血；其家属曾送羽绒服和《圣经》书籍亦遭拒收；<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">9</span>日，刑满获释。此前在上海市白茅岭监狱七监区服刑。<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">31</span>日季孝龙再次被上海警方带走，后获悉其当日被刑事拘留。羁押在上海浦东新区看守所。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;
