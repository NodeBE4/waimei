[国泰航空将对拒绝接种疫苗员工采取纪律行动](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210902-1189484)
------

<div>
<p>香港《南华早报》引述消息指，国泰航空有近60至80名机组人员没有在8月31日前打疫苗，公司正采取纪律行动。</p><p>据《南华早报》报道，周二起，国泰所有航班的机组人员均为已打疫苗员工。</p><p>国泰早前曾称，将解雇未于9月前打针的员工。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>另据《明报》报道，国泰发言人回应明报查询时指，正跟进少数个别事件，同时继续检视没有打疫苗的员工聘用情况，及评估相关人士能否续获聘用为国泰的机组人员。</p><p>集团强调，已考量未能符合疫苗接种要求的机组人员个案，大部分具备充份的医疗理据而无法接种疫苗，或正在长期休假。</p><p>国泰又指，员工疫苗接种率非常高，93%的驻香港员工已预约接种或已接种疫苗，当中包括99%的驻港机师及约93%的驻港机舱服务员。</p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/zbpe-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=zbpe399&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_desktop-zbpe399_2.png?dHEiGouxxZpWwWTJE04dyGyUN74JOU2z" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_desktop-zbpe399_2.png?dHEiGouxxZpWwWTJE04dyGyUN74JOU2z" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/zbpe-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=zbpe399&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_mobile-zbpe399_2.png?qZnBcIGCJvPR8A2IAZOZuOxdoGZDatiI" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_mobile-zbpe399_2.png?qZnBcIGCJvPR8A2IAZOZuOxdoGZDatiI" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
