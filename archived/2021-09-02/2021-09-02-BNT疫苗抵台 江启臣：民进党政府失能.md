[BNT疫苗抵台 江启臣：民进党政府失能](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210902-1189455)
------

<div>
<p>首批BNT疫苗（也称辉瑞疫苗或复必泰疫苗）今晨（2日）抵台，国民党主席江启臣在面簿感谢永龄基金会、台积电与慈济之余，批评蔡英文政府“失能”，抢不走民间组织的贡献。</p><p>江启臣表示，庆幸在政府丧失应该发挥的功能后，民间机构能够填补政府失能的缺口，从采购到催货一条龙完成。</p><p>江启臣写道，台湾人民永远会记得，曾经有一个执政党为了自身意识形态，让政治力凌驾科学，在疫情蔓延的当下，污蔑国际普遍认可有效力的辉瑞疫苗，用以掩护未经完整试验程序的高端疫苗。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>他强调，台湾需要“安全有效国际认证”的疫苗，人民需要政府，人民需要相信“科学程序”的政府，可惜国民党原本的采购遭到拦阻，不然会有更多的疫苗陆续抵达。</p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/zbpe-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=zbpe399&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_desktop-zbpe399_2.png?dHEiGouxxZpWwWTJE04dyGyUN74JOU2z" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_desktop-zbpe399_2.png?dHEiGouxxZpWwWTJE04dyGyUN74JOU2z" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/zbpe-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=zbpe399&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_mobile-zbpe399_2.png?qZnBcIGCJvPR8A2IAZOZuOxdoGZDatiI" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_mobile-zbpe399_2.png?qZnBcIGCJvPR8A2IAZOZuOxdoGZDatiI" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
