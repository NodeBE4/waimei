[恒大称已向9月到期投资产品持有人支付部分款项](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210930-1198871)
------

<div>
<p>中国恒大集团债券持有人透露，恒大第二度没有如期支付离岸债券利息，但公司称今天（30日）向持有9月份到期投资产品的人士支付部分款项。</p><p>据路透社报道，这些投资产品持有者大部分为在岸散户投资者。恒大在官网公告中说，他们完成首期10%的兑付，相关款项已经发放到相应投资人账户。</p><p>陷入债务危机的恒大负债高达3050亿美元（4130亿新元），上周并没有如期向离岸债券持有人支付8350万美元的利息，昨天还须支付另一笔4750万美元的利息。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>知情人士透露，一些离岸恒大债券持有人截至美东昨天既没有收到利息，也没有相关人员与他们沟通。<br> </p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/zbpe-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=zbpe399&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_desktop-zbpe399_2.png?dHEiGouxxZpWwWTJE04dyGyUN74JOU2z" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_desktop-zbpe399_2.png?dHEiGouxxZpWwWTJE04dyGyUN74JOU2z" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/zbpe-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=zbpe399&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_mobile-zbpe399_2.png?qZnBcIGCJvPR8A2IAZOZuOxdoGZDatiI" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_mobile-zbpe399_2.png?qZnBcIGCJvPR8A2IAZOZuOxdoGZDatiI" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
