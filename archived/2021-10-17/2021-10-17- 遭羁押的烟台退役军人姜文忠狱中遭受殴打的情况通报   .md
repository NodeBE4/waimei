<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">17</span>日，本网获悉遭羁押的烟台退役军人姜文忠狱中遭受殴打的情况，现通报如下：<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">退役军人姜文忠，烟台海阳市发城镇中夏屋庄村农民。<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月底被以寻衅滋事罪刑拘，随后被逮捕，初被羁押在海阳看守所，后被转至烟台市看守所。在烟台市看守所羁押至今期间被同室羁押人员殴打过几十次，他曾绝食四天多以示抗争，现虽然已被调换监室，但被殴打的问题依然没有彻底解决，昨天会见时他告诉律师，调换监室后现在开始遭受辱骂，随时可能被打。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2020</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年因烟台交警违规执法，姜文忠被交警的车撞伤，医生说最低将造成九级伤残。为讨回公道，姜文忠向有关部门控告烟台交警，烟台交警最后同意赔偿<span lang="EN-US">30</span>万，向姜文忠支付了<span lang="EN-US">15</span>万，另<span lang="EN-US">15</span>万转给了发城镇政府。为了讨回这<span lang="EN-US">15</span>万，姜文忠继续控告烟台交警，<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月底姜文忠到北京后，被海阳截访人员强行带回，从此失去自由。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">多年前姜文忠曾竞选为村主任，因主持公道被镇干部打压下台。姜文忠说，迫害他的正是发城镇现任镇委书记任飞，海阳公安局沦为任飞为非作歹的帮凶。他说发城镇委书记任飞存在非法采沙、雇佣黑社会欺压百姓等多种问题。 <span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ansi-language: EN-US; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-bidi-font-family: &quot;Times New Roman&quot;; mso-bidi-language: AR-SA; mso-bidi-theme-font: minor-bidi; mso-fareast-language: ZH-CN; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">身体已遭重创的姜文忠现被羁押，脚伤和腿伤不得医治，还遭受殴打，如此下去，将彻底变成残疾人。据了解姜文忠的辩护人将与姜文忠的好友崔祥取等，对姜文忠遭受殴打、迫害等问题进行揭露和控告。&nbsp;</span>
