<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;"><span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2021</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">28</span>日到<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">25</span>日因反对政府强制非法征地被群殴三次，次次骨折。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2021</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">28</span>日，高速项目部冀通路桥公司副经理李吉平纠集指使一群土匪强盗，侵占我基本农田，把我群殴轻伤二级，至今还在侦查，现场视频都给里城道派出所了。里城道派出所所长任康维一次次骗我。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">本来就是一伙的吗！在这之前冀通路桥多次毁坏我的基本农田，多次报警，一个人没处理。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">一个个张开国家闭口国家，国务院批准你们征地了？你们都是国家的蛀虫、败类、腐败分子，贪赃枉法。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2022</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">25</span>日，我去里城道派出所问我的案子，被假警察真走狗杨军波打断<span lang="EN-US">6</span>根肋骨，眼眶骨折，还拘留我<span lang="EN-US">10</span>日，我绝食<span lang="EN-US">12</span>天讨不回我的体检结果。至今不受理不立案，不给出伤情鉴定聘请书。我听说里城道派出所给杨军波在所里装修了一间房，住所里了，一打成名，功狗。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">我三次被群殴，我都是一个人面对一群人。我到里城道派出所举牌，到无极县退役军人服务中心举牌，无极县政府门口举牌，到无极县公安局举牌，到无极县法院举牌，找个拍照录像的都难。好不容易到检察院、法院，请不到律师。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">我还有个案子上诉快<span lang="EN-US">20</span>年了，没消息。</span><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt;">2003</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;">年夏以贾国臣为首的东关村委会强行收回我家承包地，撬我粮库抢窃我近<span lang="EN-US">60</span>亩地的小麦。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt;">2003</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;">年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">3</span>号我妈自杀。<span lang="EN-US">2005</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">9</span>号我爹自杀。害我家破人亡，毁我一生</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 16px;">。</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;">报警后警察说是经济纠纷，无奈告到无极县法院，败诉。</span><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt;">2003</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;">年</span><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt;">9</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;">月我把上诉状、上诉费交给无极县法院王进来。19</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;">年多了至今没有消息！</span></p><p class="MsoNormal"></p><div class="separator" style="clear: both;
