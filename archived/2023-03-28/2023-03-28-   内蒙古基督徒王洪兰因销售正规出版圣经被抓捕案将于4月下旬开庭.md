[内蒙古基督徒王洪兰因销售正规出版圣经被抓捕案将于4月下旬开庭](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2023/03/4_28.html)
------

<div>
<p><span></span></p><a name='more'></a><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;"><br /></span><p></p><p><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">28</span>日，本网获悉：</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">内蒙古呼和浩特市基督徒王洪兰因销售正规出版圣经被抓捕案最近召开了庭前会议，正式开庭定于</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">4</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">月下旬，拟开庭三周。</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">王洪兰等人因销售正规出版的圣经于<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月被抓捕，并被指控为非法经营罪。与王洪兰一起被抓捕的还有王洪兰的丈夫、王洪兰的二儿子以及已故大儿子的妻子等四人。王洪兰今年<span lang="EN-US">66</span>岁，自<span lang="EN-US">24</span>岁信主以来，一生都在侍奉教会。检察机关认定他们销售圣经的金额超过<span lang="EN-US">4000</span>万元，王洪兰作为第一被告检察院建议量刑<span lang="EN-US">15</span>年。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">王洪兰等人其实只是把其他弟兄姊妹的奉献款补贴给其他需要购买圣经的人，没有牟利。所有圣经均是南京正规印刷的。他们教会的传统是坚持每日早上六点祷告，已经坚持了三十年没有一日间断。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">对王洪兰案进展，本网将持续关注。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p>
</div>
