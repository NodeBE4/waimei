font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2012</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年始，开始积极介入声援围观行动，如江西新余刘萍独立候选人案、山东曲阜薛福顺事件、山东淄博孙峰“煽颠案”、<span lang="EN-US">4.29</span>祭奠林昭活动、湖南衡阳赵枫生“煽颠案”开庭、郑州十君子事件、焦作张小玉案、苏州老兵范木根血拆案开庭、与王福磊等维权网友声援维权公民王健案等。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">8</span>日，曾因用微信转发“湖北大冶市驻京维稳人员名单”而遭当局抓捕，并被行政拘留<span lang="EN-US">15</span>天，<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">24</span>日被以涉嫌“泄露国家机密罪”刑拘，后被释放；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体;
