<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">30</span>日，本网获悉：因为北京冬奥会，在<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月底逃离福州到北京生活的福建维权人士唐兆星，虽然多次声明自己老了，也醒悟了，只想平平安安度晚年，不信访，请福州当局不要再截访了，但在<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">17</span>日还是被截回去了，住希尔顿逸林酒店，被天天捅鼻子。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">唐兆星说：我在北京房山区五候村临时住处，只想安度晚年，谁知元月<span lang="EN-US">17</span>号下午<span lang="EN-US">3</span>点，地方政府维稳官员翁华带十几个黑社会人，窜入我住处，强制绑架车上押往福州，<span lang="EN-US">18</span>号晚<span lang="EN-US">8</span>点到达福州仓山南三迪希尔顿逸林酒店，这边有仓山警察与黑社会勾结完，我就被强制关进<span lang="EN-US">1202</span>房间。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2018</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">12</span>日，唐兆星因荒唐的“鞭炮案”被判刑<span lang="EN-US">3</span>年。<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">12</span>日，唐兆星出狱，在福州的住处是六楼，几经折磨且患高血压与肾脏病走不上去，又是无医保、社保、低保的“三无”人员，看了一次急诊开支<span lang="EN-US">3</span>千元都是自付，这福州对于老唐来说如同覆舟，难以生存。<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月底，唐兆星逃离福州，到北京租住一层，每个月房租<span lang="EN-US">200</span>元，办理了暂住证，有老朋友照顾，还有检点破烂卖维持生活。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体;
