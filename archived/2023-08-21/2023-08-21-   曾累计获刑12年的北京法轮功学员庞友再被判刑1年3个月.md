<p><span></span></p><a name='more'></a><span style="font-size: 12pt;"><br /></span><p></p><p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">（维权网信息中心报道）</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt;">2023</span><span style="font-size: 12pt;">年</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt;">8</span><span style="font-size: 12pt;">月</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt;">21</span><span style="font-size: 12pt;">日，本网获悉：曾累计获刑</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt;">12</span><span style="font-size: 12pt;">年的北京法轮功学员庞友再被判刑</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt;">1</span><span style="font-size: 12pt;">年</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt;">3</span><span style="font-size: 12pt;">个月，其案情及简历通报如下：</span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">庞友：<span lang="EN-US">1963</span>年出生，又名庞有，陕西省延安市宜川县籍人，住北京市昌平区宏福苑小区，原北京市朝阳区洼里乡政府（现奥运村街道办事处）规划办公室主任，后任北京市旭日房地产有限责任公司经理，法轮功信仰者，原北京市朝阳区北苑大羊坊法轮功炼功点义务辅导员，中国在押良心犯。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">1990</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年代，因锻炼和追求信仰法轮功而屡遭当局拘捕和酷刑打压并被撤销公职；<span lang="EN-US">1999</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月至<span lang="EN-US">2000</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月，曾被北京市朝阳区警方<span lang="EN-US">4</span>次非法拘押，并在抄家时将其私有贵重财产侵吞占有，至今无一说法；关押期间，曾被多次毒打，并惨遭电棍电击全身等酷刑；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2000</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">25</span>日，曾因在天安门广场拉横幅为法轮功正名而和平请愿吁呼，故而连续遭广场便衣警察及通州某派出所警方暴力殴打致昏迷；后被关押于通州乔庄看守所期间，为胁迫其放弃法轮功信仰，又连续遭警方恶意暴打、电棍电击及强行灌食等非人道逼迫；同年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">27</span>日，曾因在家制作法轮功信仰者遭迫害讯息，而再遭北京市朝阳区警方抓捕，并被施以酷刑，最终导致其一只胳膊残废不能动，之后被北京市高级法院以“邪教罪”枉法重判<span lang="EN-US">8</span>年，后送北京市前进监狱服刑；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">刑满出狱后，<span lang="EN-US">2009</span>年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">3</span>日，又因自由信仰事宜而被北京市朝阳区警方拘捕，后被朝阳区法院判刑<span lang="EN-US">4</span>年；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">第二次出狱后，<span lang="EN-US">2014</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月，因其曾陪同亲戚在陕西老家拜访生意上的朋友，并讲述法轮功遭逼迫的事实真相，遂被延安市宜川县警方传唤带走，后被刑拘于陕西省黄龙县看守所和宜川县看守所共计<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月，并再遭多种酷刑虐折磨，令其痛不欲生；之后其案被地方检察院起诉和移交至陕西省黄龙县法院，最终经次年北京<span lang="EN-US">1004</span>名普通民众的紧急呼吁签名和著名人权律师对其案的有力辩护与质询，<span lang="EN-US">2015</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">11</span>日，宜川县法院对其案下达了无罪释放判决书；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2016</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月、<span lang="EN-US">2017</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月，曾因参加法轮功家庭式交流聚会和向他人宣传法轮功信息，而先后两次被当地警方刑拘；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2022</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">2</span>日，庞友又因在北京街头向他人发法轮功宣传单张而被北京市海淀区西三旗派出所警方抓捕，后被取保释放；同年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">28</span>日，再被北京市昌平区警方以涉嫌邪教罪拘捕；同年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">7</span>日，其案被北京市昌平区检察院提起公诉，并被很快移交至北京市昌平区法院；<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月，其案开始被北京市昌平区法院正式庭审，至同年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月，终被该法院一审以“利用邪教组织破坏法律实施罪”判处有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">1</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>个月，刑期至<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">27</span>日；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 16px;">庞友不服判决，表示上诉</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;">；据悉，数年来其曾无数次遭非法拘押，并曾两次被冤判共计<span lang="EN-US">12</span>年，而此次庭审，身体状况令人堪忧；另悉，其家人因其被抓判备受牵连，其母因受警方多次惊吓而致含恨离世，其妻也因与其共同信仰而被枉判刑<span lang="EN-US">3</span>年，留下幼子孤身一人在家，以致于至今其心理疾患严重。</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;">目前被羁押于北京市昌平区看守所。</span></p><p class="MsoNormal"></p><div class="separator" style="clear: both;
