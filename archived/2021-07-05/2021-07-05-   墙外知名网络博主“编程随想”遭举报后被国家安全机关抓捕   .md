font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">山东东营人，居住地位于山东青岛，被捕前担任某网络科技公司工程师。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: &quot;MS Gothic&quot;; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2021</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年，有五毛根据其被发现的<span lang="EN-US">2009</span>年之前注册的同名社交网站账户，经整理其大量浏览记录，确认了该账户系“编程随想”本人所有，与其墙外发言内容高度吻合。随后，该五毛于今年五月将有关调查资料举报至国家安全机关，“编程随想”因此被捕。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体;
