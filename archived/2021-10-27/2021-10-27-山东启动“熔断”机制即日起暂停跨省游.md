[山东启动“熔断”机制即日起暂停跨省游](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20211027-1207594)
------

<div>
<p>山东省文化和旅游厅今天下发通知，要求暂停全省旅行社及在线旅游企业经营跨省团队旅游及“机票+酒店”旅游产品，待省内无中高风险地区后，自行恢复跨省旅游经营活动。</p><p>根据大众日报报道，针对山东省日照市五莲县洪凝街道学府壹号小区宣布为中风险地区，按照中国文化和旅游部办公厅相关文件要求，山东省文化和旅游厅发出上述通知。</p><p>通知还要求旅行社及在线旅游企业要及时做好善后工作，妥善处置游客退团退款事宜，最大限度满足游客合理化诉求。加强在途团队游客疫情防控，强化游客健康监测和安全管理，做好健康防护。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>根据“健康山东”微信公众号消息，山东省昨天报告新增确诊病例五例，其中日照市报告本土病例四例，系外省返鲁确诊病例的密切接触者；青岛市报告韩国输入一例。正在住院治疗的确诊病例30例。</p><p>针对近期多地暴发冠病疫情都与跨省旅游有关，中国文化和旅游部上周出台了实施跨省旅游经营活动管理“熔断”机制和全国暂停经营旅游专列业务等针对性措施。</p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/zbpe-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=zbpe399&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_desktop-zbpe399_2.png?dHEiGouxxZpWwWTJE04dyGyUN74JOU2z" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_desktop-zbpe399_2.png?dHEiGouxxZpWwWTJE04dyGyUN74JOU2z" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/zbpe-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=zbpe399&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_mobile-zbpe399_2.png?qZnBcIGCJvPR8A2IAZOZuOxdoGZDatiI" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_mobile-zbpe399_2.png?qZnBcIGCJvPR8A2IAZOZuOxdoGZDatiI" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
