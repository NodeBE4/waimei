[去年中秋节前夕遭抓捕的湖北维权人士何斌、徐彩虹夫妇的“寻衅滋事”案已到检察院](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2023/03/blog-post_69.html)
------

<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;"><br /></div><p> </p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">6</span>日，本网获悉：去年中秋节前夕（<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">9</span>日），湖北维权人士何斌、徐彩虹夫妇被当地警方以涉嫌寻衅滋事罪为名刑事拘留，关押至今，目前案件已经到检察院，估计四月份能开庭。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">目前徐彩虹被羁押在湖北襄阳市第一看守所；何斌被羁押在襄阳市第二看守所。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">何斌、徐彩虹夫妇简介：夫妇都是湖北襄阳人，<span lang="EN-US">1999</span>年夫妇创立自己第一个实体店品牌“爱点”超市，生意快速发展并成为该区域同行最大最红火。何斌、徐彩虹夫妇的生意因触动了利益集团的利益被找上了麻烦，黑白两道联动出手打击。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2001</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年丈夫何斌被构陷入狱，而其出狱后第二天，黑道又将夫妇店面打砸，将何斌打伤，并让黑道人员言明是得罪法院刑厅长的结果。夫妇二人被打击报复后就走上了维权道路，经历了这么多年问题不但没有得到解决，反而遭到当局不断的打击报复，十几年来何斌、徐彩虹夫妇遭遇了各种权益的侵害。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">仅何斌、徐彩虹夫妇因维权被非法关押经历就有：<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2008</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">3</span>日徐彩虹因到全国人大旁邮箱投信，被带回襄阳关法教班黑监狱，遭受非人道待遇，期间被殴打虐待致流产，紧接着又转入拘留所关押至两会结束。此次经历伤害导致身体严重受损（如）夫妻俩一直没有孩子。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2014</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">4</span>日徐彩虹因<span lang="EN-US">6.4</span>敏感，以寻衅滋事关押于北京东城看守所<span lang="EN-US">31</span>天。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2018</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;
