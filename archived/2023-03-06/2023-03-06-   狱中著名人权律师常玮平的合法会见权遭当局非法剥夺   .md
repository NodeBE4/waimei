[狱中著名人权律师常玮平的合法会见权遭当局非法剥夺](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2023/03/blog-post_6.html)

<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">6</span>日，本网获悉：狱中著名人权律师常玮平合法会见权遭当局非法剥夺。常玮平律师的所谓“颠覆国家政权罪”案已进入法院审判阶段，代理律师多次会见要求均被无理拒绝。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">据常玮平妻子陈紫娟介绍：自去年<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">26</span>日对常玮平律师秘密审判以来，中共当局非法剥夺了常玮平的合法会见权，以种种借口不予律师会见，而陈紫娟多次向看守所打电话要求解释，相关部门和人员竟然连陈紫娟的电话都不敢接。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">而常玮平案被中共当局以各种借口延期审理，常玮平案已经被延期到<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">7</span>日。中共当局以这种延期审理的方式达到长期羁押并实施酷刑迫害的目的。<b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></b></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">常玮平简历</span></b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">：<span lang="EN-US">1984</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">14</span>日出生，陕西省宝鸡市凤翔县城关镇周家门前村人，原陕西立刚律师事务所执业律师，人权律师，中国在押政治犯。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">早年曾就读于重庆大学；毕业后，<span lang="EN-US">2009</span>年—<span lang="EN-US">2013</span>年，通过一系列的律师资格自修和考试，最终获取律师执业资格证，后在陕西立刚律师事务所成为正式律师；工作期间，不仅曾代理多起公益诉讼（含艾滋歧视、乙肝歧视、性别歧视等）案件，亦曾代理过多起诸如“无锡<span lang="EN-US">4</span>·<span lang="EN-US">13</span>寻衅滋事案”、“无锡访民王金娣寻衅滋事案”、“南京王健寻衅滋事案”等敏感人权及弱势群体维权案件；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2018</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">14</span>日，曾因此而被陕西宝鸡司法局停业<span lang="EN-US"> 3 </span>个月；同年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">22 </span>日，其律师资格证被当地律师事务所注销；<span lang="EN-US">2019</span>年，在停业期满后，其基本处于无法在其他律师事务所获得工作机会的状态，因其曾在陕西省境内与至少<span lang="EN-US">10</span>家律师事务所接洽，但皆不顺利，或即将签约时临时变卦，或虽签约后又突然解除合约；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2019</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月，曾因参加了一个与多名维权律师和异见人士在厦门的非正式私人聚会，后因被中共官方知晓后定性为“厦门聚会案”，多人遭严打狠抓，<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">12</span>日，其亦被陕西省宝鸡市警方跨省抓走，并被以“危害国家安全”为由指定居所监视居住；后被取保候审，<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月，又因对外发视频称其在拘押期间，遭受酷刑，即每天（<span lang="EN-US">24</span>小时<span lang="EN-US">/</span>次）都把他绑在一种叫做“老虎凳”的刑具上，连续审问<span lang="EN-US">16</span>次，致其右手拇指到食指至今都毫无知觉，且在取保获释后仍受到严密监视等，<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">22</span>日，随再次被陕西省宝鸡市警方抓走，后被以涉嫌“煽动颠覆国家政权罪”指定居所监视居住；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2021</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">7</span>日，被宝鸡市检察院以涉嫌“颠覆国家政权罪”予以批捕；据悉，经过宝鸡市警方常玮平项目组长期的折磨，截至目前，其身体各种疾病全是刑讯逼供后遗症。不公开开庭审理后连续被延期审理至<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">7</span>日。目前被羁押于宝鸡市凤县看守所（陕西省宝鸡市凤县凤州镇锦绣路，邮政编码：<span lang="EN-US">721703</span>）。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体;
