[台湾慈济已就采购疫苗与政府签署文件](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210710-1167136)
------

<div>
<p>台湾慈济基金会上月送件申请采购500万剂BNT德国原厂疫苗有了新进展，慈济今天表示，已与政府完成必要法律文件签署，对政府诚摰感恩，将努力迅速完成后续采购及捐赠疫苗的相关流程。</p><p>根据联合新闻网报道，慈济上月23日由基金会执行长颜博文、副执行长何日生等人，送件到卫福部食药署申请采购疫苗，历经两个多星期沟通协调，台湾总统蔡英文当时还为此事与证严上人视频，慈济昨天终于与政府签署必要法律文件，将展开后续的洽购作业。</p><p>基金会表示，自申请采购疫苗以来，受到社会各界善心人士与海内外慈济人许多的鼓励与关心。证严上人采购疫苗之心愿、蔡英文总统及政府部门亦积极与慈济商议，大家同心协力以维护民众的健康。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>基金会说，面对世间无常，大家难免惶恐不安；在这样艰困的时刻，相信在全体人民之团结一致下，必能众志成城，化解一切困难，难行能行，让民众尽速远离疫情威胁，早日回归平安与健康的社会。<br> </p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
