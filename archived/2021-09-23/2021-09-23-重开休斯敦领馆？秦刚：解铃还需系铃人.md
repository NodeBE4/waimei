[重开休斯敦领馆？秦刚：解铃还需系铃人](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210923-1196440)
------

<div>
<p>被问及美中是否开始协商重开成都和休斯敦总领馆时，中国驻美国大使秦刚回复表示，解铃还需系铃人，要解决需要美方首先采取行动。</p><p>据中国驻美国大使馆网站消息，秦刚22日应邀出席卡特中心和乔治·布什美中关系基金会联合举办的线上对话会并发表演讲。他被问及，美中是否开始协商重开成都和休斯敦总领馆。</p><p>秦刚回复说，去年7月，美方突然单方面要求中方关闭驻休斯敦总领馆，违反中美领事条约有关规定以及国际法和国际关系基本准则，对中美关系造成严重破坏。面对美方悍然关闭中国驻休斯敦总领馆，中方不得不对等反制，关闭了美国驻成都总领馆。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>他表示，解铃还需系铃人。这个问题是美方单方面挑起的，要解决也需要美方首先采取行动。<br> </p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/zbpe-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=zbpe399&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_desktop-zbpe399_2.png?dHEiGouxxZpWwWTJE04dyGyUN74JOU2z" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_desktop-zbpe399_2.png?dHEiGouxxZpWwWTJE04dyGyUN74JOU2z" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/zbpe-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=zbpe399&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_mobile-zbpe399_2.png?qZnBcIGCJvPR8A2IAZOZuOxdoGZDatiI" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_mobile-zbpe399_2.png?qZnBcIGCJvPR8A2IAZOZuOxdoGZDatiI" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
