<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">15</span>日，本网获悉：发生于<span lang="EN-US">2018</span>年新疆库尔勒市“耶和华见证人”教案的基本情况，现通报如下：<span lang="EN-US">2018</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">21</span>日，于秉汝及多名基督教传道人、信徒骨干共计<span lang="EN-US">12</span>人被库尔勒市警方一同抓捕、刑拘；<span lang="EN-US">2018</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">26</span>日，其等<span lang="EN-US">12</span>人被库尔勒市检察院以涉嫌“组织、利用邪教组织破坏法律实施罪”正式批捕，并关押在库尔勒市看守所。<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">13</span>日，于秉汝等<span lang="EN-US">12</span>名基督徒被一审以“组织、利用邪教组织破坏法律实施罪”分别判处<span lang="EN-US">2</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月至<span lang="EN-US">6</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月不等刑期。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">以下是“耶和华见证人”教案之于秉汝的案情及简历：<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">于秉汝：</span></b><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">1966</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">17</span>日出生，曾用名于新学，广东省肇庆市籍人，住新维库尔勒市文化西路退水渠路国家电网家属院<span lang="EN-US">1</span>号楼，新疆百德教育服务咨询有限公司法人代表，基督徒，“耶和华见证人”骨干信徒，中国曾押良心犯。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2011</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">-2013</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月，曾因与“耶和华见证人”长老等多人一起在新疆巴音郭楞蒙古自治州（以下简称“巴州”）库尔勒市滨河路旁边的巴州医院家属院内敬拜聚会<span lang="EN-US">100</span>多次，发展圣经学生，<span lang="EN-US">2012</span>年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月，而被库尔勒市民宗委查获，给予警告处理，并将聚会点查封取缔；后因其宗教组织又将其及另几名长老和信徒的住处作为聚会点进行定期聚会<span lang="EN-US">100</span>余次，并设捐款箱鼓励信徒捐款，且每次收取信徒奉献款<span lang="EN-US">500</span>余元，其中将<span lang="EN-US">300</span>元寄给香港分部作为支撑全球传道工作，剩余奉献款用来制作书籍和宣传单张以向周边人士散发传教，积极宣传“政治中立”，不参加所谓爱国主义教育，遂被当地民宗委再次查获并予以驱离；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2012</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">-2015</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月，其等因再次利用自己的住处组织数百次宗教聚会，发展圣经学生多人，<span lang="EN-US">2018</span>年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">25</span>日，故又被库尔勒市民宗局给予行政处罚，并没收相关物品；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2016</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">-2018</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月，因其自愿再次将自己所属的百德教育服务咨询有限公司地址作为教会长期秘密聚会地点，并将信徒奉献款寄往海外以支持全球性传道工作，还经常在聚会时向信徒们发放宗教书籍、刊物及传道等数据，同时多次组织信徒利用周末时间向周边的亲朋好友、路人散发传单和名片，最后终被库尔勒当局发现，<span lang="EN-US">2018</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">21</span>日，其及多名传道人、信徒骨干共计<span lang="EN-US">12</span>人被库尔勒市警方一同抓捕、刑拘；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2018</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">26</span>日，其等<span lang="EN-US">12</span>人被库尔勒市检察院以涉嫌“组织、利用邪教组织破坏法律实施罪”正式批捕，并关押在库尔勒市看守所；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2019</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">24</span>日，其案被库尔勒市检察院提起公诉，后被很快移交到巴州库尔勒市法院；同年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">3</span>日<span lang="EN-US">-4</span>日、<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">9</span>日<span lang="EN-US">-15</span>日 、<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">5</span>日<span lang="EN-US">-7</span>日，其案在库尔勒市法院经三次庭审，因检察院指控其及五代“耶和华见证人”长老等多人员违反国家宗教法规，“建立库尔勒会众体系，发展成员<span lang="EN-US">63</span>人，蛊惑、煽动信徒不入党，不入团，不服兵役，不升国旗，不向国旗敬礼，不唱国歌，不参加选举，与境外人员勾结，利用定期聚会控制信徒，借机敛财，影响群众的正常宗教信仰，扰乱社会秩序，破坏法律实施”，故建议量刑严判；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2020</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">13</span>日，其等<span lang="EN-US">12</span>人终被一审以“组织、利用邪教组织破坏法律实施罪”统一判处<span lang="EN-US">2</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月至<span lang="EN-US">6</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月不等刑期，其中，于秉汝被判处有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">3</span>年，并处罚金<span lang="EN-US">15000</span>元；其等不服判决，均提起上诉；后经巴音郭楞蒙古自治州中级法院二审裁决，依然维持原判；<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">20</span>日，刑满获释。</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;
