font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">：<span lang="EN-US">1990</span>年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">11</span>日出生，新疆维吾尔自治区伊犁哈萨克自治州人，哈萨克族，伊宁县卡拉亚尕奇乡奥依曼布拉克村居民，曾任当地协警，哈萨克斯坦斯坦国绿卡持有者，中国在押政治犯。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">早期曾到上海市某高中上学；毕业后，<span lang="EN-US">2012</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月又曾前往中国邻国——哈萨克斯坦共和国（又称哈萨克斯坦斯坦国，简称“哈国”），在阿拉木图市大学攻读学位，并于同年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月返回中国新疆；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">被囚长达<span lang="EN-US">1</span>年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>个月后，因其对培训方式及内容等心存抵触，培训结果表现不佳，故不仅未被释放返家，反被正式逮捕；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2018</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">11</span>日，被新疆伊宁县法院一审以“煽动民族仇恨罪”重判有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">10</span>年，剥夺政治权利<span lang="EN-US">5</span>年，刑期至<span lang="EN-US">2028</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">19</span>日。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体;
