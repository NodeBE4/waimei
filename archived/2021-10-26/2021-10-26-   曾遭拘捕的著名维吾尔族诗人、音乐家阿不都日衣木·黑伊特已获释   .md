[曾遭拘捕的著名维吾尔族诗人、音乐家阿不都日衣木·黑伊特已获释](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2021/10/blog-post_99.html)
------

<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">26</span>日，本网获悉：于<span lang="EN-US">2017</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月曾遭拘捕的著名维吾尔族诗人、音乐家阿不都日衣木•黑伊特已获释。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">阿不都日衣木•黑伊特：</span></b><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">1964</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">1</span>日出生，新疆维吾尔自治区喀什市人，原北京中央民族歌舞团演员，新疆歌舞团演员，著名维吾尔族诗人，音乐家，维吾尔族人权捍卫者，中国曾押政治犯。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">因擅长演奏传统维吾尔族歌曲，尤其擅弹都塔尔琴，酷爱民族历史文化，备受维吾尔族民众的喜爱欢迎，在新疆维吾尔族及土耳其国等有较高知名度；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2017</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月，因创作歌曲《父亲》，歌词提及“战争的烈士”，遂触及中共当局敏感神经，很快被新疆喀什警方以有“伊斯兰极端主义倾向”而刑拘，后被以涉嫌“危害国家安全罪”转正式逮捕；<span lang="EN-US">2019</span>年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月，因有外媒传出其已死于新疆再教育营消息，中国官方立即回应称其仍然在世，并且“身体状况良好”；后悉，<span lang="EN-US">2019</span>年后，因在国际社会的关注下，其已经获释出狱。但目前应在居住小区被管控中。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"></p><div class="separator" style="clear: both;
