[遭抓捕的湖北维权人士毛善春取保申请遭拒](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2024/05/blog-post_59.html)
------

<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">28</span>日，本网获悉：湖北维权人士毛善春于<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">30</span>日因湖北省两会开幕被迫从武汉回湖北公安县老家过年，于<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">31</span>日被国保带走，随即关进了看守所，但未给家属任何法律手续。毛善春的老父亲在得知儿子被抓后悲愤交加，已于<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">15</span>日不幸去世。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">近日，湖北省公安县公安局承办人员告知<a name="_Hlk167793374">毛善春</a>家属称变更羁押可能有有人身危险性，不同意对毛善春取保。家属认为这也意料之中的事情。毛善春的父亲因为毛被关押自杀身亡，办案人员应该是怕毛善春出来予以报复而不敢释放。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">毛善春，<span lang="EN-US">1974</span>年生，湖北公民、秦永敏中国人权观察团队成员，籍贯湖北省荆州市公安县章庄铺镇毛家坪村人，曾于<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">03</span>月<span lang="EN-US">19</span>日公开发表了《关于退出中国共产党的声明》。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"></p><div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;"><a href="https://blogger.googleusercontent.com/img/b/R29vZ2xl/AVvXsEgxc4fmB14E4eo7J7X_2vhiv0fWSNGXq4Sj94hBzbEZ8toD56PmglMBlN42a50RNaDHYhWjAc-bKWftmCCtur3FPBqyUnLvY9oh8qmLjzXbGsJllb9vQiiKHypCd0ha8DsHpZsxWnX0feUNTdAvIfBZ-xL4PT7hfBg6n_LuZn2eHxgMcne_Iswk1LfgOsCy/s320/photo_2024-02-17_13-42-10.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;"><img border="0" data-original-height="241" data-original-width="320" height="241" src="https://blogger.googleusercontent.com/img/b/R29vZ2xl/AVvXsEgxc4fmB14E4eo7J7X_2vhiv0fWSNGXq4Sj94hBzbEZ8toD56PmglMBlN42a50RNaDHYhWjAc-bKWftmCCtur3FPBqyUnLvY9oh8qmLjzXbGsJllb9vQiiKHypCd0ha8DsHpZsxWnX0feUNTdAvIfBZ-xL4PT7hfBg6n_LuZn2eHxgMcne_Iswk1LfgOsCy/s1600/photo_2024-02-17_13-42-10.jpg" width="320" /></a></div><p></p>
