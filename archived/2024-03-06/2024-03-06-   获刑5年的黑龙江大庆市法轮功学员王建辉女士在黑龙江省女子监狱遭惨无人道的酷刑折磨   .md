<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">6</span>日，本网获悉：获刑<span lang="EN-US">5</span>年的黑龙江大庆市法轮功学员王建辉女士在黑龙江省女子监狱遭惨无人道的酷刑折磨。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><a name="_Hlk160624453"><b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">王建辉</span></b></a><b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">：</span></b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">黑龙江大庆市法轮功学员。女，<span lang="EN-US">1972</span>年生，大庆油田总医院职工，家住大庆市萨尔图区。在中共对法轮功持续至今二十多年的迫害中，王建辉屡遭中共人员绑架、抄家、关押、劳教、判刑等迫害：<span lang="EN-US">2001</span>年被劳教<span lang="EN-US">3</span>年，关押在黑龙江省女子戒毒劳教所：<span lang="EN-US">2010</span>年被判刑<span lang="EN-US">6</span>年。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">2020</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">22</span>日被大庆市萨尔图区铁人公安分局便衣警察抓捕，关押在大庆市第二看守所，遭构陷，<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">3</span>日被判刑<span lang="EN-US">5</span>年并罚款三万元。刑期至：<span lang="EN-US">2025</span>年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">21</span>日。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">2022</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">7</span>日，王建辉被转监到黑龙江省女子监狱八监区。王建辉在八监区遭受监狱指使的包夹犯人惨无人道的酷刑折磨，甚至将厕所拖布塞其嘴、反复碾踩其十趾。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">对王建辉女士狱中境况，本网将持续关注。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p>
