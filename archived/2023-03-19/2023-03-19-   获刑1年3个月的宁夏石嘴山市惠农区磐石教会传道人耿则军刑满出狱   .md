[获刑1年3个月的宁夏石嘴山市惠农区磐石教会传道人耿则军刑满出狱](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2023/03/13_19.html)
------

<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">19</span>日，本网获悉：今天九点多，获刑<span lang="EN-US">1</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>个月的宁夏石嘴山市惠农区磐石教会传道人耿则军刑满出狱，远道而来的基督徒在看守所大门口守候，大家含泪相拥。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">耿则军：</span></b><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">1972</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">31</span>日出生，宁夏回族自治区石嘴山市人，基督徒，石嘴山市惠农区盘石教会传道人，中国良心犯。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">耿则军因在宁夏回族自治区石嘴山市惠农区盘石教会做全职传道人，其所在教会属归正福音家庭教会，拒不接受有官方“三自教会”管理，遂长期遭石嘴山市当局打压；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2021</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">5</span>日，因其家庭教会坚持一贯性的聚会（即每个礼拜天<span lang="EN-US">/</span>星期天 的主日敬拜），正当其及教会众信徒分别在惠农区育才路<span lang="EN-US">140</span>号、静安一区<span lang="EN-US">3-3-402</span>号举行聚会时，突遭石嘴山市惠农区警方、两地派出所民警及宗教局人员联合冲击，除在场老年人被登记之后准予送回住处外，其等多人均被抓捕；次日，其被警方以“扰乱社会秩序”为由处行政拘留<span lang="EN-US">15</span>天，并送是最上市行政拘留所关押；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">另有其妻子郝瑛和教友罗时平、叶宁芳、朱俊英及王素芳等<span lang="EN-US">5</span>人亦被警方以同罪由处行政拘留<span lang="EN-US">10</span>天；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2022</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">4</span>日，其再次被传唤，后被嘴山市惠农区警方以涉嫌“组织、资助非法聚集罪”刑拘；同年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">25</span>日，又被嘴山市惠农区检察院以涉嫌“组织非法聚集罪”正式批捕，指控其因多次在惠农区多地“利用基督教进行非法聚会”，严重扰乱了社会秩序，故其行为触犯了政府相关法规；后其案被地方检察院提起公诉，又移交至石嘴山市惠农区法院；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2022</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">13</span>日，其案在嘴山市惠农区法院开庭审理；庭审中，其坚持不认罪，被判刑<span lang="EN-US">1</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>个月。刑期至<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">19</span>日。</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;">此前被羁押于石嘴山市看守所</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;">。</span></p><p class="MsoNormal"></p><div class="separator" style="clear: both;
