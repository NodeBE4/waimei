<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">22</span>日，本网获悉：广州圣经归正教会会友张强被以寻衅滋事罪判刑<span lang="EN-US">2</span>年。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">广州圣经归正教会会友张强因质疑广州市公安局政治部主任李世全不是因公牺牲，于<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月底被广州市公安局海珠分局以涉嫌寻衅滋事为由立案侦查，<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月被广州市海珠区人民检察院以涉嫌寻衅滋事起诉，<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月被广州市海珠区人民法院判决寻衅滋事罪成立，处以有期徒刑二年，<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月底已经上诉到广州市中级人民法院。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">目前，二审待判。张强坚持自己无罪，辩护人亦作无罪辩护。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p>
