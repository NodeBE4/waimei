[南昌法轮功学员、原南昌市血站护师付金凤女士遭判刑入狱](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2024/06/blog-post_44.html)
------

<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">13</span>日，本网获悉：南昌法轮功学员、原南昌市血站护师付金凤遭判刑入狱。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">付金凤：</span></b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">江西南昌法轮功学员，女，原南昌市血站护师。<span lang="EN-US">1962</span>年生，现年今年<span lang="EN-US">62</span>岁，原南昌市血站护师，她于一九九七年十一月开始修炼法轮大法。在中共邪党对法轮功的疯狂迫害中，她多次被抓捕、关押，曾两次遭劳教、两次遭判刑，在看守所、洗脑班、劳教所、监狱遭受迫害。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">付金凤女士<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">24</span>日宣讲法轮功时遭人举报，被南昌市经开区蛟桥派出所警察抓捕。之后一直被关押在南昌市第一看守所。<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月，付金凤已遭秘密判刑，刑期待查。目前付金凤已被转监到江西省女子监狱关押迫害。这是她第三次遭中共法院非法判刑。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"></p><div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;"><a href="https://blogger.googleusercontent.com/img/b/R29vZ2xl/AVvXsEgTrvsNLtEdzpxpU-4XYdJEByw-9WVXzIh9yn7Zo5ZlFLr8_NjbCdb4jWxE4X_XNWvR5zGA8YWqGRdHNyY0KF3jpDq8X86Ix26Fu9S9CSK9S_i3VmFSwBtR5x0arfixjgi_Bt2m_bor_3FWyFQJ3OSVacmx27GaICKgN2aAuX99MTCWo5W_GB5ckzSkmjVu/s300/2024-6-12-201715-0.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;"><img border="0" data-original-height="300" data-original-width="226" height="300" src="https://blogger.googleusercontent.com/img/b/R29vZ2xl/AVvXsEgTrvsNLtEdzpxpU-4XYdJEByw-9WVXzIh9yn7Zo5ZlFLr8_NjbCdb4jWxE4X_XNWvR5zGA8YWqGRdHNyY0KF3jpDq8X86Ix26Fu9S9CSK9S_i3VmFSwBtR5x0arfixjgi_Bt2m_bor_3FWyFQJ3OSVacmx27GaICKgN2aAuX99MTCWo5W_GB5ckzSkmjVu/s1600/2024-6-12-201715-0.jpg" width="226" /></a></div><br /><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;"><br /></span><p></p>
