[Linux 5.17 释出](https://www.solidot.org/story?sid=71011)
------

<div>
Linus Torvalds 在内核开发者邮件列表上<a href="https://lore.kernel.org/lkml/CAHk-=wj4fFjx2pgbGNBM4wJs3=eReZ05EQyprzgT2Jv8qJ2vJg@mail.gmail.com/T/#u">宣布</a>释出 Linux 5.17。主要新特性包括： arm64 架构支持 <a href="https://www.kernel.org/doc/html/latest/dev-tools/kcsan.html">KCSAN</a>，支持<a href="https://lwn.net/Articles/837566/">递归 ID-mapped 文件系统挂载</a>，新 AMD P-State 驱动，改进随机数生成器，重写网络文件系统缓存后端，Real-Time Linux Analysis (RTLA)工具，缓解 <a href="https://lwn.net/Articles/877845/" target="_blank">straight-line 预测攻击</a>，<a href="https://lwn.net/Articles/877062/">bpf_loop() helper</a>，<a href="https://lwn.net/Articles/877603/" target="_blank">参考计数跟踪设施</a>，等等，更多可浏览 <a href="https://kernelnewbies.org/Linux_5.17">KernelNewbies 5.17</a>。
