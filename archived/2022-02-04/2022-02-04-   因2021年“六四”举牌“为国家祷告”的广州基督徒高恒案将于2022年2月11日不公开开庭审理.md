<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">4</span>日，本网获悉：因<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年“六四”举牌“为国家祷告”的广州基督徒高恒案将于<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">11</span>日不公开开庭审理。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">高恒：</span></b><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">1984</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">1</span>日，曾因在儿童节当天举牌呼吁“尊重生命”、“反对堕胎”，而令当局不满，次日即被广州市番禺区南浦岛警方强行要求房东对其驱离，后因其及房东持守立场，顶住压力，终未妥协；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">4</span>日，“六四事件”第<span lang="EN-US">32</span>周年受难者纪念日，因其在广州地铁<span lang="EN-US">2</span>号线某地铁站举牌“六月四日，为国家祷告”，并将相关图片发于网上，同时在微博上写下：“一切社会层面上的苦难，虽然往往被归结于制度与政治的丑恶，然而，究其根本，它无不源于人心中根深蒂固的罪性。就这一罪性而言，我们与屠杀的决定者、指挥者和执行者，并无根本的不同。感谢主，我们虽同为罪人，却蒙了从上帝而来的奇异恩典，悔改认罪，接受耶稣为主，从而成为上帝的儿女”等心得，很快被广东网警发现告发，遂顿遭广州当局强力打击；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体;
