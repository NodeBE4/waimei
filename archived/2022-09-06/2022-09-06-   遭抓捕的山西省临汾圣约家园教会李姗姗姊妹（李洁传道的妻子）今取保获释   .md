<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">6</span>日，本网获悉：此前被指定监视居住的山西省临汾圣约家园教会李姗姗姊妹（李洁传道的妻子）今取保获释。临汾圣约家园教会李姗姗自<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">19</span>日因涉嫌“诈骗罪”被指定居所监视居住，<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">6</span>日被取保候审，现得以与两个孩子团聚。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2022</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;
