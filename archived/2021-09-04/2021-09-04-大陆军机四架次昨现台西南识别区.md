[大陆军机四架次昨现台西南识别区](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210904-1190236)
------

<div>
<p>昨天是台湾九三军人节，大陆解放军当天上午派遣轰6K、运八等型战机共四架次，出现在台湾西南防空识别区（ADIZ），台湾空军采派遣空中巡逻兵力应对、广播驱离、防空飞弹追监等因应措施。</p><p>根据台湾国防部官网“即时军事动态”昨晚发布的西南空域空情动态，解放军昨天派遣运八电侦机一架次、运八反潜机一架次、轰6K轰炸机两架次，出现在西南防空识别区。</p><p>从公布的飞航轨迹看，运八电侦机切台湾防空识别区的最西南角往返，运八反潜机与轰6K则由福建与广东两省省界出海、由海峡中线以南进入西南防空识别区作往返飞行。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/zbpe-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=zbpe399&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_desktop-zbpe399_2.png?dHEiGouxxZpWwWTJE04dyGyUN74JOU2z" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_desktop-zbpe399_2.png?dHEiGouxxZpWwWTJE04dyGyUN74JOU2z" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/zbpe-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=zbpe399&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_mobile-zbpe399_2.png?qZnBcIGCJvPR8A2IAZOZuOxdoGZDatiI" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_mobile-zbpe399_2.png?qZnBcIGCJvPR8A2IAZOZuOxdoGZDatiI" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
