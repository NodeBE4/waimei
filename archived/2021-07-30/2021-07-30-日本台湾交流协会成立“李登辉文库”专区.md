[日本台湾交流协会成立“李登辉文库”专区](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210730-1175613)
------

<div>
<p>台湾前总统李登辉逝世周年之际，日本台湾交流协会今天成立“李登辉文库”专区，专区内有约100本由李登辉收藏的中文、日文书籍。</p><p>根据联合报报道，交流协会今天在面簿上表示，台北事务所图书室正式设立了“李登辉文库”专区，他们感谢李登辉基金会提供共计约100本由李登辉所收藏的中日文书籍。</p><p>交协表示，李登辉是位饱读诗书的贤者，希望那些和李登辉共度了许多光阴的贵重书籍，能够成为指引众多读者们的灯塔，同时也透过阅读将这份日本和台湾的羁绊传承下去。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>交协还表示，四季更迭，天上人间，一年过去了。“李登辉先生，我们很想念您，此后也请您继续引领我们，让我们能够长长久久的维系这份难能可贵的日台友情”。<br> </p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
