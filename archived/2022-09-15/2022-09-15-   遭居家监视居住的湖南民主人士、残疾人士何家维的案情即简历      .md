[遭居家监视居住的湖南民主人士、残疾人士何家维的案情即简历](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2022/09/blog-post_44.html)

<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">15</span>日，本网获悉遭居家监视居住的湖南民主人士、残疾人士何家维的案情即简历，现通报如下：<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">何家维：</span></b><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">1969</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">30</span>日出生，本名何峻辉，湖南省株洲市人，株洲市芦淞区纺织路<span lang="EN-US">18</span>号<span lang="EN-US">1</span>栋<span lang="EN-US">802</span>号公民，基督徒，八九六四亲历者，残疾人士，三无人员，民主维权公民，公民行动践行者。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">近年来，因热心投入社会运动和公民抗争行动，而曾参与过多起公共事件的现场围观，并常以举牌、拉横幅等方式进行网络声援，随屡遭当局维稳监控与打压；<span lang="EN-US">2015</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月，曾因突患脑血栓，经治疗后留下后遗症，导致半身不遂行动不便，又兼患有较为严重的糖尿病，每天必须注射胰岛素维持生理平衡，但仍坚持参与各种围观声援行动；曾因长期关注公民权利、宣传《世界人权公约》、帮助其他公民维权，而被湖南警方多次抓捕、维稳；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2017</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">10</span>日<span lang="EN-US">-14</span>日，曾因在“国际人权日”与维权公民陈思明等人来到株洲市中级法院门前举牌要求“司法独立”，而被株洲市警方带走，做笔录扣押 ；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">同年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">25</span>日，又因与陈思明 、谢周等维权公民前往湖南省邵阳市大山岭陵园凭吊已故“民主斗士”李旺阳，而被邵阳市警方抓捕，之后被驱离大山岭陵园；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2018</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">29</span>日，曾因参与声援<span lang="EN-US">709</span>系列案件，如屠夫吴淦、谢阳以及江天勇等人案（均属于“煽动颠覆国家政权”案或“颠覆国家政权”案）的活动，而被株洲市警方以涉嫌“其他危害治安管理行为”为由带走传唤，后又被以“寻衅滋事”为由处行政拘留<span lang="EN-US">5</span>天；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">同年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">26</span>日，曾因去天津参与旁听“<span lang="EN-US">709</span>”王全璋案开庭，而被警方拦截带回，后其家人受要挟，强令其妹及妹夫负责对其监管（尤指“两会”、“六四”等敏日期的言行），禁止其离开住所，否则工作单位对其工资奖金的发放及工作升迁等问题受限制；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2022</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">7</span>日<span lang="EN-US">-9</span>日，因要去广州围观王爱忠案开庭，在高铁站购买车票时被实名制锁定，后被送芦淞区贺家土派出所扣押；<span lang="EN-US">2</span>天后其将被转为刑拘，但株洲市芦淞区警方因鉴于其身体状况极差，遂于<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">9</span>日改为以涉嫌“掩饰隐瞒犯罪所得罪”监视居住；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">原定<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">15</span>日其案将在株洲市芦淞区法院<span lang="EN-US">1</span>楼审判厅开庭，但庭审前一日又被取消，称将择日再审。目前被监视居住于家中。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"></p><div class="separator" style="clear: both;
