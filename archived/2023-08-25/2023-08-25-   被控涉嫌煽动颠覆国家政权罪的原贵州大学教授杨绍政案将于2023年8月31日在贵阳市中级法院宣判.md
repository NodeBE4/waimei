[被控涉嫌煽动颠覆国家政权罪的原贵州大学教授杨绍政案将于2023年8月31日在贵阳市中级法院宣判](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2023/08/2023831.html)
------

<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">25</span>日，本网获悉：被控涉嫌煽动颠覆国家政权罪的原贵州大学教授杨绍政案将于<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">31</span>日在贵阳市中级法院宣判。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">杨绍政教授是于<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">18</span>日被国保秘密抓捕，以煽颠罪指定监居半年后批捕，后关押在贵阳市第一看守所。<span lang="EN-US">2022 7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">29</span>日在贵阳市中级人民法院开庭，至今未判。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">杨绍政教授不认为自己有任何违法犯罪行为，杨绍政教授认为如果任由掌握公权的人不受制约的枉法滥权，每一个中国人都有可能受到自己所受到的可怕遭遇。此前杨绍政教授的辩护律师曾代理控告办案人员刑讯逼供、虐待被监管人，贵阳市人民检察院答复称控告不属实。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">杨绍政：</span></b><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">1969</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">12</span>日出生，四川省巴中市人，成都市温江区学府尚郡暂住居民，中国农工党党员，西南财经大学经济学博士，前重庆工商大学教师，原贵州大学经济学院教授、硕士生导师和贵州大学高等教育评估与研究专家，贵州大学经济类学科首个被引进人才，中国知名经济学者，民主异见人士，中国在押政治犯。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">自<span lang="EN-US">1991</span>年大学毕业参加工作后，又于<span lang="EN-US">2006</span>年起进入贵州大学任教，被该校视为经济类学科首个引进的人才以来，一直深得该校的好评和荣耀，然之后因其经常发表令中共当局敏感和忌讳的政治言论，随屡遭贵州当局的监控与维稳打压；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2017</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">10</span>日，曾因网发揭露中共党政人员每年耗费<span lang="EN-US">20</span>万亿元人民币等的相关信息，而被贵州大学叫停授课，且此前就曾因涉政言论而两次被贵州省公安厅约谈和被明确警告在中共<span lang="EN-US">19</span>大期间“闭嘴”；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2018</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">16</span>日，其被贵州大学以发表敏感言论为由正式开除；<span lang="EN-US">2019</span>年“六四”期间，曾因其在微信群讲<span lang="EN-US">38</span>军军长徐勤先的司机披露说六四杀了<span lang="EN-US">3000-5000</span>名学生，随立刻被贵州省政法委、贵州省网信办及多名警察以“寻衅滋事”为由传唤；传唤中，遭虐待式审讯长达<span lang="EN-US">8</span>个多小时，双手被戴手铐、脚镣，和固定在老虎椅子上，并被要求其衣服脱光、抽血、采集十指指纹及尿检等；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2019</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年中共“十·一”期间，曾又遭贵州省政法委控制而软禁多日；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2021</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">18</span>日，因杨绍政频发政治敏感言论，虽遭警方警告也不停息，遂再次被贵州省贵阳市花溪区警方秘密带走。自此失联；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">对杨绍政先生的境况，本网将持续关注。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"></p><div class="separator" style="clear: both;
