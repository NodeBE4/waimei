<p></p><p class="MsoNormal"><b><span></span></b></p><a name='more'></a><b><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">当事人：韩良&nbsp; &nbsp;</span></b><b><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">一号证人兼法律顾问：谢丹</span></b></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">重庆公民韩良，被控“寻衅滋事罪”，被秘密抓捕，秘密审判，秘密服刑<span lang="EN-US">3</span>年半，于<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">5</span>日刑满后，又被秘密非法拘禁一个月，终于在<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">4</span>日美国独立日回到家中。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">审看针对韩良的《起诉书》、《一审判决书》、《二审裁定书》后，韩良及其法律顾问谢丹现发表如下声明：<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">一、针对韩良的抓捕、审判，从相关证据和以上法律文书来看，严重违反事实和相关法律规定；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">二、韩良涉案的行为，均系公民合法的言论自由权利，无任何违法犯罪嫌疑；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">三、即使执行机关和司法机关认为韩良上述行为违法或犯罪，涉案案件当时均已经公安机关按行政案件处理完毕。按“一罪不二罚”的原则，均不应再次提出指控和进行处罚；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">四、本法律顾问谢丹，被上述法律文书列为“一号证人”。据回忆，韩良一案，重庆市渝中区刑事侦察支队曾粗略来向谢丹本人进行询问，并作笔录。除了明确与韩良相互认识，并且是微信好友之外，谢丹未提供其它任何信息。谢丹是如何成为本案“一号证人”的？相关证据和证言笔录必须全部如实公开公布！韩良和谢丹均表示放弃涉案个人隐私保护。事实是谢丹曾多次向办案机关要求担任韩良的代理人，韩良也多次要求委托谢丹担任其辩护人，均不被允许。甚至本是公开审理的本案，连庭审也不允许旁听；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">五、办案机关甚至违法阻止韩良家属探视；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">六、刑满之后继续非法拘禁一个月，是严重的违法犯罪行为！<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">以上为韩良和谢丹郑重声明。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">我们保留依法进一步追究相关机构和个人责任的合法权利。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;
