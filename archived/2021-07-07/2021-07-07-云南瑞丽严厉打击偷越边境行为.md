[云南瑞丽严厉打击偷越边境行为](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210707-1165818)
------

<div>
<p>云南省瑞丽市今天召开新闻发布会介绍疫情防控最新情况，强调要严厉打击偷越国（边）境行为，严惩偷渡者及其组织、协助、容留者，持续强化边境管控措施。</p><p>根据央视新闻报道，瑞丽市委书记翟玉龙在会上指出，云南瑞丽持续强化边境管控措施。严厉打击偷越国（边）境行为，严惩偷渡者及其组织、协助、容留者。加快推进边境立体化防控体系建设，全面提升边境地区社会治理能力现代化水平，围绕管住人、管住村、管住通道、管住证件、管住边境线五个方面，切实将“五个管住”工作落到实处。</p><p>云南省昨天新增本土确诊病例15例、无症状感染者两例。新增境外输入无症状感染者一例。新增病例中，三名为中国籍，其余均为缅甸籍，且均为瑞丽市全员核酸检测中发现。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>瑞丽市主城区已从今天零时起实行封闭管理，全员居家隔离。<br> </p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
