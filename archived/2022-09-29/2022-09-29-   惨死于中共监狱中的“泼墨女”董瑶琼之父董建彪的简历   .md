[惨死于中共监狱中的“泼墨女”董瑶琼之父董建彪的简历](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2022/09/blog-post_72.html)

<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">29</span>日，本网获悉惨死于中共监狱中的“泼墨女”董瑶琼之父董建彪的简历，现通报如下：<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">董建彪：</span></b><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">1967</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">6</span>日出生，湖南省株洲市攸县人，株洲市攸县桃水镇谢家坪村万坪组万坪<span lang="EN-US">002</span>号村民，原湖南省耒阳市源江山煤矿工人，原湖南源江山煤矿“<span lang="EN-US">11</span>•<span lang="EN-US">29</span>”重大透水事故<span lang="EN-US">2</span>名成功逃生者之一，“泼墨女孩”董瑶琼之父，维权公民，中国曾押维权人士。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2018</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">4</span>日，曾因其女董瑶琼（人称“泼墨女孩”）借用推特（<span lang="EN-US">Twitter</span>）在上海市海航大厦前直播对中共独裁专制不满，抨击当局洗脑民众，并朝习近平头像泼墨汁，而被上海警方以“攻击国家领导人”为由抓捕，后又于<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">16</span>日被秘送回湖南省攸县老家，强制关押在株洲市第三医院，遂其为寻找女儿下落而奋力奔走呼号，直至同年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">1</span>日才在医院与女儿相见，并获知被抓因由；因其与前妻（又称董母）之间，为是否坚信其女没有精神病、是否有必要与官方配合而签署让女儿被关进精神病医院之协议等问题产生了极大分歧，遂与前妻多次发生争执；后其女曾一度获释，但很快又被失踪，秘押于精神病院里遭酷刑折磨及摧残，而其在外努力营救女儿及为之讨公道的过程中，也曾几经株洲市攸县警方软禁和威胁；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2021</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月初，其女第三次被失踪；其因女儿本次被失踪与董母自愿签署将女儿送进精神病院的协议有直接关系，故二人又起激烈纷争；同年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">12</span>日，因急生恨，其拿着煤气罐前往前妻住处，威胁说要烧前妻的房子，前妻报警，遂其当天被当地攸县警方拘捕，并于同年快速被株洲市攸县法院以“寻衅滋事罪”判刑<span lang="EN-US">3</span>年，而后送湖南省茶陵监狱服刑；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2022</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">24</span>日，其女儿的堂兄突然外曝，其已于<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">23</span>日惨死监狱，死因不明，并称警方虽只让亲属匆匆看了一眼其遗体就被迅速带离，但能明显看见其尸体遍身都有被打的伤痕，而且肛门出血，眼睛未闭；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">次日，其遗体在未经亲属的同意下，被官方诡异而迅速火化，至<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">26</span>日，其遗体骨灰已被强制安葬；同期，其爱女董瑶琼自第三次被失踪后，至今依然杳无音讯。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体;
