mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">7</span>日，本网获悉：<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">5</span>日，河南省许昌市魏都区郭楼村<span lang="EN-US">3</span>组村民张金离因《存折一本通》内的部分存款不翼而飞，向许昌市魏都区人民法院提起民事诉讼，该法院不予受理，但不作出“不予受理”的裁定，明显属于程序违法。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">张金离持有河南省农村信用社《存折一本通》编号为：<span lang="EN-US">075060152161</span>，开户和发折机构皆为许昌市魏都区北郊农村信用合作社。该《存折一本通》显示：<span lang="EN-US">2014</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">4</span>日取现<span lang="EN-US">11</span>万元。但张金离并未到该信用社处办理取款手续，也未提取此款。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">张金离系拆迁户，拆迁方将拆迁补偿款项打入上述账户，但又莫名其妙地被他人提取了。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">张金离认为，该信用社没有仔细审查取款人的证件及其签字，导致此款被冒领，故应由该信用社赔偿张金离的损失。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">令人感到不可思议的是，张金离多次到许昌魏都法院请求立案。但该法院不予立案，百般推卸。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p>
