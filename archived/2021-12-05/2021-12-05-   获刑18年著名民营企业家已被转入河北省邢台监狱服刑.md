<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">5</span>日，本网获悉：获刑<span lang="EN-US">18</span>年著名民营企业家已被转入河北省邢台监狱服刑。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">1954</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">8</span>日月出生，曾用名孙大悟，河北省保定市徐水区人，保定市徐水区郎五庄村居民，河北大午农牧集团有限公司创办人、原董事长、现监事长，大午学校校长，前保定市人大代表，前保定市禽蛋产业联合会理事长，原中国农业大学农民问题研究所高级研究员，著名民营企业家，维权公民，良知人士，中国在押政治犯。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">1985</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年，曾以<span lang="EN-US">1,000</span>只鸡和<span lang="EN-US">50</span>头猪起家，创立大午集团（后被称为中国五百大私营企业之一）；<span lang="EN-US">2003</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">31</span>日，曾因在大午网站发表《小康社会的建设及难点》、《悼念李慎之》、《两位民间商人关于中国的时局及历史的对话》等文章，被控严重损害国家机关形象，网站随后遭停业整顿<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月，罚款<span lang="EN-US">15000</span>元，此事曾在中国法学界引起广泛热议；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2003</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">29</span>日，曾因被控向<span lang="EN-US">3000</span>多户农民发放借款逾<span lang="EN-US">1.8</span>亿元，被河北省当局以涉嫌“非法集资、非法持有弹药” 罪由刑拘，后被地方法院以“非法吸收公众存款罪”判刑<span lang="EN-US">3</span>年、缓刑<span lang="EN-US">4</span>年，罚金<span lang="EN-US">10</span>万元，其集团公司亦被罚款<span lang="EN-US">30</span>万元；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2020</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月—<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月，因大午集团员工为阻止徐水国营农场强拆大午公司房屋，而与强拆方及徐水区警方发生了两次冲突（后被称为“<span lang="EN-US">6.21</span>事件”和“<span lang="EN-US">8.4</span>事件”），事发之后，大午集团为求公道随召开职工大会，公开抗议地方当局的打压行为；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">同年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">11</span>日，大午集团公司高层主管及亲属、幕僚等<span lang="EN-US">28</span>人突遭河北省高碑店市警方抓捕，其亦被以涉嫌“寻衅滋事罪和破坏生产经营罪”指定居所监视居住，自此失联；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">据悉，此次大抓捕与大午集团公司和徐水国营农场土地发生纠纷，维权遭报复有关，但另有深层原因，即亦与<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月其在网上对许志永等公义人士及维权律师表达赞慕之情有关；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2021</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">21</span>日，被河北省高碑店市警方以涉嫌“非法吸收公众存款罪、寻衅滋事罪、非法采矿罪”等八宗罪正式逮捕，且被关押于高碑店市看守所；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">同年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">5</span>日，其多人案被高碑店市检察院移交至高碑店市法院；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2021</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">15</span>日，其一行<span lang="EN-US">20</span>余人案统一在河北省高碑店市法院开庭审理；<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">28</span>日，终被该法院一审予以宣判：其中，其被以“非法吸收公众存款罪（判<span lang="EN-US">5</span>年）、寻衅滋事罪（判<span lang="EN-US">1</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月）、非法采矿罪（判<span lang="EN-US">1</span>年）、聚众冲击国家机关罪（判<span lang="EN-US">2</span>年）、非法占用农用地罪（判<span lang="EN-US">5</span>年）、强迫交易罪（判<span lang="EN-US">4</span>年）、破坏生产经营罪（判<span lang="EN-US">3</span>年）、妨害公务罪（判<span lang="EN-US">6</span>年）”，数罪并罚，最终执行有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">18</span>年，并处罚金<span lang="EN-US">311</span>万元；其等不服，均提起上诉；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2021</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">31</span>日，经保定市中级法院二审裁决，除其二儿子孙福硕由<span lang="EN-US">1</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月改判缓刑、纪玮莲略减成<span lang="EN-US">1</span>年实刑外，其余人等均驳回上诉，维持原判；其刑期至<span lang="EN-US">2038</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月。目前孙大午已经被转入河北省邢台监狱服刑。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"></p><div class="separator" style="clear: both;
