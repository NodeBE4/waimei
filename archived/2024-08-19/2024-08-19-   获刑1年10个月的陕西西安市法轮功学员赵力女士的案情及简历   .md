<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">20</span>日，本网获悉获刑<span lang="EN-US">1</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>个月的陕西西安市法轮功学员赵力女士的案情及简历，现在通报如下：<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">赵力：</span></b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">陕西西安市法轮功学员，女，<span lang="EN-US">1958</span>年生，<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年六十六岁。家住陕西省西安市雁塔区，她近年到重庆帮女儿带孩子。赵力女士于一九九七年开始修炼法轮功。中共一九九九年七月开始迫害法轮功，赵力也遭到中共的绑架、关押，曾于<span lang="EN-US">2000</span>年被劳动教养<span lang="EN-US">1</span>年半、<span lang="EN-US">2016</span>年被判刑<span lang="EN-US">2</span>年半。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">赵力女士因邮寄一封宣传法轮功的信，<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">20</span>日在重庆被警察抓捕、抄家，对其刑事拘留，但当晚让她以“取保候审”回家。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">2023</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">17</span>日，赵力被重庆市江北区法院判刑<span lang="EN-US">1</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>个月，随即遭收监被关押在重庆市渝北区看守所。<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年初，赵力被转监到重庆市女子监狱，至今已八个月，狱方一直不让家属接见，也不让给生活费。家人不知赵力近况，非常担忧。刑期至：<span lang="EN-US">2025</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">16</span>日。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p>
