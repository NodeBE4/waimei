mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">贾小玉为了儿子的治疗费和生存保障于<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">1</span>日在国家信访局信访出来后，被顺平县卫健公安黑保安暴力截访回去。随即被顺平县公安连续三次拘留直至被逮捕。在羁押<span lang="EN-US">10</span>个月后，于<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">27</span>日被保定顺平县法院判刑<span lang="EN-US">4</span>年，对疫苗受害儿童家长予以残酷迫害和镇压。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;
