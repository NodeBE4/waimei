<p></p><span><a name='more'></a></span><p class="MsoNormal"><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">18</span>日，本网获悉因言获罪<span lang="EN-US">2</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月的王尊建的案情及简历，现通报如下：<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;"><b>王尊建：</b><span lang="EN-US">1972</span>年出生，湖北省孝感市人，网络活跃人士。因追求民主理念，<span lang="EN-US">2017</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月—<span lang="EN-US">2019</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月，曾多次积极参与敏感案件及事件的现场围观，并在在网上发布维权声援信息等；曾多次在网上发帖，传播中共内部特定人物（主指高层领导者等）和特定事件等的相关信息，从而被湖北省孝感市当局视为需要维稳监控的重点对象；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">尤其是，<span lang="EN-US">2017</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">29</span>日，曾在武汉市青山公安分局门口参与声援秦永敏涉嫌“煽动颠覆国家政权罪”一案，<span lang="EN-US">2018</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">24</span>日，曾转发江苏省镇江市老兵维权的事件，<span lang="EN-US">2019</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月，曾转发和发布大量香港“反送中运动”的各种信息，发布纪念六四<span lang="EN-US">30</span>周年的相关言论等等，遂引起中共当局的极度不满与强烈打压；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2019</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">4</span>日，被孝感市云梦县警方行政拘留<span lang="EN-US">15</span>天，同年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">18</span>日，又被以“寻衅滋事” 罪由转刑事拘留，并被关押在孝感市云梦县第一看守所；同年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">21</span>日，被云梦县检察院以同罪由予以批捕，指控其因多次转发和发布中共“特定人物”的负面信息，“辱骂、诋毁党和国家领导人”，“利用网络平台破坏社会管理秩序，情节恶劣”，故而构成寻衅滋事罪；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2020</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">16</span>日，被湖北省孝感市云梦县法院一审以“寻衅滋事罪”判处有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">2</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月；其不服上诉；后经孝感市中级法院二审裁决，依然维持原判；<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">3</span>日，刑满释放。</span></p><p class="MsoNormal"></p><div class="separator" style="clear: both;
