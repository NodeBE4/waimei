[山西“知名法律人士”郝劲松案将于2021年10月12至13日在山西定襄县法院开庭审理](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2021/10/blog-post_82.html)
------

<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">10</span>日，本网获悉：山西“知名法律人士”郝劲松案将于<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">12</span>至<span lang="EN-US">13</span>日在山西定襄县法院开庭审理。其代理律师、辩护人常伯阳律师近日已经收到郝劲松涉嫌寻衅滋事、诽谤、诈骗案即将开庭审理的山西省定襄县法院的两份开庭通知书。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">郝劲松</span></b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">于<span lang="EN-US">2019</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">10</span>日、<span lang="EN-US">11</span>日就连续两天遭到户籍地山西省定襄县晋昌镇派出所以“寻衅滋事”为由传唤后，<span lang="EN-US">2019</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">17</span>日再遭该派出所传唤。随后被行政拘留<span lang="EN-US">15</span>天。<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">2</span>日，行政拘留期满被转刑事拘留，控罪为涉嫌寻衅滋事。当日，郝劲松被控涉嫌寻衅滋事遭定襄县公安局刑事拘留。羁押于五台县看守所。案件久不审理，一直拖延至今。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">郝劲松：</span></b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">生于<span lang="EN-US">1972</span>年，山西忻州人。著名人权捍卫者。郝劲松自<span lang="EN-US">2004</span>年开始先后<span lang="EN-US">7</span>次将国家税务总局、北京地铁运营公司、北京铁路局告上法庭，原他因对“打破行业‘霸王条款’起到了一定作用”而入选“<span lang="EN-US">2004</span>年构建经济和谐十大受尊崇人物”。郝劲松被先后提名为<span lang="EN-US">2005</span>中国法制新闻人物，<span lang="EN-US">2005</span>年度十大法制人物，并以“维权战士”的身份写入《<span lang="EN-US">2005</span>中国法治蓝皮书》。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">因介入推动铁道部春运不涨价案件入选《<span lang="EN-US">2006</span>中国法治蓝皮书》，并当选<span lang="EN-US">2006</span>中国十大消费维权人物，<span lang="EN-US">2008</span>年因介入陕西华南虎案件和杨佳案被《南方人物周刊》评选为“中国魅力人物——公义之魅”。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2009</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年，介入上海“钓鱼执法事件”。后成立山西定襄县劲松法务咨询中心。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2017</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年，郝劲松向定襄县环保局实名举报当地的诚泰纺织印染公司涉嫌污染空气和水源。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体;
