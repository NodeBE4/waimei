font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">14</span>日，当其为求维权、投诉、讨公道而准备长居北京，一边打工一边上访之时，又被固镇县警方抓回，并被以涉嫌“寻衅滋事罪”刑拘；后被固镇县检察院以同罪由正式批捕和起诉；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: left;"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体;
