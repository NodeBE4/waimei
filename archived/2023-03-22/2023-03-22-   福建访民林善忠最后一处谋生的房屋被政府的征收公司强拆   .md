[福建访民林善忠最后一处谋生的房屋被政府的征收公司强拆](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2023/03/blog-post_23.html)

<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">22</span>日，本网获悉：前天（<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">20</span>日）下午<span lang="EN-US">15</span>点，正在福建省福州市马尾区打工的晋安区鼓山镇访民林善忠，突然接到辖区派出所片警与鼓山镇信访办主任庄辉的电话，通知他回家谈房屋拆迁事宜。昨天下午，又有手机号<span lang="EN-US">18108366886</span>联系他，说有人正在他房屋往外搬东西。林善忠立刻放下手上的活回家，结果在到家的路口遭到十多人绑架！晚上获释后回家见自己的房屋已是一片废墟。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2023</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">1</span>日上午，福建省福州市晋安区城管带着公证处的共计<span lang="EN-US">10</span>多人，到鼓山镇远东村访民林善忠的<span lang="EN-US">694</span>号房屋处，说根据<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">30</span>日由福州市宏榕房屋征收工程公司贴门口的通知，要对该房屋进行测量评估，然后实施强拆。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2022</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">12</span>日，福建省福州市晋安区鼓山镇政府拆违办，就曾到林善忠的<span lang="EN-US">694</span>号房屋门口贴出公告，称该房屋属于违法建筑，限令<span lang="EN-US">3</span>天内自己拆除，否则后果自负。在贴公告前两天，房屋就被断水断电了。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">据林善忠说，该房屋是在<span lang="EN-US">1989</span>年与其他村民一起统一报批统建的，土地使用权已经获得了，村委会在<span lang="EN-US">2003</span>年还出证明，请求有关部门给予办理房产证，该房屋共计<span lang="EN-US">281</span>平米，林善忠一直以该房屋经营装修、五金等谋生，所以根本不存在违法建筑之说。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2002</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年，林善忠户籍地的晋安区鼓山镇远东村<span lang="EN-US">154</span>号被强拆，该房屋登记建筑面积<span lang="EN-US">300</span>余平米，是福州市建设委员会拆迁办跟远东村村委会签订了房屋拆迁协议，至今没有得到解决，林善忠还因为上访、被关押黑监狱无数次，<span lang="EN-US">2016</span>年刑事拘留一次，后取保候审，<span lang="EN-US">2017</span>年全国“两会”关押看守所一年半，最后以“寻衅滋事罪”判刑一年半。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">林善忠说，这是自己最后一处经营、居住的两用房屋，前天派出所片警与鼓山镇信访办主任庄辉就电话警告他，如果不回来接受拆迁公司的拆迁补偿协议，就会跟之前的房屋一样被偷拆强拆那样，而且至今得不到安置补偿。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">林善忠电话：<span lang="EN-US">13625021838</span>。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体;
