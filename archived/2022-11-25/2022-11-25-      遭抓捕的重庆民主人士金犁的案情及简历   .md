<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">1</span>日出生，四川省自贡市人，自由职业者，公民权利追求者，民主异见人士，中国在押政治犯。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">同年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">27</span>日，又被自贡市检察院追加罪名，以涉嫌“资助危害国家安全犯罪活动罪”和“寻衅滋事罪”予以正式批捕；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">至今其案已被提交到自贡市检察院审查起诉；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">据悉，受其子金展浩之委托，并以是其朋友的身份名义，包龙军与另一名成都籍律师愿共同为其案代理辩护；但当其二人为了解案件要求会见当事人时，自贡市看守所却以疫情之名拒绝他们会见，同时，自贡市检察院也以涉密为由拒绝代理辩护人复制、拍摄卷宗，仅同意他们做部分摘阅，并有<span lang="EN-US">20</span>余张光盘不让查看。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体;
