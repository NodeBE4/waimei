<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">11</span>日，本网获悉获刑<span lang="EN-US">5</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月的北京法轮功学员龚瑞平女士的案情及简历，现在通报如下：<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">龚瑞平：</span></b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">北京法轮功学员，女，<span lang="EN-US">1964</span>年生，北京市平谷区刘家店镇刘家店村人，原北京市平谷区大华山小学教师，因为修炼法轮大法，被平谷区教育局非法开除工职，并关押到“北京法制培训中心”（洗脑班）折磨。<span lang="EN-US">2001</span>年，曾被判刑<span lang="EN-US">4</span>年，关押在北京女子监狱，遭残暴酷刑。<span lang="EN-US">2008</span>年和<span lang="EN-US">2012</span>年分别被中共当局劳教<span lang="EN-US">2</span>年半和<span lang="EN-US">2</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>个月。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">2021</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">20</span>日，龚瑞平被平谷区渔阳派出所警察抓捕，后被平谷区法院判刑<span lang="EN-US">5</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月，现被关押在北京女子监狱三监区。刑期至<span lang="EN-US">2027</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">19</span>日。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p>
