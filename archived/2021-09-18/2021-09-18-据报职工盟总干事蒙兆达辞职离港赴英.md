[据报职工盟总干事蒙兆达辞职离港赴英](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210918-1194871)
------

<div>
<p>香港职工会联盟将启动解散程序，并于明日召开记者会交代，总干事蒙兆达亦已辞职。有消息指，蒙兆达日前已离开香港前往英国。</p><p>根据《星岛日报》报道，职工盟执委会前晚(16日)通过解散决议，将启动程序，在10月3日再召开会员大会正式表决，总干事蒙兆达亦已辞职。</p><p>消息指，蒙兆达日前已离开香港，并前往英国。职工盟主席黄迺元仅表示，一切留待明日召开的记者会再详述。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>继民阵、教协和支联会解散后，成立31年的香港职工盟也进入解散筹备过程，香港警务处处长萧泽颐今天对此指出，若有违法行为，警方一定会追究。至于团体是否有违法行为，相信他们心中有数，并强调警方会依法办事。<br> </p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/zbpe-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=zbpe399&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_desktop-zbpe399_2.png?dHEiGouxxZpWwWTJE04dyGyUN74JOU2z" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_desktop-zbpe399_2.png?dHEiGouxxZpWwWTJE04dyGyUN74JOU2z" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/zbpe-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=zbpe399&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_mobile-zbpe399_2.png?qZnBcIGCJvPR8A2IAZOZuOxdoGZDatiI" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_mobile-zbpe399_2.png?qZnBcIGCJvPR8A2IAZOZuOxdoGZDatiI" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
