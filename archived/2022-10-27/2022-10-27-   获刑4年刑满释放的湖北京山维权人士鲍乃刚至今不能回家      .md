<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">28</span>日，本网获悉：获刑<span lang="EN-US">4</span>年的湖北京山维权人士鲍乃刚先生于<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">15</span>日从沙洋长宁监狱满刑出狱，但湖北省京山市当局不让其回家，也不让他见朋友及妻子罗汉娥，更</span><span style="font-family: SimSun-ExtB; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-bidi-font-family: SimSun-ExtB;">𣎴</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">让他与外界联系。到长宁监狱去接他出狱的朋友们扑了个空，当局直接将他关进京山市宋河镇宾馆看管，他出狱之时恰逢<span lang="EN-US">"20</span>大″召开的敏感时期，据悉鲍乃刚的身体状况不佳。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">1966</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">1998</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年，因其父曾为生计问题被湖北省京山县城管殴打至重伤偏瘫，自此为父上访申冤<span lang="EN-US">10</span>年，遂屡遭中共湖北当局的仇视与维稳打压；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2003</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年，曾因上访被湖北省京山县法院枉判<span lang="EN-US">2</span>年，其父亦在被殴打致重伤、卧病在床<span lang="EN-US">7</span>年之后含冤逝世；期间，其因不断上访维权，开始逐步认识到自身维权悲惨命运的根源及对社会公共<span lang="EN-US">/</span>敏感事件参与的必要；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2008</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">,</span>曾以志愿者身份积极投身到四川汶川地震的抗震救灾行动中，受到四川省绵阳市志愿者协会的书面嘉奖；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2011</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月，曾因在网上回应“茉莉花革命”，而被四川省绵阳市北川芜族自治县警方以涉嫌“煽动颠覆国家政权罪”刑拘，关押于绵阳市看守所；同年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月，被以同罪名转正式逮捕，后又于<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">21</span>日被取保获释，并遣返湖北省京山县老家；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年，曾因其弟在广东省佛山市一家公司打工过程中发生工伤事故致死，而该公司逃避责任、不尽责处理，且将其母打伤，故而促其被迫再次上访投诉，却又被警方处行政拘留数天；后因其欲进京维权控诉，而被原籍维稳人员截访并施以刑拘和抄家；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">10</span>日，曾因参与旁听武汉维权人士王芳涉嫌“寻衅滋事”案庭审，又被京山县警方处行政拘留<span lang="EN-US">3</span>天；<span lang="EN-US">2017</span>年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">17</span>日，曾因向京山县法院对京山县公安局提起行政诉状，要求撤销京山县公安局对其发出的行政处罚决定书，并予公开道歉，而又遭法院枉法判决；<span lang="EN-US">2018</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">18</span>日，曾因到北京信访投诉，再次遭京山市（此时京山县已改为京山市）维稳人员劫持遣返，次日旋即被京山市警方传唤、抄家，至<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">20</span>日，又被以涉嫌“寻衅滋事罪”刑拘；同年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">3</span>日，被京山市检察院以同罪名正式批捕，并关押在京山市看守所；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2019</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">25</span>日，其案在湖北省京山市法院开庭审理；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体;
