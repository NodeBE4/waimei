<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2020</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2020</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">30</span>日，其二人案在扎兰屯市法院被以网络视频方式开庭受审；同年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月，其被该法院以“利用邪教组织破坏法律实施罪”判处有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">3</span>年，并处罚金<span lang="EN-US">2</span>万元，刑期至<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">8</span>日；同案任小梅亦被该法院以同罪名判处有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">9</span>个月，并处罚金<span lang="EN-US">2</span>千元，<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">3</span>日已刑满释放。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体;
