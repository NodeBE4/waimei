<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">25</span>日。本网获悉近期遭天津警方刑事拘留的维权人士张建中的案情及简历，现通报如下：<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">张建中：</span></b><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">1959</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年出生，天津市人，职业不详，“反腐义勇军”工作室负责人，实名举报人，民主维权公民，中国在押维权人士。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">自<span lang="EN-US">2004</span>年以来，因其一直实名举报天津开发区（滨海新区）泰达俱乐部董事长董文胜<span lang="EN-US">(</span>在押）等人，将国有资产变相流失及勾结公安人员违法乱纪等行为，故而屡遭天津公权部门（含滨海新区及河北区法院等）灭绝人性式的打压摧残和逼迫；曾因长期在反腐和推进民主法治等维权运动中被多次软禁及饱受人权被践踏之苦，而导致被开除公职，并一直被中共当局标暗号为“贩毒”人员，剥夺其居住权、低保权和婚姻存续权；期间，曾多次遭警察殴打，打至骨折并致残，迄今重病缠身，生活极度贫困；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2018</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月，其因长期揭露中共恶行，预料可能随时会被再次抓捕，故而在网上公开发表“绝不自杀”声明；<span lang="EN-US">2018</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">26</span>日，曾因关注人权律师“王全璋案”而去天津市第二中级法院围观和参加旁听，而被天津市河北区墙子派出所警方拦截带走，后被以涉嫌“寻衅滋事罪”刑拘、逮捕，并被关押在天津市河北区看守所；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2019</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年，被天津市河北区法院以其在“<span lang="EN-US">facebook</span>发布或转发涉及国内重大事件、严重损害国家形像等不实帖文，混淆视听，起哄闹事，造成恶劣的社会影响，严重扰乱了公共秩序”为由，以“寻衅滋事罪”判处有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">2</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>个月；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2021</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月出狱后，其依然遭受着公权力的打压和迫害；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">同年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">13</span>日，曾因其应江苏省昆山市维权人士王和英之邀，欲于次日乘高铁前往昆山参加王定于<span lang="EN-US">16</span>日的“和盈茶庄”的开业，而被辖区的警察拦截软禁<span lang="EN-US">3</span>天；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">同年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">28</span>日，又因六四将至，紧接着是中共建党一百周年，为求维稳，遂河北区警方在其居所附近距离几米处，紧急安装了一组监控摄像头；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2022</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">19</span>日，获悉其于六四前夕又被天津市河北区警方带走，自此失联，后经网友确认，知其已被河北区警方正式刑拘，涉嫌罪名暂不明晰。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体;
