[因给四通桥勇士彭载舟画像而遭中共当局逮捕的南昌画家萧亮获刑1年3个月](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2023/12/13.html)
------

<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">5</span>日，本网获悉：因为四通桥勇士彭载舟画像的南昌画家萧亮于<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">16</span>日被中共当局抓捕。他的案子<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月就已经到了法院，检察院当时建议量刑一年三个月，起诉理由为推特画“危险人物”并获得大量点赞。现获悉，萧亮先生已被判<span lang="EN-US">1</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>个月。现关押在南昌市第一看守所。刑期至：<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">15</span>日。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">萧亮：</span></b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">江西省南昌市人，网络活跃人士，常用推特号：<span lang="EN-US">@xiaoliang999</span>，南昌画家，异见表达者，中国在押政治犯。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2022</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">13</span>日，因中共二十大前夕，有“四通桥勇士”彭立发（网名彭载舟）在北京四通桥上悬挂倒习横幅，指责习近平是独裁者，要求他下台，随后即被抓捕；此事件一经传播，很快促使国内外许多华人站出来表达他们的对彭载舟先生的支持。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">此期间，萧亮因画了一幅四通桥勇士彭立发的画像并发到推特上，遂于<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">16</span>日被江西省南昌市东湖区警方带走，家遭查抄，而后被以“寻衅滋事”为由行政拘留；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">但之后其很快又被警方以同罪由转为刑拘，同时，其妻子被问讯，主要涉及其有何嗜好，都交些什么朋友，有参加什么组织或收谁的钱等等问题，均被其妻子一一否定；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2022</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">7</span>日，其被南昌市东湖区检察院以涉嫌“寻衅滋事罪”予以批捕；后遭起诉。现获悉萧亮被判刑<span lang="EN-US">1</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>个月。刑期至：<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">15</span>日。目前被羁押于南昌市第一看守所（南昌市新建县南昌警察学校<span lang="EN-US">100</span>米附近，邮政编码：<span lang="EN-US">210036</span>）。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"></p><div class="separator" style="clear: both;
