[安徽访民李本才家属昨天收到宿州市中级法院去年的刑事终审裁决](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2023/02/blog-post_26.html)
------

<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">26</span>日，本网获悉：一直关注安徽访民李本才被寻衅滋事罪的吴继新，昨天收到李本才家属的信息，说李本才被寻衅滋事犯罪判刑一案上诉案，安徽省宿州市中级法院在<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">13</span>日就作出了维持原判的终审判决，但直到今年的<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">24</span>日才送达。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">安徽省宿州市埇桥区三八街道办事处访民李本才因为<span lang="EN-US">2013</span>年房屋被强拆而上访（包括其经营的果园被偷偷毁灭）。<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月，李本才因为一句话地方不解决就找中央而被抓捕，同年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">29</span>日宿州市埇桥区法院开庭审理了该案，最后以“寻衅滋事罪”之强拿硬要进行了速判，判处<span lang="EN-US">2</span>年10个月徒刑，并收缴所谓强拿三八街道办事处的<span lang="EN-US">41400</span>元损失。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">从安徽省宿州市中级法院作出的（<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>）皖行终<span lang="EN-US">501</span>号刑事裁定书中，可以看出当年强拆李本才房屋时，仅有李本才妻子杨云英在三八街道办事处的拆迁安置协议上签字，而没有房屋的其他共有人、包括作为房主李本才的签字，也就是这种涉及不动产转移的协议在法律上不具有合法性。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">因为李本才到北京上访多年，也多次遭到暴力截访导致财物损失，埇桥区政府三八街道办事处主动给予了<span lang="EN-US">41400</span>元人民币的补偿，当局就以“强拿硬要”为由，按寻衅滋事犯罪判处其有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">2</span>年10个月。李本才不服一审判决提起上诉，由妻子杨云英和女儿为其做无罪辩护。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">据杨云英反映，她在<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">3</span>号还提交了书面辩护意见，同年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">27</span>日又给承办法官打电话，询问什么时候开庭，法官还回答说不知道，案件还正在审理中。<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">22</span>日，宿州市中级法院法官打电话通知她，说终审裁定书已经出来了，问她到法院来拿还是邮寄，她同意法院邮寄，周五收到终审裁定书，发现裁定书居然在去年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">13</span>日就已经制作了。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">法院对刑事案件不仅不开庭审理，而且作出并送达裁判文书如此随意性，可见该案有多么荒唐。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">杨云英电话：<span lang="EN-US">15755741235</span>。</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">吴继新电话：</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">13051785798</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">。</span></p><p class="MsoNormal"></p><div class="separator" style="clear: both;
