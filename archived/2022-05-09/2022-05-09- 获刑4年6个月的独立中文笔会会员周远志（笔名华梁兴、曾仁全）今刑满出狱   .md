[获刑4年6个月的独立中文笔会会员周远志（笔名华梁兴、曾仁全）今刑满出狱](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2022/05/46.html)

<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">9</span>日，本网获悉：因言获罪<span lang="EN-US">4</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月的独立中文笔会会员周远志（笔名华梁兴、曾仁全）今刑满出狱，现以回到家中。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">周远志：</span></b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">笔名华梁兴、曾仁全。<span lang="EN-US">1961</span>年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">22</span>日生。是一名自由撰稿人、独立中文笔会会员。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">由于长期撰写、发表关于各类社会问题及揭露官员腐败的文章，在<span lang="EN-US">2008</span>年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月被湖北省钟祥市国家安全局以“涉嫌煽动颠覆国家政权罪”抓捕，获释后周远志在家长期被当局监视居住。根据独立中文笔会的网页显示，他以笔名曾仁全发表多篇敏感文章，其中题目包括“农民以特殊方式表达对江泽民的憎恶”及“人民的名义远离普世价值”。周远志被<span lang="EN-US">2017</span>年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">24</span>日被以“织非法聚集罪”刑事拘留。此前，周远志被指组织访民上访而被当局关押，被行政拘留<span lang="EN-US">15</span>天原本应于<span lang="EN-US">2017</span>年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">25</span>日释放，但未获释放，随后，被确认其在<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">24</span>日即遭湖北荆门市公安局东宝分局以“织非法聚集罪”刑事拘留。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">其妻子张中凤说由于他一向身体不好，她非常担心丈夫的情况。羁押在荆门市看守所。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2019</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">21</span>日在荆门掇刀法院开庭，<span lang="EN-US">2019</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">28</span>日以“组织非法聚集罪判刑期<span lang="EN-US">2</span>年，网上寻衅滋事罪<span lang="EN-US">3</span>年，诽谤罪<span lang="EN-US">1</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月，决定合并执行<span lang="EN-US">4</span>年零<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月”，刑期<span lang="EN-US">2017</span>年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">10</span>日至<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">9</span>日。</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">此前关押在湖北省荆门市东宝区牌楼镇荆门监狱。</span></p><p class="MsoNormal"></p><div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;"><a href="https://blogger.googleusercontent.com/img/b/R29vZ2xl/AVvXsEjTGE5d9hUqS3FvT-spQx6St2N89gzITOWyKdxxZ-kLz9hydtuxHlZCaRL5PDfDwaUTDcMPNhnSL64qkwyxYZ8wGapbWWbnyz6hZ4Mr7cGCLOZJoI0DnLBcVqCx95-yp5Qp_6rb_30Izke0j1xbK02f9bWjNti_XINED-zyGqXUXP_wUV75U80p19pudw/s549/QQ%E6%88%AA%E5%9B%BE20220509221229.png" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em;
