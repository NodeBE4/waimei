[遭抓捕的江苏南通如皋市维权人士张岳兵的案情及简历](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2022/06/blog-post_92.html)

<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">18</span>日，本网获悉遭抓捕的江苏南通如皋市维权人士张岳兵的案情及简历，现通报如下：<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">张岳兵：</span></b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">江苏省南通市如皋市人，南通市如皋市如城街道西郊居九组<span lang="EN-US">70</span>号公民，维权公民，公益举报人，中国在押维权人士。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">早年，曾因其房屋遭政府强拆而被迫上访维权，后在上访过程中逐渐成长为维权公民，并开始致力于关注公益维权事业；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">自<span lang="EN-US">2016</span>年起，曾因举报江苏省如皋市公交违规收费事宜，而如皋市政府和相关部门却一直并未履行法定职责查处和纠正这一违法行为，故而随后其又举报了多个公共场所长期违反消防法占用消防疏散信道经营等违法事项；尽管其举报意图旨在为了社会公益，但地方当局及职能部门却为了自身的集团利益，无视其举报的相关问题，公然失职渎职，维护违反消防法、占用消防车信道和疏散信道等现象，甚至层层谎报，拒不整改、消除障碍及依法查处问题所在；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">由于如皋市当局对其举报多年的违法事实不予理会也不立案查处，因而为了掩盖政府部门的不作为和乱作为（即几次出现谎报现象），遂长期高薪雇佣多个保安非法限制其人身自由，并将其住所用钢材焊死，非法安装监视器，对其全天候的拍照与跟踪；其虽多次报警但无果；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2021</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">9</span>日，其因担心电瓶车长久不充电耗损电瓶（因正被长期软禁中），故而到车库欲给电瓶车充电，但竟被监控者跟着，一脚踹开车库门，将其殴打致伤；其即刻电话报警，不料如皋市警方反将其带走，被莫名处行政拘留<span lang="EN-US">10</span>天；同年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">20</span>日，又被以涉嫌“寻衅滋事罪”转为刑拘；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2022</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">26</span>日，被如皋市检察院以同罪名正式批捕；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2022</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">29</span>日，其案被地方检察院起诉并移交到江苏省南通市如皋市法院；至今，未审待判；据悉，其律师认为其不构成寻衅滋事罪，已向检察院提交不起诉意见书。目前被羁押于江苏省如皋市看守所。</span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体;
