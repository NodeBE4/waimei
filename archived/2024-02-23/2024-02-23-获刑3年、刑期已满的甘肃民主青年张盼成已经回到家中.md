<p><span></span></p><a name='more'></a><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;"><br /></span><p></p><p><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">（维权网信息中心报道）</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">2018</span><span style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月，被抓捕刑拘；<span lang="EN-US">2018</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">18</span>日，被北京市警方以涉嫌<span lang="EN-US">“</span>寻衅滋事罪<span lang="EN-US">”</span>正式逮捕；审讯中，拒绝写保证书，并拒绝给律师签委托书；<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">21</span>日，其案在北京西城区法院开庭审理；<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">4</span>日，被北京市西城区法院一审以<span lang="EN-US">“</span>寻衅滋事罪<span lang="EN-US">”</span>判处有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">1</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月，至<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">20</span>日，刑满释放；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 楷体;
