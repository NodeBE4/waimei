mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">金敏：</span></b><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">女，<span lang="EN-US">1967</span>年出生，吉林省通化市人，原吉林省通化市钢铁公司职工，法轮功锻炼和信仰者，中国在押良心犯。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2020</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">11</span>日，因在吉林市永吉县双河镇给出租车司机宣传法轮功信息，遂被双河镇派出所警方知晓后抓捕，<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">14</span>日，被吉林市船营区警方以涉嫌邪教罪刑拘；同年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月下旬，又被吉林市船营区检察院以同罪由正式批捕，并关押于吉林市看守所；同年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月，其案被检察院提起公诉，后被移交至吉林市船营区法院审理；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2021</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月，在未通知家属的情况下，其案在吉林市船营区法院秘密开庭，最终再次被以“利用邪教组织破坏法律实施罪”重判处有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">6</span>年；其不服，上诉；<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月，经吉林市中级法院二审裁决：驳回上诉，维持原判；刑期至<span lang="EN-US">2026</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">13</span>日；据悉，狱中其再次惨遭酷刑折磨。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;
