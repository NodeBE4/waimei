<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p><p class="MsoNormal" style="tab-stops: 66.0pt;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">24</span>日，本网获悉：获刑<span lang="EN-US">8</span>年的四川达州市开江县法轮功学员邓道恒日前被嘉州监狱送重症监护室，家人心急如焚。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal" style="tab-stops: 66.0pt;"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体;
