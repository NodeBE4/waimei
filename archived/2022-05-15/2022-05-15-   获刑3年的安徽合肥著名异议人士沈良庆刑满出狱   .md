<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">15</span>日，本网获悉：获刑<span lang="EN-US">3</span>年的安徽合肥著名异议人士沈良庆今天刑满出狱。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">沈良庆今早已经刚刚刑满获释到家，身体还好。目前没有手机，所以暂时无法联系其他朋友。因处于防疫政策原因，要在家三天不能外出，在家做核酸，然后才能出门。这三年的寻衅滋事生活使他苍桑了许多。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">沈良庆先生简历：</span></b><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">1962</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">6</span>日出生，安徽省合肥市人，前安徽省检察院公职人员，著名政治异见人士，政治犯。<b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></b></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">自<span lang="EN-US">1984</span>年起便投身于民主运动，自创民间刊物《大学生与社会》，弘扬自由人权与普世价值；因其个性刚直不阿，常在<span lang="EN-US">Twitter</span>上针砭时弊，而遭当局长期监控、打压与逼迫，多次被地方警方传唤、拘押或软禁等；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2013</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">22</span>日，曾因声援“张安妮失学事件”（安妮系前政治犯张林之女），而被合肥市蜀山区警方以涉嫌“聚众扰乱社会秩序罪”传唤和审讯；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2015</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月和<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月，曾因网络言论和转帖而被警方分别行政拘留<span lang="EN-US">13</span>天和<span lang="EN-US">9</span>天；<span lang="EN-US">2016</span>年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">3</span>日，曾因向合肥市包河区法院提起行政诉讼，后被法院裁决驳回，随遭中共当局仇视、忌恨；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2016</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">28</span>日，曾因欲往苏州参加<span lang="EN-US">4.29</span>林昭殉难日祭奠活动而遭警方绑架，且被长时失踪；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2018</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">6</span>日，曾因在推特上发表自由言论而遭一大群警察到家传唤，并搜走手机、计算机及复制其中数据等；<span lang="EN-US">2019</span>年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">16</span>日，因接受日本媒体采访谈论对六四事件的看法等，遂被赶来的合肥警方以涉嫌“寻衅滋事罪”传唤、刑拘；同年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">20</span>日，被合肥市包河区警方以同罪由转正式逮捕；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2021</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">13</span>日，其案因疫情影响而被合肥市包河区法院以远程视频模式开庭审理，直至同年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">17</span>日，被该法院一审以“寻衅滋事罪”判处有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">3</span>年，刑期至<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">15</span>日；其不服，提起上诉；二审被维持原判；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">据悉，羁押审讯期间，曾被戴黑头套、<span lang="EN-US">24</span>小时禁吃喝、禁止上厕所，遭长时间疲劳审讯等酷刑。此前被羁押于合肥市看守所。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"></p><div class="separator" style="clear: both;
