<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">该拍卖活动原本预定在昨日上午进行，然而现场临时被法院以防疫为名取消。但今日下午，法院又突然口头通知继续进行拍卖。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">据悉，入围竞拍人三名：代号<span lang="EN-US">005</span>，<span lang="EN-US">009</span>和<span lang="EN-US">018</span>（没有具体公司名称）。<span lang="EN-US">009</span>号未到场。起拍价<span lang="EN-US">6.861</span>亿，<span lang="EN-US">005</span>号举牌，第一次加价<span lang="EN-US">500</span>万没有人竞价，第二次加价降到<span lang="EN-US">300</span>万也没有竞价，第三次降到加价<span lang="EN-US">100</span>万照旧沉默。最后<span lang="EN-US">005</span>号竞拍人在实际上没有竞争的情况下“竞拍”成功，成交价<span lang="EN-US">6.861</span>亿。该拍卖价格就是评估价格。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体;
