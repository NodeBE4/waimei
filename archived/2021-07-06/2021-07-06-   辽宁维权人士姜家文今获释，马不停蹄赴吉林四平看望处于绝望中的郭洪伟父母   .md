[辽宁维权人士姜家文今获释，马不停蹄赴吉林四平看望处于绝望中的郭洪伟父母](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2021/07/blog-post_60.html)
------

<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">6</span>日，本网获悉：因建党百年庆典而稳控<span lang="EN-US">16</span>天、接着拘留<span lang="EN-US">10</span>天的辽宁维权人士姜家文，于今天终于获得人身自由。老姜回家看望一下母亲，整理一下衣物，就马不停蹄奔赴吉林市四平市，看望目前处于极度绝望中的郭荫起、肖蕴苓老夫妻。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2021</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">10</span>日，辽宁丹东维权人士姜家文在北京租住屋内休息，被丹东政府、公安、信访组成的截访专班共六人，通过绑架手段押回到了户籍地辽宁丹东，关押在元宝区疫情隔离区。到<span lang="EN-US">26</span>日下午<span lang="EN-US">13</span>点，元宝区公安分局七道派出所副所长孙大勇到隔离区，带老姜去做核酸检测，告知结果出来后以“虚构事实扰乱公共秩序”送拘留所，关押到百年党庆维稳结束后的<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">6</span>日。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">郭洪伟原本是四平市发电厂医护室职工，十多年前在承包经营的经济纠纷案中，因警察干预司法程序而败诉，又因维权被判刑，出狱后继续进京维权而遭到更暴力对待，导致身体落下残疾。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2015</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">7</span>日，郭洪伟与母亲肖蕴苓在政府与公安警察的一起陪同下去了北京，在政府与警察执法部门的监督下且没有任何违法行为的情况下，被吉林四平警方从北京接回后，未经体检被直接送进看守所关押。<span lang="EN-US">2016</span>年，郭洪伟再被以“寻衅滋事罪”、“敲诈勒索罪”判刑<span lang="EN-US">13</span>年。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2021</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月初，郭洪伟在吉林公主岭监狱服刑期间，突发脑溢血送医院，通过两次手术后任然没有挽回生命，于<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">10</span>日上午<span lang="EN-US">10</span>点<span lang="EN-US">59</span>分在医院去世，享年<span lang="EN-US">57</span>岁。郭洪伟去世后，其父母亲怀疑郭洪伟的死因，委托维权律师谢燕益调查郭洪伟的死亡真相，结果在<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">12</span>日晚上<span lang="EN-US">20</span>点<span lang="EN-US">30</span>分，到四平办案的谢律师被失踪，案卷材料遭洗劫一空。郭洪伟的父母亲虽然报案，但公安机关不予理睬。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">原本就准备前往看望郭荫起、肖蕴苓这对老夫妻的姜家文先生，因为“六<span lang="EN-US">.</span>四”与“党庆百年”维稳而失去了人身自由，今天刚获得释放，就马不停蹄前往四平市，希望在老两口最最无助与绝望之际，给老人一丝安慰。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体;
