[浙江温州基督徒林学岁在四川古蔺礼拜天聚会被刑事拘留后正式批捕](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2022/08/blog-post_51.html)

<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">21</span>日，本网获悉：四川古蔺公安局以“组织、资助非法聚集”为由，<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">14</span>日将温州基督徒林学岁刑事拘留，另外两位基督徒也以同样的罪名被刑拘，三人关押在古蔺看守所。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2022</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">22</span>日，林学岁正式被逮捕，其他两位基督徒暂被取保候审，林学岁现在仍羁押于古蔺看守所。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">林学岁是一名普通的基督徒，曾受邀去到古蔺县某偏远的山区看望基督徒，因看到四川这片土地信仰的荒凉，为此他决定留在古蔺，也想通过自己的见证能结出福音的果子。而他所做的，是基督徒的本分，却被冒然扣上“犯罪”两字，被定义为“有社会危险性”人员，实在荒谬可笑。按照相关法律，基督徒礼拜天聚会，并不违法，更构不成犯罪。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">对林学岁的境况，本网将持续关注。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p>
