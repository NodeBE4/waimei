[圣约家园教案进展：控告侦查机关违法，申请召开羁押必要性审查听证会](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2023/04/blog-post_17.html)

<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">临汾圣约家园教案<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">19</span>日，临汾市公安局直属分局对该教会大抓捕，先以治安案件立案，<span lang="EN-US">20</span>号作出行政拘留的处罚决定，<span lang="EN-US">21</span>号转为刑事案件。 <span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">案件经尧都区人民检察院退回一次补充侦查后，该院在<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">26</span>日报送临汾市检察院审查起诉。<span lang="EN-US"> <o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2023</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">13</span>日，部分律师走访临汾市检察院，与承办检察官交流，反映圣约家园教会李洁、韩晓栋、王强等人不可能构成诈骗罪，并指出：<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">1.</span></b><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">侦查机关的指控逻辑极其荒谬：家庭教会没有在民政局登记，就是非法组织，传道人没有在宗教管理部门注册，就是非宗教教职人员。信徒依圣经教导，基督教<span lang="EN-US">2</span>千年传统进行奉献，教会核心同工就构成诈骗</span></b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">。 <span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">该指控严重破坏了我国一贯的宗教包容政策。<span lang="EN-US">1949</span>年后，我国教会一直以登记了的三自教会，和没有登记的家庭教会两种形态并存。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">家庭教会的合法性，得到了<span lang="EN-US">82</span>年<span lang="EN-US">19</span>号文，<span lang="EN-US">2007</span>年宗教白皮书，<span lang="EN-US">2018</span>年宗教白皮书的确认。<span lang="EN-US"> <o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">按临汾市公安局 的指控逻辑，所有家庭教会的带领人都在“诈骗”，都要抓起来，显然与我国一贯的宗教包容政策相抵触。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2.</span></b><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">侦查机关－临汾市公安局直属分局先罗织罪名立案侦查，再逼迫“受害人”报假案，蓄意制造冤假错案。<span lang="EN-US"> <o:p></o:p></span></span></b></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">诈骗案的一般逻辑是：受害人向公安机关报案，称其遭受诈骗，公安机关经初步审查后立案或不予立案。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">先立案，后有“受害人”报案，猫腻太大，显然是公安机关在蓄意制造冤假错案。 <span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">本案所谓的“受害人”，在被逼书写报案材料后，又出具情况说明，说知道是家庭教会，没有被骗，之前的报案材料不属实。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2023</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">11</span>日，部分律师就此问题形成书面材料，向各级机关反映。截止目前，没收到任何有效反馈。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">3.</span></b><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">向临汾市检察院申请召开羁押必要性审查听证会。 <span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></b></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">直属分局本来办的就是假案，李洁、韩晓栋、王强等人完全不构成犯罪，也不可能有社会危害性，根据《人民检察院办理羁押必要性审查案件规定》第<span lang="EN-US">17</span>条第（<span lang="EN-US">2</span>）（<span lang="EN-US">4</span>）项之规定，检察院应当向办案机关提出释放或者变更强制措施的建议。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">同时根据《人民检察院审查案件听证工作规定》第<span lang="EN-US">4</span>条第<span lang="EN-US">1</span>款规定，临汾市检察院办理羁押必要性审查案件，在事实认定、法律适用、案件处理等方面存在较大争议，或者有重大社会影响，需要当面听取当事人和其他相关人员意见的，经检察长批准，可以召开听证会。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">我们申明：<span lang="EN-US"> <o:p></o:p></span></span></b></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">1.</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">教会注册登记，只是国家宗教事务部门从行政管理角度作出的规定，不登记不意味着不是教会，传道人、牧师没有宗教部门颁发的教职人员证，其教职身份不意味着不被上帝和信徒认可。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">实际上，政府与教会分离，是世界各国处理政教关系的基本原则。政府，没有权柄任免牧师、传道人等教职人员。<span lang="EN-US"> <o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2.</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">宗教信仰自由是最基本的人权。人类的宗教行为早于国家出现，在氏族时代就有图腾崇拜。宗教，是人的天然需求，区别于其它受造物的重要标志。宗教本身也成为人类文明的重要组成部分。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">世俗国家无权，也不可能真正规范到每个公民必须信仰或者不信仰任何宗教。进入近代社会以来，各国宪法及国际公约，都确认了宗教信仰自由这一基本人权。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">如我国宪法第<span lang="EN-US">36</span>条，《世界人权宣言》第<span lang="EN-US">18</span>条，《公民权利和政治权利国际公约》第<span lang="EN-US">18</span>条都对信仰自由予以确认。 <span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">请临汾市检察院、临汾市公安局、山西省公安局、山西省检察院、公安部刑侦局、最高人民检察院等机关：<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">对临汾市公安局直属分局先罗织罪名立案侦查，再逼迫“受害人”报假案，蓄意制造冤假错案的行为进行调查，把涉嫌违法犯罪的有关公职人员移送有权机关调查！<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2.</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">督促临汾市检察院对李洁、韩晓栋、王强等人召开羁押必要性审查听证会，作出取保候审决定，并最终作出不起诉决定。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: 宋体;
