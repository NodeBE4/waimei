<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">15</span>日，本网获悉：获刑<span lang="EN-US">2</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月的四川维权人士谢俊彪已经送四川省眉山监狱服刑，家属尚未收到监狱通知。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">谢俊彪于<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月被一审判刑<span lang="EN-US">2</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月，其不服判决上诉，后遭四川成都市中级人民法院二审驳回，维持原判。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2013</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年—<span lang="EN-US">2015</span>年，曾因所在成都市双流区政府在未提供任何有效档，也未与当地村民达成拆迁补偿协议的前提下，便强征强拆、驱逐村民，令其及众村民个人权益严重受损，随在信访投诉无望情况下，走上了从成都到北京等地的维权抗争之路；<span lang="EN-US">2017</span>年，曾因多次在成都市和双流区的上访无果，而两次进京上访投诉，随数次遭双流区警方传唤及登门威胁警告；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2018</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">10</span>日，曾因习近平等中共高层常委将到成都市双流区视察，而遭双流区公兴派出所警方莫名破门辱骂和殴打，事后，又因其将此不幸经历和猜测发于微信群，爆料“明天可能有大爷到双流视察”，随旋即被双流区警方以“寻衅滋事”罪由行政拘留<span lang="EN-US">10</span>天；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">7</span>日，其案在双流区法院开庭审理，同年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月，终被该法院一审以“寻衅滋事罪”判处有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">2</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月，刑期至<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">6</span>日；其不服，上诉，二审维持原判。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体;
