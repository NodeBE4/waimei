[香港民主党主席罗健熙今辞区议员](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210711-1167526)
------

<div>
<p>香港民主党主席、南区区议会主席罗健熙昨（10日）向部分党员发信息，称今天（11日）会辞任南区区议员一职。</p><p>据明报报道，罗健熙说，自己已有一连串在地区继续服务的计划，希望离任后专心致志地落实，毋须持续受DQ威胁而影响地区工作。</p><p>有消息称港府将追讨被DQ的区议员薪津，罗健熙于信息中说，即使完全无法理解追回在任期间薪津的理据，但要花精力和金钱在法庭处理这些问题也十分折腾，希望其辞任区议员的决定得到理解、见谅。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>香港政府本月内将完成区议员的宣誓程序，有消息指逾200名泛民区议员会被DQ，当局将向他们追讨就任一年半以来的薪津，估计每人“负债”百万元，但若宣誓前辞职可免被追讨。</p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
