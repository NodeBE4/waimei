[黑龙江饶河东北虎出没 当地发紧急提醒](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210711-1167735)
------

<div>
<p>一段10日流出的视频显示，黑龙江省双鸭山市饶河县永幸村附近公路旁发现一只东北虎。</p><p>据中新网报道，记者从饶河县委宣传部获悉，发现东北虎的地区属于东方红林业局。饶河县林草局已在东北虎易出现重点林区架设远红外相机40部，监测东北虎的动向。</p><p>饶河县林草局出动一台巡护车辆进行巡护，同时发布紧急提醒，要求村民不要入山作业，养殖户要做好安全防范，避免人与东北虎相遇，防止人和家畜受到伤害。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>饶河县林草局已和东方红林业局取得联系，达成共识，由其上报中国龙江森林工业集团总公司。</p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
