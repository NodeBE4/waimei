[云南孟连县将全员核酸检测  全县封闭管理](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210711-1167513)
------

<div>
<p>云南省普洱市孟连县决定于12日—14日对孟连县范围内全体居民（含本地常住人口、流动人口、外籍人员等）开展全员免费核酸检测。</p><p>据中国央视新闻报道，同时，12日0时—14日24时，孟连县实行全县封闭管理，所有人员非必要不进入孟连；孟连县辖区内人员“非必要不外出”。</p><p>因特殊情况需要外出的，需持三天内核酸检测阴性证明和出行审批单，各乡（镇）指挥部、县指挥部或市指挥部均可出具出行审批单。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>云南省昨天新增冠病本土确诊病例12例；新增境外输入确诊病例四例、无症状感染者两例。无症状感染者解除隔离医学观察一例（境外输入）。</p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
