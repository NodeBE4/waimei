[参与悼念刺警者　前港大学生会内阁道歉](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210711-1167474)
------

<div>
<p>已辞职的港大学生会内阁“薪燧”发表声明，指港大学生会评议会通过有关悼念七一刺伤警员案疑犯的议案，内容有严重不当之处，中央干事已意识到议案的严重性和对大学和社会带来的负面影响，并对参与悼念及支持议案深感抱歉。</p><p>据香港电台网站报道，声明提到，学生会作为学生组织绝对不应煽动、宣扬任何暴力行为、恐怖活动或其他非法行为，“薪燧”为表歉意及承担责任，中央干事已全数请辞。中央干事感激投下信任票的学生，并就辜负学生的期望及信任致上最深切的歉意。</p><p>港大学生会评议会早前晚通过动议，对七一在铜锣湾涉嫌刺伤警员后自杀的男子表示深切哀悼，又感激对方为香港作出的牺牲。港大学生会其后召开记者会，鞠躬道歉，及宣布撤回悼念议案。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
