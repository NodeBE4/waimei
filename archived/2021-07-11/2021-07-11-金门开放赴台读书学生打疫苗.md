[金门开放赴台读书学生打疫苗](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210711-1167729)
------

<div>
<p>台湾金门县政府今日宣布，下周末为“金门籍18岁以上赴台就学学生”接种疫苗，7月14日下周三开始接受预约。</p><p>据联合网报道，台湾金门县长杨镇浯介绍，金门县府团检视了疫苗库存以及医护的接种量能，将开放“金门籍18岁以上赴台就学学生”接种疫苗，保障学子们从金门这个疫情平稳的地方，要到热区或三级警戒区就读的安全性。</p><p>杨镇浯说，只要疫苗打完了，就一定会再跟台湾中央政府争取，请符合接种资格的群众，尽快踊跃接种，提高全岛疫苗覆盖率，提高群体免疫力。未来，县府也会视中央政府提供的疫苗数及疫情发展，随时调整接种对象。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>最近不少家长在面簿反映，9月开学日将至，希望金门县府能开放让要回台就学的学子接种疫苗，让家长能放心。<br> </p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
