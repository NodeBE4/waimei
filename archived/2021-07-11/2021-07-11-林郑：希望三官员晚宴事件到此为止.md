[林郑：希望三官员晚宴事件到此为止](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210711-1167499)
------

<div>
<p>香港行政长官林郑月娥回应三名政府高官违反限聚令被票控一事时说，事件显示三人不够敏感、有点疏忽。但她表示，三人已承担法律后果并公开致歉，希望事件到此为止。</p><p>据香港电台报道，林郑月娥今早出席一个电台节目时回应事件说，三人受邀出席晚宴，以为属于私人地点聚会。对于他们用餐豪华的报道，林郑月娥说，他们应邀出席看不到菜单，如果见到食材有鲍鱼也不宜就此离场，希望大家从较人性化角度看待事件。</p><p>香港保安局副局长区志光、入境处处长区嘉宏及海关关长邓以海，今年3月初出席一个私人晚宴违反限聚令被票控。媒体报道后，事件才曝光。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>林郑月娥认为，事件显示执法一视同仁，现在应告一段落，三人尽心尽力服务社会，而高级官员最重要是名声，他们已付出很大代价。<br> </p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
