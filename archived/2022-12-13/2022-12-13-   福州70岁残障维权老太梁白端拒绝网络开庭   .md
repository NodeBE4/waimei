[福州70岁残障维权老太梁白端拒绝网络开庭](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2022/12/70.html)
------

<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">13</span>日，本网获悉：福州残障维权人士梁白端诉福州市公安局鼓楼分局违法行政拘留一案原定于今天（<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">13</span>日）上午<span lang="EN-US">9</span>点网络开庭，今已取消。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">事情经过：</span></b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">本月<span lang="EN-US">2</span>日，梁白端接到晋安区法院电话，说是疫情原因，拟网络开庭，梁表示她不会操作。<span lang="EN-US">7</span>日收到传票，通知其于<span lang="EN-US">13</span>日上午进行网络开庭。梁电话联系法院，始终无法联系。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">9</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">日，她寄出《延期开庭申请书》，上载：<span lang="EN-US">1.</span>因<span lang="EN-US">70</span>岁老太太且没有文化，无法操作手机进行网络开庭，代理人也没有网络开庭经验。<span lang="EN-US">2.</span>随着国家新闻播报，新冠病毒致病力减弱，全国优化疫情防控新十条落实，要求最大限度减少疫情对社会发展的影响。并且<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">8</span>日起，福州地铁不再查验核酸阴性证明和健康码，火车站乘车不再查验<span lang="EN-US">48</span>小时核酸阴性证明、健康码，由此认为福州各行政部门应该回归正常工作方式，依法行政，不要给老百姓添麻烦，切实做到维护民众权益。于是向法院申请延期开庭，等待正常到庭参加诉讼，请予准许，并书面回复。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体;
