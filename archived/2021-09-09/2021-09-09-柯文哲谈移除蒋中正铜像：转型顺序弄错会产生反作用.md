[柯文哲谈移除蒋中正铜像：转型顺序弄错会产生反作用](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210909-1192001)
------

<div>
<p>台湾促转会要求中正纪念堂转型为“反省威权历史公园”，并移除蒋中正铜像，国民党主席江启臣今天喊话台北市长柯文哲，应从文史保护的角度设法阻止这件事。柯文哲今天回应说，转型正义三件事，一个是解决现在的问题，避免以后再发生，最后追究过去的责任，但重点是顺序要对，否则一定会产生很多反作用力。</p><p>根据联合新闻网报道，柯文哲说，这一题，他好多年前到波兰拜访华勒沙时，对方就给过建议。他直言，华勒沙讲的话，可以给台湾社会参考，也是所有面临转型正义的政府都应采用的。</p><p>柯文哲说，转型正义有三件事，首先是“解决现在问题”、其次是“避免以后再发生”，最后才是“追究过去的责任”，但重点是顺序要对。若将追究过去的责任，一下子拿到第一顺位，保证乱七八糟，一定会产生很多作用力、反作用力。<br> </p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/zbpe-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=zbpe399&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_desktop-zbpe399_2.png?dHEiGouxxZpWwWTJE04dyGyUN74JOU2z" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_desktop-zbpe399_2.png?dHEiGouxxZpWwWTJE04dyGyUN74JOU2z" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/zbpe-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=zbpe399&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_mobile-zbpe399_2.png?qZnBcIGCJvPR8A2IAZOZuOxdoGZDatiI" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_mobile-zbpe399_2.png?qZnBcIGCJvPR8A2IAZOZuOxdoGZDatiI" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
