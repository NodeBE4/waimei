[中国在世贸就中美光伏电池关税争端提上诉](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210921-1195728)
------

<div>
<p>中国对世界贸易组织(WTO)一项争端裁决提出上诉。世贸组织在裁决中驳回中国就特朗普政府对中国制造商出口光伏面板征收关税提起的诉讼。</p><p>据彭博社报道，世贸组织一个争端小组本月稍早裁决，中国未能证实美国针对特定晶体硅光伏电池进口采取的保护措施不符合世贸组织相关规定。世贸组织在电邮发送的声明中表示，中国周一向该组织通知了上诉决定。</p><p>世贸组织上诉机构成员达不到法定数量，无法批准新的裁定，意味着中国的行动实际上是否决了裁定。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>根据世贸组织网站公布的一份文件，中国表示“对目前上诉机构还没有建立能受理此次上诉的部门感到遗憾”。</p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/zbpe-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=zbpe399&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_desktop-zbpe399_2.png?dHEiGouxxZpWwWTJE04dyGyUN74JOU2z" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_desktop-zbpe399_2.png?dHEiGouxxZpWwWTJE04dyGyUN74JOU2z" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/zbpe-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=zbpe399&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_mobile-zbpe399_2.png?qZnBcIGCJvPR8A2IAZOZuOxdoGZDatiI" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_mobile-zbpe399_2.png?qZnBcIGCJvPR8A2IAZOZuOxdoGZDatiI" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
