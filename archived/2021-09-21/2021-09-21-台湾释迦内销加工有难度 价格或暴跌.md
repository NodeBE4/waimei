[台湾释迦内销加工有难度 价格或暴跌](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210921-1195883)
------

<div>
<p>中国大陆昨（20日）起暂停进口台湾释迦及莲雾，台湾一位农产品商人指出，释迦遇热就会苦涩，加工成冰淇淋也无法消耗这么大的量，若全部回销到台湾市场，年底价格势必暴跌。</p><p>据中时电子报报道，因应大陆暂停台湾释迦与莲雾输入，台农委会将强打内外销。内销将着重产销调节、推动释迦收入保险、农游券加码、企业采购平台等、启动加工等；外销则提高外销奖励额度，强化中国大陆以外市场，如新加坡、马来西亚、港澳等，目标外销释迦5000吨，莲雾1000吨。</p><p>大王释迦农特产品行负责人王俊雄说，释迦一遇热就会苦涩，做成饼干、果干都不行，通常只能加工成冰淇淋或冰棒，但冰淇淋能消耗的量又有多少？卖不完也吃不完。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>逢甲大学国贸系教授杨明宪强调，既然大陆已经暂停输入，这些多出来的量一定要销往境外，不能再回销到台湾，现阶段对中国大陆重启输入的期望不能太高，改变产品形式、分散市场还是重点工作。</p><p> </p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/zbpe-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=zbpe399&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_desktop-zbpe399_2.png?dHEiGouxxZpWwWTJE04dyGyUN74JOU2z" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_desktop-zbpe399_2.png?dHEiGouxxZpWwWTJE04dyGyUN74JOU2z" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/zbpe-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=zbpe399&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_mobile-zbpe399_2.png?qZnBcIGCJvPR8A2IAZOZuOxdoGZDatiI" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_mobile-zbpe399_2.png?qZnBcIGCJvPR8A2IAZOZuOxdoGZDatiI" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
