[台国防部：大陆075舰将改变对台作战模式](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210921-1195771)
------

<div>
<p>台湾国防部研判，随着中国大陆的075型两栖攻击舰已陆续加入作战序列，解放军将改变对台作战模式，并将能对台三栖登陆作战。</p><p>据联合新闻网报道，台国防部将“海空战力提升计划采购特别条例草案”送立法院待审，称大陆恐加速对台动用武力，因此台湾必须在短时间内建构“不对称战力”，以有效吓阻战端发生。</p><p>台国防部指出，排水量4万吨的075两栖攻击舰能投送大量部队与装备，并作为陆基直升机返程补给平台，能成攻台三栖作战平台，整理陆方言论，多称075两栖攻击舰除南中国海岛礁外，另一重点是执行对台军事任务。美国也曾就此威胁向台预警。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>台国防部称，075型两栖攻击舰将改变大陆对台作战模式，规模扩大为三栖登陆作战，大幅压缩台湾军队的接战应变时间，台湾亟须在短时间内建构相对性的武器装备，以空间换取时间，确保安全。</p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/zbpe-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=zbpe399&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_desktop-zbpe399_2.png?dHEiGouxxZpWwWTJE04dyGyUN74JOU2z" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_desktop-zbpe399_2.png?dHEiGouxxZpWwWTJE04dyGyUN74JOU2z" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/zbpe-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=zbpe399&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_mobile-zbpe399_2.png?qZnBcIGCJvPR8A2IAZOZuOxdoGZDatiI" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_mobile-zbpe399_2.png?qZnBcIGCJvPR8A2IAZOZuOxdoGZDatiI" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
