font-size: 12.0pt; line-height: 107%;">三天之后，其家遭查抄；</span><span style="font-family: SimSun; font-size: 12.0pt; line-height: 107%;"><o:p></o:p></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun; font-size: 12.0pt; line-height: 107%;">为使丈夫尽快平安返家，次日其妻尤修林紧急发出公开代祷信，以期其能早日无罪释放；但拘押至今仍不释放，且其被押后的具体情况一概缺详。</span><span style="font-family: SimSun; font-size: 12.0pt; line-height: 107%;"><o:p></o:p></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;
