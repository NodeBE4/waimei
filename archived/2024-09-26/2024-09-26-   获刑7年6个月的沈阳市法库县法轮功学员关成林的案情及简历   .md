<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p><p class="MsoNormal"><a name="_Hlk178297863"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">27</span>日，本网获悉</span></a><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">获刑<span lang="EN-US">7</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月的沈阳市法库县法轮功学员关成林的案情及简历，现在通报如下：<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">关成林：</span></b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">沈阳市法库县法轮功学员，男，<span lang="EN-US">1954</span>年生，<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">70</span>岁。家住法库县秀水河子镇喇嘛营子村。他于一九九七年开始修炼法轮功。一九九九年中共迫害法轮功后，关成林因为坚持法轮功信仰，被法库县公安局绑架到当地洗脑班，失去人身自由，遭受所谓的强制“转化学习”、非法监禁三个月。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">2023</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">29</span>日，关成林老人被法库县公安局秀水河派出所警察抓捕、审讯，被关押到法库县看守所。<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">28</span>日，沈阳市辽中区检察院作出不批准逮捕决定，关成林被刑事拘留一个月后，被释放回家，但仍继续遭到监视居住。后遭起诉，<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">10</span>日，关成林在家中再次被办案警察抓捕、关押到新民市看守所。<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">30</span>日，新民市法院对关成林被庭审。<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">22</span>日他被新民市法院判刑<span lang="EN-US">7</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月，并罚金二万元。刑期至<span lang="EN-US">2031</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">30</span>日。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p>
