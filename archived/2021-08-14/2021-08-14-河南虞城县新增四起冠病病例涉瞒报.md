[河南虞城县新增四起冠病病例涉瞒报](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210814-1181984)
------

<div>
<p>河南商丘疫情防控指挥部办公室今天通报疫情情况指出，虞城县昨天新增确诊病例四例，是因虞城县疫情管控不力并涉嫌瞒报疫情所致。同时，该县两地也被调整为高风险地区。</p><p>根据“网信商丘”微信公号和微博消息，商丘市虞城县昨天新增确诊病例四例，分别为病例尹某的儿子和女儿、尹某丈夫的两个妹妹。</p><p>经流调溯源确定，新增四例病例之一郭某某，上月30日到过商丘大商新玛特，与商丘士此次第二例确诊病例刘某某有过近距离无防护接触。通报指，因虞城县疫情管控不力并涉嫌瞒报疫情，导致此次疫情，相关情况正在调查。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>根据国家和河南省关于应对疫情精准防控工作要求，结合商丘市当前疫情形势，商丘市冠病疫情防控指挥部决定：自今日起，将虞城县人民医院东院区（健康路）、虞城县谷熟镇刘大庄村委何庄村疫情风险等级调整为高风险地区。实行封闭管理，人员足不出户，最大限度严防疫情风险传播。</p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
