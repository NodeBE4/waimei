[台湾将拟定民众到海外接种疫苗认证程序](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210814-1182184)
------

<div>
<p>台湾中央流行疫情指挥中心指挥官陈时中今天说，民众在海外接种COVID-19（2019冠状病毒疾病）疫苗须取得接种证明，未来也会拟定认证程序，符合规定就承认。</p><p>据台湾中央社报道，台湾帕劳旅游泡泡团今天上午重新出团，不少旅客想到帕劳打疫苗，也有人的目标是混打疫苗，但接种后回台，政府是否认可尚在未知。</p><p>陈时中下午在疫情记者会表示，不只帕劳，也有民众到美国或其他地方打疫苗，接种后都会核发相关证明，碍于目前没双边认证，只能以当地开立的文件去证明疫苗效果、有效性。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>陈时中说，未来民众接种后，须请当地接种单位开立证明，台湾会拟定认证程序，只要打的是台湾有紧急使用授权（EUA）疫苗、施打顺序符合台湾规定，就承认相关证明、疫苗效果。</p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
