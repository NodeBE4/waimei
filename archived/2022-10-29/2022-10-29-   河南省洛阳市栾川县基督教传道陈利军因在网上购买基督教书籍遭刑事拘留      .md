[河南省洛阳市栾川县基督教传道陈利军因在网上购买基督教书籍遭刑事拘留](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2022/10/blog-post_29.html)

<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">29</span>日，本网获悉：河南省洛阳市栾川县基督教传道陈利军因在网上购买基督教书籍于<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">17</span>日遭栾川县公安局以涉嫌诈骗罪刑事拘留。目前关押在栾川县看守所。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">陈利军于<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">13</span>号晚上<span lang="EN-US">21</span>点被当地公安机关司法部门派出所叫走，是因为信基督教信耶稣，在网上购买了几本有关与信基督教的书籍，就因为这买这些基督教书， 被抓捕至今未放。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体;
