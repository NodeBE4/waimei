<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">21</span>日，本网获悉被中共判刑<span lang="EN-US">13</span>年的云南省临沧市云县基督教家庭教会传道人鞠殿红的案情及简历，现在通报如下：<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">鞠殿红：</span></b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">女，<span lang="EN-US">1971</span>年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">10</span>日出生，吉林省临江市人，基督徒，云南省临沧市云县基督教一家庭教会传道人，云南教案受害人，中国在押良心犯。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">自<span lang="EN-US">2015</span>年以来，因其一行多人在云南省多地以家庭教会形式进行基督教传道和发展信徒，势力逐渐壮大，随遭中共云南当局警觉恐慌，很快组成<span lang="EN-US">2016</span>“<span lang="EN-US">YN3</span>﹒<span lang="EN-US">15</span>三班仆人派邪教组织项目”对其主要成员严打迫害；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2016</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">21</span>日，其及数十人在“云县教案大逮捕”中被临沧市云县警方统一抓捕、刑拘，被关押在临沧市云县临翔区看守所，同时，其住所亦遭查抄，搜走多份传道宣传品、传单、手机以及计算机等对象；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">同年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">25</span>日，其及其另外<span lang="EN-US">5</span>人均被以涉嫌“利用邪教组织破坏法律事实罪”转正式逮捕，被控“到云南省多地州市秘密聚会、传经布道和发展信徒，继续鼓吹、宣扬世界末日论，培养组织骨干，为秘密传教布道聚会创造条件和提供便利，重建<span lang="EN-US">'</span>三班仆人派<span lang="EN-US">'</span>组织体系等”，属于违法行为，并将其视为云县教案首犯；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2017</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">28</span>日，其被云南省云县法院一审以“组织、利用邪教组织破坏法律事实罪”重判有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">13</span>年，并处罚金人民币<span lang="EN-US">15</span>万元；其不服上诉；后经二审裁决，依然维持原判，刑期至<span lang="EN-US">2029</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">20</span>日；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">据悉，其坚称信仰自由、信仰无罪，并称信仰的不是邪教，其所有书籍在官方认可的“三自”教会里都可买到。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体;
