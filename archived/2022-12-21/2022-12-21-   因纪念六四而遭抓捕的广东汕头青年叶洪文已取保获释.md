<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal" style="tab-stops: 322.0pt;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">21</span>日，本网获悉：今年六四前夕因发文念六四而遭抓捕的广东汕头青年叶洪文已取保获释。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">叶洪文：</span></b><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">因其身为左派工运支持者，在近年来的广东劳工维权与声援活动中，逐渐从对劳工经济的要求转变成对政治的要求，并意识到纪念“六四天安门事件”就是中国劳工运动的“首要部分”，遂于<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">19</span>日发推文称：“六四去中心广场去游行，无论代价。因为这是我的责任，立帖为证”；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2022</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">4</span>日，尽管当天户外风雨交加，其依然身着白衣黑裤，手提两个承装祭奠物品的白色塑料袋，额绑红布，来到广东省汕头市人民广场，点燃蜡烛，纪念<span lang="EN-US">8964</span>那些为争取自由民主而牺牲的前辈，并拍下“坦克人”照片发于网上；随后，其即与外界失联；为知其下落，曾有网友多次尝试在<span lang="EN-US">QQ</span>与微信上与其联系，但都未予回复；原定若被警方带走，其女友就立即代为发推，但也未见发推；后几经辗转终于获知，其因纪念六四，已被汕头市警方带走秘押，罪由不详，同时其<span lang="EN-US">QQ</span>号及相关真实信息被腾讯公司隐藏，唯其推文（尤其是浏览量几十万的示威推文）有幸尚存；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2022</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">15</span>日获悉，其已被取保释放。此前被羁押地点不明。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"></p><div class="separator" style="clear: both;
