<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">18</span>日，本网获悉：曾获刑<span lang="EN-US">4</span>年的江苏省南京市残疾异见人士邵明亮于<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">22</span>日再次被抓捕，羁押至今。现被关押在南京市第二看守所（原鼓楼区、建邺区看守所合并），地址：南京市建邺区下圩村<span lang="EN-US">19</span>号，联系电话<span lang="EN-US"> 02584427947</span>。而家属一直没有收到通知，连衣服都送不进去。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">南京公民邵明亮，草根出身，民主斗士，多年来遭受南京当局的疯狂报复，被精神病、被车祸致残。<span lang="EN-US">2019</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月入狱后四年监禁期间遭受非法虐待，坚决不认罪。<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月，九死一生出狱后又立即被南京当局非法软禁、不让看病。<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">22</span>日，再次被抓捕，羁押至今。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><b><span style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">因反对马列主义而一直高调反共，横遭中共当局的极端打压逼迫，长期被囚禁在响塘村家中，禁止外出购物、就医等；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">2013</span><span style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">年，曾因在南京市审计学院门口、浦口区闹市区、浦口区政府前举牌宣传宪政、民主、自由、人权遭警方殴打，后又多次被警方强制送精神病院关押；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">2014</span><span style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">25</span>日，曾在南京浦口区政府门前遭政府默许的车辆辗压全身，导致住院两年，落下终身残疾（盆骨、骶骨、耻骨、肋骨、脊椎、颈椎、胸骨骨折、头、肺、脊骨损伤<span lang="EN-US">)</span>，只能靠轮椅生活；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">2016</span><span style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月、<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月，又因反党言论两次被刑拘，其后一直遭家中囚禁；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">2018</span><span style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">14</span>日，中共两会期间，因发反党及嘲讽修宪言论被南京警方处以行政拘留<span lang="EN-US">14</span>天，期间又遭警方暴力殴打；<span lang="EN-US">2019</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">16</span>日，再次被南京市浦口区警方抓捕，自此失联；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">2019</span><span style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 12.0pt;">年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">22</span>日，被浦口区检察院以涉嫌<span lang="EN-US">“</span>寻衅滋事罪<span lang="EN-US">”</span>正式批捕，并被关押在南京市浦口区看守所，指控因其在网上发涉及民主宪政要求的微信和几十条推特内容，所以属于违法行为；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 楷体;
