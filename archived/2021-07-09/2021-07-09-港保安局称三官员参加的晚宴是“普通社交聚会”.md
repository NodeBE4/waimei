[港保安局称三官员参加的晚宴是“普通社交聚会”](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210709-1166749)
------

<div>
<p>香港保安局副局长区志光、入境处长区嘉宏及海关关长邓以海出席一个私人会所的饭局时违反限聚令被票控，饭局中有人涉嫌在住所企图强奸被起诉。保安局发言人今（9日）天进一步澄清，出席晚宴的政府官员只有三人，三人受邀赴宴，因宴请者表示晚宴地点是私人地方，因此误以为地点不受限聚令所规管。</p><p>据明报报道，保安局称晚宴菜式是一般火锅食材，并强调当日晚宴是“普通社交聚会”。</p><p>港保安局发言人说，三人再次承认有疏忽及敏感度不足，就事件表示歉意，日后出席活动会加倍谨慎。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>保安局发言人强调，三名官员不涉及上述的刑事案件。 <br> </p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
