[梵蒂冈感谢各界关心教宗手术  蔡英文列首位](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210709-1166694)
------

<div>
<p>天主教教宗方济各（Pope Francis）因大肠炎住院，梵蒂冈官网昨天特别列名感谢收到的慰问讯息，其中台湾总统蔡英文排在首位。</p><p>据台湾中央社报道，84岁的教宗方济各4日在罗马双子医院（Gemelli Hospital）做大肠炎手术，术后一度发烧。教廷昨天表示，他的手术顺利，未引发并发症。</p><p>教宗7日下午在推特感谢来自世界各地的温暖讯息，也感谢大家对他健康情况的关心。教宗推文指出，这几天收到的关怀讯息让他十分感动，要谢谢大家与他同在并为他祷告。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>教廷官方新闻网站“梵蒂冈新闻”（Vatican News）与“罗马观察报”（L'Osservatore Romano）都报道，多位领导人向教宗表达了亲切关怀，其中包括台湾总统蔡英文。蔡英文在5日致函了关心教宗的健康状况。</p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
