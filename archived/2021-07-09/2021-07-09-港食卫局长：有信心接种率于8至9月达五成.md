[港食卫局长：有信心接种率于8至9月达五成](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210709-1166727)
------

<div>
<p>香港食物及卫生局长陈肇始今天出席立法会一个委员会时表示，香港目前接种率约36.4%，有信心在8至9月接种率达五成，并称目前正研究逐步恢复与大陆及澳门的跨境往来，完成磋商后将公布细节。</p><p>据星岛日报报道，陈肇始说，现在至下月底为接种疫苗的关键时刻，截至前日，香港接种率是约36.4%。按目前进度推算，有信心在8至9月，香港接种率达五成，但最少要达七成才足够。</p><p>另外，有关香港与大陆及澳门的通关进度，陈肇始指，政府一直透过联防联控机制，与两地密切沟通及联系，正积极研究在疫情受控，且不会增加双方风险下，逐步恢复与两地居民跨境往来，完成磋商后会公布细节。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
