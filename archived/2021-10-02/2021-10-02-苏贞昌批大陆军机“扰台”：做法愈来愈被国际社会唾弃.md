------

<div>
<p>中国大陆昨天派38架军机进入台湾防空识别区。台湾行政院长苏贞昌今天（2日）批评，中国大陆这样做法，已经愈来愈被国际社会唾弃。</p><p>据《自由时报》报道，苏贞昌今天南下高雄，视察桥头科学园区联外道路。他接受媒体联访时发表以上评论。</p><p>苏贞昌说，中国大陆一直穷兵黩武，破坏区域和平，并且有很多“鸭霸”行径，“像这次自由潜水世锦赛，硬要我们拿掉国旗，有10个国家也愿意撤下自己的国旗跟台湾同步抗议！”</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>苏贞昌称：“可见中国（大陆）这样做法，已经愈来愈被国际社会、被世界唾弃！”</p><p>中国大陆在十一国庆当天共派出38架次军机进入台湾西南防空识别区，是台湾国防部公布解放军动态以来架次最多的一次。</p><p> </p><div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>            <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/zbpe-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=zbpe399&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_desktop-zbpe399_2.png?dHEiGouxxZpWwWTJE04dyGyUN74JOU2z" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_desktop-zbpe399_2.png?dHEiGouxxZpWwWTJE04dyGyUN74JOU2z" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/zbpe-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=zbpe399&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_mobile-zbpe399_2.png?qZnBcIGCJvPR8A2IAZOZuOxdoGZDatiI" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_mobile-zbpe399_2.png?qZnBcIGCJvPR8A2IAZOZuOxdoGZDatiI" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
