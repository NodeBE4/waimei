[因言获罪的河南民主异见人士曹培植被判刑2年6个月](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2023/07/26.html)
------

<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">23</span>日，本网获悉：因言获罪遭抓捕的河南异议人士曹培植于<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">21</span>日被法院判刑<span lang="EN-US">2</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月。曹培植此次被抓的起因是在他建的微信群“温馨小屋”评论习近平及他的执政方针。法院说判决书和起诉书不让家属带走，只让家人看一眼。目前家属还没有看到起诉书和判决书。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">曹培植先生多年坚持民主自由的普世价值原则，<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">3</span>日被河南省义马市公安局以寻衅滋事罪刑事拘留，<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">4</span>日被批准逮捕，现被异地关押在渑池县看守所。现被判刑<span lang="EN-US">2</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">曹培植简历：</span></b><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">1960</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年代出生，河南省义马市人，六四亲历者，《零八宪章》第<span lang="EN-US">10</span>批签署人，原“温馨小屋”群主，社会时政关注者，民主异见人士，中国在押政治犯。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">1989</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年，曾因参与年天安门民主运动并亲历“六四惨案”，遂自此改变了人生轨迹，坚定的走上一条中国民主运动不归路；曾因以民主推手、民权精英、博客骑士自许，而创建“温馨小屋”博客群，并在其努力经营下，使该博客群的点击量高达<span lang="EN-US">70</span>万人次；也正是因其频发民主信息和对中共当局不利的时政批评性信息，故而引起河南省当局的极度不悦与伺机打击；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2022</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">3</span>日，因其在微信群里传播“未经官方证实的海外消息，并对党和国家领导人有侮辱性言论”，遂被河南省义马市警方以涉嫌“寻衅滋事罪”刑事拘留；同年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">4</span>日，又被义马市检察院以同罪名正式批捕。曹培植于<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">21</span>日被法院判刑<span lang="EN-US">2</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月。刑期至：<span lang="EN-US">2025</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">2</span>日。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体;
