mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">），又被译为岗吉珠巴嘉，四川省甘孜藏族羌族自治州（简称甘孜州）色达县人，原色达县霍西乡某私立学校教师，笔名血火狮（藏语称“岗麦达”），藏族知名作家，政治异见人士，中国在押政治犯。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">）》、《今日的眼泪（</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: &quot;Microsoft Himalaya&quot;; font-size: 12.0pt;">དེ་རིང་གི་མིག་ཆུ</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">）》、《<span lang="EN-US">2008</span>年全境红色血书（</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: &quot;Microsoft Himalaya&quot;; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">）》等多部著作，涉猎小说、杂文、评述、诗歌等多种文体，并曾独立编辑两份藏文报刊《岁月的颜色》（</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: &quot;Microsoft Himalaya&quot;; font-size: 12.0pt;">ལོ་ཟླའི་ཁ་དོག</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">ཁ་བཏགས་དཀར་པོའི་ཚགས་དེབ</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">），故而获得藏区同胞读者的广泛好评；虽然其多数作品并无明显触及中共当局敏感神经的话题，仅表达了其对藏族文化、藏族宗教信仰以及对民族和故乡故土的深厚情感，但因其两首短诗揭露了<span lang="EN-US">2008</span>年中共镇压西藏期间，藏民遭受的痛苦折磨以及在铺天盖地的官员贪腐和牟取暴利之下，百姓所面临的艰难处境，遂很快引起发了中共四川当局的高度紧张，并成为被政府严管监控和伺机打击的对象；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2012</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">15</span>日，其被甘孜州色达县警方突然莫名抓走，后又被甘孜州雅江县检察院以涉嫌“分裂、危害国家安全罪”正式批捕，指控其因是“西藏活动人士”并建立名为“抗红组织”的团体，遂起言行触犯了国家的法律法规，应予批捕、重判；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2013</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">1</span>日，其案在四川省甘孜州法院开庭审理，至同年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">16</span>日，终被雅江县法院以“分裂、危害国家安全罪”重判有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">5</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月，并送色达县监狱服刑；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2016</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">16</span>日，被减刑提前释放，但次日又因其将达赖喇嘛的法相放在头上而被当局指其认罪“态度不良”，故又被色达县派出所警方拘留<span lang="EN-US">17</span>天；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2021</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">23</span>日，其再次因之前撰写并发表过数本有关西藏命运及局势的书本和报刊，并且创建了“反红抵抗组织”，而被甘孜州色达县警方以涉嫌“分裂和危害国家安全罪”拘捕；与其同期被捕的另有<span lang="EN-US">5</span>名同区域藏人作家及社会活动人士，涉嫌罪名相同；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2022</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月，其一行<span lang="EN-US">6</span>人案在四川省甘孜州中级法院开庭审理，最终被以“分裂、危害国家安全罪” 统一判处<span lang="EN-US">4-14</span>年不等刑期；其中，其被重判有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">14</span>年，刑期至<span lang="EN-US">2035</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">22</span>日；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">据悉，首次服刑期间，曾惨遭狱警酷刑折磨，后因健康持续恶化，狱方才不得不将其提前释放；但获释后，因其身心状况一直未见好转，不仅出现严重的精神障碍，视力及记忆力也急剧下降，肾和胃的功能严重退化，故其现在狱中的身体状况令人堪忧。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体;
