font-size: 12.0pt; line-height: 107%; mso-ansi-language: EN-US; mso-bidi-font-family: &quot;Times New Roman&quot;;">女，<span lang="EN-US">1958</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">3</span>日出生，山西省霍州市人，基督徒，前山西师范大学老干部处公职人员，原山西省临汾金灯台家庭教会负责人、传道人，中国在押良心犯。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal" style="line-height: 107%; margin-bottom: 8.0pt; tab-stops: 122.5pt;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; line-height: 107%; mso-ansi-language: EN-US; mso-bidi-font-family: &quot;Times New Roman&quot;;">自<span lang="EN-US">1998</span>年全职负责教会以来，将原本几十人的小教会发展成拥有<span lang="EN-US">5</span>万名基督徒的大型教会<span lang="EN-US">; <o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal" style="line-height: 107%; margin-bottom: 8.0pt; tab-stops: 122.5pt;"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; line-height: 107%; <o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal" style="line-height: 107%; margin-bottom: 8.0pt; tab-stops: 122.5pt;"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; line-height: 107%; </span>后因双方就当地警方野蛮冲击打砸、围殴教会及基督徒的理赔事宜等在协商中发生异议，<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">23</span>日该教会再遭武警包围<span lang="EN-US">; </span>同年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">25</span>日，因其与<span lang="EN-US">6</span>名教会领袖到太原市上访维权，而被警方在途中劫持<span lang="EN-US">; </span>同年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">11</span>日，又被山西省临汾市警方以涉嫌<span lang="EN-US">"</span>非法占用农用地罪<span lang="EN-US">"</span>、<span lang="EN-US">"</span>聚众扰乱交通秩序罪<span lang="EN-US">"</span>刑拘，关押于山西省太原拘留所<span lang="EN-US">; 2009</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">21</span>日，被山西省临汾市尧都区批准以涉嫌同罪名正式批捕<span lang="EN-US">;<o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal" style="line-height: 107%; margin-bottom: 8.0pt; font-size: 12.0pt; line-height: 107%; mso-ansi-language: EN-US; mso-bidi-font-family: &quot;Times New Roman&quot;;">年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">25</span>日，被山西省临汾市尧都区法院以同罪名判处有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">7</span>年，并处罚金<span lang="EN-US">3</span>万元，后送往山西省新康监狱服刑<span lang="EN-US">; <o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal" style="line-height: 107%; margin-bottom: 8.0pt; tab-stops: 122.5pt;"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; line-height: 107%; mso-ansi-language: EN-US; mso-bidi-font-family: &quot;Times New Roman&quot;;">2016</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; line-height: 107%; mso-ansi-language: EN-US; mso-bidi-font-family: &quot;Times New Roman&quot;;">年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">10</span>日，刑满释放<span lang="EN-US">;
