<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; line-height: 115%; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-GB">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-GB">1</span>月<span lang="EN-GB">4</span>日，本网获悉山西汾阳基督徒 “偷越国境”案受害者之一张耀文简历，现通报如下：<span lang="EN-GB"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; line-height: 115%; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">张耀文<span lang="EN-GB">:</span>山西省籍人，基督徒，山西省太原市郇城归正教会骨干信徒，郇城归正教会在汾阳市的同工，中国在押良心犯。<span lang="EN-GB"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; line-height: 115%; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">因笃信耶稣基督而成为基督徒，并在山西省太原市郇城教会做同工（即为教会一起努力侍奉、祷告、义务服务的人），又因所在教会是家庭教会，且拒不接受中共当局认定的<span lang="EN-GB">"</span>三自<span lang="EN-GB">"</span>教会管辖，故而其及在教会的活动深受当局监控和 骚扰<span lang="EN-GB">;<o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-GB" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; line-height: 115%; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;"><span style="mso-spacerun: yes;">&nbsp;</span>2020</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; line-height: 115%; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-GB">1</span>月<span lang="EN-GB">28</span>日—<span lang="EN-GB">31</span>日，因与郇城归正教会传道人安彦魁、同工张乘豪，以及该教会派往吕梁市汾阳市的同工张立功、王润云、王世强、宋守山等共七位神职人员和事工成员，一同前往马来西亚首都吉隆坡参加唐崇荣牧师（著名基督教华人布道家和神学家）组织的<span lang="EN-GB">"2020</span>福音与文化大会<span lang="EN-GB">"</span>，尽管当时其持护照及签证出入海关均属正常，也无任何违法违规行为，但其等七人的统一出境参会行动最终仍引起中共山西当局的高度警觉与关注<span lang="EN-GB">;<o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-GB" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; line-height: 115%; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;"><span style="mso-spacerun: yes;">&nbsp;</span>2021</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; line-height: 115%; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-GB">7</span>月<span lang="EN-GB">28</span>日，其被汾阳市警方突然以涉嫌「偷越国（边）境罪」刑拘<span lang="EN-GB">; </span>另有<span lang="EN-GB">6</span>名曾与其一同前往境外参加唐崇荣牧师组织的<span lang="EN-GB">"2020</span>福音与文化大会<span lang="EN-GB">"</span>的神职人员与同工亦在同天被刑拘<span lang="EN-GB">; </span>后其被汾阳市检察院以涉嫌同罪名予以正式批捕<span lang="EN-GB">;<o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-GB" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; line-height: 115%; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;"><span style="mso-spacerun: yes;">&nbsp;</span>2021</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; </span>据悉，其<span lang="EN-GB">5</span>人在庭审时均对自己的信仰与追求倍感喜乐和坚定，并当庭向法官宣讲福音，使在场之人对福音有了深刻认识。 <span lang="EN-GB"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;
