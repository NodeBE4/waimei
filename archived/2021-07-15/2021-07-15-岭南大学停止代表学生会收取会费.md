[岭南大学停止代表学生会收取会费](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210715-1169268)
------

<div>
<p>香港岭南大学学生会代表会临时行政委员会表示，今早收到校方通知，即时停止代表学生会收取会费。</p><p>据香港电台网站报道，岭南大学学生会表示，校方今早向全体师生发出电邮，指近年时常接获由同学、家长及公众就有关学生会的投诉，将学生会费连同学费一同收取，属不恰当和造成误导。</p><p>校方同时指出，岭南大学学生会为根据《社团条例》注册的独立社团，因此由学生会负责收取会费及管理财政为更合适安排。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>此前，香港城市大学、香港中文大学、香港大学、香港理工大学均已宣布停止代学生会收取会费。</p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
