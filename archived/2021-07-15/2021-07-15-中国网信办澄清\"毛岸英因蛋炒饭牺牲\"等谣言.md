[中国网信办澄清"毛岸英因蛋炒饭牺牲"等谣言](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210715-1169475)
------

<div>
<p>中国网信办举报中心主办“2021中国网络诚信大会”，澄清一系列谣言，其中就包括"毛岸英因蛋炒饭牺牲"。</p><p>据澎湃新闻报道，上述大会今日（15日）发布涉党史辟谣榜。大会表示，涉党史网络谣言，严重污染网络生态、误导社会公众、损害党的形象，发布辟谣榜是为了澄清模糊认识、纠正误解误读、批驳错误言论，为建党百年营造良好网上舆论氛围。</p><p>榜单内容主要包括批驳诋毁、抹黑革命领袖等谣言，如毛泽东并非《沁园春·雪》原作者、“中共启封‘邓颖超日记’”供党史研究的不实信息；辟除否定、污蔑英雄烈士等谣言，如歪曲编造狼牙山五壮士两名战士不是“跳崖”而是“溜崖”、捏造毛岸英同志牺牲是因为做蛋炒饭暴露了目标、诬蔑雷锋同志做好事是假的等谣言。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
