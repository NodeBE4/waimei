[郑州中原区政府违法占地达八年之久，宋会春申请行政复议要求依法予以查处](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2021/11/blog-post_18.html)

<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">6</span>日，本网获悉：<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">5</span>日，郑州宋会春向自然资源部邮寄行政复议申请书，请求责令国家自然资源督察济南局对宋会春的举报依法作出处置。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">宋会春系郑州市河南省郑州市中原区宋庄村村民。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2021</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">6</span>日。宋会春向国家自然资源督察济南局邮寄举报信，举报郑州市中原区人民政府自<span lang="EN-US">2013</span>年以来大规模非法占用郑州中原区宋庄集体土地至今，已达八年之久，请求该局依法查处。但该局于<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">8</span>日收到宋会春的举报信后，至今未作出处置，也未告知未作出处置的理由。宋会春提起行政复议申请，请求查处中原区政府违法占地的行为。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2013</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月，在没有完成土地征收、也没有取得《施工许可证》的情况下，郑州市中原区政府与郑州市中原区宋庄村民签订了拆迁安置补偿协议，违反了《土地管理法》有关规定。因手续不全，中原区政府八年未能交付安置房，村民至今不能回迁，叫苦不迭。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">宋会春希望自然资源部依法作出行政复议决定，促使有权机关对中原区政府的非法占地作出处置，维护法律尊严，维护村民的合法权益。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p>
