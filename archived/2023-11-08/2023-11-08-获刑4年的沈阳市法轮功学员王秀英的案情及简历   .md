[获刑4年的沈阳市法轮功学员王秀英的案情及简历](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2023/11/4_8.html)
------

<div>
<p><span></span></p><a name='more'></a><p class="MsoNormal"><br /></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月8日，本网获悉获刑<span lang="EN-US">4</span>年的沈阳市法轮功学员王秀英的案情及简历，现在通报如下：<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;"><b>王秀英：</b>沈阳市法轮功学员，女，<span lang="EN-US">1965</span>年生，一九九八年，王秀英开始修炼法轮功。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt;">2019</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;">年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">30</span>日，王秀英因向路人宣传法轮功，被于洪区城东湖派出所警察抓捕。年后获刑一年六个月，罚金五千元。王秀英于<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">29</span>日刑满释放回家。仅仅两年多后，<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">16</span>日，王秀英因宣传法轮功，再次被于洪区城东湖派出所警察抓捕，因血压超高、看守所拒收，次日，她被改成监视居住。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt;">2023</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;">年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">28</span>日，王秀英被于洪区检察院起诉至于洪区法院。于<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">7</span>日被抓进沈阳市第一看守所关押。<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">24</span>日王秀英被于洪区法院判刑<span lang="EN-US">4</span>年。王秀英上诉到沈阳市中级法院，<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">24</span>日，二审法院维持原判。刑期至：<span lang="EN-US">2027</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><br /><p></p>
