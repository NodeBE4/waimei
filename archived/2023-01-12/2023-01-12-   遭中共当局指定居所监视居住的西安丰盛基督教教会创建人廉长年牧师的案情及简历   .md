[遭中共当局指定居所监视居住的西安丰盛基督教教会创建人廉长年牧师的案情及简历](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2023/01/blog-post_34.html)
------

<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">12</span>日，本网获悉遭中共当局指定居所监视居住的西安丰盛基督教教会创建人廉长年的案情及简历，现在通报如下：<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">廉长年<span lang="EN-US">:</span></span></b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">河南省人，基督徒，原河南“中华福音团契”（中国五大家庭教会之一）老一辈牧者，西安丰盛基督教教会创建人，主任牧师，中国在押良心犯。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">自<span lang="EN-US">1970</span>年代末<span lang="EN-US">-1980</span>年代初起，因笃信基督教，即开始全职服侍教会；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">1988</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年，“中华福音团契”在河南省南阳市唐河县成立，故其曾长期在该教会进行司职；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">后又因该教会发展壮大，成为拥有一千万多信众、数百名教牧团队、数十个对外宣教差会的最具规模的中国家庭教会，故而几十年来，其作为教会颇具影响力的神职人员，曾屡遭逼迫；曾因秉承基督信仰而被中共当局多次抓捕、拘禁和遭殴打；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">1991</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月，曾以“中华福音团契”差传者的身份到湖北省襄阳市和襄樊市发展教会，而被湖北省警方以非法传教为由拘捕，后被判囚<span lang="EN-US">2</span>个月；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">获释后，因又到陕西省进行宣教活动，自此其就在西安市建立了丰盛基督教教会；但自<span lang="EN-US">2018</span>年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月中共新《宗教事务条例》正式生效实施起，全国各地基督教家庭教会纷纷遭遇空前严打和迫害，其所建西安丰盛教会亦遭严打和冲击；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2022</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年，自<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">20</span>日始，其教会信徒突遭西安市灞桥区十里铺派出所警方大规模口头传唤和上门问讯，甚至有些人家被多次搜查，旨在胁迫信徒们作证，控告牧师诈骗和传播邪教，甚至要求信徒捏造有关教会奉献的虚假信息；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2022</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">17</span>日，其及妻子、儿子、儿媳以及教会同工等<span lang="EN-US">6</span>人被当地派出所警方强行带走并被抄家，次日，其父子二人和教会财务同工付娟等人被西安市灞桥区警方以涉嫌“诈骗罪”指定居所监视居住；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2022</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">19</span>日，其教会被西安市民政局迅速发布通告取缔，理由是其与“中华福音总会”、“中华福音总会”西安区联会，“未经登记”就擅自以社会团体名义开展活动，故被认定为非法社会组织；迄今为止，其案虽已有多名维权律师介入，但其等<span lang="EN-US">3</span>人被秘押将近半年，警方一直以当事人案情涉及危害国家安全为由拒绝律师会见。目前被羁押地点不详。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"></p><div class="separator" style="clear: both;
