[屡遭中共迫害的辽宁抚顺维权人士赵振甲在北京房山去世](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2022/11/blog-post_87.html)

<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">10</span>日，本网获悉：今天<span lang="EN-US">12:40</span>左右，辽宁抚顺维权人士赵振甲 在北京市房山区星城医院因抢救无效过世。赵振甲生前屡遭中共迫害，在生命的最后时期仍在维权的路上。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">赵振甲简介：</span></b><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">1950</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">13</span>日出生，辽宁省抚顺市人，民主维权公民，政治犯。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">曾于<span lang="EN-US">1974</span>年因写评论性文章被中共当局定为“反革命集团主犯”，以“反革命罪”判处死刑，后缓期<span lang="EN-US">2</span>年执行，直至<span lang="EN-US">1985</span>年才被辽宁省沈阳市中级法院改判为无罪；之后又因其平反的善后工作得不到落实，遂多次进京上访，至<span lang="EN-US">2000</span>年又被辽宁省抚顺市当局劳动教养<span lang="EN-US">3</span>年；<span lang="EN-US">2008</span>年再次被抚顺市当局劳动教养<span lang="EN-US">1</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月；<span lang="EN-US">2012</span>年因参与解救被非法关押在湖南省郴州市驻京办“黑监狱”中的访民，第三次被北京市当局劳动教养<span lang="EN-US">1</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月；<span lang="EN-US">2013</span>年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">19</span>日，期满获释；获释后，因与张福英等多个维权公民在北京南站附近等地，拉横幅举行“平反赵紫阳”﹑“纪念六四”活动，再度引起中共当局的恐慌忌恨，随于<span lang="EN-US">2013</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">9</span>日被北京警方以涉嫌“寻衅滋事罪”刑事拘留；同年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">16</span>日，被北京市海淀区检察院以涉嫌“聚众扰乱公共场所秩序罪”正式批捕；<span lang="EN-US">2014</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">18</span>日，被北京市淀区法院更换罪名，以“寻衅滋事罪”被判有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">1</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>个月，至<span lang="EN-US">2015</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月刑满获释。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2017</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月—<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月，因再次在北京发起要求落实“两办”反截访《意见》活动，遂数次遭截访、训诫等恐吓打压，至<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">9</span>日，被辽宁省抚顺市新抚区警方突然以涉嫌“寻衅滋事罪” 刑拘；<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">24</span>日，被转正式逮捕；<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">8</span>日，被监视居住于家中；<span lang="EN-US">2017</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">13</span>日，突然又被抚顺市新抚区法院以“寻衅滋事罪”判处有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">1</span>年，并被收监。获释后，赵振甲一直继续维权，直到生命的最后一刻。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"></p><div class="separator" style="clear: both;
