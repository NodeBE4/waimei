[马英九：蔡英文应为城中城火灾道歉](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20211016-1204037)
------

<div>
<p>高雄“城中城”大楼发生重大死伤火灾，台湾前总统马英九今天（16日）说，蔡英文总统应该为此道歉。</p><p>据中时电子报报道，马英九今天下午到花莲参加花莲市公所举办的健走活动，被媒体问到“城中城”大火时说，他昨天已前往表达哀悼。</p><p>马英九指，这件事显然高雄市政府责无旁贷，希望主管机关赶快查明真相，确定责任归属，要保证以后不会再发生，并说：“我觉得蔡总统应该要道歉”。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>经过13小时的马拉松式灭火、抢救，这场发生在高雄“城中城”40年老旧大楼的火灾，直到前天下午4时完成火场搜索。消防局公布大火酿成46死41伤，创下台湾史上单一建筑物死亡人数第二多的火患纪录，仅次于1995年台中卫尔康西餐厅大火的64死11伤。</p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/zbpe-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=zbpe399&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_desktop-zbpe399_2.png?dHEiGouxxZpWwWTJE04dyGyUN74JOU2z" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_desktop-zbpe399_2.png?dHEiGouxxZpWwWTJE04dyGyUN74JOU2z" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/zbpe-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=zbpe399&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_mobile-zbpe399_2.png?qZnBcIGCJvPR8A2IAZOZuOxdoGZDatiI" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_mobile-zbpe399_2.png?qZnBcIGCJvPR8A2IAZOZuOxdoGZDatiI" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
