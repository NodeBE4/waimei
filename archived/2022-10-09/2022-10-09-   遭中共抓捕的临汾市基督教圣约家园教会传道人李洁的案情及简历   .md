font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">李洁：</span></b><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">1988</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">7</span>日出生，山西省临汾市人，基督徒，临汾市基督教圣约家园教会传道人，中国在押良心犯。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2018</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月，曾因参与成都秋雨圣约教会王怡牧师发起的“牧者联署：为基督信仰的声明”签名（该声明呼吁中国当局停止一切针对基督教会的压制和迫害，尊重人类的信仰和良心自由），而被山西当局视为重点维稳对象；曾多次遭遇被喝茶、被警告、被断水断电断煤气，甚至寒冷的冬天遭强逼搬家，其教会亦时常被民宗局官员带着多人上门骚扰、威胁和冲击，试图以此来对其等神职人员与众信徒施压，接受管控；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2022</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">19</span>日，因其教会在户外举办<span lang="EN-US">70</span>人的“亲子营”活动，故而突遭当地警方<span lang="EN-US">100</span>余人的围堵和冲击，并将现场所有人悉数带走，并扣押所有人的手机、计算机、硬盘、投影仪、凳子、汽车等物品；之后虽有部分小孩被送回，但所有成人均被扣留，且其本人住处被搜查；<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">23</span>日，其与妻子李珊珊、教会传道同工韩晓栋（共<span lang="EN-US">3</span>人）被临汾市警方统一以涉嫌“诈骗罪”指定居所监视居住；同年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">2</span>日，其妻子终获取保释放，但其及韩晓栋传道仍被续押，并被山西省临汾市警方以涉嫌“诈骗罪”转为刑拘；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2022</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">30</span>日，其与韩晓栋二人又被临汾市检察院以同罪名正式批捕。</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">目前被羁押于山西省尧都区看守所。</span></p>
