<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">29</span>日，本网获悉获刑<span lang="EN-US">5</span>年的山东省法轮功学员彭树明的案情及简历，现在通报如下：<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">彭树明：</span></b><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">1971</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年出生，吉林省白城市洮南市籍人，长住山东省聊城市高唐县时风路团结小区，自办中医诊所医师，自由信仰追求者，法轮功信仰者，中国在押良心犯。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">早年自<span lang="EN-US">20</span>岁左右起，因师承一位老中医学习中医治疗，后在聊城市高唐县城开中医诊所行医<span lang="EN-US">20</span>余年，医治了许多疑难顽症患者，故而成为当地有口皆碑、受人尊敬和信任的好中医（大夫）；但自从<span lang="EN-US">1996</span>年开始锻炼和信仰法轮功以来，屡遭山东当局关押和迫害；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2005</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">8</span>日，曾因在当地高唐县城里喷法轮功宣传标语，而被高唐县人和派出所警方抓捕，并关押在高唐看守所，为此其曾绝食<span lang="EN-US">4</span>天反迫害；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2014</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">1</span>日，其在诊所行医时，又被高唐县汇鑫派出所警方莫名抓走，并被送高唐看守所关押；在被抓捕过程中，遂其在被非法关押在高唐看守所<span lang="EN-US">1</span>年多后，竟以此为因由被山东省高唐县法院，因一警察自己摔断脚脖子冤判<span lang="EN-US">3</span>年，后在当地聊城监狱服刑；刑满出狱后，其一边行医工作一边继续锻炼并宣传法轮功，故而遭到中共政府的<span lang="EN-US">610</span>法轮功监管办公室的更强烈的打压；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2021</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">12</span>日，因其用手机在微信中传播法轮功相关信息，被中共网警发现，遂被原籍吉林省洮南市警方跨省抓捕，后被以涉嫌“邪教罪”关押在吉林省白城市洮南市看守所；同年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">14</span>日，又被洮南市检察院以同罪名正式批捕；<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">31</span>日，其案被洮南市检察院提起公诉，并被移交至吉林省白城市洮南市法院；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2022</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月，知其已被洮南市法院最终以“利用邪教组织破坏法律实施罪”判处有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US"> 5</span>年，刑期至<span lang="EN-US">2026</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">11</span>日；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">据悉，其在洮南市看守所关押期间，曾屡遭犯人恶意毒打，警方却故意视而不见；其老母病故，妻子乳腺癌手术，家属多次请求让其回家探视一下，均遭拒绝；家属为其聘请律师作无罪辩护，亦被洮南市法院非法阻止。目前在山东省济南监狱服刑。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"></p><div class="separator" style="clear: both;
