[常州万邦拆迁公司强拆民房致老人受惊死亡，李晓亮维权三年未果还遭遇四次传唤](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2021/12/blog-post_35.html)

<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal" style="line-height: 107%; margin-bottom: 8.0pt; tab-stops: 122.5pt;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; line-height: 107%; mso-ansi-language: EN-US; mso-bidi-font-family: &quot;Times New Roman&quot;;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">31</span>日，本网获悉：<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">20</span>日早上六点许，江苏省常州市武进区湖塘镇古方村村民李晓亮在北京上访时，途径丰台区王佐镇沙锅村公交站附近遭遇不明身份六人拦截，被塞进别克商务车，中途转换为<span lang="EN-US">15</span>座面包车（车牌：京<span lang="EN-US">EH7061</span>），当晚九点许，押送到常州武进区湖塘中心派出所。该该派出所以“扰乱单位秩序”为由，对李晓亮进行传唤，至次日晚九点许释放，并出具了传唤证。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal" style="line-height: 107%; margin-bottom: 8.0pt; tab-stops: 122.5pt;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; line-height: 107%; mso-ansi-language: EN-US; mso-bidi-font-family: &quot;Times New Roman&quot;;">李晓亮家有三处房屋，即常州市武进区湖塘镇古方村东村<span lang="EN-US">161</span>、<span lang="EN-US">163</span>、<span lang="EN-US">163-2</span>号，总建筑面积为<span lang="EN-US">1008.48</span>平方米。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal" style="line-height: 107%; margin-bottom: 8.0pt; tab-stops: 122.5pt;"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; line-height: 107%; mso-ansi-language: EN-US; mso-bidi-font-family: &quot;Times New Roman&quot;;">2007</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; line-height: 107%; mso-ansi-language: EN-US; mso-bidi-font-family: &quot;Times New Roman&quot;;">年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">26</span>日，常州市国土资源局武进分局发布【<span lang="EN-US">2007</span>】第<span lang="EN-US">07-02-01-04</span>号《征地补偿安置方案公告》，实际上并未完成集体土地征收。<span lang="EN-US">2017</span>年，常州市武进区国家高新区北区工作组单方面委托常州市基建土地房地产评估咨询有限公司进行评估。该评估公司出具的《房屋搬迁评估结果汇总表》显示：李晓亮家建筑面积为<span lang="EN-US">1008.48</span>平方米的房屋评估为<span lang="EN-US">847837</span>元。如此评估，无异于抢劫，李晓亮一家难以接受。于是，拆迁方采取停水、停电进行逼迁。但拆迁方并未得逞。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal" style="line-height: 107%; margin-bottom: 8.0pt; tab-stops: 122.5pt;"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; line-height: 107%; mso-ansi-language: EN-US; mso-bidi-font-family: &quot;Times New Roman&quot;;">2018</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; line-height: 107%; mso-ansi-language: EN-US; mso-bidi-font-family: &quot;Times New Roman&quot;;">年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">6</span>日深夜<span lang="EN-US">11</span>点许，夜深人静，万邦房屋拆迁有限公司偷偷地拆除了李晓亮家的部分房屋，计<span lang="EN-US">18</span>平方米。李晓亮的父亲受此惊吓，精神崩溃，在强拆后不到<span lang="EN-US">10</span>天就去世了。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal" style="line-height: 107%; margin-bottom: 8.0pt; tab-stops: 122.5pt;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; line-height: 107%; mso-ansi-language: EN-US; mso-bidi-font-family: &quot;Times New Roman&quot;;">李晓亮一家至今无家可归，四次报警，公安机关只受理，却不处理。李晓亮多次赴京上访，或遭遇拦截，或在上访过程中被押送到原籍。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p>
