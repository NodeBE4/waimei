<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">16</span>日，本网获悉遭中共当局抓捕的湖北省鄂州民主人士余钱的案情及简历，现在通报如下：<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">余钱：</span></b><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">1986</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">8</span>日出生，湖北省鄂州市人，鄂州市鄂城区泽林镇兴桥村公民，曾在上海工作，基督徒，网络活跃人士，推特常用账号“余钱<span lang="EN-US">100 @tianfushiming</span>”，民主人士，人权捍卫者，中国在押政治犯。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">早年因家境贫寒，遍尝人间疾苦，长大后曾在上海工作；曾因极富正义感，在工作之余曾多次痛批高层领导目无法治，并在推特上自称“既是冲塔的坦克人，亦是湖北的李文亮”，故使得上海市和湖北省鄂州市相关高层领导极度不满，将其定为急需维稳监控的打压对象；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2010</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">4</span>日，曾因纪念六四<span lang="EN-US">21</span>周年而禁食祈祷并发绝食短信，两天后遂被上海市奉贤区江海派出所警方以涉嫌“扰乱公共秩序”罪由传唤，并处行政处罚<span lang="EN-US">200</span>元；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">后其不服处罚决定，随即发起行政复议决定，将上海市奉贤区政府告上上海市第一中级法院，以期该法院裁决撤销对其的行政处罚；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">之后其被迫返回湖北老家生活，此期间，因仍持续发声，揭批鄂州市贪腐官商勾结、遍地黑厂、癌症村、打黑运动、机场圈地强拆三万户、政府行政造假等问题，故被鄂州市警方在其老家村里沿路电线杆上布满摄像头；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2022</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">12</span>日，曾因湖北省长王忠林到鄂州市指导疫情防控，官方虽称实施封控范围只限鄂州中心城区约<span lang="EN-US">30</span>万人，但各乡镇立即在省长指示下以乡镇名义发布鄂州市整体静态管理通知，实际封控多达近百万人；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">对此，其遂网发诘问：各乡镇在没有合法文件情况下非法额外封控<span lang="EN-US">70</span>万民众，非法拘禁街上民众并送集中强制学习是否合情合法；因其网络异议立引社会热议，遂于同年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">20</span>日、<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">24</span>日和<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">20</span>日先后三次被以“寻衅滋事”或“境外推特发帖和恶意抹黑中国政府和中国共产党”为罪由传唤，并且第三次传唤时，一无传唤证，二被关小黑屋<span lang="EN-US">7</span>个多小时，三还被行政拘留<span lang="EN-US">15</span>天；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2022</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月，因其再次在推特上发布评论中国政府和中国共产党的文图资讯，故而让湖北当局十分恼火，遂派当地警方在其住宅四周又安装上新的监控摄像头，以进行全天候居家监控，且再遭几次“寻衅滋事“罪由传唤；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">因其对此不服，故而于同月先后两次向鄂州市华容区法院发起行政复议和行政起诉状，但无果；<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">12</span>日，因余钱再次向鄂州市中级法院起诉，并发布推文“六四发声无罪，监听公民通信才有罪”；遂被鄂州市鄂城区泽林派出所警方迅速抓走，自此失联；拘押至今，一直未放。目前被羁押地点不明。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体;
