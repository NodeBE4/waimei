<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">16</span>日，本网获悉：近年来<span lang="EN-US">,</span>中国数据分析产业兴起后<span lang="EN-US">,</span>极其耗电的数位挖掘与数位分析给中国本来就供需关系紧张的电力资源带来了新的矛盾。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">在布满互联网企业的浙江省<span lang="EN-US">,</span>广东省粤港澳大湾区<span lang="EN-US">,</span>部分二三线城市也面临缺乏电力的困境<span lang="EN-US">,</span>为解决数位挖掘所需要的电力资源问题<span lang="EN-US">,</span>浙江省和广东省的互联网公司已瞄准水电资源丰厚的藏区<span lang="EN-US">,</span>浙江省和广东省政府打着“援藏”的名义<span lang="EN-US">,</span>开展“东数西算”<span lang="EN-US">(</span>东部数位<span lang="EN-US">,</span>西部运算<span lang="EN-US">)</span>的跨区域互联网数位分析项目。并在藏区建设更多的水电设施对这一项目开展支持<span lang="EN-US">,</span>无形中加剧了对生态平衡的破坏。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">数年前<span lang="EN-US">,</span>甘孜州因比特币挖矿设施前往该地运行<span lang="EN-US">,</span>机房被洪水泥石流冲垮后<span lang="EN-US">,</span>当地藏民举行抗议<span lang="EN-US">,</span>甘孜州政府才被迫下发文件禁止比特币挖矿项目在甘孜州境内运行。数位时代的西藏环境问题<span lang="EN-US">,</span>值得有关专家学者更多关注<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p>
