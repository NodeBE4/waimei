[被控"组织偷越国境罪"获刑七年的安徽蚌埠怀远"活水团契"沈绍春牧师提前9个月获释出狱](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2025/01/9.html)
------

<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2025</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">10</span>日，本网获悉：被控<span lang="EN-US">"</span>组织偷越国境罪<span lang="EN-US">"</span>获刑七年的安徽蚌埠怀远<span lang="EN-US">"</span>活水团契<span lang="EN-US">"</span>沈绍春牧师提前<span lang="EN-US">9</span>个月获释出狱。沈绍春牧师已于<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月出狱，妻子、儿子、儿媳和孙子专程到云南西双版纳监狱迎接沈绍春牧师。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">沈绍春：</span></b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">安徽蚌埠怀远活水团契牧师，<span lang="EN-US">1966</span>年生。因在缅甸佤邦办学传福音，<span lang="EN-US">2018</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">30</span>日被抓捕后遣返中国大陆。后被以“组织他人偷越国境罪”判刑<span lang="EN-US">7</span>年。刑期至<span lang="EN-US">2025</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">沈绍春牧师的父母都是基督教的传道人，其早年即皈依基督教，就读神学院，<span lang="EN-US">2006</span>年开始全职传教，<span lang="EN-US">2016</span>年在缅甸佤邦创办“活水小学”。<span lang="EN-US">2018</span>年，其<span lang="EN-US">4</span>名在校上课的学生被缅甸当局抓捕移交中方，其随后也被抓捕，并移交中方。后被“组织他人偷越国境罪”判刑<span lang="EN-US">7</span>年。刑期原本至<span lang="EN-US">2025</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月。<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月提前<span lang="EN-US">9</span>个月获释，此前前羁押在云南西双版纳的监狱中。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"></p><div class="separator" style="clear: both;
