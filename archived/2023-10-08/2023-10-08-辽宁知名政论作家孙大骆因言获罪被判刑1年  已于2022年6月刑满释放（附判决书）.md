<div>
<p><span></span></p><a name='more'></a><span></span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;"><br /></span><p></p><p><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">（维权网信息中心报道）</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">2023</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">年</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">10</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">月</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">8</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">日，本网获悉：于</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">2021</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">年</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">6</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">月</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">23</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">日被刑事拘留的辽宁知名政论作家孙大骆因言获罪被判刑</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">1</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">年，已于</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">2022</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">年</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">6</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">月刑满释放。本网从中国裁判文书网上下载的判决书介绍了全部案情，附后。</span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">孙大骆：</span></b><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">1957</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年出生，原名孙志明，辽宁省鞍山市人，北京市长驻居民，早期曾为某企业工人和某公司经理，后为北京市某民办学校老师、北京市某残疾人学校校长，职业作家，知名政论作家，民主异见人士，中国政治犯。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">自<span lang="EN-US">1990</span>年代末以来，曾因长期在境外媒体或杂志上发表政论性敏感话题文章，而遭中共当局的关注和严密监控；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2021</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年，因在香港与台湾出版《毛泽东权术史》、《习近平权术史》和《当西方哲学压倒中国思想》等多部书籍，并在香港《前哨》和台湾《新纪元》杂志以及境外媒体（如“独立中文笔会”、“北京之春”等）发布政治评论<span lang="EN-US">100</span>余篇，遂于同年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">23</span>日，被辽宁省沈阳市警方以涉嫌“寻衅滋事罪”刑拘，同时，其家被查抄<span lang="EN-US">5</span>次，其丈母娘家及大姨子家各被抄家<span lang="EN-US">1</span>次；同年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">31</span>日，又被沈阳市经济技术开发区检察院以同罪名予以批捕，并被关押于沈阳市第一看守所；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2021</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">5</span>日，其案在沈阳市经济技术开发区法院秘密开庭审理，最终被一审以“寻衅滋事罪”判处有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">1</span>年，刑期至<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">22</span>日；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">因其代理律师及其家属遭警方威胁，不准将其案情向外透露，故其庭审情况、被判日期和被判刑期等讯息，一直鲜为人知。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体;
