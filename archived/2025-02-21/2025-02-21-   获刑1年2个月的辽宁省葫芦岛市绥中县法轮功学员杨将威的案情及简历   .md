<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2025</span>年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">21</span>日，本网获悉获刑<span lang="EN-US">1</span>年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>个月的辽宁省葫芦岛市绥中县法轮功学员杨将威的案情及简历，现在通报如下：<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">杨将威：</span></b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">辽宁省葫芦岛市绥中县法轮功学员，男，<span lang="EN-US">1970</span>年生，原绥中县大洋电力安装公司职工。因修炼法轮功，在中共对法轮功的疯狂迫害中，杨将威多次被抓捕、关押，三次被劳教长达<span lang="EN-US">7</span>年半，<span lang="EN-US">2008</span>年又被判重刑<span lang="EN-US">10</span>年，牢狱长达<span lang="EN-US">17</span>年半。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">杨将威因在一个电线杆上贴了张“真善忍好”的不干胶，于<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">19</span>日被绥中县公安局高岭派出所警察抓捕，后两次被葫芦岛市连山区法院庭审。最终杨将威被连山区法院判刑<span lang="EN-US">1</span>年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>个月。刑期至：<span lang="EN-US">2025</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">18</span>日。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p>
