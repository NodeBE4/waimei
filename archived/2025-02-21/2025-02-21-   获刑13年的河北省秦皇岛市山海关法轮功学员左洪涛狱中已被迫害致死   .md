[获刑13年的河北省秦皇岛市山海关法轮功学员左洪涛狱中已被迫害致死](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2025/02/13.html)
------

<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2025</span>年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">21</span>日，本网获悉：获刑<span lang="EN-US">13</span>年的河北省秦皇岛市山海关法轮功学员左洪涛已经于<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">6</span>日在保定监狱被迫害致死，终年<span lang="EN-US">67</span>岁。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">左洪涛：</span></b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">河北省秦皇岛市山海关法轮功学员，<span lang="EN-US">1957</span>年生。左洪涛与妻子崔求荣（未炼功），<span lang="EN-US">2017</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">9</span>日被山海关公安分局及南关派出所等十几个警察抓捕、关押构陷，后<span lang="EN-US">2019</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月，左洪涛被判刑<span lang="EN-US">13</span>年，崔秋荣被判<span lang="EN-US">1</span>年多。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">判刑后左洪涛被关押在保定监狱<span lang="EN-US">19</span>监区，据悉<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">19</span>日其病危，送进医院<span lang="EN-US">ICU</span>（重症）抢救，<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">6</span>日离世。据医生说左洪涛全身栓塞、浮肿。左洪涛去世后，监狱方强行火化，家属没能近身查看亲人遗体。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p>
