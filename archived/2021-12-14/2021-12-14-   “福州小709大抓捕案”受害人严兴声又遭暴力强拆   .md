[“福州小709大抓捕案”受害人严兴声又遭暴力强拆](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2021/12/709.html)
------

<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">14</span>日，本网获悉“福州小<span lang="EN-US">709</span>大抓捕案”受害人严兴声又遭暴力强拆。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">据严兴声来电：今天（<span lang="EN-US">2021.12.14</span>）早上五点多，<span lang="EN-US">30</span>多个暴徒，冲进我家，对我及我大哥进行暴力绑架、殴打，企图暴力强拆；我与我大哥拼死反抗，对方用防爆棍棒器具施暴，朝我们脸上身上喷化学泡沫，我挥舞砍柴刀阻止牠们，并夺下暴徒手中一根暴力器械。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">对方看到无法绑架我俩兄弟就撤退了。我俩兄弟报警，城门派出所出警到现场了解情况，就离开了。接着十多个暴徒又来了，我手拿菜刀，举着手机走到大门外拍照取证。再次报警，这次民警没来，但是暴徒看到我俩兄弟敢拼死反抗，就开着闽<span lang="EN-US">JFM021</span>、闽<span lang="EN-US">DN1T98</span>两部“黑车”仓惶离开……<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">事情经过：</span></b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">我老房子所在土地，在未经我家允许，未落实安置补偿，未履行相关征收法律程序的情况下，已于今年被福州市自然资源和规划局拍卖了，拆迁办却一直拒绝跟我们签署拆迁协议。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">昨天（<span lang="EN-US">2021.12.13</span>）下午三点多，我们与下洋村书记刘南勇，城门镇官员严孙东还在协商，基本达成一致了，我做了最大妥协让步：同意近期只要签署<span lang="EN-US">2016</span>年新房子被暴力强拆的人口政策<span lang="EN-US">45</span>平方的协议；我老房子<span lang="EN-US">380</span>平方，按拆迁政策价值<span lang="EN-US">500</span>多万，我只要求拿<span lang="EN-US">60-70</span>万现金补偿，我就搬家离开。就这样最低的诉求，对方基本同意了；然而，今天当局却出尔反尔，竟对我家进行暴力强拆。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">目前我了解到真实情况<span lang="EN-US">:</span>今天早晨至少来了<span lang="EN-US">200-300</span>个黑保安，黑城管，冲到我家里动手，我亲眼目睹至少五六十个，据乡亲反映，外围还有两三百个包围在外边，勾机就在附近等着，一旦暴力绑架我俩兄弟出来，就马上把房屋推平铲除。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">我们誓死捍卫自己的尊严，维权到底，绝不让此股黑恶势力得逞！<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体;
