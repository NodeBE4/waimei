<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">16</span>日，本网获悉：被以寻衅滋事罪获刑<span lang="EN-US">2</span>年的云南昆明民主维权人士徐昆今刑满释放。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">1961</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2019</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">5</span>日，因其曾多次在推特上转发香港反送中运动的相关信息，而被昆明市盘龙区东华派出所警方突然传唤，其手机被扣押；<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">17</span>日，其再次被盘龙区警方带走传唤，并被以“使用手机在自己的推特账号上多次转发散布扰乱社会秩序的不当内容和言论”为由处行政拘留<span lang="EN-US">15</span>日；<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">30</span>日，在其拘留期限届满之际，又被昆明市盘龙区警方继续拘押，至<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">1</span>日被以涉嫌“寻衅滋事罪”刑事拘留；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2019</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">30</span>日，其被昆明市盘龙区警方以涉嫌“寻衅滋事罪”正式逮捕，被控因其在推特上转发鲍彤先生“搞乱香港是林郑、是中共”的推文、及自己原创的有关“八九六四”推文等，其言论已抵触了中共当局的相关网络规定，属于违法行为。被羁押于昆明市盘龙区第二看守所三监。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体;
