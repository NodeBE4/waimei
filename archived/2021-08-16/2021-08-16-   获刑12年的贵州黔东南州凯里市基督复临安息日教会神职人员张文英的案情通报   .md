font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">女，贵州省黔东南苗族侗族自治州（简称黔东南州）凯里市人，侗族（需再核实），基督徒，黔东南州凯里市基督复临安息日教会神职人员，聚会点负责人及长老，中国在押良心犯。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2020</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月，又因其教会聚会点信徒不断增多，教会势力日渐强大，所以很快引起贵州省黔东南州当局关注与恐慌，并刻意成立项目组，对其聚会点进行全面侦查；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">之后，其被黔东南州凯里市警方以涉嫌“诈骗罪”刑拘，自此失联，另有<span lang="EN-US">3</span>名教会神职人员亦被以“同案犯”抓捕刑拘；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">后其<span lang="EN-US">4</span>人均被黔东南州凯里市检察院以同罪名予以批捕，指控其因聚会点申请未被国家审批，众信徒所交奉献款均属于诈骗款，故而其行为属于违法；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2021</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">14</span>日，其<span lang="EN-US">4</span>人案被当地凯里市法院一审以“诈骗罪”统一宣判，其因是教会长老而被枉法重判有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">12</span>年，刑期至<span lang="EN-US">2032</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">另<span lang="EN-US">3</span>位神职人员亦被枉判<span lang="EN-US">6</span>—<span lang="EN-US">3</span>年不等刑期；其<span lang="EN-US">4</span>人不服判决，已向黔东南州中级法院提起上诉，二审正在等待中。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体;
