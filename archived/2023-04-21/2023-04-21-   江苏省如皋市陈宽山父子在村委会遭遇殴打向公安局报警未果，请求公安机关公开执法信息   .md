[江苏省如皋市陈宽山父子在村委会遭遇殴打向公安局报警未果，请求公安机关公开执法信息](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2023/04/blog-post_32.html)
------

<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">21</span>日，本网获悉：江苏省如皋市如城街道顾庄村<span lang="EN-US">15</span>组村民陈宽山和陈大红系父子关系于<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">16</span>日向如皋市公安局迎春派出所邮寄了《公安执法公开申请书》，请求公开其在拆迁过程中被殴打后报警的公安机关执法信息：<span lang="EN-US">1</span>、有关本案的调查情况和结果；<span lang="EN-US">2</span>、何人何时为何拔掉凌青村村委会会议室内两个监控摄像头线及其调查笔录。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">早在<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">22</span>日晚至<span lang="EN-US">23</span>日凌晨，如皋市如城街道顾庄村十五组村民陈宽山父子在如城街道凌青村村委会被殴打致伤，当即报警。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">如皋迎春派出所受案后，出具了《受案回执》。迎春派出所民警进行现场勘查，发现凌青村村委会会议室内两个监控摄像头线均被拔掉。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">由于，迎春派出所不能查出打人凶手，致使拆迁方更加嚣张，骚扰并限制拆迁户人身自由的事情连续不断地发生。<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">19</span>日、<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">3</span>日，陈宽山分别向法院提起诉讼，以如泉市人民政府如城街道办事处<span lang="EN-US">(</span>以下简称如城街办<span lang="EN-US">)</span>长时间限制其人身自由、家人受到殴打等胁迫情形下签订了房屋搬迁补偿安置协议、后强制拆除其房屋为由，要求确认拆除行为违法以及撤销双方签订的补偿安置协议。一审法院分别作出<span lang="EN-US">(2021)</span>苏<span lang="EN-US">0691</span>行初<span lang="EN-US">1488 </span>号、<span lang="EN-US">1647</span>号《行政判决书》，分别判决确认如城街办<span lang="EN-US"> 2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">7</span>日强制拆除陈宽山位于如皋市如城街道顾庄村十五组<span lang="EN-US">28</span>号房屋的行为违法，撤销如皋市人民政府如城街道办事处于<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">22</span>日与陈宽山签订的《如泉市房屋搬迁补偿安置协议》。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">但陈宽山父子被打事件至今未处理。令人疑惑不解的是，陈大红向警方明确指控的三名人员均否认实施了殴打行为<span lang="EN-US">,</span>其他当天参与谈拆迁的人员也均陈述未看见存在殴打行为。换言之，没有人承担打人的法律责任。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">村民不配合违法拆迁被打的事情时有发生，虽经报案，往往不了了之。但打人事情愈演愈烈，甚至于明目张胆在村委会内打人，也查不出真凶。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">按理来说，在村委会打人一般是不难查清楚的。即使拔掉了摄像头，也可从在场者中查明真相的。如此明目张胆的共同违法，之所以至今不能查明真相，明显的有保护伞起作用，否则是很难隐瞒的！<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">公安机关能否公正查处，人们将拭目以待。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p>
