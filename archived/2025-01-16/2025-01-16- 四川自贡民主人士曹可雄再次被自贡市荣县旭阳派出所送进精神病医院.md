[四川自贡民主人士曹可雄再次被自贡市荣县旭阳派出所送进精神病医院](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2025/01/blog-post_16.html)
------

<div>
<p><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;"><span></span></span></p><a name='more'></a><br /><p></p><p>（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">2025</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">年</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">1</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">月</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">16</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">日，本网获悉：四川自贡民主人士曹可雄，网名雄心壮志，经了解，已确认曹可雄于</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">2024</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">年</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">12</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">月</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">13</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">日再次被自贡市荣县旭阳派出所（电话</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">0813-6201156</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">）送进自贡市第五人民医院（精神病医院）,至今已经一月有余。</span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">曹可雄在<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">11</span>日曾被荣县旭阳派出所（同上）送入自贡市第五人民医院（精神病院）（同上）强制“治疗”，直到<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">4</span>日被释放，在精神病院有被电疗、强迫吃药。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体;
