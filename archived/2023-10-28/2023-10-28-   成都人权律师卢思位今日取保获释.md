[成都人权律师卢思位今日取保获释](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2023/10/blog-post_44.html)
------

<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">28</span>日，本网获悉：在老挝被抓捕后遭遣返回中国大陆，随即被羁押的成都人权律师卢思位今日取保获释。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">卢思位律师的妻子张春晓今通报说：“与卢思位失去联系<span lang="EN-US">91</span>天后，终于听到他的声音。他现在被取保候审，虽然自由是有限的，但是能听到声音对我已是极大的安慰。这几个月以来，在你们的陪伴鼓励支持下，我终于挨过慢慢黑夜，看到一丝光亮……他现在身心疲惫，需要好好休息一段时间……我和思位永远感谢大家，祝福所有的朋友！”<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">卢思位律师于<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">28</span>日前往泰国，在老挝国过境的时候，被老挝警方扣押，关在老挝国拘留所。两个月后，卢律师被遣返回中国大陆。随即卢律师被关押在成都新都看守所。<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">28</span>日卢思位律师取保获释。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">卢思位律师简介：</span></b><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">1973</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年生，四川省知名人权律师，办理过成都纪念「八九六四」酒案，是成都「四君子」之一张隽勇的辩护人，曾代理 十二港人“偷渡案”、余文生、王藏 、覃永沛 、陈家鸿 等“煽动颠覆国家政权案”。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2017</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年，卢思位律师就发起公民联署，呼吁全国人大成立特别调查委员会，就“<span lang="EN-US">709</span>”系列案件是否存在强迫服药等酷刑进行独立调查，调查委员会的组成人员应当包括律师和公民代表，并向公开调查结论。厦门聚会案发生后，卢思位律师受到传唤及威胁，并被禁止出境。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2020</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">24</span>日卢思位律师因办理过广西律师陈家鸿「煽动颠覆国家政权案」，曾遭受四川司法当局和律师协会打压和处分。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2020</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月，余文生律师因煽动颠覆国家政权罪成，被徐州市中级法院判处徒刑<span lang="EN-US">4</span>年，卢思位律师作为二审辩护人参与辩护工作。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2020</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月，有十二位香港社运人士被深圳公安扣押在盐田看守所，卢思位律师曾经探访十二位被扣押香港人，又向他们提供法律援助。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2020</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">3</span>日成都市羊青区司法局对卢思位律师代理的所有案件材料检查。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2021</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">4</span>日，四川省司法厅对卢思位律师开出行政处罚案件当事人听证权利告知书，准备吊销卢律师的律师执业证。后卢思位遭吊销律师证。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">卢思位先生于<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">28</span>日上午在准备登上一列开往泰国的火车时，被老挝警方逮捕。卢思位的原定行程是在泰国搭乘班机前往美国与家人团聚。<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月卢思位被老挝政府连同其他<span lang="EN-US">50</span>名中国人，一起分乘两辆车，遣返中国。随后，卢思位被中共当局关押到四川成都市新都看守所。<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">28</span>日，卢思位律师取保获释。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"></p><div class="separator" style="clear: both;
