[山东烟台莱山区区长等官员因疫情防控不力被免职](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210804-1177979)
------

<div>
<p>烟台今天举行的疫情防控新闻发布会宣布，经烟台市委研究，免去莱山区区长、分管副区长职务；莱山区委研究，免去黄海路街道党工委主要负责人员职务。</p><p>根据“闪电新闻”微博消息，莱山区黄海路街道上周六（31日）确诊一例冠病本土病例。</p><p>烟台防控新闻发布会指出，莱山区政府主要负责同志、分管负责同志，以及黄海路街道负责同志，抓疫情防控工作履责不力，落实防控措施不到位，按照失责必追责的原则，免去莱山区区长、分管副区长以及其他相关人员的职务。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>会议还强调，今后将加大监督检查力度，对不担当作为、失职渎职等问题，发现一起处理一起，从严问责追责。 </p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
