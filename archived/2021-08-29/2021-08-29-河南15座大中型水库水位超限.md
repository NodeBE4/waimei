[河南15座大中型水库水位超限](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210829-1187951)
------

<div>
<p>河南省目前已有15座大中型水库超汛限水位。按照《河南省水利厅水旱灾害防御应急预案》有关规定，河南省水利厅决定于今天（29日）12时起，启动水旱灾害防御Ⅳ级应急响应。</p><p>据河南日报报道，气象预报显示，8月29日至31日，河南省将再次出现强降雨过程，局部降雨量可达100毫米以上。</p><p>河南省水利厅要求，各级水利部门要严密监测雨水汛情，加强会商研判，做好监测预报预警，适时启动相应级别的水旱灾害防御应急响应，依法依规科学调度水工程，加强防汛值守、山洪灾害防御、堤防巡查和抢险技术支撑，确保民众生命安全，最大程度减少灾害损失。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
