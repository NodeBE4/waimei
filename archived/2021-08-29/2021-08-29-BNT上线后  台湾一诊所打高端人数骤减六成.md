[BNT上线后  台湾一诊所打高端人数骤减六成](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210829-1187742)
------

<div>
<p>BNT疫苗即将抵达台湾，指挥中心昨开放民众上网登记施打意愿，板桥一名医师在面簿上透露，其所在的诊所内，预约接种高端疫苗的施打率竟瞬间蒸发六成，只剩四成的民众依约接种。</p><p>据中时电子报报道，该医师昨（28）晚指，200万剂BNT即将抵台，指挥中心昨下午2时开放民众上预约平台登记施打意愿，加上接受第七轮AZ疫苗预约，凡是29岁以上民众以及18岁以上有重大伤病的第九类对象都可上网预约，让高端疫苗的施打率昨下午暴跌。</p><p>该医师说，200万剂BNT将保留120万剂给12至18岁以下学生造册施打，剩下约80万剂则给1至10类尚未接种第一剂新冠疫苗的民众，依年龄往下施打，“但给学生打完后，实际剩多少分给大家？”</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>该医师预计，爽约不打高端的民众，即便第七轮预约AZ后，也会因为BNT再次爽约。<br> </p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
