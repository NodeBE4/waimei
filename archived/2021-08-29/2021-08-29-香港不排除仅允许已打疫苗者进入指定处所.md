[香港不排除仅允许已打疫苗者进入指定处所](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210829-1187843)
------

<div>
<p>香港公务员事务局长聂德权今天说，未来不排除仅允许完成接种疫苗的民众进入指定处所。</p><p>据明报报道，聂德权在无线节目《讲清讲楚》上说，当局会朝着全民接种冠病疫苗方向继续努力，并称考虑社交距离和跨境出行的安排，“不是机械式地要到百分之几就可做哪样，而是永远要风险评估”。</p><p>聂德权称，据专家之前所说，若接种率达七成，可以在社区建立免疫屏障，但变种病毒出现增添建立免疫屏障的难度。但他表示社区仍需建立保护屏障，以防止大规模扩散。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>他又称，不排除将来施加限制，仅让已接种疫苗者进入指定处所。<br> </p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
