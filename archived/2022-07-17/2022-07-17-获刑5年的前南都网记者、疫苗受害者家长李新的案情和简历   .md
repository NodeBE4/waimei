[获刑5年的前南都网记者、疫苗受害者家长李新的案情和简历](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2022/07/5_17.html)

<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p><p>&nbsp;<span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">（维权网信息中心报道）</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">2022</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">年</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">7</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">月</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">17</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">日，本网获悉获刑</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">5</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">年的前南都网记者、疫苗受害者家长李新的案情和简历，现通报如下：</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">李新：</span></b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">河南省辉县市人，辉县市城关镇吕巷村居民，南都网深圳法制频道前记者和编辑，疫苗受害者家长，维权公民，人权捍卫者，中国在押维权人士。李新是“疫苗宝宝之家”维权团体发起人何方美的丈夫。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">李新为人性格温和，从不和人起冲突；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2015</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月，曾因追求自由理念而逃离中国大陆，并于<span lang="EN-US">2016</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">30</span>日持短期旅游签证经香港抵达印度申请政治庇护，但因其签证很快到期，而未能及时获取庇护，遂辗转到泰国准备向美国官方提出庇护申请；然当其到达泰国后，被中方知晓，遂于<span lang="EN-US">2017</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">11</span>日被中国警方跨境抓捕回国；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">获释后，因其当时<span lang="EN-US">1</span>岁<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月龄的幼女打了政府指定的百白破疫苗之后导致全身瘫痪，其多方求助均遭拒绝，遂与妻子何方美（“疫苗宝宝之家”维权团体发起人）一起投诉维权，要求当局关注并给予合理赔偿和救治；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">然地方当局却一拖再拖，其夫妻二人被迫进京上访，故又频遭报复与打压；<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年，因其在北京逗留且为疫苗致瘫女儿上访，与此同时，其妻何方美因在辉县市政府门前怒而泼墨被警方扣押，故而其被辉县各级维稳部门多次电话强逼离开北京，并以停其手机号码相威胁；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">为此，其向原籍村委会提出<span lang="EN-US">4</span>点要求：“<span lang="EN-US">1</span>、回去后能见到妻子十三妹和孩子们，让十三妹自由；<span lang="EN-US">2</span>、女儿能够顺利看病治疗；<span lang="EN-US">3</span>、兑现以前承诺的国家赔偿；<span lang="EN-US">4</span>、撤去监控，以后不再跟踪监控他们一家”；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2020</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">14</span>日，李新被辉县市维稳人员通过微信定位找到后随即带走，走之前仅在微信上留言一句：“我被他们带走了”，自此长期失联；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2022</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月，其妻何方美被辉县市警方以涉嫌“重婚罪”和“寻衅滋事罪”正式逮捕，此时，才有消息传出，河南省辉县市政府（含维稳办人员、辉县警方、辉县市检察院及法院等）为报复其夫妇二人的上访维权，刻意伪造假证，构陷其二人涉嫌“重婚、寻衅滋事”二罪；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2022</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月左右，其在家属不知情的情况下，被辉县市法院以“重婚罪”和“寻衅滋事罪”秘密判处有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">5</span>年，刑期至<span lang="EN-US">2025</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月。目前在河南省焦作市监狱服刑。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">对李新和何方美夫妇的境况，本网将持续关注。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><div class="separator" style="clear: both;
