[因传福音屡遭拘留的湖南基督徒陈文胜的简历](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2022/04/blog-post_0.html)
------

<div>
<p><span></span></p><a name='more'></a><p class="MsoNormal"><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">20</span>日，本网获悉因传福音屡遭拘留的湖南基督徒陈文胜的简历，现通报如下：<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">陈文胜：</span></b><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">1969</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">30</span>日出生，湖南省衡阳市石鼓区人，衡阳市石鼓区西湖一村<span lang="EN-US">13</span>栋<span lang="EN-US">507</span>户公民，职业不详，基督徒，湖南衡阳小群教会信徒，街头传道人，中国曾押良心犯。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">早年曾吸毒成性十余年，后因在戒毒所听到福音而成为基督徒，并凭信心而除掉了毒瘾，故而自此致力于广传基督教福音；曾因经常举着写有“荣耀救主”、“悔改信靠得救”的十字架独自上街宣传，或与教会其他基督徒一起走街串巷，到城市闹市区（甚至到过西藏）传福音，随屡遭当局打压，多次被警方警告、威胁和被关押在衡阳市拘留所，甚至一年之中竟有<span lang="EN-US">6</span>次被行政拘留<span lang="EN-US">10</span>日；</span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;"><span lang="EN-US">2019</span>年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月，曾因到广西壮族自治区宣传基督教福音，而被南宁市警方行政拘留<span lang="EN-US">10</span>天；</span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;"><span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">3</span>日，又因上街传福音，而被湖南省衡阳市石鼓区警方行政拘留<span lang="EN-US">10</span>天；</span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">同年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">31</span>日，因与另一基督教信徒一起先后在衡阳市商业步行街附近和中医医院附近，利用喇叭、广播播放及发福音单张等方式宣传基督教，而被衡阳市蒸湘区警方以“扰乱社会秩序”为由行政拘留<span lang="EN-US">10</span>天；</span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;"><span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">1</span>日，因与其母为跨农历新年传福音，而被衡阳市警方行政拘留<span lang="EN-US">10</span>天；</span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">之后，因其继续走街串巷传福音，而在该年又曾先后<span lang="EN-US">5</span>次被行政拘留<span lang="EN-US">10</span>天；</span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">同年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">8</span>日，曾因到浙江省传福音，而被辖区警方跨境抓捕，后被指定居所监视居住于西湖山庄，<span lang="EN-US">20</span>天后，才在轮番轰炸般的约谈之后予以释放；</span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;"><span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">28</span>日，因其到云南向少数民族朋友传福音，同年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">5</span>日，即被云南省文山壮族苗族自治州文山市警方拦截，且被移交给衡阳市警方遣回原籍；获释后，其接续外出传福音，甚至向国保警方和当地街道办事处工作人员等传福音；</span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;"><span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">10</span>日，因其到湖南省益阳市安化县传福音，被衡阳市政法委、统战部及国保警方联合跨区抓回，次日被衡阳市青山派出所警方行政拘留<span lang="EN-US">15</span>天；</span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">同年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">26</span>日，其重获自由，此前曾被羁押于衡阳市拘留所。但释放当天又去上街传福音；据悉，其妻子、儿媳及孙女等家人因受牵连，曾遭当局多次恐吓和威胁，被要求务必说服、阻止其外出传福音，否则不仅儿媳会被单位开除公职，而且其孙女也会在将来不能考取国家公务员与教师等工作。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><br /><p></p><div class="separator" style="clear: both;
