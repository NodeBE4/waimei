mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">27</span>日：本网获悉：遭逮捕的湖南人权律师谢阳的代理律师要求会见遭拒。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">谢阳于<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">11</span>日被以涉嫌煽动颠覆国家政权、寻衅滋事罪刑事拘留，后于<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">17</span>日被以“涉嫌煽动颠覆国家政权罪”正式逮捕。现羁押在湖南省长沙市第一看守所。（看守所地址：长沙县泉塘街道远大二路<span lang="EN-US">1736</span>号）<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">谢阳：</span></b><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">湖南人权律师，自<span lang="EN-US">2011 </span>年执业以来，曾因代理多起维权案件而遭当局打压。<span lang="EN-US">2015</span>年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月在代理一起广西南宁的维权案件中，遭不明身份人员持械群殴，被打至重伤，右腿骨折；<span lang="EN-US">2015</span>年在中共对维权律师的“<span lang="EN-US">709</span>大抓捕”中，谢阳被湖南省长沙市警方以涉嫌“扰乱法庭秩序罪”“煽动颠覆国家政权罪”指定地点监视居住，<span lang="EN-US">2016</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月被长沙市警方以涉嫌“煽动颠覆国家政权罪”正式逮捕，后被免予刑事处罚。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">在今年被再次刑拘前，谢阳律师曾前往湖南永顺县声援因发表网络言论而被强行关进精神病院的怀孕女教师李田田。在李田田获释后不久，谢阳于<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">11</span>日即被刑事拘留。现被正式逮捕。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;
