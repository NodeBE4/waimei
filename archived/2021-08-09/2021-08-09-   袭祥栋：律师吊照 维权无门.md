[袭祥栋：律师吊照 维权无门](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2021/08/blog-post_57.html)
------

<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">今天，<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">8</span>日，是我被吊销律师执照六个月截止日，这将意味着我被行政处罚一案的法律救济之门，已被彻底关闭。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">半年来，诸多关心我的师友、同仁以及未曾谋面的网友不时发来消息问，对吊照处罚决定申请行政复议了没？行政起诉了没？<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">现就我的维权经历公布如下，权当留作记录，给公众一交代，盼早日摘帽平反。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2021</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">8</span>日，山东省司法厅给我送达“吊销律师执业证”行政处罚决定书，该决定书载明“如不服本处罚决定，可在收到本决定书之日起<span lang="EN-US">60</span>日内向中华人民共和国司法部或山东省人民政府申请行政复议；或者在收到本决定书之日起六个月内依法向济南市历下区人民法院提起行政诉讼”。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">同年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">9</span>日，山东省律师协会给我送达“取消律师会员资格”行业处分决定书，该决定书载明“如不服本处分决定书，可在决定书送达之日起<span lang="EN-US">15</span>日内，以书面形式向山东省律师协会复查委员会申请复查”。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">1</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">、因律协的行业处分复查时限短，我在收到处分决定书<span lang="EN-US">15</span>日内向山东省律师协会邮寄了《复查申请书》，至今未收到任何消息或复查结果。早我一月吊照的四川卢思位律师，也曾向四川省律师协会申请复查，四川律协还通知其听证，复查结果肯定是维持，但至少给了一个“复查”程序。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">、<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">6</span>日，在行政复议申请期限内，我向司法部邮寄了《行政复议申请书》等相关复议材料，经查询邮政快递记录，<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">7</span>日司法部收发室签收，至今，石沉大海，没收到任何答复。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">3</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">、<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月初，在行政诉讼时效内，我在“山东移动微法院”平台申请行政立案，经数次操作，无法提交材料。电话咨询济南市历下区人民法院，工作人员告知“行政立案，必须到法院诉讼服务大厅用法院电脑上传立案材料，其他终端无法操作”。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">6</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">月<span lang="EN-US">7</span>日，我带着诉讼材料去历下区法院立案，诉讼服务大厅不设人工立案窗口，只有两排电脑，工作人员指导网上提交立案材料，当天我收到历下区法院短信回复“所立案件已经提交”。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">又等了<span lang="EN-US">7</span>天，未收到法院是否立案的任何消息，我登入网上立案平台看到“审核未通过，审核时间<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">14</span>日<span lang="EN-US">19:07:43</span>”，至于什么原因，法官未留言回复，后电话询问审核不通过的原因，工作人员告知“你的案子没法立”。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">听说四川卢思位律师到法院交涉行政诉讼不立案事宜，差点被法警打了。我害怕被打，没敢去交涉。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">以上便是我被吊照后的“复查、复议、诉讼”经历，维权无门！但凡被非正常吊照处罚的律师，其维权经历大致都是如此，自送达吊照处罚决定之日起，再无任何救济途径。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">我们只能期待“拨乱反正、纠错平反”早日到来。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">袭祥栋于遵义记<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体;
