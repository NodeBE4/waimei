[青海省同仁市僧人向巴曲培在微信朋友圈分享达赖喇嘛尊者一段语音遭判刑1年6个月](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2024/09/16_26.html)
------

<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">26</span>日，本网获悉：青海省同仁市僧人向巴曲培在微信朋友圈分享达赖喇嘛尊者一段语音遭判刑<span lang="EN-US">1</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">2024</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt;">年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月份向巴曲培被中共当局判处<span lang="EN-US">1</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月的有期徒刑，目前他被关押在西宁市监狱。他被拘捕或起诉时从未通知家属。他曾经在印度达兰萨拉修学<span lang="EN-US">10</span>年后于<span lang="EN-US">1996</span>年返回西藏，是中共眼里的敏感人物。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"></p><div class="separator" style="clear: both;
