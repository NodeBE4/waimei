[遭中共当局构陷“煽动颠覆国家政权罪”获刑4年6个月的上海人权捍卫者陈建芳女士案二审维持原判](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2023/08/46_14.html)

<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-bidi-font-weight: bold; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">14</span>日，本网获悉：遭中共当局构陷“煽动颠覆国家政权罪”获刑<span lang="EN-US">4</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月上海人权捍卫者陈建芳女士二审维持原判。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">王宇律师今天通报：“针对上海市高级人民法院刑庭徐法官违法侵害上诉人辩护权的问题，上诉人陈建芳的女儿已向上级法院及同级检察院提起控告，并提出其在本案中回避的申请。但上海市高级人民法院今天仍做出了维持原判的上诉裁定。”<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">陈建芳简历：</span></b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">上海著名维权人士、人权捍卫者，<span lang="EN-US">2018</span>年“曹顺利人权捍卫者纪念奖”获奖人。陈建芳从一位抗争土地权的农民开始，长期从事维权活动。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">十多年前，她家的土地被上海市政府和房屋开发商非法征收和抢占，因得不到合法合理的安置补偿，走上了上访之路捍卫自己的权益。在多年的维权与打压的磨练中，陈建芳逐渐成为当地土地征收和房屋拆迁受害者的代言人。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2013</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月，陈建芳和曹顺利女士共同接受邀请，参加当月在日内瓦举办的人权培训活动并同时观摩联合国人权理事会会议。陈建芳在广州机场被阻拦出境。在同一天，曹顺利女士在北京机场被警方拦截并被强迫失踪。警方将陈建芳带回上海并对其拘留、调查讯问、并密切监视多日。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">陈建芳的人权倡导活动，招来当局对她不断地打击报复，包括恐吓，殴打和任意拘留。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2019</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">19</span>日早晨八点陈建芳在家中遭上海警方秘密抓捕，同年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月，被上海市浦东新区检察院以涉嫌“颠覆国家政权罪”批捕，被指控因为聚集举牌、拉横幅、网络言论等违法了国家法律。后遭起诉。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">遭中共当局司法机关的违法阻扰，律师一直无法介入案件。目前陈建芳女士被羁押于上海市看守所。当局始终不准其代理律师会见。因此一直案情不详。<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月才获悉陈建芳被中共当局以“煽动颠覆国家政权罪”判刑<span lang="EN-US">4</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月，刑期至<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">21</span>日。其不服判决，提起上诉。今日上诉被驳回，维持原判。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体;
