[被控涉嫌寻衅滋事罪的江西南昌公民张晓东案将于2024年5月10日宣判](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2024/04/2024510.html)
------

<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">30</span>日，本网获悉：被控涉嫌寻衅滋事罪的江西南昌公民张晓东案将于<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">10</span>日宣判。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt;">2022</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;">年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">15</span>日被抓捕的江西南昌公民张晓东已被羁押一年五个月了。其被起诉书指控涉嫌寻衅滋事罪，而证据全是其在微信群、朋友圈、<span lang="EN-US">QQ</span>群里的所谓言论，是典型的因言获罪。<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">19</span>日上午<span lang="EN-US">10:00</span>张晓东的寻衅滋事案在南昌市青山湖区法院审理，据悉张晓东坚信自己无罪。本案将于<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">10</span>日宣判。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;">张晓东：江西南昌人士，<span lang="EN-US">1960</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">5</span>日生。铁路部门停薪留职，下海经商多年，已退休。为人热情慷慨，好交友。每有朋友落难，包括本地公民朋友与律师落难，他都会奔走帮助。张晓东<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">15</span>日被当地警方抓捕，随后就遭刑事拘留。后于<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">23</span>日被批准逮捕。张晓东与<span lang="EN-US">‘</span>江西九江四公民<span lang="EN-US">’</span>案被捕人员均为好友。<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">20</span>日被移送检察院起诉。<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">19</span>日开庭审理。目前张晓东现羁押在南昌市第一看守所。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;">对张晓东案的进展，本网将持续关注。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"></p><div class="separator" style="clear: both;
