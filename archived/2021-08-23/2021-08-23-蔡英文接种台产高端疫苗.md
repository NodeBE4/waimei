[蔡英文接种台产高端疫苗](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210823-1185422)
------

<div>
<p>台湾总统蔡英文今日接种高端疫苗。</p><p>据联合网报道，蔡英文一早到达预约地点“台大医学院体育馆”，透过网路直播，先量体温为36.9度，并由医师问诊、说明注意事项，随后签下接种同意书。她由台大副校长张上淳及立委邱泰源等全程陪同。</p><p>蔡英文接种过程中，还提醒护理师将高端疫苗拿给媒体“照一下”，她也向现场的台大医护团队表示：“这样大阵仗打针，不好意思。”护理师将手部消毒后，接着帮蔡英文种疫苗，经过一秒钟就完成，蔡英文还问：“这样就结束了？没什么感觉。”</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>台湾总统府昨天公告，经过维安团队及台大医院协调，为降低对其他民众影响，蔡英文将于7时30分至现场，依照相关流程，报到、问诊、接种疫苗，接种完毕后离开现场，交由医疗团队后续掌握身体状况，总统府将进行网络直播。<br> </p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
