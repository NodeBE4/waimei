[北京冀明年与亚细安完成南中国海行为准则商谈](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210913-1193184)
------

<div>
<p>中国外长王毅昨天（12日）在柬埔寨与柬埔寨首相洪森会面时说，中国希望柬埔寨明年出任亚细安轮值主席国期间，中国同亚细安国家能完成南中国海行为准则商谈，制定符合包括《联合国海洋法公约》在内的国际法、实质、有效的准则。</p><p>据中国外交部官网提供的信息，王毅说，这彰显中国和亚细安国家有意愿、有能力把自己的事情办好。</p><p>王毅强调，中国是柬埔寨的铁杆朋友，并将是柬埔寨最可靠、最长期的战略伙伴。中国将支持柬埔寨不畏强权、反对霸凌，维护国家主权和民族尊严，支持柬埔寨探索符合自身国情的发展道路。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>王毅早前同洪森共同出席了中国政府援柬体育场项目交接仪式。据法新社报道，这座体育场耗资1亿5000万美元建造。</p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/zbpe-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=zbpe399&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_desktop-zbpe399_2.png?dHEiGouxxZpWwWTJE04dyGyUN74JOU2z" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_desktop-zbpe399_2.png?dHEiGouxxZpWwWTJE04dyGyUN74JOU2z" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/zbpe-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=zbpe399&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_mobile-zbpe399_2.png?qZnBcIGCJvPR8A2IAZOZuOxdoGZDatiI" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210901/paywall_mobile-zbpe399_2.png?qZnBcIGCJvPR8A2IAZOZuOxdoGZDatiI" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
